Agents don't work that way. They loop. Calling tools, branching, backtracking,
holding contacts across dozens of steps. That's a completely different hardware problem.
Current GPUs hit 30 to 40% of peak utilization on these workloads because the work is bursty,
bouncing between memory bound model calls, IO bound tool use, and CPU bound orchestration.
That gap is where purpose-built silicon wins.

Google built TPUv7 for inference specifically.
