*Narrator:*

AI models are improving really fast. They're now able to do complex work far beyond software engineering. Services like accounting, tax, administration started as manual services and then became software. More recently, that software became AI copilots. That's what most startups from 2023 to 2025 built — tools for the existing industry.

What we're now excited about is the next step: AI-native companies that don't sell software, they sell the service. Instead of giving you a tool, they just do the work. Often they will embed a professional in their company as well.

The reason this matters is simple. The total spend on services is many times larger than the spend on software, and a lot of these services are already outsourced, which makes it much easier to replace with an AI-native product.

Areas we're specifically interested in are insurance brokerage, accounting, tax, audit, compliance, healthcare administration. If you're building a company that replaces a service, not just improves it, we'd love to hear from you. Thank you.
