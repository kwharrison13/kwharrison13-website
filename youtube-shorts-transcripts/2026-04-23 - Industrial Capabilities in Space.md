*Adi Oltean (co-founder and chief engineer, Star Cloud):*

Hi, I am Adi Oltean, co-founder and chief engineer at Star Cloud, building data centers in space. The broad idea I would like to see is developing industrial capabilities on the moon and in deep space — particularly extracting raw materials such as silicon, aluminum, iron, and titanium through electrolysis, and 3D printing of complex structures from molten regolith on the moon. We should be more efficient than on earth due to lack of supports.

If you are working on something like that in the space, then Y Combinator would like to hear from you.
