Back in September [I announced](https://investing1012dot0.substack.com/p/open-source-knowledge) the launch of a new project I had been building: [Contrary Research](http://research.contrary.com). **This past week, less than 6 months in, we hit a big milestone! We launched our 100th memo.** We’ve written about companies like [OpenAI](https://research.contrary.com/reports/openai), [Ramp](https://research.contrary.com/reports/ramp), [Hugging Face](https://research.contrary.com/reports/hugging-face), [Anduril](https://research.contrary.com/reports/anduril) and a ton more. You can see all of our reports [here](https://research.contrary.com/reports).

We also produce a number of deep dives where we dive into a specific category. Thus far we’ve gone deep on how [Snowflake and Databricks will increasingly go head to head](https://research.contrary.com/reports/databrick-vs-snowflake), [how design companies like Figma and Canva are more important than just making simple logos and presentations](https://research.contrary.com/reports/the-designer-class), and how cybersecurity is [evolving rapidly](https://research.contrary.com/reports/unlocking-the-cybersecurity-landscape) whether it’s [endpoints](https://research.contrary.com/reports/era-of-endpoints), [cloud security](https://research.contrary.com/reports/taking-the-fight-to-the-cloud) (which we just published this week), or identity (coming in the next few months.)

In addition to co-authoring our recent piece on [cloud security](https://research.contrary.com/reports/taking-the-fight-to-the-cloud), I’ve spent a lot of time pouring my heart and soul into building our content library at Contrary Research. If you’re a fan of my writing on Substack, and you’re intrigued by the names and topics I’ve mentioned above, **I’d love to have you subscribe below to get our weekly newsletter from Contrary Research.**

Each week we share a couple of different things:

- A breakdown of an interesting topic touching private companies. In the past we’ve covered semiconductors, aerospace & defense tech, company building, and more
- 5 new memos every week on different private tech companies
- A rundown of the most interesting research, analysis, and commentary that we’ve been reading
- The latest events from Contrary Research (including an [upcoming webinar](https://lu.ma/s7xz0t9z) on Building Customer Love with panelists from Sourcegraph, Vercel, and HashiCorp)
