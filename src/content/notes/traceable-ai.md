# Traceable.ai

**One-line:** *An [API Security](/notes/api-security) vendor that competes with [Salt Security](/notes/salt-security) by spanning the full [DevSecOps](/notes/devsecops) lifecycle — testing and production — with a particular focus on [CI/CD](/notes/ci-cd).*

## What we know

- Focused on [CI/CD](/notes/ci-cd), covering both testing and managing the full [DevSecOps](/notes/devsecops) process. ([Nir Rothenberg](/notes/nir-rothenberg), CISO at Rapyd, in Salt Security Diligence — 2021)
- When [Nir Rothenberg](/notes/nir-rothenberg) evaluated it in early 2021 he found it "a bit rudimentary and just raw in terms of their product" — "a good tool but at the point they looked at it they were less impressed." (Salt Security Diligence — 2021)
- Surfaced repeatedly as a Salt competitor across multiple reference calls in the 2021 API security market sweep. (Salt Security Diligence — 2021)
- [Prabhath Karanth](/notes/prabhath-karanth) (Director of Security, [TripActions](/notes/tripactions)) said peers had looked at Traceable and he "hears good things about them," though it was "unclear if they're a pure API player." [TripActions](/notes/tripactions) ultimately chose Salt over Traceable. (Salt Security Diligence — 2021)
- [David Mark](/notes/david-mark) (DevOps Team Lead, Appsflyer) named Traceable as a name that had come up as Appsflyer looked around for alternatives to Salt. (Salt Security Diligence — 2021)
