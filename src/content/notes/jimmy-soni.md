# Jimmy Soni

**One-line:** *Author and historian; author of [The Founders](/books/the-founders), a granular history of PayPal as a constellation of subsequent Silicon Valley founders.*

## What we know

- Author of [The Founders](/books/the-founders) (Jimmy Soni). The book is structured less as a payments-company history and more as a study of a "scenius" — the talent vortex (Brian Eno's term) that produced the so-called PayPal Mafia.
- Source-of-truth credit: the book draws heavily on interviews with first-person participants. Many cited passages are direct first-person recollections.

## Mentioned in

- [The Founders](/books/the-founders) — as author

## Open questions

- Soni's earlier biographies (Claude Shannon, e.g.) are not in the corpus. Worth knowing his other work to gauge how his methodology shapes [The Founders](/books/the-founders).
