# John Mulaney

**One-line:** *Comedian; origin of the line "likability is a jail" that anchors Kyle's argument against agreeableness.*

## What we know

- Coined the line "likability is a jail," which [Alex Karp](/notes/alex-karp) later borrowed for his essay on agreeableness and creative output.
- Kyle uses it as the compact statement of the whole thesis: optimizing for approval imprisons you.
