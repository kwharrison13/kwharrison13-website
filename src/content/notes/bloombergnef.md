# BloombergNEF

Bloomberg's research arm covering renewable energy, transportation, and commodities — one of two research products (alongside Bloomberg Intelligence's equity analysts) layered on top of the core Bloomberg Terminal data product. It surfaces in the Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 dossier through an expert call with [Kyle Harrison (Bloomberg)](/notes/kyle-harrison-bloomberg), a BloombergNEF analyst (a name-coincidence with the wiki's owner), on November 2nd, 2021. He framed corporate decarbonization around three questions — how to collect and report ESG data, how to set net-zero goals, and how to finance them — and argued that investor pressure (notably Climate Action 100+) is the dominant driver of change, since voluntary corporate action stalls until capital allocators change how they invest. BloombergNEF's published research is cited in the dossier on corporate net-zero assessment and the surge in net-zero commitments.

**Context:** BloombergNEF (formerly Bloomberg New Energy Finance) is a strategic research provider, owned by Bloomberg L.P., covering clean energy, advanced transport, digital industry, and commodity markets.
