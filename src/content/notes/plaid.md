# Plaid

In Kyle's notes [Plaid](/notes/plaid) shows up most often as a **talent and reference node** rather than a diligence target in its own right. Several Plaid people appear as research contacts: [Shuli Song](/notes/shuli-song) (who runs/leads RevOps — reps, quota-setting, plan administration, commission calculation — at Plaid, and was a CaptivateIQ customer reference), [Hayden Pirkle](/notes/hayden-pirkle), and [Sundeep Peechu](/notes/sundeep-peechu) are all "surveyed for Kyle's talent-vortex research." Plaid is one of the high-growth fintech operators sampled in The Talent Vortex - Research alongside [Stripe](/notes/stripe) and [Dropbox](/notes/dropbox). The January 2022 recruiting call with Shuli Song (who did banking/consulting, then GitHub, then Plaid) is the clearest first-hand window into the company.

Plaid also appears as **fintech infrastructure** in diligence contexts: in the Pave Diligence — 2021 notes it's named as the source for financial data; in Salt Security Diligence — 2021 it's cited as one of the heavily-connected fintech endpoints (with [Stripe](/notes/stripe) and [Modern Treasury](/notes/modern-treasury)); and the CaptivateIQ notes describe Plaid as software used by thousands of digital financial apps (Betterment, Expensify, Venmo) and many large banks to let consumers connect their financial accounts.

**Context:** Plaid is a San Francisco financial-infrastructure company (founded 2013) that provides APIs connecting consumer bank accounts to fintech apps and services; its proposed 2020 acquisition by Visa was abandoned in 2021 amid antitrust scrutiny.
