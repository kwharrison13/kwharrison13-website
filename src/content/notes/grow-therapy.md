# Grow Therapy

**One-line definition:** *Provider-enablement platform that does marketing, insurance credentialing/billing, EHR, and scheduling for therapists, taking a cut of each session.*

## How sources describe it

- Per provider [Nicole Kleiman-Reck](/notes/nicole-kleiman-reck) — Grow does marketing (incl. Psychology Today), insurance contracting, billing, consent, EHR, and scheduling; providers keep their own notes (Grow offers templates); Grow takes ~5% (she also cited ~20% on some sessions); steady client flow, mostly insurance-based; credentialing follows the provider even if they leave; convenient mainly because it handles insurance. (September 22nd, 2021 — Nicole Kleiman-Reck (Grow Therapy))

## Where it shows up

- September 22nd, 2021 — Nicole Kleiman-Reck (Grow Therapy) — expert call with a Grow Therapy provider.
