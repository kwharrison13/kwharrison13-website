# Patrick Henry

Patrick Henry (1736–1799) was a Founding-era American orator and statesman, best remembered for his "Give me liberty, or give me death!" speech to the Virginia Convention. In [Speeches That Changed The World](/books/speeches-that-changed-the-world) Kyle's highlight catches his line to the Continental Congress — "I know of no other way of judging the future but by the past" — which the anthology uses as a meditation on history as the only instrument we have for reading what's ahead.
