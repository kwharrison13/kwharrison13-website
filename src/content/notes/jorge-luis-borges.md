# Jorge Luis Borges

Naval Ravikant names Borges, "an Argentine author," in his recommended-reading list, praising the short story collection *Ficciones* (also published as *Labyrinths*) as "amazing." He calls Borges "probably the most powerful author I have read who wasn't just outright writing philosophy," noting there was philosophy woven in alongside the sci-fi.
