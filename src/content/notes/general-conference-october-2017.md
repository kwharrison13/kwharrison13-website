# General Conference: October 2017

A semiannual general conference of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints that Kyle references as the source of a talk he drew on during his Alma 13–16 study. He cites "[The Priesthood and the Savior's Atoning Power](/notes/the-priesthood-and-the-savior-s-atoning-power)" by [Dale G. Renlund](/notes/dale-g-renlund), tagging it #[General Conference: October 2017](/notes/general-conference-october-2017), for the teaching that the [Atonement](/notes/atonement) gave [Jesus Christ](/notes/jesus) the power and authority to redeem us — "otherwise we would be infinitely lost." Kyle uses the talk to deepen the chapters' theme of entering "the rest of the Lord."

**Context:** General conference is held twice yearly (April and October) and broadcast worldwide; the October 2017 session is where Elder Dale G. Renlund delivered his address on the priesthood and the Savior's atoning power.
