# Atomic Ventures

**One-line definition:** *Orem-based venture-building firm that partners with strong engineers to build startups to traction, then steps back to advisor/passive-investor roles.*

## How sources describe it

- Raising a ~$35M fund focused on venture building; small team of two principals/associates ([Tyler Payne](/notes/tyler-payne) one of them) plus a Mexico-based partner running a dev team (6 designers, 50 engineers). (source: raw/calls/May 31st, 2018.md)
- Hands-on until a seed round, then advisory through Series A, then passive; invests ~$150–250K initially, ~$500K at seed, ~$1–3M at Series A. (source: raw/calls/May 31st, 2018.md)
- Builds around machine learning / analytics, monetizing on pain points; projects include oil-field logistics, coffee-distributor ops software, agtech. (source: raw/calls/May 31st, 2018.md)
- Note: distinct from "Atomico." (source: raw/calls/May 31st, 2018.md)
