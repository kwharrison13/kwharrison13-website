# John H. Groberg

Attributed in [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book) for a passage on "Making Decisions The Lord's Way" — the principle that the Lord more reliably confirms a wrong course than a right one, so rather than waiting for a burning confirmation before acting, "I will move unless I feel it is wrong." By eliminating wrong paths, you converge on the right direction and receive assurance you are "not doing the things he does not want me to do." This is the only attribution to Groberg in the collection.

**Context:** John H. Groberg (b. 1934) is an emeritus General Authority Seventy of the LDS Church, widely known for his missionary service in Tonga, recounted in *In the Eye of the Storm* and dramatized in the film *The Other Side of Heaven*.
