# Nicodemus

Nicodemus appears in Kyle's Alma 36–38 study under the "Get Used To Different" thread, drawn from [The Chosen (show)](/notes/the-chosen-show)'s depiction of how Christ chose his disciples. Kyle's note observes that Christ told Simon "you didn't understand when I chose you either," and that Christ "invited Nicodemus to follow if he would have listened" — framing Nicodemus as someone given the same invitation but who did not (yet) act on it. The point in context is about the unexpected, counterintuitive nature of Christ's calling.

**Context:** Nicodemus was a Pharisee and member of the Jewish ruling council who, in the Gospel of John (chapter 3), came to Jesus by night and was told "ye must be born again." He later defended Jesus before the Sanhedrin and helped Joseph of Arimathea prepare the body for burial.
