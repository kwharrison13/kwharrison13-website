# First Group

A large outsourced ground-transportation operator that engaged transportation/mobility consultant [Matt Curwood](/notes/matt-curwood). Per the [Matt Curwood](/notes/matt-curwood) page (drawn from a May 2018 networking call), First Group is described as the "largest outsourced transportation operator" and brought Curwood in on acquisitions and on a new division to compete in employee shuttles, as well as on Australia market entry (source: [Matt Curwood](/notes/matt-curwood)). In Kyle's corpus it appears as one of Curwood's named clients in the shuttle/coach and employee-commuter [Transportation](/notes/transportation) space.

**Context:** FirstGroup plc is a UK-based multinational transport operator running bus and rail services across the UK and, historically, large North American student-transport and transit-contracting operations. (General background — only the Curwood consulting engagement is attested in this wiki.)
