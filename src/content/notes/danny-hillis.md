# Danny Hillis

Credited (via an editorial correction in the text) as the actual source of the line "technology is the set of things that don't quite work yet" — the book initially attributes it to Alan Kay before flagging Hillis as the correct originator. Naval Ravikant uses the quote to argue that once something works reliably it's no longer "technology," it's just infrastructure society expects. No other detail on Hillis is given.
