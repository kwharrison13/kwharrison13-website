# Palmer Luckey

**One-line:** *Founder of Oculus and [Anduril](/notes/anduril); case study in pushing past dependencies with conviction.*

## What we know

- Founded Oculus (VR) and later [Anduril](/notes/anduril) (defense), persevering through "Layer 2" naysayers — investors who said VR couldn't produce a large company and that defense was an immoral endeavor.
- Kyle frames him as someone who "has never really struggled with Layer 1 dependencies" — indifferent to broad social approval from the start.
- On Layer 3 (unavoidable) dependencies: ["you need to care about that 1% of the world that is going to be your ride or die."](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Pnc5G0RzTLw) Anduril deliberately solicits true believers.
