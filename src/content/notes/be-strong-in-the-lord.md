# Be Strong In The Lord

A "mini-talk" referenced from Kyle's Everyone Is Human topical study on forgiveness, confession, and forsaking sin. It is invoked at the point where that study turns from confession to **forsaking** — the resolve, "physical and mental," never to repeat a sin: you may slip, but the resolve must hold, and afterward you stop talking about it rather than "dumping the bucket a little at a time," so that [Satan](/notes/satan) has no power over you. Everyone Is Human lists it alongside Mighty Repentance and How To Apply The Atonement as related studies. The page is currently a pointer to that strength-to-forsake theme; its own standalone content has not yet been ingested.
