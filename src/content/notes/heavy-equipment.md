# Heavy Equipment

**One-line:** *The industrial machinery market (excavators, combines, Bobcat/John Deere gear) that [Arrow XYZ](/notes/arrow-xyz) is building an "Amazon" for.*

## What we know

- Commodity-like machines sold through dealers; the industry "woke up" during COVID and started buying online. Incumbent listings marketplace [Sandhills Publishing](/notes/sandhills-publishing) does ~$187B of transactions. [Arrow XYZ](/notes/arrow-xyz) aims to be the buying-and-selling "Amazon" for the category via a two-sided marketplace (dealer software for supply + social-media demand gen). (September 26th, 2022 — Charles Anderson (Arrow XYZ))
