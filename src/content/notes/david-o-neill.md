# David O'Neill

A founder/operator at [APIMetrics](/notes/apimetrics) whom Kyle spoke with on November 8th, 2021 as part of his Index Ventures sourcing work — see November 8th, 2021 — David O'Neill (APIMetrics). O'Neill positioned APIMetrics as the trusted, independent source of truth for API performance, started around the same time as [Postman](/notes/postman), Runscope, and API Science. He framed the core problem as limited visibility into API performance analytics — tools like [Appdynamics](/notes/appdynamics) and [Dynatrace](/notes/dynatrace) never really worked — which left banks and their third-party providers fighting over who was at fault.

Per his account the product resonated strongly with the banking community (US Bank as a customer, ~70% of UK high-street banks), with fintech contracts ranging from $14K to $170K (potentially $300K+ at HSBC Global), and traction that had stalled at ~$400K ARR for two years before doubling in the prior six months, against a ~$5M ARR pipeline. He described plans to add security monitoring (talking to [42 Crunch](/notes/42-crunch)), to store every synthetic test, and to position the company as a "nexus" for verifying compliance and governance in production.
