# Seth Klarman

Seth Klarman is a value investor quoted in [On the Nature of Long-Term Holds](/notes/on-the-nature-of-long-term-holds) on [Compounding](/notes/compounding): "The effects of compounding even moderate returns over many years are compelling, if not downright mind boggling."

**Context:** Seth Klarman is the founder of the Baupost Group, a Boston-based value-investing hedge fund, and author of the cult investing classic *Margin of Safety*.

## Where this appears

- [On the Nature of Long-Term Holds](/notes/on-the-nature-of-long-term-holds) — quoted on the long-run power of compounding even moderate returns.
