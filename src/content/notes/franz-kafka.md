# Franz Kafka

Author of [Metamorphosis](/books/metamorphosis), which Kyle has read. The novella follows Gregor Samsa, who wakes transformed into an insect and slowly becomes a burden to the family he had supported — a story Kyle's highlights read through the lens of [Family](/notes/family), duty, and the loss of one's role within it.

**Context:** Franz Kafka (1883–1924) was a German-language writer from Prague whose work — including *The Metamorphosis*, *The Trial*, and *The Castle* — gave rise to the term "Kafkaesque" for surreal, alienating, bureaucratic dread.
