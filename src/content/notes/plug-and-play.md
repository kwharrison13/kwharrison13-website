# Plug and Play

Global startup accelerator and stage-agnostic investor that surfaced in Kyle's notes through a brief May 2018 intro call with [Elizabeth Kukka](/notes/elizabeth-kukka). Per that call, Plug and Play writes $25K–$200K checks, runs a [Startup Accelerator](/notes/startup-accelerator) in addition to investing, and is generally stage-agnostic with a seed–Series B+ focus. It operated offices in New York, the Bay Area, and Munich, with Singapore and Beijing opening at the time.

**Context:** Plug and Play is a Silicon Valley (Sunnyvale)–based innovation platform, accelerator, and venture investor that runs industry-focused programs connecting startups with corporate partners worldwide.
