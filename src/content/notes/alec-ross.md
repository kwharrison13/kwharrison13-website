# Alec Ross

Author of [Industries of the Future](/notes/industries-of-the-future), cited in [The Future of Work (Article)](/notes/the-future-of-work-article). His West Virginia vs. [Estonia](/notes/estonia) parable anchors the essay: West Virginia clung to coal and chemicals as both automated and offshored (Charleston losing 40% of its jobs), while Estonia leaned into the digital future.

**Context:** Alec Ross is an American technology-policy expert who served as Senior Advisor for Innovation to Secretary of State Hillary Clinton. He is the author of *The Industries of the Future* (2016).
