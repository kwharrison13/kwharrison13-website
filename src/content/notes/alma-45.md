# Alma 45

A chapter of the Book of Mormon falling within the Come Follow Me reading block for August 3–9, 2020 (Alma 43–52). It is listed in that study's chapter range, but Kyle's notes for that week cover [Alma 43](/notes/alma-43) only — there is no annotation specific to Alma 45 in the source.

**Context:** In the Book of Mormon narrative, Alma 45 records Alma the Younger blessing his son Helaman, prophesying the eventual destruction of the Nephites, and then departing — never to be heard from again.
