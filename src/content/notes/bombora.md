# Bombora

Bombora shows up in Kyle's notes as a comparison company in the September 13th, 2022 — Nisha Swarup call, where [Nisha Swarup](/notes/nisha-swarup) was building a vendor [Discovery + Diligence](/notes/discovery-diligence) product for [Procurement](/notes/procurement). In mapping out case studies, Bombora was grouped under the [Sales Intelligence](/notes/sales-intelligence) category alongside [6sense](/notes/6sense) and [ZoomInfo](/notes/zoominfo) — the intent-data / buyer-research end of the comp set (as distinct from competitive-intelligence tools like [Crayon](/notes/crayon) and [Klue](/notes/klue)). It serves as a reference point for how buyers gather and act on signals about companies, not as a diligence target in its own right.

**Context:** Bombora is a B2B intent-data provider; it aggregates content-consumption signals across a cooperative of publishers to tell sales and marketing teams which accounts are actively researching a given topic or product category.
