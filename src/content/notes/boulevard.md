# Boulevard

Surfaces once in Pave Diligence — 2021, where it is cited — alongside Instabase, Anrok, [Discord](/notes/discord), [Newfront Insurance](/notes/newfront-insurance), Scale AI, and [Applyboard](/notes/applyboard) — as one of the 40+ [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) portfolio companies contributing data into [Pave](/notes/pave)'s give-to-get compensation-benchmarking network. It appears as an example data contributor, not as a subject of the diligence itself.

**Context:** Boulevard is a vertical-SaaS company (founded 2016, Los Angeles) building a client-experience and business-management platform for salons, spas, and other appointment-based self-care businesses.
