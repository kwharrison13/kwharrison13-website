# Genesis 2

A chapter of the Old Testament book of Genesis, cited in Kyle's Abrahamic Covenant gospel-study notes. There it is referenced (Genesis 2:9–13, the rivers of Eden) in connection with the birthright and lineage theme — the note ties the passage to the claim that the Arab nations' historic enmity toward Christians and Jews traces back to the disputed birthright.

**Context:** Genesis 2 gives the second creation account, the garden of Eden, the placing of Adam, and the rivers (Pison, Gihon, Hiddekel, Euphrates) that watered it.

## Where this appears

- Abrahamic Covenant — cited (Gen. 2:9–13) within the birthright/lineage discussion of the covenant and the divided inheritance of Abraham's seed.
