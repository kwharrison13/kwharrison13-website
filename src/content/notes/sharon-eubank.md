# Sharon Eubank

A speaker Kyle quotes from the October 2021 General Conference of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, identified in his notes as part of the Relief Society Presidency. Her remarks center on the Church's humanitarian mandate: that "The Church of Jesus Christ is under divine mandate to care for the poor — it's one of the pillars of the plan of salvation," and that "Prophets have charge for the whole earth, not just for members of the church." Kyle's notes record her report that the Church ran more than 1,500 COVID-19 projects over the prior 18 months while also responding to 933 natural disasters and refugee crises across 108 countries. She is also quoted on the essence of the gospel being "contained in how we answer the question 'Do you love me?'"

**Context:** Sharon Eubank served in the General Presidency of the Relief Society and as director of Latter-day Saint Charities, the Church's humanitarian arm.
