# Predicting the Future

A recurring theme in Kyle's wiki about the difficulty of forecasting technology and the value of scoring old predictions against what actually happened. In [What You Don't Know About The Headphone Jack](/notes/what-you-don-t-know-about-the-headphone-jack), Perell's 2016 forecasts (driverless cars, VR headsets replacing daily video, cord-cutting toward streaming) become a prediction scorecard — Kyle's hindsight grades land only one of three, illustrating how hard prediction is.
