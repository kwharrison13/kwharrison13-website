# Emsi

Emsi appears in Kyle's wiki as a portfolio company of [Strada Education Network](/notes/strada-education-network) (formerly USA Funds), discussed in the May 31st, 2018 intro call with Strada investor [Andre Bennin](/notes/andre-bennin). Bennin helped Emsi on the board-presentation side. Per that call, Emsi was "a critical investment" to Strada's ability to provide resources for career and workforce transitions: where competitors like Burning Glass relied on scraping data, Emsi's data-gathering approach was described as more novel, with more tools to leverage for clients — and Emsi's targeting of state governments and local communities made it strategically important to Strada's [Future of Work](/notes/future-of-work) thesis.

**Context:** Emsi (Economic Modeling Specialists International) is a labor-market analytics firm providing workforce, skills, and economic data to colleges, employers, and governments; it later merged with Burning Glass Technologies to form Lightcast.
