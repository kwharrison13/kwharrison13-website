# John 16

A chapter of the Gospel of John in the New Testament. In Kyle's Spiritual Gifts gospel-study notes, John 16 supplies several verses on the workings of the Holy Ghost: 16:13 ("if we are too proud, we cannot be guided"; "a seer sees by the Holy Ghost") and 16:14 ("it glorifies Christ, making Him more than a good or great man"). The chapter sits in the source's scripture block alongside [1 Corinthians 12](/notes/1-corinthians-12), [Moroni 10](/notes/moroni-10), [Moroni 7](/notes/moroni-7), and several Doctrine and Covenants sections as a cross-reference for the doctrine of spiritual gifts and revelation.

**Context:** John 16 records part of Christ's farewell discourse to the Apostles, including the promise that the Comforter (the Holy Ghost) "will guide you into all truth" (16:13) and "shall glorify me" (16:14).

## Where this appears

- Spiritual Gifts — John 16:13–14 cited on guidance by the Holy Ghost, seership, and the Spirit glorifying Christ.
