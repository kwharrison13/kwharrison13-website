# Censys

Censys surfaces in the Noname Security - Diligence dossier as an example of the security-research ecosystem in Ann Arbor, Michigan. In the October 2021 call, [Dave Thompson](/notes/dave-thompson) of [Kong](/notes/kong) described "an interesting security network in Ann Arbor because they attracted a ton of security researchers" — citing Duo Labs and Deep Field — and noted that the "3rd generation of companies in Ann Arbor" includes companies like Censys, which he characterized as building "a map of the internet." The reference is an aside about the geography of cybersecurity talent rather than a competitive comparison to Noname.

**Context:** *Censys is an internet-wide scanning and attack-surface management company spun out of academic research at the University of Michigan (Ann Arbor); its product continuously scans the public internet to map hosts, certificates, and exposed services, used by security teams for external attack-surface discovery.*
