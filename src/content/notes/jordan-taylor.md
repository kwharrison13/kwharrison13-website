# Jordan Taylor

Co-founder and Head of Product at Seso, the agricultural-labor marketplace Kyle's firm diligenced for its Series A. Per the December 13th, 2021 — Seso (Series A) notes, Taylor previously worked in product at Farmers Business Network — described as the largest marketplace for agricultural supplies in the US — and earlier was a PM at Dropbox; he holds an undergraduate degree from UPenn. He teamed up with CEO [Michael Guirguis](/notes/michael-guirguis) (with [William Dembinski](/notes/william-dembinski) as Head of Engineering) after concluding that labor was the next big problem area to streamline for farmers. Kyle's notes flag relatively fewer direct interactions with Taylor but rate his agricultural domain expertise as relevant to the bet.
