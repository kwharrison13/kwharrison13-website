# Ether 15

Ether 15 closes the Jaredite record and Kyle's November 23–29, 2020. Ether 12–15: "By Faith All Things Are Fulfilled" Come Follow Me study. It carries the final scene of the civilization's self-destruction: Kyle records Ether 15:29 ("when they had all fallen by the sword, save it were Coriantumr and Shiz, behold Shiz had fainted with the loss of blood") and Ether 15:32 ("Coriantumr fell to the earth, and became as if he had no life") as the endpoint of the warning that Coriantumr "repented not." In the study's own notes the chapter is still marked a stub ("No notes recorded yet"), but it functions as the terminal beat of the unit's themes — the unquenchability of wickedness developed in [Ether 14](/notes/ether-14) and [The Pride Cycle](/notes/the-pride-cycle) — where a once-mighty people is reduced to two survivors.

**Context:** Ether 15 is the final chapter of the Book of Ether (Book of Mormon). It narrates the last battle of the Jaredites, in which the entire nation is destroyed except for the kings Coriantumr and Shiz, ending when Coriantumr beheads the fainting Shiz — fulfilling the prophet Ether's prophecy of the people's complete destruction.
