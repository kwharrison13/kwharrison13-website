# 1 Nephi 21

A chapter in the Book of Mormon's first book of Nephi, covered in Kyle's weekly Come Follow Me study 1 Nephi 16–22 — I Will Prepare the Way before You, where it is listed among the week's chapters ([1 Nephi 16](/notes/1-nephi-16) through [1 Nephi 22](/notes/1-nephi-22)) keyed to themes of [Hope](/notes/hope), the [Liahona](/notes/liahona), being [Anxiously Engaged](/notes/anxiously-engaged), and [Lessons From Laman and Lemuel](/notes/lessons-from-laman-and-lemuel). The page does not yet draw verse-level commentary specifically from this chapter.

**Context:** 1 Nephi 21 is Nephi quoting Isaiah 49 — the Lord's covenant promise that scattered Israel will be remembered and gathered ("I will not forget thee... I have graven thee upon the palms of my hands"). It pairs with the week's theme that the Lord prepares the way before His people.
