# GO1

GO1 recurs throughout Kyle's 2021 Pave Diligence — 2021 research, where it functions as a node in Pave's customer and reference network rather than a diligence subject itself. Pave introduced its own customers to GO1 (alongside [Persona](/notes/persona) and [Mercury](/notes/mercury)), signaling a peer/partner relationship among portfolio-adjacent companies. The notes also track outreach to Johnny Curl at GO1 as a comp-benchmarking reference contact, and log GO1 as a customer reference dated October 5th, 2021. The appearances are all in service of building conviction on Pave, not evaluating GO1.

**Context:** GO1 is an Australian-founded corporate learning and training content aggregation platform, providing organizations a single subscription to a large library of professional development courses.
