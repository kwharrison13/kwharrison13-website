# M. Russell Ballard

An Apostle of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (member of the Quorum of the Twelve). In the corpus he appears as the author of the BYU devotional "Follow the Doctrine and Gospel of Christ", where he urges young adults to "keep the gospel simple" by anchoring to the five principles of the [Doctrine of Christ](/notes/doctrine-of-christ), and as a source in [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book).

## Where this appears

- Follow the Doctrine and Gospel of Christ — M. Russell Ballard — BYU devotional on the [Doctrine of Christ](/notes/doctrine-of-christ) (faith, repentance, baptism, the Holy Ghost, enduring to the end).
- [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book)

## Connections

- [Doctrine of Christ](/notes/doctrine-of-christ) — the five-principle framework Ballard urges young adults to anchor to in his devotional.
- [Faith](/notes/faith) — first principle of the Doctrine of Christ he teaches.
- [Repentance](/notes/repentance) — second principle of the Doctrine of Christ he teaches.
- [Baptism](/notes/baptism) — third principle of the Doctrine of Christ he teaches.
- [Holy Ghost](/notes/holy-ghost) — receiving the gift of the Holy Ghost, the fourth principle he teaches.
- [Enduring to the End](/notes/enduring-to-the-end) — the fifth principle, completing the Doctrine of Christ in his devotional.
