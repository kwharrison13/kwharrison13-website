# M. Russell Ballard

An Apostle of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (member of the Quorum of the Twelve). In the corpus he appears as the author of the BYU devotional "Follow the Doctrine and Gospel of Christ", where he urges young adults to "keep the gospel simple" by anchoring to the five principles of the Doctrine of Christ, and as a source in [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book).

## Where this appears

- Follow the Doctrine and Gospel of Christ — M. Russell Ballard — BYU devotional on the Doctrine of Christ (faith, repentance, baptism, the Holy Ghost, enduring to the end).
