# Sylva

A company led by [Tom Guthrie](/notes/tom-guthrie), appearing in Kyle's notes as a Contrary portfolio update he tracked from early 2022 through 2023. The thesis, as a backer described it, is that "you can acquire professional communities and there is a playbook you can run to improve revenue / profit... and create an amalgamation of several that can trade for an attractive multiple" — a roll-up of professional communities (October 16th, 2023 — Mark Neider (Tritium Partners)). Monthly updates Kyle read tracked operating goals like establishing a profitable individual-member acquisition channel for each community, diversifying revenue, and implementing Sylva's core data infrastructure (November 7th, 2022 — Cleared Email).

By late 2023, Mark Neider of Tritium Partners — an investor in the company — gave Kyle a candid read: all positive feedback on [Tom Guthrie](/notes/tom-guthrie) personally and on the involvement of experienced execs (notably David Samuelson, a former CEO of a large professional community), but not yet enough data to see "legs 2 and 3" of the roll-up play out. Neider framed the delay as economy-driven rather than a flaw in the playbook, projecting $6-7M EBITDA by year-end as the threshold that would unlock more conviction — "the company is right on the cusp of achieving greatness if they can just see the playbook start to work" (October 16th, 2023 — Mark Neider (Tritium Partners)).
