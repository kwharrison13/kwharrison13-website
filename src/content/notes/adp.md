# ADP

ADP appears in Kyle's Pave Diligence — 2021 dossier as the incumbent HRIS/payroll system that Pave's compensation product integrates against and partly displaces. In the [Allbirds](/notes/allbirds) expert call, ADP is named as Allbirds' HRIS and payroll platform — including a built-in comp-planning tool the customer "bought and immediately regretted" because it offered no benchmarking. ADP is also cited as the friction point in the manual status-quo workflow Pave replaces: total-rewards leaders described pulling reports out of ADP, [Carta](/notes/carta), and a performance tool, then stitching them together in Excel. A notable competitive detail from the diligence: a reason Carta could not easily become a Pave competitor is that "they don't have an integration with [ADP](/notes/adp)," underscoring ADP's position as a system-of-record everyone must connect to.

**Context:** ADP (Automatic Data Processing) is one of the largest payroll and human-capital-management providers in the U.S., serving employers of all sizes with payroll, HR, benefits, and compliance software.
