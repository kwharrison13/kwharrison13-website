# Ryan Holiday's Daily Stoic

Daily Stoic is the company [Ryan Holiday](/notes/ryan-holiday) founded, and the descriptor under which Holiday appears on the [Maximus](/notes/maximus) cap table in Kyle's notes. When Kyle reviewed Maximus's AngelList docs in June 2022, Holiday — "founder of Daily Stoic" — showed up among a roster of consumer- and crypto-founder [Super Angels](/notes/super-angels) backing the direct-to-consumer men's-health company, alongside figures like [Dave Asprey](/notes/dave-asprey) (Bulletproof), Fred Ehrsam ([Coinbase](/notes/coinbase)), and Jeff Weiser (former [Shopify](/notes/shopify) CMO). In the wiki, Daily Stoic functions mainly as Holiday's identifying venture on that super-angel roster rather than as a subject of its own diligence.

**Context:** Daily Stoic is Ryan Holiday's Stoicism-focused media and commerce brand — a daily email, books, podcast, and merchandise — built off his popular Stoic-philosophy writing (*The Obstacle Is the Way*, *Ego Is the Enemy*, the four-part Stoic Virtues series including *Discipline Is Destiny*).
