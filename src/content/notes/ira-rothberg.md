# Ira Rothberg

Ira Rothberg is an investor quoted in [On the Nature of Long-Term Holds](/notes/on-the-nature-of-long-term-holds) on the primacy of [Compounding](/notes/compounding): "The power of compounding is so great that our first job as investors is to avoid anything that might short circuit it."

## Where this appears

- [On the Nature of Long-Term Holds](/notes/on-the-nature-of-long-term-holds) — quoted on protecting compounding as the investor's first job.
