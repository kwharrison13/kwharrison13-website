# Greg Lukianoff

Greg Lukianoff appears in Kyle's notes as the co-author (with [Jonathan Haidt](/notes/jonathan-haidt)) of [The Coddling of the American Mind](/notes/the-coddling-of-the-american-mind), which sits in Kyle's anti-library. The book was recommended by [Zach Edwards](/notes/zach-edwards) during the September 17th, 2021 call (September 17th, 2021 — Zach Edwards (Clearstone Associates)), in a discussion of secularism, autonomy, and millennials' desire for self-direction (the "[Rise Against Authority](/notes/rise-against-authority)" and [Creator Economy](/notes/creator-economy) threads). Its argument — that three "Great Untruths" of fragility, emotional reasoning, and us-vs-them thinking are miseducating a generation — is the through-line wherever it surfaces.

**Context:** Greg Lukianoff is an attorney and free-speech advocate, president of the Foundation for Individual Rights and Expression (FIRE), and co-author of *The Coddling of the American Mind* (2018) and *The Canceling of the American Mind*.
