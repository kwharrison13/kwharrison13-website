# Christopher Weber

A sustainability contact at [BlackRock](/notes/blackrock) who appears on the outreach list in Index Ventures' 2021 Watershed diligence. In the "Contacts → Emailed" section of that dossier, Weber (at BlackRock) is one of the corporate-sustainability practitioners Kyle reached out to as a reference/expert while building the diligence — alongside contacts at Apple, Google, Salesforce, IKEA, Goldman Sachs, Lyft, Walmart, Microsoft, and others. The notes don't record the content of any conversation with him; he is a named outreach target only. BlackRock is otherwise material to the Watershed thesis, which cites Larry Fink's letter declaring the firm expects every portfolio company to plan for net-zero emissions.
