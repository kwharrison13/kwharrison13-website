# D&C 37

A section of the [Doctrine and Covenants](/notes/doctrine-and-covenants) that anchors Kyle's Teaching Repentance study. **D&C 37:7–10** is cited as a charge to "open our mouths boldly" — Kyle glosses it as *"The fields are white, OYM!"* (open your mouth) — paired with [D&C 75](/notes/dandc-75):2–5. The boldness it commands is framed not as hellfire preaching but as extending invitations to repent, trusting the Holy Ghost to teach ([D&C 50](/notes/dandc-50):13–22).

**Context:** Doctrine and Covenants section 37 is a December 1830 revelation given to [Joseph Smith](/notes/joseph-smith) and [Sidney Rigdon](/notes/sidney-rigdon), the first commandment for the Saints to gather (to Ohio). Its verses on lifting up the voice are commonly read as a missionary charge to proclaim the gospel boldly.
