# Ben Casnocha

Entrepreneur, writer, and investor — co-author with [Reid Hoffman](/notes/reid-hoffman) of [The Start-Up of You](/books/the-start-up-of-you), the book that argues you should manage your career like a start-up (network, adapt, take intelligent risks). The book's frontmatter and people list both credit him as Hoffman's co-author. He also surfaces in [Talent (Book)](/books/talent) (by Tyler Cowen and Daniel Gross) among the acknowledged contributors to the talent-evaluation discussion, listed alongside figures like [Ben Horowitz](/notes/ben-horowitz), [Reid Hoffman](/notes/reid-hoffman), [Patrick Collison](/notes/patrick-collison), and Peter Thiel — placing him within the same Silicon Valley network the talent-spotting book draws on.

**Context:** Ben Casnocha is an American entrepreneur and author who has worked closely with Reid Hoffman; beyond co-authoring *The Start-Up of You*, he later helped run Hoffman's "Blitzscaling" course and the venture firm Village Global.
