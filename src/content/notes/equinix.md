# Equinix

Equinix appears in Kyle's cybersecurity diligence notes primarily through its security leadership. [George Do](/notes/george-do) is recorded as concurrently CISO at Equinix (alongside his role as SVP & CISO at [Gojek](/notes/gojek)), where he "implemented global security platforms across 200+ locations" — a detail that frames Equinix as a globally distributed colocation/data-center operator. The [Nir Rothenberg](/notes/nir-rothenberg) page also lists him as concurrently or historically CISO at Equinix. Separately, in the Salt Security Diligence — 2021 dossier, Equinix's then-CISO Michael Montoya is quoted endorsing [Salt Security](/notes/salt-security): "the security landscape is shifting to APIs, and Salt Security is on the cutting edge of enterprise security strategy."

**Context:** *Equinix (NASDAQ: EQIX) is a global data-center and colocation REIT operating interconnection facilities ("IBX" data centers) across more than 200 locations worldwide, providing the physical infrastructure and network interconnection that many cloud and enterprise customers depend on.*
