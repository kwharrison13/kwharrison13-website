# Syndio

Syndio appears in the Pave Diligence — 2021 research as part of Kyle's competitive landscape mapping for [Pave](/notes/pave). It is filed under "Add-Ons" in the compensation-tooling map, grouped with [Pequity](/notes/pequity) — adjacent point tools that sit alongside the core comp-planning and benchmarking incumbents (like [Radford](/notes/radford) / [AON](/notes/aon)) Pave aims to disrupt.

**Context:** Syndio is a workplace-equity analytics platform focused on pay equity and pay-gap analysis, helping companies measure and remediate compensation disparities across gender and race.
