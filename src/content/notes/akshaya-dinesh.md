# Akshaya Dinesh

**One-line:** *Founder of [Spellbound](/notes/spellbound) (no-code interactive in-email experiences); Stanford dropout who previously started a venture-backed consumer startup.*

## What we know

- Building [Spellbound](/notes/spellbound), which lets companies embed interactive experiences (forms, surveys, meeting bookings, event signups, transactions) inside emails without code. (source: December 14th, 2021 daily-note)
- Raised a pre-seed co-led by Shahed at Hyper; signed brands to a private beta via a design-partner program and was weighing a larger seed. (source: December 14th, 2021 daily-note)
- Backed by founders/CEOs of Github, Notion, Loom, Kayak, Sendgrid; design partners included Product Hunt, Mailgun, DoNotPay, and Pave. (source: December 14th, 2021 daily-note)
