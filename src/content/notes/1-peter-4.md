# 1 Peter 4

A New Testament chapter cited in Kyle's Eye single to the glory of God gospel-study notes. There, 1 Peter 4:6 ("the gospel preached also to them that are dead") supports a "Query or Conjecture" that some born in times of apostasy may have been mercifully spared a gospel call they could not yet withstand — so that they might learn of and accept the gospel later in the spirit world. The reference is paired with [D&C 121](/notes/dandc-121):34 ("many are called, but few are chosen") to frame this as a "grand act of mercy."

**Context:** 1 Peter 4 exhorts believers to live by the will of God rather than the flesh, references the gospel being preached to the dead, and counsels charity and rejoicing in shared suffering with Christ.
