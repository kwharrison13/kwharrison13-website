# Abish

A Lamanitish woman in the Book of Mormon, appearing in Kyle's Alma 17–22 study. When king Lamoni, the queen, and Ammon all fell to the earth under the power of God, Abish alone did not — she "having been converted unto the Lord for many years, on account of a remarkable vision of her father" (Alma 19:16). Kyle drew this under the heading **"Parenting in the Scriptures,"** noting that Abish's father didn't have to share his vision — "it was probably socially unacceptable" — but he did, and what's more, she believed him. Her secret faith, carried for years until it found its moment, is the parental lesson Kyle pulled from the episode.

**Context:** Abish is one of the few women named in the Book of Mormon. After the royal household collapses under the Spirit, she runs to gather the people to witness the scene, setting in motion the conversion of Lamoni's people (Alma 19).
