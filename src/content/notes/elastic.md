# Elastic

Elastic surfaces in two of Kyle's 2021 sourcing notes as a reference point for search and for cloud-security M&A. In the November 8th, 2021 [George Sivulka](/notes/george-sivulka) ([Hebbia](/notes/hebbia)) call, Elastic is invoked as a model for Hebbia's distribution: Sivulka suggested that to make their neural-search product less general, "they could model Elastic and sell it as an API" — positioning Elastic as the canonical search-infrastructure-as-API comparable.

In the November 1st, 2021 Otterize pre-brief from [Carlos Gonzalez-Cardenas](/notes/carlos-gonzalez-cardenas), Elastic appears on the buy side of the authorization landscape: **build.security** (a service-to-service authorization startup built on Open Policy Agent) was noted as "acquired by Elastic recently," placing it among the more directly competitive players to Otterize alongside Styra.

**Context:** Elastic (NYSE: ESTC) is the company behind Elasticsearch, the open-source search and analytics engine, and the Elastic Stack. This general background goes beyond what Kyle's notes record.

## Where this appears

- November 8th, 2021 — George Sivulka (Hebbia) — cited as a model Hebbia could follow to sell neural search as an API
- November 1st, 2021 — Otterize (Pre-Brief from Carlos Gonzalez-Cardenas) — noted as the acquirer of build.security, a service-to-service authorization competitor to Otterize
