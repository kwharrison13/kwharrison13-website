# Zach Edwards

Zach Edwards is a recurring contact in Kyle's investing-network notes, appearing across several years of calls under [Clearstone Associates](/notes/clearstone-associates) (and an earlier [Lead Edge Capital](/notes/lead-edge-capital) entry). The conversations range widely: an operator-first investing philosophy ("go get a great, smart person to run something"; "[Theory of Masters](/notes/theory-of-masters) — I'd rather call the author than read their book") and idea-generation like a "Roll up billboards" play in the July 2020 call; and a wide-ranging September 2021 call on diversity-focused products (Sequin, Canvas) and secularism, where he recommended [A Secular Age](/notes/a-secular-age) by [Charles Taylor](/notes/charles-taylor) and [The Coddling of the American Mind](/notes/the-coddling-of-the-american-mind) by [Greg Lukianoff](/notes/greg-lukianoff) and [Jonathan Haidt](/notes/jonathan-haidt).

He also functions as a connector: in April 2022 Zach introduced Kyle to [Nick Anger](/notes/nick-anger) and [Brian Blair](/notes/brian-blair) at [RBC](/notes/rbc). Note the March 2020 Lead Edge entry (a Teamshares reference call, with conviction-and-creativity framing on leaving Coatue) may be a *different* Zach Edwards than the Clearstone/RBC contact — verify before merging.
