# Greenblatt

In [The Expertise Economy](/books/the-expertise-economy), Greenblatt is cited for a reciprocity philosophy on talent and value-sharing, summed up as: "they do well, we do well; we do well, they do well. That's the deal." The authors invoke this stance to argue that companies have forgotten basic management principles about aligning their success with their employees' — a framing the book uses in its broader case for investing in workforce skills.
