# Mario Gabriele

**One-line:** *Writer behind [The Generalist](/notes/the-generalist), author of the A24 briefing Kyle calls "one of my very favorite pieces about the movie business."*

## What we know

- Wrote ["A24: The Rise of a Cultural Conglomerate"](https://www.generalist.com/briefing/a24), the backbone source of [The Hits Business](/essays/the-hits-business).
- His framing — "your size is your strategy," A24's "venture-like search for outliers," and backing "true auteurs" — supplies the essay's central film-to-venture analogy.
