# WHOOP

A consumer health-sensor company, grouped in [HealthOS](/notes/healthos) alongside [Oura](/notes/oura) and [Eight Sleep](/notes/eight-sleep) as one of the sensor specialists Apple is content to be "good enough" against while deepening its own health-data moat via the Health app and HealthKit API.

**Context:** WHOOP makes a screenless fitness band sold on a subscription model, tracking strain, recovery, and sleep for athletes and high performers.

## Where this appears

- [HealthOS](/notes/healthos) — cited with Oura and Eight Sleep as a third-party sensor maker building on Apple's platform while Apple keeps the data layer.
