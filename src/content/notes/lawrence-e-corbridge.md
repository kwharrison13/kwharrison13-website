# Lawrence E. Corbridge

A Latter-day Saint general authority whose teaching on **the will** anchors Kyle's Virtue topical study. Corbridge's central line — that "in the end, your heart and your will is all that you have to give that the Lord does not already have" — frames consecration as the one offering that originates in us rather than in God: time, strength, and even each heartbeat are "granted to you," but "your will" is the exception. The study pairs this with his teaching on inner alignment as the precondition for happiness: "you can do the very things that should produce happiness, but if you are all the time fighting them within … you will not be happy," because a divided "frame of mind … will be contrary to the nature of happiness."

**Context:** Lawrence E. Corbridge (b. 1949) is an American religious leader who served as a General Authority Seventy in The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (2008–2020), and is widely known for his BYU devotional addresses on faith and discipleship.
