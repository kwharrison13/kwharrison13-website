# Matthew 23

A New Testament chapter Kyle cites in his study of Alma 13–16 — Enter into the Rest of the Lord for its teaching on servant-leadership (Matthew 23:11–12): "But he that is greatest among you shall be your servant. And whosoever shall exalt himself shall be abased; and he that shall humble himself shall be exalted." He pairs it with Alma's point that the [Priesthood](/notes/priesthood) is "to teach the commandments of God unto the children of men" — not to the lifting up of those who would be teachers. The chapter anchors the theme that authority in the gospel runs through humility and service rather than self-exaltation.
