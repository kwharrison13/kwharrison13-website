# Opendoor

**One-line definition:** *Real-estate iBuyer startup that scaled single-family-rental warehouse-debt financing; where [JD Rasmussen](/notes/jd-rasmussen) built the capital-markets function.*

## How sources describe it

- Series B company (~65 people, 2 on finance) when [JD Rasmussen](/notes/jd-rasmussen) joined; grew from <$300M to >$1B valuation (per March 5th, 2020 — JD Rasmussen (Opendoor)).
- Funded home purchases with ~$1B of credit-hedge-fund debt at 10–15% spread across a portfolio of homes, later transitioning to lower-cost debt as they scaled.
