# William Ury

Co-author (with [Roger Fisher](/notes/roger-fisher) and [Bruce Patton](/notes/bruce-patton)) of [Getting to Yes](/notes/getting-to-yes), a book in Kyle's anti-library (books-to-read).

**Context:** William Ury is an American anthropologist, negotiation expert, and co-founder of the Harvard Negotiation Project. Beyond *Getting to Yes*, he wrote *Getting Past No* and *The Power of a Positive No*, and has worked as a mediator in international conflicts.
