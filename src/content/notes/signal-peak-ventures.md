# Signal Peak Ventures

A venture firm that surfaces twice in Kyle's corpus. In the September 7th, 2021 — Steve Shillingford (Deepsee.ai) meeting, Steve Shillingford recounts leaving and spinning out technology — with support from Ron Heinz at Signal Peak Ventures — out of one of Heinz's portfolio companies, the founding story behind Deepsee.ai. Separately, Signal Peak Ventures is credited (alongside [David Blake](/notes/david-blake)) as a source of the recommendation that led Kyle to read [The Expertise Economy](/books/the-expertise-economy) in January 2019.

**Context:** Signal Peak Ventures is a Salt Lake City-based venture capital firm investing primarily in early-stage technology companies across the Intermountain West; Ron Heinz is a managing director there.

## Where this appears

- September 7th, 2021 — Steve Shillingford (Deepsee.ai) — named as the firm whose partner Ron Heinz backed Steve Shillingford's spin-out of Deepsee.ai from a portfolio company.
- [The Expertise Economy](/books/the-expertise-economy) — credited, alongside [David Blake](/notes/david-blake), with recommending the book to Kyle.
