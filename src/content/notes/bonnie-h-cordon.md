# Bonnie H. Cordon

Bonnie H. Cordon appears in Kyle's General Conference notes as a speaker in both General Conference — April 2020 and General Conference — October 2021. In October 2021 Kyle filed her talk under the "Love your neighbor" and "Love yourself" threads, capturing her teaching that "anything we do to help people make and keep covenants, we are helping to gather Israel" (tagged #[Gathering of Israel](/notes/gathering-of-israel)), her charge to "come now — but don't come alone," and the assurance that "the Savior's love for us is unfailing even when we fail." Her emphasis on youth understanding "their divine identity and their purpose" recurs across his notes.

**Context:** Bonnie H. Cordon served as the 14th Young Women general president of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints from 2018 to 2024, having previously served as Primary general president.
