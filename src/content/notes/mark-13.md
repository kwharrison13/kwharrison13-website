# Mark 13

A chapter in the New Testament Gospel of Mark, cited in Kyle's gospel study. In Christ Defines His Gospel it appears as a cross-reference for the principle of enduring to the end: Mark 13:13 — "endure to the end and be saved" — paired with [Matthew 10](/notes/matthew-10):22 as scriptural support for endurance as part of the gospel.

**Context:** Mark 13 is the "Olivet Discourse" in Mark, in which Jesus foretells the destruction of the temple, signs of the last days, and his Second Coming, urging his disciples to watch and endure.

## Where this appears

- Christ Defines His Gospel — Mark 13:13 cited (with Matthew 10:22) for the principle of enduring to the end and being saved.
