# Fareed Zakaria

Fareed Zakaria appears in [The Future of Work (Article)](/notes/the-future-of-work-article) on the nationalist backlash to globalization: he is cited as seeing a new political divide between openness to globalization and technology versus national sovereignty and border control, illustrated by Brexit voters skewing older, less-educated, poorer, and working-class.

**Context:** Fareed Zakaria is an Indian-American journalist and political commentator, host of CNN's *Fareed Zakaria GPS* and author of books including *The Post-American World*.

## Where this appears

- [The Future of Work (Article)](/notes/the-future-of-work-article) — cited for his framing of the globalization-vs-sovereignty divide behind nationalist backlash.
