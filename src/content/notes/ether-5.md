# Ether 5

Ether 5 is the closing chapter of Kyle's November 9–15, 2020. Ether 1–5: "Rend That Veil of Unbelief" Come Follow Me study, which covers the arc from [Ether 1](/notes/ether-1) through Ether 5 — [Jared](/notes/jared) and the [Brother of Jared](/notes/brother-of-jared) at the great tower, their crossing of the great sea in sealed barges "directed continually by the hand of the Lord," and the Brother of Jared's faith so exceeding that the veil was rent and he saw the finger and then the face of the Lord. In that study, Ether 5 itself is marked "No notes recorded for this chapter" — Kyle's recorded reflections cluster on the earlier chapters (the protection of [Ether 1](/notes/ether-1), the chastening and three barge problems of Ether 2, and the vision of Ether 3). Ether 5 closes the unit by directing how the sealed plates and interpreters are to be kept and shown.

**Context:** Ether 5 is a short chapter in the Book of Mormon in which Moroni records the Lord's instructions to the future translator about the sealed portion of the record and the three witnesses who would testify of its truth — a passage often read as foreshadowing the Three Witnesses of the Book of Mormon.
