# Max Scholten

A representative of [Heirloom](/notes/heirloom), the direct-air-capture [Carbon Removal](/notes/carbon-removal) startup, who appears in Kyle's Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 dossier. Scholten is listed alongside [Shashank Samala](/notes/shashank-samala) as Heirloom's participant at Watershed's 2021 Climate Week event, where Heirloom was paired with [Running Tide](/notes/running-tide) (founder [Marty Odlin](/notes/marty-odlin)) as one of "two cutting-edge carbon removal startups" whose founders spoke with the climate leads at [Stripe](/notes/stripe) and [Shopify ($SHOP)](/notes/shopify) — buyers who had "collectively kickstarted the market with $10m+ of purchasing commitments." The diligence note frames both companies not as "speculative non-profits" but as tech companies "with customers, investors, deliverables—and competitors."
