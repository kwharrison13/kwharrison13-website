# Boyd K. Packer

An Apostle of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints — a member, and later President, of the Quorum of the Twelve. In the corpus he appears as the author of "The Candle of the Lord", a talk to new mission presidents on how spiritual knowledge is received, why it cannot be conveyed in words alone, and how "a testimony is to be *found* in the *bearing* of it."

## Where this appears

- The Candle of the Lord — Boyd K. Packer — talk at a seminar for new mission presidents (25 June 1982; published *Ensign*, Jan. 1983) on the [Holy Ghost](/notes/holy-ghost), [Revelation](/notes/revelation), and [Testimony](/notes/testimony).

## Connections

- [Holy Ghost](/notes/holy-ghost) — the central subject of "The Candle of the Lord": how the gift is received and recognized.
- Still Small Voice — the Spirit whispers gently rather than shouting.
- [Revelation](/notes/revelation) — "pure intelligence flowing into you… sudden strokes of ideas."
- [Testimony](/notes/testimony) — found in the bearing of it; grows by increments, often "and they knew it not."
