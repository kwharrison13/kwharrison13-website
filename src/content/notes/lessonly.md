# Lessonly

Sales coaching, training, and enablement tool that recurs in Kyle's diligence notes as a category reference point. In the Cresta.ai - Diligence competitive landscape, Lessonly is listed under "coaching/enablement" — one of the adjacent vendors mapped around Cresta's real-time contact-center guidance product (alongside [Observe.ai](/notes/observe-ai), [Balto.ai](/notes/balto-ai), [PlayVox](/notes/playvox), and [Gong](/notes/gong)/[Chorus](/notes/chorus)). It also appears in the CaptivateIQ Diligence — 2021 notes, where a customer reasoning about product roadmap pointed to "companies like Lessonly" as the gamification-and-coaching direction a sales-tooling vendor might naturally expand into to justify higher pricing.

**Context:** Lessonly was an Indianapolis-based training and enablement software company founded in 2012, focused on onboarding and coaching for sales and customer-service teams; it was acquired by Seismic in 2021.
