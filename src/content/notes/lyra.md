# Lyra

**One-line definition:** *Employer mental-health benefit; therapist-focused (barely any medication) and [Spring Health](/notes/spring-health)'s main competitor.*

## How sources describe it

- Network of 1099 providers; very focused on therapists and does little medication ("can't even get a basic anti-depressant"); along with [Spring Health](/notes/spring-health) has run away with the category by measuring quality of care; always in the big employer RFPs alongside Spring and Ginger. (September 22nd, 2021 — Adam Chekroud (Spring Health))

## Where it shows up

- September 22nd, 2021 — Adam Chekroud (Spring Health) — discussed as Spring Health's main competitor.
