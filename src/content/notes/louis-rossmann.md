# Louis Rossmann

**One-line:** *Repair-advocate and YouTuber whose video on social anxiety frames caring-what-others-think as a self-imposed prison.*

## What we know

- In a [video on social anxiety](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=W9AHCymAbyg): "Most people are incapable of having an identity because most people are stuck within the prison of caring."
- Argues the pattern starts in childhood (looking to others to know what to like), and the fix is to "stop caring what other people think," care only what you think of yourself, while still being someone you can live with.
- "Stop allowing other people to take their problems and make them your problems. Stop allowing other people to park their baggage in your driveway."
- Kyle maps this directly onto "Layer 1" dependencies — the phantom dependency that must be conquered first.
