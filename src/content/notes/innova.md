# Innova

**One-line definition:** *A first/last-mile electric mobility-as-a-service company (Innova EV) whose core thesis is heavy IoT data collection from instrumented vehicles.*

## How sources describe it

- Runs electric low/medium on-highway vehicles instrumented with an IoT data-analytics platform (sensors, cameras, air-quality, ground radar), gathering data every ~10ms; serves commuter and campus carshare/rideshare use cases and is testing autonomous driving at Ohio State. (May 16th, 2018 — Roman (Innova))
- Monetizes across rides (~$3/ride, with intent to go free), on-vehicle advertising, an in-vehicle media/coupon platform, and a data-analytics platform sold to cities, utilities (ComEd), transit (Metra), and employers; a separate entity ("Inverom") buys cars and leases them to the service over 60 months.
- ~40 cars deployed (targeting 150 by year-end), sub-$1M ARR (targeting ~$2M in Q1 '19), raising $5–7M at a ~$18M pre-money discussed late the prior year.
- Introduced to Kyle via [David Fidalgo](/notes/david-fidalgo) of [Y-Mobility](/notes/y-mobility).
