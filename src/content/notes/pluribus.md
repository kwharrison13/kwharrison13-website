# Pluribus

Kyle flags *Pluribus* in his notes on [Story of Your Life](/books/story-of-your-life) as a screen-media echo of the novella's central paradox: the heptapods' "free-but-bound" condition, in which beings act to enact a chronology they already know is coming. The connection notes that this premise — choosing into a fixed future — mirrors the show's setup. The reference is a cross-medium pointer rather than a developed concept.

**Context:** *Pluribus* is a 2025 Apple TV+ science-fiction drama created by Vince Gilligan (of *Breaking Bad* and *Better Call Saul*), starring Rhea Seehorn. Its premise involves a world-altering event that constrains characters' choices against a known outcome.
