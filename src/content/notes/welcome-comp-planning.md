# Welcome (Comp Planning)

An early-stage compensation-planning startup (heywelcome.com) that surfaces in the Pave Diligence — 2021 dossier as one of [Pave](/notes/pave)'s competitors. In [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures)' 2021 diligence on Pave, Welcome is grouped with [Assemble](/notes/assemble) as an earlier-stage startup competitor (alongside legacy players like [PayScale](/notes/payscale) and [Shareworks](/notes/shareworks)). The memo's framing has Pave already pulling ahead — Welcome is named among the "other competitors… already in the dust" — with the partnership concluding that the "only viable threat" to Pave was [Carta](/notes/carta), not the smaller comp-planning startups.

**Context:** Welcome (heywelcome.com) was a compensation-planning / total-rewards software startup; the name is generic enough that this page carries a "(Company)"-style disambiguation suffix in Kyle's wiki.

## Where this appears
