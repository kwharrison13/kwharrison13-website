# Apoorv Sharma

Head of Marketing & Growth at [Apple ($AAPL)](/notes/apple) (India), leading product marketing, growth, and global customer support. He appears in Cresta.ai - Diligence as an **expert-call customer reference** (November 2nd, 2021), having used [Cresta.ai](/notes/cresta-ai) for roughly 2–3 years across Apple's agent-driven customer experience.

His account is a substantive customer-side view of Cresta's product: Apple's support is heavily agent-conversation driven (B2C and B2B) and runs through many BPO vendors worldwide, so Apple maintains a centrally-driven relationship with Cresta at an agreed rate while the third-party vendors actually pay. Sharma previously tried [Observe.ai](/notes/observe-ai) for six months before adopting Cresta, which now covers roughly **35% of agents** at **under $1M of spend**. He reported direct revenue impact, central importance of coaching/agent maturity, and Cresta's value in consolidating *quality* (not just quantity) metrics — and rated switching costs "a 7 out of 10 at least," framing a full switch as a year-long effort. Earlier in his career he was at Amazon (which had its own in-house tool).
