# Investors to Know

A working tag from Kyle's Contrary Capital era (originally the Roam hashtag #[investors-to-know](/notes/investors-to-know)) that he used to capture investor names worth knowing — typically harvested from portfolio-company updates and cap tables. The pattern: when a founder's update lists the angels, advisors, or funds backing them, Kyle would tag that roster `#investors-to-know` as a sourcing / relationship-mapping aid.

It appears twice in the captured notes. In the GiveMomentum March update, [Ari Kagan](/notes/ari-kagan)'s email surfaced a long list of investor contacts (Clint Korver, Dan Heath, Elizabeth Yin, Eric Ries, Mark Cuban, Jaan Tallinn, and many others) that Kyle flagged with the tag. In the January 31st, 2022 daily log, reading [Knoetic](/notes/knoetic)'s update from [Joseph Quan](/notes/joseph-quan) produced another `#investors-to-know` roster — the ~100 super-angels (Bradley Horowitz, Gokul Rajaram, Mar Hershenson, Pejman Nozad, and others) who backed Knoetic's Series A.
