# Matthew Kobach

Co-presenter, with [David Perell](/notes/david-perell), of the workshop captured in [How to Crush It on Twitter — David Perell and Matthew Kobach Workshop](/notes/how-to-crush-it-on-twitter-david-perell-and-matthew-kobach-workshop). The long read cites him on becoming "a lighthouse for like-minded people" — he can reach anyone in finance and get a response within hours by replying to big accounts only when he genuinely adds to the conversation.

**Context:** Matthew Kobach is a social-media and brand marketer known for his work running digital/social for the New York Stock Exchange and later in fintech, and for his widely-followed writing on building an audience on Twitter/X.
