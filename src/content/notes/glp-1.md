# GLP-1

**One-line definition:** *The class of weight-loss / diabetes drugs (Wegovy, Ozempic, compounded semaglutide) whose insatiable US demand is the actual fuel behind the [MEDVI](/notes/medvi) story.*

## How sources describe it

- A precondition independent of AI: GLP-1 demand had to be "absolutely insatiable" — and it is. People want it now and don't want to sit in a doctor's office. (source: The $1B Rorschach Test - Research)
- "Offering GLP-1s via [Telehealth](/notes/telehealth) is such a gangbusters business you literally can't NOT make money doing it." (source: The $1B Rorschach Test - Research)
- The FDA has been cracking down on grey-market / Chinese GLP-1s; legitimate players like [Hims & Hers](/notes/hims-and-hers) bought compounding pharmacies + a peptide facility ahead of it, raising the sustainability question for pure marketing layers. (source: The $1B Rorschach Test - Research)
