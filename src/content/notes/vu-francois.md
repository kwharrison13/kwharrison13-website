# Vu Francois

Vu Francois is the Chief Product Officer (CPO) of [Soko](/notes/soko), the independent-contractor compliance engine Kyle reviewed in the August 18th, 2021 — Soko Data Room notes. Per the data-room team bio, he is a Cornell Tech MBA grad, a product manager and multi-disciplinary designer, and previously founded Craterland and OKO. He appears alongside co-founders [Farhaj Mayan](/notes/farhaj-mayan) (CEO), [Eve Epstein](/notes/eve-epstein) (CLO), and [Bradley Faught](/notes/bradley-faught) (CTO) — note that he, Mayan, and Faught share the OKO founding background.
