# TripActions

**One-line:** *A B2B SaaS travel and expense-management platform, and a [Salt Security](/notes/salt-security) customer surfaced through API-security diligence.*

## What we know

- B2B SaaS travel and expense-management platform; [Prabhath Karanth](/notes/prabhath-karanth) was its Director of Security and a Salt reference-call subject. (Salt Security Diligence — 2021)
- Leans heavily on its API ecosystem, connecting to hundreds of partners — including its fintech product's links to [Stripe](/notes/stripe), Plaid, and Modern Treasury — so it has hundreds of APIs. ([Prabhath Karanth](/notes/prabhath-karanth), Salt Security Diligence — 2021)
- Evaluated [Signal Sciences](/notes/signal-sciences), [Noname Security](/notes/noname-security), and [Traceable.ai](/notes/traceable-ai) and chose [Salt Security](/notes/salt-security) as a "forward-thinking vendor" that fit their cutting-edge, fast-moving culture (vs. slower-innovating Cisco/[Palo Alto Networks ($PANW)](/notes/palo-alto-networks)). (Salt Security Diligence — 2021)
- Runs Salt agent-less via API-based connectivity (up in ~a week) feeding a centralized Security Operations Center; uses AWS cloud-native API gateways and a popular WAF underneath. ([Prabhath Karanth](/notes/prabhath-karanth), Salt Security Diligence — 2021)
- [Prabhath Karanth](/notes/prabhath-karanth) previously spent 9 years at Adobe building its security efforts through its cloud transition. (Salt Security Diligence — 2021)
