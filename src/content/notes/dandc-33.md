# D&C 33

Section 33 of the Doctrine and Covenants recurs in Kyle's gospel study as a **harvest-and-restoration** text. In Christian Reformation, Zion, and Seekers he cites D&C 33:4–5 alongside [D&C 86](/notes/dandc-86):3, [D&C 5](/notes/dandc-5):14, and [D&C 109](/notes/dandc-109):73 as scriptures about restoring Christianity "to a healthy condition" and restoring truth. In Miscellaneous Scriptures he pulls D&C 33:7–10 as the "fields are white, ready to harvest" passage — the call to labor before the harvest.

He also reads it as a Book-of-Mormon witness text: in his Mormon 7–9 study he pairs D&C 33:16 with [D&C 42](/notes/dandc-42):12–13, noting that the Book of Mormon and the holy scriptures are "given of me for your instruction" and contain "the fulness of the gospel" — part of his broader argument that the Book of Mormon is itself a pearl of great price.

## Where this appears

- Christian Reformation, Zion, and Seekers — D&C 33:4–5 grouped with other restoration scriptures about restoring Christianity and truth
- Miscellaneous Scriptures — D&C 33:7–10 as the "fields are white, ready to harvest" passage
- November 2–8, 2020. Mormon 7–9: "I Speak unto You As If Ye Were Present" — D&C 33:16 cited with [D&C 42](/notes/dandc-42) as scriptures given "for your instruction" containing the fulness of the gospel
