# Comcast

In Kyle's wiki, Comcast appears as a reference customer of [Uptycs](/notes/uptycs), the security-analytics platform led by [Ganesh Pai](/notes/ganesh-pai). Per the September 2021 [Uptycs](/notes/uptycs) sourcing call, Comcast evaluated Uptycs side-by-side against [CrowdStrike ($CRWD)](/notes/crowdstrike) across roughly 65,000 machines, with the account growing from about $1M to $3.5M. The detail is used as evidence of Uptycs winning head-to-head against CrowdStrike inside large enterprise accounts.

**Context:** Comcast is a major US telecommunications and media conglomerate, parent of Xfinity (cable/broadband) and NBCUniversal.
