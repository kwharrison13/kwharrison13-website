# Nick Maggiulli

Surfaces in the Historical Futurism - Research notes as the writer behind *Dollars and Data*, cited for the argument that **failed predictions don't matter** — a thread Kyle gathers under the essay's claim that being wrong about the future is normal and not disqualifying. In Kyle's notes, Maggiulli is paired with the "Four Pillar Freedom" framing and the Michael Jordan "I've failed over and over" quote, sitting alongside other forecasting-humility sources like *Back to the Future 2* and [Sriram Krishnan](/notes/sriram-krishnan) on timing.

**Context:** Nick Maggiulli is an American writer and data scientist who authors the *Of Dollars and Data* blog and wrote *Just Keep Buying*; he is COO at Ritholtz Wealth Management.

## Where this appears

- Historical Futurism - Research — cited (via *Dollars and Data*) for the point that failed predictions don't matter, supporting the essay's case on forecasting humility.
- September 23rd, 2024 — Morgan Housel (On Jack Raines) — [Morgan Housel](/notes/morgan-housel) names Maggiulli as the only writer comparable to [Jack Raines](/notes/jack-raines), and one of just two writers in the last decade whose first post made him think "this guy has it."
