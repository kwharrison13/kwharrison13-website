# AES Corporation

**One-line:** *Fortune 500 global electric and gas provider (founded 1980s), a leading developer of renewable power plants set up as a non-recourse-financed holding company — where [Matt Harsha-Strong](/notes/matt-harsha-strong) led M&A for ~11 years.*

## What we know

- Fortune 500 global electric/gas provider founded in the 1980s; a leading developer of renewable (solar, wind, hydro) power plants; owns utilities across the US, LATAM, and Europe and is active in 13+ markets (source: June 20th, 2023 — Matt Harsha-Strong (Uplight)).
- Structured as a holding company: DC-based parent with subsidiaries financed on a non-recourse basis at various levels (source: June 20th, 2023 — Matt Harsha-Strong (Uplight)).
- Built its renewables position by acquiring development platforms — Mainstreet Power (rooftop solar, 2015), S Power (wind, 2017) — plus project portfolios, including a series of wind farms from [Carlyle](/notes/carlyle) in 2021 (source: June 20th, 2023 — Matt Harsha-Strong (Uplight)).
- Customer-driven: signed a contract with Google to supply 24/7 green power to Virginia data centers, running RFPs to third-party developers to fulfill it (source: June 20th, 2023 — Matt Harsha-Strong (Uplight)).
