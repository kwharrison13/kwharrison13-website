# Cartooli

Cartooli is the company run by Rob, a [WhaleSync](/notes/whalesync) customer Kyle spoke with on a customer-reference call on August 18th, 2021. Rob describes himself as having no technical background, having leveled up from Excel into [Airtable](/notes/airtable) to build data products quickly — basic job boards, news feeds, and similar information products built on [Bubble](/notes/bubble) and [Webflow](/notes/webflow). His core frustration was that collecting/leveraging data in Airtable and pushing it into an attractive front end were always two disjointed tasks, forcing him through [Zapier](/notes/zapier) hoops (this was before Airtable shipped automations).

In the reference call, Rob is positioned as a representative no-code builder for whom WhaleSync solves the Airtable→Webflow sync problem: keeping Airtable's data flexibility while displaying it through a more attractive Webflow front end, without manual monitoring. He praised WhaleSync's team (Curtis) for proactively catching sync errors and saw a future where WhaleSync could become a dashboard-driven alternative to [Zapier](/notes/zapier) for running a business as a series of data connections.
