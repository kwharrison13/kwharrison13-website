# Commitment

The product a brand sells when it frames its offering as a moral discipline rather than a service — central to [Equinox Is More Than A Gym. It's A Church](/notes/equinox-is-more-than-a-gym-it-s-a-church), which argues Equinox "sells commitment, not equipment." Its brand voice ("Commitment is discipline. Commitment is guts."; "Morning people aren't born. They're made.") frames fitness as a discipline, and the average member visits four times per week — more than double the average American gym member.
