# Doug McCullough

**One-line:** *CIO of the [City of Dublin](/notes/city-of-dublin), Ohio, an early-adopting small city in the smart-city / autonomous-mobility space.*

## What we know

- Background in IT and systems; as CIO has worked across AI, autonomous driving, IoT, and AR. Dublin, OH (~45K people) is the second city he's worked for, after agencies and Richmond.
- Built the "Dublin Digital Experience Lab" — a cross-functional group (~15 people, monthly) that vets immature ideas and has vendors pitch it; sponsored by the CIO but not owned solely by IT.
- Looks for procurement "loopholes" to buy and test early-stage technology ($10–15K experiments); much more likely to engage a company that comes via introduction than a cold call.
