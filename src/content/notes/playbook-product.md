# Playbook: Product

One of the per-function entries in Kyle's [Playbooks](/notes/playbooks) collection — a running attempt to assemble operating playbooks by discipline. The product playbook is, so far, a curated reading queue toward [Product Management](/notes/product-management) craft rather than a synthesized framework. The links Kyle saved in his June 29th, 2021 Roam "Quick Capture" log cluster around a recognizable PM canon: Ken Norton's Bring the Donuts essays (how to work with software engineers, what a product manager does, leading cross-functional teams — Kyle also tags this set [Best-in-Class Tech Stacks](/notes/best-in-class-tech-stacks)), Paul Graham's essays (maker's schedule, "do things that don't scale," how to get startup ideas), Joel Spolsky on software inventory, and two foundational books — Christensen's *The Innovator's Solution* and Ben Horowitz's *The Hard Thing About Hard Things*. It sits alongside [Playbook: Growth](/notes/playbook-growth) and [Playbook: Sales](/notes/playbook-sales) under [Playbooks](/notes/playbooks).
