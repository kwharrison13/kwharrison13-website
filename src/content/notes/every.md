# Every

The writing platform / publication associated with [Nathan Baschez](/notes/nathan-baschez), the venue for his strategy writing under the [Divinations](/notes/divinations) column. In Kyle's corpus it surfaces as the home of Baschez's essay *"What good is strategy right now?"* (June 2020), which framed the tension between strategic business value-creation and basic human dignity that Kyle leans on in Unions, Unions Everywhere - Research (source: [Nathan Baschez](/notes/nathan-baschez)). It is also where Baschez published a [Divinations](/notes/divinations) essay on Apple building the "OS for health" (the [healthOS](/notes/healthos) thread).

**Context:** Every is a bundle of business-and-technology newsletters co-founded by Nathan Baschez and Dan Shipper; Baschez's column there is *Divinations*, focused on strategy and product. (General background — confirm against a primary source before relying on it.)
