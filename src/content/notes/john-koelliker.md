# John Koelliker

Founder/operator at [Leland](/notes/leland) and the author of its investor updates to [Contrary](/notes/contrary), which Kyle followed through 2022. In the May 2022 update (June 6th, 2022 — Leland May Update (John Koelliker)), Koelliker noted "a lot of inbound from potential Series A investors and strategic partners (e.g. Indeed)" while staying focused on building. The July update (August 29th, 2022) framed [Leland](/notes/leland)'s search for [Product-Market Fit](/notes/product-market-fit), and the September update (November 7th, 2022 — Cleared Email) reported the marketplace crossing a $1M annualized GMV run rate, ~3.25x YoY GMV growth, and 18 active categories — with Koelliker noting constant Series A inbound but no plan to raise until fall 2023, taking only occasional meetings with top marketplace investors to find the right partner.
