# Founder-Led Firms

The organizational theme that founder-led firms struggle to genuinely delegate a new discipline: when buy-in at the top is only nominal, the lack of real commitment corrodes everything beneath it. Surfaced in the Coatue quant-fund post-mortem as a structural reason the new strategy never took hold.
