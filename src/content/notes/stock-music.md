# Stock Music

A topic Kyle captured in his June 29th, 2021 Roam "Quick Capture" reading log, anchored on a single saved link to Amper Music — an AI-driven music composition tool. The capture sits in the same reading session as his other "to explore" items and carries no commentary, so it reads as an interest flag in the space of stock/royalty-free and AI-generated music rather than a worked-out note.

**Context:** "Stock music" (also production or royalty-free music) is pre-recorded music licensed for use in video, ads, and other media. Amper Music was an AI music-generation startup (founded 2014) that let users compose custom tracks; it was acquired by Shutterstock in 2020.
