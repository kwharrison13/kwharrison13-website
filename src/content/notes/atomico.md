# Atomico

European venture firm referenced in Kyle's The Talent Vortex - Research as the prior employer of [Mark Temple](/notes/mark-temple), who contributed framing to that research — specifically on [Keith Rabois](/notes/keith-rabois)'s methods for sizing up talent and tapping the Thiel/PayPal network (identifying and rewarding "barrels, not ammunition"). Atomico itself is mentioned only as Temple's background, not analyzed in the note.

**Context:** Atomico is a London-based venture capital firm founded in 2006 by Niklas Zennström (a Skype co-founder), focused on European technology companies across stages.
