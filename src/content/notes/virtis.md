# Virtis

An [API Security](/notes/api-security) vendor evaluated alongside [Salt Security](/notes/salt-security) and [Noname Security](/notes/noname-security) in the 2021 Salt Security diligence. Per [Prabhakar Kasu](/notes/prabhakar-kasu) (Head of Cyber at [AON](/notes/aon)), Virtis is "based in New Zealand or something" and an interesting player in the space: for legacy APIs it ships out-of-the-box remediation code snippets that block specific known vulnerabilities, letting a team drop in a temporary protective layer ("deflective flares" in vulnerable spots) when full remediation would be cost-prohibitive. It also surfaces in the diligence's competitor list (with Noname, Traceable.ai, and 42Crunch) as one of the alternatives a buyer would weigh against Salt.

**Context:** Virtis is a small API-security vendor; Kasu's account places it as a remediation-snippet-oriented player rather than a discovery-first platform like Salt or Noname. (Real-world details are not corroborated beyond the diligence notes.)
