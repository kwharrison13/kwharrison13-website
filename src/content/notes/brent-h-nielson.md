# Brent H. Nielson

A member of the Presidency of the Seventy in The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, captured in Kyle's notes on General Conference- October 2021. His talk — tagged #[But If Not...](/notes/but-if-not) — recounts praying through a loved one's terminal illness: "First we prayed for a miracle. Then we prayed that he wouldn't feel pain. Finally we prayed that he would pass quickly." Kyle ties the message to scriptural healing accounts (Mark 2:3-5, where friends lower a paralytic through a roof, and Jesus heals "when [he] saw their faith") and the "balm in Gilead" question of Jeremiah 8:22. The talk prompts Kyle's own reflection on faith as a skill rather than a binary, and whether unanswered prayers were in fact answered differently than asked.

**Context:** Brent H. Nielson is a General Authority Seventy of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, sustained to the Presidency of the Seventy.
