# 2 Nephi 30

A chapter of the Book of Mormon cited in Kyle's study of the Abrahamic Covenant. There, 2 Nephi 30:2 is paired with [Jacob 5](/notes/jacob-5):9–10 (the allegory of the olive tree) to make the point that, regardless of lineage, the covenants of God come only to those who repent — that covenant belonging is conditioned on repentance rather than birthright.

**Context:** 2 Nephi 30 is a prophetic chapter in which Nephi describes the latter-day restoration and gathering of Israel, declaring that the blessings of the covenant are extended to all who repent and come unto Christ, Jew and Gentile alike.
