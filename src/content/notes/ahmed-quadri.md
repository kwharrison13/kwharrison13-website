# Ahmed Quadri

Customer-success operator interviewed as an expert source in Kyle's Planhat - Diligence work. He is described there as Chief Customer Officer at Heap (product analytics), formerly SVP of Success at Reltio, with experience spanning Salesforce, Informatica (via Siperian), IBM, PwC, and Accenture — roughly 8–9 years in customer success. He recounts the early days when "CS started on spreadsheets," and credits [Gainsight](/notes/gainsight) with flourishing by building on top of Salesforce and, through early entry, helping *define* the customer-success category.

On Planhat specifically, Quadri is one of the diligence's "bear" data points on US brand awareness — he "had not heard of them," supporting the dossier's conclusion that Planhat has strong EMEA traction but limited US mindshare. On pricing he anchors the willingness-to-pay range, noting CS contracts running sub-$100K (versus $200–300K+ for CRM).
