# Mark Dagostino

Co-author of [The Magnolia Story](/books/the-magnolia-story), the memoir by [Chip Gaines](/notes/chip-gaines) and [Joanna Gaines](/notes/joanna-gaines). Kyle's notes credit him as the writer who "shaped the memoir" — the collaborator who turned the Gaineses' story into the book's narrative form.

**Context:** Mark Dagostino is an American author and former People magazine senior writer who has co-written several celebrity memoirs and ghostwritten/collaborated narratives, including The Magnolia Story (2016) with Chip and Joanna Gaines.
