# Matt Bogrand

Matt Bogrand surfaces twice in Kyle's notes, both as a contact rather than a subject. In the Pave Diligence — 2021 dossier he is listed as a board-member back-channel target at [YC Continuity](/notes/yc-continuity) — a planned outreach (alongside Anu Hariharan) to get a read on Pave ahead of Index's pre-emptive Series C attempt. In The Talent Vortex - Research he appears as one of the [Dropbox](/notes/dropbox) operators Kyle surveyed for the talent-vortex essay, in the cohort of people who "grew up" inside companies widely cited as talent magnets.
