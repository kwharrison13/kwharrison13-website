# Matt Clancy

**One-line:** *Innovation economist; author of "Free Knowledge and Innovation," the source for Kyle's claim that access to knowledge drives innovation.*

## What we know

- Wrote [Free Knowledge and Innovation](https://mattsclancy.substack.com/p/free-knowledge-and-innovation): "If you want to encourage more innovation, give people better access to knowledge."
- Synthesizes evidence (Berkes & Nencka on libraries; Thompson & Hanley on [Wikipedia](/notes/wikipedia)) that new access to knowledge — public libraries, patent depositories, Wikipedia — has measurable effects on patenting and citations.
