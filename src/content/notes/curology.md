# Curology

Curology surfaces in Kyle's notes only as a customer of [Lightyear](/notes/lightyear), the telecom procurement startup run by [Dennis Thankachan](/notes/dennis-thankachan). In Lightyear's March 2022 portfolio update, Thankachan flagged that Curology — described as one of Lightyear's earlier customers — was undergoing a round of layoffs that cut Lightyear's biggest internal champion, Curology's then–Head of IT. The mention is purely a customer-health data point about Lightyear, not a standalone diligence note on Curology itself.

**Context:** Curology is a direct-to-consumer skincare company offering custom-formulated prescription treatments for acne and aging, founded in 2014 and based in San Francisco.
