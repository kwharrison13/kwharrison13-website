# Jim Cyb

Former CRO of Duo and [Zendesk](/notes/zendesk), surfaced in the Wiz.io pre-brief as the referral source behind Wiz's likely SVP Sales hire, Colin Jones. Cyb recruited Jones seven years earlier as Duo's first enterprise AE; Jones went on to book Duo's first $1M in enterprise business and grew to run a ~$600M book. The note describes Jones as "a rare combination of sales person + analytical from the Urlocker/Cyb school of sales ops + high EQ/culture creator" — positioning Cyb (alongside Zach Urlocker) as the lineage of analytically rigorous, ops-driven enterprise sales leadership the diligence values.
