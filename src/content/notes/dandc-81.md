# D&C 81

A section of the Doctrine and Covenants that Kyle cites in his Charity & Love topical study. The page invokes D&C 81:5 — the charge to "lift up the hands which hang down" and "strengthen the feeble knees" — to argue that meeting every individual's needs is itself the love of Christ, and that charity not only lifts the discouraged but moves the work forward. It pairs this with [Alma 15](/notes/alma-15):18, where Alma pauses his ministry to nurse Amulek in his own house.

**Context:** Doctrine and Covenants Section 81 is an 1832 revelation given through Joseph Smith at Hiram, Ohio, appointing a counselor in the Presidency of the High Priesthood. Verse 5 admonishes: "Wherefore, be faithful; stand in the office which I have appointed unto you; succor the weak, lift up the hands which hang down, and strengthen the feeble knees."
