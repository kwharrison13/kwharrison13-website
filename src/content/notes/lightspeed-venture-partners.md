# Lightspeed Venture Partners

Lightspeed Venture Partners is a venture firm that recurs across Kyle's [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures)-era sourcing notes and his venture-industry essay research as a peer multistage firm. [Natalie Luu](/notes/natalie-luu) is the firm's most-cited contact — she worked the [Persona](/notes/persona) deal at Lightspeed (September 22nd, 2021 — Natalie Luu (Lightspeed Venture Partners)) — and several other Lightspeed partners appear in Kyle's outreach and research: Anoushka Vaswani, Nnamdi Iregbulem, and [Sebastian Duesterhoeft](/notes/sebastian-duesterhoeft) (The Productization of Venture Capital - Research, January 24th, 2024 — Outreach for SF Trip).

The richest single record is the August 2022 call with Will Kohler and Bejul Somaia, framing Lightspeed as an established firm built to "set everyone up to succeed" and looking for thought-partnership over transactional deal-doing — with marquee deals including Airtable, Snyk, and Kustomer and a pattern-recognition edge in data infrastructure (Databricks, Snowflake, Confluent). Lightspeed partners also appear as voices in Kyle's venture-fund essay research on firm performance and judgment.

**Context:** Lightspeed Venture Partners is a global multistage venture capital firm (founded 1999, Menlo Park) backing enterprise, consumer, and infrastructure companies; notable investments include Snap, Affirm, Snowflake, and Nutanix.
