# Amy Poehler

In Kyle's notes, Amy Poehler appears only as the actress who plays [Leslie Knope](/notes/leslie-knope), the protagonist of NBC's [Parks and Recreation](/notes/parks-and-recreation). The Knope character — not Poehler herself — does the work in the corpus, serving as a "cultural barometer" for generational attitudes toward earnest, relentless civic overwork (the "Gen Z hates Leslie Knope" tidbit from [Rex Woodbury](/notes/rex-woodbury)).

**Context:** Amy Poehler is an American comedian, actress, writer, and producer, best known for her years on *Saturday Night Live* and for creating and starring as Leslie Knope in *Parks and Recreation* (2009–2015).
