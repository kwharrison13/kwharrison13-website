# Ryan Petersen

Ryan Petersen is the founder and CEO of [Flexport](/notes/flexport), and in Kyle's corpus he is [Katherine Boyle](/notes/katherine-boyle)'s central example of a founder "doing important civic work" in [Building American Dynamism](/notes/building-american-dynamism). The anchoring anecdote: when politicians panicked at the supply-chain crisis at the Port of Long Beach in November 2021, the person who got on a boat to figure out immediate solutions was not an elected official but Petersen — pushing forward fixes that government lacked the tools and voices to understand. Boyle uses him to argue that startups are "usurping the responsibilities of governments at breathtaking pace" and that [American Dynamism](/notes/american-dynamism) is being driven by technologists solving problems of national importance rather than by policy in Washington.

**Context:** Ryan Petersen founded Flexport, a digital freight-forwarding and logistics-technology company, in 2013. He drew wide attention in late 2021 for a viral Twitter thread proposing concrete operational fixes for the Long Beach port congestion during the COVID-era supply-chain crisis.
