# Childcare

Childcare appears in the wiki as a reading-list thread Kyle saved in his June 29th, 2021 Roam "Quick Capture" log, sitting alongside [Parenting](/notes/parenting) material. The capture pairs a NYT Upshot piece ("The Relentlessness of Modern Parenting") and an Axios Future newsletter item with three childcare-business links — The Mom Project, MyVillage, and KinderCare — suggesting Kyle was scanning the topic as both a cultural-strain question (the cost and intensity of modern parenting) and a market/operator question (childcare-provider and parent-employment platforms).
