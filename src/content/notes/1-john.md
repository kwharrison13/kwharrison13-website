# 1 John

The first epistle of John. In Kyle's 8 Points of the Gospel study it appears in the section on [consecration](/notes/consecration), referenced (with 2 Corinthians 8:14–15) under "Communism vs. consecration" and a pointer to the anti-Christ section of his Topical Journal — drawing on John's epistle to distinguish true Christian consecration from communism.

**Context:** 1 John is a New Testament epistle traditionally attributed to the Apostle John, written to assure believers of their salvation and to test true fellowship by love, obedience, and right belief about Jesus Christ. It includes the well-known declarations that "God is love" and that brotherly love is the mark of those who walk in the light.

## Where this appears
