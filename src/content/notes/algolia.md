# Algolia

Surfaces once in Pave Diligence — 2021, where it is named — alongside [Alation](/notes/alation), Benchling, GitLab, Reddit, and Viz.ai — as a customer of [Compaas](/notes/compaas), one of the compensation-planning startups mapped as a direct competitor to [Pave](/notes/pave). Algolia itself is not a subject of the diligence; it appears only as a logo in Compaas's customer roster during the competitive-landscape sweep.

**Context:** Algolia is a hosted search-and-discovery API company (founded 2012) that lets developers add fast, relevant search to websites and apps — a frequently-cited example of the API-first developer-tools wave.
