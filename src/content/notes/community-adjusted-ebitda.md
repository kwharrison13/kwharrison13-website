# Community-Adjusted EBITDA

**One-line definition:** *A proxy/vanity metric that redirects investor attention away from traditional valuation — WeWork's infamous example.*

## How it shows up

- [Bill Gurley](/notes/bill-gurley)'s 1999 essay frames the lineage: [Craig McCaw](/notes/craig-mccaw)'s "homes passed" and "POPs" were the 1990s ancestors. "Don't look at this, look at that" — changing the investor's consideration process.
