# Neil L. Anderson

A recurring conference speaker in Kyle's gospel-study notes. In General Conference — October 2021, Kyle recorded Anderson teaching that "the doctrine of Christ is eternal and everlasting" while "other steps are revealed at different times," and quoting [Russell M. Nelson](/notes/russell-m-nelson) that "the restoration is a process, not an event" — Kyle flagged several of these lines for his #[Find the Quote](/notes/find-the-quote) list. In General Conference — April 2020, Kyle noted Anderson's theme that God "personally and powerfully reaches out to us," and that "spiritually defining memories from our book of life are like luminous stones that help brighten the road ahead" through difficulty, doubt, or discouragement.

**Context:** Neil L. Andersen is a member of the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (Kyle's notes spell the surname "Anderson").

## Where this appears

- General Conference — October 2021 — talk on revealed doctrine as ongoing process, quoting Russell M. Nelson
- General Conference — April 2020 — talk on personal revelation and "spiritually defining memories" during hardship
