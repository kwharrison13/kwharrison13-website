# Shay Levi

Shay Levi is co-founder and CTO of [Noname Security](/notes/noname-security), the API-security company. In a November 2021 [Index Ventures sourcing call](/notes/index-sourcing-calls), Levi walked the team through Noname's product — discovery, anomaly detection, misconfiguration, prevention/remediation, and the active-testing (Secure SDLC) module. He framed the company's defensibility as accumulated integration know-how: building a reliable integration for each traffic/configuration source is hard, but once done Noname covers ~98% of customers and that depth earns customer trust, making it non-trivial for a new entrant to replicate.

Per his own account on the call, Levi served in Unit 8200 (alongside co-founder [Oz Golan](/notes/oz-golan)), then worked across several companies including IronSource, did stints at Facebook on mobile web and Facebook Live, and started a profitable company his wife now runs before founding Noname.

**Context:** Noname Security was an API-security startup founded in 2020 by Oz Golan and Shay Levi, both Israeli Unit 8200 veterans. It was acquired by Akamai in 2024.
