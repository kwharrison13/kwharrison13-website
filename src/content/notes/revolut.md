# Revolut

In the 2021 Index Ventures diligence on [Watershed Climate](/notes/watershed-climate), Revolut appears as one of Watershed's carbon-accounting customers — named in both the traction summary (the ~50-customer roster that took ARR from $700K to $5M that year, alongside Stripe, Shopify, Doordash, Sweetgreen, Square, Figma, and Airbnb) and the broader customer list (Airbnb, Figma, Everlane, Stripe, Imperfect Foods, Vimeo, Expensify, Wise, Doordash, Klarna, MongoDB, Revolut, Sweetgreen, Shopify, Square). Its presence is cited as evidence of Watershed's pull with forward-thinking tech companies, and — as a UK-headquartered fintech — it fits the dossier's note that UK firms with 250+ employees or ~$50M revenue would soon be required to disclose carbon emissions, a regulatory tailwind for carbon-reporting demand.

**Context:** Revolut is a London-based financial technology company (founded 2015) offering banking, currency exchange, payments, and investing through a mobile app; it is one of Europe's largest fintechs by valuation and user base.
