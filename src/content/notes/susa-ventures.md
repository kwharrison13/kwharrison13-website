# Susa Ventures

Susa Ventures appears in the June 6th, 2022 call with [Katherine Boyle](/notes/katherine-boyle) at [a16z](/notes/a16z) as one of the earlier seed-stage backers of [Anduril](/notes/anduril). Boyle recounted that she met founder Alex (Karp/Luckey) at the seed round but that Susa Ventures and [Uncork Capital](/notes/uncork-capital) had already come in — so she "accosted him to get in," and it took her a year to win a spot in the Series A. The firm is named only as prior context for how competitive and early the Anduril round was; it is a touchpoint in Boyle's broader story about boutique firms and differentiated missions ([American Dynamism](/notes/american-dynamism)).

**Context:** Susa Ventures is a San Francisco-based seed-stage venture capital firm (founded 2013) known for early bets including Robinhood, Flexport, and Andela.
