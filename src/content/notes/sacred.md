# Sacred

A recurring theme in Kyle's gospel study, surfaced in his notes on September 7–13, 2020. 3 Nephi 1–7: "Lift Up Your Head and Be of Good Cheer" under the idea that **Satan goes after your most sacred experiences**. Reading [3 Nephi 1](/notes/3-nephi-1):22 — where lies are "sent forth among the people, by Satan, to harden their hearts" against the very signs they had seen — Kyle notes that "Satan will go after your most sacred experiences if you let him." He pairs this with a [Joseph Smith](/notes/joseph-smith) teaching on why the Lord withholds His secrets from those who won't keep them: "The reason we do not have the secrets of the Lord revealed unto us, is because we do not keep them, but reveal them." The throughline is that sacred things must be guarded — both because they are targets of adversarial attack and because revelation is entrusted only to those who hold it in confidence.

## Where this appears

- September 7–13, 2020. 3 Nephi 1–7: "Lift Up Your Head and Be of Good Cheer" — tagged to the theme that Satan targets one's most sacred experiences ([3 Nephi 1](/notes/3-nephi-1):22) and that the Lord's secrets are kept from those who won't guard them.
