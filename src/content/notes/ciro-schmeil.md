# Ciro Schmeil

Ciro Schmeil, identified in Kyle's notes as "of the Seventy," appears in General Conference- October 2021 as the source of a becoming-centered message Kyle saved. He quotes [Russell M. Nelson](/notes/russell-m-nelson)'s "You are called for what you can become," then frames the Final Judgment as "not just an evaluation of a sum total of good and evil acts — what we have *done*… It is an acknowledgment of the final effect of our acts and thoughts — what we have *become*." Kyle's notes also record Schmeil's application of the theme — "You are called to become a better husband and father, and that can be accomplished through becoming a better disciple of Jesus Christ" — and his line that "To do anything well it requires hard work. Becoming a disciple of Jesus Christ is no exception."

**Context:** Ciro Schmeil is a Brazilian general authority of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, a member of the Quorums of the Seventy.

## Where this appears

- General Conference- October 2021 — quoted on the Final Judgment as what we *become*, and on discipleship requiring hard work
