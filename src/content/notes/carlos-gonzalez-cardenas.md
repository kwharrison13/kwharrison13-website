# Carlos Gonzalez-Cardenas

An [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) partner who recurs across Kyle's 2021 diligence notes, working deals on both sides of Index's thesis: climate/[Carbon Offsets](/notes/carbon-offsets) and cloud security / [Zero Trust](/notes/zero-trust). On the climate side, he and [Julia Andre](/notes/julia-andre) worked the [Sylvera](/notes/sylvera) (carbon-offset ratings) deal — Index invested at seed in January 2021, and the November 2021 Series A pre-brief proposed co-leading a ~$25M round at a $125M post — and the Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 notes flag introducing portfolio companies to Sylvera because "Carlos Gonzalez-Cardenas and Julia Andre did the deal."

At the December 13th, 2021 — NCX Partner Presentation (Series B review of the forest-carbon marketplace [NCX](/notes/ncx)), his read is recorded in detail: he "went into it quite positive for all the macro reasons" and noted "people in the industry will always tell you supply is clearly the issue," but the presentation "gave him pause because they're saying they're going to land-grab" and he feared the supply "doesn't scale fast enough to become a meaningful part of the puzzle" — landing him "on the fence" pending more clarity.

On the security side, Carlos authored the November 1st, 2021 — Otterize (Pre-Brief from Carlos Gonzalez-Cardenas), pitching a fast-moving Israeli seed in Otterize (service-to-service authorization infrastructure for zero trust) and asking for a license to invest up to $8M at a $40M post. He is also referenced in the November 1st, 2021 — Dazz (Pre-Brief from Shardul Shah) cloud-security pre-brief (he had "spent hours" with the Dazz technical cofounder Tomer and was expected to "have a stronger view" after the presentation), and his Otterize work surfaces [Elastic](/notes/elastic)'s acquisition of build.security as a competitive data point.
