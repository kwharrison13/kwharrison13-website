# Smart Cities Council

**One-line:** *Industry coalition of vendors, universities, and development banks that accelerates smart-city readiness through vendor-agnostic content, coaching, competitions, and cohorts.*

## What we know

- Coalition known for a "triple bottom line" focus; helps cities, counties, states, and private developers via (1) readiness content/guides, (2) readiness coaching, (3) readiness competitions/challenges, and (4) readiness cohorts, with a recent expansion into financing (per [Jesse Berst](/notes/jesse-berst), June 28th, 2018 — Jesse Berst (Smart Cities Council)).
- Operates in North America, Europe, India, and Australia; carries both an altruistic mission (cities = 1% of land, 55% of population, 70% of energy use, 80% of greenhouse emissions) and a commercial mission to help vendor partners succeed (June 28th, 2018 — Jesse Berst (Smart Cities Council)).
- Maintains a smart-cities framework of 15 verticals with specialists per vertical, aggregates data on smart-city projects, and runs a money-losing special rate for startups (June 28th, 2018 — Jesse Berst (Smart Cities Council)).
