# Temporal

In the Pave Diligence — 2021 notes, Temporal is cited as a precedent for a deal-making strategy rather than as a diligence target itself. The team reasons that the winning move with Pave — where founder Matt had said he would not fundraise until the next year — would be "to make a pre-emptive offer this month," noting this is "similar to situations like [Temporal](/notes/temporal)." In other words, Temporal is invoked as a recent comparable where a pre-emptive, off-cycle term sheet won a competitive round.

The context is a contemplated Series C: a roughly $50m lead check from [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) in a ~$100m round at a $1B valuation.

**Context:** Temporal Technologies is a developer-infrastructure company behind the open-source Temporal workflow-orchestration engine (durable execution for distributed applications), which has raised multiple high-profile venture rounds. It is plausibly the "Temporal" referenced as a hot, pre-empted deal — though the notes do not name the company beyond the bare term.
