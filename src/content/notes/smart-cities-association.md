# Smart Cities Association

The Smart Cities Association is an organization [Michael Mulquin](/notes/michael-mulquin) set up — described in his May 2018 call with Kyle as a body created "in order to help smart cities work together to solve general problems." It fits Mulquin's standards-first approach to smart cities: rather than working on any specific city's implementation, he focuses on capturing best practices and spreading them broadly across standards bodies (ISO, IEC, ITU, TM Forum), and the Association is presented as the cooperative vehicle for that cross-city problem-solving. In the wiki it is tied closely to Mulquin's own consultancy, [IS Communications](/notes/is-communications), the vehicle through which he advances smart-city standards work.
