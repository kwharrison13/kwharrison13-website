# Paul Wernick

Wernick appears in the wiki via the [Ryan Reynolds](/notes/ryan-reynolds) page, where he is named alongside [Rhett Reese](/notes/rhett-reese) and Reynolds as one of the writers who "acted the role of auteurs" in shaping Deadpool as a character. The framing comes from the essay [The Hits Business](/essays/the-hits-business), which uses the Deadpool franchise to argue that big [Intellectual Property](/notes/intellectual-property) can still be *good* — and credits a small, consistent creative team (Reynolds with Reese and Wernick) as the [auteurs](/notes/auteur-theory) behind that quality.

**Context:** Paul Wernick is an American screenwriter who, with longtime writing partner Rhett Reese, co-wrote *Zombieland*, *Deadpool*, and *Deadpool 2*.
