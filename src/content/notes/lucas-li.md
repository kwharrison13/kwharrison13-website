# Lucas Li

Author of the 2014 business plan for [Your Passion, Your Solution (YPYS)](/notes/your-passion-your-solution-ypys), a not-for-profit designed to leverage college and high-school student energy to address social issues. Per the plan, YPYS was piloted from Provo, UT, with plans to expand state-by-state, and was developed in preparation for an Oxford Social Seminar. Li is named in the opening line of the document; the wiki holds no further detail about him beyond his authorship of this plan.
