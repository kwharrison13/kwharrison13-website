# Eat What You Kill

**One-line:** *Venture-firm compensation/power dynamic where a couple of senior partners control everything and economics flow to whoever sourced the deal — fragile across partner transitions.*

## What it means

- Surfaced in the [Sam Crowder](/notes/sam-crowder) / [Bain Capital Ventures](/notes/bain-capital-ventures) call: the model is really two senior partners controlling everything, with a chasm when breadwinners leave the firm — whether it gets bridged depends on the grit of who's left behind (source: July 27th, 2022 call).
- Contrast: Sequoia's multi-decade succession plan; the critique that performance-based comp can be built in a more scalable way. Related: [Peter Fenton](/notes/peter-fenton)'s point that firms keeping partners in power past 55 fall off the leaderboard.
