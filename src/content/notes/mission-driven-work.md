# Mission-Driven Work

A concept Kyle began assembling in his June 29th, 2021 Roam "Quick Capture" reading log, where it is co-tagged with the original NCEO ESOP primer that seeded the [ESOP](/notes/esop) page. The saved sources sketch the territory: an NCEO introduction to employee stock ownership plans, a Quartz piece arguing the self-care industry is "the best hope for the American Main Street," Axios "future of work" newsletters and a FRED U.S. population series, an HBR piece on retailers squandering their most potent weapons, and a LinkedIn story on the restaurant labor crunch. The common thread is work that ties workers to something beyond a paycheck — ownership stakes, Main Street revitalization, and the labor-market dynamics of front-line service jobs. The strongest grounded connection is to [ESOP](/notes/esop) and the broader [Employee Ownership](/notes/employee-ownership) thread, where giving workers a real stake in the enterprise is the structural expression of the same instinct. Beyond the saved links the capture carries no written synthesis, so the specific thesis is not attested.

## Where this appears
