# Ari Kagan

Founder/operator at [GiveMomentum.com](/notes/givemomentum-com), a fundraising-software company in the [Contrary](/notes/contrary) portfolio that Kyle tracked through periodic founder updates. In the March 2022 update, Kagan described pivoting GiveMomentum to primarily target political organizations with a campaign-page product, after finding mid-sized nonprofits reluctant to switch their entire fundraising systems; several political campaigns had signed and were launching that month.

By the October 2022 update (read in November), Kagan reported that the company had paused all sales and engineering to run over 100 product interviews after missing sales targets, and used that research to narrow its target market — concluding that political fundraising was monopolized by ActBlue, small charities were too focused on grants, and large charities too bureaucratic, leaving mid-sized charities (which care about lower-level individual donors) as GiveMomentum's specialty.
