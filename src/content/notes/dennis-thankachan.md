# Dennis Thankachan

Founder/CEO of [Lightyear](/notes/lightyear), a telecom-procurement startup in [Contrary Capital](/notes/contrary)'s portfolio that Kyle tracked through its periodic investor updates. Lightyear's pitch (from a December 2021 update) is to "buy and manage your business telecom services (internet, voice, WAN, data center) through a single, trustworthy pane of glass" — carrier-neutral, transparent, and fast. Kyle read Dennis's monthly portfolio updates across 2021–2022 (December 2021, March, July, August, and September 2022).

The March 2022 update is the most detailed: Lightyear signed its first-ever paying SaaS customer in Bowery Farming (a bill-consolidation one-year trial), hired a new VP of [Customer Success](/notes/customer-success) (Imran Ulla) and Head of [Solutions Engineering](/notes/solutions-engineering) (Matt Pinto), and booked meetings with Apple, Neiman Marcus, and Five Guys via outbound. The update also noted that an earlier customer, [Curology](/notes/curology), was undergoing layoffs that hit Lightyear's biggest champion there. A later update reported that the SVPs of Service Delivery at Zayo and GTT (two of the largest global carriers) had joined Lightyear as advisors.
