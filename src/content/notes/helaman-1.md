# Helaman 1

A chapter of the Book of Mormon studied in Kyle's Come Follow Me page on August 17–23, 2020. Helaman 1–6: "The Rock of Our Redeemer". In Kyle's notes the chapter anchors the theme of **"the politics of dissent"**: Kishkumen murders the chief judge Pahoran in the judgment seat ([Helaman 1](/notes/helaman-1):9-11), and the conspirators enter into a covenant to conceal it — *"these people allowed their political opinions to lead them into secret combinations that became a plague among their society."* It is the entry point for the Gadianton-robbers throughline that the broader study traces as a warning about leaders whose power rests on manipulation, even when they share your cause (continued in [Helaman 2](/notes/helaman-2)).
