# John Hancock Energy

John Hancock Energy is the anchor early investor behind [CleanCapital](/notes/cleancapital), as recorded from Kyle's May 31, 2018 call with Jon Powers. In CleanCapital's first year it assembled a $20M portfolio of clean-energy assets, of which John Hancock Energy supplied $14M — the bulk of the early capital base. It appears as a recurring counterparty in the company's investor stack, listed alongside Carval, as CleanCapital later took on additional capital partners and ramped acquisitions sharply (24 MW in a single month by 2017) while eyeing larger institutional capital like [CalPERS](/notes/calpers).

**Context:** John Hancock (a Manulife subsidiary) runs energy and infrastructure investment vehicles that deploy institutional capital into renewable and clean-energy projects; "John Hancock Energy" here refers to that investment arm acting as a project-finance/anchor capital partner.
