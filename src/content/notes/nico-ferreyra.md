# Nico Ferreyra

Co-founder of [Default](/notes/default) (the lead-routing / inbound-sales startup at withdefault.com), where he sends Kyle the company's investor updates. His November 8th, 2021 update covered Default's "best month yet" — 15+ shipped features and integrations, first customers onboarded, the default.com domain acquired, and the start of SOC 2 certification — and noted new investors including Siqi Chen (Runway), Lauryn Isford (Airtable), Kyle Parrish and Sean Whitney (Figma), and Thibault Imbert (GitHub). His asks were RTs/likes on his launch tweet and intros to full-stack and ML engineers. He signs the updates jointly as "Nico & Victor," indicating a co-founder named Victor.
