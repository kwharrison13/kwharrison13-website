# Barry Diller

**One-line:** *Media mogul (IAC) whose 2005 "there is not that much talent in the world" claim Kyle rebuts as the internet-unlocks-talent foil.*

## What we know

- In a [2005 Wired interview](https://www.wired.com/2005/10/are-you-ready-for-web-2-0/): *"There is not that much talent in the world… People with talent and expertise at making entertainment products are not going to be displaced by 1,800 people coming up with their videos."* Kyle (via [Rex Woodbury](/notes/rex-woodbury)) uses this to argue the opposite — the internet *did* unlock enormous latent talent (see [MrBeast](/notes/mrbeast)). (source: The Rising Generation - Research)
- The same Diller "gatekeeper" quote anchors The Unbundling of Venture Capital - Research.
