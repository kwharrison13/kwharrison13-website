# Bird

Bird surfaces in Kyle's notes through the consulting record of [Matt Curwood](/notes/matt-curwood), the transportation/mobility consultant behind Curwood Consulting. Curwood helped Bird launch in San Francisco, handling the political and permitting side of standing up its scooter operation — work he did alongside the lobbying firm New Deal Advisors. Bird sits in his portfolio of ground-transportation clients next to [Cabin](/notes/cabin) (overnight LA–SF bus) and Motor Coach Industries, consistent with his read that micro-mobility and big-city commuter services are the trends to watch.

**Context:** Bird Global is a shared electric-scooter company founded in 2017 in Santa Monica by Travis VanderZanden; it was one of the earliest and fastest-scaling players in the dockless micro-mobility wave, whose city launches frequently hinged on navigating local permitting and regulatory fights.
