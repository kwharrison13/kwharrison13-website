# John F.C. Turner

**One-line:** *Architect and housing theorist known for the "housing as a verb" argument — that the poor are better served building their own housing than receiving top-down government projects.*

## What we know

- Contrasted "housing as a noun" (top-down government projects that build housing for the poor, sequestering them from opportunity) with "housing as a verb" (bottom-up efforts where poor residents build their own housing near sites of economic opportunity). (source: [The New Urban Crisis](/books/the-new-urban-crisis))
