# Joel D'sa

Joel D'sa appears in the Salt Security Diligence — 2021 dossier as an outreach target and a resource author during Kyle's diligence on [Salt Security](/notes/salt-security). He was at [Google](/notes/google) at the time (late 2021), and Kyle's notes flag reaching out to him on LinkedIn as part of the expert-reference effort. He is also cited as a resource: his talk "Designing the Right Security Architecture for your APIs" (YouTube) is on the diligence "To Read" list — connecting him to the [API Security](/notes/api-security) thesis the dossier was built around.
