# Andrew Reed

Investor at [Sequoia](/notes/sequoia), quoted in [Product Led Growth — The End User Era](/books/product-led-growth-the-end-user-era) for a skeptical take on consumerization. As of August 5th, 2019, Reed is cited saying: "The consumerization of the enterprise is largely a failed promise to date, and it's certainly not for a lack of effort. It's just a really hard problem to solve." The book uses his line as a counterpoint inside its broader argument about Product Led Growth and the [Consumerization of the Enterprise](/notes/consumerization-of-the-enterprise) — that the bottoms-up, end-user-first software-buying motion is a hard problem that PLG is one attempt to crack.
