# Lars Hamilton

Kyle's contact at [High Alpha Innovation](/notes/high-alpha-innovation), the source for his early mapping of the [Venture Studio](/notes/venture-studio) model. In their July 2nd, 2020 call (July 2nd, 2020 — Lars Hamilton (High Alpha Innovation)), Hamilton had just run a "sprint week" and walked Kyle through how the studio model works — putting capital into *ideas for new businesses* rather than existing ones (contrasted with Touchdown Ventures), distinct from both Think Tanks and pure investors. He pointed Kyle at material to define the space: an article out of Italy attempting to quantify entrepreneurship and predict the variables behind success (slightly challenging the [The Lean Startup](/books/the-lean-startup) hypothesis), plus Startups and the Remaking of the Firm by Elliott Parker and The Acorn Method. The conversation seeded Kyle's interest in alternatives to the glorified "high-risk, 1% success rate" venture model.
