# Alma 61

A chapter in the Book of Mormon's war narrative, part of the Alma 53–63 block Kyle studied for the [Come, Follow Me](/notes/come-follow-me) week of August 10–16, 2020 (August 10–16, 2020. Alma 53–63: "Preserved by His Marvelous Power"). It falls in the stretch following the account of [Helaman](/notes/helaman) and the 2,000 stripling warriors. Kyle's study notes for that week cover [Alma 53](/notes/alma-53) through [Alma 57](/notes/alma-57) only; [Alma 58](/notes/alma-58) through [Alma 63](/notes/alma-63) are listed in the chapter index but carry no specific notes in the source, so this page records the chapter's place in the study rather than a synthesized reading.

**Context:** Alma 61 (Book of Mormon) is Pahoran's reply to Moroni, explaining that a rebellion (the king-men) had driven him from Zarahemla and that he had not neglected the armies out of indifference.
