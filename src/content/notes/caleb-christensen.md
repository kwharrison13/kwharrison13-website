# Caleb Christensen

Caleb Christensen appears in Kyle's gospel study as an Elder cited in the Humility — Topical Study for a teaching on spiritual self-sufficiency: "As soon as I deem myself qualified and sufficiently strong to do the work (alone), I disqualify myself." Kyle uses the quote to support the study's thread that strength comes not from qualifying ourselves but from following God's will — the same idea the page draws from prophets like Nephi, Ammon, and Alma, who were "spiritual giants BECAUSE they recognized their inadequacy." Within the page he sits in the people index alongside [Dieter F. Uchtdorf](/notes/dieter-f-uchtdorf), [John Bytheway](/notes/john-bytheway), [Robert D. Hales](/notes/robert-d-hales), [C.S. Lewis](/notes/c-s-lewis), and [Tad R. Callister](/notes/tad-r-callister).

## Where this appears
