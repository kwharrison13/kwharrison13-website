# Tom McGovern

Tom McGovern is quoted in [The Unusual Ambitions Of Chamath Palihapitiya](/notes/the-unusual-ambitions-of-chamath-palihapitiya) describing [Chamath Palihapitiya](/notes/chamath-palihapitiya) as someone who "speaks the truth… says the stuff people are thinking but no one has the balls to say out loud." The profile identifies him as the managing director of Idealab, an early-stage investor.

## Where this appears

- [The Unusual Ambitions Of Chamath Palihapitiya](/notes/the-unusual-ambitions-of-chamath-palihapitiya) — quoted as the Idealab managing director vouching for Chamath's bluntness.
