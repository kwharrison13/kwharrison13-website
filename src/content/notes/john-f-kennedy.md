# John F. Kennedy

John F. Kennedy appears in [The Magic of Learning](/notes/the-magic-of-learning) via his "ask not what your country can do for you — ask what you can do for your country" line, which [David Perell](/notes/david-perell) weaves into a civic-service thread about contributing rather than consuming.

**Context:** John F. Kennedy (1917–1963) was the 35th President of the United States. The quoted line is from his January 1961 inaugural address.

## Where this appears

- [The Magic of Learning](/notes/the-magic-of-learning) — quoted ("ask not what your country can do for you…") as the civic-service motif of contributing over consuming.
