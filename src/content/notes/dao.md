# DAO

DAO (decentralized autonomous organization) surfaces in Kyle's notes as part of the "community-first funding" thesis for the future of venture capital. In his February 2022 call, Morgan Schwanke of Mana Ventures argued that "community-first funding is going to become a genre of the investing world. That's where Rolling Funds and DAOs are going" — grouping DAOs alongside rolling funds and [Solo Capitalists](/notes/solo-capitalists) as emergent, community-pulled vehicles reshaping how capital gets aggregated and deployed.

**Context:** A DAO is a blockchain-based organization whose governance and treasury decisions are encoded in smart contracts and made collectively by token-holding members, rather than by a traditional management hierarchy. In the venture context, DAOs (e.g. investment DAOs) pool capital from a community to fund startups or crypto projects.
