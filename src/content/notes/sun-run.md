# Sun Run

In the Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021, Sun Run is mentioned in passing as an example of a clean-energy product a company could "plug in" to strengthen its sustainability proposition. The note reasons that a climate offering "feels like a much stronger proposition if you have awareness and tracking, but also plug in an offer with [Sun Run](/notes/sun-run) or something" — i.e. pairing emissions measurement with an actionable consumer clean-energy product such as residential solar. It is a one-line illustrative reference rather than a diligence target.

**Context:** Sunrun (NASDAQ: RUN) is a leading U.S. residential solar and home-battery company that designs, installs, finances, and maintains rooftop solar systems for homeowners.
