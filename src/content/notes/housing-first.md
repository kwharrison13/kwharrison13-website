# Housing First

The supportive-housing strategy [Destination: Home](/notes/destination-home) uses to address [Homelessness](/notes/homelessness) in Santa Clara County. As described in Kyle's notes, the approach tackles homelessness via "housing first" / supportive housing combined with homelessness prevention, aligning service interventions with new housing stock. The premise is to provide housing first and wrap services around it, rather than requiring people to resolve other issues before being housed.

The strategy was backed by data: a cost study (2005–2012) of the county's cost of managing homelessness helped build voter support for a $950M supportive-housing bond. [Chad Bojorquez](/notes/chad-bojorquez) is cited as describing Destination: Home's mission and the cost study behind that bond.

**Context:** "Housing First" is an established homelessness-policy model that prioritizes giving people permanent housing without preconditions (such as sobriety or treatment compliance), then offering voluntary support services — contrasted with "treatment first" / staircase models.
