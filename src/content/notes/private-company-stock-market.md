# Private Company Stock Market

A thesis/theme topic captured in Kyle's June 29th, 2021 Roam "Quick Capture" reading log — one of the ~69 reading-list concepts in that batch (see INDEX.md). The interest is secondary markets and liquidity for shares of private (pre-IPO) companies. The two links Kyle saved under it both point at infrastructure for trading or accessing private stock: [Forge Global](https://forgeglobal.com/) (a private-market marketplace for buying and selling shares of late-stage private companies) and [Kiln](https://kiln.co/). The capture is a reading pointer, not yet a synthesized thesis.

**Context:** A "private company stock market" refers to secondary marketplaces — Forge Global, Nasdaq Private Market, EquityZen, and others — where employees and early investors in private companies can sell shares to new buyers before an IPO, providing liquidity that public markets normally supply only after listing.
