# Instagram

The photo-and-video social platform that recurs across Kyle's long reads as the engine of geography-collapsing, interest-driven brand growth. On Instagram, what you see is shaped by desire more than coordinates, so brands and creators can scale audiences regardless of location ([America Runs On Instagram](/notes/america-runs-on-instagram)) and connect directly with each other ([The Magical Touch of Finn Beales](/notes/the-magical-touch-of-finn-beales)).

**Context:** Instagram is a photo- and video-sharing social network launched in 2010 and acquired by Facebook (now Meta) in 2012. Its Stories feature was widely seen as a direct response to [Snap](/notes/snapchat)'s Snapchat.

## Where this appears

- [America Runs On Instagram](/notes/america-runs-on-instagram) — interests beat geography; the platform dissolves geographic limits on brand growth.
- [Snapchat's Big Advantage](/notes/snapchat-s-big-advantage) — Snap vs. Instagram, and Kyle's hindsight skepticism that effortlessness/ephemerality were ever sustainable moats.
- [The Magical Touch of Finn Beales](/notes/the-magical-touch-of-finn-beales) — lets a creator connect directly with brands as commoditized photo supply rises.
