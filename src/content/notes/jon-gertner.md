# Jon Gertner

Author of [The Idea Factory](/books/the-idea-factory), a history of Bell Labs that Kyle read as a case study in what happens when an organization combines patience, talent identification, and a tolerance for non-obvious R&D. Gertner narrates the Labs through its people and its institutional habits; one thread Kyle flagged ties Bill Baker's knack for extracting information from any conversational partner to the same trait in [Gordon B. Hinckley](/notes/gordon-b-hinckley).

**Context:** Jon Gertner is an American journalist and author. *The Idea Factory: Bell Labs and the Great Age of American Innovation* (2012) is his best-known book; he has also written for *The New York Times Magazine* and authored *The Ice at the End of the World* on Greenland's ice sheet.
