# Brother Stice

Brother Stice appears in Kyle's notes on [The Expertise Economy](/books/the-expertise-economy) as one of [My Mentors](/notes/my-mentors) and the source of a recurring maxim Kyle attaches to two separate highlights in the book: **"[Sometimes doing nothing is the most important something](/notes/sometimes-doing-nothing-is-the-most-important-something)."** The attribution shows up in the book's "Kyle's threads" header and twice inline as a `Kyle:` note, marking Brother Stice as a personal mentor whose advice Kyle threads back into how he reads expertise and human capital.

## Where this appears

- [The Expertise Economy](/books/the-expertise-economy) — credited as a mentor; source of the line "Sometimes doing nothing is the most important something," which Kyle attaches to two highlights
