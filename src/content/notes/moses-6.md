# Moses 6

A chapter in the Pearl of Great Price (Joseph Smith's translation, the Enoch account) that Kyle returns to across several gospel-study pages. It anchors his Pattern of Prophetic Callings: prophets feel inadequate — "slow in speech" — and God's answer is "open your mouth and it shall be filled" (Moses 6:32), a message of *chosen adequacy* sometimes enacted by anointing with mud and washing from the eyes (Moses 6:35) — a symbol Kyle reads as being blinded by the world's filth and made to see by Christ, so the prophet sees with more than the "natural eye" (Moses 6:36).

The chapter also supplies Kyle's Born Again thread: we "receive power to become sons of God" (Moses 6:68 — "Behold, thou art one in me, a Son of God; and THUS may all become my sons"), the same verse cited in Abrahamic Covenant for the idea that entering the covenant makes one God's son. In Plan of Salvation — Topical Study it appears (Moses 6:57) in the "Hotel Hell" exposition on no unclean thing dwelling with God, and in Spiritual Gifts (Moses 6:23) as the "emissary of truth."

**Context:** Moses 6 records the genealogy from Adam to Enoch and Enoch's prophetic call. It contains foundational Latter-day Saint doctrine on the [Plan of Salvation](/notes/plan-of-salvation), the language and record-keeping of the early patriarchs, and the necessity of being born again of water and the Spirit.
