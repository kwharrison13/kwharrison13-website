# D&C 90

A section of the Doctrine and Covenants that Kyle cites in his Christian Reformation, Zion, and Seekers study under the theme of trial and providence. The page pulls D&C 90:24 — "all things will work together for your good" — alongside the observation that "for every blessing you receive, there will be an equal and opposite temptation," and that severe trials exist to prove individuals' ability to keep their covenants no matter what.

**Context:** Doctrine and Covenants Section 90 is an 1833 revelation given through Joseph Smith at Kirtland, Ohio, concerning the organization of the First Presidency. Verse 24 promises: "Search diligently, pray always, and be believing, and all things shall work together for your good, if ye walk uprightly and remember the covenant wherewith ye have covenanted one with another."
