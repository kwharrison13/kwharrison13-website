# Bruce D. Porter

Referenced in Kyle's Come Follow Me study of August 17–23, 2020. Helaman 1–6: "The Rock of Our Redeemer" as the source of a sobering reading of the parable of the ten virgins: that the five who are led astray represent not the membership of the Church broadly but its *active* members — those who professed membership — implying that even committed believers can be deceived. The note sits alongside Kyle's references to other Church leaders ([Boyd K. Packer](/notes/boyd-k-packer), [Jeffrey R. Holland](/notes/jeffrey-r-holland), [Ezra Taft Benson](/notes/ezra-taft-benson)) gathered for that week's study.

**Context:** Bruce D. Porter (1952–2016) was a General Authority Seventy of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints and a scholar of international affairs.

## Where this appears

- August 17–23, 2020. Helaman 1–6: "The Rock of Our Redeemer" — quoted on the parable of the ten virgins as a warning to active, professing Church members.
