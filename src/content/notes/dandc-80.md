# D&C 80

A section of the Doctrine and Covenants that Kyle cites in his Teaching Repentance topical study, alongside [D&C 60](/notes/dandc-60) and [D&C 75](/notes/dandc-75), as scriptural backing for bold, expansive missionary work. Teaching Repentance pulls D&C 80:3 with the gloss "Preach my gospel EVERYWHERE" — supporting the study's charge to open one's mouth and declare repentance without restriction.

**Context:** Doctrine and Covenants Section 80 is an 1832 revelation given through Joseph Smith at Hiram, Ohio, calling Stephen Burnett on a mission. Verse 3 tells the missionary it matters not where he goes — to preach to whomever he will, since he is sent to declare the gospel.

## Where this appears

- Teaching Repentance — cited as D&C 80:3, "Preach my gospel EVERYWHERE," supporting the Open-Your-Mouth / boldness theme of the study
