# Userinterviews.com

A participant-recruiting / research-panel vendor that recurs in Kyle's Sprig fka UserLeap - Diligence as part of the competitive [User Research](/notes/user-research) tooling landscape. [Yue Wu](/notes/yue-wu) (GPM at [Dropbox](/notes/dropbox) and a Sprig customer) named it among the other research vendors she uses alongside [UserTesting](/notes/usertesting), [Ethn.io](/notes/ethn-io), and [Dscout](/notes/dscout). In the diligence's landscape framing, Userinterviews.com sits in the **recruited-panels-and-interviews** bucket — distinct from contextual in-app micro-surveys ([Sprig](/notes/sprig)) and live recorded interviews ([Lookback](/notes/lookback), [Great Question](/notes/great-question)) — i.e. a panel-recruiting layer rather than a direct Sprig competitor.

**Context:** User Interviews (userinterviews.com) is a US company that operates a marketplace and recruiting tool for sourcing, screening, scheduling, and paying research participants, used by UX researchers and product teams to staff studies.

## Where this appears

- [Yue Wu](/notes/yue-wu) — names Userinterviews.com among her user-research vendors (with UserTesting, Ethn.io, Dscout).
- [User Research](/notes/user-research) — placed in the recruited-panels-plus-interviews segment of the landscape.
- Sprig fka UserLeap - Diligence — surfaced in customer calls as a panel-recruiting tool adjacent to Sprig.
