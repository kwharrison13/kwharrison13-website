# Ryan Holiday

Author of [Discipline Is Destiny](/books/discipline-is-destiny), the Stoic discipline book Kyle read in 2024 (and annotated with a heavy LDS overlay). Holiday surfaces in two other corners of the wiki: in the Frederik Gieschen reading notes, where [Jimmy Soni](/notes/jimmy-soni) credits Holiday with introducing him to [Peter Thiel](/notes/peter-thiel) — the connection that got Soni's PayPal book *The Founders* rolling; and on the [Maximus](/notes/maximus) cap table, where Holiday (founder of Daily Stoic) appears as one of the [Super Angels](/notes/super-angels) backing the direct-to-consumer men's-health company.

**Context:** Ryan Holiday is an American author and media strategist known for popularizing Stoic philosophy through books like *The Obstacle Is the Way*, *Ego Is the Enemy*, and the four-part Stoic Virtues series (which includes *Discipline Is Destiny*). He runs the Daily Stoic brand and previously worked in marketing (American Apparel).
