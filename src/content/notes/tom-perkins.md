# Tom Perkins

**One-line:** *Co-founder of Kleiner Perkins and one of the early godfathers of activist venture capital; famous for insisting "we are not investors… we are entrepreneurs ourselves."*

_Stub page — created from [What Is An Investor - Research](/notes/what-is-an-investor-research) and [The Power Law — Venture Capital & the Making of the New Future](/books/the-power-law-venture-capital-and-the-making-of-the-new-future). Expand on a future pass._

## What we know

- "We distinguished ourselves right from the beginning by saying: **We are not investors. We're not Wall-Street, stock-picker, investor people.** We are entrepreneurs ourselves, and we will work with entrepreneurs in an entrepreneurial way. We will be in it up to our elbows." — quoted in [What Is An Investor](/essays/what-is-an-investor) and [The Power Law — Venture Capital & the Making of the New Future](/books/the-power-law-venture-capital-and-the-making-of-the-new-future).
- A Ferrari-driving, yacht-owning showman; the first VC to revel openly in the promoter/front-man role (see the Genentech chapters in [The Power Law — Venture Capital & the Making of the New Future](/books/the-power-law-venture-capital-and-the-making-of-the-new-future)).
- Originated "Perkins's law": market risk is inversely proportional to technical risk — solve a truly hard technical problem and you face minimal competition.
