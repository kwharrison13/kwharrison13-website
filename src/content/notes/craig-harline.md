# Craig Harline

Craig Harline is a BYU historian and the author of [Way Below the Angels](/notes/way-below-the-angels), the memoir of his Latter-day Saint mission that surfaces in Kyle's anti-library.

**Context:** Craig Harline is a professor of history at Brigham Young University specializing in early modern Europe and the Reformation. Among his books is *Way Below the Angels*, a candid memoir of his two-year LDS mission to Belgium.
