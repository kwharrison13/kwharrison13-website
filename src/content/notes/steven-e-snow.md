# Steven E. Snow

Latter-day Saint general authority quoted in Kyle's Hope — Topical Study for a caution about the limits of hope: "Hope can inspire dreams and spur us to realize those dreams. Hope alone, however, does not cause us to succeed. Many honorable hopes have gone unfulfilled, shipwrecked on the reefs of good intentions and laziness." The line anchors the study's argument that hope must be paired with effort and rest ultimately in the [Atonement](/notes/atonement) rather than in mere good intentions.

**Context:** Steven E. Snow (b. 1949) is an emeritus general authority of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. He served in the Presidency of the Seventy and was the Church Historian and Recorder from 2012 to 2019.
