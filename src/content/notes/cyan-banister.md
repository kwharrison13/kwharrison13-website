# Cyan Banister

Cyan Banister is one of the three named investing partners at [Long Journey Ventures](/notes/long-journey-ventures), alongside [Arielle Zuckerberg](/notes/arielle-zuckerberg) and [Lee Jacobs](/notes/lee-jacobs). She surfaces in Kyle's notes through his June 6th, 2022 call with Zuckerberg (June 6th, 2022 — Arielle Zuckerberg (Long Journey Ventures)), which captured the firm's distinctive model: $800K–$1M checks (smaller at pre-seed, deliberately not taking the whole round), competing on **speed over "value add,"** and a values-led pitch ("Chase the magic weirdly," "Be a bobe," "Not monopoly money") meant to be a "hell yeah" for the right identity group rather than a fit for everyone.

**Context:** Cyan Banister is a prominent early-stage investor and entrepreneur, an early backer of companies like Uber, SpaceX, and Postmates, and a former partner at Founders Fund before joining Long Journey Ventures.
