# Cube Software

**Cube** (Cube Software) is a spreadsheet-native FP&A tool — it lets finance teams keep living in their existing spreadsheets rather than migrating into a new interface. Referenced in Kyle's [Vareto](/notes/vareto) diligence as a direct competitor.

Per the Ada Support reference call: a "marginal improvement from spreadsheets" that "works seamlessly" precisely because you stay in the spreadsheet. Priced ~$30K, but **per-integration** (adding BambooHR is another charge, Salesforce another) — contrasted with [Vareto](/notes/vareto)'s all-in pricing.
