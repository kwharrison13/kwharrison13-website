# Ed Rothschild

A Harvard Law School classmate of Charlie Munger's, mentioned in passing in [Charlie Munger — The Psychology of Human Misjudgment](/notes/charlie-munger-the-psychology-of-human-misjudgment) as Munger reflects on how an old man "popping off" outside his formal training risks perceptions of arrogance.

## Where this appears

- [Charlie Munger — The Psychology of Human Misjudgment](/notes/charlie-munger-the-psychology-of-human-misjudgment) — Munger references his "old Harvard Law classmate, Ed Rothschild" while discussing how unconventional views are received.
