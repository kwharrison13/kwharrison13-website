# Jan Hammer

A partner at [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) who appears in Kyle's 2021 partner-presentation notes weighing in on deals during partner-meeting deliberations. In the December 13th, 2021 — NCX Partner Presentation he pushed on NCX's business model — noting the underlying carbon business "should be quite profitable" but questioning whether acquiring landowners was an expensive "detour" given they were trying to be both project developer and marketplace.

In the November 15th, 2021 — Anrok Partner Presentation his feedback was the sharpest skeptical note in the room: he flagged that the founder "didn't articulate a grand vision," worried the low ACV signaled "a feature, not a product," and observed that something quick to build is "probably not all that hard." His comments read as a recurring partner-meeting voice probing for durable TAM and defensibility.
