# A24

**One-line:** *Independent entertainment company founded in 2012 (David Fenkel & Daniel Katz) that became "less a film business and more an emerging cultural conglomerate" by backing auteurs and reading the cultural zeitgeist.*

## How sources describe it

- The central case study of [The Hits Business](/essays/the-hits-business): proof that **scale and taste need not be mutually exclusive.** Behind *Everything Everywhere All At Once* (7 Oscars, $140M on a $25M budget), *Moonlight*, *Uncut Gems*, *Lady Bird*, *Swiss Army Man*.
- "Rather than underwrite safe bets, founders David Fenkel and Daniel Katz look for true auteurs — then back them to the hilt." Makes **purposefully small investments** to spread risk. ([Mario Gabriele](/notes/mario-gabriele), [The Generalist](/notes/the-generalist))
- "An incredible triumph of branding" (critic Alison Willmore); reached a ~$2.5B valuation; expanding into TV, music, publishing, cosmetics — "a redux of Walt's strategy designed for the 21st century" ([Walt Disney](/notes/walt-disney)).
