# Destination: Home

**One-line definition:** *Santa Clara County public-private partnership using a collective-impact model to end homelessness, anchored by a $50M Cisco grant seeding the Supportive Housing and Innovation Fund.*

## How sources describe it

- Grew out of a "blue ribbon commission" of the San Jose mayor and Santa Clara County board of supervisors; Jennifer Loving became its leader and adopted the Stanford collective-impact model. (source: raw/calls/May 31st, 2018.md)
- ~5-person team (Jen, [Chad Bojorquez](/notes/chad-bojorquez) as operations lead, Robert on communications, Ray as chief impact officer / grantmaking). (source: raw/calls/May 31st, 2018.md)
- Acts as a "backbone" and strategy builder for service organizations like Abode; funds developers (often for-profit) with cheap or 0% forgivable loans. (source: raw/calls/May 31st, 2018.md)
- Goals: ~5,000–6,000 supportive-housing units, a $1B private-sector target; the $950M county bond can't fund services, so private capital fills the gap. 90% of the Cisco investment goes to acquiring housing stock. (source: raw/calls/May 31st, 2018.md)
- Four active initiatives: All The Way Home (veteran homelessness), Destination: Work (employment), a homelessness-prevention system, and Housing Ready Communities (grassroots organizing). (source: raw/calls/May 31st, 2018.md)
