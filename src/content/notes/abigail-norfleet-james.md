# Abigail Norfleet James

Author of [The Parents' Guide to Boys](/books/the-parents-guide-to-boys), the parenting book in Kyle's library on raising sons. The book argues for strict guidelines paired with room to make mistakes — an authoritative rather than psychologically controlling parenting style — and holds that intrinsic [Motivation](/notes/motivation) lasts where extrinsic motivation (like money) is temporary, that boys "learn well what they like," and that [Failure](/notes/failure) is the first step toward success. It frames reading to a child as the single most important contributor to school success.

**Context:** Abigail Norfleet James is an educator and researcher specializing in gender differences in learning, known for work on how boys learn and on single-sex education.
