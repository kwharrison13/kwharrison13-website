# Robert Wright

Author, listed as the writer of [Three Scientists and Their Gods](/notes/three-scientists-and-their-gods) in Kyle's anti-library.

**Context:** Robert Wright is an American journalist and author known for books on science, evolutionary psychology, and religion, including *Three Scientists and Their Gods: Looking for Meaning in an Age of Information* (1988), *The Moral Animal*, *Nonzero*, and *The Evolution of God*. *Three Scientists and Their Gods* profiles thinkers grappling with information, complexity, and meaning.
