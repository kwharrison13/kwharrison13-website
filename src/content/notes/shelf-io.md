# Shelf.io

A knowledge-management company that appears in Kyle's notes as a competitive reference in the November 8th, 2021 — Workbounce (Pre-Brief from Hannah Seal) memo. There, Shelf.io is listed (alongside [Guru](/notes/guru)) under the "existing solutions are 'good enough'" risk for [Workbounce](/notes/workbounce) — the search-first knowledge hub for sales that [Hannah Seal](/notes/hannah-seal) pre-briefed. The implicit comparison: incumbent knowledge tools like Shelf and Guru are the bar Workbounce would have to beat to pull sales reps away from their existing workflows.

**Context:** Shelf is a U.S. knowledge-management and contact-center automation company providing AI-powered search and answer-surfacing across an organization's content and support tooling.
