# Matt Irwin

Matt Irwin is a [Watershed Climate](/notes/watershed-climate) team member who led a pitch-and-leads call on November 15, 2021, during Index Ventures' late-2021 proprietary diligence on the Series B ($50–100M at $1B post). In that call he framed Watershed's positioning — "knowing your footprint is great but very few people focus on the reduction piece," and that despite billing itself as a SaaS platform, Watershed is "also a team of ex-sustainability consultants… and they want you to have a hand to hold" (a point he said the company under-emphasizes). The bulk of his call was walking through prospective customer leads (Peloton, Discord, IKEA, Robinhood, GoCardless, AMEX, Goldman, and others) by sector, consistent with a go-to-market / partnerships role. He is also listed among the diligence attendees alongside founders [Christian Anderson](/notes/christian-anderson) and [Taylor Francis](/notes/taylor-francis) and demo lead [Zach Beattie](/notes/zach-beattie).
