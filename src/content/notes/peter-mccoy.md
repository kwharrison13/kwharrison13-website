# Peter McCoy

An investor at [Baton](/notes/baton) with a heavy operating background, surfaced in Kyle's March 30th, 2022 call with [Kyle Gay](/notes/kyle-gay) at [Kensington Permanent Capital](/notes/kensington-permanent-capital). McCoy previously worked at [Bridgewater Associates](/notes/bridgewater-associates), sold a company, and then was at [Activant Capital](/notes/activant-capital), where he sourced their investment in [Bolt](/notes/bolt). At the time of the call he and Kyle Gay were considering raising a fund together around a distinctive thesis: institutionalizing early-employee access — taking the follow-ons and direct-round access that early operators accumulate (rather than the "weird secondaries the company doesn't know about") and turning it into a strategy, on the logic that "the greatest companies will always go with the very best firms."
