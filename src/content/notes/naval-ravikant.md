# Naval Ravikant

**One-line:** *AngelList co-founder, investor, and aphorist; source of the canonical definition of an "optimistic contrarian."*

## What we know

- Per [The Navalmanack](https://www.navalmanack.com/): "A contrarian isn't one who always objects—that's a conformist of a different sort. A contrarian reasons independently from the ground up and resists pressure to conform. Cynicism is easy. Mimicry is easy. Optimistic contrarians are the rarest breed."
- Kyle uses this framing to argue that reflexive anti-conformism is itself a kind of conformism — true [Contrarianism](/notes/contrarianism) is reasoning from first principles, not opposition for its own sake.
