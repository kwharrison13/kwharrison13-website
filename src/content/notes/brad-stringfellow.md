# Brad Stringfellow

A fellow LDS missionary (referred to as "Elder Brad Stringfellow" / "Brad S. Stringfellow") quoted twice in [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book), the collection of gospel quotations Kyle's brother Chad assembled and gave him. The two lines attributed to him are missionary-craft aphorisms: "The way that you show people that you love them is by asking them to change," and "A lawyer never asks a question that he doesn't already know the answer to. Neither should a missionary." Beyond these quotes, Kyle's notes give no further detail on him.
