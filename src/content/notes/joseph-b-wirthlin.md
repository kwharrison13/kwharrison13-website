# Joseph B. Wirthlin

Latter-day Saint church leader Kyle quotes on priesthood service and on the demands of faith. In [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book), Kyle records his line "Priesthood means service; bearing the priesthood means I will serve." In Faith — Topical Study, Wirthlin anchors the study's argument that faith must be paired with action and conformity: "Without absolute confidence, action, absolute conformity, we will have a weak and watered down faith." Kyle pairs that with the principle that obedience precedes further knowledge — "We cannot know more than we now know unless we comply with or keep those we have already received" ([Joseph Smith](/notes/joseph-smith)).

**Context:** Joseph B. Wirthlin (1917–2008) was a member of the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, remembered for conference addresses such as "Come What May, and Love It."
