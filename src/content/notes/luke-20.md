# Luke 20

Luke 20 appears in Kyle's Find To Teach topical study, cited as Luke 20:1 to mark the distinction between *teaching* and *preaching*. The study is about finding people in order to teach them — the inner **Process** of teaching by the Spirit ([D&C 42](/notes/dandc-42):14, [Alma 18](/notes/alma-18)) and the outer **Pattern** of a teaching visit (rapport → teach → testify → invite → promise → follow up) — and Luke 20:1 anchors the teaching-versus-preaching point within that framework.

**Context:** Luke 20 is a New Testament chapter in which Jesus, teaching in the temple, is challenged by the chief priests and scribes; verse 1 describes him "teaching the people in the temple, and preaching the gospel."
