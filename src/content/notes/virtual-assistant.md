# Virtual Assistant

A voice-driven AI interface (Siri being the canonical example) that lets a user query and act on the world's information by speaking rather than tapping. In [What You Don't Know About The Headphone Jack](/notes/what-you-don-t-know-about-the-headphone-jack), the thesis is that [Airpods](/notes/airpods) turn Siri into an always-on virtual assistant — "tap your headphones to instantly search the world's information" — the front door to the voice-first, [augmented reality](/notes/augmented-reality) endgame.

## Where this appears

- [What You Don't Know About The Headphone Jack](/notes/what-you-don-t-know-about-the-headphone-jack) — Siri-as-always-on-assistant cast as the next interface, paired with AR as the voice-first endgame.
