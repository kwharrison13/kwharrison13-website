# About Gospel Study

This is the landing page for the **gospel-study** section of the wiki — a home for
scripture study, talks, and reflections.

Pages here are `publish: false` by default. They still mirror into the website's
`notes` collection so any [link](/notes/link) to them resolves, but a `publish: false` page
shows the **"🔒 private wiki note"** landing instead of its full content — the same
behavior as `concepts` and `people`. Flip `publish: true` on a page to make its
body public.

New pages copy from [templates/gospel-study.md](../../templates/gospel-study.md).
