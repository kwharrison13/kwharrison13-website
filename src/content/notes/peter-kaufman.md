# Peter Kaufman

Peter Kaufman is named in [The Eleven Talks of Poor Charlie's Almanack](/notes/the-eleven-talks-of-poor-charlie-s-almanack) as the editor who gathered and annotated the eleven speeches [Charlie Munger](/notes/charlie-munger) delivered between 1986 and 2000 into Chapter Four of [Poor Charlie's Almanack](/books/poor-charlie-s-almanack). Kyle's notes treat that chapter as a collection Kaufman assembled, giving each talk its own long-form sub-page so the highlights and annotations can stand alone and cross-link.

**Context:** Peter D. Kaufman is the chairman and CEO of Glenair and the editor/designer of *Poor Charlie's Almanack*, the compilation of Charlie Munger's speeches, writings, and talks that he organized, annotated, and published. He is himself a frequent speaker on multidisciplinary thinking and Munger's mental-models approach.
