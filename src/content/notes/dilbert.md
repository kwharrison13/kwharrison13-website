# Dilbert

Named by Naval Ravikant alongside Calvin and Hobbes as a work so authentically its creator's own that it can't be out-competed: "Is somebody else going to come along and write a better Dilbert? No." Purely a passing example within his "escape competition through authenticity" argument.
