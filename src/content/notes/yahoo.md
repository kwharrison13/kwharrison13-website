# Yahoo

Yahoo appears in Kyle's notes on [The New Urban Crisis](/books/the-new-urban-crisis) inside a Kyle-authored annotation about remote work and whether tech talent will leave the Bay Area. Kyle recalls that in 2013 Yahoo decided its lax work-from-home policies had hurt innovation and required everyone back to the office — citing it as the counter-precedent in a reflection on whether post-pandemic workplaces (shuttles, shared utensils, off-sites) make remote life durable and prompt people to ask "Once the reality takes hold, why would we live in SF?"

**Context:** Yahoo is an internet company founded in 1994; under CEO Marissa Mayer it issued a widely covered 2013 mandate ending remote work and requiring employees to return to the office.
