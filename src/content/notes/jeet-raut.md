# Jeet Raut

**One-line:** *A potential CEO Kyle spoke with about building a city-data product (scraped + questionnaire data) for municipalities.*

## What we know

- Advised starting by understanding where value can be created, scraping basic municipal data and supplementing it with questionnaire data.
- Recommended rolling out a consulting product first to clarify needs and define a scalable MVP, cautioning against throwing tech at the problem without human understanding.
- Outlined a hiring path: first a data analyst (to clean data and build scrapers), then a full-stack platform engineer, then an experienced data engineer to ramp automation.
