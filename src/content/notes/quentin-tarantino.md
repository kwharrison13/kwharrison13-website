**One-line:** *Filmmaker Kyle cites as a quintessential [auteur](/notes/auteur-theory) — an artist who truly becomes "the author" of his films.*

- Among the directors Kyle "voraciously consumed" growing up. *Pulp Fiction* and *Reservoir Dogs* appear in the research notes as auteur-defining debuts/works.
- Held up (with the Coen Brothers and [Martin Scorsese](/notes/martin-scorsese)) as the kind of artist whose ownership of a creative vision Kyle is drawn to.
