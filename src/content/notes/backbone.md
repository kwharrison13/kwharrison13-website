# Backbone

A mobile-gaming company that surfaced in Kyle's Index Ventures diligence — pitched internally as "Roku for gaming." [Damir Becirovic](/notes/damir-becirovic) circulated a pre-brief on Nov 18, 2021 seeking approval to invest $10–15M for 10% ownership at a $120–150M post. The product is a low-price-point controller ($99.99 one-time) that plugs into a phone to give a console-like experience, paired with a software app (the intended value-accrual layer) that lets users socialize, discover controller-enabled games, sign up to services like Xbox Ultimate Game Pass and Apple Arcade, and record/stream gameplay. Founded 2019 by Maneet Khaira (24 at the time), it was selling ~20K units/month, a top-200 social app on iOS, with 4.9/5 reviews and revenue scaling from <$1M (2020) to $20M (2021E) to a projected $143M (2022E). The thesis: AAA mobile gameplay keeps taking share, and Backbone's hardware wedge plus an emerging social/streaming software layer compound on top of it.

Backbone also appears as a one-line entry in Pave Diligence — 2021 — an action item to "introduce Maneet at Backbone to Pave."
