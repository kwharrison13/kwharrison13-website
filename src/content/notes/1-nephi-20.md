# 1 Nephi 20

A chapter of the Book of Mormon (Nephi's quotation of [Isaiah 48](/notes/isaiah-48)) that recurs across Kyle's gospel study almost entirely through a single verse: **1 Nephi 20:10 — "For, behold, I have refined thee, I have chosen thee in the furnace of affliction."** That line is Kyle's go-to scriptural anchor for the idea that being chosen, refined, and perfected happens *through* suffering rather than apart from it.

The verse threads through several of Kyle's studies. In Chosen Leaders it grounds the distinction between the merely *called* and the truly *chosen* — "it is in the 'furnace of affliction' that we are chosen" ([D. Todd Christofferson](/notes/d-todd-christofferson)). In Diligence it frames mortal life as "a purifying furnace of affliction built for the sole purpose of our perfection." In The Reasons The Spirit Withdraws it pairs with [Paul V. Johnson](/notes/paul-v-johnson)'s teaching that "the furnace of affliction helps purify even the very best of saints by burning away the dross." And in his General Conference — October 2021 notes, the verse supports the point that Christ is uniquely aware of our suffering. Across all of these the chapter is read alongside its source, [Isaiah 48](/notes/isaiah-48).

**Context:** 1 Nephi 20 is a chapter in the First Book of Nephi (Book of Mormon) in which the prophet Nephi quotes Isaiah 48, exhorting the house of Israel to hearken to the Lord who has refined them.

## Where this appears

- General Conference — October 2021 — 1 Nephi 20:10 cited for the point that Christ is uniquely aware of our suffering.
- Chosen Leaders — the furnace-of-affliction verse anchors the called-vs-chosen distinction (via [D. Todd Christofferson](/notes/d-todd-christofferson)).
- Diligence — mortal life framed as a purifying furnace of affliction built for our perfection.
- The Reasons The Spirit Withdraws — paired with [Paul V. Johnson](/notes/paul-v-johnson) on affliction purifying even the best of saints.
- 1 Nephi 16–22 — I Will Prepare the Way before You — listed among the Come Follow Me chapters for this study block.
