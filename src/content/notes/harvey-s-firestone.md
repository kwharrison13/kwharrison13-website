# Harvey S. Firestone

Author of [Men and Rubber](/notes/men-and-rubber), his business memoir, in Kyle's anti-library (books-to-read).

**Context:** Harvey S. Firestone (1868–1938) founded the Firestone Tire and Rubber Company and was a close friend of Henry Ford and Thomas Edison. His memoir *Men and Rubber: The Story of Business* sets out his philosophy of business and management.
