# 3 Nephi 17

A Book of Mormon chapter — the risen [Jesus Christ](/notes/jesus)'s tender ministry among the Nephites — that surfaces in two of Kyle's studies. In Prayer — Topical Study, 17:4 is gathered into the catalog of *how* to pray: "take time to ponder and listen (use a pencil)," modeling a posture of pondering and waiting on the Lord. In Publication of the Book of Mormon, verses 16 and 25 raise the question of "bearing record," connected to the law of witnesses (Deuteronomy 19:15; [2 Corinthians 13](/notes/2-corinthians-13):1) and the idea of triangulation.

**Context:** 3 Nephi 17 records Christ's visit to the Nephites in the Book of Mormon — he perceives the people are not ready to comprehend all his words, bids them ponder and pray, heals their sick, and blesses their children with angels descending. It is among the most-cited chapters for Christ's compassion.

## Where this appears

- Prayer — Topical Study — 17:4, on pondering and listening when you pray
- Publication of the Book of Mormon — 17:16, 25 on "bearing record" and the law of witnesses
