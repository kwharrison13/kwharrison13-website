# Peter Nicholson

The executive at Scotiabank under whom [Elon Musk](/notes/elon-musk) interned early in his career, per the [The Founders](/books/the-founders)-sourced account on Musk's page. Musk has described the internship as teaching him "how lame banks are" — a formative experience that, in the wiki's telling, underwrote his later confidence to attack the financial space with X.com.

**Context:** Peter Nicholson is a Canadian economist and executive who held senior roles at Scotiabank (Bank of Nova Scotia), where the young Elon Musk worked as a summer intern.
