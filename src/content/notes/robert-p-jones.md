# Robert P. Jones

In [The Next Mormons](/books/the-next-mormons), Robert P. Jones is cited in his capacity as CEO of PRRI (the Public Religion Research Institute) for an observation about generational attitudes toward religion and sexuality: "Young adults are generally uncomfortable with politicized religion, and for them, same-sex marriage is not a moral battleground but simply a feature of everyday life." The book uses his framing to contextualize why younger Mormons and other young adults are pulling away from a religion fused with conservative politics.

**Context:** Robert P. Jones is an American author and pollster who founded the Public Religion Research Institute (PRRI). He is known for books on American religion and identity, including *The End of White Christian America* and *White Too Long*.

## Where this appears

- [The Next Mormons](/books/the-next-mormons) — quoted as PRRI CEO on young adults' discomfort with politicized religion and their normalization of same-sex marriage.
