# Mark Fiorentino

Mark Fiorentino is a venture investor at [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) (and ex-[Stripe](/notes/stripe)) who appears in Kyle's notes as a deal-sourcing counterparty. He authored the Point Card pre-brief — pitching [Point Card](/notes/point-card) as the spend platform for Millennials ("Chime, but for a younger demographic"), built on an upfront subscription that unlocks optimized rewards — and the Eisen pre-brief, an early-stage crypto-fiat payments-infrastructure deal whose pitch he framed through his [Stripe](/notes/stripe) experience (Coinbase's failed attempt to use Stripe Connect for crypto pay-ins/pay-outs). He is also on Kyle's 2024 SF-trip outreach list, tagged explicitly at [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures).

His [Stripe](/notes/stripe) alumnus status is why he shows up in The Talent Vortex - Research, listed among Stripe people Kyle surveyed for the talent-vortex thesis, and he is one of the VCs catalogued in The Productization of Venture Capital - Research.
