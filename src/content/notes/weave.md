# Weave

Weave appears in Pave Diligence — 2021 only as a customer-reference contact: [Amber Kammer](/notes/amber-kammer) at Weave was on the "Reached Out" list of people the deal team contacted while diligencing [Pave](/notes/pave)'s compensation-planning product.
