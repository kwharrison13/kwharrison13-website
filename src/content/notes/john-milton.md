# John Milton

English poet, referenced in [Charlie Munger — Harvard School Commencement Speech](/notes/charlie-munger-harvard-school-commencement-speech) via Samuel Johnson's quip about Milton's epic [Paradise Lost](/notes/paradise-lost) — "No one ever wished it longer" — which Munger borrows to set up his own remarks.

**Context:** John Milton (1608–1674) was an English poet and intellectual best known for the epic poem *Paradise Lost* (1667). He was also a polemicist and civil servant during the English Commonwealth.

## Where this appears

- [Charlie Munger — Harvard School Commencement Speech](/notes/charlie-munger-harvard-school-commencement-speech) — Munger quotes Samuel Johnson's joke about the length of Milton's *Paradise Lost*.
