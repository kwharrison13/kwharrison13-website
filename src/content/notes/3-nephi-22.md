# 3 Nephi 22

A Book of Mormon chapter quoting Isaiah on the enduring covenant of the Lord's mercy. In Kyle's notes on Russell M. Nelson's talk, verse 10 is the key citation — "the mountains shall depart and the hills be removed, but my kindness shall not depart from thee, neither shall the covenant of my peace be removed, saith the Lord that hath mercy on thee" — used to frame the talk's promise that faith in Christ can move mountains and that his kindness outlasts any obstacle. The chapter also appears as one of the chapters in Kyle's Come Follow Me week, though it is listed there rather than annotated in depth.

**Context:** 3 Nephi 22 reproduces Isaiah 54 within the Book of Mormon, a passage of covenant reassurance addressed to scattered Israel.
