# Ruxanda Renita

**One-line:** *At [Urban Catalyst Lab](/notes/urban-catalyst-lab), using art as a change catalyst at the community, city, and federal level — "cities as museums" and IoT-enabled "intelligent art."*

## What we know

- Works with [Urban Catalyst Lab](/notes/urban-catalyst-lab), which helps local governments come together with larger organizations like the UN around "The New Urban Agenda," using art at the community, city, and federal level to drive change (June 28th, 2018 — Ruxanda Renita (Urban Catalyst Lab)).
- Frames public spaces and art as community leaders that can transform people and communities; cites work in Jone's Creek bringing an international artist together with city officials to address social cohesion (June 28th, 2018 — Ruxanda Renita (Urban Catalyst Lab)).
- The next iteration leverages IoT — "intelligent art" — to measure behavioral change, social cohesion, education, and safety; their biggest project is a summit to catapult intelligent art as a change catalyst (June 28th, 2018 — Ruxanda Renita (Urban Catalyst Lab)).
