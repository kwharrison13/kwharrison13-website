# Lattice

In [The Kill Chain](/books/the-kill-chain), Lattice is [Anduril](/notes/anduril)'s networked **battle-network platform** — the concrete endpoint of the book's [Anti-Platform](/notes/anti-platform) thesis. The argument is that the "exquisite-platform" paradigm (carriers, bombers, the F-35) is the wrong shape for the next conflict; the right shape is **lots of cheaper, more autonomous nodes networked into a lattice**, treated as the obvious destination of the argument. Kyle flags it directly in his highlights ("*Lattice*"), and it sits in the book's concept cluster alongside [Anduril](/notes/anduril) and the anti-platform / decentralized-decision-systems frame.

**Context:** Lattice is Anduril Industries' AI-powered command-and-control software that fuses sensor data from drones, towers, and other assets into a single autonomous "mesh" picture of a battlespace — the software layer underneath Anduril's hardware.

Note: distinct from the HR / performance-management software company also named Lattice; this page refers to Anduril's battle-network platform as used in The Kill Chain.
