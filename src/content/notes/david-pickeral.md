# David Pickeral

**One-line:** *Advisor focused on "New Mobility" — transportation, smart cities, and institutional frameworks / public-private partnerships.*

## What we know

- Has a law degree (can help with contracts) but describes his core work as developing institutional frameworks and partnerships around smart-city / transportation efforts; sits on the board of ~12 startups, 6 serious.
- Argues smart transportation won't get done without significant government-industry collaboration, and that the immediate value is cost-takeout for inefficient government solutions rather than profit on services to the public.
- Skeptical of big tech's "smart city" rhetoric — believes Microsoft, IBM, Cisco, Intel mainly want to sell to cities rather than invest in/partner with them; views the lack of a middle market as a key investor challenge.
