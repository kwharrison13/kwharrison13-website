# eRepublic

**One-line:** *Public-sector media company (research, events, media, micro-VC) behind the GovTech 100; an ad-agency / lead-gen channel for marketing to local government.*

## What we know

- Media company revolving around the public sector with four arms: research, events, media, and a micro-VC model; produces the GovTech 100 analyzing the state of GovTech (per [Dustin Haisler](/notes/dustin-haisler), June 28th, 2018 — Dustin Haisler (eRepublic)).
- Functions like an ad agency: companies pay; governments never do. Produces qualified leads (not a transacting channel partner) and designs platforms for each government buyer persona (CIO, CFO, business leaders, clerks) (June 28th, 2018 — Dustin Haisler (eRepublic)).
- Near-universal US coverage built from an organically grown community (90K local agencies, 19K small cities); owns two market think tanks staffed by former government officials; releases an annual report on venture activity in the space (June 28th, 2018 — Dustin Haisler (eRepublic)).
