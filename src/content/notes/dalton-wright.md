# Dalton Wright

A Utah-based venture investor (associated with [Kickstart Seed Fund](/notes/kickstart-seed-fund) in Kyle's notes) who recurs across several of Kyle's 2021–2024 sourcing and diligence calls as an early, conviction-driven backer of Utah startups. He led [Kickstart Seed Fund](/notes/kickstart-seed-fund)'s deal into [Trinsic.id](/notes/trinsic-id), the self-sovereign-identity company — [Serene Papenfuss](/notes/serene-papenfuss) flagged that "Dalton Wright led the deal" and that the founder saw the space as "inevitable" with dozens of use cases (BMW, COVID-vaccine credentials, etc.) (November 8th, 2021 — Serene Papenfuss (Kickstart Seed Fund)). [Trinsic.id](/notes/trinsic-id)'s founder Riley Hughes separately named Wright (alongside Dan Goldsmith) as "super helpful" during Trinsic's $5–8M fundraise (November 2nd, 2021 — Riley Hughes (Trinsic)).

His pattern of writing early, high-conviction checks shows up again in 2024: Wright was "the first check in Salad.com 6 years ago," per founder Bob Miles' account of the distributed-consumer-compute company's origins (April 9th, 2024 — Bob Miles (Salad.com)).
