# Francisco Partners

Appears in the [Pave](/notes/pave) Series diligence (2021) within the financing history of incumbent comp-data vendor [PayScale](/notes/payscale): after a $100M acquisition by [Warburg Pincus](/notes/warburg-pincus) in 2014, PayScale was acquired by Francisco Partners in 2019 for $325M. The diligence cites this as part of mapping the legacy compensation-software landscape Pave was breaking away from — PayScale having grown headcount from 130 to 450 and 5x'd revenue in the intervening five years. Francisco Partners is referenced as PayScale's private-equity owner rather than discussed in its own right.

**Context:** Francisco Partners is a San Francisco–based private equity firm founded in 1999, specializing in technology and technology-enabled businesses.

## Where this appears

- Pave Diligence — 2021 — named as the PE firm that acquired PayScale for $325M in 2019, in the competitive/financing landscape around Pave.
