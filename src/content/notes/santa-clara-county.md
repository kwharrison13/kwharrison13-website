# Santa Clara County

In Kyle's notes, Santa Clara County is the specific geography anchoring his [Homelessness](/notes/homelessness) thinking. It is the jurisdiction [Destination: Home](/notes/destination-home) is organized to serve, addressing homelessness through a "housing first" supportive-housing strategy. A cost study covering 2005–2012 measured the county's cost of managing homelessness and helped build voter support for a $950M supportive-housing bond (per the May 31st, 2018 call with [Chad Bojorquez](/notes/chad-bojorquez) cited on the [Homelessness](/notes/homelessness) page).

**Context:** Santa Clara County is the most populous county in the San Francisco Bay Area, encompassing San Jose and much of Silicon Valley. Its acute homelessness and housing-affordability pressures, driven by the tech economy, made it an early site for "housing first" and data-driven cost-of-homelessness studies.
