# Carval

Carval appears in Kyle's notes as a capital partner/investor in [CleanCapital](/notes/cleancapital), the clean-energy investment platform co-founded by [Jon Powers](/notes/jon-powers). Per the 2018 intro call, CleanCapital took on new capital partners in 2017 to ramp its acquisitions of yielding commercial/industrial solar assets; Carval is listed among its investors alongside John Hancock Energy. That is the only attestation in the source — Carval appears as a named funder rather than a diligence subject.

**Context:** CarVal Investors is a global alternative-investment manager (spun out of Cargill, later acquired by AllianceBernstein in 2022) specializing in distressed and credit-intensive assets, including renewable-energy and project-finance investments.
