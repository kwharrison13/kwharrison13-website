# DivvyCloud

DivvyCloud is a cloud-security company that surfaces in Kyle's notes as the source of a senior go-to-market hire for [Uptycs](/notes/uptycs). In a September 2021 sourcing call, [Ganesh Pai](/notes/ganesh-pai) (founder of Uptycs) said the company's new SVP came from DivvyCloud and would build a team of ~60 beneath him, with the objective of building the sales machinery to complement what the founding team had already done well (September 22nd, 2021 — Ganesh Pai (Uptycs)). The sources describe DivvyCloud only in this context — as a talent source — not as a competitor or target.

**Context:** DivvyCloud was a cloud security posture management (CSPM) company that provided automated security and compliance for multi-cloud and container environments. It was acquired by Rapid7 in 2020.

## Where this appears

- September 22nd, 2021 — Ganesh Pai (Uptycs) — Uptycs's new sales SVP came from DivvyCloud, hired to build out the GTM organization.
