# Nathan Latka

Author of [How To Be a Capitalist Without Any Capital](/books/how-to-be-a-capitalist-without-any-capital), the book on building businesses without large up-front capital that Kyle has highlighted. The notes capture Latka's tactical, self-referential style — e.g. his recommendation to batch time using a scheduling tool, pointed at his own site (NathanLatka.com/schedule).

**Context:** Nathan Latka is an entrepreneur, investor, and author known for his SaaS-focused content, the "Latka" podcast interviewing software founders about their revenue and metrics, and a database of private-company financials.
