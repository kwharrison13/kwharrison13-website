# David Wallace

An early PayPal figure quoted in [The Founders](/books/the-founders) (Jimmy Soni) and cited on Kyle's [Talent Vortex](/notes/talent-vortex) concept page to describe the company's "network-hiring effect." His line — *"It was this network hiring sort of effect where we had a great deal of trust that everybody was reasonably bright, and trying to work toward making this successful… Trust produced speed."* — is used as evidence for how a talent vortex compounds: trust across a tightly networked team converts into execution speed, one of the mechanisms behind the [PayPal Mafia](/notes/paypal-mafia)'s output.

That single attributed quote is the only grounded reference in Kyle's notes; his broader real-world role at PayPal is not detailed in the sources here.
