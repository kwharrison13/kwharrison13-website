# Jennifer Paustenbaugh

Author of the BYU devotional address "When Your Bow Breaks," cited in Kyle's gospel-study notes on 1 Nephi 16–22 — I Will Prepare the Way before You. The talk is referenced alongside the episode of [Nephi](/notes/nephi) breaking his fine steel bow in the wilderness — the moment when, with the family's hunting tool destroyed and the group murmuring, Nephi makes a new bow and asks his father where to hunt. Paustenbaugh's talk is grouped in Kyle's notes with other treatments of resilience and trusting the Lord when one's instruments fail.

**Context:** Jennifer Paustenbaugh was University Librarian at Brigham Young University; "When Your Bow Breaks" was a BYU devotional speech (published at speeches.byu.edu) using Nephi's broken bow as a frame for responding faithfully to setbacks.
