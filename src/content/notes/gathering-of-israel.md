# Gathering of Israel

Across Kyle's gospel study the Gathering of Israel is the latter-day expression of the Abrahamic Covenant. The Abrahamic Covenant topical study ties it to Ezekiel 37's "stick" prophecy — the stick of Judah (the Bible) and the stick of Joseph (the Book of Mormon) becoming one — and to Daniel 2's image of empires culminating in the stone "cut without hands," the restored church (1830). As holders of the birthright (mostly of [Ephraim](/notes/ephraim)), the seed of Abraham bear the responsibility "to gather Israel and bless the nations through the ordinances of salvation."

The practical, everyday definition Kyle pulled from General Conference — October 2021 comes via [Bonnie H. Cordon](/notes/bonnie-h-cordon): "Anything we do to help people make and keep covenants, we are helping to gather Israel." The Come Follow Me study October 12–18, 2020. 3 Nephi 20–26: "Ye Are the Children of the Covenant" reads the gathering as the sign the Father gives the Gentiles, set up as a free people on this land and bound to the [Destiny of America](/notes/destiny-of-america) and the building of the [New Jerusalem](/notes/new-jerusalem). In [Saints — No Unhallowed Hand](/books/saints-no-unhallowed-hand) the concept becomes literal and institutional — Wilford Woodruff "piloting a road for the house of Israel" and the Salt Lake Valley settled as a place to "gather God's children to the peace and safety of its borders."

**Context:** In Latter-day Saint theology, the "Gathering of Israel" refers to bringing the scattered house of Israel back into covenant — historically through nineteenth-century immigration to Zion in the American West, and doctrinally (in modern usage) through missionary work, temple ordinances, and helping people make and keep covenants.

## Where this appears

- Abrahamic Covenant — the gathering as the Restoration: Ezekiel 37's sticks, Daniel 2's image, and Ephraim's birthright responsibility.
- General Conference — October 2021 — Bonnie H. Cordon's covenant-keeping definition of gathering.
- October 12–18, 2020. 3 Nephi 20–26: "Ye Are the Children of the Covenant" — the gathering as a sign to the Gentiles, tied to the New Jerusalem and the destiny of America.
- [Saints — No Unhallowed Hand](/books/saints-no-unhallowed-hand) — the literal nineteenth-century gathering to the Salt Lake Valley.
