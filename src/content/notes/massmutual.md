# MassMutual

MassMutual surfaces in Kyle's notes as the former employer of [Jim Routh](/notes/jim-routh), cited in the September 2021 [Wiz.io](/notes/wiz-io) pre-brief from [Shardul Shah](/notes/shardul-shah). Routh — "former MassMutual, is in the highest echelon of CISOs in financial services" — is referenced as a marquee Wiz customer whose signing of a $1.3M three-year deal is described as exceptional social proof for Wiz's enterprise traction in financial-services security. MassMutual appears only as the credentialing context for Routh, not as a company Kyle diligenced.

**Context:** MassMutual (Massachusetts Mutual Life Insurance Company) is a Springfield, MA–based mutual life insurer founded in 1851, one of the largest insurance and financial-services firms in the U.S.
