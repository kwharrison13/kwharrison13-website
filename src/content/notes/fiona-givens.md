# Fiona Givens

Co-author, with [Terryl Givens](/notes/terryl-givens), of [The Crucible of Doubt](/books/the-crucible-of-doubt) — the Latter-day Saint book on doubt and faith that Kyle reads alongside a sustained tension about creeds, certainty, and how Mormon thought handles disagreement. In the wiki she appears as one half of the authorial pair ("the Givens's book on doubt-and-faith"), credited in the book's frontmatter and grouped with Terryl as co-author. The book's themes Kyle tracks include the science-and-religion intersection (Dyson, Rees, the anthropic principle) and figures like [Joseph Smith](/notes/joseph-smith), [Dietrich Bonhoeffer](/notes/dietrich-bonhoeffer), and [Russell M. Nelson](/notes/russell-m-nelson).

**Context:** Fiona Givens is a Latter-day Saint author and independent scholar who has co-written several books with her husband Terryl Givens, including *The God Who Weeps* and *The Crucible of Doubt*, focused on faith, doubt, and theology within Mormonism.
