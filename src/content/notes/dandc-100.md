# D&C 100

A section of the [Doctrine and Covenants](/notes/doctrine-and-covenants) that Kyle cites in his missionary-skills study. In Scripture Study — Topical Study, **D&C 100:5–8** is paired with [Alma 13](/notes/alma-13):2–6 under the *teaching* skill (drawn from [Preach My Gospel](/notes/preach-my-gospel) chapter 10), as a proof-text that those who teach the gospel are given in the very moment what they should say.

**Context:** Doctrine and Covenants section 100 is a revelation given to [Joseph Smith](/notes/joseph-smith) and [Sidney Rigdon](/notes/sidney-rigdon) in October 1833. Verses 5–8 contain the promise that if the Lord's servants lift up their voices, "it shall be given you in the very hour, yea, in the very moment, what ye shall say," because the Holy Ghost "shall be in your heart" — a passage commonly invoked about Spirit-led teaching.

## Where this appears

- Scripture Study — Topical Study — D&C 100:5–8 listed under the missionary "teaching" skill (PMG ch. 10), alongside [Alma 13](/notes/alma-13)
