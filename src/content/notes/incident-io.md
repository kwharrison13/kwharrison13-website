# Incident.io

Incident.io appears once in Kyle's notes, in the Noname Security - Diligence expert call. An engineering leader walking through his on-call / incident-management tooling stack mentions adopting [Blameless](/notes/blameless) ("feels very promising but it's early days") and flags it as possibly competitive to Incident.io — placing both in the same incident-response category alongside [PagerDuty](/notes/pagerduty). The wiki carries no independent detail on Incident.io beyond this competitive-landscape mention.

**Context:** incident.io is a UK-based incident-management platform that runs incident response inside Slack — declaring, coordinating, and documenting outages — and has since expanded into on-call and status-page products, positioning against PagerDuty and similar tools.
