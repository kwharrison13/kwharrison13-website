# Eight Sleep

A consumer health-sensor company, grouped in [HealthOS](/notes/healthos) alongside [WHOOP](/notes/whoop) and [Oura](/notes/oura) as one of the sensor specialists Apple is content to be "good enough" against while deepening its own health-data moat via the Health app and HealthKit API.

**Context:** Eight Sleep makes a smart mattress / "Pod" cover that tracks sleep and biometrics and actively regulates bed temperature, marketed to athletes and high performers.

## Where this appears

- [HealthOS](/notes/healthos) — cited with WHOOP and Oura as a third-party sensor maker building on Apple's platform while Apple keeps the data layer.
