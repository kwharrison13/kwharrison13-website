# ARS Traffic & Transport Technology

**One-line:** *Dutch-based software company for traffic management, routing/navigation, smart mobility, and freight logistics — building on a big-data model of the Dutch transportation system.*

## What we know

- Sells software for traffic management, routing and navigation, smart mobility applications, and planning/logistics for goods movement; core business is traffic-management consulting, spanning 8 business lines from smart sensors to freight routing (per [Scott Shephard](/notes/scott-shephard), June 28th, 2018 — Scott Shephard (ARS Traffic & Transport Technology)).
- Operates a big-data model for the Dutch transportation system; strategic partner of Cisco, with whom (plus Teradata) it ran a smart-city project in San Diego (June 28th, 2018 — Scott Shephard (ARS Traffic & Transport Technology)).
- Positions as advisor to the private sector and partner to the public sector, focused on opening new revenue channels for cities through public-private partnerships (June 28th, 2018 — Scott Shephard (ARS Traffic & Transport Technology)).
