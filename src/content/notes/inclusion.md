# Inclusion

In Kyle's wiki, inclusion is the research focus of [Banu Ozkazanc-Pan](/notes/banu-ozkazanc-pan), a researcher at [Brown University](/notes/brown-university) who studies inclusion in investing alongside the [Future of Work](/notes/future-of-work). Her work concentrates on how investors handle inclusion and, specifically, on "the concentration of investment dollars in young white males" — working with minority and LGBTQ groups on the structural skew in who receives capital. The concept enters the corpus via a May 2018 networking call (May 16th, 2018 — Banu Ozkazanc-Pan (Brown University)) on inclusion in investing and the future of work.

## Where this appears
