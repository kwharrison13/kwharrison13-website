# Samuel Callum

Samuel Callum appears in [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book) as the attributed source of a single epigram: "A church which weds itself to the cultures of the day will be a widow within each passing age." The wider section collects missionary- and doctrine-themed quotations (alongside [Harold B. Lee](/notes/harold-b-lee) and [Gordon B. Hinckley](/notes/gordon-b-hinckley)). Beyond this attribution, the source gives no further biographical detail.

## Where this appears

- [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book) — credited for the aphorism that a church wedding itself to the cultures of the day "will be a widow within each passing age."
