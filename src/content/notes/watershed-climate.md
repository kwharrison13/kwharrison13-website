# Watershed Climate

**One-line:** *An end-to-end carbon platform that lets companies measure their [carbon footprint](/notes/carbon-footprint), plan emission reductions, and purchase [offsets](/notes/carbon-offsets) for what remains — founded by two ex-[Stripe](/notes/stripe) climate leads.*

## What we know

- End-to-end platform with three modules: (1) carbon accounting (measure your footprint), (2) emission-reduction planning, and (3) a marketplace to buy [offsets](/notes/carbon-offsets) — being all three is what reference calls cite as its winning differentiator. (Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021)
- Founded by [Taylor Francis](/notes/taylor-francis) and [Christian Anderson](/notes/christian-anderson), who spent ~2 years at [Stripe](/notes/stripe) building its carbon-removal program and reviewing thousands of spreadsheets/PDFs to understand Stripe's footprint — the origin of the product idea. (Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021)
- Index Ventures' thesis called it "the best-referenced product and team we've seen in climate tech," with strong references from Claire Hughes Johnson (Stripe) and Dylan Field. (Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021)
- Traction: grew from $700K to ~$5M ARR in the year, ~$100K ACVs across ~50 customers, burning only ~$2.5M to add ~$4M net new ARR. (Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021)
- Customers skew to forward-thinking tech: [Stripe](/notes/stripe), Shopify, Doordash, Sweetgreen, Square, Revolut, Figma, Airbnb, MongoDB, Klarna, Imperfect Foods. (Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021)
- Raised a $14.7M Series A at ~$57M post co-led by [Sequoia](/notes/sequoia) and [Kleiner Perkins](/notes/kleiner-perkins) (with Laurene Powell Jobs) in July 2020; the 2021 diligence is on a proprietary-look $50–100M Series B at $1B post that [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) was given. (Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021)
- Competitive map: accounting-only tools (Metric, [Persefoni](/notes/persefoni), Emitwise — often heavily consultant-serviced), marketplaces (Pachama, Climate Care, Patch), and strategy tools; [Patch](/notes/patch) and [Planetly](/notes/planetly) go for marketplace/strategy but lack the depth to actually build a footprint. (Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021)
- Key risks flagged: a heavy services component today (data lives in PDFs/spreadsheets) being mitigated by image recognition, and an initial customer base of lower-complexity tech footprints. (Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021)
