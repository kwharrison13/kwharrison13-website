# Personal Therapist

`Personal Therapist` is Kyle's tag for his personal-therapy sessions and the work that comes out of them. His therapist is [Kristina Kardanow](/notes/kristina-kardanow), and the tag threads together the recurring session notes split out of his daily logs. The most substantive captured session (Nov 5th, 2021) worked on [Anger](/notes/anger) — framing anger as a secondary emotion masking a more vulnerable one, the displacement dynamic ("kick the dog") of redirecting onto safe people, and healthy expression via "I" statements. A later session (Jan 11th, 2022) is logged but carried no captured notes beyond the call itself.
