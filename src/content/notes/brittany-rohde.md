# Brittany Rohde

Brittany Rohde appears in Kyle's Pave Diligence — 2021 dossier as Senior Manager, Total Rewards at [GitLab](/notes/gitlab). She is cited inside a [Compaas](/notes/compaas)/[GitLab](/notes/gitlab) co-authored resource, "Best Practices for Remote Compensation," which Kyle pulled while mapping Pave's competitive landscape against comp-planning tools. The resource emphasizes GitLab's all-remote scale ("more than 1,300 team members in more than 65 countries") and the discipline of comparing employees across level, location, experience, and job function — with GitLab's handbook including a "Report a Discrepancy" provision — as the kind of remote-comp rigor that justifies investing in compensation software.
