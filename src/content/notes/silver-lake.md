# Silver Lake

Silver Lake appears in the wiki as the firm two people in Kyle's network are (or may be) attached to. Caroline Newman was at Silver Lake when Kyle had a January 2022 recruiting call with her — she was his "top priority for the associate role," and the conversation covered compensation (she valued learning over comp, given a low burn rate), fund alignment ("would only go to work for us if they believed in the fund"), and risk appetite, with reflections on LBOs, converts, and structured deals from a growth-investing seat. Separately, [Nate Quinn](/notes/nate-quinn) — a peer of [Tyler Payne](/notes/tyler-payne) from their accounting program — is flagged as "possibly at Silver Lake," but the role is unconfirmed and stands as the one open question on his page.

**Context:** Silver Lake is a large U.S. technology-focused private equity firm, known for major buyouts and growth investments in tech companies (Dell, Skype, Airbnb, and others).
