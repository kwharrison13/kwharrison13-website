# Alma 50

A war chapter in the Book of Mormon, falling within the range of Kyle's August 3–9, 2020. Alma 43–52: "Stand Fast in the Faith of Christ" study (whose annotations cover [Alma 43](/notes/alma-43) only). Its grounded use comes from the Scripture Study — Topical Study, where Kyle catalogs Book of Mormon "[And thus we see](/notes/and-thus-we-see)" reflection statements. He reads Alma 50:19 this way: "Because we see that EVERY TIME the Nephites are righteous, they prosper. We KNOW God keeps His promises, we just have to keep ours." The verse functions for Kyle as a clean statement of the promise-and-prosperity logic that runs through the [The Pride Cycle](/notes/the-pride-cycle) in the Book of Mormon.

**Context:** Alma 50 is a chapter in the Book of Alma in the Book of Mormon, describing Captain Moroni's continued fortification of Nephite lands, the migration northward, and the prosperity of the people during a period of righteousness.
