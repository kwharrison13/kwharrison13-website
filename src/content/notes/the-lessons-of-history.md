# The Lessons of History

Will & Ariel Durant's short distillation of their multi-volume *The Story of Civilization*, the subject of [David Perell](/notes/david-perell)'s [The Lessons Of History Book Review](/notes/the-lessons-of-history-book-review). The book compresses 100 centuries into roughly 100 pages, arguing that competition, inequality, and the tension between freedom and order are recurring features of human history.

**Context:** *The Lessons of History* (1968) by Will and Ariel Durant is a concise survey of historical patterns drawn from their eleven-volume *The Story of Civilization*, treating biology, economics, religion, and government as lenses on what history teaches.
