# NFT

NFT appears in Kyle's notes as the ecosystem [Hook.xyz](/notes/hook-xyz) operates in — a [Contrary](/notes/contrary) portfolio company "building partnerships across the NFT ecosystem." Per the Hook.xyz page, the company segments potential NFT partners into categories (branded platforms, white-label platforms, individual projects, launchpads, DAOs) and tags them by function (collect, incubate, promote) to tailor partnership pitches. The concept is grounded here as the market the company maps and sells into, rather than as a topic Kyle has written about analytically; it links closely to [Crypto](/notes/crypto) as the broader category.

**Context:** An NFT (non-fungible token) is a unique cryptographic token recorded on a blockchain (typically Ethereum) that certifies ownership of a specific digital item. NFTs saw a speculative boom around 2021–2022, spawning a wave of marketplaces, launchpads, and DAOs of the kind Hook.xyz aimed to partner across.
