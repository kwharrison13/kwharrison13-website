# Lily Pads of Your Career

"Lily Pads of Your Career" is one of Kyle's recurring career frames, applied as the explicit reading lens for [The Ride of a Lifetime](/books/the-ride-of-a-lifetime) (Bob Iger's Disney memoir) alongside its companion frame [Natural Selection of Time](/notes/natural-selection-of-time). The image: a career advances by hopping across discrete pads — opportunities and inflection points — that are "often not the most obvious or dramatic ones." Kyle attaches the note "Lily pads of your career" to Iger's passage about a quiet, non-dramatic inflection point (whether to leave a place he knew), and again pairs it with Natural Selection of Time on Iger's instinct to "say yes to every opportunity." The pairing suggests a model where saying yes broadly (the natural-selection survivorship) and recognizing which pads to land on (the lily-pad navigation) compound into a career.

The frame treats career inflection as a learnable skill — spotting the quiet pivots rather than only the headline ones — which connects to Iger's emphasis on knowing what you don't know, asking clarifying questions ([Intellectual Honesty](/notes/intellectual-honesty)), and saying yes to unfamiliar opportunities.
