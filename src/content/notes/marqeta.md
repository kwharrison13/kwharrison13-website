# Marqeta

Marqeta appears in Kyle's reading notes on the [Berkshire Hathaway Annual Letters](/books/berkshire-hathaway-annual-letters). Buffett recounts his "brilliant insight" to market a GEICO-branded credit card to GEICO's millions of loyal customers — reasoning they would be good credit risks — only to find the venture attracted "business of the wrong type." Kyle's margin note pushes back on whether that lesson still holds: *"How is this different now with all the cards Marqeta is helping to create?"* — using Marqeta as the modern card-issuing counterexample. It is also listed among the live portfolio names Kyle's broader reading notes track (Shopify, Costco, Constellation Software, Twilio/Segment, etc.).

**Context:** Marqeta (NASDAQ: MQ) is a modern card-issuing platform whose APIs let companies create and issue physical and virtual payment cards programmatically, powering embedded-finance and fintech card products.
