# Long Journey Ventures

**One-line:** *Values-led early-stage venture firm ([Arielle Zuckerberg](/notes/arielle-zuckerberg), [Cyan Banister](/notes/cyan-banister), [Lee Jacobs](/notes/lee-jacobs)) that competes on speed and identity rather than "value add."*

## What we know

- Early-stage [Venture Capital](/notes/venture-capital) firm; [Arielle Zuckerberg](/notes/arielle-zuckerberg) invests alongside [Cyan Banister](/notes/cyan-banister) and [Lee Jacobs](/notes/lee-jacobs) (June 6th, 2022 — Arielle Zuckerberg (Long Journey Ventures)).
- Check size $800K–$1M, smaller at pre-seed — deliberately not taking the whole round or elbowing others out (June 6th, 2022 — Arielle Zuckerberg (Long Journey Ventures)).
- Competes on speed (little process, will commit in the room and be a founder's first big believer) rather than the standard "value add" pitch, despite having 5 venture partners (June 6th, 2022 — Arielle Zuckerberg (Long Journey Ventures)).
- Pitches its values — "Chase the magic weirdly," "Be a bobe" (Yiddish for grandma), "Not monopoly money" — as a "hell yeah" for the right identity group and deliberately not a fit for everyone (June 6th, 2022 — Arielle Zuckerberg (Long Journey Ventures)).
- Backed by a deep roster of [Super Angels](/notes/super-angels) including Alfred Lin (via Sequoia), Peter Thiel, Marc Andreessen, Chris Dixon, Keith Rabois, David Sacks, Lee Fixel, and others (June 6th, 2022 — Arielle Zuckerberg (Long Journey Ventures)).
