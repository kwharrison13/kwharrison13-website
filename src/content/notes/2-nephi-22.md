# 2 Nephi 22

Nephi's quotation of a short Isaiah psalm of praise. In Kyle's Diligence study, it is cited for verse 3 — "Therefore, with joy ye shall draw water out of the wells of salvation" — used to argue that diligently seeking righteousness depends on understanding the reward: drawing water from a well requires the faith that water lies below. The verse parallels [Isaiah 12](/notes/isaiah-12):3.

**Context:** 2 Nephi 22 in the Book of Mormon reproduces Isaiah 12, a brief song of thanksgiving promising that the redeemed will "with joy... draw water out of the wells of salvation." Nephi includes it within his extended Isaiah quotations as a witness of the Lord's deliverance.

## Where this appears

- Diligence — cited (22:3) on drawing "water out of the wells of salvation," illustrating that diligent seeking flows from faith in the promised reward
