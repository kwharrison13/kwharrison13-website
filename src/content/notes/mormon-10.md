# Mormon 10

A Book of Mormon scripture reference Kyle cites in his topical scripture study. In Scripture Study — Topical Study, Mormon 10:33–35 appears under the "Personal Worthiness" thread of sensitivity to the Spirit, grouped with [D&C 20](/notes/dandc-20):31 and [D&C 43](/notes/dandc-43):16 as scriptures on sanctifying oneself through prayer and fasting — the discipline of being "quiet in our hearts to hear the still small voice."

## Where this appears

- Scripture Study — Topical Study — Mormon 10:33–35 cited (with [D&C 20](/notes/dandc-20) and [D&C 43](/notes/dandc-43)) on sanctifying yourself through prayer and fasting to recognize the voice of the Spirit.
