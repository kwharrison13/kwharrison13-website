# McKay Coppins

Journalist and biographer, and the author of [Romney — A Reckoning](/books/romney-a-reckoning), the Mitt Romney biography Kyle read "with explicit admiration" and a politics-of-character thesis running through the notes. Coppins is the biographer and the framing voice of that book.

His work also surfaces as a cross-reference inside Kyle's notes on [American Zion](/books/american-zion) (Benjamin E. Park's history of the LDS Church): Coppins's Romney biography is "referenced explicitly as the same author's earlier work on the same tradition," tying together the political-and-faith intersection that both books circle. The two pages share the through-line of character, politics, and the Latter-day Saint tradition.

**Context:** McKay Coppins is a staff writer at The Atlantic and a Latter-day Saint himself; *Romney: A Reckoning* (2023) was built on extensive access to Romney's private journals and correspondence.
