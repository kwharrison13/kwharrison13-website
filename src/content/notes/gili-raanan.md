# Gili Raanan

Gili Raanan appears in Kyle's wiki as a cybersecurity-focused VC whose conviction reference call fed the Noname Security - Diligence dossier (2021). Writing from a Cyberstarts email while affiliated with [Sequoia](/notes/sequoia), he made the bull case for [Noname Security](/notes/noname-security) in stark terms: "APIs are the new datacenter, and Noname is the only run-time security solution for them." He cited the company's revenue ramp (200k → 450k → 1m, with $3m forecast for Q4 and $15m+ in new ARR projected the following year), noted Lightspeed and Insight on the cap table, and flagged an outside acquisition offer at $1B that he expected to close higher — calling it a "terrific team, large market." The diligence also lists his broader cyber portfolio: [Armis](/notes/armis), [Transmit Security](/notes/transmit-security), [Wiz.io](/notes/wiz-io), and Noname.

**Context:** Gili Raanan is an Israeli venture capitalist, founder of the cybersecurity-specialist seed fund Cyberstarts and a longtime general partner at Sequoia Capital. He is known for early bets on cyber companies including Wiz, Armis, and Transmit Security.
