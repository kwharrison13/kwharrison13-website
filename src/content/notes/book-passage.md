# Book Passage

Appears in [Evicted](/books/evicted) as the `recommended_by` source for that book — i.e. Kyle's record that he heard about Matthew Desmond's *Evicted: Poverty and Profit in the American City* through Book Passage. It serves as reading-recommendation provenance, not as a subject of any note.

**Context:** Book Passage is an independent bookstore in the San Francisco Bay Area (Corte Madera and San Francisco's Ferry Building), well known for its busy author-events and writing-conference programming.
