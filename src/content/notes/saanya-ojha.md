# Saanya Ojha

Saanya Ojha is a close collaborator of Kyle's in his Contrary-era venture work, appearing across a cluster of 2021–2022 brainstorm and diligence notes. The richest source is the November 8th, 2021 [Contrary Growth](/notes/contrary-growth) brainstorm, where she and Kyle jointly prepared a list of questions for [Eric Tarczynski](/notes/eric-tarczynski) about a proposed growth fund — explicitly noting that "[Saanya](/notes/saanya-ojha) and Kyle work well together" and treating a potential KH/SO partnership as a shared career bet (fund size, comp, exit strategy, "why we win"). Her questions in that session probe fund mechanics with an investor's eye: the anatomy of a deal (opportunity-first vs. person-first), conflict policy (Jeeves vs. Ramp as comps), pricing/speed as the edge from early-stage networks, fundraise de-risking, and how "productizable" Contrary's early-talent engine actually is.

She also appears in the June 24th, 2021 brainstorm alongside [Gokul Rajaram](/notes/gokul-rajaram) and [Eric Tarczynski](/notes/eric-tarczynski), and she delivered a [Replit](/notes/replit) debrief to Kyle on January 11th, 2022 (captured as screenshots in the source). She is listed as a reference/source in The Productization of Venture Capital - Research, consistent with her recurring role as a thought partner on fund-design and the "productization of VC" questions Kyle was working through.

**Context:** Saanya Ojha is a venture investor (publicly known as a partner at Bain Capital Ventures) and an active writer/commentator on startups and venture capital. The notes here predate that and capture her in the Contrary orbit.
