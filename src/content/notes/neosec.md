# NeoSec

**One-line definition:** *Israeli API security startup; log-analysis approach (vs. traffic mirroring); XDR model; described as feeling "geared towards being acquired by [CrowdStrike ($CRWD)](/notes/crowdstrike-crwd)."*

## How sources describe it

- Raised from Atlas Ventures and other Israeli VCs; founders previously sold a startup to [Palo Alto Networks ($PANW)](/notes/palo-alto-networks-panw).
- Differentiated approach: analyzes logs rather than doing live traffic analysis; parses for anomalies with their own queries; XDR model where they identify risks themselves vs. having a human threat hunter.
- "Super low friction" to evaluate. ([Nir Rothenberg](/notes/nir-rothenberg), Salt Security Diligence — 2021)
- Problem: baselines take longer to build when analyzing logs vs. live traffic.
- Rapyd was already in POC with Noname before finishing their NeoSec evaluation — chose Noname.
- Felt like product was "geared towards being acquired by CrowdStrike." ([Nir Rothenberg](/notes/nir-rothenberg), Salt Security Diligence — 2021)
