# Tim Hurd

A [PayPal](/notes/paypal) board member, quoted on the [Peter Thiel](/notes/peter-thiel) page describing Thiel's audacity in predicting the dot-com crash early and proposing that PayPal move $100M of its raised capital into Thiel Capital to short the public markets. Hurd's appraisal: "It was beautiful logic. One of the elements of PayPal was that they were untethered from how people did stuff in the real world."

**Context:** Tim Hurd is a private-equity investor (Madison Dearborn Partners) who sat on PayPal's board; the quote appears in accounts of PayPal's early history such as *The Founders* / "PayPal Mafia" retellings.

## Where this appears

- [Peter Thiel](/notes/peter-thiel) — quoted as a PayPal board member on Thiel's proposal to short the public markets with PayPal's capital.
