# Erik Popaja

An investor at [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) whom Kyle consulted on growth-stage diligence craft during his Contrary work. In their September 2021 call, Erik walked Kyle through how to read team and traffic growth metrics: 100%+ growth as a first cut on team scale; unique-visitor benchmarks (10K+ as a bare minimum, 50K+ for a growth business, and the trap of treating 1K→5K as impressive growth); and the interpretation pitfalls of MoM versus YoY traffic (anytime MoM runs above YoY the signal may be misleading).

Erik also appears in Kyle's global deal-flow sourcing notes, where a company was "reflagged with data science" — Erik having surfaced it through a data-science sourcing effort, echoing prior flags from a colleague named Rex.
