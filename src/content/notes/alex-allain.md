# Alex Allain

Former Director of Engineering at [Dropbox](/notes/dropbox) (~7 years), surfaced in The Talent Vortex - Research as a contact/voice on engineering hiring. He authored a Holloway guide drawing on conducting 500+ technical interviews, which Kyle cites for the line that "there was a thoughtfulness to interviewing, as if it were a skill to be practiced." That sits alongside the research's broader Dropbox hiring ethos — "false negatives, but no false positives" — and the practice of hiring people who might be overqualified for their starting role but could grow into a new one as the company scaled.

He is one of several Dropbox alumni (with [Alex Yen](/notes/alex-yen), [Karim Anchassi](/notes/karim-anchassi), [Kyle Parrish](/notes/kyle-parrish)) whom Kyle treats as primary evidence for Dropbox as a [Talent Vortex](/notes/talent-vortex) — an org whose unusually high hiring bar created a flywheel of strong operators.
