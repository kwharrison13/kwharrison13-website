# 3 Nephi 2

A Book of Mormon chapter covered in the Come Follow Me study of 3 Nephi 1–7. It returns as a key cross-reference in the 3 Nephi 8–11 study, where Kyle uses it to illustrate how [Sign Seeking](/notes/sign-seeking) hardens hearts over time: despite "so many signs," the people in [3 Nephi 8](/notes/3-nephi-8):4 doubt and dispute, and — like the people in [3 Nephi 2](/notes/3-nephi-2) (vv. 1–2) — they grow "less and less astonished at a sign," become hard in their hearts, and let Satan blind their eyes to the doctrine of Christ.

**Context:** 3 Nephi 2 narrates the Nephites beginning to disbelieve the signs and wonders they had been given, growing hardened, and wickedness spreading, as the Gadianton robbers gain strength in the years before Christ's appearance.
