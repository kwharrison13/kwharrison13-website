# Tim Cook

Tim Cook appears in [HealthOS](/notes/healthos) as the architect of [Apple](/notes/apple)'s platform-over-hardware move in health — owning the OS and the critical chokepoint that all players must integrate with — framed as a "distinctly Tim Cook contribution" and a break from the past.

**Context:** Tim Cook is the CEO of Apple, succeeding Steve Jobs in 2011. He is known for scaling Apple's operations and services, and for pushing the company into health, privacy, and platform strategy.
