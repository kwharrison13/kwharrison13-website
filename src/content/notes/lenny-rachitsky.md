# Lenny Rachitsky

Lenny Rachitsky is the product-and-growth writer whose framework "How to Kickstart and Scale a Marketplace Business" is captured in Kyle's [Investment Frameworks](/notes/investment-frameworks) learning page.

**Context:** Lenny Rachitsky is a former product lead at Airbnb and the author of *Lenny's Newsletter*, a widely-read publication on product management, growth, and marketplaces. His marketplace cold-start writing is frequently cited as a reference on how two-sided businesses bootstrap supply and demand.
