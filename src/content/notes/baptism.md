# Baptism

Baptism appears across Kyle's gospel study as a step in the doctrine of Christ — faith, repentance, baptism, the [Holy Ghost](/notes/holy-ghost), and [Enduring to the End](/notes/enduring-to-the-end). In his notes on M. Russell Ballard's talk it sits in exactly that sequence, and in The King Follett Sermon he records [Joseph Smith](/notes/joseph-smith)'s teaching on "the necessity of baptism by water and by fire." Kyle also treats it operationally: Interviewing (a topical study) works through the baptismal interview process alongside methods of communication and listening.

The concept threads through his Come Follow Me weeks, including Alma 13–16 and the Moroni 1–6 study, where baptism and ward record-keeping are paired.

## Where this appears

- Follow the Doctrine and Gospel of Christ — M. Russell Ballard — baptism as a step in the doctrine of Christ (faith → repentance → baptism → Holy Ghost → enduring)
- The King Follett Sermon — Joseph Smith on the necessity of baptism by water and by fire
- Interviewing — the baptismal interview process as a topical study of communication and listening
- November 30–December 6, 2020. Moroni 1–6: "To Keep Them in the Right Way" — baptism paired with ward record review
- June 15–21, 2020. Alma 13–16: "Enter into the Rest of the Lord" — referenced in the Come Follow Me study
- [M. Russell Ballard](/notes/m-russell-ballard) — the person page for the talk's author
