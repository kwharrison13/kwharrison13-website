# Greta Gerwig

**One-line:** *Director of Barbie — the auteur-driven megahit that, per [The Hits Business](/essays/the-hits-business), "vindicated" the [A24](/notes/a24) model even though A24 didn't make it.*

## What we know

- *Barbie* [grossed](https://collider.com/barbie-global-box-office-1-point-4-billion/) over $1.4B — the 14th film ever to do so and the highest-grossing film helmed by a woman.
- Per [Mario Gabriele](/notes/mario-gabriele): "Without Gerwig's humor, knowing wink, and subversion, *Barbie* would never have become a megahit." Cited as evidence that "there is no substitute for true artistry, for a real sense of voice." A model [auteur](/notes/auteur-theory).
