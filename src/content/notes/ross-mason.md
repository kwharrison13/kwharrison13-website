# Ross Mason

Founder of [MuleSoft](/notes/mulesoft) (acquired by Salesforce for $6.5B) and an active angel/LP whose name surfaces twice across Kyle's diligence notes. In the Francesco Corea call, Mason is the reason [Francesco Corea](/notes/francesco-corea) had heard of Contrary — he is an LP in the firm. In the Otterize pre-brief, Mason appears as the connective tissue on the deal: Index Ventures was introduced to Otterize via Dig Ventures, Mason's family office, and Otterize CPO Uri Sarid — until recently CTO at MuleSoft — had worked closely with Mason over the prior decade.

**Context:** Ross Mason is an Irish software entrepreneur who founded MuleSoft (the open-source integration platform behind Mule ESB and Anypoint), which went public in 2017 and was acquired by Salesforce in 2018. He later launched the family office / venture vehicle Dig Ventures.

## Where this appears

- April 5th, 2022 — Francesco Corea (Balderton Capital) — as a Contrary LP and how Corea first heard about the firm.
- November 1st, 2021 — Otterize (Pre-Brief from Carlos Gonzalez-Cardenas) — his family office Dig Ventures sourced the Otterize deal; close ties to ex-MuleSoft CTO Uri Sarid.
