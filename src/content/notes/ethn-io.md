# Ethn.io

Ethn.io shows up in Kyle's notes as a **participant-recruiting tool** within the [User Research](/notes/user-research) landscape, surfaced in the Sprig fka UserLeap - Diligence expert calls. The User Research page groups it under "recruited panels + interviews" alongside [UserTesting](/notes/usertesting), [Dscout](/notes/dscout), and [Userinterviews.com](/notes/userinterviews-com), as opposed to the in-app micro-survey approach of [Sprig](/notes/sprig) or the live-interview tools like [Lookback](/notes/lookback). In the diligence notes, one expert's tech-stack rundown describes Ethn.io as used for **ad-hoc panel recruiting and continued engagement over time**.

[Yue Wu](/notes/yue-wu) (a [Dropbox](/notes/dropbox) Group Product Manager and Sprig customer) named Ethn.io as one of the other vendors she uses for research alongside Sprig.

**Context:** Ethnio is a research-operations tool focused on recruiting and screening participants — including intercepting live website/app visitors with screener prompts — and scheduling/incentivizing them for user-research studies.
