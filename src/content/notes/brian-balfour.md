# Brian Balfour

Brian Balfour appears in Kyle's notes on [Product Led Growth — The End User Era](/books/product-led-growth-the-end-user-era) as the source of the book's definition of a growth team. The book quotes him from 2014 describing **growth as the "blending of marketing, product and engineering into one tight knit team"** — teams that "focus on the funnel in a holistic way […] because their mission is about the growth rate of a metric that tells a more complete picture of the business." His framing anchors the book's case that [Growth](/notes/growth) is a cross-functional discipline rather than a marketing sub-function.

**Context:** Brian Balfour is a growth expert and entrepreneur, formerly VP of Growth at HubSpot, and founder/CEO of Reforge, a platform offering programs on growth, product, and marketing for tech operators.
