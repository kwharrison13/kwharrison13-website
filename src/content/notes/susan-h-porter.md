# Susan H. Porter

A speaker in the October 2021 General Conference, captured in Kyle's notes among messages on becoming Christlike and on God's unconditional love. In one note she teaches that "the more committed we become to patterning our life after his the purer our lives will become" (quoting [Russell M. Nelson](/notes/russell-m-nelson)); in another, under the theme of loving yourself, she is recorded as saying "when you know and understand how completely you are loved as a child of God, it changes everything. It changes the way you feel about yourself when you make mistakes."

**Context:** Susan H. Porter is a leader in The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints; she was called as Primary General President in 2022, having served in the Primary General Presidency previously.

## Where this appears

- General Conference- October 2021 — quoted twice in Kyle's conference notes, on patterning life after Christ and on understanding oneself as a beloved child of God.
