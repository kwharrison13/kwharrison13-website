# 2 Nephi 3

A chapter of the Book of Mormon cited in Kyle's gospel study as part of the case that the Book of Mormon is a "Pearl of Great Price" whose coming forth was prepared by God in advance. In the November 2–8, 2020. Mormon 7–9: "I Speak unto You As If Ye Were Present" study, 2 Nephi 3:11–12 is invoked for the prophecy of a "seer raised up… to convince men of the word" — set alongside [1 Nephi 13](/notes/1-nephi-13) (Nephi foreseeing the book "which had proceeded forth from the mouth of the Jew" coming to the Gentiles) and [D&C 33](/notes/dandc-33) / [D&C 42](/notes/dandc-42) (the scriptures "given of me for your instruction") as evidence of the deliberate preparation God put in place to bring the record forth.

**Context:** 2 Nephi 3 records the patriarch Lehi's blessing to his son Joseph, containing a prophecy (drawn from the writings of Joseph of Egypt) of a latter-day "choice seer" — understood in Latter-day Saint tradition as Joseph Smith — raised up to bring forth scripture and gather Israel.
