# Charles Koch

Charles Koch appears in [Talent](/books/talent) only in the acknowledgments, listed among the people [Tyler Cowen](/notes/tyler-cowen) and Daniel Gross thank "for useful comments and discussion and assistance" alongside figures such as Sam Altman, Marc Andreessen, Patrick and John Collison, Reid Hoffman, and Peter Thiel. The book gives no further substantive treatment of him; the reference is a thin attribution rather than a discussion of his ideas. He is the principal of [Koch Industries](/notes/koch-industries), which has its own page in the wiki.

**Context:** Charles Koch (b. 1935) is the longtime chairman and CEO of Koch Industries, one of the largest privately held companies in the United States, and an author and funder known for his "Market-Based Management" philosophy and political/philanthropic activity.
