# Tastemakers

**One-line:** *Those who set or curate cultural taste — and, in [The Hits Business](/essays/the-hits-business), the advantage that lets a company like [A24](/notes/a24) capture the zeitgeist rather than chase scale.*

## How sources describe it

- In [The Hits Business](/essays/the-hits-business) Kyle distinguishes a **creator/tastemaker** (Mr. Beast, MKBHD — who *make* taste) from a **taste connoisseur** (A24 — which *curates* it). Both require a pulse on the market; for entertainment, social media is that pulse.
- The essay's framing: "You either have tastemaking or you have scale" — a bifurcation it ultimately rejects, arguing the winners increasingly have **both**.
