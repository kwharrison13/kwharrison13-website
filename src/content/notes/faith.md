# Faith

Faith is one of the densest hubs in Kyle's gospel-study corpus, threaded through nearly every weekly Come Follow Me study, General Conference note, and a cluster of books. The fullest articulation lives in Faith — Topical Study (Topical Journal 1.11–1.14, 1.177–1.183), which Kyle frames as faith being "the substance of things hoped for" ([Hebrews 11](/notes/hebrews-11):1) and "the result of evidence presented to the mind" ([Orson Pratt](/notes/orson-pratt)) — a hope for unseen things that are true, not a [Perfect Knowledge](/notes/perfect-knowledge). His pivotal distinction is between faith that something *can* happen (belief) and faith that it *will* happen: Shadrach, Meshach, and Abed-nego resolved to serve God *whether or not* He would deliver them. Belief, faith, and perfect knowledge form a ladder, and once one knows, one carries "the responsibility of a perfect knowledge" — to deny it is to sin against light.

In the books, faith is examined more as lived and tested experience. [Saints — No Unhallowed Hand](/books/saints-no-unhallowed-hand) (read 2021, tagged #faith) clusters its highlights around the *interior* problem of building an institution from fallible people, with Lorena Larsen's Abraham-and-Isaac "dark night" as the narrative case. [The Broken Heart](/books/the-broken-heart) situates understanding of the [Atonement](/notes/atonement) as a source of strength to bear mortal heartbreak. [The Faith of a Scientist](/books/the-faith-of-a-scientist) (Henry Eyring) and [The Next Mormons](/books/the-next-mormons) approach faith from the science-and-doubt and sociological angles respectively. Across the corpus, faith pairs with Hope — Topical Study and Charity & Love as the triad of gospel virtues, and recurs in talks by [Russell M. Nelson](/notes/russell-m-nelson) and [M. Russell Ballard](/notes/m-russell-ballard).

**Context:** In Latter-day Saint theology, faith (specifically faith in Jesus Christ) is the first principle of the gospel — confidence and trust in God that motivates action, distinguished from a perfect knowledge and understood to grow through obedience and spiritual witness.

## Where this appears

- Faith — Topical Study — Kyle's master topical study; faith as substance/evidence, the can-vs-will distinction, the belief→faith→knowledge ladder.
- [Saints — No Unhallowed Hand](/books/saints-no-unhallowed-hand) — faith as the interior test of institution-building; Lorena Larsen's dark night.
- [The Broken Heart](/books/the-broken-heart) — understanding the Atonement as faith's source of strength in heartbreak.
- [The Faith of a Scientist](/books/the-faith-of-a-scientist) — Henry Eyring on reconciling faith and science.
- [The Next Mormons](/books/the-next-mormons) — the sociological study of belief and its decline.
- [Future Mormon](/books/future-mormon) — the grace-first theological reading in which faith responds to grace already given.
