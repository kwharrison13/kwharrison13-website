# Clinton D. Topham

Appears in Kyle's General Conference- October 2021 notes as the person who asked [Spencer W. Zwick](/notes/spencer-w-zwick) about a scripture on the love of God — referenced as "the best talk on the love of God scripture." The note pairs this with the reframe of approaching a calling as "how can I show God's love through this calling?" rather than "how can I fulfill this calling?"
