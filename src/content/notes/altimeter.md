# Altimeter

Growth/crossover investment firm that appears in Kyle's corpus as a reference point in venture-thesis discussions. In the Mark Goldberg career-feedback call, Altimeter anchors Kyle's [Ramp](/notes/ramp) thesis: he frames a counterfactual where, having already done the corporate-card category work during the [Brex](/notes/brex) cycle, he could argue Ramp deserved an immediate markup — noting that an Altimeter principal led the round and made the case that "SMB card spend is the best part of payments, tiny penetration, [and] if this is the winning product this could be a $100B company." Altimeter is thus catalogued in the wiki via the Brex/Ramp comparison ([Brex](/notes/brex) cross-references it the same way).

The firm also surfaces in the January 24th, 2024 — Outreach for SF Trip notes, where Jamin Ball (the author of the *Clouded Judgement* SaaS newsletter) is listed at Altimeter as an SF-trip outreach target.

**Context:** Altimeter Capital is a technology-focused growth-equity / crossover investment firm founded by Brad Gerstner, active across both public and late-stage private software companies.
