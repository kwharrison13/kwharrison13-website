# Anna Sheppard

In the 2021 Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 notes, Anna Sheppard appears on a list of sustainability/corporate-climate contacts that Kyle planned to reach out to during diligence on [Watershed](/notes/watershed-climate) — specifically as the contact at [Microsoft](/notes/microsoft). The list groups her alongside peers at other large enterprises ([Goldman Sachs](/notes/goldman-sachs), [Google](/notes/google), [Walmart](/notes/walmart), [Lyft](/notes/lyft), etc.) who were the kind of corporate sustainability buyers Watershed was selling carbon-measurement software to. She is named only as an outreach target; the notes carry no further detail about her role beyond the Microsoft affiliation.

## Where this appears

- Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 — listed as the [Microsoft](/notes/microsoft) sustainability contact among diligence-outreach targets for the Watershed Series B work.
