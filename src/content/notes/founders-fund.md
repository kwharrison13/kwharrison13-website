# Founders Fund

Venture firm co-founded by [Peter Thiel](/notes/peter-thiel); [Keith Rabois](/notes/keith-rabois) and [Delian Asparouhov](/notes/delian-asparouhov) are partners. Recurring node in the PayPal-Mafia network. [Peter Thiel's Religion](/notes/peter-thiel-s-religion) notes its investments outside Thiel's "avoid competition, seek monopoly" companies (Palantir, DeepMind) also include highly competitive-market bets like Airbnb, Stripe, and Postmates — a 2011 Thiel essay, "The End of the Future," lives on the firm's homepage and argues technological progress has stagnated.
