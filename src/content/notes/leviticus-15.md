# Leviticus 15

The Mosaic law of bodily "issues," prescribing washing of clothing and bathing in running water after contact with the unclean (vv. 1–13). [Russell M. Nelson](/notes/russell-m-nelson) — a former heart surgeon — uses it in "Where Is Wisdom?" as his central illustration that the Lord revealed "principles of clean technique … more than three thousand years ago," in "complete harmony with modern medical guidelines," yet generations of mothers and children died of preventable infection because "man's quest for knowledge had failed to incorporate the word of the Lord." A case study in [Wisdom](/notes/wisdom) withheld until people are "prepared to receive" it.

## Where this appears

- Where Is Wisdom? — Russell M. Nelson — vv. 1–5, 7, 13, the sanitation-vs-germ-theory illustration.
