# Night at the Museum

Referenced in [The Founders](/books/the-founders) as one of Kyle's connection tags, attached to the moment where two of the PayPal founders (Chris and Stephen) visualize their "Master-Plan" while in prison. Kyle flags the film as an analogy for that scene of imagination-amid-confinement / things-coming-to-life-when-no-one-is-watching.

**Context:** *Night at the Museum* (2006) is a comedy film in which museum exhibits — historical figures and artifacts — come to life after dark.
