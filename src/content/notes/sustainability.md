# Sustainability

A tag Kyle pairs with [Climate Change](/notes/climate-change) to mark notes touching clean-energy and climate theses. It appears on the Altaport thread, where [Pranav Iyer](/notes/pranav-iyer) replies to Kyle's forward of [Robert Carroll](/notes/robert-carroll)'s vertiport startup with two energy theses he's tracking: (1) fixing intermittent renewable energy via better battery storage (citing Stanford's alkali-metal-chlorine battery work and vehicle-to-grid charging), and (2) fixing it via other sources — nuclear, desalination, wave energy. Carroll himself is ex-[Oklo](/notes/oklo) with a Kennedy School thesis on advanced nuclear, tying the tag to Kyle's interest in clean-energy founders and the grid-intermittency problem.
