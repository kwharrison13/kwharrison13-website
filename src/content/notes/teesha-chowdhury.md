# Teesha Chowdhury

A sustainability contact at [Google](/notes/google) whom Kyle emailed as part of his 2021 diligence on [Watershed](/notes/watershed-climate). In the diligence log she appears in the "Emailed" list of corporate sustainability leads — alongside contacts at [Apple ($AAPL)](/notes/apple), [Salesforce ($CRM)](/notes/salesforce), [BlackRock](/notes/blackrock), [IKEA](/notes/ikea), [Nu Skin](/notes/nu-skin), and [Sweetgreen](/notes/sweetgreen) — that Kyle worked to reach reference customers and gauge enterprise demand for carbon-accounting software during the Watershed evaluation.
