# Replacing Church

The pattern of secular brands and institutions filling the community, ritual, and identity void left by declining religious participation — the core thesis of [Equinox Is More Than A Gym. It's A Church](/notes/equinox-is-more-than-a-gym-it-s-a-church), where a gym functions less like a fitness service than a secular congregation. As the world secularizes, younger generations seek the community the church and neighborhood once provided.
