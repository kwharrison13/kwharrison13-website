# Omni 1

Omni 1 is the single chapter of the Book of Omni in the [Book of Mormon](/notes/book-of-mormon), cited across several of Kyle's gospel-study notes. Two verses recur. [Omni 1](/notes/omni-1):26 — "come unto Christ... offer your whole souls as an offering unto him, and continue in fasting and praying, and endure to the end" — anchors his Fasting - September 5, 2021 study as a scriptural call to wholehearted consecration. [Omni 1](/notes/omni-1):6 — "he would not suffer that the words should not be verified... Inasmuch as ye will not keep my commandments ye shall not prosper in the land" — appears in his The Process of the Book of Mormon and Alma 36–38 notes as one link in the chain of "keep commandments > prosper / keep not > cut off" passages that runs through [2 Nephi 1](/notes/2-nephi-1), [Jarom 1](/notes/jarom-1), [Mosiah 1](/notes/mosiah-1), and [Alma 9](/notes/alma-9).

**Context:** The Book of Omni is one of the shortest books in the Book of Mormon, written by a succession of record-keepers and ending with the discovery of the people of Zarahemla and the reign of King Mosiah. Its verses are commonly cited for the recurring covenant promise tying prosperity in the land to obedience.

## Where this appears

- Fasting - September 5, 2021 — Omni 1:26 cited as the call to "offer your whole souls" and "continue in fasting and praying."
- July 20–26, 2020. Alma 36–38: "Look to God and Live" — Omni 1:6 listed among the "prosper in the land" covenant passages.
- The Process of the Book of Mormon — Omni 1:6 in the obedience-and-prosperity scripture chain alongside Jarom, Mosiah, and Alma.
