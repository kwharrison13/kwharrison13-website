# Singapore

The governance model under study in [The Story of Singapore](/notes/the-story-of-singapore): how the country leaped from third-world to first in 53 years under [Lee Kuan Yew](/notes/lee-kuan-yew) — running clean systems, courting global capital, paying officials world-class salaries, and choosing strict rule of law over corruption.

**Context:** Singapore is a Southeast Asian city-state that gained independence in 1965 and became one of the world's wealthiest nations per capita, frequently cited as a case study in state capacity, anti-corruption, and economic development.
