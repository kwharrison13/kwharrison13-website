# Alternative Company Buying

The theme of acquiring and holding a business to operate and grow it indefinitely, rather than building toward an exit — the [SmugMug](/notes/smugmug) / [Don MacAskill](/notes/don-macaskill) model of an enduring, owner-controlled, customer-centric company that deliberately avoids outside capital. Tagged alongside [Small Business](/notes/small-business) in Kyle's notes on long-term holds.

## Where this appears

- [On the Nature of Long-Term Holds](/notes/on-the-nature-of-long-term-holds) — applied to Don MacAskill's SmugMug philosophy: building an enduring company you control instead of optimizing for an exit.
