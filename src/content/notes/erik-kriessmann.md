# Erik Kriessmann

**One-line:** *Talent partner at [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) (previously [Khosla Ventures](/notes/khosla-ventures) and [Coatue](/notes/coatue)) who builds executive-recruiting and super-connector relationships as a value play to help portfolio companies make key hires and customer intros.*

## What we know

- Runs talent at [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures); argues firms that can't win on price (vs. Coatue/Tiger) must win on value via talent and access. (source: December 14th, 2021 daily-note)
- Preemptively maps a portfolio company's future hiring needs by asking founders many questions ("what outcomes are you shooting for?") and backing into a capacity model. (source: December 14th, 2021 daily-note)
- Treats super-connector relationships as a two-sided "buyer's journey" — figure out what an exec wants (angel investing, advising, boards, peers, deal flow) and deliver it before asking for anything; cites landing the Okta CMO relationship and the Transcend deal as an example. (source: December 14th, 2021 daily-note)
- At [Khosla Ventures](/notes/khosla-ventures), [Vinod Khosla](/notes/vinod-khosla)'s only metric was whether founders liked him; contrasts with Index's founder-vs-board-member stakeholder balance. (source: December 14th, 2021 daily-note)
- At [Coatue](/notes/coatue), talent was unprogrammatic; [Jess Oynick](/notes/jess-oynick) was strong but underutilized as a search-fund coordinator. (source: December 14th, 2021 daily-note)
