# Disneyland

Disneyland is invoked as the archetype of a designed, friction-as-feature [experience](/notes/experiences) — the deliberate "park far, shuttle in" journey that makes a golf major feel monumental, the same way Disney choreographs arrival and anticipation.

**Context:** Disneyland is the Anaheim, California theme park opened by Walt Disney in 1955, often cited in experience-design and brand discussions as a benchmark for orchestrated, immersive customer journeys.
