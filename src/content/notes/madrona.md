# Madrona

Madrona appears in the Pave Diligence — 2021 dossier as one of the venture funds participating in Pave's give-to-get compensation data network. Pave's data set originated through strategic relationships with VCs, and by the time of Index's 2021 diligence it was working with 70+ funds — Madrona listed alongside [Y Combinator](/notes/y-combinator), [IVP](/notes/ivp), and [Sequoia](/notes/sequoia) as part of that network. In Kyle's notes Madrona is a data-partner reference within Pave's competitive moat narrative, not a fund he engaged directly.

**Context:** Madrona (Madrona Venture Group) is a Seattle-based venture firm founded in 1995, historically focused on Pacific Northwest technology companies and an early backer of Amazon.
