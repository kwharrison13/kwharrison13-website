# Generalist Capital

[Mario Gabriele](/notes/mario-gabriele)'s investment vehicle, an offshoot of his media business [The Generalist](/notes/the-generalist). In Kyle's Storytelling of Investing notes, Generalist Capital appears as a live example of a newer fund built explicitly on **narrative as a competitive edge** — the [Soft Power](/notes/soft-power) thesis applied to venture itself. Kyle cites its framing directly: *"Narratives move the world. For early-stage founders, an impactful story is magnetic to talent, capital, and customers."*

It sits in the same lineage as the firms Kyle studies for their writing and storytelling cultures (Stripe Press, [USV](/notes/usv)'s public ontology), but it is the more direct case: a fund whose pitch is that compelling stories attract talent, capital, and customers — and that a media/creator company can import that attractive power into investing. See [Storytelling](/notes/storytelling), [Soft Power](/notes/soft-power).

**Context:** Generalist Capital is the venture fund launched by Mario Gabriele, founder of the tech/business publication The Generalist, extending his writing platform into early-stage investing.
