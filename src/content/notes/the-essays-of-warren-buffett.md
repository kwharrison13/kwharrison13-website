# The Essays of Warren Buffett

**Author:** Curated & arranged by Lawrence A. Cunningham from [Warren Buffett](/notes/warren-buffett)'s Berkshire Hathaway shareholder letters.

**Why it's on the list:** *Cunningham organizes decades of Buffett's shareholder-letter writing into a single thematic course on capital allocation, owner-mindset investing, and corporate governance — the canonical distillation of Buffett's thinking.*
