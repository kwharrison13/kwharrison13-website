# John 10

A chapter from the Gospel of John. In Kyle's Prayer — Topical Study, John 10:27 — "My sheep hear my voice, and I know them, and they follow me" — is cited under "How to pray" for the principle that the Father knows us individually. It sits alongside [2 Nephi 9](/notes/2-nephi-9):43 (humble prayer is most effective) and a Bible Dictionary reference on having faith in God's answers.

**Context:** John 10 contains Jesus' "Good Shepherd" discourse, in which he describes himself as the shepherd whose sheep know and follow his voice — the basis for the study's point that God knows each person individually.
