# Altaport

Early-stage company founded by [Robert Carroll](/notes/robert-carroll) (ex-[Oklo](/notes/oklo), Harvard Kennedy School), described in Kyle's notes as an **"AirBnB-esque helipad / vertiport marketplace"** — infrastructure planning 5–10 years ahead for the eVTOL / advanced-air-mobility era (altaport.aero). Kyle forwarded the company to [Pranav Iyer](/notes/pranav-iyer) in December 2021 as something likely too early for Pranav's interest but worth flagging, alongside Carroll's grad-school thesis on advanced nuclear energy. The conversation that followed centered less on Altaport's product and more on Carroll's energy background and Pranav's intermittent-energy theses (better battery storage; nuclear/desalination/wave alternatives), since Carroll had joined Oklo during his Kennedy School time.

**Context:** Altaport builds software for managing vertiports and advanced air mobility operations (scheduling, ground control, and airspace coordination for eVTOL and helicopter landing infrastructure).
