# Behavioral Design

The idea that systems and incentives can be deliberately shaped to steer human behavior. In [Surge Pricing Can Save Hundreds of People](/notes/surge-pricing-can-save-hundreds-of-people), it's the lens on price as an incentive signal that reallocates scarce supply toward the most urgent need — surge pricing as a behavioral-design mechanism rather than mere profiteering.
