# Ludwig Schoenack

Co-founder of [Kyte](/notes/kyte), the car-rental / mobility startup and [Contrary](/notes/contrary) portfolio company, and the author of its investor updates that Kyle tracked through 2022. In the May 2022 update Schoenack reported that while revenue grew 23%, Kyte grew gross profit 33% and net profit 270% across markets, framing this as confidence in the core business's ability to generate free cash flow within ~12 months "in an incredibly capital efficient manner." The July 2022 update reported revenue up 18% to $3.95M (≈$48M annualized), customer acquisition cost held at ~$30/booking, and organic customers at 57%+. By the September 2022 update, Schoenack reported closing the first $30M of Kyte's Series B with a final close expected by end of October above the $40M target, followed by a press announcement in early November.

Kyle read these updates while clearing his inbox at Contrary, so his exposure to Schoenack runs entirely through the Kyte portfolio reporting rather than direct calls.
