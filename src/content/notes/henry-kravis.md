# Henry Kravis

In Kyle's wiki, Kravis appears as one of the named backers of [Steve Case](/notes/steve-case)'s [Rise of the Rest](/notes/rise-of-the-rest) seed fund — the $150M vehicle (co-led with J.D. Vance) that grew out of four years and six bus tours across 33 cities. He is grouped with [Jeff Bezos](/notes/jeff-bezos) and [Howard Schultz](/notes/howard-schultz) as a high-profile investor lending capital and credibility to the thesis of backing startups outside the coastal venture hubs.

**Context:** Henry Kravis (b. 1944) is an American financier who co-founded the private-equity firm KKR (Kohlberg Kravis Roberts) in 1976, a pioneer of the leveraged buyout best known for the RJR Nabisco deal chronicled in *Barbarians at the Gate*.
