# Martin Mignot

Martin Mignot appears in Kyle's notes as a partner at [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures), showing up in two 2021 deal contexts. In the December 13th, 2021 — NCX Partner Presentation for [NCX](/notes/ncx) (the forest-carbon marketplace), Mignot was part of the partnership review and landed in the "Same place as [Danny Rimer](/notes/danny-rimer)" camp — sharing Rimer's Series B heartache that the deal asked the firm to pay up for a lot of risk without much more clarity than it would have had at Series A. Earlier that year he co-authored, with [Julia Andre](/notes/julia-andre), a read pre-brief for Shara, the community-driven SME-finance product for [Africa](/notes/africa) that Kyle reviewed (see June 29th, 2021 — Shara (Pre-Brief)).

**Context:** Martin Mignot is a London-based General Partner at Index Ventures, known for early investments in consumer and marketplace companies including Deliveroo, Revolut, and BlaBlaCar.

## Where this appears

- December 13th, 2021 — NCX Partner Presentation — listed among the Index partnership; his partner review echoed [Danny Rimer](/notes/danny-rimer)'s reservations about the NCX Series B.
- June 29th, 2021 — Shara (Pre-Brief) — co-author (with [Julia Andre](/notes/julia-andre)) of the pre-brief Kyle read on Shara.
