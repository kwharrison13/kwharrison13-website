# Bradley Faught

CTO and co-founder of [Soko](/notes/soko), the compliance engine for independent contractors that Kyle diligenced from the [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) side in August 2021. Per the data room, Faught is a full-stack engineer with experience growing teams and launching B2B and B2B2C software in regulated industries. Before Soko, he co-founded Fade (a barber-booking marketplace) and OKO (a cannabis labor marketplace) alongside CEO [Farhaj Mayan](/notes/farhaj-mayan) and CPO [Vu Francois](/notes/vu-francois); the OKO team's work building an independent-contractor compliance process — done together with [Eve Epstein](/notes/eve-epstein)'s SoleVenture — became the inception point for Soko.
