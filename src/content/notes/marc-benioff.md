# Marc Benioff

Co-founder and CEO of [Salesforce](/notes/salesforce), referenced across Kyle's notes in two distinct contexts. In The Renaissance of Rise and Grind - Research he appears in the "Not Just a Twitter Trend" section as a non-Twitter example of the same back-to-the-grind, output-policing turn — captured via a LinkedIn news item about the Salesforce CEO calling out new hires for low productivity, set against Salesforce's "we're a family" framing and large layoffs.

Separately, in the Claudine Emeott call, Benioff is cited as having made the **Sustainability Cloud a much bigger priority and pouring substantial resources into it** — context for [Salesforce Ventures](/notes/salesforce-ventures)' climate-investing focus and Kyle's [Carbon Accounting](/notes/carbon-accounting) diligence.

**Context:** Marc Benioff co-founded Salesforce in 1999 and built it into one of the largest enterprise-software companies. He also owns Time magazine and is known for his advocacy on stakeholder capitalism and corporate philanthropy (the "1-1-1" model).
