# Memberstack

Memberstack appears in Kyle's notes in the August 2021 customer-reference call with Rob at [Cartooli](/notes/cartooli) about [WhaleSync](/notes/whalesync). Discussing his vision for where no-code tooling is headed, Rob cites Memberstack as an example of "a lot of niche tools getting built up to let people monetize communities" — the broader category of point solutions that WhaleSync sits among in the no-code stack ([Airtable](/notes/airtable), [Webflow](/notes/webflow), [Zapier](/notes/zapier), [Bubble](/notes/bubble)). In the notes Memberstack is a category example, not a tool Kyle diligenced.

**Context:** Memberstack is a no-code tool for adding user authentication, memberships, and gated payments to websites built on platforms like Webflow, popular for spinning up membership and community-monetization sites without custom backend code.
