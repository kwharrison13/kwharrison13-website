# 1 Nephi 19

The chapter Kyle leans on most from 1 Nephi 19 is Nephi's statement of how to read scripture: "For I did liken all scriptures unto us, for our profit and learning" (19:23). In Spiritual Gifts he quotes it directly as the principle that scripture is meant to be applied to one's own life, not merely studied historically. The chapter is also one of the chapters in the broader Come Follow Me study 1 Nephi 16–22 — I Will Prepare the Way before You, where Nephi records his people's journey and the words of Isaiah for their instruction.

**Context:** In 1 Nephi 19, Nephi makes the small plates, records his people's history and prophecies of Christ, and explains that he "likens" the scriptures — including Isaiah — to his people for their profit and learning.

## Where this appears

- Spiritual Gifts — "I did liken all scriptures unto us, for our profit and learning" (19:23) as the model for personally applying scripture
- 1 Nephi 16–22 — I Will Prepare the Way before You — a chapter in the Come Follow Me arc on Nephi's journey and teachings
