- An early home for [Keith Rabois](/notes/keith-rabois) and one of the alumni feeders behind [Persona](/notes/persona). (The Talent Vortex - Research)
- A Sprig customer: [Rishi Ahuja](/notes/rishi-ahuja), a PM at Square, was an expert reference on [Sprig](/notes/sprig) — uses it for in-app micro-surveys (e.g., a funnel to switch direct deposit to Square), estimates ~100 potential Sprig seats across Square and Cash App with ~20 users today.
