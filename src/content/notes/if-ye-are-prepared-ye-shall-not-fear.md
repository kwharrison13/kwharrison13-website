# If Ye Are Prepared Ye Shall Not Fear

A talk by [Gordon B. Hinckley](/notes/gordon-b-hinckley) that Kyle referenced in his June 22–28, 2020. Alma 17–22: "I Will Make an Instrument of Thee" Come Follow Me study, in the "Be Ye Comforted" section. He paired the talk's title scripture — "if ye are prepared ye shall not fear" (D&C 38:30) — with the Spirit comforting the sons of Mosiah. Kyle's notes capture the talk's key takeaways: that the world is a hard place that can terrify us into hopelessness ("men's hearts shall fail them; for fear shall come upon all people," D&C 88:91); that present calamities were foretold and more are expected; and that the remedy is to "treasure up wisdom" and live so "that we can have confidence before God."

**Context:** "If Ye Are Prepared Ye Shall Not Fear" is the title of a 2005 general conference address by Gordon B. Hinckley, then President of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, taking its title from Doctrine and Covenants 38:30 and counseling temporal and spiritual preparedness.
