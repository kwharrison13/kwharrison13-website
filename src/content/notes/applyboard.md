# Applyboard

Surfaces once in Pave Diligence — 2021, where it is cited — alongside Instabase, Anrok, [Discord](/notes/discord), [Newfront Insurance](/notes/newfront-insurance), Scale AI, and [Boulevard](/notes/boulevard) — as one of the 40+ [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) portfolio companies contributing data into [Pave](/notes/pave)'s give-to-get compensation-benchmarking network. It appears as an example data contributor, not as a subject of the diligence itself.

**Context:** ApplyBoard is a Canadian edtech marketplace (founded 2015, Kitchener-Waterloo) that connects international students with schools and recruiters, streamlining the study-abroad application process.
