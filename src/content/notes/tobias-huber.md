# Tobias Huber

Co-author, with [Byrne Hobart](/notes/byrne-hobart), of [Boom — Bubbles & The End of Stagnation](/books/boom-bubbles-and-the-end-of-stagnation) — the pro-bubble book arguing that financial bubbles, far from being pure pathologies, can be the coordinating mechanism that funds ambitious, definite-optimist projects and breaks technological stagnation. In Kyle's notes the book is read through the framing that bubbles channel capital and belief toward moonshot bets, drawing on threads like [Progress Studies](/notes/progress-studies), [Historical Futurism](/notes/historical-futurism), [Mimetics](/notes/mimetics), and [Thymos](/notes/thymos).

**Context:** Tobias Huber is a writer and researcher who, with Byrne Hobart (author of the *The Diff* newsletter), wrote *Boom: Bubbles and the End of Stagnation* (2024). His work sits in the progress-studies / definite-optimism orbit associated with thinkers like [Peter Thiel](/notes/peter-thiel).
