# Deliveroo

The UK food-delivery platform, surfaced in the [Watershed Climate](/notes/watershed-climate) Series B diligence (Index Ventures, 2021) as a customer introduction the diligence team made for Watershed — listed alongside Peloton, Okta, Robinhood, Otrium, Confluent, and Sonos as prospective Watershed customers. The notes record a November 22, 2021 conversation with [Paul Bedford](/notes/paul-bedford) and [David Hancock](/notes/david-hancock) at Deliveroo (Bedford described as head of policy, previously focused on climate tax and ESG/sustainability work), and elsewhere flag Deliveroo as "doing some amazing stuff with DoorDash" in the deal pipeline. Deliveroo functions here as a reference point for Watershed's carbon-measurement go-to-market rather than a diligence subject in its own right.

**Context:** Deliveroo is a London-based online food-delivery company founded in 2013; it went public on the London Stock Exchange in 2021.
