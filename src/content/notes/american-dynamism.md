# American Dynamism

**One-line definition:** *The investment/cultural movement backing companies that build in the national interest — defense, manufacturing, aerospace, and other "hard" American industries.*

## How sources describe it

- Used in [Conviction-Led Contrarianism](/essays/conviction-led-contrarianism) to illustrate hype cycles: [Anduril](/notes/anduril) was "deeply hype countercyclical in 2017, but is now riding a moment of American Dynamism." Kyle's point is that the *moment* may pass, but the founding team's conviction won't.
