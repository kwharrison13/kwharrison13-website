# Jared Diamond

A geographer and historian who appears in Kyle's wiki as the author of two anti-library entries: [Guns, Germs, and Steel](/notes/guns-germs-and-steel) and [The Third Chimpanzee](/notes/the-third-chimpanzee).

**Context:** Jared Diamond is an American geographer, historian, and UCLA professor whose Pulitzer Prize-winning *Guns, Germs, and Steel* (1997) argues that geography and environment, rather than innate differences, explain the divergent fates of human societies.
