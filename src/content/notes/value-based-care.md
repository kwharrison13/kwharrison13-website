**Context:** Value-based care is a healthcare delivery and payment model that ties provider reimbursement to patient outcomes and total cost of care, in contrast to traditional fee-for-service (which pays per procedure or visit). It is one of the larger structural reform threads within U.S. healthcare. (General background.)
