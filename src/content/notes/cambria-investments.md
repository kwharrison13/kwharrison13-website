# Cambria Investments

Investment management firm founded by [Meb Faber](/notes/meb-faber) and Eric Richardson (2007), cited in [Naked Brands- The Future of Finance](/notes/naked-brands-the-future-of-finance) as a case study in transparency-as-moat: "Content is sales. We put out a bunch of the content and people find us." The firm reportedly crossed $1B AUM largely through daily connection with readers and clients, echoing [Charlie Munger](/notes/charlie-munger)'s rule of not selling anything you wouldn't buy yourself.

**Context:** Cambria Investment Management is a California-based asset manager known for ETFs and quantitative, value-tilted global strategies, co-founded by Meb Faber, who is also a prolific writer and podcaster.
