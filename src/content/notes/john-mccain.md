# John McCain

McCain appears in Kyle's notes as [Mitt Romney](/notes/mitt-romney)'s 2008 rival turned kindred pragmatist. In the [Data Over Dogma](/notes/data-over-dogma) concept — drawn from [Romney — A Reckoning](/books/romney-a-reckoning) — McCain and Romney are paired as sharing a pragmatism "skeptical of ideological dogmas," part of the book's institutional-norms thread that judges policy by human outcomes rather than partisan signaling.

**Context:** John McCain (1936–2018) was a U.S. Senator from Arizona, Navy veteran and Vietnam POW, and the 2008 Republican presidential nominee. He cultivated a reputation as a "maverick" willing to break with party orthodoxy.
