# Hims & Hers

**One-line:** *A publicly traded ($HIMS) [Telehealth](/notes/telehealth) company — the legitimate, compliant comparable used to expose [MEDVI](/notes/medvi)'s margins as a compliance-spending tell.*

## What we know

- Did **$2.4B in GLP-1 revenue with 2,442 employees at a 5.5% net margin** — vs. MEDVI's claimed 16.2% margin with 2 people. The gap is roughly what legitimate players spend on compliance and patient care. (source: The $1B Rorschach Test - Research)
- Made a deal with Novo and bought compounding pharmacies + a peptide facility ahead of the FDA crackdown on grey-market / Chinese [GLP-1](/notes/glp-1)s — i.e., invested in durability where MEDVI did not. (source: The $1B Rorschach Test - Research)
- Cited (with Ro and Noom) as proof the telehealth-GLP-1 model isn't inherently a scam: "The model is identical. The regulatory framework is the same. The drugs are the same" — the difference is in execution and compliance. (source: The $1B Rorschach Test - Research)
