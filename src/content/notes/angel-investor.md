# Angel Investor

Angel investing — individual early-stage investment in startups — surfaces in Kyle's notes as a personal-investing thread inside the Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 page, where he logged an angel directory (angels.vc) under an "Investing" note. The reference is a bookmark for finding and participating in angel deals rather than a developed thesis.

**Context:** An angel investor is an individual who invests their own money into early-stage startups, typically at the pre-seed or seed stage, before institutional venture capital. Angels often invest smaller checks than VC funds, sometimes coordinate through syndicates or directories, and may provide mentorship and network access alongside capital.
