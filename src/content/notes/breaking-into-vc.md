# Breaking into VC

**One-line definition:** *A book by [Bradley Miles](/notes/bradley-miles) on getting into venture capital via a research-driven, expertise-first approach rather than waiting for "permission."*

## How sources describe it

- Written by [Bradley Miles](/notes/bradley-miles) with a research-driven thesis: become the recognized expert on a person/topic until you have to be hired. (source: raw/calls/May 31st, 2018.md)
- The book became a "data point" / credential alongside his CoinDesk research, opening doors to VCs and fund offers. (source: raw/calls/May 31st, 2018.md)
