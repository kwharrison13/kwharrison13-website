# Daniel Appel

A strategic-finance leader at [Instructure](/notes/instructure) (the education software company, ~$3B revenue and ~2K employees) whom Kyle spoke with on March 30th, 2022 — see March 30th, 2022 — Daniel Appel (Instructure). Appel had been at Instructure roughly five years and was exploring his next move, with a stated goal of doing something mission-driven rather than purely for money: an early-stage startup, possibly founding one himself, or part-time work. His background is in strategic finance — modeling new product builds in new markets rather than FP&A or close — and since [Thoma Bravo](/notes/thoma-bravo) acquired Instructure he had been working full-time on CorpDev (inorganic growth). He named [Teamshares](/notes/teamshares) (whose mission-and-purpose model matched what he was looking for) and [Synthesis School](/notes/synthesis-school) (asking whether they sold to districts or direct to parents) as companies on his radar.
