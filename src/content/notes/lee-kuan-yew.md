# Lee Kuan Yew

The architect of modern [Singapore](/notes/singapore), central to [The Story of Singapore](/notes/the-story-of-singapore) — which credits him with leading the country from third-world to first in 53 years by running clean systems, courting global capital, paying officials world-class salaries, and sticking to the rule of law: "They are not clean systems; we run clean systems. Their rule of law is wonky; we stick to the law."

**Context:** Lee Kuan Yew (1923–2015) was the first Prime Minister of Singapore (1959–1990) and is widely regarded as the founding father of the modern Singaporean state.
