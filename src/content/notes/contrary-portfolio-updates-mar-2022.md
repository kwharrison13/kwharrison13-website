# Contrary - Portfolio Updates (Mar 2022)

A **collection tag**, not a concept — Kyle used #[Contrary - Portfolio Updates (Mar 2022)](/notes/contrary-portfolio-updates-mar-2022) in his late-March / early-April 2022 Roam daily notes to group the [Contrary Capital](/notes/contrary) portfolio-company investor updates he read while clearing email. Each tagged update was later split into its own private meetings page (all `publish: false`, "Private portfolio update. Never public."). The tag therefore functions as an index over a batch of founder updates from that quarter rather than as a standalone idea.

The updates it gathers include [Aryeo](/notes/aryeo) (Q1, from [Branick Weix](/notes/branick-weix)), [Knoetic](/notes/knoetic) (Q1, from [Joseph Quan](/notes/joseph-quan)), [Uprise](/notes/uprise) (April, from [Jessica Riolfi](/notes/jessica-riolfi)), [Contenda](/notes/contenda) (March, from [Lilly Chen](/notes/lilly-chen)), and [GiveMomentum.com](/notes/givemomentum-com) (March, from [Ari Kagan](/notes/ari-kagan)) — the last notable for a sharp pivot from mid-sized nonprofits to political organizations. Treat this page as a navigational hub; the substance lives on the individual meeting pages.
