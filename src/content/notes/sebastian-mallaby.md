# Sebastian Mallaby

Sebastian Mallaby is the author of *[The Power Law — Venture Capital & the Making of the New Future](/books/the-power-law-venture-capital-and-the-making-of-the-new-future)*, a history of the venture-capital industry. Kyle read the book with active skepticism about the industry it documents — treating Mallaby's account as a record to interrogate rather than endorse. Across the book Mallaby narrates the careers and arguments of figures including [Tom Perkins](/notes/tom-perkins), [Vinod Khosla](/notes/vinod-khosla), [Ben Horowitz](/notes/ben-horowitz), [Peter Thiel](/notes/peter-thiel), [Bill Gurley](/notes/bill-gurley), and [Ho Nam](/notes/ho-nam), and surfaces [Jason Fried](/notes/jason-fried)'s contention that "venture capital kills more businesses than it helps."

**Context:** Sebastian Mallaby is a British-American journalist and author, a senior fellow at the Council on Foreign Relations and former Economist and Washington Post writer, known for financial-history books including *More Money Than God* and *The Man Who Knew* (a biography of Alan Greenspan).
