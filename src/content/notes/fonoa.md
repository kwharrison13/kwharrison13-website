# Fonoa

Fonoa appears in the November 8th, 2021 [Workbounce](/notes/workbounce) pre-brief from [Hannah Seal](/notes/hannah-seal) as part of the deal's sourcing and reference network. David Pevec, head of BD at Fonoa, introduced Index to Workbounce after reviewing the product and wanting to use it internally. In the deal dynamics, [Hannah Seal](/notes/hannah-seal) notes that the team mobilized a number of early-stage founders — Fonoa among them, alongside Sophie Adelman and SeedLegals — to help "sell on our behalf" and move quickly ahead of competing top-tier funds. Fonoa is referenced here as an existing Index-adjacent portfolio/founder relationship, not as a diligence target.

**Context:** Fonoa is a tax-automation platform for digital businesses, providing tax determination, e-invoicing, and reporting APIs. This general background goes beyond what Kyle's note records.
