# 3 Nephi 29

A chapter in the Book of Mormon's third book of Nephi, studied in Kyle's Come Follow Me week on [3 Nephi 27](/notes/3-nephi-27)–[4 Nephi 1](/notes/4-nephi-1) (October 19–25, 2020. 3 Nephi 27–4 Nephi: "There Could Not Be a Happier People"). Kyle reads this chapter (along with [3 Nephi 30](/notes/3-nephi-30)) as Mormon reacting directly to the people he has seen in vision — those who refuse to acknowledge the power of Jesus Christ. He flags verse 6 under the heading "A Bible, We Have a Bible": "wo unto him that shall deny the revelations of the Lord… by revelation, or by prophecy, or by gifts, or by tongues, or by healings, or by the power of the Holy Ghost," and cross-references it with the parallel "A Bible! A Bible!" passage in [2 Nephi 29](/notes/2-nephi-29).

**Context:** In the Book of Mormon, 3 Nephi 29 is a short editorial chapter in which Mormon addresses a future audience, warning the Gentiles not to dismiss the coming forth of the Book of Mormon or deny that God continues to work by revelation and gifts of the Spirit.
