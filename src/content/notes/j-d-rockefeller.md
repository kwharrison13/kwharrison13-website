# J.D. Rockefeller

A passing historical name-drop in [The Almanack of Naval Ravikant](/books/the-almanack-of-naval-ravikant)'s discussion of technology and wealth: the text cites oil as "a technology that made J.D. Rockefeller rich," paired with a parallel mention of cars making [Henry Ford](/notes/henry-ford) rich. No further detail is given.
