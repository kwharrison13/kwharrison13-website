# Gene Hurley

Gene Hurley appears once in the corpus, quoted in Kyle's Hope — Topical Study as the source of a line about pressing forward in faith: "Heavenly Father lives, Jesus is the Christ and the gospel is true. So let's go on!" Kyle pairs the quote with the idea that because of a testimony of the restored gospel we can be happy. The reference gives no further biographical detail; Hurley reads as a personal source of encouragement Kyle recorded alongside [Henry B. Eyring](/notes/henry-b-eyring) and [James E. Faust](/notes/james-e-faust) in the same study.
