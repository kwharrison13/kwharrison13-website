# Zac Townsend

Co-founder, alongside [Max Gasner](/notes/max-gasner), of [Standard.re](/notes/standard-re) — an on-chain life-insurance startup pursuing a Bermuda-first regulatory path to issue crypto-denominated insurance products. Kyle's only logged contact is the June 6th, 2022 — Max Gasner & Zac Townsend (Standard.re) fundraise call, held just after "Luna Wednesday" (the day the Luna collapse blew up the market). On that call Townsend and Gasner walked through how Dragonfly had pulled a signed term sheet and the round had stalled, but framed the capital as optional — *"the capital isn't something we need; we have 2.5 years of runway either way"* — emphasizing instead an 18-month regulatory roadmap (license in Bermuda by end of summer, then Singapore, UAE, and a single-state US license) and a ~10-person team drawn from Slack, Ramp, Airbnb, and the life-insurance industry. They had just hired engineer [Glenn Ren](/notes/glenn-ren) (ex-[Ramp](/notes/ramp)) and still needed Solidity talent.

**Context:** Zac Townsend is a fintech/govtech entrepreneur who previously co-founded Standard Treasury (acquired by Silicon Valley Bank), served as California's first Chief Data Officer, and worked at McKinsey; he co-founded the crypto life-insurance company Standard before its later pivots. (General background; Kyle's notes attest only the Standard.re fundraise call.)
