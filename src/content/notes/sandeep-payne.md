# Sandeep Payne

Sandeep Payne appears in Kyle's notes as a PayPal-era voice quoted in [The Founders](/books/the-founders) (Jimmy Soni's history of the company) on [Elon Musk](/notes/elon-musk)'s gift for [Storytelling as Leadership](/notes/storytelling-as-leadership). Payne's observation is one of the page's anchor quotes for the idea that, in fundraising and recruiting, narrative outweighs the math: "What was more potent than the mathematical exercise was the story. And Elon was very good at pointing to the future — just as he is today — and saying the objective is over there, and I know it's over there, and we should all go over there." Both Kyle's [Elon Musk](/notes/elon-musk) page and the [Storytelling as Leadership](/notes/storytelling-as-leadership) concept page lean on this line to make the case that even in "hyperrational Silicon Valley, vision weighed as much as data."
