# Persona

[Persona](/notes/persona) is an identity-verification (IDV / KYC) company that recurs across Kyle's 2021 diligence-research and call notes as one of the converging players in the identity space. In a November 2021 call with founder Rick Song, Kyle captured Persona's customer health and pipeline: [Robinhood](/notes/robinhood) (struggling with user growth, which flows through to Persona) and DoorDash ($DASH) (expanding into multiple use cases) as anchor customers; a pursued deal with [Apple ($AAPL)](/notes/apple) alongside CLEAR for airlines (~60% likelihood); and inbound interest from Fidelity and most of the major banks. Song framed the competitive set as "all starting to collide in the same strategy" — Alloy (Identity), Jumio, Socure (pushing aggressively into fintech), and Veriff.

In the Identity & Access Management Diligence — 2021 notes, Persona is listed among the KYC vendors (with [Onfido](/notes/onfido)) mapped during the IAM landscape work. It also surfaces in the Pave Diligence — 2021 file both as a deal contact (Natalie Gavello at Persona, dated October 11th 2021) and as a company [Pave](/notes/pave) introduced to its own customers — placing Persona inside Kyle's broader sourcing network.

**Context:** Persona is a San Francisco-based identity verification and compliance platform founded by Rick Song and Charles Yeh, used by companies to run KYC, fraud, and onboarding checks via a configurable building-block API.
