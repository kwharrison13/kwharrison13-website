# Gerald N. Lund

Cited in Kyle's The Godhead gospel-study notes for his framework "The Continuum of Revelation," which the notes use to organize how the Spirit communicates — distinguishing revelation given "to the mind" (thoughts, aha moments, remembrance, voice in the mind, dreams, enlightenment, discernment, testimony) from revelation given "to the heart" (peace, feeling that something is right, burning, stupor, negative feelings). His model anchors that page's section on the Comforter and the continuum of revelation.

**Context:** Gerald N. Lund is a Latter-day Saint author and educator, known for the *Work and the Glory* historical-fiction series and for gospel-doctrine writing; he later served as a General Authority Seventy of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.
