# Lee Iacocca

American auto executive who surfaces in Kyle's notes on [Charlie Munger — The Psychology of Human Misjudgment](/notes/charlie-munger-the-psychology-of-human-misjudgment), where Kyle recalls the [Ford vs. Ferrari](/notes/ford-vs-ferrari) scene of Iacocca telling the Ford board "We've been thinking wrong" — an example of the board reacting negatively to bad news rather than to its merits.

**Context:** Lee Iacocca (1924–2019) was an American automobile executive best known for championing the Ford Mustang and the Ford Pinto at Ford, and later for engineering Chrysler's turnaround as its CEO in the late 1970s and 1980s, including securing federal loan guarantees that saved the company from bankruptcy.
