# Intermountain Healthcare

Intermountain Healthcare appears in Kyle's [Watershed Climate](/notes/watershed-climate) Series B diligence (2021) as one of the named enterprise participants in Watershed's sustainability ecosystem — listed via [Nickolas Mark](/notes/nickolas-mark) at Intermountain Healthcare among a roster of corporate climate leads (alongside contacts at [Apple ($AAPL)](/notes/apple), [Salesforce ($CRM)](/notes/salesforce), [BlackRock](/notes/blackrock), [Google](/notes/google), [Goldman Sachs](/notes/goldman-sachs), [Microsoft](/notes/microsoft), [Walmart](/notes/walmart), and others). In the diligence framing it reads as a health-system customer / sustainability-outreach target rather than a deeply analyzed entity — its presence signals the breadth of large enterprises engaging with Watershed's carbon-measurement platform.

**Context:** Intermountain Healthcare (now Intermountain Health) is a large not-for-profit integrated health system headquartered in Utah, frequently cited as a model for value-based, lower-cost care delivery.
