# Business is the New Sports

"Business is the New Sports" is a [Packy McCormick](/notes/packy-mccormick) essay published in his [Not Boring](/notes/not-boring) newsletter, which Kyle cites in his [Open-Source Knowledge](/essays/open-source-knowledge) research as an example of the emerging public, participatory layer around investing. The essay's argument, as Kyle records it: once people understand the game, they get invested in how it's played — and "[intrinsic motivation](/notes/intrinsic-motivation) is the currency of the commons." The piece is the kind of long, entertaining company narrative that turns following business into spectator culture, akin to following a sport.
