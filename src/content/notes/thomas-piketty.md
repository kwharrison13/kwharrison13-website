# Thomas Piketty

**One-line:** *French economist, author of* Capital in the Twenty-First Century*. Central thesis: when the rate of return on capital exceeds the growth rate of the economy (**r > g**), inherited wealth compounds faster than output and income, widening inequality between capital and labor.*
