# Ether 3

In Kyle's [gospel-study](/notes/gospel-study) notes, **Ether 3** is the chapter of the [Brother of Jared](/notes/brother-of-jared) seeing the finger — and then the body — of the premortal Christ, and it carries two distinct lessons. In Faith — Topical Study, it shows faith brought to its limit by *action*: the Brother of Jared went from being chastened three hours for failing to pray (Ether 2:14) to seeing the finger of Christ (3:6) precisely by exercising the faith he already had — "Behold, O Lord, thou canst do this… we know that thou art able to show forth great power" (3:5). In Eye single to the glory of God, the same episode illustrates that as faith grows so does our **capacity to bear God's glory**: like the Brother of Jared the believer can reach the point where "he could no longer be kept without the veil" (3:13, 26), and "because thou knowest these things, ye are redeemed from the fall" becomes the link between revealed knowledge and accountability.

3 Answers To Every Prayer places Ether 3 alongside Ether 2 as the **three problems of the barges** (breathing, light, steering) — the "Light" problem being the one where God tells the Brother of Jared to use his own agency and study it out, modeling one of the three ways God answers prayer. The chapter sits inside the November 9–15, 2020. Ether 1–5: "Rend That Veil of Unbelief" Come Follow Me study.

**Context:** Ether chapter 3 is part of the Book of Mormon's account of the Jaredites: the Brother of Jared asks the Lord to touch sixteen molten stones to give light inside the sealed barges, and his faith is so great that he sees the Lord's finger and is then shown the Lord's spirit body — a foundational Book of Mormon theophany.
