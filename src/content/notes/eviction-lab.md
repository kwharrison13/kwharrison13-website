# Eviction Lab

The research project led by sociologist [Matthew Desmond](/notes/matthew-desmond), the author of [Evicted](/books/evicted). In Kyle's corpus it appears as part of Desmond's "ongoing work on eviction data" — flagged on the [Matthew Desmond](/notes/matthew-desmond) page as central to his work but not yet otherwise documented in the wiki (source: [Matthew Desmond](/notes/matthew-desmond)). It extends the data-openness instinct already visible in [Evicted](/books/evicted), where Desmond released his Milwaukee survey data publicly through the Harvard Dataverse Network.

**Context:** The Eviction Lab is a team based at Princeton University, founded by Matthew Desmond, that builds the first nationwide database of evictions in the United States and publishes eviction rates and maps as a public research resource.
