# LivePerson

LivePerson appears in Kyle's notes as a component of an enterprise contact-center tech stack. In the Cresta.ai - Diligence, [Klasey Gage](/notes/klasey-gage) (Head of Global Customer Support at [Adobe](/notes/adobe)) describes Adobe running **[LivePerson](/notes/liveperson) for chat plus [Zendesk](/notes/zendesk)** as its existing support stack, with Cresta layered on the chat side for agent coaching. The mention is contextual — establishing the incumbent tooling Cresta sits alongside rather than evaluating LivePerson itself.

**Context:** LivePerson is a US-based conversational-commerce and messaging software company, founded in 1995, known for its live-chat and AI-powered messaging platform (Conversational Cloud) for customer engagement.
