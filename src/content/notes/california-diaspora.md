# California Diaspora

The outflow of residents from California — part of a broader pattern of movement from high cost-of-living to low cost-of-living places.

## Key observations

*(From a tweet thread by Priya Rose, April 25, 2020, tagged #Prosperable)*

- The California Diaspora is part of a **larger diaspora from high-COL to low-COL places**.
- A notable side effect: blue-state residents are moving to red states, turning them into swing states.
- Probable drivers of the asymmetry: highly educated people move *to* California while young, lower-income people leave; other states have lower incomes relative to California, so the broader U.S. population can't afford to move there.
