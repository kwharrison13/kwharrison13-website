# Adam Neumann

**One-line:** *WeWork founder, cited in [The Hits Business](/essays/the-hits-business) as the archetype of "big, soulless, monolithic" company-building optimized for pure profit motive.*

## What we know

- [WeWork](/notes/wework) founder. Kyle quotes Matt Levine's [summary](https://stansberryresearch.com/articles/adam-neumanns-last-laugh): Neumann "figured out maybe the best and funniest way anyone has ever made money in the history of capitalism, which is 'act crazy around Masayoshi Son until money spews out of him.'"
- "Neumann's at it again" — his next venture (Kyle's [The Rise of The Cash Man](https://investing1012dot0.substack.com/p/the-rise-of-the-cash-man)).
