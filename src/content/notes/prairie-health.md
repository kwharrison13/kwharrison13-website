# Prairie Health

Prairie Health is a data-driven telemedicine company for anxiety and depression, tracked by Kyle as a portfolio company (it appears in his January 11th, 2022 reading of a Q4 2021 [Contrary](/notes/contrary) investor update). Per those notes, Prairie's care model aims to reduce the trial-and-error approach to psychiatric medication: it offers members a genetic test in their first month to help partner psychiatrists set the right starting dosage, then pairs them with "Care Partners" (trained mental-health professionals) for ongoing counseling over text between monthly psychiatry appointments. The notes name two in-house products — the "Prairie Ecosystem" one-stop-shop for acuity-appropriate care, and "PrairiePrecision," a pharmacogenomic interpretation platform and clinical-decision-support tool. By March 30th, 2022, Kyle recorded that Prairie Health had been acquired by [Carbon Health](/notes/carbon-health) (see March 30th, 2022 — Prairie Health Acquired by Carbon Health).

**Context:** Prairie Health was a telepsychiatry startup focused on personalized, pharmacogenomics-informed treatment for depression and anxiety; it was acquired by primary-care provider Carbon Health in 2022.
