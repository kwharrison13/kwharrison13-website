# Brian Chesky

Brian Chesky appears in Kyle's notes on [Product Led Growth — The End User Era](/books/product-led-growth-the-end-user-era) as the source of one of the book's core product maxims: **"Build something 100 people love, not something 1,000 people kind of like."** He is cited as CEO of Airbnb. The quote is used to make the product-led-growth case that intensity of love for a product (a small base of devoted users) beats broad, lukewarm appeal — the seed condition for organic, end-user-driven adoption.

**Context:** Brian Chesky is the co-founder and CEO of Airbnb. The "100 people who love it" formulation is a well-known piece of startup advice associated with Paul Graham and Y Combinator that Chesky has frequently repeated and championed.
