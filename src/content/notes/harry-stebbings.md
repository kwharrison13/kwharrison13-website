# Harry Stebbings

Host of the venture podcast 20VC (The Twenty Minute VC) and a recurring node across Kyle's venture-world notes. Kyle prepped for and appeared on the show in the September 13th, 2022 — Harry Stebbings Podcast interview on the future of venture, framed around the premise that "venture capital is a product." That conversation is the source of several ideas in Kyle's corpus — the [Jobs to Be Done](/notes/jobs-to-be-done) reframe of venture ("what job am I hiring this firm for?"), the [TAM Arbitrage](/notes/tam-arbitrage) lesson from [Coatue](/notes/coatue) (do the work to understand a market better than anyone, then pay up), and [Contrary](/notes/contrary)'s community-first-then-fund growth model (a ~100-person founder community before a fund, scaling past $100M AUM).

Beyond hosting Kyle, Stebbings functions as a connective figure in the ecosystem. Vedika Jain broke into venture partly by sending him her Fantasy Angel Investing memos — when Ryan Hoover was filling a role at Weekend Fund, Harry recommended her (June 24th, 2021 — Vedika Jain (Weekend Fund)). His 20VC interview with [Chris Sacca](/notes/chris-sacca) (the "coming out of retirement" conversation) is a primary source for Kyle's [Renegade Spotlight — Lowercarbon](/essays/lowercarbon) essay research, where Sacca tells Harry he'll "get way richer" from climate than tech. And Kyle's essay [Natural Selection Among Startups](/essays/natural-selection-among-startups) grew out of a deleted Stebbings tweet about healthy talent recycling among startups. The [Mark Goldberg](/notes/mark-goldberg) "best cold email" origin story is itself recounted as part of the 20VC prep.

**Context:** Harry Stebbings is a British venture capitalist and podcaster who founded The Twenty Minute VC (20VC), one of the most widely listened-to venture-capital podcasts, and later raised his own fund, 20VC Fund.
