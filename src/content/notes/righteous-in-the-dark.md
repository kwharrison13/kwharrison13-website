# Righteous in the Dark

The disposition to do right when no one is watching and no external structure compels it — to "act independently and yet humbly" before God. [Brigham Young](/notes/brigham-young): "man is destined to be a God… **It is the way it is because we must learn to be righteous in the dark.**" For Kyle this is the deepest thing his parents taught him — not how to tract or teach or study, but a self-reliance that let him "find what I needed, no matter the circumstance" (see [What I Want My Son to Know before He Leaves on His Mission](/notes/what-i-want-my-son-to-know-before-he-leaves-on-his-mission)).
