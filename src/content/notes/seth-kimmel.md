# Seth Kimmel

A colleague Kyle worked with at [Contrary Capital](/notes/contrary) on the firm's data and knowledge-management infrastructure. In a January 31st, 2022 working session, Kimmel walked Kyle through the PRISM data architecture — a decoupled Snowflake instance pulling in Harmonic.ai data, an Airtable replica, and Proxycurl enrichment of the VP/Fellow universe — designed to flag when interesting people leave companies or start new things, and ultimately to become "one massive observable table of the Contrary universe" that scores people on quality and network strength. The conversation also covered hiring Hiroki Hayama and the broader thesis of building feedback loops for [Data in Investing](/notes/data-in-investing). In a March 30th, 2022 1x1, the two discussed needing "culture and systems for capturing information" — a good start on collecting data, but the need for a good output for what to do with it (e.g., answering questions).
