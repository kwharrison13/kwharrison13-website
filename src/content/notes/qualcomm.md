# Qualcomm

**One-line definition:** *Merchant-silicon chip designer; the "lowest common denominator" foil to Apple's bespoke A-series in the chip-integration debate.*

## How sources describe it

- Has a "lowest common denominator problem": even with an advanced chip, it must segment across many customers (high-end/low-end, every feature), a liability versus [Apple](/notes/apple) optimizing its A-series for exactly its own needs (source: [Apple Earnings, An Interview with Jay Goldberg About Chips and Intel](/notes/apple-earnings-an-interview-with-jay-goldberg-about-chips-and-intel)).
