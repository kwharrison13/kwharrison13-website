# Bruce Patton

Co-author (with [Roger Fisher](/notes/roger-fisher) and [William Ury](/notes/william-ury)) of [Getting to Yes](/notes/getting-to-yes), a book in Kyle's anti-library (books-to-read).

**Context:** Bruce Patton is a co-founder of the Harvard Negotiation Project and a deputy director there; he co-authored *Getting to Yes* and later co-wrote *Difficult Conversations*.
