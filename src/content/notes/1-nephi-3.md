# 1 Nephi 3

A chapter in the Book of Mormon's first book of Nephi, cited as a cross-reference in Kyle's Come Follow Me study 1 Nephi 16–22 — I Will Prepare the Way before You. There it feeds the running case study on [Lessons From Laman and Lemuel](/notes/lessons-from-laman-and-lemuel): [1 Nephi 3](/notes/1-nephi-3):28 is quoted as an early instance of the elder brothers' anger — "Laman was angry with me, and also with my father... they did smite us even with a rod" — establishing the pattern of murmuring and rebellion the page traces across First Nephi.

**Context:** 1 Nephi 3 is the account of Lehi's sons returning to Jerusalem to obtain the brass plates from Laban, including Nephi's resolve to "go and do the things which the Lord hath commanded." The verse Kyle pulls (3:28) comes after the brothers' first failed attempt, when an angel intervenes.
