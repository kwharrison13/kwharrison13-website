# Labor Day

**One-line definition:** *US federal holiday honoring the labor movement and workers' contributions ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Labor_Day)) — and, in Kyle's writing, the recurring annual prompt to finally write about unions.*

## How sources describe it

- The framing device of Unions, Unions Everywhere - Research: *"Every Labor Day since 2015, I've thought 'I'd like to write about this stuff.' Yet, every Labor Day comes and goes, mocking me for my inadequacy."*
- Paired with the Kyle & Camden exchange about emotional distance from such holidays: *"I don't even know what Labor Day is for." "Yeah, neither do I."* — the observation that the holiday's meaning has faded, mirroring the fade of [Unions](/notes/unions) themselves.
