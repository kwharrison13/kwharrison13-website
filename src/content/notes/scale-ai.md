# Scale AI

In Kyle's wiki, Scale AI surfaces only as a data point inside the Pave Diligence — 2021 dossier. There it is named as one of "40+ [Index](/notes/index-ventures) portfolio companies" — alongside [Instabase](/notes/instabase), [Anrok](/notes/anrok), [Discord](/notes/discord), [Newfront Insurance](/notes/newfront-insurance), [Boulevard](/notes/boulevard), and [Applyboard](/notes/applyboard) — that contribute compensation data into [Pave](/notes/pave)'s give-to-get network. It's cited as evidence that Pave had already pulled leading venture-backed companies into its real-time comp-benchmarking data set, reinforcing the network-effects moat thesis. No standalone diligence on Scale AI itself appears in the corpus.

**Context:** Scale AI is a San Francisco data-labeling and AI-infrastructure company founded by Alexandr Wang in 2016, known for providing training-data and human-feedback services to AI/ML teams and the U.S. government.
