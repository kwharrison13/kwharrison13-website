# Mike Volpi

Mike Volpi is a partner at [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) who recurs across Kyle's sourcing, diligence, and essay research from Kyle's time at Index. He appears in the partnership on the December 13th, 2021 — NCX Partner Presentation, where he pushed back on carbon-capture economics — most projects "still in academia and ridiculously more expensive," with tree-planting offsets taking 15+ years to matter, and verification as the key exposure. Kyle shared the Actively seed memo with Volpi, Bryan Offutt, and Erin Price-Wright for their read; the three partners' consensus was that the MLOps/viz space was too crowded to differentiate, warranting at most a small founder-bet check (October 28th, 2021 — Actively Memo (Mike Volpi, Bryan Offutt, Erin Price-Wright)).

Volpi's investing worldview maps onto Kyle's venture-industry essays. In a call captured in The Productization of Venture Capital - Research, Volpi likened the venture industry's consolidation to investment banking — "you really only want to be a bulge bracket bank" — anticipating that a handful of firms will become true platforms (Agglomerators). He also surfaces in Starburst - Diligence, where he met Jay Parikh to help bring the Presto/Facebook and Presto/Starburst forks closer together, and in the December 13th, 2021 — Zabie Elmgren (Human Capital) notes regarding an Arthur.ai Series A lead.

**Context:** Mike Volpi is a longtime general partner at Index Ventures and a former Chief Strategy Officer at Cisco, known for early-stage and infrastructure investments including Confluent, Sonos, Aurora, and Wealthfront.
