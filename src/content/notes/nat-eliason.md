# Nat Eliason

A writer and marketer cited in Kyle's writing-craft long reads. He is the origin of the "Wiki Strategy" term — interlinking evergreen content into a compounding network — that anchors [Hyper Publishing- The Wiki Strategy](/notes/hyper-publishing-the-wiki-strategy), and the source of the "TK" placeholder tactic and much of the editing toolkit in [Riding the Writing Wave](/notes/riding-the-writing-wave).

**Context:** Nat Eliason is a writer, entrepreneur, and online-course creator known for essays on writing, marketing, note-taking, and personal knowledge management.
