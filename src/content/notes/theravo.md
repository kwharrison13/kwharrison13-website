# Theravo

The mental-health agency that [Nicole Kleiman-Reck](/notes/nicole-kleiman-reck) contracted through for 11 years before going independent. Per her September 2021 expert-network call (an [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) sourcing/diligence call on the [Grow Therapy](/notes/grow-therapy) provider experience), she ran teletherapy through Theravo until COVID, when she struck out on her own and began taking [Grow Therapy](/notes/grow-therapy) clients in April 2021. Theravo is the "before" in her career arc — the legacy agency model that platform-based teletherapy ([Grow Therapy](/notes/grow-therapy), BetterHelp) later displaced for her.
