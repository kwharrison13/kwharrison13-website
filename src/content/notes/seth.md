# Seth

**One-line:** *Third named son of [Adam](/notes/adam) and [Eve](/notes/eve), appointed "another seed instead of [Abel](/notes/abel), whom [Cain](/notes/cain) slew" (Genesis 4).*

## What we know

- Born to Adam and Eve, who named him Seth: "For God, said she, hath appointed me another seed instead of Abel, whom Cain slew" (Genesis 4:25).
- His son Enos is born, after which "began men to call upon the name of the LORD" (Genesis 4:26).

## Mentioned in

- Genesis 4 — born in Abel's place; father of Enos
