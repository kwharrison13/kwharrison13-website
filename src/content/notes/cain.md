# Cain

**One-line:** *Firstborn son of [Adam](/notes/adam) and [Eve](/notes/eve), a tiller of the ground, whose offering the Lord did not respect and who slew his brother [Abel](/notes/abel) (Genesis 4).*

## What we know

- Firstborn of Adam and Eve; "a tiller of the ground" (Genesis 4:2).
- Brought of the fruit of the ground as an offering, but the Lord "had not respect" to it — a verse Kyle highlighted (Genesis 4:3–5).
- Wroth, he rose up against [Abel](/notes/abel) in the field and slew him (Genesis 4:8).
- Cursed from the earth to be "a fugitive and a vagabond"; the Lord set a mark upon him lest any finding him should kill him (Genesis 4:11–15).
- Dwelt in the land of Nod, east of Eden; his line runs through Enoch to Lamech (Genesis 4:16–24).
