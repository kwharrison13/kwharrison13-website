# Permanent Capital

**One-line definition:** *Investor capital that can't be redeemed at will, giving a manager the freedom to act long-term.*

## How it shows up

- [Morgan Housel](/notes/morgan-housel)'s flywheel: content → trust → permanent capital → durable advantage. "[Buffett](/notes/warren-buffett) and [Marks](/notes/howard-marks) used content to instill trust, trust gave them permanent capital, and permanent capital gave them a massive financial advantage."
