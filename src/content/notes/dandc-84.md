# D&C 84

[D&C 84](/notes/dandc-84) is a Doctrine and Covenants section that recurs across Kyle's gospel study as a touchstone for **priesthood, ordinances, and submission of the will**. The most cited verse is **84:20** — "in the ordinances thereof, the power of godliness is manifest" — which [Russell M. Nelson](/notes/russell-m-nelson) uses in Christ Is Risen; Faith in Him Will Move Mountains — Russell M. Nelson as one of five ways to develop faith (partaking of sacred ordinances worthily). The Abrahamic Covenant study anchors the **Oath and Covenant of the Priesthood** to 84:33–39 and reads 84:20–21 as evidence that "the power of the priesthood is firmly fixed in the family." [Neal A. Maxwell](/notes/neal-a-maxwell) draws on **84:38** — receiving "all that He hath" — in 8 Points of the Gospel to make the point that surrendering our will to God yields an enhanced, more capable individuality ("What an exchange rate!").

Other verses thread through the Miscellaneous Scriptures web: **84:33–34** (magnifying callings and becoming "the elect of God") and **84:85** ("take no thought beforehand," with Revelation — Topical Study reading it as God revealing more once we conform to what He's already revealed). In Missionary Work — Topical Study, **84:88** frames the missionary's struggles as the Lord bearing him up — angels sent so he doesn't "dash [his] foot against a stone." It also appears in the Christian Reformation, Zion, and Seekers notes (84:46–51).

**Context:** Doctrine and Covenants section 84 is a revelation of Joseph Smith (1832) in the scriptural canon of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, often called the "revelation on priesthood," containing the Oath and Covenant of the Priesthood.

## Where this appears

- Christ Is Risen; Faith in Him Will Move Mountains — Russell M. Nelson — 84:20 ("in the ordinances thereof, the power of godliness is manifest") as a means to develop faith.
- Abrahamic Covenant — the Oath and Covenant of the Priesthood (84:33–39); priesthood "firmly fixed in the family" (84:20–21).
- 8 Points of the Gospel — Neal A. Maxwell on 84:38, receiving "all that He hath" through submission of will.
- Miscellaneous Scriptures — 84:33–34 (magnifying callings, the elect of God) and 84:85 ("take no thought beforehand").
- Revelation — Topical Study — 84:85 read as God revealing more once we conform.
- Missionary Work — Topical Study — 84:88, struggles as growth and angels bearing the missionary up.
- Christian Reformation, Zion, and Seekers — 84:46–51 on denying the faith and being wrapped in sin.
