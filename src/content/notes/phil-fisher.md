# Phil Fisher

Phil Fisher is the growth-investing pioneer whose "15 Questions" checklist for evaluating a company is recorded in Kyle's [Investment Frameworks](/notes/investment-frameworks) learning page.

**Context:** Philip A. Fisher (1907–2004) was an American investor and author of *Common Stocks and Uncommon Profits*, where he laid out his "Fifteen Points to Look for in a Common Stock." A foundational figure in growth and qualitative ("scuttlebutt") investing, his thinking heavily influenced Warren Buffett.
