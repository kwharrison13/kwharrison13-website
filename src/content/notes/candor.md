# Candor

A Contrary Capital portfolio company founded by [Kelsey Bishop](/notes/kelsey-bishop), tracked through Kyle's investor-update reading from early-to-late 2022. Across the monthly updates Kyle logged, Candor "toed a line between consumer and HR tech" before deciding to "tak[e] a swing squarely in consumer and making Candor the most beautiful ReadMe ever made" (July 18th, 2022 daily log, quoting the June update). The product positions itself as a team-building / professional-reflection tool: by mid-2022 Bishop was promoting a "Candor Offsite Playbook for Founders" and pitching the product for "team building activities during offsites so that teams can connect and reflect on what's working and what's not" (August 29th, 2022 daily log). Kyle read these updates passively while clearing emails — the February update and the September update (logged November 7th, 2022) — all tagged into Contrary's portfolio-update stream.

**Context:** Candor (joincandor.com) is a startup associated with founder Kelsey Bishop, building software around workplace feedback, team connection, and professional self-presentation ("ReadMe"-style profiles).
