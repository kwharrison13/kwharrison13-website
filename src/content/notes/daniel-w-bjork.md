# Daniel W. Bjork

Author cited in Kyle's notes on [Charlie Munger — A Lesson on Elementary Worldly Wisdom, Revisited](/notes/charlie-munger-a-lesson-on-elementary-worldly-wisdom-revisited) as the writer of the [B.F. Skinner](/notes/b-f-skinner) biography referenced in a discussion of how humans learn by biologically-enhanced trial and error.

**Context:** Daniel W. Bjork is an American historian and biographer, author of *B. F. Skinner: A Life* (1993), a biography of the influential behaviorist psychologist.
