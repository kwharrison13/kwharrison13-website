# Jacob 1

In Kyle's Scripture Study — Topical Study, [Jacob 1](/notes/jacob-1) supplies the note's image of teaching as a sacred office with real accountability. He quotes [Jacob 1](/notes/jacob-1):19 — those who "magnify our office unto the Lord" take on "the responsibility, answering the sins of the people upon our own heads if we did not teach them the word of God with all diligence; wherefore by laboring with our might their blood might not come upon our garments." Kyle frames it as a sobering but true charge: never forget the potential of those you teach, who "stand as God's finest, the choice, the elect." The verse pairs in the study with [Mosiah 2](/notes/mosiah-2):29 and [Daniel Webster](/notes/daniel-webster)'s line about working "upon immortal minds."
