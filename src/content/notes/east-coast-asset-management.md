# East Coast Asset Management

East Coast Asset Management is the investment firm co-founded by [Christopher Begg](/notes/christopher-begg), who serves as its CEO and Chief Investment Officer. It surfaces in [On the Nature of Long-Term Holds](/notes/on-the-nature-of-long-term-holds) via Begg's quoted view that investing is fundamentally about maximizing the rate of [Compounding](/notes/compounding).

## Where this appears

- [On the Nature of Long-Term Holds](/notes/on-the-nature-of-long-term-holds) — named as Christopher Begg's firm in the context of his compounding-focused investing philosophy.
