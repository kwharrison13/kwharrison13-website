# Lightning Talks

Lightning Talks surfaced in Kyle's 1x1 with Luke Piette as an analogue for an informal knowledge-transfer ritual: John-Luca runs "Knowledge Transfer" sessions that Kyle likened to [Lightning Talks](/notes/lightning-talks) and tagged with [Starting a Junto](/notes/starting-a-junto). The grounded example was John-Luca presenting on [Credit Card Churning](/notes/credit-card-churning) — gaming credit-card point systems for cash back and air miles — with the explicit rule that the topic "has to be outside of what you're working on." The idea is short, informal peer talks on tangential interests as a community/learning device.

**Context:** "Lightning talks" is a common format at conferences and meetups — very short (typically 5-minute) presentations given in rapid succession, letting many speakers share ideas quickly.
