# Addepar

Addepar appears in Pave Diligence — 2021 as a customer of [Pave](/notes/pave). In the customer-feedback section, Pave's founder names Addepar (alongside Flipkart and Ro) as a happy customer who loves the product, contrasting it against the "nightmare" status-quo process of pulling HRIS reports and building compensation spreadsheets by hand. Addepar is cited as evidence of product-market traction in Kyle's diligence.

**Context:** Addepar is a wealth-management technology company that provides a software platform for aggregating, analyzing, and reporting on investment portfolios, used by financial advisors, family offices, and institutions.

## Where this appears

- Pave Diligence — 2021 — named as a satisfied [Pave](/notes/pave) customer in the diligence's customer-feedback section.
