# Crowdsourced News

The idea that distributed, independent writers now out-explain legacy newspapers on technical subjects — the expertise gap being widest in technical and scientific fields. The canonical example: after the second Boeing 737 Max crash, no-name pilots and obscure-blog writers out-reported the world's best papers.
