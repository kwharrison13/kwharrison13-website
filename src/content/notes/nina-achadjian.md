# Nina Achadjian

A partner at [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) and one of Kyle's recurring deal-sourcing/diligence contacts, focused on **vertical SaaS**. In a September 2021 deal review she walked Kyle through three companies: [Arrow XYZ](/notes/arrow-xyz) (auto-dealership software — ~40% of dealerships already use something, the product "can sit on top of the CRM as a Trojan horse," she "wouldn't go above $100M post," with open questions on monthly contracts and why a dealership would buy a *second* CRM unless switching); [Monograph](/notes/monograph) (architecture-firm practice-management software, "$120K and got $120M post"); and [Openspace.ai](/notes/openspace-ai) (construction-site capture, flagged as a crowded market — drones, crane cameras, walkie-talkie robots — with open questions on defensibility, margin, and greenfield-vs-switching sales) (September 22nd, 2021 — Nina Achadjian (Index Ventures)).

She authored the [Seso](/notes/seso) Series A brief Kyle read ahead of an Index partnership presentation: vertical software for agricultural workforce management starting with H-2A migrant-worker onboarding/compliance, with a recommendation to invest $15M of a $17M round at $120M post for 12.5%, against an insider Founders Fund term sheet (December 13th, 2021 — Seso (Series A)). She is listed among the Index partnership in the December 13th, 2021 — NCX Partner Presentation, and remained on Kyle's [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) outreach list for an SF trip into 2024 (January 24th, 2024 — Outreach for SF Trip).

**Context:** Nina Achadjian is a Partner at Index Ventures, where she invests in vertical SaaS and AI/software companies; her portfolio has included companies in construction, logistics, and other industry-specific software.
