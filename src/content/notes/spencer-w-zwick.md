# Spencer W. Zwick

Spencer W. Zwick appears in Kyle's General Conference — October 2021 notes within the discussion of [Susan H. Porter](/notes/susan-h-porter)'s talk on the love of God. The note records that [Clinton D. Topham](/notes/clinton-d-topham) asked Spencer W. Zwick about what Kyle marks as the "best talk on the love of God scripture" — placing Zwick in the conversation around that talk's themes ([1 Nephi 11](/notes/1-nephi-11), the tree of life as the love of God). The source captures only this mention.

**Context:** Spencer W. Zwick is an American businessman and Latter-day Saint, co-founder of the investment firm Solamere Capital and a longtime political finance aide to Mitt Romney.
