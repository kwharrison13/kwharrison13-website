# Follow-Up

A learning-and-development idea drawn from [The Expertise Economy](/books/the-expertise-economy): post-training reinforcement is what makes new skills stick. The book frames its absence as a failure mode — "Without this follow-up, employees will likely forget what they've learned after a few months and consequently fail to apply their knowledge on the job." In Kyle's reading it sits with the book's broader argument that one-off training events are insufficient and that durable capability-building requires sustained reinforcement after the initial instruction.
