# Les Schwab

Author of [Les Schwab Pride in Performance](/notes/les-schwab-pride-in-performance), his business memoir, in Kyle's anti-library (books-to-read).

**Context:** Les Schwab (1917–2007) was an American entrepreneur who built Les Schwab Tire Centers from a single Oregon store into a large Pacific Northwest tire-retail chain, famous for its profit-sharing culture. His memoir *Pride in Performance: Keep It Going!* recounts the company's growth.
