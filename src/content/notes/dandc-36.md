# D&C 36

Section 36 of the Doctrine and Covenants surfaces in two of Kyle's gospel-study notes. In Radiating Your Example he reads D&C 36:4–7 as proof that **missions are a commandment of the [Melchizedek priesthood](/notes/priesthood)** — predating [Spencer W. Kimball](/notes/spencer-w-kimball)'s "every young man should serve a mission" call by more than a century — and ties it to the title "Elder," one of only two offices (missionaries and General Authorities) so named, each representing Christ 24/7.

In Spiritual Gifts he draws on D&C 36:2, where the Lord promises His Spirit will be upon the recipient — which Kyle reads as **the Comforter, comforting the heavy laden.**

## Where this appears

- Radiating Your Example — D&C 36:4–7 cited as evidence that missions are a Melchizedek-priesthood commandment, predating Spencer W. Kimball's call to service
- Spiritual Gifts — D&C 36:2 read as the Comforter comforting the heavy laden
