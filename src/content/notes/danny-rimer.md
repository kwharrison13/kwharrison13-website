# Danny Rimer

A partner at [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) who participated in the December 13th, 2021 — NCX Partner Presentation for the [NCX](/notes/ncx) (Natural Capital Exchange) Series B. In the partner discussion he voiced "heartache over Series B," arguing that the round meant paying up for a lot of risk without much more clarity than a Series A would have offered. [Martin Mignot](/notes/martin-mignot) aligned with the same position.

**Context:** Danny Rimer is a longtime general partner at Index Ventures, known for early investments in companies including Dropbox, Figma, and Discord.
