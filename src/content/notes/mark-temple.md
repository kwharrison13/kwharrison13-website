# Mark Temple

A contributor to Kyle's The Talent Vortex - Research, previously at the European venture firm [Atomico](/notes/atomico). In a Contrary Slack thread feeding that research, Temple pointed to [Keith Rabois](/notes/keith-rabois)'s methods for sizing up talent and tapping the Thiel/PayPal network as the key framing — specifically the "barrels, not ammunition" idea (identifying and rewarding the rare people who can independently drive outcomes), with [Delian Asparouhov](http://delian.io/lessons-5)'s summary noted as a less in-depth framework by comparison. The [Atomico](/notes/atomico) page records Temple only as background — Atomico itself is mentioned as his prior employer, not analyzed.
