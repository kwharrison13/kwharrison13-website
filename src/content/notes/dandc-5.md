# D&C 5

Section 5 of the [Doctrine and Covenants](/notes/doctrine-and-covenants) appears in Kyle's gospel study in two contexts. In Publication of the Book of Mormon, it is the revelation where [Martin Harris](/notes/martin-harris) is called a "wicked man" and rebuked harshly for the ultimatum he gives the Lord, commanded to repent if he is to be permitted to see the plates as a witness. In Christian Reformation, Zion, and Seekers, verse 14 is grouped with [D&C 86](/notes/dandc-86):3, [D&C 33](/notes/dandc-33):4–5, and [D&C 109](/notes/dandc-109):73 as scriptures about restoring Christianity "to a healthy condition" and restoring truth — placing the Restoration within the longer arc of Christian Reformation.

**Context:** Doctrine and Covenants Section 5 is a revelation given to Joseph Smith in March 1829 through Martin Harris, who had sought permission to view the plates. It rebukes Harris, names the future Three Witnesses arrangement, and conditions the privilege of witnessing on humility and repentance.

## Where this appears

- Publication of the Book of Mormon — Martin Harris rebuked as a "wicked man," commanded to repent before he can see the plates.
- Christian Reformation, Zion, and Seekers — verse 14 cited among restoration-of-truth scriptures.
