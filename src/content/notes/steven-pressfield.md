# Steven Pressfield

Author quoted in [Find Your Territory](/notes/find-your-territory) from [The War of Art](/notes/the-war-of-art): "Our job in this life is not to shape ourselves into some ideal we imagine we ought to be, but to find out who we already are and become it." The long read uses him for the imperative to become who you already are rather than chasing an external ideal.

**Context:** Steven Pressfield is an American author best known for *The War of Art*, a book on creative resistance and the discipline of doing the work, and for historical novels including *Gates of Fire* and *The Legend of Bagger Vance*.
