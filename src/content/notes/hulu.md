# Hulu

Hulu shows up only in a margin note Kyle left while reading the [Berkshire Hathaway Annual Letters](/books/berkshire-hathaway-annual-letters). Reacting to Buffett's observation that pushing advertising "frequency" too hard produces diminishing returns — "The third ad in an hour on a given cable channel is simply not going to be as effective as the first" — Kyle quips: *"If you watch Hulu you know they still haven't learned their lesson."* The reference is illustrative, using Hulu's heavy ad load as a present-day example of the over-frequency mistake Buffett described in his GEICO advertising commentary.

**Context:** Hulu is a US streaming video service, majority-owned by The Walt Disney Company, known for its ad-supported subscription tier and its frequent in-program ad breaks.
