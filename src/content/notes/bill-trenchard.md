# Bill Trenchard

A partner at [First Round Capital](/notes/first-round-capital) who led the firm's **$4M seed round** in [Sprig](/notes/sprig) (then UserLeap) in September 2019. In Kyle's diligence on Sprig, Trenchard / First Round sit at the earliest layer of the cap table, ahead of [Accel](/notes/accel)'s $16M Series A (Dec 2020) and [a16z](/notes/a16z)'s $38M Series B (June 2021) (Sprig fka UserLeap - Diligence).

**Context:** Bill Trenchard is a partner at First Round Capital, where he focuses on early-stage software, dev tools, and future-of-work startups. Before investing, he was a repeat founder (co-founder/CEO of LiveOps and JotSpot, the latter acquired by Google).
