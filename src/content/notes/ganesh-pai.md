# Ganesh Pai

Ganesh Pai is the founder and CEO of [Uptycs](/notes/uptycs), a security-analytics platform spanning endpoints and cloud that competes directly with [CrowdStrike ($CRWD)](/notes/crowdstrike). Kyle ran a sourcing/diligence call with him for [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) in September 2021 (September 22nd, 2021 — Ganesh Pai (Uptycs)). At the time Pai's team was ~100 in India and ~75 elsewhere; he had hired an SVP from [DivvyCloud](/notes/divvycloud) to build out a ~60-person sales org and add go-to-market "machinery" to complement the founding team's sales strength.

On the business, Pai walked through a customer book including Netflix, Stripe (which expanded from $1.4M to $3.2M annually), Comcast (a side-by-side win against [CrowdStrike ($CRWD)](/notes/crowdstrike) on 65K machines, growing $1M → $3.5M), Salesforce, [Paypal](/notes/paypal), and Twitter. On fundraising, he had raised from [Sapphire Ventures](/notes/sapphire-ventures), had inbound from [Meritech](/notes/meritech) and interest from [ServiceNow ($NOW)](/notes/servicenow) and [Salesforce](/notes/salesforce) (the latter blocked by [Tanium](/notes/tanium)), and still held ~$80M cash in the bank. He noted a [CapitalG](/notes/capitalg) term sheet had been shut down by the [CrowdStrike ($CRWD)](/notes/crowdstrike) team.
