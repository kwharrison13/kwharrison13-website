# Colossians 1

Paul's first chapter to the Colossians appears in Kyle's Hope — Topical Study as scriptural backing for the idea that the [Atonement](/notes/atonement) of Jesus Christ is the source of hope. He cites Colossians 1:22–23 — Christ's sacrifice allows us to stand "holy and unblameable and unreprovable in his sight," *if* we remember that sacrifice and keep the covenants of his remembrance. Kyle's gloss: "NEVER forget your part!"

**Context:** Colossians 1 is the opening chapter of Paul's epistle to the church at Colossae, containing his thanksgiving for their faith, hope, and love, and a hymn on the preeminence of Christ as the one who reconciles all things to God through the blood of his cross.
