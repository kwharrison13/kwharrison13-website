# Jim Routh

Former [MassMutual](/notes/massmutual) CISO, described in the [Shardul Shah](/notes/shardul-shah) pre-brief on [Wiz.io](/notes/wiz-io) as "in the highest echelon of CISOs in financial services." His signing of a $1.3M, three-year Wiz deal was cited as exceptional early enterprise traction — listed alongside an [Andrew Stanley](/notes/andrew-stanley) $500K ACV deal closed in under six weeks and a [Steve Ward](/notes/steve-ward) / [Home Depot](/notes/home-depot) 500K+ deal in discussion — as evidence of Wiz's pull with the most demanding security buyers.

**Context:** Jim Routh is a widely recognized cybersecurity executive who has held chief security / CISO roles at firms including MassMutual, Aetna, JPMorgan Chase, and KPMG, and is a frequent advisor and speaker on application and enterprise security.
