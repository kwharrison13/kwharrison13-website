# Cloudeagle.ai

CloudEagle is a data-backed, all-in-one SaaS research and procurement platform that Kyle was introduced to via [Jacob Jofe](/notes/jacob-jofe) on December 13th, 2021. Per the intro materials, it helps the "citizen SaaS buyer spend smarter and buy faster" while giving the CIO visibility and cost optimization, and provides SaaS vendors with high-quality leads. Its differentiator is a proprietary **SaaSMap** — a database of ~100,000 vendors, 10M reviews, and 200K case studies — pitched as the "Zoominfo of SaaS vendors."

The intro note describes a seasoned GTM/B2B team: CEO Nidhi (20+ years of investment research and SaaS-partnership experience at Goldman Sachs, Box, ServiceNow) and CTO Prasanna (10+ years at Airbnb and Oracle Cloud). Early traction came from inbound/outbound leads on multi-year agreements, and the company was backed by F7 Ventures plus operator angels (ex-CIO of Zoom, GM of Medallia, ex-CPO of Uber) — flagged in the note as [Super Angels](/notes/super-angels).

**Context:** CloudEagle.ai is a SaaS management and procurement platform headquartered in the Bay Area. This general background is consistent with, but extends beyond, what Kyle's note records.
