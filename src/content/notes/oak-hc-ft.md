# Oak HC/FT

Oak HC/FT appears in Kyle's notes mainly as a voice of skepticism about the [SignalRank](/notes/signalrank) model. In the June 6th, 2022 call with [Rob Hodgkinson](/notes/rob-hodgkinson), Oak HC/FT had just told Hodgkinson that the SignalRank index/co-investment model "doesn't make sense for most VCs because the ownership is too small" — a critique that bears directly on how SignalRank's own fundraise is positioned. Separately, in the March 30th, 2022 Kyle Gay (Kensington Permanent Capital) notes, Oak HC/FT is referenced as the prior firm of someone for whom Kensington led a seed round to build a new growth-buyout vehicle.

**Context:** Oak HC/FT is a venture and growth-equity firm (founded 2014 by Annie Lamont and Patricia Kemp, spun out of Oak Investment Partners) focused on healthcare ("HC") and fintech ("FT"), known for backing companies like Devoted Health, Quartet Health, and Aspire Health.
