# Homelessness

**One-line definition:** *The social problem [Destination: Home](/notes/destination-home) is organized to solve in Santa Clara County, addressed through a "housing first" supportive-housing strategy.*

## How sources describe it

- Framed as solvable at the local level: "the further you get from your sphere of influence, the less control you end up having." (source: raw/calls/May 31st, 2018.md)
- Tackled via "housing first" / supportive housing, homelessness prevention, and aligning service interventions with new housing stock. (source: raw/calls/May 31st, 2018.md)
- A cost study (2005–2012) of the county's cost of managing homelessness helped build voter support for a $950M supportive-housing bond. (source: raw/calls/May 31st, 2018.md)
