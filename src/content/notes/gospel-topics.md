# Gospel Topics

A source reference Kyle cites in his gospel study — the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints' "Gospel Topics" reference library. In Fasting - September 5, 2021 he studies Fasting directly from the Gospel Topics entry "Fasting and Fast Offerings," using it as the doctrinal starting point for the purpose of the fast (drawing closer to God and caring for the poor), its benefits, and how to fast more meaningfully.

**Context:** "Gospel Topics" is an official reference collection on the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints' website (ChurchofJesusChrist.org) — concise, topic-by-topic explanations of doctrine and practice, including the more detailed "Gospel Topics Essays" series.
