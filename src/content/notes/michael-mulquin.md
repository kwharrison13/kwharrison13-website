# Michael Mulquin

**One-line:** *Smart-city standards expert (IS Communications) deeply involved across ISO, IEC, ITU, and the TM Forum.*

## What we know

- Calls himself a "jack of all trades, master of none" but claims to know more than anyone about smart-city standards — capturing best practices and spreading them broadly; involved with ISO, IEC (smart grids), ITU (telecom), and set up a Smart Cities Association.
- Points to the TM Forum's Smart City Maturity Model (four levels: leadership & governance; citizen & partnerships; effective use of data; IT infrastructure) and the "City as a Platform" manifesto as key frameworks.
- Argues the market must move from highly tailored per-city solutions toward massively customizable, standards-conformant solutions, and that getting the investor perspective into the standards work is a key need.
