# Pathlight

Pathlight is a performance-management software vendor for support and sales teams that surfaces twice in Kyle's notes as a category reference point. In the Cresta.ai - Diligence, Kyle left himself a TODO to *"introduce [SoFi](/notes/sofi) to [Pathlight](/notes/pathlight) and other performance-management vendors on the list"* — placing Pathlight in the team-performance / agent-management layer adjacent to the conversation-intelligence tools the Cresta diligence centers on, and treating it as a live intro/sourcing target.

Pathlight also appears as a comparable in the July 2nd, 2020 — Nick Calla (High Alpha) deal-intel call: [Nick Calla](/notes/nick-calla) of the [High Alpha](/notes/high-alpha) venture studio described Canopy.io — a company High Alpha had invested in — as "very similar to Pathlight," using it as the recognizable benchmark for the performance-management category.

**Context:** Pathlight is a San Francisco-based team-performance and real-time coaching platform (founded 2017) that aggregates performance data for customer-facing teams; it has since pivoted toward conversational-AI / customer-experience intelligence.
