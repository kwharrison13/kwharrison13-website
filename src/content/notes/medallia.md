# Medallia

Medallia is the experience-management platform that surfaces in two of Kyle's diligence dossiers as an adjacency to the categories being evaluated. In the Cresta.ai - Diligence, it is paired with [Qualtrics](/notes/qualtrics) as an "experience-management adjacency" to the agent-assist/conversation-intelligence space, and one expert floats a company like Medallia (or [Five9](/notes/five9)) as a potential acquirer of agent-coaching tools such as [Balto.ai](/notes/balto-ai) — i.e. Medallia as a possible consolidator at the edge of the contact-center stack. In the Planhat - Diligence, Medallia is mapped onto the [Customer Success](/notes/customer-success) tooling landscape as a long-tenured (20–25-year) experience-management player, cited as in use at IBM, sitting adjacent to the pure-CS incumbents like [Gainsight](/notes/gainsight) and [ToTango](/notes/totango).

**Context:** Medallia is a US-based experience-management software company, founded in 2001, that captures and analyzes customer and employee feedback signals across channels; it IPO'd in 2019 and was taken private by Thoma Bravo in 2021.
