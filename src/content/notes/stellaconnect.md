# StellaConnect

Customer-feedback and survey-response tool. In the Cresta.ai - Diligence, StellaConnect appears in the contact-center stack [Chris Purpura](/notes/chris-purpura) runs at [Neiman Marcus](/notes/neiman-marcus), where it handles **survey response with a 35% response rate** — one of several point tools (alongside [Twilio Flex](/notes/twilio-flex), Calabrio, Khoros Care) that a Cresta deployment plugs into. It also surfaces in Kyle's July 2nd, 2020 daily log as an 08:30 call, indicating he had direct contact with the company that day.

**Context:** Stella Connect (by Medallia) is a customer-feedback and quality-management platform built for contact centers — it collects real-time CSAT/feedback tied to individual agent interactions, used to drive coaching, recognition, and QA workflows.
