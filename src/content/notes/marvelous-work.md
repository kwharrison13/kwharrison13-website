# Marvelous Work

A scriptural phrase Kyle studied in his 3 Nephi 20–3 Nephi 26 note ("Ye Are the Children of the Covenant"). It is the Father's "great and marvelous work" promised in 3 Nephi 21:9 — done, in the wording Kyle highlights, **"for my sake,"** linking it to the [Intercession of Christ](/notes/intercession-of-christ) and the recurring [For My Sake](/notes/for-my-sake) theme in that chapter. He pairs it with D&C 4:1 ("a marvelous work is about to come forth among the children of men"), reading the two as describing the same latter-day work of God among the covenant people, tied to the [Gathering of Israel](/notes/gathering-of-israel) and the building of the [New Jerusalem](/notes/new-jerusalem).

**Context:** "Marvelous work and a wonder" is a phrase from Isaiah 29:14, picked up in 2 Nephi and Doctrine and Covenants, and read in Latter-day Saint scripture as a prophecy of the Restoration of the gospel in the latter days.
