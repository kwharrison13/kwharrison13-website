# Design

Bare `#Design` tag in *How to Take Smart Notes*, attached to the idea that "good tools do not add features and more options to what we already have, but help to reduce distractions from the main work" — the slip-box is framed as an external scaffold whose design supports thinking rather than adding complexity. Also invoked via Kyle's own note on the "How To Take Smart Notes" page: as [Automation](/notes/automation) spreads, the differentiator becomes an individual's ability to design content, systems, and structures.
