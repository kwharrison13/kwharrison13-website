# Christian Anderson

Co-founder of [Watershed Climate](/notes/watershed-climate), the corporate carbon-measurement platform Kyle diligenced for its Series B (Index Ventures, 2021). Anderson and co-founder [Taylor Francis](/notes/taylor-francis) spent roughly two years at [Stripe](/notes/stripe) building its carbon-removal program — reviewing thousands of spreadsheets and PDFs to understand Stripe's own footprint — which became the origin of the Watershed product idea. The diligence captures a partner presentation he gave alongside Francis, and lists his HBR podcast appearance ("How Data Helps Companies Honor Their Climate Goals" with [Azeem Azhar](/notes/azeem-azhar)) as a public reference.

Anderson also surfaces as a node in Kyle's Talent Vortex research, which maps "the association of the like-minded" across companies like Stripe, [Plaid](/notes/plaid), and [Dropbox](/notes/dropbox); he appears in the surveyed Stripe cohort alongside [Weiheng Zhang](/notes/weiheng-zhang), [Ryan Ma](/notes/ryan-ma), and others. One reference call (Nan Ransohoff at Stripe) notes that when she started at Stripe, Anderson "was like a mentor for her" — he was a few years above her at Harvard undergrad — underscoring his standing within the Stripe network that the Talent Vortex research traces.
