# Netlify

Web-development / hosting platform that appears twice in Kyle's 2022 meeting notes as an illustrative example rather than a diligence target. In the September 13th, 2022 — Nisha Swarup call, Netlify (cited alongside [Valon](/notes/valon)) is given as a case where the team was focused on *discovery* rather than diligence, on the reasoning that diligence requires more of a [System of Record](/notes/system-of-record). In the November 7th, 2022 — Emilie Schario & Walt Laws-MacDonald (Turbine) call, Netlify is cited for scale — "powering 11% of the internet."

**Context:** Netlify is a San Francisco–based platform for building, deploying, and hosting modern web applications, central to the Jamstack developer ecosystem.
