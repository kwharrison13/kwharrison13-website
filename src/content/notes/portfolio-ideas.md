# Portfolio Ideas

"Portfolio Ideas" is Kyle's running output of investable companies surfaced by his [Investing 101 2.0](/notes/investing-101-2-0) process — the named "output: companies surfaced by the process" within that publication's stack, alongside the [Networked Conviction](/essays/networked-conviction-roam-investing) method and [Roam Research](/notes/roam-research) as the tool. In Kyle's wiki/tweets/ system the slug also functions as a taxonomy that gates which saved items feed a given Portfolio Idea's "Latest Saved" stream on the website. The habit it instantiates is captured in his note on [The Everything Store](/books/the-everything-store), where he tags Bezos's discipline — "constantly recording ideas in a notebook he carried with him, as if they might float out of his mind if he didn't jot them down" — as a #[Portfolio Ideas](/notes/portfolio-ideas) practice: capture promising ideas continuously so none are lost.
