# IEEE Smart City Standards Committee

The IEEE Smart City Standards Committee surfaces in Kyle's notes through [Venture Smarter](/notes/venture-smarter), the smart-city / IoT community and Cisco partner he took a networking call with in May 2018. Venture Smarter's co-founder Zach started the IEEE Smart City Standards Committee, and it appears in the notes as the standards-body credential that anchors the firm's authority in the smart-city space — the institutional backing behind a company working with 150+ governments on getting "smart projects to completion in real life." It has no independent treatment in the wiki beyond that provenance.

**Context:** IEEE (the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers) is the world's largest technical professional organization and a major standards-setting body. Standards committees under IEEE develop technical specifications for interoperability across domains; smart-city standardization efforts aim to make IoT infrastructure, data, and services across municipal systems interoperable.
