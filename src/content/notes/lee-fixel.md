Founder of the venture firm [Addition](/notes/addition); previously a star investor at Tiger Global. In [Stewards of Capital](/essays/stewards-of-capital), Kyle distinguishes Fixel from the "Tiger 2.0" archetype of massive markups and capital excess — characterizing him instead as making "very intellectually interesting bets," and Addition as a capable, thoughtful firm investing across construction, lending, and AI/ML.
