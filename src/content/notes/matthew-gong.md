# Matthew Gong

Openly queer son of [Gerrit W. Gong](/notes/gerrit-w-gong), an apostle of [The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints](/notes/the-church-of-jesus-christ-of-latter-day-saints). Author of the personal "birthday letter" Birthday Letters — Matthew Gong, a multi-year (2017–2019) reflection on coming out, his relationship with his parents, and his complicated relationship with the Church — which he parses into three distinct entities (organization, religion, culture) and hopes can change "from the bottom up." A self-described humanist and "math geek/engineer" who evaluates life choices against a small set of governing metrics (net human good, net creation, intentionality).
