# Fireblocks

**One-line:** *Enterprise crypto wallet infrastructure — secured by multi-party computation (MPC) for both security and speed — the market leader for hedge funds, exchanges, retail apps, and traditional FIs.*

## What we know

- Market leader for enterprise wallet infrastructure and a top-5 brand in crypto; serves Galaxy Digital, FalconX, Revolut, Robinhood, Blockfi, BNY Mellon, and Credit Suisse (per November 9th, 2021 — Fireblocks Brief (from Chris Ahn)).
- Offers a software ("hot") wallet secured by multi-party computation (MPC), giving both security and transfer speed vs. the cold-storage incumbent model (e.g. Bitgo).
- Stickiness from its governance/compliance engine; expansion from network effects on transaction volume. Retained 94% of customers in Q3 2021; NRR of 214%; $2tn+ annualized transaction volume.
- Traction: ARR $9m (2020) → $56m (2021E) → $173m (2022E); 150 → 792 → 2,117 customers; 8-month CAC payback; ~$60k avg ARR per customer.
- Founders: Michael Shaulov (CEO), Idan Ofrat (CTO), Pavel Berengoltz (VP R&D); Matt Maloney (SVP Sales). ~202 FTEs, HQ in NY (Nov 2021).
- [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) considered up to a $100m check (target $50m of a ~$300m primary / up to $150m secondary) at a $7.5bn post-money valuation in November 2021.
