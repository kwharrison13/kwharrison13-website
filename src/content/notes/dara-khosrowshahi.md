# Dara Khosrowshahi

CEO of [Uber](/notes/uber), referenced in Index Ventures' 2021 Watershed diligence via an expert call with Alex Mennella (Uber). Mennella's point: when Khosrowshahi took over, safety became "the forefront" of Uber's brand recognition — and the analogy carried into how Uber was then approaching carbon. The diligence frames Uber's climate strategy as driven less by internal accounting than by its driver network (pushing toward EVs and programs like Uber Clean), with Khosrowshahi's safety-first brand pivot cited as precedent for how a company-wide priority becomes part of the brand. He is a referenced authority in a customer/expert call, not a participant in the diligence itself.

**Context:** Dara Khosrowshahi has been CEO of Uber since 2017, hired from Expedia to lead the company after Travis Kalanick's departure; he is widely associated with a turnaround emphasizing safety, regulatory repair, and brand rehabilitation.
