# DCG

DCG (Digital Currency Group) appears in Kyle's wiki as one of the crypto strategics [Standard.re](/notes/standard-re) was courting during its mid-2022 fundraise. Per the June 6th, 2022 call with [Max Gasner](/notes/max-gasner) and [Zac Townsend](/notes/zac-townsend), Standard.re was talking to several strategics — Coinbase, Alchemy, and DCG — plus reinsurer venture arms, after [Dragonfly](/notes/dragonfly) pulled a signed term sheet on "Luna Wednesday." DCG is named only as a prospective strategic investor in that context; the wiki carries no further detail.

**Context:** Digital Currency Group is a crypto-focused holding company and investor founded by Barry Silbert, parent of subsidiaries including Grayscale and (formerly) Genesis.
