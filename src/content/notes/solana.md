# Solana

Solana is the high-performance Layer-1 blockchain at the center of the [FormFn](/notes/formfn) seed investment-committee brief (December 13th, 2021 — FormFn (Seed IC)). FormFn — a creator-first 1/1 NFT marketplace — bet entirely on Solana because minting an NFT there cost ~$0.015 versus $100+ on Ethereum, putting NFT creation within reach of non-crypto-native artists. The brief frames Solana as the third-largest blockchain by market cap (~$52B, behind Bitcoin's $926B and Ethereum's $478B), one of the fastest-growing chains, having grown from roughly $90M to $65B market cap in twelve months, and backed by [a16z](/notes/a16z) and Polychain Capital. The core thesis risk noted was timing: that Ethereum scalability solutions might mature before Solana NFTs became a standard for independent artists, though as a single Layer-1 chain Solana's ecosystem was less fragmented and onboarding faster than an Ethereum L2.

Solana also surfaces in the November 1st, 2021 — Garnet (Pre-Brief from Shardul Shah) note as one of Garnet's five design partners (alongside Phantom, Audius, Metric Insights, and Banana) — evidence cited for the founders' resourcefulness in lining up opinionated, willing early customers.

**Context:** Solana is a proof-of-stake Layer-1 blockchain launched in 2020, known for high throughput and very low transaction fees; it became a major hub for NFTs and DeFi during the 2021 crypto cycle.
