# Scott Shephard

**One-line:** *Business development at [ARS Traffic & Transport Technology](/notes/ars-traffic-and-transport-technology) (Dutch traffic/mobility software); a prolific LinkedIn voice on the data–urban planning–mobility nexus.*

## What we know

- Holds a master's in urban and regional planning; worked in Bay Area urban planning, on the Oakland metropolitan ITS side, and as a transportation/highway consultant before running new-market business development for the Dutch-based [ARS Traffic & Transport Technology](/notes/ars-traffic-and-transport-technology) (June 28th, 2018 — Scott Shephard (ARS Traffic & Transport Technology)).
- Uses LinkedIn heavily as a content-marketing / lead-gen strategy; Kyle calls him the best source of content he's seen on the platform (June 28th, 2018 — Scott Shephard (ARS Traffic & Transport Technology)).
- Argues city data should be seen as interconnected nodes (not silos) and that bottom-up engagement with subject-matter experts — not a top-down approach — is necessary to win the market, even though it stretches the sales cycle (June 28th, 2018 — Scott Shephard (ARS Traffic & Transport Technology)).
