# Young Money

[Jack Raines](/notes/jack-raines)'s newsletter/blog (youngmoney.co), the publication behind two pieces Kyle saved. [Infinite Games](/notes/infinite-games) — Raines's application of [James Carse](/notes/james-carse)'s finite-vs-infinite-games framework to how we live, arguing that optimizing for achievements is a trap and you should "spend your 4,680 weeks in pursuit of the things that you actually enjoy pursuing" — was published here (youngmoney.co/p/infinite-games). Raines's "Ask Good Questions," cited in Embrace The Nuance - Research, is the other Young Money piece in Kyle's reading: as AI drives the cost of finding and creating information to zero, "if you ask the right questions, the information filters itself."

**Context:** Young Money is the personal-finance-and-life-philosophy newsletter written by Jack Raines, which grew a large readership for essays blending markets, career, and how to live.
