# Smule

Smule surfaces in the wiki as a prior employer of [Yue Wu](/notes/yue-wu), the Dropbox group product manager interviewed as an expert source in the Sprig fka UserLeap - Diligence. Per her work history, she worked in Product at Smule (Consumer Apps, Search, SEO) from August 2015 to December 2016, where she led a full search redesign and the go-to-market for the Sing! app, before moving on to [Yelp](/notes/yelp) and then Dropbox. The company is referenced only as a credential in her ~12-year product background; the diligence itself is about Sprig's in-app survey tooling, not about Smule.

**Context:** Smule is a consumer mobile-app company best known for Sing! (a social karaoke app), letting users record and share singing performances; it was an early entrant in social music apps.
