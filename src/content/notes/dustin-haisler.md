# Dustin Haisler

**One-line:** *Chief Innovation Officer at [eRepublic](/notes/erepublic), the public-sector media company behind the GovTech 100; bridges industry tech-commercialization and government trend-education.*

## What we know

- Started in local government and worked with startups before joining [eRepublic](/notes/erepublic), a media company revolving around the public sector with four arms: research, events, media, and a micro-VC model (June 28th, 2018 — Dustin Haisler (eRepublic)).
- As CIO, splits time 50/50 between helping industry commercialize technology and helping government understand trends; eRepublic produces the GovTech 100 (June 28th, 2018 — Dustin Haisler (eRepublic)).
- Describes eRepublic's ad-agency / lead-gen model: governments never pay, monetization is always company-side; near-universal US coverage built from an organically grown community (90K local agencies, 19K small cities) (June 28th, 2018 — Dustin Haisler (eRepublic)).
