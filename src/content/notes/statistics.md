# Statistics

The study of probability and outcomes under uncertainty. Kyle tags it on [On the Nature of Long-Term Holds](/notes/on-the-nature-of-long-term-holds) beside his question — "How do you increase your capacity to get lucky?" — framing [Luck](/notes/luck) as something with a statistical, base-rate structure that can be shaped rather than left purely to chance.
