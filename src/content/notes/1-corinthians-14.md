# 1 Corinthians 14

Paul's chapter on spiritual gifts. In Kyle's Revelation — Topical Study it is cited (1 Corinthians 14:12, alongside [D&C 121](/notes/dandc-121):37) for the principle that there is a wide gap between *wanting* the gifts of the Spirit and wanting them *for the right reasons* — striving for spiritual gifts is good, but they should be sought to bless others rather than to praise oneself.

**Context:** 1 Corinthians 14 is the New Testament chapter in which the Apostle Paul instructs the Corinthian church on the proper use of spiritual gifts, contrasting prophecy with speaking in tongues and urging that gifts be exercised to edify the whole congregation.
