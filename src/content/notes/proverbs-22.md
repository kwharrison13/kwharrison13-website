# Proverbs 22

A chapter from the Old Testament book of Proverbs, used in Kyle's scripture study as a cross-reference on raising children in righteousness. In the July 20–26, 2020. Alma 36–38: "Look to God and Live" study, Proverbs 22:6 is cited verbatim — "Train up a child in the way he should go: and when he is old, he will not depart from it" — connecting to that page's theme of Alma's counsel to his sons. The chapter appears in that study's scripture cross-reference list.

**Context:** Proverbs is a book of Hebrew wisdom literature in the Old Testament, traditionally attributed largely to King Solomon, collecting maxims on righteous living. Proverbs 22:6 is one of its most frequently cited verses on parenting.
