# Twitch

Twitch surfaces twice across Kyle's notes, both as a reference point rather than a diligence target. In the Pave Diligence — 2021 dossier it appears as career backstory: a [Carta](/notes/carta) employee came out with a white paper on offer-letter / compensation visualization "when she was at Twitch and wanted to build it," part of the thread tracing where Pave's comp-visualization ideas originated. In the June 8th, 2023 — Amir Shevat (Darkmode Ventures) call, Twitch is part of Amir Shevat's operator pedigree — he was VP of API for Twitch (alongside earlier roles at Slack and Twitter), which grounds his developer-tools and DevOps investment thesis at Darkmode Ventures.

**Context:** Twitch is a live-streaming platform, primarily for gaming, acquired by Amazon in 2014. Kyle's notes reference it only as employment/credential background for people in his network, not as a company he evaluated.

## Where this appears

- Pave Diligence — 2021 — cited as where a Carta team member had earlier wanted to build offer-letter/compensation visualization, in the lineage of Pave's product ideas.
- June 8th, 2023 — Amir Shevat (Darkmode Ventures) — listed in Amir Shevat's background (VP of API for Twitch) underpinning his dev-tools thesis.
