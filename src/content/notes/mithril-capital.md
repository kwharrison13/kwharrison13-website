# Mithril Capital

Mithril Capital appears in Kyle's notes in two places. In the November 2021 [Helion](/notes/helion) news brief, Mithril is listed among the investors — alongside [Dustin Moskovitz](/notes/dustin-moskovitz) and Capricorn Investment Group — in **Helion's $500M Series E at a $3B post-money valuation** (led by [Sam Altman](/notes/sam-altman)), described as the largest-ever VC investment into nuclear fusion. Separately, in the June 2021 call with Vedika Jain of Weekend Fund, Vedika cites Mithril as where she **interned early in her career** ("was so much younger"), part of the origin story of how she came to think about venture capital as a product/platform business.

**Context:** Mithril Capital is a growth-stage venture fund co-founded by Peter Thiel and Ajay Royan, named after the rare, durable metal in Tolkien's legendarium and known for concentrated, long-horizon bets on technology companies.

## Where this appears

- November 8th, 2021 — Helion — listed as a co-investor in Helion's $500M Series E (the largest-ever VC investment into nuclear fusion).
- June 24th, 2021 — Vedika Jain (Weekend Fund) — named as where Vedika Jain interned early on, part of her path into venture.
