# Alation

Surfaces once in Pave Diligence — 2021, where it is named — alongside [Algolia](/notes/algolia), Benchling, GitLab, Reddit, and Viz.ai — as a customer of [Compaas](/notes/compaas), one of the compensation-planning startups mapped as a direct competitor to [Pave](/notes/pave). Alation itself is not a subject of the diligence; it appears only as a logo in Compaas's customer roster during the competitive-landscape sweep.

**Context:** Alation is an enterprise data-catalog company (founded 2012) that helps organizations index, search, and govern their internal data assets — a leader in the data-intelligence / metadata-management category.
