# Checkr

Appears in the [Pave](/notes/pave) Series diligence (2021) as one of Pave's customers for its compensation-planning platform — listed alongside [Shopify](/notes/shopify), [Fivetran](/notes/fivetran), [Discord](/notes/discord), and [Allbirds](/notes/allbirds) as logos using Pave's digital offer letter, total-rewards, and comp-planning tools. The diligence notes that customers (Flipkart, Addepar, Ro) loved Pave's product relative to the spreadsheet-and-HRIS-export status quo; Checkr is cited as part of that customer base rather than discussed in its own right.

**Context:** Checkr is a San Francisco–based company providing AI-powered background checks and identity verification, primarily for hiring and the gig economy.
