# Charity

Charity in Kyle's notes is "the pure love of Christ" (Moroni 7:47) — both what we do and what we *become*. Its dedicated topical study, Charity & Love, treats it as the crowning virtue, drawing on a conference session where Ezra Taft Benson is recalled returning to his father's mission call as "a *gift of charity* whose missionary legacy still blesses the Benson family into the third and fourth generations" — charity as soteriological, that "seeks only the eternal growth and joy of others."

It anchors the Moroni 7–9 Come Follow Me week — Mormon's sermon pairing [Faith](/notes/faith), [Hope](/notes/hope), and Charity with the doctrine of [Real Intent](/notes/real-intent) — and appears in the 3 Nephi 1–7 study. In [The Priesthood Power of Women](/books/the-priesthood-power-of-women), Kyle ties charity to the Relief Society's purpose "to save souls" as "the end point of charity."
