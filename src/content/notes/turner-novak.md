# Turner Novak

An investor in [Candor](/notes/candor), noted in Kyle's record of the company's February 2022 update from [Kelsey Bishop](/notes/kelsey-bishop). The update lists Novak among Candor's existing investors, alongside [Ryan Delk](/notes/ryan-delk) of [Primer](/notes/primer) and [Alek Koenig](/notes/alek-koenig), CEO of [Settle.co](/notes/settle-co). Kyle's firm led Candor's seed round, with participation from [Brianne Kimmel](/notes/brianne-kimmel), [Ellen DeSilva](/notes/ellen-desilva), [Joseph Quan](/notes/joseph-quan), [Andrew Farah](/notes/andrew-farah), and [Anamitra Banerji](/notes/anamitra-banerji).

**Context:** Turner Novak is the founder of Banana Capital, an early-stage venture firm, and writer of the *Public/Private* newsletter; he is an active seed investor and online commentator on startups.
