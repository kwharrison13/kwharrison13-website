# 2 Nephi 25

2 Nephi 25 anchors several recurring themes in Kyle's gospel study. Its best-known verse, **"by grace that we are saved, after all we can do" (25:23)**, is the keystone of the Obedience — Topical Study argument that we do all we can and God does the rest — obedience as the appreciation we show God rather than a price paid. Christ Defines His Gospel works the same verse (25:25, "all we can do THEN sin no more") alongside [Alma 24](/notes/alma-24):11.

The chapter also frames **why we study and testify of Christ**. The August 17–23, 2020. Helaman 1–6: "The Rock of Our Redeemer" notes cite 25:26 — "we talk of Christ, we rejoice in Christ, we preach of Christ, we prophesy of Christ" — to show the Book of Mormon peoples were anxiously awaiting His [Atonement](/notes/atonement), not misunderstanding it. The Scripture Study — Topical Study cites 25:28 (scripture is "sufficient to teach any man the right way").

**Context:** 2 Nephi 25 is Nephi's chapter on the "plainness" of his prophecy and on Christ — declaring that his people labor to persuade their children to believe in Christ, that they "talk of Christ" and "rejoice in Christ," and that it is "by grace that we are saved, after all we can do."

## Where this appears

- Obedience — Topical Study — 2 Nephi 25:23, "after all we can do," as the keystone of grace-plus-effort obedience
- Christ Defines His Gospel — 25:25 ("all we can do, then sin no more") paired with [Alma 24](/notes/alma-24):11
- August 17–23, 2020. Helaman 1–6: "The Rock of Our Redeemer" — 25:26 ("we talk of Christ… rejoice in Christ") on the people awaiting the [Atonement](/notes/atonement)
- Scripture Study — Topical Study — 25:28, scripture "sufficient to teach any man the right way"
