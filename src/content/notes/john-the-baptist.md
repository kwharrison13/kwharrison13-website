# John the Baptist

In Kyle's [gospel-study](/notes/gospel-study) opening the 2019 New Testament Come Follow Me year, John the Baptist appears through the moment his disciples first follow [Jesus Christ](/notes/jesus): "'What seek ye?' Jesus asked the disciples of John the Baptist." The note turns the question on the reader — what you find in the New Testament this year depends on what you seek — pairing it with the Savior's promise, "Seek, and ye shall find," to frame the year's study as an act of personal, intentional learning.

**Context:** John the Baptist is the New Testament prophet who preached repentance and baptized Jesus in the Jordan River, identified in the Gospels as the forerunner who prepared the way for Christ's ministry. The exchange referenced is from John 1, where two of John's own disciples turn to follow Jesus.
