# Tommy Tudehope

A reference contact surfaced during [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures)' 2021 Series B diligence on [Watershed Climate](/notes/watershed-climate). Per the Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 dossier, Tudehope was approached at [Revolut](/notes/revolut) — one of Watershed's ~50 early customers, alongside Stripe, Shopify, Sweetgreen, and Airbnb — to give a customer's-eye view of the product during the reference-call sweep Kyle ran while supporting the deal. The single attestation in the wiki is the diligence note itself; nothing further about his role or his read on Watershed is recorded.
