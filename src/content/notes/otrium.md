# Otrium

A fashion-resale platform that appears in the Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 notes as a customer-introduction target for [Watershed Climate](/notes/watershed-climate). Kyle's contact there is [Marlot Kiveron](/notes/marlot-kiveron) (intro logged November 4th, 2021), and the notes flag that Otrium "is doing a lot of work on their sustainability program" with a sustainability lead Kyle knew well — making it a warm lead for Watershed's carbon-accounting product alongside other prospects like Peloton, Okta, Robinhood, Confluent, Deliveroo, and Sonos.

**Context:** Otrium is an online fashion-outlet marketplace (founded in the Netherlands) that sells past-season designer inventory, positioning itself partly on reducing fashion waste.
