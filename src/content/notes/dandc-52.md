# D&C 52

Section 52 of the [Doctrine and Covenants](/notes/doctrine-and-covenants) appears in Kyle's Early Mormonism notes as the revelation tied to [Joseph Smith](/notes/joseph-smith)'s plan to build [Zion](/notes/zion) into "a new world order." Drawing on [Richard Bushman](/notes/richard-bushman), the notes frame this against the Missouri slavery controversy: the directive to gather and build Zion in Jackson County "guaranteed conflict," because to claim revelation and build a separate kingdom is the surest way to be branded a fanatic. The section is also cited where the lands of Missouri are declared to be "in the hands of God's enemies," sharpening the collision between the rapidly growing Saints and the Jacksonians who resented their obedience and their teaching "without respect of persons."

**Context:** Doctrine and Covenants Section 52 is a revelation given to Joseph Smith in June 1831 at the close of a Church conference in Kirtland, directing the elders to travel two by two to Missouri, where the land of Zion would be made known. It set in motion the gathering to Jackson County, Missouri.
