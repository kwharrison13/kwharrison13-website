# Spring Health

**One-line definition:** *Comprehensive, outcomes-based mental-health benefit for employers; vertically integrated and differentiated by measuring quality of care.*

## How sources describe it

- Locks in rates and brings patients to providers but can lose money if people don't get better (or make more if they recover faster) — performance guarantees / shared risk on outcomes; tracks outcomes and reports who used it and who got better, unlike status-quo EAPs; differentiated by personalization (algorithms/IP matching symptoms to treatment) and comprehensiveness (therapy + medication + substance-abuse + residential), a [Vertical Integration](/notes/vertical-integration) play; Series C at $2B; competes with [Lyra](/notes/lyra) and Ginger; biggest new customer launching next year is Guardian Life (a payer). Co-founded by [Adam Chekroud](/notes/adam-chekroud). (September 22nd, 2021 — Adam Chekroud (Spring Health))
