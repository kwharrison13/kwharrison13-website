# George Leonard

George Leonard was a senior editor at *Look* magazine in the 1960s who reported on brain physiology, schools, and human potential. In [Education Nation](/books/education-nation), [Milton Chen](/notes/milton-chen) quotes Leonard's 1964 conclusion that "most of the innate capability of most people is routinely squandered," with the mode of education itself a major cause of the waste.

## Where this appears

- [Education Nation](/books/education-nation) — quoted on the untapped reserves of human potential and education as a cause of squandered capability.
