# Morgan Stanley

Morgan Stanley appears in Kyle's Pave Diligence — 2021 notes as the parent of competitive compensation-data products in the cap-table / comp-benchmarking landscape. The notes flag [Shareworks](/notes/shareworks) / Option Impact as "owned by Morgan Stanley," noting Shareworks was "acquired by Morgan Stanley for ~$1B in 2019; doing ~$80M of revenue," and that its "core business competes with [Carta](/notes/carta)." In the diligence framing Morgan Stanley sits on the incumbent side of the comp-data market Pave is disrupting — Shareworks' comp-planning tool is described as having "super antiquated" UI and customers who "would rather be in Carta."

**Context:** Morgan Stanley is a major US investment bank and financial-services firm; it acquired equity-management platform Shareworks (formerly Solium) in 2019, the same year it also acquired Solium's E*TRADE-adjacent stock-plan business.
