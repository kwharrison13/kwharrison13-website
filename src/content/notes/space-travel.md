# Space Travel

In [The Magic of Elon Musk](/notes/the-magic-of-elon-musk), space travel is the organizing mission behind everything Musk does: he judges his own success by asking whether his work helps humanity become a multi-planetary species, and that single question gives his deep commitment and [Focus](/notes/focus) their direction.

## Where this appears

- [The Magic of Elon Musk](/notes/the-magic-of-elon-musk) — the multi-planetary mission framed as the question that organizes all of Musk's work.
