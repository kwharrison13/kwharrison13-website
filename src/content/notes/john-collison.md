# John Collison

Co-founder and president of [Stripe](/notes/stripe), appearing in Kyle's notes in two contexts. In the Watershed Series B diligence, John Collison and his brother [Patrick Collison](/notes/patrick-collison) are noted (alongside [Chris Cox](/notes/chris-cox) of [Facebook ($META)](/notes/facebook)) as among the angel/early investors in Watershed — the climate-software and carbon-marketplace company whose founders aimed to enable corporate [Decarbonization](/notes/decarbonization) at scale.

He also turns up in [Talent](/books/talent) (by [Tyler Cowen](/notes/tyler-cowen) and Daniel Gross) among the acknowledged contributors the authors thank for comments and discussion on the book's talent-evaluation ideas — a list that also includes his brother Patrick, [Sam Altman](/notes/sam-altman), [Marc Andreessen](/notes/marc-andreessen), [Reid Hoffman](/notes/reid-hoffman), and [Peter Thiel](/notes/peter-thiel).

**Context:** John Collison is the Irish-born co-founder and president of Stripe, the payments-infrastructure company he started with his older brother Patrick in 2010.
