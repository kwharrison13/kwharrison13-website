# StartApp

StartApp appears in Kyle's wiki only as a prior employer of [David Mark](/notes/david-mark), a Salt Security customer he interviewed during the 2021 API-security diligence. Per Mark's page, he was DevOps Team Lead and Cloud Architect at StartApp before leaving in June 2019 to join [Appsflyer](/notes/appsflyer), where the substantive part of the reference call took place. The company carries no further detail in the corpus beyond marking this point in Mark's career history.

**Context:** StartApp was a mobile advertising and monetization platform (later operating as StartIO), providing an in-app ad SDK and audience-data products for app developers.
