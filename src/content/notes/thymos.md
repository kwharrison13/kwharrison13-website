# Thymos

**One-line:** *The Ancient Greek term — from the Platonic tripartite soul (thymos / spiritedness, logos / reason, eros / desire) — for the "relentless drive to transcend the limitations of a listless present." In [We Need a Renaissance of Rhetoric](/essays/we-need-a-renaissance-of-rhetoric) it is the "why" to rhetoric's "how": we need "reinvigorated rhetoric in support of worthy thymos."*
