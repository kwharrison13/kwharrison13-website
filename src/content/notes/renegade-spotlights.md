# Renegade Spotlights

Renegade Spotlights is a recurring writing format Kyle authors, referenced in [Wordsmiths](/notes/wordsmiths) as the template he plans to imitate for a series of "Wordsmith Spotlights." In that note, Kyle wants to profile companies with distinctive writing cultures — [Amazon](/notes/amazon)'s six-page narratives, [Stripe](/notes/stripe)'s documentation, [Berkshire Hathaway](/notes/berkshire-hathaway)'s Buffett letters, [Bridgewater Associates](/notes/bridgewater-associates)'s Daily Observations — "the way he writes Renegade Spotlights." The reference establishes Renegade Spotlights as an existing, repeatable profile/spotlight format whose structure the new Wordsmith series would borrow, but the source does not spell out its full scope.
