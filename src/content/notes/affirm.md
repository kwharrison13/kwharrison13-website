# Affirm

The buy-now-pay-later / consumer-credit company founded by [Max Levchin](/notes/max-levchin) after PayPal — referenced across Kyle's corpus mostly as a **pedigree marker** for startup talent rather than as a diligence subject in its own right. In the Pave Diligence — 2021 notes, [Quan Zhou](/notes/quan-zhou) is cited as a positive team signal precisely because he joined [Pave](/notes/pave) from Affirm and [Zabie Elmgren](/notes/zabie-elmgren) "speaks super highly of" him; Affirm sits alongside [Carta](/notes/carta), [Uber](/notes/uber), [Segment](/notes/segment), and [Facebook](/notes/meta-company) as the credentialing roster behind Pave's team. In the Armand Farrokh call, Affirm is offered as "another great example" in the same vein. It also appears in the CaptivateIQ Diligence — 2021 notes as a named enterprise customer of CaptivateIQ's commission-management platform.

**Context:** Affirm Holdings ($AFRM) is a publicly traded fintech founded in 2012 by Max Levchin (a PayPal co-founder), offering point-of-sale installment loans / buy-now-pay-later financing to consumers.
