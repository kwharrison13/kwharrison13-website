# Royal Skousen

A scholar who appears in Kyle's wiki as the author / editor of the critical edition behind the anti-library entry [The Book of Mormon — The Earliest Text](/notes/the-book-of-mormon-the-earliest-text).

**Context:** Royal Skousen is a linguistics professor at Brigham Young University and the longtime editor of the Book of Mormon Critical Text Project, a decades-long scholarly effort to reconstruct the earliest text of the Book of Mormon.
