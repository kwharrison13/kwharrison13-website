# B2B Marketplaces

A capture concept in the marketplace cluster of Kyle's June 18, 2026 saved-bookmark batch, alongside the parent [Marketplaces](/notes/marketplaces) page and [SaaS + Marketplace](/notes/saas-marketplace). All three share the same seed link — Casey Winters' essay on [the SaaS + marketplace model](https://caseyaccidental.com/saas-marketplace/) — and cross-tag one another. The thread is the question of how marketplace dynamics apply business-to-business: matching buyers and sellers among companies rather than consumers, and how a software layer can sit on top of (or seed) the transaction. The concept is captured rather than synthesized; the wiki carries the link and the cross-links but no developed argument yet.

**Context:** B2B marketplaces are platforms that connect business buyers and sellers (wholesale goods, freight, materials, services). A recurring growth pattern is starting with SaaS tooling to acquire one side, then layering transactions on top — the "SaaS-enabled marketplace" model.
