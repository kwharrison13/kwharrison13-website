# FMRIB Centre

The Oxford neuroimaging research centre where [Adam Chekroud](/notes/adam-chekroud) did his pre-[Spring Health](/notes/spring-health) academic work. Per the [Adam Chekroud](/notes/adam-chekroud) page, Chekroud "came up doing research at the FMRIB Centre, University of Oxford under Dr. [Holly Bridge](/notes/holly-bridge) at the Brain Imaging Unit, John Radcliffe Hospital" before going on to co-found [Spring Health](/notes/spring-health) (the outcomes-based employer mental-health benefit) in March 2016 (source: [Adam Chekroud](/notes/adam-chekroud)). In Kyle's corpus it functions as the academic origin point of Chekroud's transition from brain-imaging research into the behavioral-health company-building world.

**Context:** The FMRIB Centre (Oxford Centre for Functional MRI of the Brain) is a University of Oxford research facility specializing in functional magnetic resonance imaging of the brain, since absorbed into the Wellcome Centre for Integrative Neuroimaging. (General background — only the Chekroud / Holly Bridge connection is attested in this wiki.)
