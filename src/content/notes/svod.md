# SVOD

Subscription video on demand — the streaming model (Netflix and the like) that, in Kyle's notes on [College Students- Focus On This](/notes/college-students-focus-on-this), displaced the cable bundle as part of the internet's "tectonic shift" away from the pre-mobile media world.

**Context:** SVOD refers to subscription-funded streaming services where viewers pay a recurring fee for unlimited on-demand access to a content library (e.g. Netflix, Disney+), as distinct from ad-supported (AVOD) or pay-per-title (TVOD) models. Its rise unbundled the cable package.

## Where this appears

- [College Students- Focus On This](/notes/college-students-focus-on-this) — cited as the successor to the cable bundle in the list of media shifts (TV → short-form video; bundle → SVOD and social; newspapers → personalized feeds) that university comms programs fail to teach.
