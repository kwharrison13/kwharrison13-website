# IDEO

IDEO appears in Kyle's notes as a stop in [Joel Kwartler](/notes/joel-kwartler)'s career path. After leaving an in-person-events startup, Kwartler spent time at IDEO thinking through how to build "startup kits" for large Fortune 500 companies — i.e., packaging startup-style product methods for big enterprises. The detail comes from Kyle's June 6th, 2022 — Joel Kwartler (Sourcegraph) call notes and is carried forward on Kwartler's person page. IDEO sits in the same career arc that includes his discovery of [Figma](/notes/figma) while interning at [Microsoft](/notes/microsoft) and his later move to [Sourcegraph](/notes/sourcegraph).

**Context:** IDEO is a global design and innovation consultancy, widely credited with popularizing "design thinking" as a human-centered approach to product and service design. Founded in 1991, it has run innovation and product-design engagements for many large corporations.
