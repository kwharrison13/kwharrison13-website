# Peter Wehner

Cited in [Faith is Not Blind](/books/faith-is-not-blind) (by [Bruce C. Hafen](/notes/bruce-c-hafen) and [Marie K. Hafen](/notes/marie-k-hafen)) for the line Kyle pulled into his #Quotes highlights: **"We are changed by what we love more than by what we think."** In Kyle's reading of the book, the quote distills the alternative to "cosmic loneliness" — the argument that meaning is restored through love and commitment rather than abstract reasoning, against the backdrop of the twentieth-century collapse of the inherited "big box" of shared meaning.

**Context:** Peter Wehner is an American writer and political commentator, a senior fellow at the Trinity Forum and contributing writer at *The Atlantic*, who has written widely on faith, ethics, and politics.
