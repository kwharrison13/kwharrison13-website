# Amer Deeba

Incoming Chief Commercial Officer at [Monad](/notes/monad), the "Fivetran for security" data platform, per the September 17th, 2021 pre-brief Kyle read from [Max Rimpel](/notes/max-rimpel). He joins founders Christian Almenar and Jacolon Walker, who built Monad to unify and normalize fragmented security tooling data — connecting siloed sources and layering pre-built applications (vulnerability management, team performance, tooling ROI, benchmarks) on top.

**Context:** Amer Deeba is a longtime security-industry executive, known for early leadership at Qualys and as co-founder of the security analytics company Normalyze.
