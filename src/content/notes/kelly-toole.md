# Kelly Toole

Investor/colleague who sourced and ran early diligence on [LiveKit](/notes/livekit) alongside Kyle (see August 18th, 2021 — LiveKit Questions (Kelly Toole)). Toole met with founder [David Zhao](/notes/david-zhao), dug deep into the [WebRTC](/notes/webrtc) landscape, and mapped the competitive space — framing existing CPaaS solutions (Twilio, Daily.co, Agora) as easy but inflexible and scale-limited, versus build-in-house or OSS options (Janus, Jitsi), with LiveKit aiming to fill the gap as an easy, flexible, and scalable solution with a cloud product on its roadmap. Her notes captured LiveKit's post-launch traction (~1.6k GitHub stars, multiple integrations, partnership and acquisition interest) and the open diligence questions she and Kyle worked through together.
