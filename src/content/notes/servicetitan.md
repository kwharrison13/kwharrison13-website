# ServiceTitan

Vertical-SaaS platform for the trades (technicians/home services) that appears across Kyle's 2021 diligence notes mainly as a recurring customer reference and a vertical-SaaS exemplar. In the August 18th, 2021 — Teamshares Series C Deck notes, ServiceTitan is cited (alongside [ShopMonkey](/notes/shopmonkey) for car repair) as a vertical SaaS company that wants to integrate payments — illustrating the embedded-fintech land grab that [Stripe](/notes/stripe) is also chasing. In the CaptivateIQ Diligence — 2021 dossier it shows up as a CaptivateIQ customer, with Paul Conney and Till Hebel named as an expert-call reference (November 2021). In the Pave Diligence — 2021 dossier, ServiceTitan appears via [Dave Allan](/notes/dave-allan) / [Conor Theilman](/notes/conor-theilman) as an [Assemble](/notes/assemble) user, and again in a cluster with [Sourcegraph](/notes/sourcegraph) and Verkada. In the November 2nd, 2021 — Lisa Wallace (Assemble) call, ServiceTitan is named as an early Assemble adopter and "the upper end" of the product's surface area (large, public, fast-growing, with 200–300 up to ~1K employees).

**Context:** ServiceTitan is a Glendale, California–based vertical-SaaS company providing operations and field-service management software for home- and commercial-services trades (HVAC, plumbing, electrical); it went public in late 2024.
