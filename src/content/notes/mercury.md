# Mercury

Mercury surfaces in the Pave Diligence — 2021 dossier as a node in Pave's customer/network web. The diligence notes record that [Veronica Pohls](/notes/veronica-pohls) at Mercury was a reference contact, and that Pave's customer-success team had "introduced" its customers to Mercury alongside [GO1](/notes/go1) and [Persona](/notes/persona) — positioning Mercury as one of the companies Pave routes prospects toward as part of its sales-led network. Mercury also appears in a short cluster with [Unqork](/notes/unqork) and [Gatsby](/notes/gatsby) in the same diligence document. The notes don't elaborate on Mercury's own business beyond these relationship touchpoints.

**Context:** Mercury (Mercury Technologies) is a fintech company providing banking and financial-stack services for startups and other technology businesses.

## Where this appears

- Pave Diligence — 2021 — Veronica Pohls listed as a Mercury contact; Pave introduces its customers to Mercury, GO1, and Persona, with a separate mention alongside Unqork and Gatsby.
