# Black & Decker vs. DeWalt Case Study

A branding/pricing case study tagged in [Charlie Munger — The Psychology of Human Misjudgment](/notes/charlie-munger-the-psychology-of-human-misjudgment), illustrating the conditioned reflex where mere association triggers a response — buyers trained to read the highest price as the highest quality when several similar items are presented for purchase. It exemplifies how price signals can be engineered to exploit a learned heuristic.
