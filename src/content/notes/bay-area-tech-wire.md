# Bay Area Tech Wire

A Bay Area tech-news source that appears in Kyle's corpus only as one line item in his 2017 startup-media inventory — the snapshot in The State of Startup Media - Research of what he was reading and following before the post-COVID explosion of founder blogs, Substacks, and newsletters. It sits at the tail of that list beside other newsletters and digests like [StrictlyVC](/notes/strictlyvc), Robinhood Snacks, and [LAUNCH Ticker](/notes/launch-ticker). The essay's larger argument is that there used to be a shorthand — a handful of go-to places like this — for learning about startups, and that the Republic-of-Letters explosion of the internet supercharged the field to the point where there is no single go-to source anymore. Bay Area Tech Wire is one of the rotation entries from that earlier, more legible era; the wiki carries no further detail on it.
