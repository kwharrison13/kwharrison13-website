# Barrels and Ammunition

[Keith Rabois](/notes/keith-rabois)'s framing: most strong employees are 'ammunition' (high quality but need pointing), while rare 'barrels' can independently turn ideas into shipped outcomes. You can only shoot through as many barrels as you have, so over-reward them.
