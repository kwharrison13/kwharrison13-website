# Settle.co

Settle.co surfaces in Kyle's notes through its CEO [Alek Koenig](/notes/alek-koenig), who appears as one of the existing investors in [Candor](/notes/candor) — the [Kelsey Bishop](/notes/kelsey-bishop) company that Contrary led the seed round for. The reference is incidental cap-table context (Koenig is listed alongside [Ryan Delk](/notes/ryan-delk) of [Primer](/notes/primer) and [Turner Novak](/notes/turner-novak) as angels/early backers of Candor), not a diligence note on Settle itself.

**Context:** Settle is a fintech that provides working-capital financing and accounts-payable / cash-flow management software for ecommerce and consumer-goods brands. Alek Koenig, a former Affirm credit lead, founded it.
