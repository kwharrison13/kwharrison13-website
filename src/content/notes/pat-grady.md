# Pat Grady

Pat Grady appears in Kyle's notes as the source of a "latticework of memory" story. In [The Death of Expertise](/books/the-death-of-expertise), one of Kyle's notes reads: "connect to the story with Pat Grady and the Latticework of memory" — tied explicitly to [Decentralized Brain](/notes/decentralized-brain) and to "Zach's 'Theory of Masters.'" The connection is made against a passage about specialization and trust: "we prosper because we specialize, and because we develop both formal and informal mechanisms and practices that allow us to trust each other in those specializations." Pat Grady is the person Kyle associates with that latticework-of-memory idea.

He is also listed (marked as a stub) among the people referenced in [The Everything Store](/books/the-everything-store), though the notes there do not attach any specific anecdote to him.

## Where this appears

- [The Death of Expertise](/books/the-death-of-expertise) — the "Pat Grady and the Latticework of memory" story Kyle wants to connect to [Decentralized Brain](/notes/decentralized-brain) and a "Theory of Masters"
- [The Everything Store](/books/the-everything-store) — listed among referenced people (stub), without further detail in the notes
