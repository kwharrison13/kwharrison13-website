# CareValidate

**One-line:** *A telehealth platform that connects patients with doctors and pharmacies — one of the two rented-infrastructure providers behind [MEDVI](/notes/medvi).*

## What we know

- Connects patients with doctors and pharmacies; [MEDVI](/notes/medvi) outsources this regulated function to CareValidate (alongside [OpenLoop Health](/notes/openloop-health) for clinical infrastructure). (source: The $1B Rorschach Test - Research)

## Mentioned in

- The $1B Rorschach Test - Research — cited as part of the rented stack that makes MEDVI a marketing layer, not a healthcare company.
