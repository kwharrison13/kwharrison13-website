# Guru

Referenced in the [Workbounce](/notes/workbounce) pre-brief from [Hannah Seal](/notes/hannah-seal) as a comparable in the sales / knowledge-management category. That memo pitches Workbounce as a "search-first knowledge hub for sales" — a tool that integrates with Slack, Salesforce, Notion, Google Drive, Gong, etc. and lets sales reps ask a question and be surfaced the most relevant answer, rather than building yet another content silo. The memo's key risk is that existing solutions (chiefly "slacking a colleague and asking them") are "good enough"; Guru sits in the landscape of incumbent knowledge-base tools that Workbounce is implicitly positioned against.

**Context:** Guru is a SaaS company whose product is a wiki-and-knowledge-management platform that surfaces verified company knowledge inside the tools teams already use (Slack, Chrome, etc.) — a direct adjacency to the search-first knowledge-hub category Workbounce describes.
