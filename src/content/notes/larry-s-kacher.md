# Larry S. Kacher

A General Authority Seventy of [The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints](/notes/the-church-of-jesus-christ-of-latter-day-saints). In Kyle's gospel study he is the speaker of the April 2022 General Conference address Ladder of Faith — the image of faith as a ladder that "is not static… it can go up or down according to the choices we make," moving us from "What do I want?" to "What does God want?" He draws on the Tebbs family's loss, his own mission experiences in Tahiti and Nigeria, and the McCormick family's conversion to teach that "unbelief blocks our ability to see miracles, whereas a mindset of faith in the Savior unlocks the powers of heaven."
