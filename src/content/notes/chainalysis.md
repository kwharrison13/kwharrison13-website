# Chainalysis

Blockchain-analytics company that surfaces in Kyle's [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) notes as a growth-deal exemplar rather than a diligence subject. In the September 22nd, 2021 — Mark Goldberg (Career Feedback) conversation, [Mark Goldberg](/notes/mark-goldberg) listed Chainalysis among the high-conviction growth opportunities Kyle could build a thesis around in Q4 — naming it alongside [Ramp](/notes/ramp), [Benchling](/notes/benchling), and [Ada](/notes/ada) as the kind of company a "classically trained growth investor" should be able to argue hard for. The advice was framed around Index's perceived deficiency at the "growth net new" stage and the need to drive conviction on a handful of quality deals rather than picking off "small things and doing noise."

**Context:** Chainalysis is a blockchain data platform founded in 2014 that provides cryptocurrency transaction-tracing, compliance, and investigation software to governments, exchanges, and financial institutions. It became one of the most highly valued private crypto-infrastructure companies during the 2021 funding cycle.
