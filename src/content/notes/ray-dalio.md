# Ray Dalio

Dalio is named in Kyle's wiki as the founder of [Bridgewater Associates](/notes/bridgewater-associates), the hedge fund Kyle holds up as a [Wordsmith](/notes/wordsmiths) / "show, don't tell" culture exemplar — a firm that publishes its [Daily Observations](/notes/daily-observations) rather than merely describing its thinking. Bridgewater also surfaces in Kyle's writing for its distinctive culture practices (an individual "baseball card" for everyone and roughly 70% attrition), paired against [Zappos](/notes/zappos) as a high-attrition, strong-culture comparison.

**Context:** Ray Dalio is the founder of Bridgewater Associates, which grew into one of the world's largest hedge funds. He is widely known for his book *Principles* and his framework of "radical transparency" and idea-meritocratic decision-making.
