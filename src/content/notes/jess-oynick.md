# Jess Oynick

**One-line:** *Talent team member at [Coatue](/notes/coatue).*

A person from Kyle's [Coatue](/notes/coatue) network, cited mainly as a case study in how the firm under-deployed strong talent. In the December 14th, 2021 call, [Erik Kriessmann](/notes/erik-kriessmann) characterized Coatue's talent function as "unprogrammatic": Oynick "was great but they didn't know what to do with her and she ended up mostly being a coordinator with various search funds" — i.e. a strong person left underutilized. That observation carries into the [Coatue](/notes/coatue) page as evidence of the firm's hedge-fund-style culture lacking a structured people function.

Separately, in [The Everything Store](/books/the-everything-store) highlights, Kyle ties a thread about "the greatest talent training grounds" (Mulesoft/Salesforce for product marketing, Dropbox/Twitter for finance) back to "Jess Oynick talking about the most impressive talent orgs she's seen" — placing her as someone Kyle treats as a credible voice on where elite operating talent gets built.
