# Oil & Gas

A topic Kyle began researching in his June 29th, 2021 Roam "Quick Capture" reading log (an Index Ventures-era reading session). The saved sources point specifically at the **oilfield services** layer of the industry — a Wikipedia list of oilfield service companies and an Investopedia oil & gas industry overview, alongside two private Google Drive documents. The companion stub [Oil & Gas Service Business 2.0](/notes/oil-and-gas-service-business-2-0) suggests the angle Kyle was exploring: not the commodity itself but a modernized take on the services businesses that support extraction. **Context:** "Oilfield services" refers to companies (e.g. Schlumberger, Halliburton, Baker Hughes) that provide drilling, equipment, and technical services to oil and gas producers, rather than owning the reserves themselves — a distinct, often more capital-light slice of the energy value chain.
