# Andrew Stanley

A CISO cited as an early customer datapoint in the September 17th, 2021 — Wiz.io Pre-Brief (Shardul Shah) (Index Ventures' pre-brief on [Wiz.io](/notes/wiz-io), then in its first quarter of commercialization). Kyle's notes describe Stanley as "a snobbish CISO who agreed to a $500m ACV deal in <6 weeks" — invoked as evidence of Wiz's unusually fast enterprise traction, alongside a [Jim Routh](/notes/jim-routh) $1.3M three-year deal and a [Steve Ward](/notes/steve-ward) / [Home Depot](/notes/home-depot) deal in discussion. The point of the reference is the speed and size of the close from a hard-to-impress security buyer, supporting the thesis that Wiz could do "for Security what [Datadog ($DDOG)](/notes/datadog) did for Observability."

(Note: the source's "$500m ACV" figure is reproduced verbatim from the diligence note; the [Wiz.io](/notes/wiz-io) company page interprets it as a $500K ACV deal.)
