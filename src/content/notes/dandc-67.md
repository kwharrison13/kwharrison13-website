# D&C 67

Section 67 of the Doctrine and Covenants surfaces across several of Kyle's topical studies. In Patience — Topical Study, **verse 13** ("ye are not able to abide the presence of God now, wherefore continue in patience until ye are perfected") is the anchor verse — patience as the key to self-mastery and the path to perfection. Prayer — Topical Study cites verse 3 for the idea that **fear blocks answers to prayers**. Eye single to the glory of God references verse 10 in its cluster of texts on purifying one's eye toward God ([D&C 93](/notes/dandc-93), [D&C 88](/notes/dandc-88), [D&C 130](/notes/dandc-130)). And Revelation — Topical Study points to the section as a witness that standing as the voice of God is "dangerous in the eyes of men."

**Context:** Doctrine and Covenants Section 67 is a revelation given to [Joseph Smith](/notes/joseph-smith) in November 1831, at a conference where the elders were preparing to publish the revelations (the Book of Commandments). It includes the Lord's challenge to the elders to test the revelations by trying to write one themselves, and the promise that the pure in heart shall see God.

## Where this appears

- Patience — Topical Study — verse 13: continue in patience until perfected
- Prayer — Topical Study — verse 3: fear blocks answers to prayers
- Eye single to the glory of God — verse 10 among texts on the single eye toward God
- Revelation — Topical Study — the section on the danger of standing as the voice of God before men
