# Tom Mullen

**One-line:** *Chief Data Officer of [Riverside County](/notes/riverside-county) (10th largest US county), driving transparency, data infrastructure, and analytics across 125 systems of record.*

## What we know

- CDO of [Riverside County](/notes/riverside-county) — a role created ~3 years prior around transparency and citizen exposure into county operations; led a (ultimately stalled) effort to coalesce a single fiber build-out across the county's 28 cities, where private-sector investment would have run ~$2B (June 28th, 2018 — Tom Mullen (Riverside County)).
- Manages data across 125 systems of record (healthcare, financial, spatial) with no single management tool; wants a data-lake model long term and is rolling out Microsoft Power BI county-wide (June 28th, 2018 — Tom Mullen (Riverside County)).
- Articulates the obstacles to selling to government (leadership priorities, risk aversion, implementation/change-management risk, resistance to change) and the case for monetizing infrastructure access — light poles, roadway sensors, fleet telematics — instead of selling to government (June 28th, 2018 — Tom Mullen (Riverside County)).
