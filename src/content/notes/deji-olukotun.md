# Deji Olukotun

Sustainability lead at [Sonos](/notes/sonos) who served as a customer reference during Kyle's Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 (Watershed's Series B, Index Ventures). In a December 2nd, 2021 reference, he offered candid, mixed feedback: he liked Watershed's efforts but noted real friction in onboarding a 20-year-old company with highly distributed teams — "some of our data lives in PDFs that are not easily scannable." He acknowledged Watershed was building image-recognition software to ingest that data automatically, but doubted it could work without a human parsing the information, and described Sonos working to clean up how it organized its data, especially around Bill of Materials (BOM).
