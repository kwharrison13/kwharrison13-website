# David Daniels

David Daniels appears in Kyle's notes as the source of an "It Is Enough" teaching about sacrifice and acceptable offering. In [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book), "Bishop David Daniels" is quoted under "Sacrifice Brings Forth the Blessings of Heaven": a husband and father endures years of unbearable work, takes it to the temple and his bishop, and one day in the celestial room hears the Spirit whisper "it is enough" — Daniels reads this as God noticing and accepting the sacrifice, then strengthening and (in His will) freeing the man from his affliction. Kyle returns to the same story in his Fasting - September 5, 2021 gospel-study, paraphrasing the "It Is Enough" account directly after [Joseph F. Smith](/notes/joseph-f-smith)'s caution against overdoing fasting — pairing it with the idea that the Lord accepts "that which is enough" rather than that which is excessive.

## Where this appears

- [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book) — quoted as Bishop David Daniels on sacrifice and the "it is enough" temple impression
- Fasting - September 5, 2021 — his "It Is Enough" story paraphrased alongside teachings on not overdoing fasting
