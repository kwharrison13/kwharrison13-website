# D&C 31:12

A verse of the [Doctrine and Covenants](/notes/doctrine-and-covenants) quoted in [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book): *"Pray always, lest you enter into temptation and lose your reward."* It sits among the book's collection of scriptural and prophetic one-liners on discipleship.

**Context:** Doctrine and Covenants 31:12 comes from an 1830 revelation given through [Joseph Smith](/notes/joseph-smith) to Thomas B. Marsh. The verse is a short charge to constant prayer as protection against temptation.

## Where this appears
