# For My Sake

A recurring scriptural phrasing Kyle flagged while studying 3 Nephi 21, where the Father promises that "in that day, **for my sake** shall the Father work a work, which shall be a great and a marvelous work among them" (3 Nephi 21:9). Kyle reads this as an instance of the [Intercession of Christ](/notes/intercession-of-christ) — the Father's great [Marvelous Work](/notes/marvelous-work) done *for Christ's sake* — and pairs it with 3 Nephi 27:7 ("ye shall call upon the Father in my name that he will bless the church **for my sake**"). He tagged the pattern #[Gospel Topics to Explore](/notes/gospel-topics-to-explore), treating the repeated "for my sake" construction as a thread worth tracing through the Father–Son relationship.
