# Alex Koykova

A reference contact at [MongoDB](/notes/mongodb) reached during Kyle's Pave Diligence — 2021 work (Index Ventures' diligence on the compensation-planning startup Pave, fka Trove). Koykova was a [ChartHop](/notes/charthop) user, contacted via LinkedIn as part of the competitive/customer back-channeling on comp-planning tools. A ChartHop testimonial attributed to her at MongoDB is quoted in the diligence notes: ChartHop "helped us centralize over a hundred headcount planning spreadsheets into a single platform where we could easily monitor people's access, progress, and consolidate multiple plans with a couple of clicks."
