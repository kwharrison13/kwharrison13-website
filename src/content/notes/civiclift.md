# CivicLift

**One-line definition:** *[Evan Dobos](/notes/evan-dobos)'s civic-engagement platform that aggregates events and content for cities and towns and surfaces data insights for economic development.*

## How sources describe it

- Grew out of a website-development shop's municipal work; relaunched in late 2017 with traction in Connecticut (Farmington, Danbury, Waterbury); priced at $9,500/yr to stay under the $10K bid threshold. (May 16th, 2018 — Evan Dobos (CivicLift))
- Core value is aggregating content from any source (sites, social media) into one place; struggles with long municipal sales cycles and is planning a pivot away from towns as the direct customer.

## Related concepts

- [GovTech](/notes/govtech) — CivicLift sells software to municipalities, making it a govtech play subject to long public-sector sales cycles.
