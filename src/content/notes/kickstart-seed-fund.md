# Kickstart Seed Fund

Kickstart Seed Fund is a Utah-based early-stage venture firm that recurs in Kyle's notes as a node in the [Utah Tech Ecosystem](/notes/utah-tech-ecosystem) and a source of both deal flow and career/fund-building advice. Kyle held sourcing calls with two of its people: [Serene Papenfuss](/notes/serene-papenfuss) (Nov 2021), covering [Trinsic.id](/notes/trinsic-id), [CloudApp](/notes/cloudapp), and where talent was flowing in Utah (people leaving [Divvy](/notes/divvy) and [Pluralsight](/notes/pluralsight); product/engineering from [Lucid](/notes/lucid) and Pluralsight rated above average); and Gavin Christensen (Feb 2022), an advice-plus-LP-intro call.

The Gavin Christensen call is the densest reference. Christensen's advice to Kyle on fund differentiation was to "play a different game than the other guys" — "Are you willing to do something that other people think is stupid or a waste of time?" — and that going early is special because "that's when the relationship is formed... backing people when they're vulnerable." He also opened up Kickstart's LP relationships, naming Peter Madsen at Utah School & Institutional Trust Funds Office (SITFO), Brian Israelsen at Deseret Mutual Benefit Administrators, and Matt Ireland at Cumming Capital Management as LPs in Kickstart, and emphasized that Fundraising is "a very underrated part of the business for VCs." Kickstart also surfaces in Kyle's VC-research notes as the home of analysts like Sydnie Keddington and Tanner Potter.

**Context:** Kickstart Seed Fund (now often styled Kickstart Fund) is one of the most prominent early-stage venture firms in Utah and the Mountain West, an active backer of the regional startup ecosystem.
