# Alma 9

A chapter in the Book of Mormon that Kyle uses as a witness to the Book of Mormon's central covenant promise: **keep the commandments and prosper; keep them not and be cut off**. In his study of Alma 36–38, Alma 9:13–14 is cited alongside [Mosiah 1](/notes/mosiah-1):7 and [Alma 36](/notes/alma-36):30 — "Inasmuch as ye shall keep my commandments, ye shall prosper in the land … inasmuch as ye will not keep my commandments ye shall be cut off from the presence of the Lord."

The same verses anchor his The Process of the Book of Mormon notes, which collect the recurring formula across the text ([2 Nephi 1](/notes/2-nephi-1):20, [Jarom 1](/notes/jarom-1):9, [Omni 1](/notes/omni-1):6, [Mosiah 1](/notes/mosiah-1):7, Alma 9:13–14, [Alma 36](/notes/alma-36):30) and tie it to the principle that the Lord is bound when we do what He says ([D&C 82](/notes/dandc-82):10).
