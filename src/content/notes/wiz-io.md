# Wiz.io

**One-line:** *Agentless, full-stack, graph-based cloud-infrastructure security (CSPM) platform that brings context to prioritize actionable security signals across a customer's cloud estate.*

## What we know

- Building a full-stack, **agentless, context-rich** cloud-infrastructure security platform — a cloud-native scanner that collects from applications, open-source libraries, containers, VMs, cloud configs, identity providers, and the network, plus a graph that enriches the data (API-first, always on). (September 17th, 2021 — Wiz.io Pre-Brief (Shardul Shah))
- Core wedge: an "up-to-the-minute" view of security across all cloud resources that lets teams detect/respond without sacrificing developer productivity; deploys in **under 15 minutes**, avoiding the months-long POC/implementation that kills security projects. (September 17th, 2021 — Wiz.io Pre-Brief (Shardul Shah))
- Framed as potentially doing "for Security what [Datadog ($DDOG)](/notes/datadog) did for Observability" in the cloud; this was its **first quarter of commercialization** at the time of the pre-brief. (September 17th, 2021 — Wiz.io Pre-Brief (Shardul Shah))
- Early CISO traction cited: a [Jim Routh](/notes/jim-routh) $1.3M 3-year deal, an [Andrew Stanley](/notes/andrew-stanley) $500K ACV deal in under six weeks, a [Steve Ward](/notes/steve-ward)/[Home Depot](/notes/home-depot) 500K+ deal in discussion, plus Barclays and the [Cisco](/notes/cisco) CISO ("best vendor pitch in 2 years"). (September 17th, 2021 — Wiz.io Pre-Brief (Shardul Shah))
- Competition: [Palo Alto Networks ($PANW)](/notes/palo-alto-networks) (which "fumbled its first 2 acquisitions" in the space) and [Orca Security](/notes/orca-security), with well-funded startups (Apiiro and others) expected to pile in. (September 17th, 2021 — Wiz.io Pre-Brief (Shardul Shah))
- In the Salt diligence Wiz appears as a comparison point: [Prabhakar Kasu](/notes/prabhakar-kasu) described a similar onboarding/complexity process to Salt Security, and [Nir Rothenberg](/notes/nir-rothenberg) named it as the CSPM layer of the "must-have" security stack. (Salt Security Diligence — 2021)
