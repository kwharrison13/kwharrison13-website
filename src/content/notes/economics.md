# Economics

The field of economics as it appears in Naval Ravikant's intellectual arc: he studied economics and computer science and once considered a PhD, but came to distrust [macroeconomics](/notes/macroeconomics) as "the combination of voodoo, complex systems, and politics," where an economist can be found to take every side of every argument.
