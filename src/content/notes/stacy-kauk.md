# Stacy Kauk

Stacy Kauk appears in Kyle's notes in the Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 dossier as head of [Shopify ($SHOP)](/notes/shopify)'s Sustainability Fund. During Index Ventures' Series B diligence on [Watershed Climate](/notes/watershed-climate) (late 2021), she is listed under "Getting Intro" — a reference/expert-call target Kyle was working to reach through an introduction, as part of canvassing corporate-sustainability leaders to test Watershed's carbon-accounting product and the broader climate-tech market. Shopify was one of Watershed's named customers in the diligence, which made Kauk a relevant customer-side reference.

**Context:** Stacy Kauk has led Shopify's Sustainability Fund, an initiative that purchases carbon removal and offsets and helped seed early demand in the carbon-removal market.
