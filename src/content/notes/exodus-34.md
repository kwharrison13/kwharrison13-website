# Exodus 34

A scripture reference cited in Kyle's Fasting - September 5, 2021 gospel study. Exodus 34:28 — "And he was there with the LORD forty days and forty nights; he did neither eat bread, nor drink water" — is used as the scriptural example of Moses fasting, set alongside Elijah's forty-day fast ([1 Kings 19](/notes/1-kings-19):8) and Christ's forty-day fast before His ministry ([Matthew 4](/notes/matthew-4):1–4).

**Context:** Exodus 34 recounts Moses returning to Mount Sinai with the second set of stone tablets after the golden-calf episode, spending forty days and nights with the LORD without food or water while the words of the covenant are written.
