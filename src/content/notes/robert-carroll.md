# Robert Carroll

Founder of [Altaport](/notes/altaport), described by Kyle as an "AirBnB-esque helipad marketplace" / vertiport company planning 5–10 years ahead for the eVTOL future. Carroll came out of the advanced-nuclear world: he previously worked at [Oklo](/notes/oklo) (the advanced fission reactor startup) and wrote his Harvard Kennedy School graduate thesis on advanced nuclear energy, which he shared with Kyle as relevant background on why he got into nuclear. Kyle forwarded Carroll's company to [Pranav Iyer](/notes/pranav-iyer) in December 2021, noting it was probably too early-stage to be of interest but worth flagging given the strong thesis on energy and infrastructure.
