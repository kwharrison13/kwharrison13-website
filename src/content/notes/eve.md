# Eve

**One-line:** *The first woman, made from [Adam](/notes/adam)'s rib in [Genesis 2](/notes/genesis-2); beguiled by the serpent she partakes of the fruit, and is named Eve "because she was the mother of all living."*

## What we know

- Made from one of Adam's ribs to be a help meet for him ([Genesis 2](/notes/genesis-2):21–23).
- Beguiled by the serpent, she sees the tree is "to be desired to make one wise," eats of the fruit, and gives also to her husband (Genesis 3:6).
- After the Fall, Adam "called his wife's name Eve; because she was the mother of all living" (Genesis 3:20) — a verse Kyle highlighted.
- Bears [Cain](/notes/cain), [Abel](/notes/abel), and [Seth](/notes/seth) (Genesis 4).
