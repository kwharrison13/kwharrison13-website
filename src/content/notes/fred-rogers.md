# Fred Rogers

Fred Rogers appears in Kyle's notes on [Charlie Munger](/notes/charlie-munger)'s "Psychology of Human Misjudgment" as an exemplar of [Empathy](/notes/empathy) for all living things — quoted on his vegetarianism: "I can't imagine eating anything that had a mother."

**Context:** Fred Rogers (1928–2003) was an American television host, minister, and creator of *Mister Rogers' Neighborhood*, remembered for his gentle ethic of kindness, empathy, and respect for children.

- [Charlie Munger — The Psychology of Human Misjudgment](/notes/charlie-munger-the-psychology-of-human-misjudgment) — cited as an example of empathy for all creatures, with the vegetarianism quote.
