# Revelation 19

A chapter of the New Testament book of Revelation. In Kyle's Come Follow Me study July 20–26, 2020. Alma 36–38: "Look to God and Live" it is cited for [Revelation 19](/notes/revelation-19):10 — the angel's refusal of worship from John, ending with the key phrase "**for the testimony of Jesus is the spirit of prophecy.**" It is one of the scripture cross-references gathered for that week's reflection on testimony and turning to God.

**Context:** Revelation 19 depicts the heavenly rejoicing over Babylon's fall and the marriage supper of the Lamb; verse 10's line equating the testimony of Jesus with the spirit of prophecy is a frequently cited proof-text on testimony and prophecy.
