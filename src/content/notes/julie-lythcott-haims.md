# Julie Lythcott-Haims

Lythcott-Haims is cited in [The Self-Driven Child](/books/the-self-driven-child) as a supporting authority on overparenting, identified there as "former Stanford dean and author of How to Raise an Adult." The reference situates her in the same intellectual neighborhood as the book's core argument — that excessive parental control undermines a child's autonomy and sense of agency — drawing on her vantage point as a dean who observed the downstream effects of over-managed childhoods on college students.

**Context:** Julie Lythcott-Haims is an American author and educator, former Dean of Freshmen at Stanford University, whose 2015 book *How to Raise an Adult* critiques overparenting and the "checklisted childhood."
