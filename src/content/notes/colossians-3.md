# Colossians 3

Colossians 3 appears in Kyle's Mighty Repentance study as one of a cluster of "new creature" scriptures on conversion. He cites Colossians 3:10 — "Put on a new man" — alongside [Romans 6](/notes/romans-6):6 (crucify the old man of sin), [2 Corinthians 5](/notes/2-corinthians-5):17 (become a new creature), [Alma 36](/notes/alma-36), and [Mosiah 27](/notes/mosiah-27), grouping them around the theme that genuine repentance is not just behavioral correction but a remaking of the self in Christ.

**Context:** Colossians 3 is Paul's exhortation to the Colossians to "seek those things which are above," to put off the "old man" with its sins and "put on the new man, which is renewed in knowledge after the image of him that created him" — a foundational New Testament text on spiritual rebirth.

## Where this appears

- Mighty Repentance — Colossians 3:10 ("Put on a new man") grouped with other "new creature" scriptures to frame repentance as being remade in Christ.
