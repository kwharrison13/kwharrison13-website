# William Wright

**One-line:** *Works in the City of [Salt Lake](/notes/salt-lake)'s economic development department, recruiting and retaining tech and green-tech companies and building a climate for local entrepreneurship.*

## What we know

- In Salt Lake's economic development department, focused on recruiting and retaining companies (and employees) to expand Utah's tech scene, with tech and green technology as the two big target sectors (June 28th, 2018 — William Wright (City of Salt Lake)).
- Describes the foreign trade zone (a tariff-advantaged way to keep jobs in the US) and an in-land port in progress, with goods rail-shipped from San Diego to Salt Lake and considered imported there (June 28th, 2018 — William Wright (City of Salt Lake)).
- Spends most of his time on local business and entrepreneurship — making it easier, for example, for University of Utah people to stay in Utah (June 28th, 2018 — William Wright (City of Salt Lake)).
