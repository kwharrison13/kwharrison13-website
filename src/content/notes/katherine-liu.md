# Katherine Liu

Katherine Liu is the co-founder and designer of [FormFn](/notes/formfn), the NFT-marketplace startup Kyle's team took to a seed investment committee on December 13, 2021. The IC memo describes her as an NFT artist and product designer (Stanford, IDEO, Instagram; Twitter @petrichorate) and co-founder alongside engineer [Matt Lim](/notes/matt-lim). The two had known each other for seven years — having met at a summer internship — and previously worked together on Oculus, Instagram, and other Facebook teams. The memo flags their complementary skillsets (Matt full-stack engineering, Katherine design) and notes that the first hundred artists on FormFn would come from her network.

Liu's prior work is cited as evidence of fast execution and genuine passion for the independent-artist audience: she ran a creative studio (Meridrop) for two years and co-built Mycoverse, Nifty Pixels, and Nifty Generator. The diligence treats her artist-side credibility and a16z "designathon" relationships as a sourcing advantage, but the round was expected to be competitive given help from Pear VC and interest from Li Jin at Variant.
