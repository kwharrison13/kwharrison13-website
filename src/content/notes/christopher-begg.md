# Christopher Begg

Christopher Begg is CEO, Chief Investment Officer, and co-founder of [East Coast Asset Management](/notes/east-coast-asset-management). He appears in [On the Nature of Long-Term Holds](/notes/on-the-nature-of-long-term-holds) as a voice on [Compounding](/notes/compounding), quoted as defining investing as "simply maximizing the rate of compounding for as long as capital can be employed net of fees and taxes" and noting how little compounding is discussed in investing.

## Where this appears

- [On the Nature of Long-Term Holds](/notes/on-the-nature-of-long-term-holds) — quoted on compounding as the essence of investing.
