# Honeycomb

Surfaces in Kyle's notes as a prospective design partner for [Contenda](/notes/contenda). In a March 2022 portfolio update, [Lilly Chen](/notes/lilly-chen) reported that Contenda was demoing its product for Honeycomb's CTO (linking honeycomb.io) and looking to secure a design partnership. That is the only context in which Honeycomb appears here — as a target enterprise reference for a portfolio company's content tooling, not as a company Kyle diligenced directly.

**Context:** Honeycomb (honeycomb.io) is an observability platform for debugging production software, founded in 2016 by Charity Majors and Christine Yen, known for popularizing high-cardinality event-based observability and the practice of "observability-driven development."
