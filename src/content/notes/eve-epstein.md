# Eve Epstein

Eve Epstein appears in Kyle's August 18th, 2021 — Soko Data Room diligence notes as the **Chief Legal Officer (CLO)** of [Soko](/notes/soko), a pre-seed startup building a compliance engine and database of compliant independent contractors. The notes describe her as a former Trial Attorney at the U.S. Department of Labor with over 10 years of experience in independent-contractor law across the public and private sectors, and as the founder of SoleVenture. Her DOL background is central to Soko's thesis, which turns on programmatically monitoring contractor classification and compliance — the exact regulatory terrain she previously litigated.
