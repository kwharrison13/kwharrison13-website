# Everlane

Everlane appears in Kyle's corpus only as a named customer of [Watershed Climate](/notes/watershed-climate), listed in Index Ventures' 2021 Series B diligence dossier (Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021) among the apparel/consumer brands using Watershed's carbon-accounting platform. It is one of the customer logos cited as evidence that Watershed was expanding beyond its original tech-company base into businesses with more complex, physical-goods carbon footprints. The diligence does not record a direct reference call with Everlane — it surfaces only in the customer roster.

**Context:** Everlane is a San Francisco-based direct-to-consumer apparel brand founded in 2010, known for its "radical transparency" pricing and an emphasis on ethical, sustainable manufacturing.
