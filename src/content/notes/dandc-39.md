# D&C 39

Section 39 of the [Doctrine and Covenants](/notes/doctrine-and-covenants) surfaces in Kyle's gospel study in two distinct registers. In Spiritual Gifts, verse 6 is cited as evidence that the Holy Ghost functions as a *teacher* — "often, we say things which are beyond us, and in the process we learn things," delivering peaceable things and pure doctrines. In Chosen Leaders, the section is invoked under the heading "God has a work for us IF we qualify," tying the call to serve to personal worthiness and readiness.

**Context:** Doctrine and Covenants Section 39 is a revelation given to Joseph Smith in January 1831 directed to James Covill, who covenanted to obey any command the Lord gave him through the prophet but then rejected the word "because of the fears of persecution and the cares of the world." It promises blessings — including being ordained and the reception of the Holy Ghost — conditioned on obedience.

## Where this appears

- Spiritual Gifts — verse 6 listed among the functions of the Holy Ghost: the Spirit as teacher of peaceable things and pure doctrines.
- Chosen Leaders — cited under "God has a work for us IF we qualify," linking divine calling to personal qualification.
