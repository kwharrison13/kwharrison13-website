# Sarah Cannon

Sarah Cannon was a partner at [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) and one of Kyle's internal colleagues/mentors there. In a September 29th, 2021 feedback call she coached Kyle on demonstrating conviction more visibly — stepping into a leading and championing role on deals (as he had with Veda), structuring communication, and "bringing people along in the way you're thinking." Her framing was constructive rather than critical: "You've been nothing but collaborative and wonderful to work with. It's just trying to find ways to demonstrate the positives more often." She is referenced in the September 7th, 2021 log as someone Kyle spoke with about fundraising "back in the day," alongside [Chris Ahn](/notes/chris-ahn).

She also surfaces in Kyle's mid-2022 conversations as a known quantity in the venture world: [Katherine Boyle](/notes/katherine-boyle) (a16z) knows her, and [Arielle Zuckerberg](/notes/arielle-zuckerberg) mentioned talking to Cannon and being surprised to hear she was worried about Index's future.

**Context:** Sarah Cannon is a venture investor who was a partner at Index Ventures (and earlier at Coatue), focused on growth-stage and crypto/web3 investing.
