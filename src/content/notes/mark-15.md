# Mark 15

A chapter in the New Testament Gospel of Mark, cited in Kyle's gospel study. In the Come Follow Me page on June 15–21, 2020. Alma 13–16: "Enter into the Rest of the Lord", Mark 15:5 is referenced — "But Jesus yet answered nothing; so that Pilate marvelled" — Christ's silence before Pilate, drawn in as a cross-reference within that week's study.

**Context:** Mark 15 narrates Jesus's trial before Pontius Pilate, his condemnation, crucifixion, death, and burial.
