# Product School

Product School appears in Kyle's notes as a line in the background of [Hasan Luongo](/notes/hasan-luongo), the marketing expert interviewed in the Sprig fka UserLeap - Diligence. The reference is biographical only — no detail on Product School's model or programs is attested beyond its role as one of Luongo's prior posts.

**Context:** Product School is a US-based education company offering certificate courses in product management, product marketing, and related disciplines, largely taught by working practitioners. (General background; Kyle's notes attest only Luongo's Senior Instructor role there.)
