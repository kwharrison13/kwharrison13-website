# Eduardo Gavarret

A General Authority Seventy of [The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints](/notes/the-church-of-jesus-christ-of-latter-day-saints), from Uruguay. In Kyle's gospel study he is the speaker of the April 2022 General Conference address A Mighty Change of Heart: “I Have Nothing More to Give You” — built around the shipwreck-recovered ring inscribed "I have nothing more to give you" as an image of offering God a [Broken heart and a contrite spirit](/notes/broken-heart-and-a-contrite-spirit), and his boyhood story of Brother Félix Espinoza inviting him to teach a Sabbath lesson, the day of his own "mighty change of heart."
