# Valued Empyrean

Valued Empyrean is [Darwin Harrison](/notes/darwin-harrison)'s impact-oriented holding-company project, discussed with Kyle (and [Conor White-Sullivan](/notes/conor-white-sullivan)) across two 2021 conversations. As described in the August 18th, 2021 call, it is "a shared collective experience facilitated by a collection of entities," where one's level of buy-in to the philosophy dictates one's level of literal inclusion. "Family member" people and organizations built "the Value Way" can sit as wholly-owned subsidiaries of the holding company across five entity types — non-profit ventures, product producers, service providers, talent facilitators, and shared organizational resources — while adjacent collaborators relate through capital investment, sweat equity, or sponsorship. The governing principle inverts the usual order: **impact first, income second** — rather than [maximizing shareholder value](/notes/maximizing-shareholder-value) and hoping for leftover impact, Valued Empyrean designs income-generating relationships with long-term impact built in, so expertise and capital from the producers/providers/facilitators flow directly into the ventures. The name VALUE is an acronym ("Venture into a better way of life through Activity, Leisure, Unity, and Education"), and the framework leans on [Douglas Engelbart](/notes/douglas-engelbart)'s hypothesis and #[Tools For Thought](/notes/tools-for-thought) for its theory of continuous improvement.

The September 17th, 2021 call grounds the abstraction in a concrete vertical: a [Behavioral Health](/notes/behavioral-health) opportunity, where Valued Empyrean's "opportunity canvassing" reframes the sector to "deliver positive outcomes vs. financial incentives / behavioral disincentives" — getting into provider/clinic economics, group-therapy margins, and the front/mid/back-office breakdown of a [Business-in-a-Box](/notes/business-in-a-box) model.

Alongside the holding-company philosophy, [Darwin Harrison](/notes/darwin-harrison) and Kyle ran a round of **user research** for the [Value Way](/notes/the-value-way) learning/personal-development thesis — interviews probing how "proactive" people select what to learn, build habits, track progress, and fail to apply what they read. That research is aggregated in Valued Empyrean — User Research (its diligence-research context hub).
