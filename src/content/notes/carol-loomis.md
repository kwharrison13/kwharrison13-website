# Carol Loomis

Passing citation in *How to Take Smart Notes*, quoted on writing as a thinking tool: "Writing itself makes you realise where there are holes in things. I'm never sure what I think until I see what I write... the analysis part of you kicks in when you sit down to construct a story or a paragraph or a sentence."
