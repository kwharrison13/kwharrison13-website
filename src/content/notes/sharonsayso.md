# sharonsayso

An Instagram creator cited in the [Why Books Don't Work](/notes/why-books-don-t-work) long-read as an example of [TikTok](/notes/tiktok)-style, short-form education — a new medium that could be "as impactful as books originally were." The note records that Camden likes following sharonsayso on Instagram as an instance of this revolutionary form of learning.

**Context:** "Sharon Says So" (handle @sharonsayso) is the social-media persona of Sharon McMahon, a former high-school government and law teacher who built a large Instagram following delivering nonpartisan civics and history education in short-form video.

- [Why Books Don't Work](/notes/why-books-don-t-work) — used as a concrete example of TikTok-style education as a new, book-rivaling medium.
