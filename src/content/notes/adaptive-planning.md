# Adaptive Planning

**Adaptive Planning** (originally **Adaptive Insights**, now **Workday Adaptive Planning**) is a cloud FP&A and business-planning platform. It covers traditional financial planning and reporting plus adjacent use cases like sales and workforce planning, and was **acquired by [Workday](/notes/workday)** — which had been using [Anaplan](/notes/anaplan) before choosing to acquire Adaptive instead.

**Positioning (per insider expert call):** its biggest differentiator is **speed**. Where competitors like [Anaplan](/notes/anaplan) require you to model everything at a programming level with no built-in financial intelligence, Adaptive calculates directly from the data by variable and ships with financial understanding out of the box. It offers **unlimited data dimensionality, versions, and storage** (framed against rivals' hidden storage-driven cost of ownership), integrates via ETL and API — deeply into [Workday](/notes/workday)'s infrastructure while staying open to others — and wins business from Excel (~2/3 of the market still plans in Excel) and legacy systems like Oracle and SAP. Customers split roughly evenly across small, medium, and enterprise.
