# Gordon B. Hinckley

**One-line:** *Fifteenth President of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints; subject of Sheri Dew's authorized biography [Gordon B. Hinckley — Go Forward With Faith](/books/gordon-b-hinckley-go-forward-with-faith).*

## What we know

Drawn from [Gordon B. Hinckley — Go Forward With Faith](/books/gordon-b-hinckley-go-forward-with-faith):

- Lived by a single maxim — "get on your knees and ask for the Lord's help, then get on your feet and go to work" — fusing faith and relentless effort.
- Personal motto: "Things will work out." An attitude of abundance over scarcity; refused to dwell on the negative.
- Married to [Marjorie Pay Hinckley](/notes/marjorie-pay-hinckley), whose humor and optimism were the family's ballast.
- Known for operational discipline: meetings that started and ended on time, fast decisions he didn't re-litigate, people over programs.
- A polymath with an "integrated intellect" — read widely and retained, conversant with specialists across medicine, law, building, and history.
- Early and persistent advocate for using media/technology to spread the gospel, and for smaller temples built closer to the people.
