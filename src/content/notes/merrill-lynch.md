# Merrill Lynch

Merrill Lynch appears in Kyle's notes as a career-history reference. [Prabhakar Kasu](/notes/prabhakar-kasu) — the Global Head of Cybersecurity at [AON](/notes/aon) interviewed in the 2021 Salt Security Diligence — 2021 expert call — lists Merrill Lynch among his prior employers across 25+ years in technology (alongside Microsoft, GE, Thompson Reuters, Accenture, and others). The reference is purely biographical; Merrill Lynch is not analyzed as a subject in its own right. The name also surfaces elsewhere in the corpus (e.g. book notes on Buffett and Amazon) in passing.

**Context:** Merrill Lynch is a wealth-management and investment-banking firm, historically one of Wall Street's largest brokerages. It was acquired by Bank of America in 2009 during the financial crisis and now operates as part of that institution.
