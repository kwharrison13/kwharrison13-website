# Dave Linhardt

Founder & CEO of [InsightStudios](/notes/insightstudios), a startup studio whose pitch is that building startups is "part art and part science." Kyle spoke with him on July 10th, 2018 — see July 10th, 2018 — Dave Linhardt (InsightStudios). Linhardt argued against shared services early in a company's life: when a startup is still searching for a business model, it doesn't yet know which services it needs, and bundling them takes decision power away from the entrepreneur (he cited a Chicago studio, Ronan, that bills ~$800K of a $1M investment back in services). He had been involved with PSL but found their VC-led decision-making — geared toward Series A companies rather than building from scratch — incompatible with the studio model he believed in.

His pitch leaned on analogies (anyone can build a Formula One car but you also need a great driver; investing in InsightStudios now is "like investing in Marvel before the first Iron Man movie") and on the defensibility of a codified "Insight-Driven Methodology" — the Insight Lab / FounderSensei platform for validating problems and solutions with customers — which he framed as the durable moat in "a sea of incubators and accelerators." See [Venture Studio](/notes/venture-studio).
