# Herbert J. Simon

Cited in [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book) as the source of an observation about how organizational position shapes a person over time: "A man does not live for months or years in a particular position in an organization, exposed to some streams of communication, shielded from others, without the most profound effects on what he knows, believes, attends to, hopes, wishes, emphasizes, fears, and proposes."

**Context:** This quotation is from Herbert A. Simon's *Administrative Behavior* (the Nobel laureate social scientist who pioneered bounded rationality and organizational decision-making). The "Herbert J. Simon" attribution in the quote book is most likely a transcription error for Herbert A. Simon.
