# Abel

**One-line:** *Second son of [Adam](/notes/adam) and [Eve](/notes/eve), a keeper of sheep, whose offering of the firstlings of his flock the Lord respected, and who was slain by his brother [Cain](/notes/cain) (Genesis 4).*

## What we know

- Second son of Adam and Eve; "a keeper of sheep" (Genesis 4:2).
- Brought of the firstlings of his flock and of the fat thereof, and "the LORD had respect unto Abel and to his offering" — a verse Kyle highlighted (Genesis 4:4).
- Slain by his brother [Cain](/notes/cain) in the field; his blood "crieth unto me from the ground" (Genesis 4:8–10).
- [Seth](/notes/seth) is later appointed "another seed instead of Abel, whom Cain slew" (Genesis 4:25).
