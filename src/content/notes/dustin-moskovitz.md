# Dustin Moskovitz

Named in the November 8th, 2021 — Helion note as one of the investors in [Helion](/notes/helion)'s $500 million Series E (at a $3 billion post-money valuation, led by [Sam Altman](/notes/sam-altman)). The round — the largest-ever VC investment into nuclear fusion — also drew [Mithril Capital](/notes/mithril-capital) and Capricorn Investment Group, plus up to $1.7 billion in milestone-based follow-on. Moskovitz appears here purely as a co-investor in the fusion-startup deal.

**Context:** Dustin Moskovitz is a co-founder of Facebook and co-founder/CEO of the work-management software company Asana; he is also a prominent funder in effective-altruism and longtermist circles.
