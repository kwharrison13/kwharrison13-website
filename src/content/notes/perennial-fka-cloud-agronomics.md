# Perennial fka Cloud Agronomics

**One-line:** *Building a soil-carbon measurement platform to unlock soil-based [Carbon Offsets](/notes/carbon-offsets) at scale — aiming to underpin the soil-carbon credit market through measurement, registration, ratings, and eventually trading.*

## What we know

- Pitch (Nov 2021): soil has the carbon capacity to service ~25% of global credit demand but today provides <1% because generating a soil credit isn't profitable; their measurement breakthrough makes it profitable for the first time (source: Nov 5th 2021 intro email).
- Claimed partnerships with the 10 largest soil-carbon project developers/buyers, including Microsoft, John Deere, Rabobank, Archer Daniels Midland, and the Australian Government; monetize per acre across a developer's portfolio.
- Team drawn from NASA, Softbank, and Brown University. Founders include Jack Roswell (CEO) and David Schurman.
- Raising a Series A as of early Nov 2021 at ~$850k ARR; had pivoted a couple of times; Insight and other firms also in conversations.
- Introduced to [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) ([Zabie Elmgren](/notes/zabie-elmgren) → Nina Achadjian) in November 2021.
