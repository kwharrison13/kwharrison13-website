# John G. Turner

Author of [Brigham Young: Pioneer Prophet](/books/brigham-young-pioneer-prophet), the biography of [Brigham Young](/notes/brigham-young) that Kyle read in 2020. Turner's account is noted for its willingness to register both Young's institutional accomplishments and the durable problems he set in motion — Kyle's takeaways credit Turner with an honest treatment that resists the smoothing-over of later Church narrative, including the observation that "much of Joseph's policy in temporal things was different from my ideas" and the reframing of the handcart episode as a preventable institutional failure rather than heroic suffering.

**Context:** John G. Turner is a historian of American religion (Professor at George Mason University). His *Brigham Young: Pioneer Prophet* (Harvard University Press, 2012) won several awards and is regarded as a leading modern scholarly biography of the second president of the LDS Church.
