# Tesla

Tesla is the electric-vehicle and energy company led by [Elon Musk](/notes/elon-musk), and it threads through Kyle's corpus along two lines. As the subject company in [Elon Musk — Tesla, SpaceX, and the Quest for a Fantastic Future](/books/elon-musk-tesla-spacex-and-the-quest-for-a-fantastic-future), it's the through-line for Musk's first-principles, force-of-vision operating style. More distinctively, Tesla anchors one of Kyle's sharpest investing notes: in his takeaways from [Walt Disney and the Promise of Progress City](/books/walt-disney-and-the-promise-of-progress-city), he writes that "[Disney](/notes/disney) needs a [Walt Disney](/notes/walt-disney)-caliber CEO, and then they need to partner with Tesla — I used to say Disney should buy Tesla, but maybe Tesla should buy Disney." Tesla here stands in for the kind of city-building, beyond-your-lifetime ambition Kyle attaches to the Walt Disney archetype.

Tesla also appears in the Renegade Spotlight — Lowercarbon - Research notes as a climate-adoption case study: the first Tesla buyers were "mostly well-off hippies who cared about the planet," whereas today's EV buyers want a cheaper commute and fewer repairs — an illustration of how a green product crosses from values-driven early adopters to mainstream economic buyers.

**Context:** Tesla, Inc. ($TSLA) is a publicly traded electric-vehicle, battery-storage, and solar company founded in 2003; Elon Musk joined as an early investor and chairman and later became CEO.
