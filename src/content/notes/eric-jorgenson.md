# Eric Jorgenson

Eric Jorgenson compiled and edited [The Almanack of Naval Ravikant](/books/the-almanack-of-naval-ravikant), gathering roughly a decade of [Naval Ravikant](/notes/naval-ravikant)'s tweets, podcasts, and interviews into the book as a public-service project. In his own "Eric's Note (About This Book)" he describes Naval as a rare combination of successful and happy, and frames the book as collecting and organizing Naval's shared wisdom on wealth and happiness so readers can achieve the same. He's also credited with writing original content for the book in June 2019.
