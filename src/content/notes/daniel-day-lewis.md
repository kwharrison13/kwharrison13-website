# Daniel Day-Lewis

Actor referenced via the *Lincoln* funding anecdote on the [Steven Spielberg](/notes/steven-spielberg) page. The story: even with Day-Lewis attached as lead and [Steven Spielberg](/notes/steven-spielberg) directing, the film nearly couldn't get funded as a feature — used in Kyle's essay [The Existential Dread of Cognitive Dissonance](/essays/the-existential-dread-of-cognitive) as evidence that the blockbuster model pushes unique, character-driven stories to the periphery.

**Context:** Daniel Day-Lewis is a British-Irish actor widely regarded as one of the finest of his generation, known for total immersion in his roles and a record three Academy Awards for Best Actor — one of them for the title role in Spielberg's *Lincoln* (2012).
