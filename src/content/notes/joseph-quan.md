# Joseph Quan

Joseph Quan is the founder/CEO of [Knoetic](/notes/knoetic), the "all-in-one platform built with CPOs, for CPOs," tracked across Kyle's [Contrary Capital](/notes/contrary) portfolio-update reading. His regular updates are the throughline: the Q1 2022 update reported that over 2021 Knoetic 5x'd ARR (from $430K to $2MM+), 5x'd analytics WAUs, 3x'd the reach of its CPOHQ community (ending the year with 1,350 CPOs), and scaled the team from 6 to 30+ — while flagging its first-ever competition-driven downsells/churn (notably [ADP](/notes/adp) building its own analytics). Earlier updates (January 2022, Contrary - Portfolio Updates (Pre-Jan 2022)) described the CPO-platform thesis and a Synack onboarding; by September 2022 Quan announced a Series B — $36M to unify HR data, reshared by 150+ Chief People Officers and covered by TechCrunch.

He also appears as an angel/co-investor outside his own company: in the Candor seed round led by [Brianne Kimmel](/notes/brianne-kimmel), Quan participated alongside [Ellen DeSilva](/notes/ellen-desilva), [Andrew Farah](/notes/andrew-farah), and [Anamitra Banerji](/notes/anamitra-banerji).
