# RingCentral

RingCentral surfaces in Kyle's Cresta.ai - Diligence as a cloud-communications player making a strategic bet on the agent-assist category: in [Randy Clapp](/notes/randy-clapp)'s call it is named as "a lead investor in [Balto.ai](/notes/balto-ai)'s latest round." That detail anchors the recurring consolidation thesis in the contact-center notes — the PBX/CCaaS and cloud-comms incumbents are expected to acquire or back one of Cresta / [Balto.ai](/notes/balto-ai) / [Observe.ai](/notes/observe-ai) and fold real-time agent coaching into their platforms. The same investment is recorded on the [Balto.ai](/notes/balto-ai) page (RingCentral Ventures among Balto's investors).

**Context:** RingCentral (NYSE: RNG) is a cloud-based business communications company (UCaaS/CCaaS — phone, messaging, video, and contact-center software), founded in 1999 and headquartered in Belmont, California; RingCentral Ventures is its corporate venture arm.
