# The Chosen (show)

*The Chosen* recurs in Kyle's gospel study as a vivid mental reference point — the show gives him a concrete, embodied image of [Jesus Christ](/notes/jesus) and the disciples that sharpens his scripture reading. In his Alma 13–16 study he notes that having "a really powerful visual in my mind of who [Jesus Christ](/notes/jesus) is has made this more poignant" — when he is being unkind, he pictures a face and a reaction that lands on him. In the Alma 36–38 study he ties the show to the doctrine of being called and chosen: "In *The Chosen* you see that with the selection of Christ's disciples," reading it alongside [Lectures on Faith](/notes/lectures-on-faith).

In Fulfilling the Measure of Our Creation - September 5, 2021, Kyle reaches for a specific scene — Mary Magdalene in *Episode 6: "Unlawful"* falling back into old habits and returning to Christ weeping, "I just can't live up to it" — as an image of the struggle to live up to discipleship and the mercy that meets it.

**Context:** *The Chosen* is a multi-season crowdfunded television drama created by Dallas Jenkins that depicts the life and ministry of Jesus Christ through the eyes of those who met him.
