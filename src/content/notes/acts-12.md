# Acts 12

A chapter in the New Testament book of Acts, cited in Kyle's Original 12 Apostles roster as the reference for the martyrdom of the apostle James — "Son of Zebedee, brother of John... First martyred apostle, beheaded by Herod Agrippa ([Acts 12](/notes/acts-12))." It is the single point of attestation in that study for James (one of the "Sons of Thunder") being the first of the Twelve to die.

**Context:** Acts 12 recounts Herod Agrippa I's persecution of the early church — the killing of James the brother of John by the sword, Peter's imprisonment and angelic deliverance from prison, and Herod's own death.

## Where this appears

- Original 12 Apostles — cited as the scriptural reference for James, son of Zebedee, being beheaded by Herod Agrippa as the first martyred apostle.
