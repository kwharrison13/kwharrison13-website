# CoinDesk

In Kyle's corpus, CoinDesk is where [Bradley Miles](/notes/bradley-miles) led research after writing [Breaking into VC](/notes/breaking-into-vc), publishing roughly 100-page quarterly "State of Blockchain" reports that made him a recognized expert in the crypto space. It is the central illustration of Miles's own thesis — that you break into a field not by asking permission but by becoming so good at and known for a specific domain that doors open. His CoinDesk research, paired with the book as a credential, gave him the bitcoin/blockchain expertise that opened conversations with VCs, generated offers to start or join funds, and ultimately seeded his crypto-native creator-economy startup [FanCoin](/notes/fancoin) (via an EIR role at [Techstars](/notes/techstars)).

**Context:** CoinDesk is a digital-media and research company covering cryptocurrency and blockchain, founded in 2013. It is known for its market data and indices and for organizing the Consensus conference; its research arm published the recurring "State of Blockchain" reports referenced here.
