# Molly Alter

Investor at [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) whom Kyle built a relationship with during his Index sourcing era. In a June 2021 get-to-know call she reflected on early-career investing "scar tissue" — the constant sense of being insensitive — and described her family of journalists (sister at TIME, father a Newsweek writer and presidential biographer, mother a journalist at the Colbert Report and Daily Show, brother a sports reporter), joking that her family intro is everyone listing qualifications and then "I'm Molly, the other one." Later, in November 2021, she flagged [Buildots](/notes/buildots) for Kyle — an Israel-based construction-tech vertical SaaS competitive with [Openspace.ai](/notes/openspace-ai), distinguished by more granular, operational data (helmet-camera digital twins of construction sites) and higher engagement.

In Kyle's productization survey of 90+ investors on "what products does your firm offer," Alter answered for Index that compensation/ESOP support (the "comp stuff that Dom works on") was one of its SKUs.
