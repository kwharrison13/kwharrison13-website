# D&C 12

A section of the [Doctrine and Covenants](/notes/doctrine-and-covenants) that Kyle cites in his missionary-attributes study. In Scripture Study — Topical Study, **D&C 12:8–10** appears under the "Characteristics" of an effective missionary (from [Preach My Gospel](/notes/preach-my-gospel) chapter 6), grouped with [D&C 4](/notes/dandc-4) and [D&C 123](/notes/dandc-123):13 and the attributes of trust, prayer, and humility.

**Context:** Doctrine and Covenants section 12 is a brief 1829 revelation given through [Joseph Smith](/notes/joseph-smith) to Joseph Knight Sr. Verses 8–10 state that "no one can assist in this work except he shall be humble and full of love," naming faith, hope, charity, and love as the qualifications for laboring in the Lord's field.
