# University of Texas (UTIMCO)

UTIMCO (the University of Texas/Texas A&M Investment Management Company) appears in Kyle's notes as a prospective [LP](/notes/lp) in a March 2022 LP sync with [Eric Tarczynski](/notes/eric-tarczynski). In that status review across Fund 4 prospects — where Brown University and Emory had both passed — UTIMCO was still in play but blocked on access: Kyle's note reads, "I'd want to know who they know before we ask them for the intro since I've been patiently hunting for a strong intro to someone senior there." It captures the LP-fundraising reality that a large institutional endowment is only as reachable as the warm intro you can assemble into its senior staff.

**Context:** UTIMCO is one of the largest university endowment managers in the United States, investing the endowment and other funds of the University of Texas and Texas A&M systems. As a major institutional limited partner, it is a sought-after LP for venture and private-equity funds.
