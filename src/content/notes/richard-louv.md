# Richard Louv

Richard Louv is the author who coined "nature-deficit disorder." In [Education Nation](/books/education-nation), Louv argues some students labeled with ADHD may instead be suffering from a deficit of nature, and that "Nature's Ritalin" has a restorative effect on children without drugs.

## Where this appears

- [Education Nation](/books/education-nation) — argues ADHD labels may mask "nature-deficit disorder"; "Nature's Ritalin."
