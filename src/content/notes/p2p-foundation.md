The P2P Foundation surfaces in Kyle's notes through [Jean M. Russell](/notes/jean-m-russell), who appears in its "100 Women in P2P" profile series. That profile is one of the link-only research pointers Kyle saved for Russell (alongside her LinkedIn, jeanmrussell.com, and Twitter) while exploring whether [Thrivability](/notes/thrivability) connects to his [The New City](/notes/the-new-city) / [City Building](/notes/city-building) thinking.
