# Startups in Residence

Startups in Residence is a cohort program that [Mariel Reed](/notes/mariel-reed) — founder of [CoProcure](/notes/coprocure) — helped scale before starting her own company. Per her wiki page, the program acts "as a bridge between startup problem-solvers and cities," pairing startups with municipal partners. It sits squarely in Kyle's [City Building](/notes/city-building) / govtech cluster: the same problem space as Reed's later work on city [Procurement](/notes/procurement) and turning one-off government contracts into recurring ones.

**Context:** STiR (Startup in Residence) is a government-technology program, originally launched by the City of San Francisco's Office of Civic Innovation, that matches early-stage startups with government agencies to co-develop solutions to public-sector problems.
