# Khosla Ventures

Venture firm founded by [Vinod Khosla](/notes/vinod-khosla), appearing in Kyle's notes mainly through the people who passed through it. It is where [Erik Kriessmann](/notes/erik-kriessmann) ran the talent function before joining [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) (via [Coatue](/notes/coatue)): per the December 14th, 2021 — Erik Kriessmann (Index Ventures) call, Khosla's only success metric for the talent partner was whether founders said "I love him" — once a founder gave that answer, Khosla "didn't need to hear anything else," and Erik's job was simply to ensure Khosla spent the right time with the right people. Kyle uses this as a contrast with Index's more balanced founder-vs-board-member stakeholder model. Khosla Ventures also surfaces in Kyle's August 29th, 2022 daily log as one of the firms he spoke with (alongside [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) and Thrive Capital).

**Context:** Khosla Ventures is a Menlo Park venture firm founded in 2004 by Vinod Khosla (co-founder of Sun Microsystems and former Kleiner Perkins partner), known for aggressive deep-tech and clean-energy "black swan" bets.
