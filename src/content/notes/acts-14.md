# Acts 14

A chapter in the New Testament book of Acts, cited in Kyle's Abrahamic Covenant study as one of a cluster of proof-texts on priesthood authority and church offices. Specifically [Acts 14](/notes/acts-14):23 appears in a scripture list (alongside 1 Corinthians 12, Ephesians 4, 1 Timothy 3, Titus 1, and others) supporting the pattern that authority and offices are conferred by ordination and the laying on of hands rather than self-assumed.

**Context:** Acts 14 recounts Paul and Barnabas's missionary work in Iconium, Lystra, and Derbe — including the healing of a lame man and the crowd mistaking them for gods — and ends with them appointing ("ordaining") elders in every church (v. 23), the verse Kyle's notes draw on.

## Where this appears

- Abrahamic Covenant — cited (v. 23) in the scripture cluster on priesthood offices and the ordination of elders by the laying on of hands.
