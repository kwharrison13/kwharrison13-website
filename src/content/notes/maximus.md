# Maximus

Maximus is a company whose AngelList investment docs Kyle reviewed in June 2022 while clearing his inbox. The detail that stood out — and the reason the page exists — is the cap table: per the AngelList doc review, the deal was backed by a roster of [Super Angels](/notes/super-angels) drawn from consumer and crypto founders, including the founder of Bulletproof ([Dave Asprey](/notes/dave-asprey)), a co-founder of [Coinbase](/notes/coinbase) (Fred Ehrsam), a co-founder of Honest Company (Brian Lee), the CPO of Tinder (Brian Norgard), the former CMO of [Shopify](/notes/shopify) (Jeff Weiser), and the founder of Daily Stoic ([Ryan Holiday](/notes/ryan-holiday)), among others. In Kyle's notes the page functions mainly as an example of a [Super Angels](/notes/super-angels)-stacked cap table rather than a deep diligence file.

**Context:** Maximus is a direct-to-consumer men's health and hormone-optimization company (testosterone/TRT, weight-loss, and longevity programs) in the telehealth space, a category adjacent to the biohacking and wellness world its angel backers come from.
