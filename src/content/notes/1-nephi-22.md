# 1 Nephi 22

The closing chapter of the First Book of Nephi, in which Nephi explains the Isaiah prophecies he has just quoted. In Kyle's study it surfaces in two distinct threads. First, as a key text for the Abrahamic Covenant and the gathering of Israel: Nephi prophesies that "after all the House of Israel has been scattered… the Lord will raise up a mighty nation among the gentiles" and "will proceed to do a marvelous work among the gentiles… unto the making known of the covenants of the Father of Heaven unto Abraham" (1 Nephi 22:8–11) — which Kyle reads as a prophecy of the Restoration, with the sticks of Judah (Bible) and Joseph (Book of Mormon) becoming one (Abrahamic Covenant).

Second, in The Lehonti Principle, 1 Nephi 22:14 supplies the reassurance that the adversary's schemes ultimately recoil on himself — "they shall fall into the pit which they digged to ensnare the children of the Lord" — read alongside Amalickiah's repeated invitations to Lehonti ([Alma 47](/notes/alma-47)) as a study of how temptation works and why Satan never stops inviting (The Lehonti Principle). The chapter also appears as part of the 1 Nephi 16–22 — I Will Prepare the Way before You Come Follow Me block, where the back-half chapters (Nephi's reading and explanation of Isaiah) were noted as still to be filled in.

**Context:** 1 Nephi 22 is the final chapter of First Nephi (Book of Mormon), in which Nephi interprets the Isaiah chapters he quoted, covering the scattering and gathering of Israel, the latter-day "marvelous work," and the eventual judgment of the wicked.

## Where this appears
