# Discounting

The retail strategy of competing on relentlessly low prices, framed in Kyle's wiki as a flywheel. In [Wisdom From Walmart](/notes/wisdom-from-walmart), discounting is the engine of the [Walmart](/notes/walmart) virtuous cycle: increasing volume lets the retailer lower prices, lower prices grow market share, and greater market share builds structural advantages that keep the cycle turning.

## Where this appears

- [Wisdom From Walmart](/notes/wisdom-from-walmart) — discounting cast as a flywheel powering Walmart's volume-to-price-to-share virtuous cycle.
