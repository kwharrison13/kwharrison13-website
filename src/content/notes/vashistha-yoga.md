# Vashistha Yoga

Named only in a passing update-note in Naval Ravikant's recommended-reading list, where he adds it (alongside Jed McKenna, Kapil Gupta, and Schopenhauer) to his list of favorite philosophers/influences beyond Jiddu Krishnamurti and Osho. The book gives no further description of it.
