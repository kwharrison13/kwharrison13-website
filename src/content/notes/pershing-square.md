# Pershing Square

The activist hedge fund founded by [Bill Ackman](/notes/bill-ackman), whose investing principles Kyle captures as a section in [Investment Frameworks](/notes/investment-frameworks).

**Context:** Pershing Square Capital Management is a concentrated, activist-oriented investment firm founded by Bill Ackman in 2004. It runs a small number of high-conviction positions in large-cap, high-quality businesses and is publicly traded via Pershing Square Holdings.
