# Planful

**Context:** Cloud FP&A / financial planning platform (formerly Host Analytics). In October 4th, 2022 — Michael McElvaney (SoFi), [SoFi](/notes/sofi) had been running Planful for its standard income-statement work but found it weak on balance sheet, cash flow, and non-dollar KPIs — and reported "lots of bias against them." SoFi planned to migrate much of that work to [IBM Planning Analytics](/notes/ibm-planning-analytics) (TM1); McElvaney noted Planful was "convinced that weekly [forecast] updates were impossible to do."
