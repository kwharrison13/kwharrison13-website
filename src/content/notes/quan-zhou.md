# Quan Zhou

A team hire at [Pave](/notes/pave) cited as a positive signal in Kyle's Pave Diligence — 2021 dossier. Zhou joined Pave from [Affirm ($AFRM)](/notes/affirm), and the note records that [Zabie Elmgren](/notes/zabie-elmgren) knows him and "speaks super highly of" him — a reference Kyle treated as supporting evidence for the strength of Pave's team. He is listed among the company's notable senior hires alongside Nitin Gupta (from [Newfront Insurance](/notes/newfront-insurance)) and GTM talent drawn from [Carta](/notes/carta), [Segment](/notes/segment), and [Uber](/notes/uber).
