# Ada Support

**Ada Support** (Ada) is a customer-service automation / AI support company. Referenced here as the employer of [Joon Suh](/notes/joon-suh) and, in Kyle's diligence, as a [Vareto](/notes/vareto) customer.

Per the July 5th, 2023 reference call: Ada had no FP&A solution before ~2021, evaluated the FP&A landscape twice ([Planful](/notes/planful), [Pigment](/notes/pigment), [Mosaic.Tech](/notes/mosaic-tech), [Cube Software](/notes/cube-software), [Adaptive Planning](/notes/adaptive-planning), [Vareto](/notes/vareto)), and adopted [Vareto](/notes/vareto) (~$20–25K) for close, reporting, and dashboards. It runs ~36 cost centers and still forecasts in Google Sheets, with plans to expand into Vareto's planning module later.
