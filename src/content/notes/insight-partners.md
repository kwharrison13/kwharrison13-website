# Insight Partners

Insight Partners surfaces in Kyle's corpus as a global venture / growth-equity firm that recurs both as a deal counterparty and as a model of platform-style "productized VC." In the Noname Security - Diligence dossier it led [Noname Security](/notes/noname-security)'s **$60M Series B** (with Lightspeed also on the cap table); Kyle used the Insight round to triangulate valuation, reasoning that a comp like [Salt Security](/notes/salt-security) raising at $625M post around ~$5M ARR implied the Insight/Noname round was likely ~$300–400M post — and that founder appetite for $1B felt rich. It also appears as a $45M-round lead in the June 8th, 2023 — Amir Shevat (Darkmode Ventures) notes.

Beyond specific deals, Insight is cited as an exemplar of two of Kyle's venture themes. In The Productization of Venture Capital - Research, Insight's Eugenia (Bejar) Lustgarten describes the firm's large "Insight Onsite" team — structured into groups for talent, sales, marketing, corporate partnerships, and pipeline acceleration to help portfolio companies scale — as "pretty unique and differentiated." And in January 11th, 2022 — Dave Yuan (Tidemark), Insight is named (alongside TA Associates) as a firm that builds enduring partnerships by hiring people early and growing them with the firm.

**Context:** Insight Partners is a New York-based global software-focused venture capital and private-equity firm, known for large growth rounds and its "Insight Onsite" operations team that supports portfolio-company scaling.
