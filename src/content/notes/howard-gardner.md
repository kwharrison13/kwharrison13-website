# Howard Gardner

Howard Gardner is the Harvard psychologist who developed the theory of [Multiple Intelligences](/notes/multiple-intelligences). In [Education Nation](/books/education-nation) he is cited for documenting the "intrapersonal" and "interpersonal" intelligences among the eight multiple intelligences, and for reframing their role for twenty-first-century thinking and behavior.
