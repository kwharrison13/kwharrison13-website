# Ronald A. Rasband

A speaker Kyle takes notes on across multiple General Conferences of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. In Kyle's General Conference — October 2021 notes, Rasband is quoted invoking [Gordon B. Hinckley](/notes/gordon-b-hinckley)'s counsel to "try a little harder to be a little better," teaching that when we are "heavy-laden with feelings of disappointment or sin" we should think of the Savior's [Atonement](/notes/atonement), and citing Joseph Smith–History 1:12 and 2 Nephi 4:16 around the theme of pondering "the things of the Lord" in one's soul. He also appears in Kyle's General Conference — April 2020 notes.

**Context:** Ronald A. Rasband is a member of the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, ordained an apostle in 2015.

## Where this appears

- General Conference — October 2021 — quoted on trying to be a little better, the Atonement, and pondering "the things of the Lord" (JS–H 1:12, 2 Nephi 4:16)
- General Conference — April 2020 — listed among the conference speakers Kyle tracked
