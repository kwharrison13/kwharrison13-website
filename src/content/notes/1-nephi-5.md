# 1 Nephi 5

A Book of Mormon chapter cited in Kyle's Abrahamic Covenant gospel-study notes. There, 1 Nephi 5:14 is used to establish [Joseph Smith](/notes/joseph-smith)'s lineage of Ephraim: as Lehi searches the brass plates he finds he is a descendant of Joseph, tying the modern restoration through Joseph Smith (who "held the stick of Joseph (Ephraim) when he held the plates") back to the tribal line. The reference is grouped with [1 Nephi 6](/notes/1-nephi-6):2 and [Alma 6](/notes/alma-6):12.

**Context:** 1 Nephi 5 recounts Lehi and his family obtaining the brass plates of Laban; in searching them, Lehi learns he is a descendant of Joseph who was sold into Egypt.

## Where this appears

- Abrahamic Covenant — verse 14 cited on Lehi's descent from Joseph and the lineage of Ephraim
