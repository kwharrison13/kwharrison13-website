# Techstars

Techstars appears in Kyle's wiki as a node in [Bradley Miles](/notes/bradley-miles)'s path from crypto research into founding. After building a reputation through his [CoinDesk](/notes/coindesk) "State of Blockchain" reports, Miles got close to a managing partner at Techstars, became an EIR (entrepreneur-in-residence), and "raised capital off their expertise" for a stealth venture — initially framed as a Goldman-Sachs-style bank advising businesses on blockchain protocols, ICOs, security, and mechanism design — that ultimately converged on his creator-economy startup [FanCoin](/notes/fancoin). In the corpus Techstars functions as the institutional springboard for Miles's move from recognized expert to founder, per Kyle's May 2018 conversation with him.

**Context:** Techstars is a global startup accelerator and pre-seed investor running mentorship-driven programs that fund early-stage companies in exchange for equity.
