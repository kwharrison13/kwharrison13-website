# W. Craig Zwick

W. Craig Zwick is quoted in Kyle's Chosen Leaders gospel study for the line "Minister to the people rather than administer the program" — a distinction the study leans on to frame leadership as ministering (extending love, service, teaching, and testimony) over mere administration. He appears in the study's people index alongside [D. Todd Christofferson](/notes/d-todd-christofferson), [Boyd K. Packer](/notes/boyd-k-packer), and [Joseph Smith](/notes/joseph-smith).

**Context:** W. Craig Zwick (b. 1947) is an emeritus General Authority Seventy of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, having served in the Quorums of the Seventy beginning in 1995.

## Where this appears

- Chosen Leaders — quoted on "minister to the people rather than administer the program," supporting the study's ministering-vs-administering theme.
