# D&C 68

Section 68 of the Doctrine and Covenants recurs across Kyle's notes around two verses. The most-cited is **D&C 68:25** — the charge that parents in Zion who "teach them not" the doctrine of repentance, faith, baptism, and the gift of the Holy Ghost by age eight bear "the sin... upon the heads of the parents." Kyle flags this verse in his highlights of [Faith is Not Blind](/books/faith-is-not-blind) and again in his Alma 39–42 study, where he cross-references it with [Alma 42](/notes/alma-42) and [D&C 121](/notes/dandc-121) on the responsibility of teaching children.

He also draws on **D&C 68:4** in Spiritual Gifts — "the will and mind of the Lord are given by the Holy Ghost as scripture" — a verse about the Spirit-given nature of revelation.

## Where this appears

- [Faith is Not Blind](/books/faith-is-not-blind) — D&C 68:25 highlighted on parents' duty to teach children the doctrine of Christ
- July 27–August 2, 2020. Alma 39–42: "The Great Plan of Happiness" — D&C 68:25 cross-referenced with [Alma 42](/notes/alma-42) and [D&C 121](/notes/dandc-121) on parental teaching responsibility
- Spiritual Gifts — D&C 68:4 cited on the will and mind of the Lord given by the Holy Ghost as scripture
