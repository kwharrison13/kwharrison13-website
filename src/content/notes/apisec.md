# Apisec

Apisec appears in Kyle's [API Security](/notes/api-security) diligence as one of the vendors in the competitive landscape around [Salt Security](/notes/salt-security). In the Salt Security Diligence — 2021 reference call, [Atanu Dasgupta](/notes/atanu-dasgupta) (VP Product Development & Engineering at [Informatica](/notes/informatica), a ~$500K/year Salt customer) listed Apisec among the API/application-security tools he was familiar with, self-rating his familiarity 4 out of 5 — placing it alongside Signal Sciences (4) and [Imperva](/notes/imperva) (4), and below his 5/5 ratings for Salt Security and [Noname Security](/notes/noname-security). The mention is a single data point in mapping who competes in the emerging API-security category; no deeper product detail is recorded.

**Context:** APIsec (also styled apisec.ai) is an API security testing company offering automated, continuous testing for API vulnerabilities. This is general background; Kyle's source records only the name and Dasgupta's familiarity rating.
