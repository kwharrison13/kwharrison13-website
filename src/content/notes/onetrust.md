# OneTrust

OneTrust appears in Kyle's annotation on the [Todd McKinnon — Creating and Defining a New Market Category](/notes/todd-mckinnon-creating-and-defining-a-new-market-category) long-read, where he asks whether [Okta ($OKTA)](/notes/okta) will try to compete with or acquire into the same category as OneTrust, [BigID](/notes/bigid), and similar players — i.e. the data-privacy / governance category adjacent to identity.

**Context:** OneTrust is a privacy, security, and data-governance software company founded in 2016 by Kabir Barday, known for its tooling around privacy management, consent, GRC (governance, risk, and compliance), and third-party risk. It became one of the fastest-growing enterprise SaaS companies of its era.
