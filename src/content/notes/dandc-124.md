# D&C 124

A section of the [Doctrine and Covenants](/notes/doctrine-and-covenants) that Kyle cites in his Spiritual Gifts study. It appears with [D&C 123](/notes/dandc-123) in a cluster of references gathered under the gift of being a *peacemaker* (tied to [Henry B. Eyring](/notes/henry-b-eyring)'s May 2011 Ensign talk "Learning in the Priesthood"), alongside [D&C 88](/notes/dandc-88), [D&C 107](/notes/dandc-107), and [D&C 138](/notes/dandc-138).

**Context:** Doctrine and Covenants section 124 is an 1841 revelation given to [Joseph Smith](/notes/joseph-smith) at Nauvoo. It commands the building of the Nauvoo Temple, reorganizes Church leadership, and details priesthood offices and quorum structure — frequently mined for its teachings on order, callings, and stewardship in the priesthood.

## Where this appears

- Spiritual Gifts — listed with [D&C 123](/notes/dandc-123) under the "Peacemakers" priesthood-gift cluster, framed by [Henry B. Eyring](/notes/henry-b-eyring)
