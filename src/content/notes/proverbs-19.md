# Proverbs 19

A chapter from the Old Testament book of Proverbs, used in Kyle's scripture study as a cross-reference on parental correction and divine chastening. In the August 31–September 6, 2020. Helaman 13–16: "Glad Tidings of Great Joy" study, Proverbs 19:18 is cited verbatim — "Chasten thy son while there is hope, and let not thy soul spare for his crying" — tagged under parenting. It is paired there with [Proverbs 3](/notes/proverbs-3):11 as Old Testament support for the study's theme that God [Chasten](/notes/chasten)s those He loves.

**Context:** Proverbs is a book of Hebrew wisdom literature in the Old Testament, traditionally associated with King Solomon, made up of short maxims on righteous and prudent living.
