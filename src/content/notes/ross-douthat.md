# Ross Douthat

Ross Douthat is the framing source behind Kyle's notes on [Building American Dynamism](/notes/building-american-dynamism). A New York Times columnist and Substack writer, Douthat released *The Decadent Society* (2020) — described in the notes as a survey of America's general apathy toward building a more dynamic future, and one of the last public critiques of American stagnation before the pandemic. His four indicators of malaise in the Western world — institutional failure and loss of civic trust, economic and technological stagnation, declining birth rates, and a derivative culture — supply the diagnostic scaffolding for the essay's argument that the technology sector is the only part of the U.S. economy to have sustained dynamism over the prior 25 years.

**Context:** Ross Douthat is an American conservative columnist for The New York Times and author of several books on politics, religion, and culture, including *The Decadent Society: How We Became the Victims of Our Own Success* (2020).
