# Headway

**One-line:** *Provider-infrastructure marketplace handling insurance credentialing and billing for therapists.*

## What we know

- Grouped with Alma and [SonderMind](/notes/sondermind) as behavioral-health "infrastructure providers": a two-sided marketplace that acquires consumers, panels/credentials providers to bill insurance, and pays out claims (September 22nd, 2021 — Adam Chekroud (Spring Health)).
- Captures only one part of the value chain and gets squeezed on rising CAC (acquiring consumers gets more expensive) and lower margin on provider payouts (September 22nd, 2021 — Adam Chekroud (Spring Health)).
- Lacks the employer channel that [Spring Health](/notes/spring-health) / [Lyra](/notes/lyra) / Ginger compete in — a B2B sales motion these infrastructure players aren't built for (September 22nd, 2021 — Adam Chekroud (Spring Health)).
