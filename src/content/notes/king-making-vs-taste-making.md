# King-Making vs. Taste-Making

**One-line:** *The distinction between conferring success by sheer capital/scale (king-making) and earning influence by curating quality and culture (taste-making).*

## How sources describe it

- Flagged in the research notes for [The Hits Business](/essays/the-hits-business) alongside [Tastemakers](/notes/tastemakers), under "At the Mercy of the Zeitgeist."
- Maps onto the essay's core tension: the **hits business** rewards king-making by scale (the biggest fund / biggest budget wins), while [A24](/notes/a24)-style players win by taste-making — and the durable winners combine both.
