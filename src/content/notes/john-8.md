# John 8

A chapter of the Gospel of John in the New Testament. In Kyle's Miscellaneous Scriptures gospel-study notes, John 8:31–33 is paired with [D&C 50](/notes/dandc-50):25 under the theme "Believe on me and know the truth" — a cross-reference linking Christ's promise that the truth "shall make you free" to its restoration-scripture echo.

**Context:** John 8 includes the discourse where Christ tells those who believed on Him, "If ye continue in my word... ye shall know the truth, and the truth shall make you free" (8:31–32).
