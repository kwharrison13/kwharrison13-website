# The Defiant Ones (movie)

*The Defiant Ones* is a documentary flagged as a recommended watch in the [David Perell](/notes/david-perell) / [Matthew Kobach](/notes/matthew-kobach) Twitter workshop notes (linked via #[movies-to-watch](/notes/movies-to-watch)).
