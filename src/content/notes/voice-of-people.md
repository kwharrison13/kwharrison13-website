# Voice of People

A spelling/tag variant of Kyle's essay-in-progress, [Voice of the People](/notes/voice-of-the-people) — the canonical page. It arose from a single #[Voice of People](/notes/voice-of-people) hashtag in [The Death of Expertise](/books/the-death-of-expertise), where Kyle ties Tomasenz/Orwell's "Thought Police" and "thought criminal" from *1984* to the dynamic in which the voice of the people, once it begins to govern, "focus[es] on resorting to the mean and eradicating any voices that fail to conform." The substantive idea — democratic-electorate epistemology, the tension between an informed electorate and mob conformity — lives on the [Voice of the People](/notes/voice-of-the-people) page, which the same book tags 5+ times as the through-line of that read.

## Where this appears

- [The Death of Expertise](/books/the-death-of-expertise) — one #[Voice of People](/notes/voice-of-people) tag linking the book's Orwellian "Thought Police" passage to the danger of the governing majority eradicating nonconforming voices; the canonical thread is [Voice of the People](/notes/voice-of-the-people)
