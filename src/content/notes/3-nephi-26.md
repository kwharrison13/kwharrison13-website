# 3 Nephi 26

A Book of Mormon chapter falling within the Come Follow Me study of 3 Nephi 20–26. In Kyle's notes it is listed as one of the week's chapters but is not yet annotated — the substantive notes for that study center on [3 Nephi 20](/notes/3-nephi-20) and [3 Nephi 21](/notes/3-nephi-21).

**Context:** 3 Nephi 26 records that Christ expounded all things to the Nephites from the beginning to the end, that the lesser part of these teachings was written so as to try the people's faith, and that the people had all things common among them.
