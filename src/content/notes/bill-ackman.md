# Bill Ackman

Activist investor and founder of [Pershing Square](/notes/pershing-square), whose investing principles Kyle captures as a section in [Investment Frameworks](/notes/investment-frameworks).

**Context:** Bill Ackman is the founder and CEO of Pershing Square Capital Management, a concentrated activist hedge fund. He is known for high-conviction public bets and campaigns (e.g., Herbalife, Canadian Pacific, Chipotle) and a concentrated, quality-business value style.
