# Self-Guided Learning

Self-Guided Learning is the thesis in [Naval Ravikant — The Angel Philosopher](/notes/naval-ravikant-the-angel-philosopher) that the desire to learn — not access to instruction — is the scarce resource. [Naval Ravikant](/notes/naval-ravikant) argues the formal education system is "completely obsolete," a path-dependent outcome from the historical need for daycare and containment, and contrasts it with learner-driven, [Reading](/notes/reading)-centric self-education.

## Where this appears

- [Naval Ravikant — The Angel Philosopher](/notes/naval-ravikant-the-angel-philosopher) — paired with [Reading](/notes/reading) as the "desire-to-learn-is-the-scarce-resource" thesis against the obsolescence of one-size-fits-all education.
