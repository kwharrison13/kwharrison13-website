# Lisa Wehden

**One-line:** *Formerly of Bloomberg Beta; working on high-skilled immigration (O-1 visas) and climate as a new lens on the economy.*

## What we know

- Formerly at Bloomberg Beta (partnered with Karen there); splitting time between SF and NYC (source: July 27th, 2022 call).
- Went through the immigration process herself and is building a database of people who want to help others with O-1/H1B visas; wrote a [guide to applying for the O-1 visa](https://lisawehden.substack.com/p/a-guide-to-applying-for-the-o-1-visa) and works with the Institute of Progress.
- Sees [climate](/notes/climate-change) as a new way to look at the economy; explored [Carbon Offsets](/notes/carbon-offsets) and a biochar-as-a-service [Carbon Removal](/notes/carbon-removal) company (decided against it — too machine-led for SaaS multiples), now looking at tools to help ESG leaders make better judgements.
