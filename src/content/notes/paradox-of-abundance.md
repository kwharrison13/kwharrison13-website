# Paradox of Abundance

The named idea at the core of [News in the Age of Abundance](/notes/news-in-the-age-of-abundance): as information becomes abundant, the average degrades while the best improves. Abundance lowers the floor (more noise, more obsessive consumption, more [Negativity Bias](/notes/negativity-bias)) even as it raises the ceiling for the disciplined few who curate well.

**Context:** The "paradox of abundance" is a framing David Perell has popularized — when something becomes freely available, average quality falls while the best becomes better, putting the burden on the individual to choose well.
