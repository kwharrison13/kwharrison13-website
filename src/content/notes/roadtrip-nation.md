# RoadTrip Nation

RoadTrip Nation surfaces in Kyle's wiki as one of the portfolio companies [Andre Bennin](/notes/andre-bennin) worked on during his time at [Strada Education Network](/notes/strada-education-network) (then USA Funds). In Bennin's May 2018 intro call on Strada's investment platform, RoadTrip Nation is named as a deal where Bennin sat on the board — Strada's convention being that whoever leads a deal takes the board seat. It sits inside Strada's education and [Future of Work](/notes/future-of-work) mandate, the thesis Bennin's ~$1.5M seed fund was built around. The page exists only as a reference flagged during a connection sweep; the corpus carries no further detail on the company itself.

**Context:** Roadtrip Nation is an education media organization and PBS series in which young people travel the country interviewing professionals about how they found their careers, paired with a career-exploration platform used in schools.
