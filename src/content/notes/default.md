# Default

A sales lead-routing and qualification startup (withdefault.com), co-founded by [Nico Ferreyra](/notes/nico-ferreyra) and Victor, that Kyle tracks via investor updates. Per the November 2021 update, Default streamlines the inbound-lead funnel: it qualifies leads, lets unqualified ones convert to self-service, and routes qualified leads to an SDR or AE's calendar via an embedded form + scheduler. The product is built around forms, rules (route, label, grade), lead triaging by attributes (job title, company size, industry), and a tracking pixel, with enrichment of lead "data points" (funding, acquisitions, contact info, social accounts).

The October 2021 month — described as their "best month yet" — shows the early-stage cadence: 15+ features shipped, first customers onboarded, design partners recruited, the default.com domain acquired, SOC 2 Type 1 certification started (with Laika), and a roster of angel investors joining (Siqi Chen, Lauryn Isford, Kyle Parrish, Sean Whitney, Thibault Imbert, and base case capital, among others). A customer testimonial frames the core value: "Default is the lynchpin for our new sales funnel," automating manual lead-qualification so unqualified leads don't book time with the only AE. The update lists integrations shipped to HubSpot, Salesforce, Slack, Gmail (via Nylas), Airtable, and Zapier.
