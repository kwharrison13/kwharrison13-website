# Alma 48

A chapter of the Book of Mormon falling within the Come Follow Me reading block for August 3–9, 2020 (Alma 43–52). It is listed in that study's chapter range, but Kyle's notes for that week cover [Alma 43](/notes/alma-43) only — there is no annotation specific to Alma 48 in the source.

**Context:** In the Book of Mormon narrative, Alma 48 describes [Moroni](/notes/moroni)'s character — "if all men had been, and were, and ever would be, like unto Moroni, behold, the very powers of hell would have been shaken forever" — and his preparations to defend the Nephites.
