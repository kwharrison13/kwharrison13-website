# Dylan Field

Mentioned in passing in Kyle's March 30th, 2022 — 1x1 with Luke Piette notes, in a thread about SF member-spaces and their social dynamics. The note records that "one of our people got invited by Dylan Field" to [Shack15](/notes/shack15) — a San Francisco space described as catering to an older crowd — where the visitor "saw a bunch of the people from [Sequoia](/notes/sequoia) and [a16z](/notes/a16z)." The reference frames Field as a node in the SF founder/investor social scene Kyle and [Luke Piette](/notes/luke-piette) were mapping while thinking about the [LocalHost](/notes/localhost) penthouse and Contrary's own member space.

**Context:** Dylan Field is the co-founder and CEO of Figma, the collaborative design tool; Figma agreed to be acquired by Adobe in 2022 (a deal later abandoned in 2023).
