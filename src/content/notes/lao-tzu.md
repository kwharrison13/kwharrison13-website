# Lao Tzu

Ancient Chinese sage quoted in [Likability](/notes/likability) for the line "care about what other people think and you will always be their prisoner." In the [Likability](/notes/likability) note the quote (encountered via Twitter) anchors the tension at the heart of the concept: the pull toward being liked versus the freedom of not needing others' approval — Lao Tzu framing approval-seeking as a form of captivity.

**Context:** Lao Tzu (Laozi) is the semi-legendary 6th-century BCE Chinese philosopher traditionally credited as the author of the *Tao Te Ching* and the founder of Taoism.
