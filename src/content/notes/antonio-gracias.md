# Antonio Gracias

Antonio Gracias appears in [Elon Musk: Tesla, SpaceX, and the Quest for a Fantastic Future](/books/elon-musk-tesla-spacex-and-the-quest-for-a-fantastic-future) as an investor close to [Elon Musk](/notes/elon-musk), used by [Ashlee Vance](/notes/ashlee-vance) to characterize Musk's operating style. In the highlight Kyle saved, Gracias frames Musk's unconventional crisis management — when a problem represented "an existential threat against the business," Musk would personally take it on rather than delegate it to, say, a PR group, treating it as "the most important problem facing Tesla at the time." Gracias acknowledges that Musk's style has at times "made me cringe," but says he trusts it will work out in the end. He functions in the book as a sympathetic-but-clear-eyed witness to Musk's quick, enormous decision-making and prioritization instincts.

**Context:** Antonio Gracias is the founder of Valor Equity Partners and one of Tesla's and SpaceX's early and long-standing investors, having served on Tesla's board.
