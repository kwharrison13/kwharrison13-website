# Angus King

Angus King was the Governor of Maine who championed one-to-one laptops for students. In [Education Nation](/books/education-nation), [Milton Chen](/notes/milton-chen) recounts King invoking [Wayne Gretzky](/notes/wayne-gretzky)'s "skate to where the puck is going to be" to justify investing in a digital, scientifically literate workforce as Maine's industries like logging declined.

## Where this appears

- [Education Nation](/books/education-nation) — champions one-to-one student laptops; invokes Gretzky to argue for skating to where the puck is going.
