# Answers: Straightforward Answers to Tough Gospel Questions

A book by [Joseph Fielding McConkie](/notes/joseph-fielding-mcconkie) that Kyle cites in his [Come, Follow Me](/notes/come-follow-me) study of Alma 13–16 (June 15–21, 2020. Alma 13–16: "Enter into the Rest of the Lord"). It surfaces under the thread on [Women and the Priesthood](/notes/women-and-the-priesthood), alongside a quotation from [Spencer W. Kimball](/notes/spencer-w-kimball) about faithful women receiving foreordained assignments in the premortal world — used as a reference point for the question of women's relationship to priesthood and foreordination.

**Context:** *Answers: Straightforward Answers to Tough Gospel Questions* is a question-and-answer volume by Latter-day Saint author and BYU religion professor Joseph Fielding McConkie, addressing doctrinal and historical questions members commonly raise.
