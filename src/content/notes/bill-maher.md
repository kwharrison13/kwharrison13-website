# Bill Maher

**One-line:** *Comedian and political commentator; source of the "don't be a hypocrite" segment on tribal, side-driven belief.*

## What we know

- In a [segment](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EaSGOtlG_qk) ("Don't Be a Hypocrite"), argues Americans "must get over the fantasy that they are a people of core convictions and deeply held beliefs. They're not. They only care which side is saying something."
- Examples: liberals loved EVs until [Elon Musk](/notes/elon-musk) turned conservative; conservatives mocked Michelle Obama's health focus until RFK Jr. rebranded it "Make America Healthy Again"; the American Academy of Pediatrics reversed on school reopening once Trump favored it (via David Zweig's *An Abundance of Caution*).
- Kyle uses this as the negative pole: politically motivated about-faces are *antithetical* to conviction-led [Contrarianism](/notes/contrarianism).
