# Editing

Discussed in *How to Take Smart Notes* as "work that needs focus" — distinct from typo-fixing, it involves rephrasing sentences, cutting redundancies, and filling argument gaps, but framed as well-defined, motivating work precisely because the finish line is in sight (unlike open-ended writing/research).
