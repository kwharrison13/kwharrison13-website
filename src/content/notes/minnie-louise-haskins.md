# Minnie Louise Haskins

Author of the poem quoted in [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book) — the "gate of the year" lines beginning *"And I said to the man who stood at the gate of the year: Give me a light, that I may tread safely into the unknown!"* The passage, paired in the quote book with 2 Corinthians 5:7 ("we walk by faith, not by sight"), is collected for its theme of stepping into uncertainty by faith rather than by a known way.

**Context:** Minnie Louise Haskins (1875–1957) was an English academic and poet. The lines above are from her poem "God Knows" (also called "The Gate of the Year"), made famous when King George VI quoted them in his 1939 Christmas broadcast at the start of World War II.
