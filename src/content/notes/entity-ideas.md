# Entity Ideas

**Entity Ideas** is one of Kyle's personal capture tags — a marker he drops in marginalia when a passage sparks a potential company, venture, or business model worth building. It is idiosyncratic to his notes rather than a term from any source. In [Our Towns](/books/our-towns), Kyle attaches it twice while reading about the [Maker Movement](/notes/maker-movement): once on the observation that makerspaces are being "franchised like gyms" with memberships, where he tags `[[Franchises]] · [[Entity Ideas]]`; and again on close-to-the-customer, fast-turnaround small-batch manufacturing, where he pairs it with [Solopreneurs](/notes/solopreneurs). In both cases the underlying source material is the book's reporting on libraries, makerspaces, and low-volume "microfactory" manufacturing as revived community-and-economic engines; the `Entity Ideas` tag is Kyle's overlay flagging the latent startup inside the trend.
