# Episode 6: "Unlawful"

An episode of [The Chosen (show)](/notes/the-chosen-show) that Kyle references in his September 5, 2021 gospel reflection ("Fulfilling the Measure of Our Creation"). He recalls a scene in which Mary Magdalene falls back into old habits and returns to Christ weeping, saying "I just can't live up to it." Kyle uses the moment as an emotional touchstone for the reflection's theme — the gap between our ideals and our actual behavior, and the way returning to Christ rather than abandoning the effort is the point.

**Context:** *The Chosen* is a multi-season streamed drama about the life of Jesus Christ and his followers, the first major series crowdfunded by its audience. "Unlawful" is an episode in its first season.
