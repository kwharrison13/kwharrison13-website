# Iyinoluwa Aboyeji

Iyinoluwa Aboyeji appears in Kyle's notes as part of the [new-cities](/notes/city-building) crowd in Silicon Valley that the founders behind the [City Building](/notes/city-building) effort consulted during their May–July 2019 research push. He is named in the same circle as [Mark Lutter](/notes/mark-lutter), [Tamara Winter](/notes/tamara-winter) (then at the [Charter Cities Institute (CCI)](/notes/charter-cities-institute)), [Patri Friedman](/notes/patri-friedman), [Mike Solana](/notes/mike-solana), and [Jason Crawford](/notes/jason-crawford) — the figures the founders sought out while reading everything they could find on ancient, modern, and future cities and meeting master-planned-community developers. The same overlapping community is described in [Tamara Winter](/notes/tamara-winter)'s page.

**Context:** Iyinoluwa "E" Aboyeji is a Nigerian entrepreneur, co-founder of Andela and a former CEO of Flutterwave, and founder of the pan-African venture firm Future Africa; he is a prominent voice in African startup and development circles, which connects him to the charter-cities and new-cities conversation.
