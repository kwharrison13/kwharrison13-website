# TaskRabbit

In the Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021, TaskRabbit appears only as a career-history reference: [Erika Balbuena](/notes/erika-balbuena) (an ESG/sustainability lead at [Robinhood](/notes/robinhood) interviewed during the diligence) lists TaskRabbit as the first of her roughly 8–9 years in tech, before stints at [Twilio ($TWLO)](/notes/twilio) and [AWS](/notes/aws). It is biographical color on a reference contact, not a diligence subject in its own right.

**Context:** TaskRabbit is an online marketplace that matches freelance "Taskers" with people who need help with errands, moving, furniture assembly, and home tasks; founded in 2008 and acquired by IKEA in 2017.
