# Dave Greenbaum

**One-line:** *Founder at [OnPlan](/notes/onplan), an FP&A tool built on top of Google Sheets and BigQuery.*

- Framed OnPlan's thesis as building "an app store for spreadsheets" — the finance analog to what GitHub is for open-source engineering. (September 26th, 2022 — Dave Greenbaum (OnPlan))
