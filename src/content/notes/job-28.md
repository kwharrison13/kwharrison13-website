# Job 28

Job's poem on the inaccessibility of wisdom, whose refrain — **"But where shall wisdom be found? and where is the place of understanding?"** (v. 12) — is the framing question of [Russell M. Nelson](/notes/russell-m-nelson)'s "Where Is Wisdom?". The chapter concludes that wisdom is not mined like silver or gold; "the fear of the Lord, that is wisdom" (v. 28).

## Where this appears

- Where Is Wisdom? — Russell M. Nelson — vv. 12, the opening and closing question of the talk.
