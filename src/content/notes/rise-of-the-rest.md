# Rise of the Rest

**One-line definition:** *[Steve Case](/notes/steve-case)'s initiative — a multi-city tour and a $150M seed fund — backing startups outside Silicon Valley, NYC, and Boston.*

## How sources describe it

- Grew from four years and six tours across 33 cities into a $150M seed fund (co-led with J.D. Vance) backed by Jeff Bezos, Howard Schultz, Henry Kravis, and others. (May 16th, 2018 — Steve Case Interview (Rise of the Rest))
- Operates as a catalyst investor — no leads, no board seats — aiming to leverage $150M into $1B+ of investment in lower-valuation regional companies; thesis is that ~75% of VC concentrates in CA/NY/MA while great founders exist everywhere.
