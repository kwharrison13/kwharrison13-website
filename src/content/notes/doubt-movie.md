# Doubt (movie)

A film Kyle invokes in his annotations on [Charlie Munger — The Psychology of Human Misjudgment](/notes/charlie-munger-the-psychology-of-human-misjudgment), quoting Sister Aloysius's line "I have doubts! I have such doubts!" — connecting the picture's theme of moral certainty versus genuine doubt to Munger's catalog of misjudgment.

**Context:** *Doubt* (2008), directed by John Patrick Shanley from his own Pulitzer-winning play, stars Meryl Streep as Sister Aloysius, a nun who suspects a priest of misconduct without proof — a study in conviction held in the absence of certainty.
