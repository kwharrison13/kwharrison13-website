# Jesse Berst

**One-line:** *Leads the [Smart Cities Council](/notes/smart-cities-council), an industry coalition that accelerates smart-city readiness through vendor-agnostic content, coaching, competitions, and cohorts.*

## What we know

- Runs the [Smart Cities Council](/notes/smart-cities-council), a coalition of vendors, universities, and development banks known for a "triple bottom line" focus; helps cities, counties, states, and developers via readiness content, coaching, competitions, and cohorts (June 28th, 2018 — Jesse Berst (Smart Cities Council)).
- Argues the gold is in the data — the council aggregates data on smart-city projects so both cities and suppliers can make data-driven decisions (June 28th, 2018 — Jesse Berst (Smart Cities Council)).
- Maps the supply-side landscape (Cisco, Microsoft, AT&T mindshare lead, Intel priming the pump) and the "integration gap"; skeptical of procurement platforms, which he says have never succeeded (June 28th, 2018 — Jesse Berst (Smart Cities Council)).
