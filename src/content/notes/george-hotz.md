# George Hotz

Surfaces in Kyle's research for The Renaissance of Rise and Grind - Research as a flashpoint in the "extremely hardcore" hard-work discourse around [Elon Musk](/notes/elon-musk)'s Twitter. Hotz took a brief, public internship at Twitter under Musk in late 2022 and resigned weeks later — an arc that became a Twitter-reaction case study in the research file, sitting alongside the broader "[Bossism](/notes/bossism)" debate (the John Ganz / NYT framing that tech bosses were clawing power back from "entitled, lazy" workers). One saved reaction frames it approvingly: "This is the attitude that builds incredible things. Let all the people who don't desire greatness leave." The episode is collected, not endorsed — it's raw material for Kyle's essay on hustle/grind culture.

**Context:** George Hotz ("geohot") is an American hacker known for the first iPhone jailbreak and an early PlayStation 3 hack, and the founder of comma.ai, a self-driving / driver-assistance startup.
