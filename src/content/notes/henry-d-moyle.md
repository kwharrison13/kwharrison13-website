# Henry D. Moyle

An LDS Church leader quoted in [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book) in the missionary-service section. The line attributed to him: "A missionary, in his or her life, never rises above the stature they care for themselves of their mission." In the quote book it sits among other missionary-work quotes from figures like [Jeffrey R. Holland](/notes/jeffrey-r-holland), [Lorenzo Snow](/notes/lorenzo-snow), and [M. Russell Ballard](/notes/m-russell-ballard).

**Context:** Henry D. Moyle (1889–1963) was a member of the LDS Church's Quorum of the Twelve Apostles and later a counselor in the First Presidency, known in church history for his emphasis on missionary work.
