# Unserious

A tag Kyle attaches in [Discipline Is Destiny](/books/discipline-is-destiny) to Ryan Holiday's critique of a culture that confuses freedom with "license for stupidity, unseriousness, and excess." Holiday's list of false heroes — reality TV stars, influencers, professional wrestlers, YouTubers, demagogues — are framed not as people to admire but as "cautionary tales." The people genuinely worth admiring, on this view, are "quiet. Dignified. Reserved. Serious. Professional. Respectful of themselves and others."

Kyle's own gloss sharpens the point into a heuristic: "we should most respect the people whose lives speak the loudest, not their voices." The seriousness that matters is demonstrated in how a person actually lives, not in volume, visibility, or performance.
