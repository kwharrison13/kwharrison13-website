# Contrary Product Road Map

The Contrary Product Road Map is an internal [Contrary Capital](/notes/contrary) framing Kyle captured during a June 6th, 2022 call with [Joel Kwartler](/notes/joel-kwartler). It organizes Contrary's "product" around the three constituencies the firm serves, each split into sub-segments: **Talent** (Senior, Junior), **Customers** (Customers, Design Partners, Users), and **Investors** (VCs, Angels). The road map sits next to the [Contrary Flywheel](/notes/contrary-flywheel) in the same notes — a structured way to think about which audiences Contrary builds offerings for, against the backdrop of Kwartler's lessons on community-building, remote hiring, and curating high-touch meet-ups for people "adjacent to the same problems."
