# GiveMomentum.com

A fundraising-software startup founded by [Ari Kagan](/notes/ari-kagan), surfacing in Kyle's notes as a [Contrary Capital](/notes/contrary-portfolio-updates-mar-2022) portfolio company through Kagan's investor updates. Across 2022 the company iterated its target market sharply: in the March 2022 update it pivoted from mid-sized nonprofits — which proved reluctant to switch their entire fundraising systems — to **political organizations**, offering a campaign-page product and signing several political campaigns launching that spring.

By the October 2022 update (read in Kyle's November 7th email-clearing session), the company had paused all sales and engineering to run 100+ product interviews, narrowing the market further: political fundraising was monopolized by ActBlue, small charities were grant-focused, large charities too bureaucratic — leaving **mid-sized charities focused on lower-level individual donors** as GiveMomentum's specialty. The arc captured in the notes is a young company repeatedly resegmenting to find product-market fit.
