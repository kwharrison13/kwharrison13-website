# 3 Nephi 30

A chapter in the Book of Mormon's third book of Nephi, studied in Kyle's Come Follow Me week on [3 Nephi 27](/notes/3-nephi-27)–[4 Nephi 1](/notes/4-nephi-1) (October 19–25, 2020. 3 Nephi 27–4 Nephi: "There Could Not Be a Happier People"). Kyle reads this chapter together with [3 Nephi 29](/notes/3-nephi-29) as Mormon stepping out of the narrative to react to the people he has just seen in vision and is most frustrated by — those who refuse to acknowledge the power of Jesus Christ. He notes that both chapters are effectively Mormon "speaking directly to the people he's seen in vision."

**Context:** In the Book of Mormon, 3 Nephi 30 is the brief closing chapter of 3 Nephi: a direct call by Mormon, in the voice of the Lord, for the Gentiles to repent, turn from their wickedness, and come unto Christ so they may be numbered with the house of Israel.

## Where this appears
