# Rob Hodgkinson

Rob Hodgkinson is the person who pitched [SignalRank](/notes/signalrank) to Kyle on a June 6th, 2022 call. He described his own background as having done work similar to Seedcamp before joining SignalRank, the company "productizing pro rata" — using [Crunchbase](/notes/crunchbase) data (built by SignalRank's founder Keith) to rank investors into every unicorn, partner with the best seed managers, and buy into their breakout portfolios at the Series B. On the call he walked Kyle through the model's mechanics (20% carry to seed managers, 5-day drawdowns, ~$14B of available pro rata from 2021 unicorns alone), its permanent-capital company structure, and the candid pushback from [Oak HC/FT](/notes/oak-hc-ft) that the model doesn't make sense for most VCs because the ownership is too small.
