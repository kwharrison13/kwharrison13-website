# Planet Money

Planet Money surfaces in Kyle's notes as the source of an interview that fed his labor-history research. Its Episode 618, "The Square Deal," is where historian [Gerald Zahavi](/notes/gerald-zahavi) — author of *Workers, Managers, and Welfare Capitalism* — delivers the line Kyle pushes back against in his essay: that the [Square Deal](/notes/the-square-deal) is "an anachronism today. People don't expect much from corporations, period." The podcast episode is the primary attestation for Zahavi's quotes in the wiki and the entry point for the [Welfare Capitalism](/notes/the-square-deal) thread in Unions, Unions Everywhere - Research.

**Context:** Planet Money is an NPR podcast that explains economics through narrative-driven episodes; it launched in 2008 out of the financial-crisis coverage. (General background; Kyle's notes attest only Episode 618, "The Square Deal.")
