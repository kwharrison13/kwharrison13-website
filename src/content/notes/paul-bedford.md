# Paul Bedford

Head of policy at [Deliveroo](/notes/deliveroo) who picked up the company's ESG and sustainability work, having previously focused on climate tax. He surfaces in Kyle's Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 dossier as a reference call on November 22, 2021, alongside [David Hancock](/notes/david-hancock) (Deliveroo's investor-relations lead). In that conversation Bedford described Deliveroo's effort to be thoughtful across all three sides of its marketplace — working with consultants on what matters to consumers and piloting a cutlery opt-in feature to cut waste — while flagging that emissions data gathering was "a pretty complex piece" because Deliveroo doesn't already collect the vendor data it would need and there's no standardization. Deliveroo had been one of the prospective Watershed customers the Index diligence team introduced, so Bedford appears here as a buyer-side voice on the maturity of corporate carbon programs rather than as a Watershed customer.
