# GovLove Podcast

A podcast about local government and city-building that serves, in Kyle's notes, as the discovery channel for one of his urban-planning reads. On [The New Urban Crisis](/books/the-new-urban-crisis) page it is logged under "Other" as *"where Kyle heard the book recommended"* — i.e., GovLove is the "how I heard about it" provenance for picking up Richard Florida's book. It sits alongside Kyle's broader [City Building](/notes/city-building) interest, which the book itself reads as a field manual for.

**Context:** GovLove is a podcast produced by ELGL (Engaging Local Government Leaders), featuring interviews with public-sector practitioners, mayors, planners, and authors on local governance and urban policy.
