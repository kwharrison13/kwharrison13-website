# Content Consumption

A recurring theme across David Perell's notes that passive consumption is a trap: it "feels so good — too good," giving a thin, two-dimensional view, while only writing about an idea yields a three-dimensional perspective ([Riding the Writing Wave](/notes/riding-the-writing-wave)). The same discipline-over-access thesis carries a warning against obsessive news consumption ([News in the Age of Abundance](/notes/news-in-the-age-of-abundance)).

## Where this appears

- [News in the Age of Abundance](/notes/news-in-the-age-of-abundance) — the discipline-over-access thesis and the warning against obsessive news consumption.
- [Riding the Writing Wave](/notes/riding-the-writing-wave) — consumption-as-procrastination; the antidote is to write rather than keep ingesting.
