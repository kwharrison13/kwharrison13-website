# Ether 4

Ether 4 is a chapter in the Book of Mormon's book of Ether, falling within the Come Follow Me study block November 9–15, 2020. Ether 1–5: "Rend That Veil of Unbelief" — the unit centered on [Faith](/notes/faith), the [First Vision](/notes/first-vision), [Spiritual Complacency](/notes/spiritual-complacency), and the [Brother of Jared](/notes/brother-of-jared)'s faith so great that the veil was rent and he saw the Lord. In that weekly page the chapter is listed among Ether 1–5 but carries no notes of its own ("No notes recorded for this chapter").

The chapter surfaces more substantively in Spiritual Gifts, where [Ether 4](/notes/ether-4):11 is cited as evidence of how the Holy Ghost works: it does not enforce, "but rather it nudges and persuades us to do good." There it joins a pattern-recognition list with [1 Nephi 1](/notes/1-nephi-1):1, [2 Nephi 32](/notes/2-nephi-32):5, [Moroni 10](/notes/moroni-10):5, and [John 14](/notes/john-14):26 on recognizing and acting on spiritual promptings.

**Context:** Ether 4 records the Lord's instruction to Moroni to seal up the writings of the brother of Jared, promising they will not be revealed to the world until people exercise faith "even as the brother of Jared did" — a chapter frequently read as a discourse on faith, revelation, and the conditions for receiving sacred knowledge.
