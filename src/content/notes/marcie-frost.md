# Marcie Frost

Marcie Frost is the CEO of [CalPERS](/notes/calpers) (the ~$380B California public-pension system) in Kyle's notes on the article [Almost $14 Billion Pulled From CalPERS Equity Managers](/notes/almost-14-billion-pulled-from-calpers-equity-managers). In an October 21 letter to the pension system's board, Frost disclosed that CalPERS had terminated most of its external equity managers — slashing their allocation from $33.6 billion to $5.5 billion — citing long-term underperformance. Her memo quantifies the case: "Over the last five years, traditional managers have underperformed their benchmarks by 48 bps and emerging managers by 126 [bps]." The reallocation pairs with CIO [Ben Meng](/notes/ben-meng)'s "renewed focus on performance and our ability to achieve our 7% assumed rate."

**Context:** Marcie Frost has served as Chief Executive Officer of the California Public Employees' Retirement System (CalPERS) since 2016.
