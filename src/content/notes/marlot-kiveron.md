# Marlot Kiveron

Marlot Kiveron is the sustainability lead at [Otrium](/notes/otrium) (a Dutch fashion-outlet marketplace) who appears throughout the Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 dossier — both as someone Kyle introduced to [Watershed Climate](/notes/watershed-climate) (a completed priority-diligence item: "`[x]` Introduce [Marlot Kiveron](/notes/marlot-kiveron) to [Watershed Climate](/notes/watershed-climate)") and as an expert reference call on November 4, 2021. Otrium is cited as a company "doing a lot of work on their sustainability program," and Kyle "know[s] the sustainability person there pretty well," making her a credible prospective customer to route into Watershed during its European expansion.

In her Nov 4 call she described Otrium's sustainability work — focused on reduce-and-remove, helping fashion brand partners report correctly, and a "Rescues" model that refurbishes damaged returns. On the vendor landscape she was a useful skeptic: she had evaluated [Patch](/notes/patch), [Sourceful](/notes/sourceful), Lume, [Planetly](/notes/planetly), and [Carbon Removal](/notes/carbon-removal) providers, complained that the "biggest BS is that it will be easy to connect data sources," noted growing skepticism of carbon removal (comparing it to "the CDOs in the financial crises"), and valued partners like Position Green that were open to integrating with other platforms.
