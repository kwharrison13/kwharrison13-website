# David Hancock

David Hancock leads investor relations at [Deliveroo](/notes/deliveroo), the UK food-delivery platform. He surfaces in the Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 (Index Ventures Series B work) as a November 22, 2021 reference call, conducted alongside [Paul Bedford](/notes/paul-bedford) (Deliveroo's head of policy, focused on climate tax and ESG/sustainability). At the time, Deliveroo had no dedicated IR team, and Hancock noted that one of the appeals of the IR role was the ESG story it lets the company tell. In the diligence, Deliveroo appears as a prospective Watershed customer rather than a diligence subject in its own right.

## Where this appears
