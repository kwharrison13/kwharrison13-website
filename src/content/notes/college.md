# College

Formal higher education as a lever on a person's opportunity set. In Where Is Wisdom? — Russell M. Nelson, Kyle pushes back on the "college doesn't matter" line as a privileged position: college remains "one of the most significant ways to change someone's opportunity set." The open question he keeps circling — also raised by [Hillbilly Elegy](/books/hillbilly-elegy) — is whether college can be a catalyst *for everyone*, or only for those who already carry the "yearning" for education that Nelson says "can make all the difference."

See [Education](/notes/education) for the broader theme; this page holds the narrower thread about the college credential specifically.
