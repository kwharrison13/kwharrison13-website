# Tamara Winter

Tamara Winter appears in Kyle's notes at the intersection of two threads: [Stripe](/notes/stripe)'s talent network and the [new-cities](/notes/city-building) community. In The Talent Vortex - Research she is listed among the [Stripe](/notes/stripe) (Stripe Press) people surveyed for Kyle's talent-vortex research. In [City Building](/notes/city-building) she shows up earlier in her career as part of the [new-cities crowd](/notes/city-building) in Silicon Valley — then at the [Charter Cities Institute (CCI)](/notes/charter-cities-institute) alongside [Mark Lutter](/notes/mark-lutter) — whom the founders consulted during their May–July 2019 research push, the same circle that included [Iyinoluwa Aboyeji](/notes/iyinoluwa-aboyeji), [Patri Friedman](/notes/patri-friedman), [Mike Solana](/notes/mike-solana), and [Jason Crawford](/notes/jason-crawford).

**Context:** Tamara Winter is the managing editor of Stripe Press, the publishing arm of Stripe, and was previously associated with the Charter Cities Institute. She is an active voice in tech and progress-studies circles.
