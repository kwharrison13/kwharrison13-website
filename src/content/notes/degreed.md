# Degreed

Degreed appears in [The Expertise Economy](/books/the-expertise-economy) as an example of where working professionals turn for ongoing learning instead of returning to their universities. The book quotes [Jaime Casap](/notes/jaime-casap), Google's education evangelist, observing that "people don't go back to their university when they need professional development. Instead, they turn to their company or to other solutions like [Degreed](/notes/degreed) or [Khan Academy](/notes/khan-academy)." Degreed is cited as part of the book's broader argument that the locus of skill development has shifted away from formal higher education toward continuous, workplace-adjacent learning platforms.

**Context:** Degreed is a workforce upskilling and learning-experience platform, founded in 2012, that lets organizations and individuals track, measure, and develop skills across formal and informal learning sources.
