# Sourceful

In the Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021, Sourceful recurs as a sustainable-packaging vendor and potential ecosystem partner. The notes repeatedly suggest introducing Sourceful as a partner to companies running carbon-removal or packaging programs — e.g. as a partner for Watershed's own carbon-removal efforts, and as a packaging supplier for a fintech prospect sending out physical cards. One referenced operator describes a workflow of reducing what she can and then "working with Sourceful to remove what she can't reduce," noting Sourceful was praised for being "super transparent" and had long courted her (it "harassed her for a long term to do the packaging part at her last job, so they were the first"). Sourceful is grouped with [Patch](/notes/patch) and "Lume" as carbon/packaging options under evaluation, marking it as a known, well-regarded player in the climate-supply-chain space inside Index Ventures' deal flow.

**Context:** Sourceful is a UK-based sustainable-packaging and supply-chain platform that helps brands source eco-friendly packaging and offers carbon measurement and removal services.
