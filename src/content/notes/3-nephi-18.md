# 3 Nephi 18

3 Nephi 18 is, in Kyle's gospel study, the chapter where **Christ teaches the pattern of prayer** — that we pray *to* the Father *in* the name of Christ. The December 14–20, 2020. Moroni 10: "Come unto Christ, and Be Perfected in Him" notes cite 3 Nephi 18:19–23 as the pattern Christ Himself taught: "ye must always pray unto the Father in my name," and whatsoever ye ask the Father in Christ's name "which is right" shall be given.

The Prayer — Topical Study draws on the same chapter twice: 3 Nephi 18:21 for praying *with faith* (in its catalog of *how* to pray), and 18:20–21 for the reminder that "it's in the name of Christ that we ask for that which is good" — tied to the power of standing as a witness of Christ.

**Context:** 3 Nephi 18 records the resurrected Christ instituting the sacrament among the Nephites and teaching them to pray always to the Father in His name, to watch and pray to avoid temptation, and to gather and minister to one another rather than cast anyone out.
