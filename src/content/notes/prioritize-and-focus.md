# Prioritize and Focus

Tagged as a bare hashtag in *How to Take Smart Notes* onto the passage introducing Niklas Luhmann's workflow — quoting him: "I never force myself to do anything I don't feel like. Whenever I am stuck, I do something else." The book frames a good structure as what lets a writer move seamlessly between tasks without losing the bigger picture. A tag rather than a discussed concept in its own right.
