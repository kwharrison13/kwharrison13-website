# Custify

Custify appears in the Planhat - Diligence expert calls as a smaller European [Customer Success](/notes/customer-success) platform — one of the alternatives to the dominant US incumbents ([Gainsight](/notes/gainsight), [ToTango](/notes/totango), [ChurnZero](/notes/churnzero)) and a European peer to [Planhat](/notes/planhat) and [Vitally](/notes/vitally). Its most concrete mention comes from Nick Taylor, Director of CS at Onfido, who evaluated ChurnZero, ToTango, Gainsight, and [Catalyst](/notes/catalyst) but ultimately **chose Custify** — selecting it because it was "the easiest to talk to and most flexible." That positioning (easy to work with, flexible for a non-standard customer) is the throughline of how Custify is described in the diligence: a nimble European option that wins on responsiveness rather than incumbency or brand.

**Context:** *Custify is a Romania-based customer-success platform for B2B SaaS companies, offering health scoring, lifecycle automation, and churn-reduction workflows; it competes in the lower/mid-market against larger US players like Gainsight and ChurnZero.*
