# Malachi 3

An Old Testament chapter cited in Kyle's Fasting - September 5, 2021 study, specifically Malachi 3:8–12 on tithes and offerings — "Will a man rob God? ... In tithes and offerings" (v. 8) and the promise to "prove me now herewith ... if I will not open you the windows of heaven, and pour you out a blessing" (v. 10). In that note it is paired with [Isaiah 58](/notes/isaiah-58):6–12 (the chosen fast that loosens the bands of wickedness and feeds the hungry), drawing a thread between fasting, fast offerings, and the covenant of giving to those in need.

**Context:** Malachi 3 is a chapter of the final book of the Old Testament, containing the messenger/"refiner's fire" prophecy and the tithing passage (vv. 8–12) frequently invoked in Latter-day Saint teaching on the law of tithing and the blessings promised to faithful givers.
