# OpenLoop Health

**One-line:** *A telehealth infrastructure startup (out of Iowa) that quietly powers the clinical backend for dozens of telehealth companies — including [MEDVI](/notes/medvi).*

## What we know

- Provides the clinical infrastructure layer that [MEDVI](/notes/medvi) rents; "the entire operation runs on OpenLoop Health." (source: The $1B Rorschach Test - Research)
- Suffered a **data breach in January 2026 that exposed 1.6M patient records** — evidence the compliance/safety problems weren't only at MEDVI's "marketing layer" but at the infrastructure layer too. (source: The $1B Rorschach Test - Research)

## Mentioned in

- The $1B Rorschach Test - Research — cited as the rented infrastructure proving MEDVI is not "solo" or an AI company.
