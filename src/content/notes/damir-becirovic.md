# Damir Becirovic

Damir Becirovic appears in Kyle's [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures)-era sourcing notes as an investor circulating consumer and frontier-tech deals and as a recognized network node in retail/ecommerce. He passed along the Muse.place brief (3D websites built on the open-source Spaces VR / WebGL framework) and authored the Backbone pre-brief — the mobile-gaming controller "Roku for gaming," where he sought approval to invest $10–15M for ~10% at a $120–150M post.

In the December 14th, 2021 — Benjy Boateng (Index Ventures) conversation on building executive relationships as a business-development function, Becirovic is cited as having a strong retail-and-ecommerce network — analogous to how [Shardul Shah](/notes/shardul-shah) "knows most of the senior people in security" — an example of the principle that each person should build relationship nodes around their own focus areas.
