# Rise Against Authority

"Rise Against Authority" is a thesis Kyle jotted in his September 17th, 2021 sourcing/diligence log: the idea that the [Creator Economy](/notes/creator-economy), the rise of entrepreneurship, and high employee turnover among millennials are all expressions of a single underlying drive — a desire for autonomy and a turn away from traditional institutional authority. It reads as a generational/cultural framing for why people increasingly opt out of conventional employment in favor of independent work and company-building.
