# Seymour B. Young

Seymour B. Young appears in [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book) as the attributed source of a quotation in the collection's section on death and God's sovereignty over life: "We know . . . that the issue of life and death and the welfare of the human family are in the hands of our great Creator," counseling the Saints to "leave all things in His hands after we have done our whole duty," to call upon the elders to anoint and pray, and to "bow in humility before Him and acknowledge His hand in all things." The surrounding quotes are drawn from early LDS leaders including [John Taylor](/notes/john-taylor) and [Wilford Woodruff](/notes/wilford-woodruff).

**Context:** Seymour B. Young was a leader in The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in the late nineteenth and early twentieth centuries, serving as a Senior President of the Seventy.
