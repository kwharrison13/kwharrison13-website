# Marcus B. Nash

Marcus B. Nash appears in the wiki through Kyle's notes on General Conference — October 2021 of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, where his Saturday Evening Session address is captured in a single line on missionary work and belonging: *"Share the gospel. Follow spiritual promptings. Share your life openly and naturally. Invite others to come and see, and come and belong."* It sits in the conference's recurring thread of loving one's neighbor and gathering others to Christ.

**Context:** Marcus B. Nash is a General Authority Seventy of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. (General background, outside the wiki sources.)
