# Psychology

The study of the mind and behavior, treated in Kyle's wiki as one of the core disciplines in Charlie Munger's [latticework of mental models](/notes/mental-models). In [Charlie Munger — A Lesson on Elementary Worldly Wisdom](/notes/charlie-munger-a-lesson-on-elementary-worldly-wisdom), Munger holds that thinking improves when knowledge is arrayed on a set of models drawn from multiple disciplines — and that psychology supplies some of the most important and most often-ignored of those models.

## Where this appears

- [Charlie Munger — A Lesson on Elementary Worldly Wisdom](/notes/charlie-munger-a-lesson-on-elementary-worldly-wisdom) — invoked as "a rule of Psychology": the discipline that explains misjudgment and belongs in any worldly-wisdom toolkit.
