# Fifth Discipline

**Why it's on the list:** *[Peter Senge](/notes/peter-senge)'s classic on the "learning organization" and systems thinking — surfaced because, in it, Senge called [Visa](/notes/visakan-veerasamy) founder Dee Hock one of the most original thinkers on the subject of organizations.*

**Where I saw it:** Cited in the Fast Company profile The Trillion-Dollar Vision of Dee Hock, where Peter Senge in The Fifth Discipline praises Hock's thinking on decentralized, self-organizing ("chaordic") institutions.
