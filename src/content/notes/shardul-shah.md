# Shardul Shah

An [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) partner and one of Kyle's most-cited sources on cloud-security and company-building during the 2021 sourcing/diligence period. Shah supplied the [Wiz.io](/notes/wiz-io) pre-brief — framing cloud-infrastructure security as the next [Datadog ($DDOG)](/notes/datadog)-scale opportunity ("Wiz has the opportunity to do the same for Security" that Datadog did for observability), built on an agentless, context-rich, full-stack platform that deploys in under 15 minutes (September 17th, 2021 — Wiz.io Pre-Brief (Shardul Shah)). His [Datadog ($DDOG)](/notes/datadog) teardown (September 22nd, 2021 — Shardul Shah on Datadog) distilled five company-building lessons Kyle preserved verbatim: be intentional about **premium pricing** as "positive friction"; **market to the practitioner** through educational content; expect the **TAM to expand** every six months; don't fear a competitive market (it signals demand); and **be creative about building ownership** by scooping up secondary.

Shah's distinguishing trait in Kyle's notes is his **security network as a moat**: December 14th, 2021 — Benjy Boateng (Index Ventures) cites him as the person who "knows most of the senior people in security so they don't have to know them," doubling as an advisory group for his own deals — with [Damir Becirovic](/notes/damir-becirovic) playing the analogous role in retail/ecommerce. He also surfaces in diligence on sales-rep tooling (September 22nd, 2021 — Shardul Shah (Sales Rep Tooling), an AB InBev deal at $8M) and the December 13th, 2021 — NCX Partner Presentation.

**Context:** Shardul Shah is a partner at Index Ventures known for leading the firm's investment in Wiz, one of the fastest-growing cybersecurity companies, alongside earlier security and infrastructure bets.
