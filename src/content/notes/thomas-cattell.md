# Thomas Cattell

A figure quoted in [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book), Kyle's verbatim collection of quotes gathered during his full-time mission. He appears once, on page 30 of the source, with a line on personal revelation: "testimonies do not come to us through osmosis, but through personal revelation from God." The surrounding quotes are from Church leaders and missionary mentors, suggesting Cattell was a mission-context speaker Kyle recorded.
