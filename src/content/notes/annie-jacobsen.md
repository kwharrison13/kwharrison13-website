# Annie Jacobsen

Author of [Nuclear War: a Scenario](/books/nuclear-war-a-scenario), which Kyle read in 2024 and rated 5 stars. The book reports a minute-by-minute nuclear-war scenario as investigative narrative — Kyle read it largely as a sourcing exercise for Anduril-thesis-adjacent material, tagging its accounts of subterranean warfare, space/satellite warfare, EMP risk, and chemical-warfare stockpiles as understudied defense surfaces. Jacobsen builds the scenario on the deterrence logic she draws from [Thomas Schelling](/notes/thomas-schelling) ("the power to hurt is bargaining power") and closes on the civilization-reset arc captured by [Albert Einstein](/notes/albert-einstein)'s "World War IV will be fought with sticks and stones."

**Context:** Annie Jacobsen is an American investigative journalist and author known for national-security books including *Area 51*, *The Pentagon's Brain*, and *Nuclear War: A Scenario* (2024). She was a Pulitzer Prize finalist.
