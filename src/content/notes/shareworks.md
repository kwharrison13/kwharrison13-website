# Shareworks

A legacy equity-management and compensation incumbent that appears in the Pave Diligence — 2021 dossier as part of [Pave](/notes/pave)'s competitive landscape. The diligence groups Shareworks with PayScale as the "legacy companies" Pave is breaking away from (versus earlier-stage startups like Assemble and Welcome), noting that customers are "super critical of the incumbents." Shareworks — owned by [Morgan Stanley](/notes/morgan-stanley) and associated with the Option Impact dataset — offers a compensation-planning module, but the diligence describes its UI as "super antiquated" and recounts a customer who is "in Shareworks but they'd rather be in [Carta](/notes/carta)." The dossier ultimately frames Carta, not Shareworks, as the only viable competitive threat to Pave.

**Context:** Shareworks by Morgan Stanley is an equity-compensation and cap-table management platform (formerly Solium, acquired by Morgan Stanley in 2019) serving primarily larger and later-stage companies; Option Impact is its private-company compensation benchmarking dataset.
