# Contrary - Portfolio Updates (Apr 2022)

A **collection tag**, not a concept — Kyle used #[Contrary - Portfolio Updates (Apr 2022)](/notes/contrary-portfolio-updates-apr-2022) in his June 15th, 2022 Roam daily note to group the [Contrary Capital](/notes/contrary) portfolio-company investor updates he read while clearing email. Each tagged update was split into its own private meetings page (all `publish: false`). The tag functions as an index over a batch of founder updates from that period rather than as a standalone idea; the substance lives on the individual meeting pages.

The April 2022 updates it gathers include Pastel (from Ted Moran — paid-marketing improvements, kudos to Ilya Abyzov of Forward Health), [Uprise](/notes/uprise) (from [Jessica Riolfi](/notes/jessica-riolfi)), [Knoetic](/notes/knoetic) (from [Joseph Quan](/notes/joseph-quan) — signed a Head of Sales, ex-[Square](/notes/square) / Box), [Hook.xyz](/notes/hook-xyz) (from [Jake Nyquist](/notes/jake-nyquist)), Brightland (from Aishwarya Iyer — term sheet with Idea Farm Ventures), [Aryeo](/notes/aryeo) (from [Branick Weix](/notes/branick-weix) — customer-support scaling), Lovd fka Chums (from Noah Elion), and [Lightyear](/notes/lightyear) (from [Dennis Thankachan](/notes/dennis-thankachan) — SaaS deals with Five Guys and Westfall Technik).
