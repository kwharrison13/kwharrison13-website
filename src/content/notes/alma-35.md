# Alma 35

A chapter in the Book of Mormon, falling within the range covered by Kyle's July 13–19, 2020. Alma 32–35: "Plant This Word in Your Hearts" Come Follow Me study. That week's range runs Alma 32–35, but Kyle's notes center entirely on [Alma 32](/notes/alma-32) (Alma's sermon to the poor Zoramites — affliction preparing the heart, compelled versus chosen humility, and [Faith](/notes/faith) as a seed). His source explicitly flags that the notes are partial and that Alma 33, 34, and 35 have no annotations of their own. Alma 35 is therefore listed here as part of the study range rather than with grounded commentary from Kyle.

**Context:** Alma 35 is a short chapter in the Book of Alma in the Book of Mormon, describing the Zoramites' reaction to Alma and Amulek's preaching and the Anti-Nephi-Lehies receiving the cast-out poor into the land of Jershon.
