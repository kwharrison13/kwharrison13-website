# The Outsiders: Eight Unconventional CEOs and Their Radically Rational Blueprint for Success

**Why it's on the list:** *[Will Thorndike](/notes/will-thorndike)'s study of eight unconventional CEOs and their capital-allocation discipline — surfaced through Thorndike's quote that the best investor outcomes come from businesses with an extremely long-term horizon that can excel "without the friction (and tax inefficiency) of worrying about exits."*

**Where I saw it:** Cited in [On the Nature of Long-Term Holds](/notes/on-the-nature-of-long-term-holds), the Yale case study Kyle annotated, where author Will Thorndike is quoted on the merits of long-horizon, exit-free ownership.
