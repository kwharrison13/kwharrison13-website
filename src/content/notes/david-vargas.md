# David Vargas

A figure in the [Roam Research](/notes/roam-research) ecosystem associated, in Kyle's notes, with the idea of a [Decentralized Community](/notes/decentralized-community) built around Roam. In Kyle's reading of [The Death of Expertise](/books/the-death-of-expertise), he repeatedly flags Vargas's "decentralized community with [Roam Research](/notes/roam-research)" discussion as a connection point — pairing it with [Malcolm Gladwell](/notes/malcolm-gladwell)'s "networks can't set goals" highlight and his own [Decentralized Brain](/notes/decentralized-brain) thread (the Pat Grady "latticework of memory" story). In [Conor White-Sullivan](/notes/conor-white-sullivan)'s June 24th, 2021 recruiting call, Vargas surfaces as someone the Roam community could "work with" — part of Conor's vision of learning from the community and using Roam Bounties as a "pressure release valve" to pay independent contributors building on the platform.

The recurring throughline is decentralized, community-driven knowledge work: Vargas appears wherever Kyle is thinking about how a distributed group of contributors (rather than a centralized org) can build and extend tools like Roam.

## Where this appears

- [The Death of Expertise](/books/the-death-of-expertise) — listed among the people Kyle ties to the book; the [Decentralized Community](/notes/decentralized-community) / Roam discussion, connected to Gladwell's networks-can't-set-goals highlight and Kyle's [Decentralized Brain](/notes/decentralized-brain) note
- June 24th, 2021 — Conor White-Sullivan (Roam Research) — named as a community contributor Roam could pay or partner with via Roam Bounties
