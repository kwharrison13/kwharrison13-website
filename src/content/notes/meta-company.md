# Meta (Company)

**One-line:** *Social-media and AI company (formerly Facebook) run by [Mark Zuckerberg](/notes/mark-zuckerberg); parent of Facebook, Instagram, and its family of apps.*

## What we know

- Reportedly building a cloud business to sell excess AI computing power, per a Bloomberg report cited in [It's Not a Lie if You Believe It](/notes/it-s-not-a-lie-if-you-believe-it) — the stock rallied ~10% on the news.
- On Meta's Q3 2025 earnings call, [Mark Zuckerberg](/notes/mark-zuckerberg) signaled the company would rather monetize overbuilt AI compute (via API services or external demand) than hoard it. (source: [It's Not a Lie if You Believe It](/notes/it-s-not-a-lie-if-you-believe-it))
