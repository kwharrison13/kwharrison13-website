# Mormon's Commentary

A Book of Mormon reading theme Kyle tracks: the editorial asides the prophet-historian Mormon inserts into his abridgement, especially the recurring reflective phrase "and thus we see." In the June 29–July 5, 2020. Alma 23–29: "They Never Did Fall Away" study, Kyle pairs Mormon's commentary with President [Boyd K. Packer](/notes/boyd-k-packer)'s "Therefore, what?" — both ways of forcing the reader to draw a lesson from the narrative — and links it to the related [And thus we see](/notes/and-thus-we-see) motif drawn from Alma 28.
