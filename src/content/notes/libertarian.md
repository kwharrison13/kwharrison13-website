# Libertarian

The libertarian label appears in [Naval Ravikant — The Angel Philosopher](/notes/naval-ravikant-the-angel-philosopher) as Naval's case study for why identities and labels are traps: he once identified as a libertarian but found himself defending positions he hadn't thought through, just because they were part of the canon — "creating identities and labels locks you in and keeps you from seeing the truth."

## Where this appears

- [Naval Ravikant — The Angel Philosopher](/notes/naval-ravikant-the-angel-philosopher) — Naval's former libertarian identity as the example of how labels lock you into unexamined positions.
