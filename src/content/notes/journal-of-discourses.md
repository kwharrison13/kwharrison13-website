# Journal of Discourses

A 26-volume collection of public sermons and addresses by early leaders of [The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints](/notes/the-church-of-jesus-christ-of-latter-day-saints), published 1854–1886, mostly delivered in the Salt Lake Tabernacle and transcribed by shorthand reporters. A primary-source window onto 19th-century Latter-day Saint teaching — including [Brigham Young](/notes/brigham-young)'s 1853 discourse on salvation (Salvation — Brigham Young, Vol. 1).
