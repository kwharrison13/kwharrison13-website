# Bethanye Blount

Associated with [Compaas](/notes/compaas), a compensation-tooling company, in Kyle's 2021 [Pave](/notes/pave) diligence work. In the November 1st, 2021 call with [Marie Szuts](/notes/marie-szuts) of [Figma](/notes/figma), Szuts said she thinks very highly of Blount, while noting that Compaas's product had ultimately failed for her use case — its comp-planning cycle tool didn't work, it was too small to fully deliver on its core product, and lacked some core functionality (she'd given the team extensive product feedback). Blount also appears in the Pave diligence research as a scheduled reference call (October 13th, 2021) under the Compaas thread.

**Context:** Bethanye McGregor Blount is a longtime engineering leader and co-founder/CEO of Compaas, a compensation-planning software company; she has also been a prominent advocate for diversity in tech.
