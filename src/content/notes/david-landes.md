# David Landes

Economic historian, listed as the author of [The Wealth and Poverty of Nations](/notes/the-wealth-and-poverty-of-nations) in Kyle's anti-library.

**Context:** David S. Landes (1924–2013) was an American economic historian and professor at Harvard. He is best known for *The Wealth and Poverty of Nations: Why Some Are So Rich and Some So Poor* (1998) and *The Unbound Prometheus*, works examining why economic development diverged so sharply across nations and the role of culture and technology in that divergence.
