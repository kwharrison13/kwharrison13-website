# Leland

Leland is a [Contrary](/notes/contrary) portfolio company whose investor updates Kyle followed through 2022; [John Koelliker](/notes/john-koelliker) writes them. The updates Kyle clipped track a marketplace business in search of [Product-Market Fit](/notes/product-market-fit). In the May 2022 update (June 6th, 2022 — Leland May Update (John Koelliker)), Koelliker noted inbound interest from potential Series A investors and strategic partners (e.g. Indeed) while staying focused on building. The July 2022 update (August 29th, 2022) framed PMF around reliable, affordable customer acquisition, "categorical resonance and discovery at scale," and the right expertise packaging.

By the September 2022 update (November 7th, 2022 — Cleared Email), Leland reported crossing a $1M annualized GMV run rate, growing GMV 3.25x year-over-year, and operating in 18 categories despite only officially launching 10 — with single-category GMV LTV north of $1,700 (net revenue LTV over $320). Koelliker noted constant inbound from firms about a Series A but said they didn't plan to raise until fall 2023, taking occasional meetings with top marketplace investors to identify the best partners.

**Context:** Leland is a marketplace startup (founded ~2021) connecting people pursuing competitive goals — graduate-school admissions, career transitions, exams — with vetted expert coaches and mentors. (Details here trace to Kyle's portfolio-update notes.)
