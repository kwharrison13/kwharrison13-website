# 2 Nephi 33

Nephi's closing chapter, which Kyle draws on for two verses. Verse 1 grounds his Testimony — Topical Study: "When a man speaketh by the power of the Holy Ghost the power of the Holy Ghost carrieth it unto the hearts of the children of men" — the basis for testimony as a living witness that invites the [Holy Ghost](/notes/holy-ghost) and carries truth to others' hearts. Verses 10–11 are the recurring proof-text on believing the words of Christ: "hearken unto these words and believe in Christ; and if ye believe not in these words believe in Christ… for they are the words of Christ… And if they are not the words of Christ, judge ye; for Christ will show unto you… that they are his words, at the last day." This passage anchors the "Christ Is Risen" study, the Mormon 7–9 week, and the Revelation — Topical Study discussion of learning what to listen for. (Testimony — Topical Study, Revelation — Topical Study)

Elsewhere the chapter supplies framing language: Plan of Salvation — Topical Study cites verse 9 on entering "the narrow gate, and walk[ing] in the straight path which leads to life," and Diligence cites verse 11 in warning that ease, comfort, and complacency are "of the devil." (Plan of Salvation — Topical Study, Diligence)

**Context:** 2 Nephi 33 is the final chapter of the Second Book of Nephi (Book of Mormon), Nephi's farewell in which he exhorts readers to believe in Christ and affirms that his words are the words of Christ.
