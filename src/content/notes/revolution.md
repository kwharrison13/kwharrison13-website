# Revolution

Revolution is the investment firm where [Steve Case](/notes/steve-case) serves as CEO/chairman, and the vehicle through which his [Rise of the Rest](/notes/rise-of-the-rest) seed fund operates. Per Kyle's notes, Case built Rise of the Rest from four years of touring 33 cities, then teamed with J.D. Vance on a $150M seed fund — backed by [Jeff Bezos](/notes/jeff-bezos), Howard Schultz, Henry Kravis, and others — to invest in founders outside Silicon Valley, NYC, and Boston. The fund is positioned as a catalyst investor: it won't lead rounds or take board seats, aiming to leverage its $150M into over $1B of follow-on capital. The thesis rests on Case's "third wave" of the internet (healthcare, farming, education), where domain expertise and proximity favor regional hubs over the three states that capture ~75% of US venture capital.

**Context:** Revolution LLC is a Washington, D.C.–based investment firm co-founded by AOL's Steve Case in 2005, spanning early-stage (Rise of the Rest), growth (Revolution Growth), and later-stage (Revolution Ventures) strategies.
