# Revelation 3

Revelation 3 is a New Testament chapter cited in Kyle's Come Follow Me study note on Helaman 13–16, August 31–September 6, 2020. Helaman 13–16: "Glad Tidings of Great Joy". It anchors the note's theme that "God [Chasten](/notes/chasten)s because He loves": Revelation 3:19 — "As many as I love, I rebuke and chasten: be zealous therefore, and repent" — is grouped with [Ether 2](/notes/ether-2), [Helaman 12](/notes/helaman-12), [Proverbs 3](/notes/proverbs-3), and [Job 5](/notes/job-5) as scriptural witnesses that divine correction is an expression of love rather than rejection.

**Context:** Revelation 3 contains the letters to the churches of Sardis, Philadelphia, and Laodicea, including the warning to the "lukewarm" Laodiceans (3:16) and the verse on loving rebuke (3:19).
