# PayPal Mafia

The network of early [PayPal](/notes/paypal) employees — [Peter Thiel](/notes/peter-thiel), [Max Levchin](/notes/max-levchin), [Elon Musk](/notes/elon-musk), [Reid Hoffman](/notes/reid-hoffman), [Roelof Botha](/notes/roelof-botha) and others — who went on to found or fund a large share of major tech companies. Central case study in [The Founders](/books/the-founders).
