# Ephesians 3

In Kyle's The Godhead study, Ephesians 3:9 is cited as scriptural evidence for the relationship between God the Father and Jesus Christ in the work of creation. The verse describes "a God, who created all things by Jesus Christ" — which Kyle reads alongside [1 Corinthians 8](/notes/1-corinthians-8):6 ("the Father OF WHOM are all things… one Lord Jesus Christ, BY WHOM are all things") to frame creation as a partnership of "the Father Architect and the Son Builder." The passage supports his argument against the classical Trinity: the Father and the Son are distinct beings working in perfect unity, not a single indivisible substance.

**Context:** Ephesians 3 is a chapter in Paul's epistle to the Ephesians in the New Testament. It centers on Paul's account of "the mystery of Christ" revealed to the Gentiles and concludes with a prayer that believers be "rooted and grounded in love." Verse 9 speaks of God who "created all things by Jesus Christ."

## Where this appears

- The Godhead — Ephesians 3:9 cited as proof that all things were created "of God BY Jesus Christ," supporting the Father-as-Architect / Son-as-Builder model of the Godhead.
