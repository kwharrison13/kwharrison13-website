# ESW Capital

ESW Capital appears in Kyle's wiki only as a prior employer of [Atanu Dasgupta](/notes/atanu-dasgupta) — the [Informatica](/notes/informatica) VP and [Salt Security](/notes/salt-security) customer who served as an expert reference call in the 2021 Salt Security diligence. Dasgupta's background lists him as VP Engineering at ESW Capital, between roles at Hewlett-Packard, Ventyx (an ABB company), and Informatica. The wiki carries no other detail on the firm itself.

**Context:** ESW Capital is a private-equity firm (associated with Joe Liemandt) that acquires and operates enterprise software companies under brands such as Aurea, Trilogy, and Crossover, known for a roll-up model and a globally distributed engineering workforce.
