Venture firm founded by [Lee Fixel](/notes/lee-fixel) (formerly Tiger Global). Led the $25M Series A in [Ernesta](/notes/ernesta) alongside True Ventures. In [Stewards of Capital](/essays/stewards-of-capital), Kyle describes Addition as a capable, thoughtful firm investing across categories from construction to lending to AI/ML — explicitly NOT a "Tiger 2.0" defined by the markups and capital excess of 2021. He notes having co-invested with them and seen that thoughtfulness firsthand.
