# Oklo

An advanced nuclear energy company that surfaces in Kyle's notes only through [Robert Carroll](/notes/robert-carroll)'s background. In the December 13th, 2021 — Altaport (Robert Carroll) thread, Carroll — the [Altaport](/notes/altaport) founder building an AirBnB-style helipad/vertiport marketplace — describes joining Oklo during his time at the Kennedy School, framing it as the operational expression of the thesis behind why he "got into nuclear." His shared Harvard grad-school thesis on advanced nuclear energy is the connective tissue: Oklo is referenced as the company where that advanced-nuclear conviction translated into work.

**Context:** Oklo Inc. is a U.S. advanced-nuclear startup developing small fast-reactor "powerhouses" (the Aurora design) aimed at producing reliable, clean baseload power from compact micro-reactors. It was co-founded by Jacob DeWitte and Caroline Cochran and later went public via a SPAC backed by Sam Altman.
