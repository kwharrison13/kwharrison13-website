# Ananyo Bhattacharya

Author of [The Man From The Future](/books/the-man-from-the-future), a biography of [John von Neumann](/notes/john-von-neumann) that Kyle read in 2024 and rated 4 stars. Kyle read the book largely as Anduril-thesis source material — von Neumann's story functions as a deep historical precedent for civil-military fusion, RAND-style applied research, self-replicating systems, and game theory. Beyond authoring that book, Bhattacharya is not discussed in Kyle's notes.

**Context:** Ananyo Bhattacharya is a British science writer and former physicist, formerly a science correspondent at *The Economist* and *Nature*. *The Man from the Future: The Visionary Life of John von Neumann* (2021) is his debut book.
