# Intermittent Fasting

Intermittent Fasting appears in Kyle's gospel study on Fasting (September 5, 2021) as the **temporal well-being** benefit of religious fasting — the link between the spiritual discipline of Fasting and its bodily, health-related effects. In that note's enumeration of fasting's benefits (closeness to the Lord, increased spiritual strength, greater compassion, stronger desire to serve), the "temporal well-being" item is cross-referenced to intermittent fasting, framing the physical-health dimension of periodically refraining from food and drink alongside the spiritual purposes.

**Context:** Intermittent fasting is a secular dietary pattern that cycles between periods of eating and voluntary abstention from food (common protocols include time-restricted eating windows). It is studied for potential metabolic and longevity benefits — a modern health framing distinct from, but here connected to, the religious practice of fasting.
