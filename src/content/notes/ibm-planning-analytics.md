# IBM Planning Analytics

**Context:** [IBM](/notes/ibm)'s enterprise planning and analysis platform, formerly known as TM1. In October 4th, 2022 — Michael McElvaney (SoFi), [SoFi](/notes/sofi) selected IBM Planning Analytics as its go-forward FP&A tool over [Anaplan](/notes/anaplan) (which came in far more expensive) and [Adaptive Planning](/notes/adaptive-planning). Cited strengths: strong out-of-the-box reporting, versioning/auditing to track changes, and the ability to consolidate data into a SQL database. Noted gap at the time: unlike Anaplan, TM1 lacked Google Sheets / Excel integrations.
