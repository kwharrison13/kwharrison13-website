# Exodus 22

A scripture reference cited in Kyle's Miscellaneous Scriptures gospel study. Exodus 22:12 appears in a list of the steps of repentance — specifically the step of "rectify problems" (restitution) — alongside confessing to God ([D&C 58](/notes/dandc-58):43), asking for forgiveness, forsaking sin ([Proverbs 28](/notes/proverbs-28):13), and receiving forgiveness.

**Context:** Exodus 22 is a chapter of case law in the Hebrew Bible covering property, restitution, and social obligations — including rules requiring a thief or a careless party to make restitution for what was lost.
