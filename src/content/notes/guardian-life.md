# Guardian Life

Guardian Life appears in Kyle's notes as a payer customer of [Spring Health](/notes/spring-health). In the September 22nd, 2021 call with [Adam Chekroud](/notes/adam-chekroud), Chekroud cites Guardian Life Insurance as "the biggest customer that will launch next year," offered as evidence that Spring's [Behavioral Health](/notes/behavioral-health) model can sell to payers and not just employers — part of his argument that even modest penetration of EAP-covered lives, multiplied by payers and government, clears $1B in ARR. Guardian thus functions in the notes as a concrete proof point for Spring's payer-channel expansion thesis.

**Context:** The Guardian Life Insurance Company of America, founded in 1860, is a large mutual life-insurance and benefits company headquartered in New York. It sells group benefits including disability, dental, and increasingly behavioral-health offerings to employers.
