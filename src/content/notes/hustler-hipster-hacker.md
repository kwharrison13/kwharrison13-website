# Hustler Hipster Hacker

The startup shorthand for the three complementary talents a founding team needs — mapped, in Kyle's margin note, onto [Scott Berkun](/notes/scott-berkun)'s "three kinds of people who are rare in this world" from [The Myths of Innovation](/books/the-myths-of-innovation):

- **Hustler** — those who are excellent communicators (sells, raises, recruits).
- **Hipster** — those who find interesting and useful ideas (design, taste, product).
- **Hacker** — those who can convert an idea into a realistic plan (builds it).

Berkun's point is that it is "exceptionally rare for one person to be good at all three" — the reason so much innovation is a team sport, and why founders who assume they are the exception "are probably wrong." Relates to [Idea Execution](/notes/idea-execution) (the hacker's domain) and the trust that lets the three actually collaborate.
