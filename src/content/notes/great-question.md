# Great Question

**One-line definition:** *A user-research tool noted for helping build panels of users and sharing insights within the app — contrasted against [Lookback](/notes/lookback)'s video-repository model.*

## How sources describe it

- Interesting difference in helping **build panels** of users (vs. [Lookback](/notes/lookback) as just a video repository). (see Sprig fka UserLeap - Diligence)
- In-app insight sharing is interesting and different; open question is how data gets into the platform. ([Rishi Ahuja](/notes/rishi-ahuja), [Square](/notes/square))
- [Yue Wu](/notes/yue-wu) ([Dropbox](/notes/dropbox)) sees it as not much different from [UserTesting](/notes/usertesting), which already has a collaborative research function.

## Where it shows up

- Sprig fka UserLeap - Diligence — discussed by Square and Dropbox experts.

## Related concepts
