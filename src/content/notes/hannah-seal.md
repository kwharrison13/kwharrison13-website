# Hannah Seal

An investor at [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) who appears in Kyle's 2021 sourcing and diligence notes as a deal-flow and pre-brief source. On a June 24th, 2021 call she compared two sales-tax/compliance plays — [Anrok](/notes/anrok) (SaaS-only, smaller companies) and Fenoa (real-time non-US invoicing, "everywhere Avalara wasn't focused," then further along with a fresh $20M raise) (June 24th, 2021 — Hannah Seal (Index Ventures)).

In November 2021 she supplied Kyle a verbatim investment pre-brief on [Workbounce](/notes/workbounce), a search-first knowledge hub for sales that integrates with existing tools (Slack, Salesforce, Notion, Google Drive) via a Chrome plugin to surface the right answer at the right moment (November 8th, 2021 — Workbounce (Pre-Brief from Hannah Seal)). The memo laid out the problem, solution, why-excited, team, and deal dynamics — the kind of structured pre-brief Kyle preserved as a diligence reference.
