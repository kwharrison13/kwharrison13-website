# William Dembinski

Head of Engineering at [Seso](/notes/seso), the agricultural-workforce-management vertical SaaS company [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) diligenced for a Series A in December 2021. Per the brief, Dembinski was previously an Engineering Lead at BuildingConnected and Autodesk, and studied at UCSB. He is listed as a co-founder/early-team member alongside CEO [Michael Guirguis](/notes/michael-guirguis) and Head of Product [Jordan Taylor](/notes/jordan-taylor) in the deal materials [Nina Achadjian](/notes/nina-achadjian) circulated ahead of the company's Tuesday presentation.

## Where this appears

- December 13th, 2021 — Seso (Series A) — listed in the team roster as Seso's Head of Engineering (ex-BuildingConnected, Autodesk; UCSB), one of the founders the investment committee was asked to evaluate.
