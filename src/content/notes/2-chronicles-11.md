# 2 Chronicles 11

An Old Testament chapter cited in Kyle's Abrahamic Covenant gospel-study notes. There, 2 Chronicles 11:14–17 is used to trace how the faithful from the northern tribes became a "remnant" — those who went back to the Southern Kingdom (Judah) when the kingdom divided, part of the note's larger discussion of scattering, gathering, and the tribes of Israel.

**Context:** 2 Chronicles 11 describes the early reign of Rehoboam after the kingdom split, including how the priests and Levites and the faithful of Israel left their lands in the north to come to Judah and Jerusalem to worship.

## Where this appears

- Abrahamic Covenant — verses 14–17 cited on the faithful returning to the Southern Kingdom as a remnant
