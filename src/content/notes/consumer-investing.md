# Consumer Investing

A theme in [A Short Guide to Spectacles](/notes/a-short-guide-to-spectacles): the thesis that new consumer form factors win by enabling content that couldn't exist before — the best videos will be the ones *only* a hands-free camera can record, formats that didn't exist before the device.

## Where this appears

- [A Short Guide to Spectacles](/notes/a-short-guide-to-spectacles) — the thesis that new form factors (like [Snapchat](/notes/snapchat) Spectacles) win by unlocking previously-impossible content
