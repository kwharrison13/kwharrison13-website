# The Information

The Information recurs across Kyle's wiki as a subscription tech-news outlet whose reporters are go-to expert sources on the venture and AI landscape. In the Updating The Hype Cycle - Research notes, Kyle's prep for the essay drew on a call with Kate Clark at The Information about Artificial Intelligence — mapping foundational models, platforms, and applications, and discussing why so much AI value was being "imagined" rather than captured. The outlet is also referenced in Kyle's startup-media writing: on the [Axios](/notes/axios) page, The Information is named alongside Axios as a "new platform" that "oscillated between larger-than-life coverage of big tech and politics… or dabbled in startup 'skepticism.'" It surfaces again in the January 24th, 2024 SF-trip outreach as the employer of journalist Natasha Mascarenhas, a contact Kyle planned to meet.

**Context:** The Information is a subscription-based technology news publication founded in 2013 by former Wall Street Journal reporter Jessica Lessin, known for deeply-reported scoops on tech companies and venture capital.
