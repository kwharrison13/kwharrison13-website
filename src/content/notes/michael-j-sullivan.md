# Michael J. Sullivan

Michael J. Sullivan is quoted in [The Magical Touch of Finn Beales](/notes/the-magical-touch-of-finn-beales) for the line on wonder and gratitude: "When you expect nothing from the world… everything is a wonder and every moment a gift."

**Context:** Michael J. Sullivan is an American fantasy author best known for the Riyria Revelations series; the quoted passage is drawn from his fiction.

## Where this appears

- [The Magical Touch of Finn Beales](/notes/the-magical-touch-of-finn-beales) — quoted for the "expect nothing… everything is a wonder" line on gratitude and presence.
