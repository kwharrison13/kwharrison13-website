# Platforms

A recurring theme in Kyle's long reads: internet platforms lower the cost of brand-building and collapse the gatekeepers of traditional media, letting individuals reach audiences directly. Bernie Sanders' unfiltered reachability builds voter trust ([Do You #FeelTheBern- Behind Bernie Sanders' Quest To Take Over The White House](/notes/do-you-feelthebern-behind-bernie-sanders-quest-to-take-over-the-white-house)); [Instagram](/notes/instagram) lets Finn Beales connect straight to brands and offset commoditized photo supply ([The Magical Touch of Finn Beales](/notes/the-magical-touch-of-finn-beales)).
