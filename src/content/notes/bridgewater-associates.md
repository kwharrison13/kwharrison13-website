# Bridgewater Associates

**One-line definition:** *Ray Dalio's hedge fund — a [Wordsmith](/notes/wordsmiths) culture that "shows, don't tells" via its published Daily Observations.*

## How it shows up

- ~70% attrition, individual "baseball cards" for everyone (cf. [Zappos](/notes/zappos)'s $1K quit bounty). "You think Bridgewater publishes their Daily Observations for fun?"
