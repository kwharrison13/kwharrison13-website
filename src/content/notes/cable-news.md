# Cable News

Cable news appears in Kyle's Great Softening research as one symptom in the "is America in decline?" thread. The notes place "the popularity of cable news" alongside [Conspiracy Theories](/notes/conspiracy-theories), the [Death of Expertise](/notes/death-of-expertise), and declining trust in institutions — grouping it with the forces that simplify complicated problems and erode shared, expert-mediated reality.

**Context:** Cable news refers to the 24-hour television news networks (CNN, Fox News, MSNBC) whose ratings-driven, opinion-heavy format is frequently cited in media criticism as a driver of polarization and declining trust in journalism.

## Where this appears

- [Conspiracy Theories](/notes/conspiracy-theories) — lists the popularity of cable news alongside conspiracy theories and the Death of Expertise as symptoms of declining institutional trust in the Great Softening research.
