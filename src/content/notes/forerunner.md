# Forerunner

Forerunner appears only once in the corpus, as the firm affiliation of Annie Wickman in a VC Platform Community webinar announcement for [Pave](/notes/pave) — the API-driven compensation-benchmarking startup Kyle was diligencing in 2021. Wickman ([Forerunner](/notes/forerunner)) was slated to speak alongside Mandy Krass (QED) about her experience using Pave's free comp-benchmarking product. The reference is incidental: Forerunner is named as a portfolio-platform partner of Pave, not as a subject of analysis.

**Context:** Forerunner Ventures is a San Francisco–based venture capital firm founded by Kirsten Green, known for early investments in consumer and commerce companies such as Warby Parker, Glossier, Bonobos, and Dollar Shave Club.
