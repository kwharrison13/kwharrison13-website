# Overconfidence Bias

Named in *How to Take Smart Notes* in the context of academics and students systematically underestimating how long writing will take, even under worst-case estimates (Kahneman 2013, 245ff). The book notes wryly that even researchers who study the Overconfidence Bias admit they fall for it too.
