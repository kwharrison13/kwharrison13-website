# MKBHD (Marques Brownlee)

In Kyle's [Tastemakers](/notes/tastemakers) note (developed in the essay [The Hits Business](/essays/the-hits-business)), MKBHD appears alongside [MrBeast](/notes/mrbeast) as an example of a **creator/tastemaker** — someone who *makes* taste — as distinguished from a **taste connoisseur** like A24, which *curates* it. Both kinds require a pulse on the market; for entertainment specifically, social media is that pulse, and MKBHD is cited as a creator who reads and shapes it.

**Context:** Marques Brownlee, known as MKBHD, is an American technology YouTuber whose consumer-electronics reviews are widely seen as influencing public reception of new gadgets — the real-world basis for treating him as a "tastemaker."
