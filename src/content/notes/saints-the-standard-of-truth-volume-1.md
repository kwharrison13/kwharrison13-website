# Saints: The Standard of Truth; Volume 1

A source Kyle draws on in his June 15–21, 2020. Alma 13–16: "Enter into the Rest of the Lord" gospel study. He cites it to resolve a detail in a [Wilford Woodruff](/notes/wilford-woodruff) account from the days of [Joseph Smith](/notes/joseph-smith): "I think in [Saints: The Standard of Truth; Volume 1](/notes/saints-the-standard-of-truth-volume-1) we learn that the person [Brigham Young](/notes/brigham-young) confronted was [Hyrum Smith](/notes/hyrum-smith)." It is listed among the week's referenced sources alongside [Lectures on Faith](/notes/lectures-on-faith), [Joseph Smith — Rough Stone Rolling](/books/joseph-smith-rough-stone-rolling), and the [Book of Mormon](/notes/book-of-mormon) — functioning in Kyle's study as a narrative-history reference for early Church events.

**Context:** *Saints: The Standard of Truth, 1815–1846* is the first volume of *Saints*, the official multi-volume narrative history of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, published by the Church and written in an accessible storytelling style.

## Where this appears

- June 15–21, 2020. Alma 13–16: "Enter into the Rest of the Lord" — cited to clarify that the person [Brigham Young](/notes/brigham-young) confronted in a Wilford Woodruff account was [Hyrum Smith](/notes/hyrum-smith); listed among the week's source references.
