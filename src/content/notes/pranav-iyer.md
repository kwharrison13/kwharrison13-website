# Pranav Iyer

An investor contact to whom Kyle forwarded [Altaport](/notes/altaport) — [Robert Carroll](/notes/robert-carroll)'s AirBnB-style helipad/vertiport marketplace — in December 2021, captured in the meeting note December 13th, 2021 — Altaport (Robert Carroll). Pranav's reply (tagged by Kyle under #[Sustainability](/notes/sustainability) and #[Climate Change](/notes/climate-change)) liked both the company and the thesis, and laid out the two intermittent-energy theses he was tracking: (1) fixing intermittency with better battery storage — citing a Stanford alkali-metal-chlorine battery paper and vehicle-to-grid charging via truck fleets — and (2) fixing it with alternative energy sources such as nuclear, desalination, and wave energy. Kyle's note frames Altaport as "probably too early" for Pranav's interest, suggesting Pranav invests at a later stage.
