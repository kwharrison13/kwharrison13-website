# Solutions Engineering

A go-to-market / technical function referenced in a March 2022 portfolio update from [Dennis Thankachan](/notes/dennis-thankachan) at [Lightyear](/notes/lightyear). The update notes Lightyear hiring Matt Pinto as its new Head of Solutions Engineering, alongside a new VP of [Customer Success](/notes/customer-success) (Imran Ulla) — the two appearing together as the buildout of Lightyear's post-sale and technical-sales motion as it signed its first paying SaaS customer (Bowery Farming) and began booking enterprise meetings (Apple, Neiman Marcus, Five Guys).

**Context:** Solutions engineering is a sales-adjacent technical role: solutions engineers (also called sales or pre-sales engineers) pair with account teams to scope, demo, and configure a product for a prospective customer's specific technical requirements, bridging the product and the buyer.
