# Irving Stone

Author of [Men to Match My Mountains](/notes/men-to-match-my-mountains), a book in Kyle's anti-library (books-to-read).

**Context:** Irving Stone (1903–1989) was an American writer best known for biographical novels including *Lust for Life* (Van Gogh) and *The Agony and the Ecstasy* (Michelangelo). *Men to Match My Mountains* is his narrative history of the opening of the American Far West.
