# Scripture Study

**Scripture study** is the discipline by which, in Kyle's gospel-study notes, reading becomes revelation rather than mere reading. The richest treatment is the concept-stub's topical-journal companion Scripture Study — Topical Study, which holds that scripture study and prayer demand attention — when the mind wanders, "don't shrug it off, stop, pay attention, and take what you wandered to before the Lord" — and that "the effect of sincere prayer and of careful scripture study is to ALWAYS feel an urging to DO things" ([Henry B. Eyring](/notes/henry-b-eyring)). 5 P's Never To Forget gives the concrete method: **Pray, Ponder, Pencil, Purpose, Pretty often** — five keys that turn Book of Mormon reading into revelation.

The theme recurs as the pairing of study with [Prayer](/notes/prayer): October 2021 General Conference is quoted that "studying is then another essential key to become a better disciple of Jesus Christ — prayer and scripture study go hand in hand." It connects outward to Parables, Spiritual Gifts, and the broader project of letting truth "sink into your soul."

**Context:** In Latter-day Saint practice, scripture study refers to regular, prayerful reading of the standard works (the Bible, Book of Mormon, Doctrine and Covenants, and Pearl of Great Price), typically encouraged daily and often paired with personal prayer.

## Where this appears

- 5 P's Never To Forget — the Pray/Ponder/Pencil/Purpose/Pretty-often method for Book of Mormon study.
- General Conference — October 2021 — study and prayer "go hand in hand" as keys to discipleship.
- Parables — lists scripture study among its core themes and related studies.
- Scripture Study — Topical Study — the rich topical-journal entry behind this theme (wandering minds, the teacher's purpose, "and thus we see").
- Spiritual Gifts — cross-references scripture study as a related study.
