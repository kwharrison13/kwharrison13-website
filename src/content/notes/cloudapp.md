# CloudApp

CloudApp came up in a November 8th, 2021 sourcing call between Kyle and [Serene Papenfuss](/notes/serene-papenfuss) of [Kickstart Seed Fund](/notes/kickstart-seed-fund). The note records it as a Utah-ecosystem company at roughly $5M ARR for the year and raising a round at the time — surfaced as a deal Kickstart was tracking in the [Utah Tech Ecosystem](/notes/utah-tech-ecosystem).

**Context:** CloudApp (now Zight) is a Utah-based visual-communication tool for capturing and sharing screen recordings, GIFs, and annotated screenshots. This general background is not in Kyle's notes.
