# Notion

Notion recurs across Kyle's [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures)-era diligence as a benchmark productivity tool and a node in his founder network. It is grouped with [Airtable](/notes/airtable) as the productivity software [Shuli Song](/notes/shuli-song) of [Plaid](/notes/plaid) was open to (January 31st, 2022 — Shuli Song (Plaid)), and is cited as an outlier on international revenue mix — "some companies (like Notion) have 50% of their revenue international, but that's typically an outlier" (November 15th, 2021 — Anrok Partner Presentation). In the Sprig diligence, [Joseph Ambeault](/notes/joseph-ambeault) characterizes Notion as the knowledge-management center of gravity ("think Sharepoint but that doesn't suck"), contrasted favorably against [Atlassian](/notes/atlassian)'s [Confluence](/notes/confluence).

Notion also functions as a relationship anchor: [Sunil Patro](/notes/sunil-patro) of [SignEasy](/notes/signeasy) "knows [Akshay Kothari](/notes/akshay-kothari) at Notion" (September 22nd, 2021 — Sunil Patro (SignEasy)), and a saved tweet references a GTM playbook from a first Head of Sales & Customer Success at [Typeform](/notes/typeform) and Notion (One Of The Most Challenging... (tweet)). In US deal-flow notes, "Ivan at Notion" (co-founder Ivan Zhao) is logged as a deal-network connection who passed on [Twelve Labs](/notes/twelve-labs) but introduced the [Loom](/notes/loom) founders (December 13th, 2021 — US Deal Flow (2022 Goals)).

**Context:** Notion is a privately held productivity software company (founded 2013) whose all-in-one workspace combines notes, docs, wikis, and databases. Co-founded by Ivan Zhao and Simon Last; Akshay Kothari is its COO.
