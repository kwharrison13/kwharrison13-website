# Abraham 4

A chapter in the Book of Abraham (Pearl of Great Price), cited in Kyle's The Godhead study under the "Jesus: Master of Elements" section. There, [Abraham 4](/notes/abraham-4):10, 18 is used to illustrate Christ's relationship to the elements — His ability to control and command them as their creator — alongside the reasoning that we are co-eternal with God, with an uncreated "eternal intelligence" that allows us to act or be acted upon and to exercise our [agency](/notes/agency).

**Context:** Abraham 4 is the Book of Abraham's account of the Creation, recounting the organizing of the heavens and the earth by the Gods across the creative periods — paralleling Genesis 1 but framed as a council of divine beings "organizing" rather than creating ex nihilo.
