# Persefoni

Persefoni appears in Kyle's notes as a competitor in the Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 landscape map. It's grouped with Metric and [Emitwise](/notes/emitwise) as **carbon-accounting-only** tools ("just measuring your footprint") that are "heavily serviced by consultants" and "more focused on heavy emitters like manufacturing" with "significant burn" — positioned against Watershed's bet on becoming an end-to-end sustainability platform rather than a measurement-only tool. The diligence notes cite Persefoni's $101M Series B (TechCrunch, Oct 2021, led by Prelude and TPG) as a marker of the category's funding heat. The [Watershed Climate](/notes/watershed-climate) company page carries Persefoni in its competitor list for the same reason.

**Context:** Persefoni is a U.S.-based climate-management and carbon-accounting software company (founded 2020) that helps enterprises and financial institutions measure, report, and disclose greenhouse-gas emissions. It raised a $101M Series B in 2021.
