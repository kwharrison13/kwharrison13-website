# 1 Nephi 6

In Kyle's study, **1 Nephi 6** surfaces in two ways. In 1 Nephi 16–22 — I Will Prepare the Way before You, verses 7–8 are read as evidence that "[Marriage](/notes/marriage) is both a commandment and a blessing": once Lehi's company has married, Lehi has "fulfilled all the commandments of the Lord" and Nephi is "blessed of the Lord exceedingly." In Abrahamic Covenant, 1 Nephi 6:2 is cited (alongside 1 Nephi 5:14 and Alma 6:12) to trace lineage — [Joseph Smith](/notes/joseph-smith) being of Ephraim, holding the stick of Joseph when he held the plates.

**Context:** 1 Nephi 6 is a short chapter in which Nephi explains his purpose for writing — not to please the world but to persuade men to come unto the God of Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob and be saved.

## Where this appears

- 1 Nephi 16–22 — I Will Prepare the Way before You — reads 1 Nephi 6:7–8 as marriage being both commandment and blessing
- Abrahamic Covenant — cites 1 Nephi 6:2 in tracing the lineage of Ephraim/Joseph and the brass plates
