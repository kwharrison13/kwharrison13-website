# Warburg Pincus

In Pave Diligence — 2021, Warburg Pincus appears in the financing history of [PayScale](/notes/payscale) — the legacy compensation offering Kyle maps as part of the competitive landscape while diligencing [Pave](/notes/pave). The note records a "$100m acquisition by Warburg Pincus in 2014," before PayScale passed to [Francisco Partners](/notes/francisco-partners) in 2019 for $325m. Warburg Pincus is referenced only as PayScale's earlier private-equity owner, not as a subject of diligence itself.

**Context:** Warburg Pincus is a large global private equity firm (founded 1966, New York) focused on growth investing across technology, financial services, healthcare, and other sectors.
