# Attentive

Cited in Kyle's long reads as the prototype of an opt-in branded-messaging channel — the kind of direct, customer-permissioned media channel that the "media reinforcement" trend in [Equinox Is More Than A Gym. It's A Church](/notes/equinox-is-more-than-a-gym-it-s-a-church) points toward, where loyal customers willfully opt into branded messaging.

**Context:** Attentive is a conversational/SMS marketing platform founded in 2016 that lets brands send personalized text-message campaigns to customers who opt in.
