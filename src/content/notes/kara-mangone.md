# Kara Mangone

Kara Mangone is named in the Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 dossier as a sustainability contact at [Goldman Sachs](/notes/goldman-sachs) and an outreach target during Index Ventures' Series B diligence on [Watershed Climate](/notes/watershed-climate). She sits in the dossier's set of corporate sustainability leaders Kyle's team wanted to reach for customer/expert reference checks on Watershed's carbon-accounting product — alongside [Sam Arons](/notes/sam-arons) at [Lyft](/notes/lyft), [Kate Brandt](/notes/kate-brandt) at [Google](/notes/google), and others. The [Goldman Sachs](/notes/goldman-sachs) page records that Goldman was "in pretty deep" with Watershed (though not yet engaged on facilities/operational-footprint measurement), with Mangone and Roy Joseph (Division Head of Facilities) named as the contacts there.

**Context:** Kara Mangone has led environmental, social, and governance (ESG) and sustainability strategy at Goldman Sachs, including as the firm's global head of sustainability.
