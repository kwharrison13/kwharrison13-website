# Aswath Damodaran

_Stub page — not yet written. NYU Stern economics/finance professor ("the Dean of Valuation"). In his [Instacart IPO breakdown](https://youtu.be/4JHdt9Jy1Xg?t=2152) he argued that "most successful VCs share more with successful traders than they do successful investors" — that they "play the pricing game," judged on timing of entry and exit, "traders on steroids" riding momentum. The framing anchors [The Value Chain of Capital](/essays/the-value-chain-of-capital)._
