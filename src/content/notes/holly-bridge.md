# Holly Bridge

Dr. Holly Bridge appears in Kyle's wiki as the Oxford research advisor of [Adam Chekroud](/notes/adam-chekroud), co-founder and Chief Product Officer of [Spring Health](/notes/spring-health). Per the grounded source, Chekroud came up doing research at the FMRIB Centre, University of Oxford, under Dr. Holly Bridge at the Brain Imaging Unit, John Radcliffe Hospital — the academic background that preceded his work on treatment-matching technology for mental health.

## Where this appears
