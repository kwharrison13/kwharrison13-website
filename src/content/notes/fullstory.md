# FullStory

**One-line definition:** *A session-replay tool focused on "what did you actually do" — contrasted with [Sprig](/notes/sprig)'s self-reported qualitative feedback, and cited as far stickier than Sprig.*

## How sources describe it

- Session replay; rolled out company-wide at Codecademy (even to customer service to replay interactions); "their pricing is outrageous, but I still crank up our spending." (see Sprig fka UserLeap - Diligence)
- Focuses on behavior ("what did you actually do") vs. Sprig's subjective self-report.
- Ripping it out once fully deployed would change how multiple groups operate — i.e., very sticky, unlike Sprig's "easy in, easy out."
- Hotjar described as "just a worse version of FullStory" and ripped out of production.

## Where it shows up

- Sprig fka UserLeap - Diligence — discussed by [Joseph Ambeault](/notes/joseph-ambeault) (Codecademy).
