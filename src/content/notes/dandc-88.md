# D&C 88

Doctrine and Covenants Section 88 — the "olive leaf" revelation — is one of the most-cited scriptural anchors across Kyle's [gospel-study](/notes/gospel-study) notes, supplying texts on prayer, light, learning, spiritual gifts, and the last days. Different verses do work in different studies: 88:76 ("a commandment that ye shall continue in prayer and fasting") anchors the Fasting study; 88:19 ("after it hath filled the measure of its creation, it shall be crowned with glory") titles and grounds Fulfilling the Measure of Our Creation - September 5, 2021; and 88:118 ("seek ye out of the best books words of wisdom; seek learning, even by study and also by faith") recurs as the core proof-text for self-directed learning, including in December 31–January 6, 2019. "We Are Responsible for Our Own Learning".

The verse Kyle returns to most is **88:40** — "truth embraceth truth; virtue loveth virtue; light cleaveth unto light" — which he reads as a doctrine of spiritual attraction: light cleaves to light. It anchors both Born Again (becoming "born of God by cleaving to light") and Radiating Your Example, where [David O. McKay](/notes/david-o-mckay)'s teaching on radiating who one *really is* is paired with the verse. Other passages thread through specific studies: 88:63 ("draw near unto me and I will draw near unto you") for the "Angelics" gift in Spiritual Gifts; 88:22 ("learn to live the celestial law") in The Process of Zion; 88:67–68 (the eye "single to the glory of God") in Eye single to the glory of God; and 88:88–91, the prophecy of "the testimony of earthquakes," in The Whole Armor of God.

**Context:** Doctrine and Covenants Section 88 is a revelation Joseph Smith dictated in December 1832 and January 1833, which he called the "olive leaf… plucked from the Tree of Paradise." It is among the longest and most doctrinally dense sections, covering light and truth ("the light of Christ"), the resurrection and degrees of glory, the law of the celestial kingdom, and instructions to establish a "school of the prophets."

## Where this appears

- Fasting - September 5, 2021 — 88:76, the commandment to "continue in prayer and fasting."
- Fulfilling the Measure of Our Creation - September 5, 2021 — 88:19, being "crowned with glory" after filling the measure of one's creation; titles the study.
- December 31–January 6, 2019. "We Are Responsible for Our Own Learning" — 88:118, "seek learning, even by study and also by faith," as the proof-text for self-directed learning.
- Eye single to the glory of God — 88:67–68, the eye single to God's glory and being "filled with light."
- Born Again — 88:40, becoming born of God by "cleaving to light."
- Radiating Your Example — 88:40 ("light cleaveth unto light") paired with [David O. McKay](/notes/david-o-mckay) on radiating one's true self.
- Spiritual Gifts — 88:63, 83, 119, 122, 133, on drawing near to God and inborn spiritual gifts.
- The Process of Zion — 88:22, learning "to live the celestial law."
- The Whole Armor of God — 88:88–91, "the testimony of earthquakes" and the last days.
