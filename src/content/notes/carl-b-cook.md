# Carl B. Cook

Carl B. Cook appears in Kyle's 3 Answers To Every Prayer topical study, quoted on the "Breathing" answer — the trap of waiting for a complete plan before acting: "As important as it is to learn Heavenly Father's plan for our lives, we sometimes get caught up in knowing every detail, start to finish, that we become afraid to act. Don't fall into this trap. Make good choices using your best judgment and move forward with your life. We're blessed as we make choices. Don't be afraid of making choices because you're afraid of making mistakes. Don't be afraid to try new things. In doing so, you will find joy in the journey." Kyle uses the quote alongside [Brigham Young](/notes/brigham-young)'s teaching on acting on one's best judgment when no direct answer comes, within the [Brother of Jared](/notes/brother-of-jared)'s pattern of agency and action.

**Context:** Carl B. Cook is a general authority Seventy of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, sustained in 2008 and later serving as a member of the Presidency of the Seventy.

## Where this appears
