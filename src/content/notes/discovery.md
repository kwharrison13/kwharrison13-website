# Discovery

Appears only as one of the enterprise accounts named in [UserTesting](/notes/usertesting)'s diligence notes. [Joseph Ambeault](/notes/joseph-ambeault) cited Discovery — alongside [Codecademy](/notes/codecademy) and Verizon — as an example where "every evaluation he ran" UserTesting won, beating research agencies on simple ROI. In context, Discovery is the media company (Discovery Communications), invoked as a customer reference point illustrating UserTesting's stickiness as the incumbent [User Research](/notes/user-research) platform, not as a concept Kyle is tracking in its own right.

**Context:** Discovery, Inc. (Discovery Communications) is the U.S. media conglomerate behind cable networks such as Discovery Channel, HGTV, and Food Network; in 2022 it merged with WarnerMedia to form Warner Bros. Discovery.

## Where this appears

- [UserTesting](/notes/usertesting) — named (with Codecademy and Verizon) as an evaluation win for UserTesting in the Sprig/UserLeap diligence notes
