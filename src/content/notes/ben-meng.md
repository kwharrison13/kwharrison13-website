# Ben Meng

Chief Investment Officer of [CalPERS](/notes/calpers) during the 2019 episode in which the $380B pension system terminated most of its external equity managers — slashing their allocation from $33.6 billion to $5.5 billion. Per the source, Meng drove a "renewed focus on performance and our ability to achieve our 7% assumed rate," with the manager terminations justified on long-term-underperformance grounds and capital reallocated toward internal index strategies. The disclosure itself came via a letter from CalPERS CEO [Marcie Frost](/notes/marcie-frost) to board members.

**Context:** Ben Meng served as CalPERS CIO from January 2019 until his resignation in August 2020.
