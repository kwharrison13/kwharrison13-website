# 3 Nephi 3

A chapter in the Book of Mormon's third book of Nephi. In Kyle's wiki it surfaces as one of the chapters covered by the Come Follow Me study September week on [3 Nephi 1](/notes/3-nephi-1)–[3 Nephi 7](/notes/3-nephi-7) ("Lift Up Your Head and Be of Good Cheer"). It is listed among that week's chapters but Kyle's substantive notes for the week focus on [3 Nephi 1](/notes/3-nephi-1) rather than this chapter, so no annotation specific to 3 Nephi 3 has yet been recorded.

**Context:** In the Book of Mormon narrative, 3 Nephi 3 contains the epistle of the Gadianton robber leader Giddianhi demanding the Nephites surrender, and Lachoneus's call for the people to gather and repent in defense — part of the run-up to the great destruction and the coming of Christ.

## Where this appears
