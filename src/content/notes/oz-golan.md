# Oz Golan

Oz Golan is associated with [Noname Security](/notes/noname-security), the API-security company. He appears alongside CTO [Shay Levi](/notes/shay-levi) in Kyle's November 2nd, 2021 sourcing call (an Index Ventures diligence call), where the two walked through Noname's product — discovery, anomaly detection, misconfiguration, prevention/remediation, and the active-testing (Secure SDLC) module. The notes capture the company's pitch that its hard-won integration know-how (cloning traffic and pulling configuration from sources like [Apigee](/notes/apigee) and [Kong](/notes/kong) to find misconfigurations) gives it a defensible lead and earns customer trust.

**Context:** Oz Golan is the co-founder and CEO of Noname Security; he and the founding team came out of the Israeli military intelligence Unit 8200 community. Noname was acquired by Akamai in 2024.
