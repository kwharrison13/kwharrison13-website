# Vanguard

Vanguard appears in [Morgan Housel- Naked Brands Interview Series](/notes/morgan-housel-naked-brands-interview-series) as the case study in information access flipping a market: it plodded for ~20 years, then "something just clicked" ~15 years ago as investors gained the ability to compare fees and talk to each other online, and money flooded in.

**Context:** The Vanguard Group is an American investment management company founded by John Bogle in 1975, known for pioneering low-cost index funds and a client-owned mutual structure. It is one of the largest asset managers in the world.

## Where this appears

- [Morgan Housel- Naked Brands Interview Series](/notes/morgan-housel-naked-brands-interview-series) — Vanguard's "exponential moment," used to illustrate how online fee transparency and investor communication flipped a market in its favor.
