# Unqork

In Pave Diligence — 2021, Unqork is named only as a customer of [Welcome](/notes/welcome-comp-planning) — one of the earlier-stage competitors mapped in the competitive landscape during Kyle's diligence on [Pave](/notes/pave). It appears in the customer list (alongside [Mercury](/notes/mercury) and [Gatsby](/notes/gatsby)) as evidence of who Welcome's compensation-planning tool had landed, not as a subject of diligence in its own right.

**Context:** Unqork is a no-code enterprise application development platform (founded 2017, New York), used by large financial-services and insurance firms to build complex software without hand-coding.
