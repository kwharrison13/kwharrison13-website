# John Gribbin

A science writer who appears in Kyle's wiki as the author of two anti-library entries: [Deep Simplicity](/notes/deep-simplicity) and [Ice Age](/notes/ice-age) (the latter co-authored with [Mary Gribbin](/notes/mary-gribbin)).

**Context:** John Gribbin is a British science writer and astrophysicist (PhD from the University of Cambridge), known for popular books on physics, cosmology, and the history of science, including *In Search of Schrödinger's Cat*.

## Where this appears

- [Deep Simplicity](/notes/deep-simplicity) — listed as author.
- [Ice Age](/notes/ice-age) — listed as author, with [Mary Gribbin](/notes/mary-gribbin).
