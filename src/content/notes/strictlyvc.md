# StrictlyVC

StrictlyVC appears in the wiki only as one entry in Kyle's inventory of startup-media sources for his "State of Startup Media" research. In The State of Startup Media - Research, it sits in the circa-2017 list of venture-news outlets Kyle tracked, grouped with [PitchBook News](/notes/pitchbook), [PE Hub](/notes/pe-hub), Robinhood Snacks, and Axios Media Trends — the landscape he was mapping when thinking about who covers startups and how.

**Context:** StrictlyVC is a daily venture-capital newsletter founded by journalist Connie Loizos (later editor of TechCrunch); it covers funding rounds, fund news, and VC commentary, and runs a recurring live interview series.
