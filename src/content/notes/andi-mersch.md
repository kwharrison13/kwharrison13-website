# Andi Mersch

Andi Mersch was a reference call (October 19th, 2021) in Kyle's Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 work — Kyle had introduced her to Watershed at [Philz Coffee](/notes/philz-coffee), where she is VP of Coffee and Sustainability. She built Philz's sustainability program from responsible coffee sourcing outward into a company-wide ESG strategy, accelerated by [TPG](/notes/tpg) (a Philz investor): after a post-investment tour of the roasting plant where she flagged sustainability angles in the build-out, she spent significant time with TPG's ESG team and joined their sustainable leadership council, leveraging that relationship to push Philz toward formal, company-wide moonshot goals (carbon zero, zero waste).

On [Carbon Footprint](/notes/carbon-footprint), her approach was deliberately unusual: rather than start with the easier Scope 1 and 2 emissions, she wanted to tackle Scope 3 first because coffee carries a very high greenhouse-gas impact and that is where the biggest gains lie, targeting progress by 2030. Philz partnered with a group called Inveritas for third-party supply-chain verification and benchmarking. She wanted a Watershed intro to help formalize tracking.
