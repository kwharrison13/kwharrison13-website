# Jed McKenna

Author Naval Ravikant recommends reading "everything by" in his book-list bonus section: "Jed spits raw truth. His style may be off-putting, but the dedication to truth is unparalleled." Naval also lists him among the writers he's now added to his always-rereading rotation (alongside Kapil Gupta, the Vashistha Yoga, and Schopenhauer), and separately mentions owning "some Jed McKenna books" when asked what he's currently reading.
