# John 15

A chapter of the Gospel of John in the New Testament. In Kyle's Abrahamic Covenant gospel-study notes, John 15:16 is cited alongside [Luke 10](/notes/luke-10), [Mark 3](/notes/mark-3):13–15, and [Luke 6](/notes/luke-6):13 as evidence that specific followers of Christ were *chosen* as Apostles — chosen by Christ rather than self-appointed. The verse anchors a thread about divine calling and election within the covenant.

**Context:** John 15 is best known for Christ's "I am the true vine" discourse, delivered to the Apostles at the Last Supper; verse 16 reads "Ye have not chosen me, but I have chosen you, and ordained you."

## Where this appears

- Abrahamic Covenant — John 15:16 cited (with Luke 10, Mark 3, Luke 6) for the idea that the Apostles were specifically chosen by Christ.
