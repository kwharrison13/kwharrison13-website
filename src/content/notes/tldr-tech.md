# TLDR Tech

Tech newsletter company founded by [Dan Ni](/notes/dan-ni).

- Started in 2018 as a side project; Dan went full time in 2021.
- Hired first employee in November 2022; a team of 6 as of the June 29th, 2023 call.
- Many employees have other jobs or prior big exits — some do it for the ~$100/hour, some to grow their Twitter followings ("the unreasonable effectiveness of providing side hustles").
- Strong growth team; can bring on people who create great content.
- Building a talent marketplace (beta testers invited June 2023): ~2,500 submissions/week filtered by an ex-Google recruiter to 200–300/week. "We want people on the FYI pay scales, not the Indeed pay scales."
