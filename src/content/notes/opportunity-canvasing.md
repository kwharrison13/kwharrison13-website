# Opportunity Canvasing

A sourcing/diligence practice surfaced in the August 18th, 2021 — Valued Empyrean (Conor White-Sullivan & Darwin Harrison) meeting notes, where each relevant vertical is outlined with opportunities that have been canvassed "the Value Way" — i.e., a structured sweep across a market to map where the openings are before committing. In Kyle's notes it reads as a repeatable method for laying out a space's opportunity surface, vertical by vertical, rather than chasing a single deal in isolation.

The single backlink does not define the term beyond this usage, so the concept is recorded here as Kyle encountered it: a vertical-by-vertical canvass of opportunities used to frame and prioritize where to look.
