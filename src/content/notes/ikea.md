# IKEA

Furniture retailer that appears in Kyle's 2021 [Watershed](/notes/watershed-climate) diligence as an enterprise prospect for the carbon-accounting platform. The Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 notes record that Watershed was connected into IKEA at the CIO, CDO, and Chief Design Officer level, flagged it as a target account "next year" (2022), and listed [Karol Gobczynski](/notes/karol-gobczynski) as the IKEA sustainability contact Kyle emailed for customer-reference diligence. IKEA sits in a roster of large-enterprise sustainability prospects — alongside Virgin Atlantic, Tesco, Booking, [Walmart](/notes/walmart), and [Apple ($AAPL)](/notes/apple) — that Kyle worked to gauge enterprise demand for Watershed's product during the Series B evaluation.

**Context:** IKEA is the Swedish multinational founded in 1943, the world's largest furniture retailer, known for flat-pack design and a publicly stated commitment to becoming "climate positive" — context for why it surfaced as a carbon-software prospect.

## Where this appears

- Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 — listed as a Watershed enterprise prospect (connected at CIO/CDO/Chief Design Officer; target account for the following year), with [Karol Gobczynski](/notes/karol-gobczynski) as the emailed sustainability contact.
