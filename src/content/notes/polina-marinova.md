# Polina Marinova

Writer behind [The Profile](/notes/the-profile), a newsletter of curated long-form profiles and recurring "Profile Dossier" single-subject deep-dives. In Kyle's notes she surfaces as the author of editions he read back to back on 2020-07-02: a Musk-themed roundup ([The Profile: steamy romance & civil rights leader](/notes/the-profile-the-musk-of-steamy-romance-and-the-civil-rights-leader-full-of-hope)) and the [Elon Musk](/notes/elon-musk) "Architect of the Future" Profile Dossier ([The Profile Dossier: Elon Musk](/notes/the-profile-dossier-elon-musk-the-architect-of-the-future)), both captured in his July 2nd, 2020 daily note (source: [The Profile](/notes/the-profile)).

Her newsletter also lent its name to one of Kyle's own idea-tags, [The Profile but for companies](/notes/the-profile-but-for-companies) — attached to passages in the [Berkshire Hathaway Annual Letters](/books/berkshire-hathaway-annual-letters) where [Warren Buffett](/notes/warren-buffett) narrates the story of a *company* the way Marinova's *The Profile* narrates the story of a *person* (source: [The Profile but for companies](/notes/the-profile-but-for-companies)).

**Context:** Polina Marinova Pompliano is the founder of *The Profile*, a newsletter and media brand that writes in-depth profiles of successful people and companies; its "Profile Dossier" series compiles a single notable figure's story, habits, and lessons. She previously was a senior editor at *Fortune*, where she ran the *Term Sheet* dealmaking newsletter, and is the author of the book *Hidden Genius*.
