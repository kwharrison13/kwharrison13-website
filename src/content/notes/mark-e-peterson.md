# Mark E. Peterson

An LDS apostle quoted in Kyle's gospel-study notes on whole-hearted obedience. Both [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book) and The Whole Armor of God carry the same passage: "There is no reward for half-hearted obedience. We must become vigorous and enthusiastic about living our religion, for God commands that we serve Him with all our heart, with all our might, with all our strength, and with the very best of our intelligence. With Him there can be no halfway measures… Then what shall we do? Put on the armor of God." In The Whole Armor of God this quote is the source anchor for the "no halfway measures" theme of the study.

**Context:** Mark E. Petersen (1900–1984) was a member of the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints and a longtime newspaper editor. (Kyle's notes spell the surname "Peterson.")

## Where this appears

- [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book) — the same "no halfway measures" quote.
