# ShareWell

**One-line:** *The core secondaries trading/brokerage business of [SaxonWeber](/notes/saxonweber) ([Derek Weber](/notes/derek-weber)), aggregating both sides of the private-markets marketplace.*

## What we know

- Focused on secondary markets around trading data plus trading/brokerage; aggregates buyers and sellers to be more conducive to trading (source: June 20th, 2023 — Derek Weber (SaxonWeber)).
- Tracks the ~10 "hot" private names with the most market interest (e.g. [OpenAI](/notes/openai)) plus a top-10 list and full market; runs secondary-trade-tracker reports on [Databricks](/notes/databricks) showing SPV trading and the pref/common split (source: June 20th, 2023 — Derek Weber (SaxonWeber)).
