# 2 Nephi 6

A chapter of the Book of Mormon cited in Kyle's June 15–21, 2020. Alma 13–16: "Enter into the Rest of the Lord" study. The quoted verse, 2 Nephi 6:3, is Jacob's expression of pastoral concern — "I am desirous for the welfare of your souls. Yea, mine anxiety is great for you… for I have exhorted you with all diligence" — used as a model of a teacher's earnest care for those he instructs.

**Context:** 2 Nephi 6 opens Jacob's discourse to the Nephites, in which (drawing on Isaiah) he teaches of the scattering and gathering of Israel and the coming of the Messiah; the cited verse frames his teaching with his deep concern for his hearers' salvation.
