# 2 Nephi 7

A chapter of the Book of Mormon cited in Kyle's Born Again gospel study. The quoted verse, 2 Nephi 7:1 — "Have I put thee away? Or have I cast thee off forever? To whom have I put thee away, or to which of my creditors have I sold you?" — is drawn on as the Lord's rhetorical assurance that He has not abandoned His people, supporting the theme of redemption and renewal.

**Context:** 2 Nephi 7 reproduces Isaiah 50, a passage in which the Lord answers the charge that He has forsaken Israel, affirming that the separation came from the people's own sins rather than any rejection on His part.
