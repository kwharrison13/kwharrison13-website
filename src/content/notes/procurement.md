# Procurement

**One-line definition:** *The buying process the vendor discovery + diligence product serves; cross-functional in larger companies.*

## How sources describe it

- In larger companies the procurement process involves many stakeholders, needing a [System of Record](/notes/system-of-record) that aggregates data; decision-makers include department heads (finance, sales) or BizOps people tasked with diligence (per September 13th, 2022 — Nisha Swarup).
