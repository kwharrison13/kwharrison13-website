# 2 Nephi 1

2 Nephi 1 surfaces in Kyle's gospel study along two threads. In The Process of the Book of Mormon, 2 Nephi 1:20 heads the cluster of "keep the commandments and prosper / keep them not and be cut off" verses (with [Jarom 1](/notes/jarom-1):9, [Omni 1](/notes/omni-1):6, [Mosiah 1](/notes/mosiah-1):7, [Alma 9](/notes/alma-9):13–14, [Alma 36](/notes/alma-36):30) — the [Covenant](/notes/covenant) chain that recurs throughout the Book of Mormon, bound by the Lord's obedience principle in [D&C 82](/notes/dandc-82):10.

In The Whole Armor of God, 2 Nephi 1:23 is one of the chapters where the armor imagery is taught (alongside [Ephesians 6](/notes/ephesians-6) and [D&C 27](/notes/dandc-27)): "Awake, my sons; put on the armor of righteousness. Shake off the chains with which ye are bound, and come forth out of obscurity, and arise from the dust" — [Lehi](/notes/lehi)'s charge to his sons.

**Context:** 2 Nephi 1 records the aging prophet Lehi's farewell counsel to his family in the New World — his promise that those who keep the commandments shall prosper in the land, his warning against the consequences of disobedience, and his plea to his sons to "awake" and "put on the armor of righteousness."

## Where this appears

- The Whole Armor of God — 2 Nephi 1:23, [Lehi](/notes/lehi)'s "put on the armor of righteousness" charge, one of the chapters teaching the armor imagery
