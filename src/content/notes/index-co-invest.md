# Index Co-Invest

Index Co-Invest is the [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) co-investment vehicle Kyle participates in as an LP, documented across two meeting notes. In the June 2021 call with Adrianna Ma, the program terms are recorded: a **$250K limit** and a **2-for-1 match** (e.g. $10K of Kyle's commitment is matched by $20K from Index). The December 2021 capital call then lays out the draw-and-repayment schedule: $36K + $36K total called, a $24K first draw across both funds due December 20, 2021, a second $24K tranche the following December plus a $9,600 first loan repayment, and a $19,200 + interest payment in December 2023 — i.e. the program is part-funded by a loan that Kyle repays in tranches.
