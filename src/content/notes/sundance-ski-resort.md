# Sundance Ski Resort

Sundance appears in Kyle's personal [Snowboarding](/notes/snowboarding) logistics note as a Utah resort option. The note records a December 10th ski opening and a frustration: Kyle got "stuck in a loop" because Sundance was only selling season passes over the phone. It is weighed alongside [Snowbird Ski Resort](/notes/snowbird-ski-resort) as candidate destinations.

**Context:** Sundance Mountain Resort is a ski resort in Provo Canyon, Utah, founded by actor Robert Redford and long associated with the Sundance Institute and Film Festival.
