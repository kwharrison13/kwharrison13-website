# Rahul Chitturi

Investor at [Coatue](/notes/coatue) whom Kyle spoke with on a March 30th, 2022 call. The conversation centered on [Mosaic (Coatue)](/notes/mosaic-coatue) — Coatue's data platform — which Chitturi described as something very few firms have managed to build: a ~$20M annual budget, 12 people on headcount, with roughly half the budget going to data sets. He noted he had considered joining another venture fund a couple of years earlier and had talked to [Damir Becirovic](/notes/damir-becirovic) around that time. He and [Parag Jain](/notes/parag-jain) started at the firm around the same period.
