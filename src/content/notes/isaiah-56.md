# Isaiah 56

In Kyle's Everyone Is Human study on [Repentance](/notes/repentance) and confession, [Isaiah 56](/notes/isaiah-56) is cited (with [Mosiah 2](/notes/mosiah-2):36) as scriptural support for the question "How does God let you know you're on the right path?" — the answer being that the Spirit stays with you. The chapter sits inside the note's larger framework on confession versus admission, and the test of whether genuine repentance has occurred: if the Spirit returns, you're good; if it remains absent, you keep going (to the one offended, then to the bishop).
