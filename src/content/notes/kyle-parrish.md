# Kyle Parrish

Kyle Parrish is listed among the [Dropbox](/notes/dropbox) alumni Kyle gathered for The Talent Vortex - Research — research mapping the networks of people who passed through high-output companies and seeded the next generation of startups. He appears in the Dropbox cluster alongside [Karim Anchassi](/notes/karim-anchassi), [Praveer Melwani](/notes/praveer-melwani), [Matt Bogrand](/notes/matt-bogrand), [Michael Miao](/notes/michael-miao), [Steven Bartel](/notes/steven-bartel), and [Veronica Pohls](/notes/veronica-pohls). The source treats him only as a node in the Dropbox talent network; no further detail is attested.
