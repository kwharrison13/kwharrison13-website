# David Rosenthal

Referenced in the "People" roster of Kyle's notes on the [Berkshire Hathaway Annual Letters](/books/berkshire-hathaway-annual-letters) — the densest engagement in the library (277 notes across 538 highlights, read as scaffolding for an in-progress investment philosophy). He appears alongside [Warren Buffett](/notes/warren-buffett), [Charlie Munger](/notes/charlie-munger), [Ben Graham](/notes/ben-graham), [Howard Marks](/notes/howard-marks), and [Andrew Wilkinson](/notes/andrew-wilkinson) in the constellation of capital-allocation thinkers the notes draw on. The notes themselves do not elaborate his specific role beyond that association.

**Context:** David Rosenthal is co-host (with Ben Gilbert) of the *Acquired* podcast, known for long, research-heavy episodes on companies including Berkshire Hathaway — which plausibly explains his appearance alongside Buffett and Munger in these reading notes. This identification is general background, not stated in the source.
