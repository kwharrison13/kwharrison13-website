# Companies to Watch

The `#companies-to-watch` tag — Kyle's marker for a company he wants to keep an eye on, the routing signal that feeds the wiki's company watchlist. It is the watchlist counterpart to the `#books-to-read` anti-library ([Books To Read](/notes/books-to-read)) and the `#movies-to-watch` watchlist ([Movies to Watch](/notes/movies-to-watch)). When a company surfaces in a call, clipping, or saved link as worth tracking — but isn't yet active work — Kyle flags it with this hashtag so it can later become a `wiki/companies-to-watch/` entry.

A `companies-to-watch` page is a lightweight "saved for later" card (often link-only), **distinct from a `wiki/companies/` entity page** (a first-class company with real context behind it). If a watched company graduates into active work, it gets promoted to a full `wiki/companies/` entity page. These entries are `publish: false` and wiki-only for now (not synced to the website).
