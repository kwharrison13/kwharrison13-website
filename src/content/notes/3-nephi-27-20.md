# 3 Nephi 27:20

A single verse from the Book of Mormon (third book of Nephi, chapter 27, verse 20) that Kyle recorded in [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book), his collection of mission quotes and scripture. It is cited there as the attribution beneath the quoted scripture: "Repent, all ye ends of the earth and be baptized in my name, that ye may be sanctified by the reception of the Holy Ghost." In the quote book it sits among missionary-themed quotations from Church leaders such as [Gordon B. Hinckley](/notes/gordon-b-hinckley) and [Jeffrey R. Holland](/notes/jeffrey-r-holland), where the verse functions as a call to repentance and baptism.

**Context:** 3 Nephi 27 records the risen Christ defining what makes his church his — that it be called in his name and built on his gospel — and verse 20 is his summons to "repent, all ye ends of the earth, and… be baptized in my name." The broader chapter is also discussed in Kyle's Come Follow Me study October 19–25, 2020. 3 Nephi 27–4 Nephi: "There Could Not Be a Happier People".
