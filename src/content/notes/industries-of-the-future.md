# Industries of the Future

[Alec Ross](/notes/alec-ross)'s survey of the economic forces shaping the coming decades, cited in [The Future of Work (Article)](/notes/the-future-of-work-article) for its West Virginia vs. [Estonia](/notes/estonia) parable about which regions adapt to automation and offshoring and which cling to declining industries.

**Context:** *The Industries of the Future* (2016) by Alec Ross maps the next wave of economic transformation — robotics, genomics, cybersecurity, big data, and the digitization of money — and which societies are positioned to thrive in it.
