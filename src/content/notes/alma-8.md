# Alma 8

A chapter in the Book of Mormon. In Kyle's wiki it surfaces as a cross-reference within the [Come, Follow Me](/notes/come-follow-me) study of Alma 23–29 (June 29–July 5, 2020. Alma 23–29: "They Never Did Fall Away"), under a thread on friendship and hospitality ("Acquaintances are at church. Friends are in our homes" — [Dieter F. Uchtdorf](/notes/dieter-f-uchtdorf)). The cited verse, Alma 8:27, notes that "Alma tarried many days with Amulek before he began to preach unto the people," paired with [Alma 15](/notes/alma-15):18, where Alma takes Amulek into his own house and ministers to him — illustrating the depth of the Alma–Amulek companionship.

**Context:** Alma 8 (Book of Mormon) recounts Alma's rejection at the city of Ammonihah, the angel that sends him back, and his meeting with Amulek, who takes Alma into his home — the beginning of their partnership in preaching to Ammonihah.
