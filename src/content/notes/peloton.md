# Peloton

The connected-fitness company, appearing in the Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 notes as a sales prospect for [Watershed Climate](/notes/watershed-climate)'s carbon-accounting product. The notes place it in Watershed's "Fitness" cohort (alongside TONAL): Watershed had one connection and had held a first conversation a few weeks prior, working a path through a director of sustainability ("Angela") whose team included a former Peloton employee. Peloton also recurs in the broader customer-target lists Kyle's firm shared with Watershed.

**Context:** Peloton ($PTON) is a connected-fitness company known for its internet-linked stationary bikes and treadmills paired with a subscription class platform; it boomed during the pandemic and contracted sharply afterward.
