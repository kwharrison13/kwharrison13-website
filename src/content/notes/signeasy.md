# SignEasy

**One-line definition:** *Mobile-first, cross-device e-signature tool for SMBs; the only e-signature solution besides [DocuSign ($DOCU)](/notes/docusign-docu) that Apple promotes.*

## How sources describe it

- Focused on being the best cross-device, mobile-first e-signature tool for businesses of all sizes; ~80% of users come through app stores/marketplaces (effectively free given long-standing presence), 20-30% self-serve with no outbound; high virality (1 of 10 "signers" becomes paid) but viral loop not yet productized; free trial + paid upgrade, no freemium yet; 3 segments — individuals, businesses ($1-50K ACV, ~110% net retention, 85% logo retention, ~30% of revenue), developer platform (40 customers, highest stickiness); overall revenue growing ~25% YoY; sees a blue-ocean $20-30B SMB market growing 30%+ post-COVID. (September 22nd, 2021 — Sunil Patro (SignEasy))
