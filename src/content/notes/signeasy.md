# SignEasy

**One-line:** *A bootstrapped, mobile-first, cross-device e-signature tool for SMBs — the only e-signature solution besides [DocuSign ($DOCU)](/notes/docusign) that Apple promotes.*

## What we know

- Founder [Sunil Patro](/notes/sunil-patro) started SignEasy ~10 years ago and bootstrapped it, growing bottom-up and organic on the back of millions of app downloads monetized via in-app purchases. (September 22nd, 2021 — Sunil Patro (SignEasy))
- Positions as the best cross-device, mobile-first e-signature tool for businesses of all sizes — avoiding both narrow point solutions and clunky enterprise tools with overwhelming feature sets. (September 22nd, 2021 — Sunil Patro (SignEasy))
- GTM is heavily PLG, compared to [Calendly](/notes/calendly) and [Loom](/notes/loom): ~80% of users come through app stores/marketplaces (effectively free given their long presence), 20–30% from individual self-serve with no outbound or customer success. (September 22nd, 2021 — Sunil Patro (SignEasy))
- High virality (1 of 10 "signers" becomes a paid user) but the viral loop is not yet productized; runs free trial + paid upgrade rather than [Freemium](/notes/freemium), which they'd like to add to open the top of funnel. (September 22nd, 2021 — Sunil Patro (SignEasy))
- Three segments: individuals; businesses ($1–50K ACV, ~110% [net retention](/notes/net-revenue-retention), 85% logo retention, ~30% of revenue, business revenue grew 80–100%); and a developer platform (40 customers, highest stickiness once the API is integrated). (September 22nd, 2021 — Sunil Patro (SignEasy))
- Overall revenue growing ~25% YoY; sees the [SMB](/notes/smb) e-signature market as a blue-ocean $20–30B opportunity growing 30%+ post-[COVID](/notes/covid). (September 22nd, 2021 — Sunil Patro (SignEasy))
- Competitors named: [HelloSign](/notes/hellosign) and an [Adobe](/notes/adobe) product; benchmark incumbent is [DocuSign ($DOCU)](/notes/docusign). Partnerships span Google, Microsoft, Salesforce, and Freshworks. (September 22nd, 2021 — Sunil Patro (SignEasy))
