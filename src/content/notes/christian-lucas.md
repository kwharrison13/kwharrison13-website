# Christian Lucas

Quoted twice in [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book), where his lines appear among missionary-era exhortations alongside Church leaders and fellow missionaries. One ("If you are not with us — your priesthood and ecclesiastical leaders — then you are against us.") frames loyalty to leadership in stark terms; the other ("You are only constrained by the limits you place upon yourself.") is a motivational note. The surrounding entries — quotes attributed to figures like [Jonathan F. Dippold](/notes/jonathan-f-dippold) and [Brad S. Stringfellow](/notes/brad-stringfellow) interleaved with prophets — suggest Lucas was a peer or leader in Kyle's mission whose remarks Kyle recorded.

## Where this appears

- [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book) — two attributed quotes on loyalty to leadership and self-imposed limits, among Kyle's collected missionary-era sayings.
