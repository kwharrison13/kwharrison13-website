# George Q. Canon

Quoted in Kyle's General Conference- October 2021 notes (under a #[Find the Quote](/notes/find-the-quote) tag) for the line: "Obedience to the gospel brings people into very close and intimate relationship with the Lord. It establishes a close connection to our creator in the heavens. It brings a perfect confidence in the almighty."

**Context:** George Q. Cannon (1827–1901) — the spelling "Canon" in Kyle's notes is a typo — was an early leader of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, a member of the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles and a counselor in the First Presidency under four church presidents.

## Where this appears

- General Conference- October 2021 — quoted on obedience to the gospel bringing a close relationship with the Lord
