# Wei Guo

Investor on the private (crossover) side of [Whale Rock](/notes/whale-rock), focused on China, FinTech, and gaming. Came up doing FinTech in banking (WalPay mergers) before carving out the FinTech coverage at his fund.

In a March 2020 career conversation with Kyle, he advised spending 4–5 years at [Coatue](/notes/coatue) to bank the brand and resources, then moving to an emerging firm for better equity and more say — and weighed at length the trade-offs of the public vs. private side (mark-to-market pressure and faster churn on the public side; brand-building and compounding over time on the private side).
