# Roger Fisher

Lead co-author (with [William Ury](/notes/william-ury) and [Bruce Patton](/notes/bruce-patton)) of [Getting to Yes](/notes/getting-to-yes), a book in Kyle's anti-library (books-to-read).

**Context:** Roger Fisher (1922–2012) was a Harvard Law School professor and the founder of the Harvard Negotiation Project. *Getting to Yes* (1981), co-authored with William Ury, popularized "principled negotiation" — separating people from the problem and focusing on interests rather than positions.
