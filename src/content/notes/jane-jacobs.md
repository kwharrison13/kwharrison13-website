# Jane Jacobs

**One-line:** *Urbanist and author whose bottom-up, street-level view of how cities actually work underpins much of modern [City Building](/notes/city-building) thought.*

## What we know

- Quoted as the epigraph of [The New Urban Crisis](/books/the-new-urban-crisis): "Cities have the capability of providing something for everybody, only because, and only when, they are created by everybody." (source: [The New Urban Crisis](/books/the-new-urban-crisis))

## Mentioned in

- [The New Urban Crisis](/books/the-new-urban-crisis) — epigraph on cities created "by everybody."
