# Camden

Camden is named in [Labor Day](/notes/labor-day) as the other voice in a short personal exchange Kyle quotes to illustrate the holiday's faded meaning: "I don't even know what Labor Day is for." "Yeah, neither do I." The line appears in the research framing for Kyle's writing on [Unions](/notes/unions), where the holiday's emotional distance mirrors the decline of the labor movement it honors. Beyond this one quoted exchange, the wiki carries no further detail on Camden.
