# 3 Nephi 25

A chapter in the Book of Mormon's third book of Nephi, listed among the chapters in Kyle's Come Follow Me study of [3 Nephi 20](/notes/3-nephi-20)–[3 Nephi 26](/notes/3-nephi-26) (October 12–18, 2020. 3 Nephi 20–26: "Ye Are the Children of the Covenant"). In that study Kyle's substantive, annotated notes cover [3 Nephi 20](/notes/3-nephi-20) and [3 Nephi 21](/notes/3-nephi-21); the remaining chapters of the week, including this one, are listed but not yet individually annotated.

**Context:** In the Book of Mormon, 3 Nephi 25 continues Christ's recitation of Malachi to the Nephites (paralleling Malachi 4) — the day that shall burn as an oven, the Sun of Righteousness arising with healing in his wings, and the promise that Elijah will be sent before the great and dreadful day of the Lord.
