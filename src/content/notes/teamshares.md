# Teamshares

[Teamshares](/notes/teamshares) is an SMB-focused fintech built around employee ownership: it acquires small businesses (often from retiring owners) and converts them into employee-owned companies, layering software and financial products on top. Kyle diligenced the company closely. The March 2020 call log (split across [Michael Schahfer](/notes/michael-schahfer) on operations/deal automation and [Tim Dussinger](/notes/tim-dussinger) on the underwriting engine) shows a team building OCR-on-tax-returns underwriting, a BizBuySell scraping/scoring engine, and business rules to automate selection and diligence — captured as Kyle weighed joining. The same diligence sweep pulled in a roster of investors and operators: [Collaborative Fund](/notes/collaborative-fund) ([Taylor Greene](/notes/taylor-greene)) led the Seed; [Inspired Capital](/notes/inspired-capital) ([Mark Batsiyan](/notes/mark-batsiyan)) joined the board; and calls touched [JD Rasmussen](/notes/jd-rasmussen) ([Opendoor](/notes/opendoor)), [Eric Tarczynski](/notes/eric-tarczynski) ([Contrary](/notes/contrary) / Contrary Capital), [Rebecca Kaden](/notes/rebecca-kaden) (USV), [Will Quist](/notes/will-quist) ([Slow Ventures](/notes/slow-ventures)), and [Moses Kagan](/notes/moses-kagan) (Adaptive Realty), among others.

By the August 2021 Series C deck, Kyle's notes turn sharply analytical and skeptical: he pushes back on the "$130M pro-forma revenue" framing (arguing the real revenue is the take-rate on portfolio-company profit plus a SaaS fee, not aggregate holdco revenue, "no more than an ecommerce company should call GMV 'revenue'"), questions the "[Stripe](/notes/stripe) for the old economy" analogy given that [ShopMonkey](/notes/shopmonkey), [ServiceTitan](/notes/servicetitan), and Stripe itself all want vertical payments, and flags SMB "going-out-of-business" risk against the ~$1.3M ARR (on track from $400K to $3.4M that year). Conceptually, Teamshares recurs as the modern employee-ownership analogue to the worker-cooperative thread in [Working Toward Zion](/books/working-toward-zion), sitting alongside Kyle's interest in [Mondragon](/notes/mondragon), [United Order](/notes/united-order), and [Value Added Ventures](/notes/value-added-ventures).

**Context:** Teamshares is a New York-based fintech founded in 2018 that buys small businesses from retiring owners and transitions them to broad-based employee ownership, providing the companies with software, financial products, and management support.
