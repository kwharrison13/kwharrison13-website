# Ana Lorena Fabrega

Educator ("Ms. Fab") and **Chief Evangelist at [Synthesis School](/notes/synthesis-school)**, profiled as "the Educator Focused on Independent Thinking and Mental Models." Per the profile Kyle collected in Cultivating Cults - Research, she **lived the problem she now works on**: her own experience as a student fueled her passion for teaching, but after five years as a classroom teacher she grew frustrated that the traditional school system "wasn't designed to nurture children's natural curiosities and interests."

Within Kyle's [Alternative Education](/notes/alternative-education) notes she stands as an educator-voice for independent thinking and mental models — adjacent to the project-based, self-directed school models the concept favors. In Cultivating Cults - Research she is flagged as an interview target *at Synthesis School*.

**Context:** Ana Lorena Fabrega is a well-known educator and author of *The Learning Game* (2023), and was an early public face of Synthesis, the online enrichment school that spun out of an experimental program at SpaceX.
