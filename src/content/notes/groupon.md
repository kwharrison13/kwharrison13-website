# Groupon

Groupon appears in Kyle's notes only as a biographical waypoint for an expert source. In the Cresta.ai - Diligence, [Chris Purpura](/notes/chris-purpura) — interviewed as VP of Customer Service at [Neiman Marcus](/notes/neiman-marcus) and a Cresta pilot customer — is logged as having previously held the same VP of Customer Service role at Groupon. He uses that tenure as a scale reference point: at Groupon he was "one of [Zendesk](/notes/zendesk)'s largest customers," and he notes that Cresta is similarly thoughtful in sizing the Neiman Marcus opportunity. The mention is purely a career/scale anchor; the diligence itself is about agent-coaching AI, not Groupon's business.

**Context:** Groupon is a Chicago-based e-commerce marketplace, launched in 2008, that built its early business on daily-deal vouchers for local merchants; it went public in 2011.
