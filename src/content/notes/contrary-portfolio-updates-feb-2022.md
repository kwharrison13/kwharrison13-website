# Contrary - Portfolio Updates (Feb 2022)

A tag grouping the batch of February 2022 portfolio company updates Kyle read (while clearing emails) and logged on March 30th, 2022 in his [Contrary](/notes/contrary) sourcing/diligence Roam daily-note. Each update was split into its own meeting page; this concept is the shared thread that ties them together as one reading session.

The companies covered span Contrary's portfolio across sectors: [Aryeo](/notes/aryeo) (real-estate listing media, via [Branick Weix](/notes/branick-weix)) deciding to drop a standalone broker product to focus on its core platform and exclusive pre-MLS/[Zillow](/notes/zillow) listing content; [Candor](/notes/candor) (via [Kelsey Bishop](/notes/kelsey-bishop)) recapping a seed round Contrary led; [Sora Schools](/notes/sora-schools) (edtech) on its online-school model and a new Head of Product, [Risto Lähdesmäki](/notes/risto-l-hdesm-ki); [Uprise](/notes/uprise) (fintech) profiling its ideal customer as single young professionals in their first five years post-graduation; and [Sylva](/notes/sylva) (via [Tom Guthrie](/notes/tom-guthrie)), header-only in the source. The throughline is operating-detail reportage — churn diagnosis, customer-segmentation work, product hires — rather than any single thesis.
