# D&C 9

Doctrine and Covenants section 9. Across Kyle's gospel-study notes this section is invoked almost exclusively for **verses 7–9** — the revelation given to Oliver Cowdery teaching the pattern for receiving answers: *"you must study it out in your mind; then you must ask me if it be right,"* with a burning in the bosom signaling "right" and a "stupor of thought" signaling "wrong." It is Kyle's go-to proof text for the doctrine that revelation comes only **after all you can do** (Revelation — Topical Study ties it to [2 Nephi 25](/notes/2-nephi-25):23's "after all we can do"; Scripture Study — Topical Study notes it "requires much effort and forethought").

The pattern recurs as a structural device. In 1 Nephi 16–22 — I Will Prepare the Way before You Kyle pairs it with the [Liahona](/notes/liahona): commandments come first, the steering tool appears only after Lehi rises to obey — "study it out, then ask." In Prayer — Topical Study he reads verses 8–9 as how God answers prayers (yes/no in your heart AND mind; no fear or stupor as a "no") and pairs the double witness with [2 Timothy 1](/notes/2-timothy-1):7. Two Come Follow Me weeks (Moroni 10 and Ether 1–5) use it to reframe Moroni's Promise as a *confirmation* of truth already in the heart rather than an academic demand to prove the Book of Mormon. The [Brother of Jared](/notes/brother-of-jared) is Kyle's favorite illustration: he molds sixteen stones himself and *only then* asks the Lord to touch them — "the struggle is where faith grows most fervent."

## Where this appears

- 1 Nephi 16–22 — I Will Prepare the Way before You — paired with the Liahona: obey first, then the "study it out, then ask" pattern (16:9–10; 9:7–9).
- December 14–20, 2020. Moroni 10: "Come unto Christ, and Be Perfected in Him" — reframes Moroni's Promise as confirmation, not academic proof.
- November 9–15, 2020. Ether 1–5: "Rend That Veil of Unbelief" — the "No Light → think for yourself" answer (3 Answers to Gospel Questions).
- Prayer — Topical Study — verses 8–9 as how God answers: yes/no in heart and mind, no stupor as a "no."
- Revelation — Topical Study — revelation comes "after all you can do" (9:2–9).
- Scripture Study — Topical Study — answers require much effort and forethought (9:7–8).
- [Brother of Jared](/notes/brother-of-jared) — the sixteen stones: do your part first, then ask the Lord to act.
