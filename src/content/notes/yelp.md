# Yelp

Yelp appears in the wiki as a prior employer of [Yue Wu](/notes/yue-wu), the Dropbox group product manager interviewed in the Sprig fka UserLeap - Diligence. Per her work history, she was Head of Product, Home & Local Services at Yelp from January 2017 to September 2020 — the most senior role in her ~12-year product background, sitting between [Smule](/notes/smule) (2015–2016) and Dropbox (2020–). The company is referenced only as a credential establishing her seniority and product judgment as an expert source for the Sprig diligence on in-app survey tooling; Yelp itself is not the subject of any diligence here.

**Context:** Yelp is a San Francisco-based company (founded 2004) operating a crowd-sourced local-business review platform and advertising marketplace, spanning restaurants, home and local services, and other consumer categories.
