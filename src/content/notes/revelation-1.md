# Revelation 1

The opening chapter of the New Testament book of Revelation. In Kyle's Abrahamic Covenant study it appears in the priesthood/baptism scripture chain: [Revelation 1](/notes/revelation-1):5–6 is read alongside [Matthew 28](/notes/matthew-28):19 and [Mark 16](/notes/mark-16):14–16 on the need for all to be baptized — a common Christian belief — with the note that Mark clarifies Jesus was speaking specifically to the eleven apostles.

**Context:** Revelation 1 is the prologue of the Apocalypse of John, introducing the vision and addressing the seven churches; verses 5–6 describe Christ who "loved us and washed us from our sins" and "made us kings and priests unto God."
