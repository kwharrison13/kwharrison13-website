# Starbucks

The global coffeehouse chain, cited in Kyle's wiki as the archetype of selling a social environment rather than a commodity. In [When Java Meets Jive](/notes/when-java-meets-jive) it is the case study in coffee-shop-as-cultural-experience: physical stores, artisanal espresso bars, regionally labeled coffee, and an alluring localized European flair that turned the store into a destination and a "third place."

**Context:** Starbucks ($SBUX) is the world's largest coffeehouse chain, founded in Seattle in 1971 and scaled by Howard Schultz into a global retail brand built around the in-store experience.
