# Move Fast and Break Things

**One-line definition:** *The startup ethos of prioritizing speed over caution — which, in the [MEDVI](/notes/medvi) story, Kyle argues has morphed into "move fast and break **laws**."*

## How sources describe it

- "at some point, 'move fast and break things' just became 'move fast and break laws'" — the origin quote for the essay's *Move Fast and Break Laws* subtitle (Corey Hoffstein, [@choffstein](https://x.com/choffstein/status/2039893406907035907)). (source: The $1B Rorschach Test - Research)
- Kyle's worry: when breaking laws becomes a "respectable playbook," it incentivizes more crimes, frauds, scams, and shortcuts — and makes the hard way (building a [Durable](/notes/durable) business) less likely. (source: The $1B Rorschach Test - Research)
