# 1 Kings 5

An Old Testament chapter cited in Kyle's Miscellaneous Scriptures gospel-study notes. There, 1 Kings 5:12 appears in a cluster of cross-references on the gifts of the Spirit and divinely given wisdom — grouped alongside [Matthew 16](/notes/matthew-16):19, [D&C 59](/notes/dandc-59):21, [D&C 46](/notes/dandc-46):17–18, and [Moroni 10](/notes/moroni-10):9–10, and compared with [D&C 8](/notes/dandc-8):2–3 ("tell you in your heart AND mind").

**Context:** 1 Kings 5 records Solomon's arrangement with Hiram, king of Tyre, to supply cedar and labor for building the temple in Jerusalem; verse 12 notes that "the Lord gave Solomon wisdom, as he promised him."
