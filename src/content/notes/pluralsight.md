# Pluralsight

Pluralsight appears in the November 8th, 2021 — Serene Papenfuss (Kickstart Seed Fund) sourcing call as a talent-flow signal within the [Utah Tech Ecosystem](/notes/utah-tech-ecosystem). Papenfuss noted she was seeing "a lot of people leaving [Divvy](/notes/divvy) and Pluralsight," and offered the read that "product / engineering people coming out of [Lucid](/notes/lucid) and Pluralsight are typically better than average" — using Pluralsight alumni as a positive hiring signal, in contrast to the more neutral signal she assigned to Divvy departures. The mention is a Utah-ecosystem talent observation, not company diligence.

**Context:** Pluralsight is a Utah-based (Draper) technology-skills and developer-training platform offering online courses and skill assessments for software and IT teams; it was taken private by Vista Equity Partners in 2021.

## Where this appears
