# Anu Hariharan

Anu Hariharan surfaces in Kyle's Pave Diligence — 2021 dossier as a back-channel reference at [YC Continuity](/notes/yc-continuity), [Y Combinator](/notes/y-combinator)'s growth fund. In the "Next Steps → Board Members" section of the [Pave](/notes/pave) (formerly Trove) work, [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) flagged reaching out to "[Anu Hariharan](/notes/anu-hariharan) or [Matt Bogrand](/notes/matt-bogrand) at [YC Continuity](/notes/yc-continuity)" — the goal being to gather a board-level perspective on Pave before pre-empting its Series C. YC Continuity had led Pave's $46M Series B in August 2021 at a $400M post, the round Index was weighing whether to fast-follow at a ~$1B valuation.

**Context:** Anu Hariharan was a managing director at YC Continuity, Y Combinator's growth-stage investment fund, and is known publicly for the "Bessemer-style" growth investing frameworks and the "No Priors"–adjacent investor commentary she has published on scaling startups.
