# Matt Curwood

**One-line:** *Transportation/mobility consultant (Curwood Consulting) with a city-planning and ground-transportation operating background.*

## What we know

- Came to the US in 2000 with a city-planning background; ran operations at shuttle/coach companies (Super Shuttle; "Loop," which grew with tech-company employee services and was the 7th-largest privately held US company, growing 87% YoY vs. ~4% industry norm).
- As a consultant has worked with Cabin (overnight LA–SF bus, fixing maintenance/operations), helped Bird launch in SF (political/permitting work with lobbying firm New Deal Advisors), and Motor Coach Industries.
- Also engaged by First Group (largest outsourced transportation operator) on acquisitions / a new division to compete in employee shuttles, and on Australia entry; sees water transportation and big-city commuter services as key trends.
