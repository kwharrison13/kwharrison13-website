# Mark 8

A chapter in the New Testament Gospel of Mark, cited in Kyle's gospel study. In Christian Reformation, Zion, and Seekers, Mark 8:15 is quoted for the Savior's repeated warning to his disciples — "take heed, beware of the leaven of the Pharisees" — paired with [Luke 12](/notes/luke-12):1, used in the study's discussion of corruption and false teaching within religious tradition.

**Context:** Mark 8 includes the feeding of the four thousand, Jesus's warning against the leaven of the Pharisees and Herod, Peter's confession that Jesus is the Christ, and the first prediction of the Passion.

## Where this appears

- Christian Reformation, Zion, and Seekers — Mark 8:15 cited (with Luke 12:1) for Christ's warning to beware the leaven of the Pharisees.
