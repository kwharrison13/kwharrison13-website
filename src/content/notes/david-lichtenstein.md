**One-line:** *Builds [Carta](/notes/carta)'s Total Compensation Management product; previously grew Carta's 409A valuations product.*

- Carta's Total Comp product "birthed in the fires of the pandemic" — development started Dec 2020, beta March 2021, GA end of April 2021 (source: October 18th, 2021 — David Lichtenstein (Carta) — Pave Diligence).
