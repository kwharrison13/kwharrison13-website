# Psalms 19

A psalm from the Old Testament book of Psalms, used in Kyle's scripture study as a cross-reference on the converting power of God's law. In the Faith — Topical Study, Psalms 19:7 is cited as "Converting the SOUL, body and spirit" — placed among scriptures (alongside [D&C 93](/notes/dandc-93):24, [Alma 32](/notes/alma-32):34, and [2 Nephi 9](/notes/2-nephi-9):12–14) supporting the study's theme that truth is knowledge and that a [Perfect Knowledge](/notes/perfect-knowledge) converts the whole soul.

**Context:** Psalm 19 praises God's revelation in both creation ("The heavens declare the glory of God") and His law. Verse 7 reads in full: "The law of the LORD is perfect, converting the soul: the testimony of the LORD is sure, making wise the simple."
