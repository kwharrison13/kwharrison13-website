# SurveyMonkey

In Kyle's notes, SurveyMonkey appears as the **legacy out-of-context survey baseline** that newer in-app tools are measured against. In the Sprig diligence, [Joseph Ambeault](/notes/joseph-ambeault) (former VP of Product, [Codecademy](/notes/codecademy)) contrasts SurveyMonkey — "out of context, out of app, ~1% response, often statistically insignificant" — against [Sprig](/notes/sprig)'s contextual in-app micro-surveys, which got 20-30% response rates at Codecademy. His verdict: "for qualitative data gather I don't know of a better solution than Sprig." SurveyMonkey is grouped with [Typeform](/notes/typeform) and Zoom Survey as the generic survey tools whose low response rates created the opening Sprig exploited.

**Context:** SurveyMonkey (the company later rebranded as Momentive) is a long-established online survey platform that lets anyone build and distribute web-based questionnaires; it went public in 2018.
