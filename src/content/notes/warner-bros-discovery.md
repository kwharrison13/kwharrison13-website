# Warner Bros. Discovery

The studio led by CEO [David Zaslav](/notes/david-zaslav), which surfaces in Kyle's [The Hits Business](/essays/the-hits-business) essay through Zaslav's November 2022 remarks to investors. His candid line — "We're going to focus on franchises. We haven't had a Superman movie in 13 years. We haven't done a Harry Potter in 15 years." — is cited as the exec willing to "say the quiet part out loud" about Hollywood's franchise dependence, exemplifying how the public-company growth motive drives studios' [Intellectual Property](/notes/intellectual-property) strategy toward Shared Universes.

**Context:** Warner Bros. Discovery is the media conglomerate formed by the 2022 merger of WarnerMedia and Discovery, Inc., home to Warner Bros. film/TV, HBO/Max, DC, and the Discovery cable networks.
