# Honey.io

Honey appears in Kyle's notes only as a reference point in [Hasan Luongo](/notes/hasan-luongo)'s background — cited as an example of the distribution challenges Luongo (VP of Global Marketing at [Chipper Cash](/notes/chipper-cash)) has focused on for tech companies. It surfaces in the Sprig fka UserLeap - Diligence expert-call notes, where Luongo frames his marketing experience around getting consumer-tech products adopted at scale. The wiki carries no independent detail on the company beyond that distribution-challenge framing.

**Context:** Honey is a browser-extension and mobile app that automatically finds and applies coupon codes and tracks price drops at checkout. Founded in 2012, it was acquired by PayPal in 2020 for roughly $4 billion. Its growth was driven heavily by influencer and YouTube marketing, which fits the "distribution challenge" framing in Kyle's notes.
