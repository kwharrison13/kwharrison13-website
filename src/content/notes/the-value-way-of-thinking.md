# The Value Way of Thinking

In Kyle's Alma 36–38 study, "The Value Way of Thinking" is tagged alongside [Paradigm Theory](/notes/paradigm-theory) in a passage on how faith is built or eroded by what a person chooses to consume. The grounded claim is agency over belief: "We are all in control of what we believe in terms of what we consume, expose ourselves to, buy into," and "We build the fire of our faith based on what we choose." It pairs with the entropy reading of Alma 37:46 — faith is "either growing or shrinking" — framing belief as the cumulative product of small, value-laden choices rather than a fixed state.
