# Calm Fund

**Context:** A fund run by [Tyler Tringas](/notes/tyler-tringas) that backs bootstrapped / capital-efficient software businesses. In October 4th, 2022 — Tyler Tringas (Calm Fund), Tringas framed funds as facing three bottlenecks — capital, deal flow, and GP bandwidth — and described Calm's diligence as running a significantly longer application process than YC's: a research team produces a rigorous 5-page brief so the fund knows it wants to invest before ever getting on the phone, at which point "it's the founder's to lose."
