# Five9

Five9 appears throughout Kyle's diligence notes as one of the canonical **[CCaaS](/notes/ccaas) (Contact-Center-as-a-Service) platforms** — the cloud layer that runs a contact center — and, more pointedly, as the leading face of the **consolidation threat** hanging over the real-time agent-assist category. In the Cresta.ai - Diligence, [Cresta.ai](/notes/cresta-ai) itself runs on Five9 as its CCaaS (where agents previously took 3–6 months to ramp on Five9 by shadowing, cut to ~1 month with Cresta). Multiple experts expect the PBX/CCaaS incumbents — Five9, [Genesys](/notes/genesys), [Amazon Connect](/notes/amazon-connect), NICE inContact — to **buy one of Cresta / [Balto.ai](/notes/balto-ai) / [Observe.ai](/notes/observe-ai) and fold real-time coaching into their toolkit**, since they already own the agent's workflow and some have coaching layers that merely lack the real-time component. One expert noted Five9 is integrated with Verint (call recording) and floated [Medallia](/notes/medallia) or Five9 as a possible Balto acquirer.

Five9 also surfaces in the July 18th, 2022 — Alex Levin (Regal Voice) call as evidence of how large the contact-center market is ("16M contact centers out there… has led to big businesses for Five9, etc.").

**Context:** Five9 is a publicly traded cloud contact-center software company providing inbound/outbound routing, IVR, omnichannel, workforce optimization, and analytics as a subscription service. Agreed to be acquired by Zoom in 2021 before that deal was called off; remained independent.

## Where this appears

- Cresta.ai - Diligence — Cresta runs on Five9 as its CCaaS; Five9 named repeatedly as a likely acquirer rolling agent-assist into its platform; integrated with Verint.
- [CCaaS](/notes/ccaas) — listed as a core CCaaS platform and part of the category's consolidation/absorption threat.
- July 18th, 2022 — Alex Levin (Regal Voice) — cited as a big business built on the scale of the contact-center market.
