# D&C 43

Section 43 of the [Doctrine and Covenants](/notes/doctrine-and-covenants) appears in Kyle's gospel study around the theme of sanctification and spiritual preparation. In Humility — Topical Study, verse 16 is cited for the principle that "sanctification lends to an endowment of power" — humbling and cleansing oneself precedes the reception of God's power. In Scripture Study — Topical Study, the same verse is grouped with [D&C 20](/notes/dandc-20):31 and [Mormon 10](/notes/mormon-10):33–35 under the instruction to "sanctify yourselves through prayer and fasting," and verses 8–9 are referenced in the context of administrative gatherings (zone and district conferences) where the Saints are taught to instruct and edify one another.

**Context:** Doctrine and Covenants Section 43 is a revelation given to Joseph Smith in February 1831, addressing the order of revelation in the Church (that only the prophet receives commandments for the whole body) and counseling the elders to assemble, instruct, and sanctify themselves so that they might be endowed with power from on high.

## Where this appears

- Humility — Topical Study — verse 16 cited: sanctification leads to an endowment of power.
- Scripture Study — Topical Study — verses 8–9 tied to administrative conferences; verse 16 grouped under sanctifying through prayer and fasting.
