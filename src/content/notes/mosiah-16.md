# Mosiah 16

A Book of Mormon scripture reference Kyle uses in his Plan of Salvation — Topical Study. Under the "Justice, judgment, and celestial glory" thread, Mosiah 16:3 is cited for the fallen state of mortal man — "carnal, sensual, devilish, knowing evil from good, subjecting themselves to the devil" — to frame why, after the Fall of Adam, only God's justice can reclaim us from the "filthy water" and set us on the strait and narrow path.

## Where this appears

- Plan of Salvation — Topical Study — Mosiah 16:3 cited on fallen man's carnal state to set up the role of God's justice and judgment in the plan of salvation.
