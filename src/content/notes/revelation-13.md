# Revelation 13

A chapter of the New Testament book of Revelation. In Kyle's Plan of Salvation — Topical Study it is cited for a single phrase: the [Atonement](/notes/atonement) is described as the work of the "Lamb slain from the foundation of the world" ([Revelation 13](/notes/revelation-13):8, paired with [Moses 7](/notes/moses-7):47) — supporting the idea that Christ's atoning sacrifice was foreordained before the world was, and that it answers both physical and spiritual death.

**Context:** Revelation 13 is best known for its imagery of the beast(s) and the mark "666"; the "Lamb slain from the foundation of the world" phrase in v. 8 is the part Kyle's study draws on for the doctrine of a premortally foreordained Atonement.
