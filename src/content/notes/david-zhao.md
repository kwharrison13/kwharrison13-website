# David Zhao

Founder of [LiveKit](/notes/livekit) — "open source infrastructure for building and scaling realtime audio and video experiences" ([WebRTC](/notes/webrtc)). He surfaces in Kyle's Index Ventures diligence on LiveKit; see August 18th, 2021 — LiveKit Questions (Kelly Toole), which works through follow-up questions for him after [Kelly Toole](/notes/kelly-toole) met with him. Zhao positioned LiveKit to fill the gap between building real-time video in-house (hard, flexible, scalable), leveraging OSS like Janus or Jitsi (medium-hard), and existing CPaaS solutions like Twilio, Daily.co, and Agora (easy but inflexible with scalability limits), with a cloud product on the roadmap. He noted that the team at Gather was trialing LiveKit, and cited post-launch traction (~1 month in): ~1.6k GitHub stars, ~188 Slack members, ~4.5k Docker pulls, three production integrations, several partnerships in discussion, and an acquisition offer from a public company.
