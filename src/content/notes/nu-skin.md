# Nu Skin

Nu Skin appears in Kyle's notes as a corporate sustainability target during his Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 — the reference-call work on [Watershed Climate](/notes/watershed-climate)'s Series B. [Heather Cruz](/notes/heather-cruz), Nu Skin's sustainability contact, was on the "Emailed" outreach list alongside leads at [Google](/notes/google), [Apple ($AAPL)](/notes/apple), [Salesforce ($CRM)](/notes/salesforce), and BlackRock, as Kyle tried to reach reference customers and gauge enterprise demand for carbon-accounting software. Nu Skin itself functions in the notes purely as the employer behind that sustainability-buyer contact; the wiki carries no further detail on the company.

**Context:** Nu Skin Enterprises is a Utah-based multi-level-marketing company selling personal-care and nutrition products, publicly traded (NYSE: NUS). It has published corporate sustainability commitments around packaging and emissions, consistent with its appearance here as a sustainability-outreach target.
