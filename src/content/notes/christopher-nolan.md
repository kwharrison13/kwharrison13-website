# Christopher Nolan

**One-line:** *Filmmaker Kyle credits with introducing him to the word "[auteur](/notes/auteur-theory)" — via reading up on the director after Batman Begins (2005).*

## What we know

- One of the directors Kyle "voraciously consumed" growing up (alongside the Coen Brothers, [Quentin Tarantino](/notes/quentin-tarantino), [Martin Scorsese](/notes/martin-scorsese), Rian Johnson).
- *Batman Begins* felt "different... unique... almost intimidating" — the gateway to Kyle's interest in directors as authors of their films.
- Directed a 2026 film adaptation of Homer's "The Odyssey" (starring Matt Damon and Zendaya) that drew pre-release backlash over casting, costumes, and anachronisms. (source: [A Very Silly Adaptation of The Odyssey](/notes/a-very-silly-adaptation-of-the-odyssey))
