# Home Depot

The home-improvement retailer, which surfaces in Kyle's notes only as an enterprise-security reference point. In the Wiz.io pre-brief, [Steve Ward](/notes/steve-ward) is identified as **Home Depot's CISO** — a relationship Index Ventures knew from TIAA-CREF and had used to help place him on the [Mimecast](/notes/mimecast) board — and is named as being in discussions on a 500K+ deal for [Wiz.io](/notes/wiz-io). Home Depot thus appears as a marquee CISO/customer reference in the social-proof case for Wiz's agentless cloud-security product, not as a subject of investment interest in its own right.

**Context:** The Home Depot (NYSE: HD) is the largest home-improvement retailer in the United States, headquartered in Atlanta.
