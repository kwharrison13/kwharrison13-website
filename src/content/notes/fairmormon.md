# FairMormon

A volunteer Latter-day Saint apologetics organization, cited as a source in Kyle's [Mormon Apologetics](/notes/mormon-apologetics) notes. In his August 17–23, 2020 Come Follow Me study of Helaman 1–6 ("The Rock of Our Redeemer"), Kyle pulls a FairMormon answer on the Book of Mormon's mention of "cement" (Helaman 3:7,9,11): once thought anachronistic, the reference is reframed as evidence of authenticity given that expert pre-Hispanic American cement technology — comparable to Greek and Roman work and dating to the first century A.D. — is now well-established archaeologically. Kyle tags the passage `#FairMormon` and `#Mormon Apologetics`, using the organization as a go-to apologetic reference for resolving claimed Book of Mormon anachronisms.

**Context:** FairMormon (now operating as FAIR — Faithful Answers, Informed Response) is a nonprofit founded in 1997 that produces volunteer-written responses to criticisms of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints and its scripture.
