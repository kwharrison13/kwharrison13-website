# Simon Sebag Montefiore

Simon Sebag Montefiore (b. 1965) is a British historian and writer, known for popular histories of Russia and the Romanovs (*Stalin: The Court of the Red Tsar*, *The Romanovs*) and for editing anthologies of historic oratory. He edited [Speeches That Changed The World](/books/speeches-that-changed-the-world), the collection Kyle read in 2017.
