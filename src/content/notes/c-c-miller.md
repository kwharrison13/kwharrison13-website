# C.C. Miller

Credited in [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book) as the author of "The Echo," a religious poem reproduced there. The poem reworks the parable of the lost sheep, drawing the distinction that it was "a sheep, not a lamb / That strayed away in the parable Jesus told" — a grown sheep, whose straying matters precisely because wayward sheep lead the lambs astray. The piece sits among the collected quotations and devotional excerpts Kyle gathered in his missionary-era quote book.

## Where this appears
