# Kristin M. Yee

Member of the Relief Society General Presidency of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints and the author of the April 2026 General Conference address Kyle studied in Ministering—"That Ye Love One Another; as I Have Loved You". Her talk centers on how the Savior ministered and the transformative blessings that come to both giver and receiver when we minister in His way (John 13:34 — "That ye love one another; as I have loved you").

She opens with her father's story: two ministering brothers visited him at a low point, drove him to the temple every week for three years without judging him, and walked with him through a genuine transformation. The talk's core counsel — to minister to people where they actually are, not where we want them to be — is the line Kyle connects to a note from [Darwin Harrison](/notes/darwin-harrison). The address was given at the session of President [Dallin H. Oaks](/notes/dallin-h-oaks)'s sustainment as President of the Church.

**Context:** Kristin M. Yee was called as Second Counselor in the Relief Society General Presidency of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in 2022, and is known as an artist whose work includes scripture-themed paintings.
