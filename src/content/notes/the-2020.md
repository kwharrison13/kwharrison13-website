# The 2020

**Author:** [Peter Turchin](/notes/peter-turchin) on [Cliodynamica](/notes/cliodynamica)
**URL:** http://peterturchin.com/cliodynamica/the_2020/
**One-line:** *The causes of revolutions resemble those of earthquakes — slow-building "pressures" plus a sudden "trigger" — and the trigger itself is nearly impossible to predict.*

## Key claims

- The causes of rebellions and revolutions resemble the processes behind earthquakes and forest fires. It's useful to distinguish **"pressures"** (structural conditions that build up slowly) from **"triggers"** (sudden releasing events that immediately precede the eruption).
- **Specific triggers are difficult, perhaps impossible, to predict with precision.** US police already killed ~400 people in the first five months of 2020 — black and white, men and women, criminals and law-abiding. **"Why was it the murder of George Floyd that sparked the wave of protests?"**
