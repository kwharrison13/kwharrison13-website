# Taylor Francis

Co-founder of [Watershed Climate](/notes/watershed-climate) (with [Christian Anderson](/notes/christian-anderson)), the carbon-accounting and emissions-planning platform Index Ventures gave a proprietary look at its Series B in late 2021. Per Kyle's Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 dossier, the idea for Watershed was born when Taylor and Christian spent two years at [Stripe](/notes/stripe) trying to understand the company's carbon footprint by wading through thousands of spreadsheets and PDFs with consultants — they launched Stripe's carbon-removal commitment before leaving to build a data platform that automates that work.

Across the dossier's expert and customer reference calls, Taylor surfaces as the founder customers first interacted with: Alter Eco's head of sustainability recalled Watershed reaching out as "a very young organization," first dealing with Taylor and then Christian. Index's investment thesis repeatedly cited the founders' Stripe-climate pedigree and their reputation as "the most well-referenced team in the space" as central to why customers and the firm wanted to work with them.

**Context:** Taylor Francis is co-founder of Watershed, an enterprise climate/carbon-accounting software company; he previously worked at Stripe where he helped launch its negative-emissions / carbon-removal program.
