# HelloSign

HelloSign surfaces in Kyle's notes as a named competitor in the e-signature landscape. In the September 22nd, 2021 — Sunil Patro (SignEasy) sourcing call, founder [Sunil Patro](/notes/sunil-patro) lists HelloSign — alongside an [Adobe](/notes/adobe) product — as part of [SignEasy](/notes/signeasy)'s competitive set, with [DocuSign ($DOCU)](/notes/docusign) as the benchmark incumbent. The framing positions these as the alternatives SignEasy differentiates against by being mobile-first, cross-device, and SMB-oriented rather than a clunky enterprise tool.

**Context:** HelloSign is an e-signature and digital-document workflow product founded in 2011; it was acquired by Dropbox in 2019 and later rebranded as Dropbox Sign.
