# Index - Sourcing Calls

A tag Kyle used in his Roam Research daily notes to mark sourcing and diligence calls done for [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) across 2021. It is not a company in its own right — it is the deal-flow log that threads together a series of founder calls, each of which has since been split into its own private wiki/meetings page and tagged back to this index. The meeting notes consistently open with a context line like "Had call with [founder] at [company] #[Index - Sourcing Calls](/notes/index-sourcing-calls)" and cite a Roam daily-note export described as an "Index Ventures sourcing/diligence call log."

The calls span a wide swath of early-stage software: e-signature for SMBs ([SignEasy](/notes/signeasy), [Sunil Patro](/notes/sunil-patro)), API monitoring/fintech ([APIMetrics](/notes/apimetrics), [David O'Neill](/notes/david-o-neill)), revenue-operations and sales forecasting ([Boostup.ai](/notes/boostup-ai), [Sharad Verma](/notes/sharad-verma), positioned against [Clari](/notes/clari)), income-share-agreement financing for trade schools (TradeUp, Eric Menees), security ([Uptycs](/notes/uptycs), [Noname Security](/notes/noname-security)), and many others (CaptivateIQ, [Trinsic](/notes/trinsic-id), Koyfin, Lantern, Lexion.ai, Stride Funding, [Openspace.ai](/notes/openspace-ai), [Sacra](/notes/sacra), Actively, Deepsee.ai). All such pages are `publish: false` private sourcing notes.
