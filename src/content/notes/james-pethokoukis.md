# James Pethokoukis

James Pethokoukis appears in Kyle's research for *[It's Time To Build... But Build What](/essays/its-time-to-build-but-build-what)* as the author of *The Conservative Futurist: How to Create the Sci-Fi World We Were Promised*. He sits in the cluster of voices Kyle surveys around [Marc Andreessen](/notes/marc-andreessen)'s "It's Time To Build" and the Techno-Optimist Manifesto — a pro-growth, pro-building strand of the [Techno-Optimism](/notes/techno-optimism) debate, listed alongside critical responses (Ezra Klein's "Reactionary Futurism," Joshua Gans, Luca Ropek) that Kyle reads against each other.

**Context:** James Pethokoukis is an economic-policy writer and longtime columnist (an American Enterprise Institute fellow and author of the *Faster, Please!* newsletter) who advocates for a more optimistic, growth- and technology-forward conservatism. *The Conservative Futurist* was published in 2023.
