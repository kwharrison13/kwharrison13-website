# Lee Jacobs

Lee Jacobs is one of the three investing partners at [Long Journey Ventures](/notes/long-journey-ventures), the values-led early-stage firm where he invests alongside [Arielle Zuckerberg](/notes/arielle-zuckerberg) and [Cyan Banister](/notes/cyan-banister). He appears in Kyle's notes via a June 6th, 2022 call with Zuckerberg, logged during Kyle's [Contrary Capital](/notes/contrary)-era sourcing, in which she described the firm's model — checks of $800K–$1M (smaller at pre-seed), competing on speed over "value add," and leading with a sharply articulated set of values rather than the standard pitch (June 6th, 2022 — Arielle Zuckerberg (Long Journey Ventures)). Jacobs is named as a partner but the source does not detail his individual role beyond that.

**Context:** Lee Jacobs is a venture investor and co-founder/partner of Long Journey Ventures; he was previously involved in early-stage investing including a stint associated with Marc Andreessen's office and the AngelList ecosystem.
