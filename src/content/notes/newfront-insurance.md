# Newfront Insurance

Newfront Insurance appears in Pave Diligence — 2021 in two supporting roles. First as a talent source: Pave hired Nitin (Nitin Gupta) from Newfront, who "helped us win Ascend," part of a GTM-talent narrative that also pulled from [Carta](/notes/carta), [Segment](/notes/segment), and [Uber](/notes/uber). Second as a customer/data-network member: Newfront is listed among the 1,500 companies feeding Pave's give-to-get compensation data set, alongside [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) portfolio companies like [Instabase](/notes/instabase), [Anrok](/notes/anrok), [Discord](/notes/discord), [Scale AI](/notes/scale-ai), [Boulevard](/notes/boulevard), and [Applyboard](/notes/applyboard). A later note also references a Pave-adjacent person who had worked at Newfront after [Bessemer Venture Partners](/notes/bessemer-venture-partners). The mentions are all relational context for Pave rather than diligence on Newfront itself.

**Context:** Newfront is a technology-forward commercial insurance brokerage that combines traditional brokerage services with software for managing business insurance and employee benefits.

## Where this appears

- Pave Diligence — 2021 — source of Pave's hire Nitin (who helped win Ascend), a contributor to Pave's compensation data network, and a prior employer of a Pave-adjacent ex-Bessemer contact.
