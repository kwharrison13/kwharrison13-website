# Streaming Makes Us Superhuman

**Author:** [David Perell](/notes/david-perell)
**URL:** https://www.perell.com/blog/superhuman
**One-line:** *A short note on music as a mood-control technology — and Perell's favorite productivity trick of looping one song for hours to enter a deep trance.*

## Key claims

- **Music lets us change our moods in an instant** — to boost focus, induce calm, add adrenaline, foster [Creativity](/notes/creativity), or amplify happiness.
- **Looping a single song for hours produces a deep trance**, Perell's favorite productivity tip.

## Notable quotes

> "Here's my favorite productivity tip: Listen to the same song on repeat for a couple hours. If you pick the right song, you'll enter a deep, deep trance."
