# Max Weber

**One-line:** *German sociologist, author of The Protestant Ethic and the Spirit of Capitalism.*

## What we know

- Cited in the [Great Softening research](/notes/the-hardening-of-the-great-softening-research) for the [Protestant Work Ethic](/notes/protestant-work-ethic): "(Germany vs Italy) → emphasis on grace makes you want to demonstrate the hand of god through prosperity." Kyle uses Weber's thesis to frame why prosperity gets read as a sign of righteousness — connecting it to the [Book of Mormon](/notes/book-of-mormon) prosperity promise.
