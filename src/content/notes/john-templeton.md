# John Templeton

In [The Psychology of Money](/books/the-psychology-of-money), Templeton is the source of one of investing's most-quoted warnings: "The four most dangerous words in investing are, 'it's different this time.'" Morgan Housel uses it as the backstop to the common, usually mocking, retort "It's different this time" — the line you drop to rebut anyone predicting the future won't mirror the past. The point underpins the book's argument that financial history rhymes and that claims of novelty are usually where investors get into trouble.

**Context:** Sir John Templeton (1912–2008) was a pioneering global value investor who founded the Templeton Growth Fund and was known for contrarian bargain-hunting and his later philanthropy through the Templeton Foundation.
