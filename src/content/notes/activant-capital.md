# Activant Capital

Venture/growth investment firm that surfaces in Kyle's March 30th, 2022 call with [Kyle Gay](/notes/kyle-gay) at [Kensington Permanent Capital](/notes/kensington-permanent-capital). The reference is to [Peter McCoy](/notes/peter-mccoy) (of [Baton](/notes/baton)), who — after [Bridgewater Associates](/notes/bridgewater-associates) and selling a company — worked at Activant Capital and sourced their investment in [Bolt](/notes/bolt). The mention is part of a broader conversation about McCoy's operating background and the early-employee/follow-on access strategy he and Kyle Gay were considering for a new fund.

**Context:** Activant Capital is a growth-stage venture capital firm (founded by Steve Sarracino) that invests in commerce, fintech, and infrastructure technology companies.
