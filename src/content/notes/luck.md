# Luck

The role of chance in entrepreneurial and investing outcomes. In [On the Nature of Long-Term Holds](/notes/on-the-nature-of-long-term-holds), the authors warn that a past entrepreneurial success does not guarantee the next — many experienced, well-capitalized entrepreneurs fail to replicate prior wins — and Kyle's adjacent note asks how one might "increase your capacity to get lucky."

## Where this appears

- [On the Nature of Long-Term Holds](/notes/on-the-nature-of-long-term-holds) — the caution that prior success is not repeatable, and the question of how to expand one's surface area for good luck.
