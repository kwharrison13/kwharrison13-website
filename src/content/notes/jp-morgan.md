# JP Morgan

JP Morgan appears in Kyle's notes as a career-history reference point. [Tyler Payne](/notes/tyler-payne) — the [Atomic Ventures](/notes/atomic-ventures) operator Kyle took an intro call with in 2018 — interned at JP Morgan in New York before doing oil & gas research at [Goldman Sachs](/notes/goldman-sachs) and moving into venture/venture-building. The reference is purely biographical: JP Morgan as a marquee early-career stop, not a subject Kyle has analyzed in its own right. It surfaces elsewhere in the corpus only in passing (e.g. essays and book notes), without dedicated treatment.

**Context:** JPMorgan Chase & Co. is the largest US bank by assets, formed through a long series of mergers (notably J.P. Morgan & Co. and Chase Manhattan). Its investment-banking arm is a top-tier global franchise across M&A advisory, capital markets, and trading.
