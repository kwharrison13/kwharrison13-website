# 2 Nephi 13

Nephi's quotation of Isaiah on the pride of the daughters of Zion. In Kyle's Come Follow Me study of Helaman 7–12, it is cited for verse 16 — "Because the daughters of Zion are haughty, and walk with stretched-forth necks and wanton eyes... and making a tinkling with their feet" — used as a vivid image of how the children of God turn haughty, paralleling Mormon's lament over the Nephites bringing about their own destruction.

**Context:** 2 Nephi 13 in the Book of Mormon reproduces Isaiah 3, where the prophet denounces the haughtiness and finery of "the daughters of Zion" and pronounces coming judgment. Nephi quotes Isaiah at length across 2 Nephi as a witness of Christ and a warning to his own people.
