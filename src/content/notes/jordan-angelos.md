# Jordan Angelos

An investor who came up in [Stripe](/notes/stripe)'s corporate-VC orbit and later moved into traditional venture. In Kyle's corporate-VC research, Angelos is named (with Justin Overdorff) as one of the two key dealmakers Stripe lost — both decamping to partner roles, Angelos to Ribbit Capital and Overdorff to Lightspeed — a departure Kyle frames as a blow to Stripe's reputation as a VC investor after its 2020 rise as a corporate VC (it led Fast's $20M Series A and backed Pulley and Accord).

Angelos appears in two further sweeps: he is listed among the Stripe-mafia alumni in The Talent Vortex - Research (Kyle's catalog of where ex-fintech operators land), and he was on Kyle's outreach list for a January 2024 San Francisco trip, flagged as "[Jordan Angelos](/notes/jordan-angelos) at Ribbit Capital."
