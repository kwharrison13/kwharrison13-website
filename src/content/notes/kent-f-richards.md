# Kent F. Richards

LDS Church leader quoted in How To Apply The Atonement on the [Atonement](/notes/atonement): "During his mortal life Christ chose to experience pains and afflictions in order to understand us." Kyle's study notes connect the quote to Alma 7:11–12, the scriptural passage on Christ taking upon himself the pains and afflictions of his people so that he might know how to succor them.

**Context:** Kent F. Richards is an emeritus General Authority Seventy of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints; the quoted line echoes his 2011 general conference address "The Atonement Covers All Pain."
