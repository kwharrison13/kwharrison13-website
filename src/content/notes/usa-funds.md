# USA Funds

The predecessor entity to [Strada Education Network](/notes/strada-education-network). Per the May 2018 intro call with [Andre Bennin](/notes/andre-bennin), USA Funds was a student-loan guarantor (it "secured student loans"); under Bill Hansen it wound down the guaranteeing business and rebranded as Strada, coalescing around a "completion with a purpose" theme — finishing education and transitioning into the workforce. USA Funds is therefore Strada's origin story and the source of the roughly $1.4B balance sheet that now funds Strada's student-lifecycle and future-of-work investing.

**Context:** USA Funds was a long-running U.S. nonprofit guarantor in the federal student-loan system; as that guaranty model wound down it reorganized into the education-focused investor and grantmaker now known as Strada Education.
