# Macroeconomics

Macroeconomics appears in [Naval Ravikant — The Angel Philosopher](/notes/naval-ravikant-the-angel-philosopher) as the discipline [Naval Ravikant](/notes/naval-ravikant) "stopped believing in" — he came to see macro as "the combination of voodoo, complex systems, and politics," where you can find an economist to take every side of every argument. He contrasts it with [Microeconomics](/notes/microeconomics) and [Game Theory](/notes/game-theory), which he holds to be fundamental.

**Context:** Macroeconomics is the branch of economics concerned with economy-wide phenomena — output, inflation, unemployment, interest rates, and growth — as opposed to the behavior of individual agents studied in microeconomics.

## Where this appears

- [Naval Ravikant — The Angel Philosopher](/notes/naval-ravikant-the-angel-philosopher) — dismissed as "junk science" ("voodoo, complex systems, and politics") versus the fundamentals of [Microeconomics](/notes/microeconomics) and game theory.
