# Nitin Gupta

An early executive at [Pave](/notes/pave) (the real-time compensation system-of-record), recruited by CEO [Matt Schulman](/notes/matt-schulman) from [Newfront Insurance](/notes/newfront-insurance) — a hire the [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) diligence notes flag as a strong signal (he "helped us win Ascend"). His earlier background was at [Bessemer Venture Partners](/notes/bessemer-venture-partners). In the [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) diligence around Pave's preemptive Series C, he appears as a Pave point of contact and co-presented (alongside Schulman) Pave's VC Platform Community pitch on its API-driven compensation-benchmarking product. Kyle's next-steps list included a planned walk with Nitin in SF.

## Where this appears

- Pave Diligence — 2021 — listed among Pave's key team hires (ex-[Newfront Insurance](/notes/newfront-insurance), formerly [Bessemer Venture Partners](/notes/bessemer-venture-partners)) and as a co-presenter on the VC Platform pitch; a relationship Kyle tracked during [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures)' diligence.
