# Uplight

**One-line:** *One of the largest providers of software to utilities in North America, formed in 2019 by merging six energy-software companies; [Matt Harsha-Strong](/notes/matt-harsha-strong) is CFO.*

## What we know

- Formed in 2019 by merging six companies: EEMe, EnergySavvy, Ecotagious, FirstFuel, [Tendril](/notes/tendril), and [Simple Energy](/notes/simple-energy) (source: June 20th, 2023 — Matt Harsha-Strong (Uplight)).
- Engages utility customers via energy-usage portals, mailed reports, and enrolling households/businesses in demand-response programs (thermostat control, rebates) (source: June 20th, 2023 — Matt Harsha-Strong (Uplight)).
- Valued at $1.6B in 2021 (source: June 20th, 2023 — Matt Harsha-Strong (Uplight)).
