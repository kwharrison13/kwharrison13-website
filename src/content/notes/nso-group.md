# NSO Group

NSO Group appears in Kyle's notes as a career-history credential for [Nir Rothenberg](/notes/nir-rothenberg), the CISO at [Rapyd](/notes/rapyd) interviewed in the 2021 Salt Security Diligence — 2021 expert call. Rothenberg's prior role as Head of Security at NSO Group is presented as part of the security-leadership pedigree that gives weight to his views — notably his judgment that [API Security](/notes/api-security) would become "the next 'must have'" alongside endpoint, firewall, and CSPM tooling, and his choice of [Noname Security](/notes/noname-security) over [Salt Security](/notes/salt-security) on posture-management grounds. The reference is biographical; NSO Group itself is not analyzed in the wiki.

**Context:** NSO Group is an Israeli cyber-intelligence firm best known for Pegasus, spyware sold to government clients that can covertly compromise mobile devices. It has been the subject of significant international controversy over surveillance abuses and was placed on the US Commerce Department's Entity List in 2021.
