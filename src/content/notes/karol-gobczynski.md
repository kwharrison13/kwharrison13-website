# Karol Gobczynski

Sustainability contact at [IKEA](/notes/ikea) whom Kyle emailed as a reference/customer-diligence outreach target during the Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 work — the evaluation of Watershed's Series B (Index Ventures, 2021). In the diligence notes, Gobczynski sits on Kyle's "Emailed" list of corporate sustainability leaders contacted to validate demand for Watershed's carbon-measurement platform; the notes separately record that Watershed was connected into IKEA at the CIO, CDO, and Chief Design Officer level and saw IKEA as a target account for the following year.
