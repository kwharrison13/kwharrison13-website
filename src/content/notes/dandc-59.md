# D&C 59

Doctrine and Covenants section 59 — the "Sabbath day" revelation, including the law of the fast and the promise of blessings to the faithful. Kyle's notes draw on several of its verses. **Verse 8** ("offer a sacrifice... even that of a broken heart and a contrite spirit") anchors A Mighty Change of Heart — Ezra Taft Benson alongside [D&C 20](/notes/dandc-20):37 in his collection of broken-heart-and-contrite-spirit passages. **Verses 12–16** on the law of the fast are cited in Fasting - September 5, 2021.

The blessing-and-gratitude verses recur most. **Verse 21** — that one offends God by *not* confessing His hand and obeying His commandments — appears in Miscellaneous Scriptures ("if you confess and obey, you cannot offend God") and in Revelation — Topical Study as a guard against pride: express gratitude to the giver. General Conference- October 2021 cites 59:23 ("he who doeth the works of righteousness shall receive his reward, even peace in this world and eternal life in the world to come"), and July 6–12, 2020. Alma 30–31: "The Virtue of the Word of God" cites 59:4 on the faithful and diligent being "crowned with blessings from above... with revelations in their time." The Lehonti Principle references 59:2.
