# David Zaslav

**One-line:** *CEO of Warner Bros. Discovery, cited in [The Hits Business](/essays/the-hits-business) as the exec willing to "say the quiet part out loud" about franchise dependence.*

## What we know

- November 2022, to investors: ["We're going to focus on franchises. We haven't had a Superman movie in 13 years. We haven't done a Harry Potter in 15 years."](https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/tv/tv-news/harry-potter-tv-series-officially-happening-at-max-1235371717/)
- In the essay, his candor exemplifies how the public-company profit motive ("there must be growth... there must be Shared Universes") drives Hollywood's [IP](/notes/intellectual-property) strategy.
