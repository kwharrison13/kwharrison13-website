# Jacob 6

A chapter from the Book of Jacob in the Book of Mormon. In Kyle's Plan of Salvation — Topical Study, [Jacob 6](/notes/jacob-6):10 is paired with [John 3](/notes/john-3):16–17 to make the point that we all would have had to suffer, but Christ saved us — supporting the study's treatment of the [Atonement](/notes/atonement), physical and spiritual death, and the tension of justice vs. mercy.

**Context:** Jacob 6 closes Jacob's discourse with an exhortation to repentance, warning that those who reject Christ face the unquenchable fire prepared for the wicked while the righteous are gathered.

## Where this appears

- Plan of Salvation — Topical Study — Jacob 6:10 cited (with John 3:16–17) on Christ saving us from the suffering we all would otherwise have borne.
