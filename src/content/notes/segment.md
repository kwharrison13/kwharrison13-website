# Segment

A real-time [Customer Data Platform](/notes/customer-data-platform) (CDP), and one of the two reference players Kyle's notes return to when mapping the category. In the mParticle — Diligence (2018) dossier, Segment is grouped with Treasure Data and [mParticle](/notes/mparticle) as a true **real-data CDP** — one that builds/collects *primary* data and ingests touchpoints in real time — as opposed to front-end players that only ingest 3rd-party tools without owning the data. The [Customer Data Platform](/notes/customer-data-platform) page captures the same 2018 read: CDP was at "peak hype cycle," and Segment/mParticle/Treasure Data were the real ones. mParticle's CEO [Michael Katz](/notes/michael-katz) positioned Segment as "much more focused on downstream customers," arguing the TAM was big enough for both, and Kyle's notes flag the dynamic where vendors increasingly line up to be *part of* mParticle and Segment rather than vice versa — with retention compounding as each added tool makes the platform stickier. (mParticle's own page frames it as one half of a "2-horse CDI race" with Segment.)

Segment also recurs as a real-world reference point in later notes — appearing as a customer/pilot target in the June 6th, 2022 — Contenda May Update (Lilly Chen) update, and surfacing across the Pave Diligence — 2021, Noname Security - Diligence, and Salt Security Diligence — 2021 dossiers as a known data-infrastructure comparable.

**Context:** Segment is a customer data platform (founded 2011, originally Segment.io) that collects, cleans, and routes first-party customer data to downstream analytics and marketing tools; it was acquired by Twilio in 2020 for roughly $3.2B.
