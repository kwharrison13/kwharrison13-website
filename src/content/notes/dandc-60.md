# D&C 60

A section of the Doctrine and Covenants that Kyle cites in his Teaching Repentance topical study as a scriptural warrant for boldness in missionary work. Teaching Repentance pulls D&C 60:2–3 under the "Open Your Mouth" (OYM) theme, glossing it as "Don't fear men, OYM" — a charge to open one's mouth and declare the gospel rather than fearing the faces of men.

**Context:** Doctrine and Covenants Section 60 is an 1831 revelation given through Joseph Smith at Kirtland, Ohio, directing elders returning from Missouri. Verses 2–3 rebuke those who hide their talent and fail to open their mouths to proclaim the gospel because of the fear of man.

## Where this appears
