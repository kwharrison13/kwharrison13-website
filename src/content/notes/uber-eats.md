# Uber Eats

In the wiki, Uber Eats functions mainly as a **talent-pedigree marker** in Kyle's Contrary Capital sourcing/recruiting notes — the prior employer of operators he was evaluating in spring 2022. [Josh Curry](/notes/josh-curry) (call on March 30th, 2022) spent time at [Uber ($UBER)](/notes/uber) "helping to build out new markets for Uber Eats" before founding a mental-health startup; Kyle's notes frame "marketplace ops" as his key role and tag him for healthcare, edtech, and climate companies. [Noemie Heinen](/notes/noemie-heinen), whom Kyle was introduced to on April 5th, 2022 via [Jen Yang-Wong](/notes/jen-yang-wong), was also previously at Uber Eats and was exploring earlier-stage ops / product / product-ops roles where she could wear multiple hats. So Uber Eats here is shorthand for marketplace-operations experience and the kind of operator Kyle was sourcing into early-stage companies.

**Context:** Uber Eats is the food-delivery platform operated by Uber, launched in 2014 and now one of the largest on-demand delivery marketplaces globally.
