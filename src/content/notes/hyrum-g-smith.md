# Hyrum G. Smith

An LDS Church leader quoted in [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book) in a section on the loss of little children. The passage attributed to him counsels mothers who have lost young children not to accuse the Lord or assume they have sinned, holding that "the Lord loves little children" and that those taken will "come forth in the morning of the resurrection with his saints." The quote sits alongside related passages on death and resurrection from figures like [John Taylor](/notes/john-taylor).

**Context:** Hyrum G. Smith (1879–1932) served as Presiding Patriarch of the LDS Church from 1912 until his death; he was a grandson of the earlier patriarch John Smith and a descendant of the Smith family central to early Mormon history.

## Where this appears

- [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book) — quoted on comforting mothers who have lost little children, in the death-and-resurrection section.
