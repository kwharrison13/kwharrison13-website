# Jennifer Loving

Loving is the named leader of [Destination Home](/notes/destination-home), the Santa Clara County public-private partnership working to end homelessness via a collective-impact model. Per the wiki, Destination: Home grew out of a "blue ribbon commission" of the San Jose mayor and Santa Clara County board of supervisors; Loving became its leader and adopted the Stanford collective-impact model. She heads a roughly five-person team — with [Chad Bojorquez](/notes/chad-bojorquez) as operations lead, Robert on communications, and Ray as chief impact officer — that acts as the "backbone" and strategy builder for service organizations, funds developers with cheap or 0% forgivable loans, and pursues a $1B private-sector target alongside the county's $950M bond.
