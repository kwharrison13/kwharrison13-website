# CleanCapital

**One-line definition:** *Clean-energy investment platform that acquires and finances operating solar/clean-energy assets, aiming to streamline sustainable-energy finance and build a secondary market for projects.*

## How sources describe it

- Co-founded by [Jon Powers](/notes/jon-powers) (ex-Obama Chief Sustainability Officer) and partner Tom; inspired by how SoFi streamlined loan refinancing — applying that to clean-energy finance. (source: raw/calls/May 31st, 2018.md)
- Acquires yielding commercial/industrial solar assets; ~60-day discovery-to-close underwriting, 2% origination fee plus a principal/equity position. (source: raw/calls/May 31st, 2018.md)
- First year did a $20M portfolio (John Hancock Energy $14M of it); by 2017 took on new capital partners and ramped acquisitions sharply (24 MW in a month). (source: raw/calls/May 31st, 2018.md)
- Outsources asset management (e.g. Bay Four Energy, working with Terraform and NexGen) but stays close to customers; investors include Carval and John Hancock Energy, with interest in reaching [CalPERS](/notes/calpers). (source: raw/calls/May 31st, 2018.md)
- Excited about "energy as a service"; runs a podcast, "Experts Only." (source: raw/calls/May 31st, 2018.md)
