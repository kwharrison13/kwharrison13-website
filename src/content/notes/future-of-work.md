# Future of Work

**One-line definition:** *The shifting nature of work and labor in the face of automation — including the role and limits of reskilling.*

## How sources describe it

- [Banu Ozkazanc-Pan](/notes/banu-ozkazanc-pan) treats reskilling (e.g. teaching displaced workers to code) as a short-term fix at the global level but transformative for the individual who gains a new skill. (May 16th, 2018 — Banu Ozkazanc-Pan (Brown University))
