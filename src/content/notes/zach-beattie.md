# Zach Beattie

A member of the [Watershed Climate](/notes/watershed-climate) team during Index Ventures' 2021 Series B diligence. He ran the product demo on December 2nd, 2021, walking through Watershed's carbon-accounting platform — the framing that carbon accounting "should be just as detailed of a process as regular accounting," how the tool calculates different emissions across AWS vs. GCP, the hands-on measurement work with customers, the [Expensify](/notes/expensify) data export, and the expectation that regulation will eventually make carbon accounting mandatory (likely aligned with TCFD).
