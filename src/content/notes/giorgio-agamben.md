# Giorgio Agamben

A philosopher who appears in Kyle's wiki as the author of the anti-library entry [The Time That Remains](/notes/the-time-that-remains).

**Context:** Giorgio Agamben is an Italian philosopher best known for his *Homo Sacer* series on sovereignty and biopolitics. *The Time That Remains* is his commentary on Paul's Letter to the Romans.
