# Isaiah 54

In Kyle's notes on Christ Is Risen; Faith in Him Will Move Mountains — Russell M. Nelson, [Isaiah 54](/notes/isaiah-54):10 anchors the talk's theme of God's unshakeable covenant: "For the mountains shall depart, and the hills be removed; but my kindness shall not depart from thee, neither shall the covenant of my peace be removed, saith the LORD that hath mercy on thee." The note pairs the verse with its Book-of-Mormon echo in [3 Nephi 22](/notes/3-nephi-22):10 — the same promise quoted nearly word-for-word — reinforcing the "faith will move mountains" image at the heart of [Russell M. Nelson](/notes/russell-m-nelson)'s message: the mountains may move, but God's kindness will not.
