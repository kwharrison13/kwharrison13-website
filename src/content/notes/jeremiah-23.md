# Jeremiah 23

A chapter from the Old Testament book of Jeremiah. In Kyle's Pattern of Prophetic Callings study, Jeremiah 23:18 is paired with [Amos 3](/notes/amos-3):7 to support the point that prophets are called to stand with God and be given his counsel — that a true prophet has "stood in the counsel of the Lord." It anchors the study's argument that prophets are foreordained and receive God's mind and word through their callings.

**Context:** Jeremiah 23 is part of the prophet Jeremiah's oracles, condemning false shepherds and false prophets while promising a righteous "Branch" from David's line. Verse 18 asks who has "stood in the counsel of the LORD," a touchstone for distinguishing true prophetic authority.
