# Rob Henderson

A writer Kyle cites in his research on whether America is in decline. In The Hardening Of The Great Softening - Research, a Henderson [tweet](https://twitter.com/robkhenderson/status/1561465318962257921) appears in the long thread weighing decline against vitality — the same decline-framing cluster that anchors [American Exceptionalism on Trial](/notes/american-exceptionalism-on-trial) (the [January 6th](/notes/january-6th) demographic analysis, the postwar data on uneducated whites, the hollowed-out middle class) set against America's entrepreneurialism and immigrant aspiration. Henderson attaches to the theme through that thread.

**Context:** Rob Henderson is a writer and psychologist best known for coining the term "luxury beliefs" — ideas and opinions that confer status on the upper class while imposing costs on the lower class.
