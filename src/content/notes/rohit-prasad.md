# Rohit Prasad

In [Amazon Unbound](/books/amazon-unbound) (Brad Stone), Prasad leads the deep-learning bet that becomes Alexa's response model. The highlights credit him with explaining why Evi's rigid knowledge graphs couldn't be Alexa's foundation — a graph-based system might mishear "Play music by Sting" as a goodbye to the artist — and why statistical deep-learning methods instead let the system infer that the intent is almost certainly to "blast 'Every Breath You Take.'" Once [Jeff Bezos](/notes/jeff-bezos) "poured gasoline" on the early sparks, Prasad described the run-up to Alexa and Echo as a "running success" that changed his life; he was promoted to VP and eventually joined Amazon's senior leadership (S-team), though he noted he had the launch playbook but "the playbook for the next five years, I didn't have."

**Context:** Rohit Prasad is an Amazon engineering leader who served as head scientist for Alexa and later as SVP and head scientist for Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) at Amazon.
