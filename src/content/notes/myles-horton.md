# Myles Horton

Appears in the Historical Futurism - Research notes as the author of *The Long Haul*, surfaced via "The Good Society Reading List" Kyle collected while gathering source material on utopias and the future. In Kyle's notes he sits alongside other utopian/society-building texts — [Ursula K. Le Guin](/notes/ursula-k-le-guin)'s *The Dispossessed*, *Gaviotas: A Village to Reinvent the World*, and *Philosophy of the Kibbutz* — as part of the essay's reading-list scaffolding rather than as a directly quoted source.

**Context:** Myles Horton (1905–1990) was an American educator and civil rights activist who co-founded the Highlander Folk School in Tennessee; *The Long Haul* is his autobiography.
