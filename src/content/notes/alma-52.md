# Alma 52

A war chapter in the Book of Mormon, falling within the range covered by Kyle's August 3–9, 2020. Alma 43–52: "Stand Fast in the Faith of Christ" Come Follow Me study. That week's range runs Alma 43–52, but Kyle's notes for the week engage only [Alma 43](/notes/alma-43) (former Mormons as opponents, manipulation vs. motivation, and [Moroni](/notes/moroni) made chief captain at twenty-five as [Small and Simple Things](/notes/small-and-simple-things)); the source explicitly flags that Alma 44 through Alma 52 carry no annotations. Alma 52 is therefore listed here as part of the study range rather than with grounded commentary from Kyle.

**Context:** Alma 52 is a chapter in the Book of Alma in the Book of Mormon, recounting the Nephite recapture of cities (including the strategem at Mulek) under Moroni, Teancum, and Lehi during the war with the Lamanites.
