# Kirsten Wyatt

**One-line:** *Co-founder of [ELGL](/notes/elgl), a cross-functional professional organization for people working in and around local government.*

## What we know

- She and her husband, working in city management, saw a need for a professional organization that wasn't siloed by job title and years of service like the legacy associations.
- Started ELGL as a Portland meetup (16 people for lunch) and grew it to ~4,000 members nationwide, building out blog/podcast/social presence.
- Treats vendor companies as necessary partners on equal terms with government employees, and makes introductions / gives companies a platform to share their work.
