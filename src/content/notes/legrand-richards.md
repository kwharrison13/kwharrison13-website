# LeGrand Richards

LeGrand Richards appears in Kyle's Things To Know Before Your Mission study (drawn from [James E. Faust](/notes/james-e-faust)'s "10 things I would like my son or grandson to know before he leaves on his mission"). He is quoted under the principle "forget yourself in the Lord's service": "There's a new group of girls every year. And the new group is just as good as the old one." In context, the line reframes a missionary's reluctance to leave behind people or a situation he has grown attached to — a reminder that nothing should distract from the message God needs delivered.

**Context:** LeGrand Richards (1886–1983) was a leader in the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints — Presiding Bishop and later an Apostle in the Quorum of the Twelve — and the author of the missionary classic *A Marvelous Work and a Wonder*.
