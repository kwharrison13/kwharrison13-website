# Temporarily Embarrassed Millionaires

**One-line definition:** *The (Steinbeck-attributed) idea that many Americans see themselves not as exploited workers but as future-rich people whose success is a question of when, not if.*

## How sources describe it

- In the published essay [The Hardening Of The Great Softening](/essays/the-hardening-of-the-great-softening): "Ambitious people often see themselves as 'temporarily embarrassed millionaires.' Success is not a question of if, but when." Kyle ties the *erosion* of that belief — among people who see less success for themselves or those like them — to the "softening" of ambition.
