# Pakistan

Pakistan appears in [Naval Ravikant — The Angel Philosopher](/notes/naval-ravikant-the-angel-philosopher) in the context of [Naval Ravikant](/notes/naval-ravikant)'s fantasy project: a low-cost, rugged Android tablet distributed worldwide with pre-built learning apps — the kind of [Self-Guided Learning](/notes/self-guided-learning) device aimed at reaching learners in places where formal education infrastructure is thin.

**Context:** Pakistan is a country in South Asia and the world's fifth-most-populous nation. Naval Ravikant was born in Delhi, India, and immigrated to the United States as a child; the region surfaces in his thinking about distributing cheap learning tools globally.

## Where this appears

- [Naval Ravikant — The Angel Philosopher](/notes/naval-ravikant-the-angel-philosopher) — referenced in Naval's vision of cheap, globally distributed self-guided learning devices.
