# L. Tom Perry

LDS apostle quoted across two of Kyle's study notes. In Obedience — Topical Study, Perry (via *Preach My Gospel*) is cited on the protective power of daily obedience: "The discipline contained in daily obedience and clean living and wholesome lives builds an armor around you of protection and safety from the temptations that beset you." In [The Priesthood Power of Women](/books/the-priesthood-power-of-women), he is quoted on the equal partnership of marriage — that "there is not a president or a vice president in a family" and, citing [Gordon B. Hinckley](/notes/gordon-b-hinckley), that a husband "neither walks ahead of his wife nor behind his wife but at her side. They are coequals."

**Context:** L. Tom Perry (1922–2015) was a member of the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints from 1974 until his death, one of the longest-serving apostles in modern Church history.
