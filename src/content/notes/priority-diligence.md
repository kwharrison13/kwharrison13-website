# Priority Diligence

"Priority Diligence" appears as a working-list heading inside the Pave Diligence — 2021 dossier — the bucket under "Next Steps" where Kyle parked the highest-leverage diligence actions on [Pave](/notes/pave): getting [Mark Goldberg](/notes/mark-goldberg) to set up dinner with CEO [Matt Schulman](/notes/matt-schulman), following up with a contact at [Figma](/notes/figma) about talking to Pave, and an intro at [Backbone](/notes/backbone). It is an organizing label for the must-do reference/relationship tasks during a live deal process, not a defined methodology or framework — useful as a tag for how Kyle structures a sprint of pre-emptive diligence.

## Where this appears

- Pave Diligence — 2021 — used as a "Next Steps" sub-heading collecting the top-priority diligence tasks (CEO dinner via Mark Goldberg, Figma and Backbone customer/contact follow-ups) during Index Ventures' attempt to pre-empt Pave's Series C.
