# Joseph Smith Lecture 3 — The Character of God

The third lecture in [Truman G. Madsen](/notes/truman-g-madsen)'s celebrated BYU series on the life and character of [Joseph Smith](/notes/joseph-smith). Per the [Truman G. Madsen](/notes/truman-g-madsen) page, Lectures 1 and 2 — Joseph Smith Lecture 1 — The First Vision and Its Aftermath and [Joseph Smith Lecture 2 — Joseph's Personality and Character](/notes/joseph-smith-lecture-2-joseph-s-personality-and-character) — have been ingested as their own pages; Lecture 3, "The Character of God," is named in that page's open questions as the next in the series but has not yet been captured from its source. This page is a placeholder so cross-references resolve; its substance awaits ingestion of the lecture text or recording.

**Context:** Truman G. Madsen (1926–2009) was a BYU philosophy professor whose lecture series on Joseph Smith, delivered and recorded in the 1970s, became a widely circulated set of devotional talks in Latter-day Saint culture.
