Flooring behemoth (NYSE: MHK), mostly focused on tile rather than rugs. Cited in [Stewards of Capital](/essays/stewards-of-capital) as a scale benchmark for the rug/flooring market: Mohawk generates ~$11B of revenue yet holds only ~2% global market share — illustrating how fragmented the category is and how much volume [Ernesta](/notes/ernesta) would need to capture for a venture-scale return.
