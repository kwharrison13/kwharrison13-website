# Marvin J. Ashton

Marvin J. Ashton appears in the wiki as the attributed author of one passage in [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book) (page 32), on the nature of dishonesty: *"'A lie is any communication with the intent to deceive.' A lie can be effectively communicated without words ever being spoken. Sometimes a nod of the head or silence can deceive."* It sits among the collection's quotes on integrity and truth.

**Context:** Marvin J. Ashton (1915–1994) was a member of the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, widely remembered for talks on honesty, kindness, and financial prudence. (General background, outside the wiki sources.)
