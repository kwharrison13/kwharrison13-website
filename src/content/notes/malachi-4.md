# Malachi 4

The closing chapter of the Old Testament, invoked in The Curse of Cain as the scriptural remedy to broken lineage. Kyle's reading: the [great apostasy](/notes/apostasy) severed sealed lineages, robbing individuals of a sealed inheritance, and Malachi is the answer — it turns "the hearts of the fathers to the children, and the hearts of the children to their fathers, lest the earth be smitten with a curse." In that study it stands as the generational mirror image of the curse on Cain: where murder against the covenant cut Cain's posterity off from their birthright, the temple ordinances Malachi prophesies of restore sealed inheritance across generations.

**Context:** Malachi 4 is the final chapter of the Hebrew Bible / Old Testament. Its prophecy that Elijah will "turn the heart of the fathers to the children" is foundational to Latter-day Saint temple and genealogy doctrine (family-history work, sealing ordinances).
