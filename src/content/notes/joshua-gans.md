# Joshua Gans

Joshua Gans appears in Kyle's research for *[It's Time To Build... But Build What](/essays/its-time-to-build-but-build-what)* as the author of a Substack response to [Marc Andreessen](/notes/marc-andreessen)'s techno-optimism. He is one of the critical voices Kyle reads against the pro-building strand of the [Techno-Optimism](/notes/techno-optimism) debate, listed alongside Ezra Klein's "Reactionary Futurism" and Luca Ropek's Gizmodo critique, opposite the more optimistic [James Pethokoukis](/notes/james-pethokoukis).

**Context:** Joshua Gans is an Australian economist and professor at the University of Toronto's Rotman School of Management, known for his work on the economics of innovation, entrepreneurship, and (with Ajay Agrawal and Avi Goldfarb) the economics of artificial intelligence.
