# Start With Why

**Why it's on the list:** *Simon Sinek's argument that leaders and organizations inspire action by leading with purpose (the "Why") before the "What" and "How" — the idea Kyle connects to [Carl Braun](/notes/carl-braun)'s rule, recounted by [Charlie Munger](/notes/charlie-munger), that every communication must tell Who, What, Where, When, and Why.*

**Where I saw it:** Tagged in [Charlie Munger — The Psychology of Human Misjudgment](/notes/charlie-munger-the-psychology-of-human-misjudgment), next to Munger's account of Carl Braun's insistence that you always explain the *Why* — leaving out the reason makes instructions far weaker.
