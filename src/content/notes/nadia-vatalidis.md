# Nadia Vatalidis

A people/HR contact at [Remote.com](/notes/remote-com) whom Kyle reached out to during his 2021 diligence on [Pave](/notes/pave) (the compensation-data startup led by [Matt Schulman](/notes/matt-schulman)). In her response, captured in Pave Diligence — 2021, she reported that Remote reviewed multiple compensation-data vendors during Q2 2021 — Pave included — but ultimately selected Kamsa instead. Her stated reasons were a customer signal worth noting for the diligence: Kamsa's global coverage (50–85 countries Remote was scaling into), quarterly rather than annual data refreshes, alignment with modern HR/tech tooling, and more current job-title/career-level taxonomies than legacy comp datasets like Mercer and Radford (AON).
