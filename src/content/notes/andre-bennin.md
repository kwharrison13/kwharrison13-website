# Andre Bennin

**One-line:** *Investor at [Strada Education Network](/notes/strada-education-network) managing a Future of Work seed fund; former lawyer turned government-relations and corp-dev/VC operator.*

- Joined Strada (then USA Funds) in 2013; took a year off to work for the Indiana governor's office, then returned. (source: raw/calls/May 31st, 2018.md)
- Covers 8 deals at Strada, 4 of which he led; whoever leads a deal sits on the board. (source: raw/calls/May 31st, 2018.md)
- Manages a newly launched ~$1.5M seed fund centered on the Future of Work. (source: raw/calls/May 31st, 2018.md)
- Worked on RoadTrip Nation (board) and helped Emsi on the board-presentation side. (source: raw/calls/May 31st, 2018.md)
