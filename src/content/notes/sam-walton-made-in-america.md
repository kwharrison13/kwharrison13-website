# Sam Walton: Made in America

**Why it's on the list:** *[Sam Walton](/notes/sam-walton)'s autobiography — the source [David Perell](/notes/david-perell) leans on in [Wisdom From Walmart](/notes/wisdom-from-walmart) for Walton's operating philosophy: competitiveness as relentless self-correction, frugality, and [Discounting](/notes/discounting) as a flywheel.*

**Where I saw it:** Cited in [Wisdom From Walmart](/notes/wisdom-from-walmart), Perell's distillation of [Walmart](/notes/walmart), which is drawn largely from this book.

## Connections

- [Sam Walton](/notes/sam-walton) — author and subject.
- [Walmart](/notes/walmart) — the company built on the operating philosophy the book lays out.
- [Wisdom From Walmart](/notes/wisdom-from-walmart) — the long-read that distills this autobiography.
