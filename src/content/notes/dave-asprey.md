# Dave Asprey

Founder of Bulletproof, named in Kyle's notes on [Maximus](/notes/maximus) as one of the [Super Angels](/notes/super-angels) on that company's cap table. When Kyle reviewed Maximus's AngelList docs in June 2022, the standout detail was a roster of consumer- and crypto-founder angels — Asprey (Bulletproof) alongside Fred Ehrsam (Coinbase), Brian Lee (Honest Company), Brian Norgard (Tinder), Jeff Weiser (former Shopify CMO), and Ryan Holiday (Daily Stoic). Asprey's presence fits the page's framing: Maximus, a direct-to-consumer men's health / hormone-optimization company, drew backers from the biohacking and wellness world that Asprey is associated with. In Kyle's notes Asprey functions as a marker of the cap table's super-angel composition rather than as a subject in his own right.

**Context:** Dave Asprey is an entrepreneur and author who founded Bulletproof (known for "Bulletproof Coffee") and is a prominent figure in the "biohacking" movement focused on longevity and human performance optimization.
