# Nathan Harkenrider

**One-line:** *Engineering leader at [Vareto](/notes/vareto); previously VP of Engineering at Smartsheet (cloud infra + enterprise features over 8 years), where he saw the FP&A dogfooding pain firsthand before joining Vareto to build its dynamic-planning product.*

- Earlier worked at a company that sold to Marketo; connected to Smartsheet via [Madrona](/notes/madrona) (a shared investor), joining as an early scaling CTO and rising to VP of Engineering (cloud infra, authentication, directory management).
- At Vareto, drove the bet to build dynamic modeling on **graph database** technology rather than in-memory **OLAP cubes** (the [Anaplan](/notes/anaplan) / [Pigment](/notes/pigment) approach) — treating scale as a distributed-systems problem to avoid vertical-scaling limits — with collaborative editing built in from day one.
