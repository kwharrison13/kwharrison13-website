# Tenaya Capital

Tenaya Capital is the venture firm that led [Salt Security](/notes/salt-security)'s Series A, per Kyle's Salt Security Diligence — 2021 dossier. The cap-table progression captured there has Tenaya's Series A followed by a $30M Series B from [Sequoia](/notes/sequoia) (Dec 2020) and a $70M Series C from [Alkeon](/notes/alkeon) + [Advent International](/notes/advent-international) (May 2021) at a $625M post-money. As the earliest institutional backer, Tenaya — through partner [Stewart Gollmer](/notes/stewart-gollmer) — was on Kyle's diligence outreach list as a back-channel reference for the Salt Security investigation, with email as the planned contact method.

**Context:** Tenaya Capital is a Silicon Valley venture firm focused on growth-stage technology companies; it spun out of Lehman Brothers Venture Partners in 2009.
