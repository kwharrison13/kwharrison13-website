# Christine Deakers

Christine Deakers is the [Bessemer Venture Partners](/notes/bessemer-venture-partners) author of Ten Lessons from a Decade of Vertical Software Investing (BVP Atlas, July 2022) — the piece sitting in Kyle's reading queue as BVP's updated playbook on [Vertical SaaS](/notes/vertical-saas). The article frames how founders choose markets wisely, maintain enduring growth, and build industry-defining companies; Kyle's notes position it as the 2022 companion to BVP's earlier 2017 "Vertical Software: A Founder's Guide to Success" (by [Brian Feinstein](/notes/brian-feinstein)). Within the wiki she appears as one of the Bessemer people in Kyle's network, anchoring the firm as a thought-leader on vertical-software investing.
