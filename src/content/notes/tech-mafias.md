# Tech Mafias

The alumni networks that spin out of [talent vortexes](/notes/talent-vortex) — e.g. the [PayPal Mafia](/notes/paypal-mafia) — where former colleagues seed, fund, and staff each other's new companies. A tech mafia is the *output*; a successful talent vortex is the *input*.
