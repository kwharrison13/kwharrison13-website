# D&C 107

Section 107 of the Doctrine and Covenants is Kyle's reference text on the [Priesthood](/notes/priesthood) — its offices, quorums, and the duties attached to them. It surfaces in his June 15–21, 2020. Alma 13–16: "Enter into the Rest of the Lord" study (paired with [D&C 121](/notes/dandc-121)) alongside Alma 13's high-doctrine sermon that the priesthood is "*His*" — the order of the Son of God, eternal, "without beginning of days or end of years." Kyle's Excommunication — Topical Study draws on verse 72 ("judges in Israel") for the priesthood's role in Church discipline, and his Spiritual Gifts notes cite verses 87 and 27 in the context of quorum order and learning together as peacemakers. In the Scripture Study — Topical Study, verse 99 ("let every man learn his duty, and to act in the office in which he is appointed, in all diligence") stands for **consistency and diligence**.

**Context:** Doctrine and Covenants Section 107 (revealed to [Joseph Smith](/notes/joseph-smith), 1835) is the principal latter-day revelation on priesthood organization — distinguishing the Melchizedek and Aaronic priesthoods, defining the offices of apostle, seventy, high priest, elder, bishop, priest, teacher, and deacon, and outlining quorum governance and the duty of each office.

## Where this appears

- Excommunication — Topical Study — verse 72 on judges in Israel and Church discipline
- Scripture Study — Topical Study — verse 99 on consistency and diligence (learn one's duty)
