# Danielle Gibbons-Hiser

Director of Customer Success at Glassdoor, where she rose through a long CS tenure (CSM → Senior Manager Strategic Accounts → Associate Director → Director). She appears as an expert-call participant in the Planhat - Diligence customer-success tooling study. Glassdoor runs three to four customer tiers with CSMs assigned by segment, and her selection criterion is a tool that "works across every customer segment." Her org is committed to [Gainsight](/notes/gainsight) — adopted ~4–5 years ago after a hard initial rollout (new behavior for CSMs) — which she likes but cautions is "very clunky" and hard to pull accurate cross-account information from; having "made a huge investment," they are not actively evaluating alternatives.
