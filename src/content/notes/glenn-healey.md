# Glenn Healey

Glenn Healey (Harvard Business School 1982) is an entrepreneur based in suburban Detroit, Michigan, who operates a roughly $36-million EBITDA portfolio of businesses (in commercial fire protection and other industries) and is not interested in exiting in the foreseeable future. He appears in [On the Nature of Long-Term Holds](/notes/on-the-nature-of-long-term-holds) as a case-study example of the long-hold, owner-operator model.

## Where this appears

- [On the Nature of Long-Term Holds](/notes/on-the-nature-of-long-term-holds) — profiled as a long-term holder running a portfolio of operating businesses with no intent to sell.
