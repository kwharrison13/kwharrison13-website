# InsightStudios

A [Venture Studio](/notes/venture-studio) founded and run by [Dave Linhardt](/notes/dave-linhardt), captured in Kyle's July 2018 call with him. Linhardt's central argument is a critique of the **shared-services studio model**: early-stage companies still searching for a business model don't yet know which services they need, and front-loading them takes decision power away from the founder. He cites a Chicago studio, Ronan, that consumed roughly $800K of a $1M investment in its own services — including an inside-sales team a startup might turn out not to need.

He contrasts InsightStudios with PSL (Pioneer Square Labs), which he says is run by venture capitalists used to evaluating Series A companies rather than building from scratch. His pitch leans on an "art plus science" framing — analogies to a Formula One car needing a great driver, and to Marvel Studios crafting movies "at a different level" (positioning a bet on InsightStudios as "investing in Marvel before the first Iron Man"). The defensibility claim is the proprietary **Insight-Driven Methodology** (codified in an "Insight Lab / FounderSensei" onboarding platform): anyone can read about lean startup, but applying it profitably takes the studio's combination of knowledge and experienced judgment.
