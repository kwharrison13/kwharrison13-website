# Alex Mennella

Alex Mennella was a reference call (October 21st, 2021) during Kyle's Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 work. He works at [Uber ($UBER)](/notes/uber) in a "very operationally heavy" BizDev / CorpDev role focused on EV partnerships, after spending four years on Uber's driver programs. As reducing emissions became a platform priority, his mandate shifted toward understanding Uber's commercial fleets and pushing drivers toward electrification.

In the call he framed Uber as distinctive because transportation is the largest source of carbon emissions, putting climate "right in line" with how the company thinks. He described carbon-emissions tracking and initiatives like Uber Clean, and reacted positively to [Watershed](/notes/watershed-climate): he liked that it leaned into end-user education and awareness, arguing that knowing your emissions is one thing but being given an avenue to act on them — e.g. pairing tracking with an offer from a provider like Sun Run — makes a much stronger proposition. He noted many people had reached out to him about carbon-tracking frameworks.
