# Ritholtz Wealth Management

Wealth management firm cited in [Naked Brands- The Future of Finance](/notes/naked-brands-the-future-of-finance) as the "Wu-Tang Clan of Finance" — a collective of financial bloggers whose Josh Brown captures the thesis: "people don't follow brands, they follow people." Used as a case study in transparency-as-moat, where podcasts become the new word-of-mouth and blogs the new business cards.

**Context:** Ritholtz Wealth Management is a registered investment advisor co-founded by Barry Ritholtz and Josh Brown, widely known for its principals' large public presence across blogs, podcasts, and financial media.
