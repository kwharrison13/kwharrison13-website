# Metagames

"Metagames" is the plural variant of [Metagame](/notes/metagame) — see that page for the full note. The term comes from the [Tyro Partners](/notes/tyro-partners) Q2 2021 Partner Letter by [Dan McMurtrie](/notes/dan-mcmurtrie), which argues that markets are now "metagames" where beliefs operate as useful constraints, paired with the letter's four-level memetic model (Content → Reference → Advocacy → Community).

## Where this appears

- [Tyro Partners](/notes/tyro-partners) — the Q2 2021 Partner Letter's claim that markets are now "metagames where beliefs are useful constraints."
