# Brian Feinstein

An investor at [Bessemer Venture Partners](/notes/bessemer-venture-partners) and the author of **"Vertical Software: A Founder's Guide to Success"** (2017), an early framework for [vertical software](/notes/vertical-saas) investing covering market selection, winner-take-most dynamics, and the economics of industry-specific SaaS. The piece is in Kyle's articles-to-read queue (Vertical Software— A Founder's Guide to Success); BVP frames it as the precursor to their 2022 "Ten Lessons from a Decade of Vertical Software Investing" update.

In Kyle's [Bessemer Venture Partners](/notes/bessemer-venture-partners) notes, Feinstein anchors the firm as a thought-leader on vertical SaaS — his guide and the later "Ten Lessons" piece are the two vertical-software essays in Kyle's reading queue published under the Bessemer banner.

**Context:** Brian Feinstein is a partner at Bessemer Venture Partners, where he has invested in software and developer/infrastructure companies; the firm has long published widely-read vertical-SaaS theses.
