# 1 Timothy 3

A New Testament chapter cited in Kyle's Abrahamic Covenant gospel-study notes. There, 1 Timothy 3:1–13 appears in a cluster of references on priesthood offices and church organization — grouped with [1 Corinthians 12](/notes/1-corinthians-12):27–29, [Ephesians 4](/notes/ephesians-4):11–13, [Titus 1](/notes/titus-1):5–9, [Acts 14](/notes/acts-14):23, and [Matthew 16](/notes/matthew-16):19 — supporting the structure of called and ordained officers within the church.

**Context:** 1 Timothy 3 sets out the qualifications for bishops (overseers) and deacons in the early Christian church, describing the character expected of those who hold office.
