# Come, Follow Me

Come, Follow Me is the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints' home-centered scripture-study curriculum, and the organizing scaffold for the bulk of Kyle's [gospel-study](/notes/gospel-study) notes — each weekly page (e.g. Alma 13–16, the Moroni weeks of late 2020) follows the program's reading schedule. The introductory 2019 New Testament week, December 31–January 6, 2019. "We Are Responsible for Our Own Learning", frames the whole approach: taking personal responsibility for our own gospel learning, learning by faith rather than passive reception, and being [agents unto ourselves](/notes/agents-unto-themselves) ([Agency](/notes/agency), [Discipleship](/notes/discipleship)).

Per Kyle's naming convention, weekly Come Follow Me pages use the `Month D–D, Year. Scripture: "Subtitle"` format and tag back to this page; topical (non-weekly) studies live separately under Topical Study.

**Context:** "Come, Follow Me — For Individuals and Families" is a study guide the LDS Church introduced in 2019, rotating annually through the New Testament, Old Testament, Book of Mormon, and Doctrine and Covenants on a weekly reading schedule.
