# Sapphire Ventures

Growth-stage venture firm that surfaces in Kyle's notes mostly as a cap-table fact: it was the lead/existing investor backing [Uptycs](/notes/uptycs), the security-analytics company founded by [Ganesh Pai](/notes/ganesh-pai) that competes with [CrowdStrike ($CRWD)](/notes/crowdstrike). In the September 2021 sourcing call, Pai noted Uptycs had raised from Sapphire Ventures and still held ~$80M of cash — context for a company that "didn't have a process," just reached out to a few people, with [ServiceNow ($NOW)](/notes/servicenow) and [Salesforce](/notes/salesforce) also eager to invest. (September 22nd, 2021 — Ganesh Pai (Uptycs))

The firm also appears in Kyle's venture-network research as the home of Casber Wang, one of the investor contacts catalogued in The Death of a Venture Fund - Research and The Productization of Venture Capital - Research.

**Context:** Sapphire Ventures is a Palo Alto-based growth-stage venture capital firm, originally spun out of SAP (as SAP Ventures) before becoming an independent multi-billion-dollar enterprise-software investor.
