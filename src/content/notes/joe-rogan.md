# Joe Rogan

Joe Rogan shows up in Kyle's wiki along two threads. As an interviewer, he's a model the author of [Stop Asking Questions](/books/stop-asking-questions) cites for the lesson that "you don't need to exhaust a topic — it's OK to move on to something more interesting." As a flashpoint, his response to the early-2022 Spotify controversy surfaces in Kyle's January 31st, 2022 — HQ Weekly Sync notes as the moment that "made me realize I had just accepted what my in-group was saying" — a personal example of the "intellectual seatbelt" problem (leaning on your in-group to feel safe believing something without evaluating it). That episode is also tagged into the Embrace The Nuance - Research doc, feeding Kyle's essay on resisting binary, in-group-driven thinking.

**Context:** Joe Rogan is an American comedian, UFC commentator, and host of *The Joe Rogan Experience*, one of the most-listened-to podcasts in the world; his Spotify deal made him a recurring figure in debates over content moderation and free speech.
