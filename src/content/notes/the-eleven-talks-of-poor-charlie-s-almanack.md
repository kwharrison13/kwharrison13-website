# The Eleven Talks of Poor Charlie's Almanack

These are Kyle's long-form notes that sit alongside his book notes on [Poor Charlie's Almanack](/books/poor-charlie-s-almanack).

**Chapter Four of Poor Charlie's Almanack is itself a collection of eleven talks** — speeches and addresses [Charlie Munger](/notes/charlie-munger) delivered between 1986 and 2000, gathered and annotated by Peter Kaufman. Rather than fold them into the single book page, each talk gets its own long-form note here so the highlights and annotations can stand on their own and cross-link freely.

## How it connects

- [Poor Charlie's Almanack](/books/poor-charlie-s-almanack) — the parent book; these talks are Chapter Four.
- [Charlie Munger](/notes/charlie-munger) — the speaker across all eleven talks.
- [Charlie Munger: A Lesson on Elementary Worldly Wisdom](/notes/charlie-munger-a-lesson-on-elementary-worldly-wisdom) and [Charlie Munger: A Lesson on Elementary Worldly Wisdom, Revisited](/notes/charlie-munger-a-lesson-on-elementary-worldly-wisdom-revisited) — Munger's core multidisciplinary "worldly wisdom" talks.
- [Charlie Munger: The Psychology of Human Misjudgment](/notes/charlie-munger-the-psychology-of-human-misjudgment) — the capstone talk cataloging the standard causes of human misjudgment.
- [Charlie Munger: Harvard School Commencement Speech](/notes/charlie-munger-harvard-school-commencement-speech) and [Charlie Munger: USC Gould School of Law Commencement Address](/notes/charlie-munger-usc-gould-school-of-law-commencement-address) — the commencement addresses among the eleven.
- [Charlie Munger: Practical Thought About Practical Thought?](/notes/charlie-munger-practical-thought-about-practical-thought) and [Charlie Munger: Academic Economics](/notes/charlie-munger-academic-economics) — talks applying his latticework of models to problem-solving and to the failings of economics.
- [Charlie Munger: Harvard Law School Fiftieth Reunion Address](/notes/charlie-munger-harvard-law-school-fiftieth-reunion-address), [Charlie Munger: Investment Practices of Leading Charitable Foundations](/notes/charlie-munger-investment-practices-of-leading-charitable-foundations), [Charlie Munger: Philanthropy Roundtable](/notes/charlie-munger-philanthropy-roundtable), and [Charlie Munger: The Great Financial Scandal of 2003](/notes/charlie-munger-the-great-financial-scandal-of-2003) — the remaining talks in the collection.
