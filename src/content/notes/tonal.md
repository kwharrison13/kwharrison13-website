# TONAL

In the Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021, Tonal appears in Watershed's customer-pipeline notes as a prospect in the "Fitness" cohort — the company reported it was "Talking to [TONAL](/notes/tonal)," listed alongside [Peloton ($PTON)](/notes/peloton) as the connected-fitness targets Watershed was pursuing for emissions-measurement work. It is a sales-lead reference, indicating which consumer-hardware/fitness brands were in Watershed's go-to-market funnel circa late 2021.

**Context:** Tonal is a U.S. connected-fitness company that makes a wall-mounted, AI-guided strength-training system using digital weight resistance, positioned as a strength-focused counterpart to cardio-oriented connected-fitness products.

## Where this appears

- Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 — listed as a fitness-cohort prospect Watershed was talking to, alongside Peloton.
