# Clearstone Associates

Clearstone Associates is the firm [Zach Edwards](/notes/zach-edwards) ran when Kyle's recurring calls with him took place; the name attaches to several years of conversations in Kyle's notes. Per the April 2020 call, Clearstone is described as "an acquisition organization working in the trucking, dental, funeral home, crematorium, and cemetery space" that "looks to take advantage of undisrupted industries through superior sourcing tactics, deep relationships, and technological implementations" — a search-fund / lower-middle-market roll-up posture rather than a venture firm. At the time Edwards was running a team of ~18 interns (with 25 new applicants) on projects spanning deal execution, market due diligence, landscape mapping, pitch-deck creation, and sourcing.

The firm also surfaced as a deal conduit: in the April 2020 call Edwards forwarded Kyle a diligence package on Pattern's Series A — a $300M-revenue e-commerce enablement business raising $20M with a 17.5% preferred return — framed as a family-and-close-network co-investment opportunity. By the September 2021 call the Clearstone label still travels with Edwards as the conversations widen into diversity-focused products and secularism (where he recommended [A Secular Age](/notes/a-secular-age) and [The Coddling of the American Mind](/notes/the-coddling-of-the-american-mind)). The firm itself is best understood as Edwards's vehicle; the substance of these notes is mostly his thinking and deal flow.
