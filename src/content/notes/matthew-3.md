# Matthew 3

A New Testament chapter Kyle cites in his Abrahamic Covenant study (Matthew 3:11 — John the Baptist's distinction between baptism by water and baptism with the Holy Ghost). In his scripture chain he groups it with [Acts 19](/notes/acts-19):1–7 and [John 1](/notes/john-1):29–34 to explain the relationship between [Baptism](/notes/baptism) and the laying on of hands — which he reads as agreeable to the 3rd through 6th Articles of Faith.
