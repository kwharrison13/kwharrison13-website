# Alma 44

A chapter of the Book of Mormon falling within the Come Follow Me reading block for August 3–9, 2020 (Alma 43–52). It is listed in that study's chapter range, but Kyle's notes for that week cover [Alma 43](/notes/alma-43) only — there is no annotation specific to Alma 44 in the source.

**Context:** In the Book of Mormon narrative, Alma 44 records the confrontation between [Moroni](/notes/moroni) and the Lamanite commander Zerahemnah, in which Moroni offers terms of peace if the Lamanites will covenant not to return to war.
