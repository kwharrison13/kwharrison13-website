# Ron Chernow

Biographer, listed as the author of [Titan](/notes/titan) in Kyle's anti-library.

**Context:** Ron Chernow is an American writer and biographer known for sweeping financial and political biographies, including *Titan: The Life of John D. Rockefeller, Sr.*, *Alexander Hamilton* (which inspired the musical), *The House of Morgan* (Pulitzer winner *Washington: A Life*), and *Grant*. *Titan* is his definitive life of the Standard Oil founder.
