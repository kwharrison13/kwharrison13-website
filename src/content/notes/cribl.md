# Cribl

Observability / security data-pipeline company that shows up in Kyle's 2021 security-infrastructure sourcing notes. In the [Monad](/notes/monad) pre-brief ("Fivetran for security"), Cribl is grouped with [Splunk](/notes/splunk) and Panther as a traditional/cloud [SIEM](/notes/siem) player — characterized as only "mildly competitive" to Monad because such systems are "closed systems that are difficult to manage, can't be accessed outside of the security teams and generally do little more than log aggregation and alerting" (September 17th, 2021 — Monad Pre-Brief (Max Rimpel)).

Cribl also surfaced as live deal flow in a call with Jeff Tang at [Coatue](/notes/coatue), who mentioned that Sequoia (co-leading Grafana with Coatue) had been "stopped… from doing Cribl" (June 29th, 2021 — Jeff Tang (Coatue)).

**Context:** Cribl is an observability data-pipeline company (best known for Cribl Stream / "Cribl LogStream") that lets enterprises route, shape, and reduce machine and security data before it lands in destinations like Splunk or a data lake.
