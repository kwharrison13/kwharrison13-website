# D&C 46

A section of the Doctrine and Covenants that anchors Kyle's study of Spiritual Gifts. Across his gospel-study notes it is cited for the doctrine that **each person is given a gift** — "to every man is given a gift by the Spirit of God" ([D&C 46](/notes/dandc-46):10–12) — and that no one who receives the [Holy Ghost](/notes/holy-ghost) is left without one. The Spiritual Gifts page pairs it with [1 Corinthians 12](/notes/1-corinthians-12) and [Moroni 10](/notes/moroni-10) as the three scriptural "lists" of gifts, and points the reader to their patriarchal blessing to discover which gift is theirs. The How to Overcome Depression note invokes D&C 46:11 the same way — your patriarchal blessing is "a catalyst to open the doors of personal revelation," because we are all given a spiritual gift.

The section is also read as a charter for **seeking** gifts and for distinguishing them. The Spiritual Gifts and Scripture Study — Topical Study notes cite D&C 46:8 ("seek ye earnestly the best gifts") and 46:31–33 (what we must do — practice virtue and holiness — to receive them). In the Knowledge — Topical Study note, verses 17–18 are used to separate two distinct gifts — "to some is given the word of wisdom… to another the word of knowledge." The Miscellaneous Scriptures working-web ties D&C 46 directly to [1 Corinthians 12](/notes/1-corinthians-12) and to the reasons we receive revelation (46:26).

**Context:** Doctrine and Covenants section 46 is a revelation received by Joseph Smith in 1831 in Kirtland, Ohio, on the conduct of Church meetings and the gifts of the Spirit. It is one of the standard LDS scriptural sources on spiritual gifts, alongside 1 Corinthians 12 and Moroni 10.

## Where this appears

- Spiritual Gifts — core proof-text: each is given a gift (46:10–12), seek the best gifts (46:8), and the conditions for receiving them (46:31–33)
- How to Overcome Depression — cited at 46:11 to argue that your patriarchal blessing reveals a spiritual gift given to you
- Knowledge — Topical Study — verses 17–18 distinguish the gift of wisdom from the gift of knowledge
- Miscellaneous Scriptures — cross-referenced with 1 Corinthians 12 on revelation and spiritual gifts (46:12–26)
- Revelation — Topical Study — listed among the scriptures on receiving and qualifying for revelation
- Scripture Study — Topical Study — cited at 46:26 and 46:33 on gifts given for the benefit of God's children and the call to virtue
