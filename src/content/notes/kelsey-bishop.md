# Kelsey Bishop

Founder/CEO of [Candor](/notes/candor), a [Contrary](/notes/contrary) portfolio company whose seed round Contrary led. Kyle tracked her monthly investor updates across 2022 while clearing emails at Contrary. Per the March 2022 update, the seed round drew participation from [Brianne Kimmel](/notes/brianne-kimmel), [Ellen DeSilva](/notes/ellen-desilva), [Joseph Quan](/notes/joseph-quan), [Andrew Farah](/notes/andrew-farah), and [Anamitra Banerji](/notes/anamitra-banerji), with other existing investors including [Ryan Delk](/notes/ryan-delk) of [Primer](/notes/primer), [Turner Novak](/notes/turner-novak), and [Alek Koenig](/notes/alek-koenig), CEO of [Settle.co](/notes/settle-co).

Her updates trace Candor's product positioning shifting between consumer and HR tech — the June 2022 note describes "taking a swing squarely in consumer" and remaking Candor as "the most beautiful ReadMe ever made," while later notes promote a "Candor Offsite Playbook for Founders" for team-building. By the September 2022 update, Candor reported September sign-ups growing 26% MoM (vs. 19.5% in August), referrals up 33%, and a migration from [SendGrid](/notes/sendgrid) to Customer.io for email, working toward a 50% MoM growth target.
