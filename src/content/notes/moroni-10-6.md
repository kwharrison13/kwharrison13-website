# Moroni 10:6

A Book of Mormon scripture reference attributed as a quote in [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book): "...nothing that is good denieth the Christ, but acknowledgeth that He is." It sits in the quote book's running collection alongside passages from [David O. McKay](/notes/david-o-mckay), [Brigham Young](/notes/brigham-young), and [C.S. Lewis](/notes/c-s-lewis) — a touchstone verse on recognizing that all genuine good points toward Christ.
