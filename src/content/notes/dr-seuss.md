# Dr. Seuss

Quoted in [Meaningful Travel](/notes/meaningful-travel) — "You have brains in your head. You have feet in your shoes. You can steer yourself any direction you choose… And YOU are the guy who'll decide where to go." — for the self-direction theme of the essay.

**Context:** Dr. Seuss was the pen name of Theodor Seuss Geisel, the American children's author and illustrator behind books like *Oh, the Places You'll Go!*, the source of the quoted lines.
