# Ho Nam

**One-line:** *Venture capitalist (Altos Ventures); his "what is an investor? Not a VC" reply frames venture as value-creation alongside founders rather than capital allocation for LP returns.*

_Stub page — created from [What Is An Investor - Research](/notes/what-is-an-investor-research). Expand on a future pass._

## What we know

- In [a tweet response](https://twitter.com/honam/status/1526364080922980354) to "what is an investor? Not a VC," he objected to comparing venture capital to investing — because company-building is so human, and an LP/returns frame can be detrimental to long-term value creation. (source: [What Is An Investor - Research](/notes/what-is-an-investor-research))
- Kyle reads his point as being about a *frame of mind*: measure success by value created, not dollars returned — and the dollars tend to follow.
- Echoes [Tom Perkins](/notes/tom-perkins)' "we are not investors" stance in [The Power Law — Venture Capital & the Making of the New Future](/books/the-power-law-venture-capital-and-the-making-of-the-new-future).
