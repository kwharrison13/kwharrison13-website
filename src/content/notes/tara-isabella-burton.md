# Tara Isabella Burton

In [Restoration — God's Call To The 21st Century](/books/restoration-god-s-call-to-the-21st-century), Tara Isabella Burton is cited via her *New York Times* essay "Christianity Gets Weird" (8 May 2020), referenced in support of the book's theme of religion challenging the assumptions of the present moment. **Context:** Tara Isabella Burton is an American writer and theologian, author of *Strange Rites: New Religions for a Godless World* and *Self-Made*, who writes on contemporary religion, secular spirituality, and the cultural movements she has dubbed the "Weird Christians."
