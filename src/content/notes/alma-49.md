# Alma 49

A war chapter in the Book of Mormon describing the fortified Nephite cities, falling within the range of Kyle's August 3–9, 2020. Alma 43–52: "Stand Fast in the Faith of Christ" study (whose annotations cover [Alma 43](/notes/alma-43) only). Its grounded use in Kyle's notes comes from a later study: in October 26–November 1, 2020. Mormon 1–6: "I Would That I Could Persuade All … to Repent", Kyle sets [Mormon 2](/notes/mormon-2):4 (Mormon's people fortify a city "with our might" and are still driven out) against Alma 49:3–5 — Captain [Moroni](/notes/moroni)'s defenses, by contrast, are "built in faith, not fear." The Nephites dig "a ridge of earth round about them" so the Lamanites "could not cast their stones and their arrows," astonishing the Lamanite captains "because of the wisdom of the Nephites in preparing their places of security." The contrast becomes Kyle's lesson: fortify in [Faith](/notes/faith), not fear.

**Context:** Alma 49 is a chapter in the Book of Alma in the Book of Mormon, recounting failed Lamanite assaults on the fortified Nephite cities of Ammonihah and Noah under Captain Moroni's defensive preparations.
