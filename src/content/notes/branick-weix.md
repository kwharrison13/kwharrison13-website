# Branick Weix

Founder/operator at [Aryeo](/notes/aryeo) (a real-estate listing-media platform) and the author of the company's monthly portfolio updates that Kyle, as a [Contrary](/notes/contrary) investor, read through 2022. His updates report Aryeo's scale and data moat: by spring 2022 more than 5% of all US home sales were listed on Aryeo, hosting 19,000+ homes in a month, and — crucially — a typical property hits Aryeo **6.82 days before** it appears on the MLS, [Zillow](/notes/zillow), or any real-estate search site, with Aryeo knowing the original media copyright holder (April 5th, 2022 — Aryeo Q1 Update (Branick Weix)).

His February 2022 update shows the strategy tightening: Weix's team decided to drop the standalone broker product and refocus on the core platform and the exclusive content/data it generates, while flagging two churn types (signups that never activated, and active customers missing scheduling features) (March 30th, 2022 — Aryeo February Update (Branick Weix)). Kyle continued reading the Aryeo updates into the fall (November 7th, 2022 — Cleared Email).
