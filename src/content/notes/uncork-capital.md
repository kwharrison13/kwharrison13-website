# Uncork Capital

Uncork Capital appears in the June 6th, 2022 call with [Katherine Boyle](/notes/katherine-boyle) at [a16z](/notes/a16z) as one of the early seed backers of [Anduril](/notes/anduril), named alongside [Susa Ventures](/notes/susa-ventures). Boyle recounted meeting Alex at the Anduril seed round only after Uncork and Susa had already come in, then working for a year to get a16z into the Series A. The firm is cited purely as context for how early and contested the Anduril round was — a supporting detail in Boyle's broader discussion of differentiated missions and boutique-firm positioning ([American Dynamism](/notes/american-dynamism)).

**Context:** Uncork Capital (formerly SoftTech VC), founded by Jeff Clavier, is one of Silicon Valley's longest-running seed-stage venture firms, with early investments including Fitbit, SendGrid, and Eventbrite.
