# Solamere Capital

Solamere Capital appears in Kyle's notes as a private-investment firm in his fundraising and LP network. [Scott Hutchins](/notes/scott-hutchins) at Solamere made the April 5th, 2022 introduction connecting Kyle to [Josh Beers](/notes/josh-beers) at [NEPC](/notes/nepc) regarding Contrary's first growth fund — a warm intro to an allocator. Separately, in the May 31st, 2018 [Strada Education Network](/notes/strada-education-network) call, Strada is recorded as a fund-of-funds investor and an LP in Solamere (alongside University Ventures, Leeds Equity, Vistria, Sterling, and others), placing Solamere inside the same allocator/fund-of-funds web that Strada participates in.

**Context:** Solamere Capital is a Boston-based private-investment firm (founded 2008) co-founded by Tagg Romney, Spencer Zwick, and Eric Scheuermann; it invests in private equity, venture, and credit, including as a fund-of-funds and direct investor.
