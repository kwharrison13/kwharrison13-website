# Alan McIntyre

Attributed in [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book) (page 27) as the source of the line: "When you're building a bridge, don't spend all your time building the approach." The quote sits alongside other aphorisms Kyle collected on focus and not over-preparing. No further biographical detail is given in the source.
