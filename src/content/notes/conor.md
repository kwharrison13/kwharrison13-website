# Conor

Conor appears in Kyle's notes on [The Everything Store](/books/the-everything-store) as a portfolio cross-reference, not a figure in the book itself — Kyle uses him to think through operating philosophy across companies he tracks. The recurring framing is "Conor's 1M jobs vs. 1M employees," invoked against Bezos's question of "how do you balance ambitions to be massive with a decentralized ethos?" Kyle also notes a "CommonStock comparison (Conor vs. David)" and ties it to a thread about trust: "Compare to Mark's comments about Conor's trust issues. Find someone you DO trust to carry out your discussions." The notes read Bezos's decentralized, loosely-coupled-teams model through Conor's contrasting framing of scale.

This is a private, idiosyncratic reference internal to Kyle's portfolio notes; the surrounding context points to CommonStock (where "Conor vs. David" frames a co-founder/leadership comparison), but the page carries no standalone biographical detail beyond what the annotations supply.
