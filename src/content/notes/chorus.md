# Chorus

A sales conversation-intelligence platform (Chorus.ai), referenced across Kyle's diligence notes as the sales-call-oriented peer to [Gong](/notes/gong). In the Cresta.ai - Diligence dossier, Chorus appears in the "agent assist / conversation intelligence" landscape but on the sales side of the dividing line: experts note that Chorus, Gong, and [Cresta.ai](/notes/cresta-ai) all handle live calls, but Chorus and Gong are "geared to sales-type calls" and lack the depth needed for contact-center / customer-support use — the gap Cresta positions into as a more service/sales-dedicated tool. One screened reference listed Chorus AI among the vendors they were familiar with alongside Gong, Balto, and Wingman.

In the CaptivateIQ Diligence — 2021 notes, Chorus comes up as the call-recording / conversation-intelligence tool a team uses internally, described (along with Gong) as "incredibly powerful."

**Context:** Chorus.ai was a conversation-intelligence company that analyzed sales calls and meetings; it was acquired by ZoomInfo in 2021 for roughly $575M.

## Where this appears

- Cresta.ai - Diligence — grouped with Gong as sales-conversation intelligence; contrasted with contact-center-purpose-built tools like Cresta.
- CaptivateIQ Diligence — 2021 — cited as a powerful internal call-recording / conversation tool (with Gong).
- [Gong](/notes/gong) — repeatedly paired as the sales-call-oriented competitor set.
