# René Bonvanie

Former CMO of [Palo Alto Networks ($PANW)](/notes/palo-alto-networks), noted in Kyle's March 30th, 2022 — Deal Announcements roundup as one of the participants in Israeli cloud data security company [Cyera](/notes/cyera)'s $60M financing round (led by Sequoia Capital, with Accel and Cyberstarts). Per the note, Bonvanie joined Cyera's board — a security-veteran operator-investor backing an early-stage company that had been founded only ten months earlier and came out of stealth that day.

**Context:** René Bonvanie spent roughly a decade as chief marketing officer of Palo Alto Networks, where he is credited with shaping the company's go-to-market and brand during its rise into a cybersecurity leader; he later moved into advising and investing in security startups.
