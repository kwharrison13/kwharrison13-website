# Snowflake ($SNOW)

Cloud data-warehouse company that Kyle uses as a diligence reference point. In a note on [Charlie Munger — The Psychology of Human Misjudgment](/notes/charlie-munger-the-psychology-of-human-misjudgment), he reflects that framing [Fivetran](/notes/fivetran) diligence questions as "how does this compare to Snowflake?" was the wrong frame because the two businesses are so different.

**Context:** Snowflake (NYSE: $SNOW) is a cloud-native data-warehousing and data-platform company founded in 2012, known for separating storage from compute and for its record-setting 2020 IPO. It is a frequent benchmark in modern-data-stack diligence.
