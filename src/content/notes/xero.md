# Xero

Xero appears in Kyle's notes as a competitive/complementary reference point, not a diligence subject. In the July 10th, 2018 — Jessica Mah (InDinero) call, [InDinero](/notes/indinero) CEO [Jessica Mah](/notes/jessica-mah) characterized her accounting-automation company as one you "could say" competes with Xero and [Quickbooks](/notes/quickbooks) — "but more than anything they're augmenting them," positioning InDinero as a layer that observes and augments accountants while pulling data from the underlying ledgers. That single mention is the extent of the grounding in Kyle's corpus.

**Context:** Xero is a New Zealand-founded cloud accounting software company serving small and medium businesses, broadly seen as a major competitor to Intuit's QuickBooks.

## Where this appears

- July 10th, 2018 — Jessica Mah (InDinero) — named by Jessica Mah as a ledger product InDinero competes with but more so augments, alongside QuickBooks.
