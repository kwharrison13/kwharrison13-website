# Fifth Partners

Fifth Partners surfaces in Kyle's notes as a firm building **modular homes for low-income families**, which is why it's tagged under [Alternative Real Estate](/notes/alternative-real-estate). The connection came through the July 2nd, 2020 — Lars Hamilton (High Alpha Innovation) meeting, where Tom Peterson is noted as an investor in Fifth Partners. Peterson is also the source of a related note Kyle flagged — pushback on glorifying "high risk, 1% success rate businesses that most people will fail at," and the idea that "you can do something great even if it doesn't feel as sexy."

Separately, Kyle uses #[Fifth Partners](/notes/fifth-partners) as a recurring **thematic tag** in his [Berkshire Hathaway Annual Letters](/books/berkshire-hathaway-annual-letters) highlights — attaching it to ideas about funds or collections of shareholders being assembled and leveraged as a collaborative body. In that usage Fifth Partners reads less as the specific modular-housing company and more as Kyle's shorthand for a collaborative-capital / aligned-ownership concept. (The name resolves to a real entity, "Fifth Partners Management LLC," captured via a March 30th, 2022 sourcing-log link.)
