# Kristina Shen

Kristina Shen appears in the Pave Diligence — 2021 log as a contact at [a16z](/notes/a16z) whom Kyle's team planned to connect with (via LinkedIn) during the Pave deal workup. In the diligence's "Next Steps," the only open question was traction — how strong Pave's product-market fit was — and the note proposes getting a16z's perspective from Shen as the way to close that gap: "If we could get perspective from Kristina on that front and everything checks out I think this would be a great one to try and go preempt." The round was expected at roughly $1B (up from a $400M post on the prior round).

**Context:** Kristina Shen is a general partner at Andreessen Horowitz (a16z), where she has focused on enterprise/SaaS investing.
