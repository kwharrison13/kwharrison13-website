# Matt Harsha-Strong

**One-line:** *CFA; CFO of [Uplight](/notes/uplight) and former Managing Director of M&A at [AES Corporation](/notes/aes-corporation) (left October 2022) — an expert on acquisition-driven capital allocation in renewables.*

## What we know

- Chief Financial Officer at [Uplight](/notes/uplight), a software company serving the energy industry; oversees all finance and accounting functions (source: June 20th, 2023 — Matt Harsha-Strong (Uplight)).
- Spent ~11 years at [AES Corporation](/notes/aes-corporation) leading corporate development and M&A; closed well over a dozen acquisitions and became one of the top 100 leaders across ~10K employees. Central to the capital-allocation process — drafted its valuation policy and presented to its committees and BoD (source: June 20th, 2023 — Matt Harsha-Strong (Uplight)).
- Originated and led a complex acquisition of a power-contract portfolio in Panama; led analysis of portfolio, capital structure, capital allocation, global power markets, and proposed investments (source: June 20th, 2023 — Matt Harsha-Strong (Uplight)).
- Engaged as an expert (rate: $300) for Kyle's [Sylva](/notes/sylva) diligence on roll-up / acquisition-platform mechanics (source: June 20th, 2023 — Matt Harsha-Strong (Uplight)).
