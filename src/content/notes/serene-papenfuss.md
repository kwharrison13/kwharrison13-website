# Serene Papenfuss

Serene Papenfuss is an investor at [Kickstart Seed Fund](/notes/kickstart-seed-fund) who Kyle spoke with on a November 8th, 2021 sourcing call while at [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures). In that call she gave a Utah-ecosystem read: she was chatting with [Trinsic.id](/notes/trinsic-id) (where [Dalton Wright](/notes/dalton-wright) led the deal and called the self-sovereign-identity space "inevitable," citing BMW and COVID-vaccine use cases), and flagged [CloudApp](/notes/cloudapp) as having hit $5M ARR and raising a round.

On the broader [Utah Tech Ecosystem](/notes/utah-tech-ecosystem), her signal was about talent flows: she was seeing a lot of people leaving [Divvy](/notes/divvy) and [Pluralsight](/notes/pluralsight), cautioned that sales-led operators out of [Qualtrics](/notes/qualtrics) tend to over-index on hiring (e.g. "30 sales reps in the first year"), noted Divvy alumni were a weaker signal, and rated product/engineering people coming out of [Lucid](/notes/lucid) and [Pluralsight](/notes/pluralsight) as typically better than average.
