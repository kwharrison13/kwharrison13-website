# JST Genesis 14

The Joseph Smith Translation of Genesis chapter 14. In Kyle's Abrahamic Covenant topical study, [JST Genesis 14](/notes/jst-genesis-14):29–31 is cited in the discussion of the three grand societies of the church to support the claim that priesthood *power* (as distinct from authority) "can come through passing through the 3 orders of the priesthood." The reference sits inside the larger theme — drawn from [Joseph Smith](/notes/joseph-smith)'s August 27, 1843 sermon on the three orders — that authority is given but power is unlocked by making and keeping covenants.

**Context:** The Joseph Smith Translation (JST) is [Joseph Smith](/notes/joseph-smith)'s revision of the King James Bible. The JST expansion of Genesis 14 contains an extended passage on the high priesthood of Melchizedek and its powers, a text central to Latter-day Saint priesthood doctrine.
