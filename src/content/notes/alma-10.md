# Alma 10

A chapter in the Book of Mormon (book of Alma), referenced in Kyle's [Come, Follow Me](/notes/come-follow-me) study of Alma 13–16 — Enter into the Rest of the Lord as cross-reference context. In Kyle's notes it anchors the question of **what [Amulek](/notes/amulek) gave up for the gospel**: his reputation (Alma 10:4), his friends (Alma 10:4), his wealth (Alma 10:4), and living up to his ancestors' faith (Alma 10:3). Kyle also notes Alma 10:6, where Amulek describes having been rebellious and not listening — the chapter removes "the guilt of not [Listening](/notes/listening)" by showing that even Amulek had to learn to hear.

**Context:** Alma chapter 10 records the testimony of Amulek, who introduces himself, recounts his lineage and former state, and joins Alma in preaching to the people of Ammonihah.
