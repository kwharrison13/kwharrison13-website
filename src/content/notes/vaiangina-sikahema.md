# Vaiangina Sikahema

Latter-day Saint general authority Seventy and former BYU football player whose October 2021 General Conference talk Kyle logged in General Conference- October 2021. Sikahema's theme is sequence in spiritual learning — "as we study, learn, and live the gospel of Jesus Christ sequence often is instructive" — drawing on the order of Restoration events (the First Vision teaching the nature of God, then Moroni and the Book of Mormon). Kyle pairs the talk with the "milk before meat" image (1 Corinthians 3:2) and notes Sikahema's line that "the Lord teaches us individually according to our capacity to learn," tying it to the [Spirit of Humility](/notes/spirit-of-humility) as prerequisite to a [Broken heart and a contrite spirit](/notes/broken-heart-and-a-contrite-spirit).

**Context:** Vaiangina (Vai) Sikahema (b. 1962), born in Tonga, was an NFL kick returner and later a Philadelphia sports broadcaster before being called as a general authority Seventy of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in 2021.
