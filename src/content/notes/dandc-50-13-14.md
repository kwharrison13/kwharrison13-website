# D&C 50:13-14

A passage of the [Doctrine and Covenants](/notes/doctrine-and-covenants) quoted in [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book): *"Wherefore, I the Lord ask you this question — unto what were ye ordained? To preach my gospel by the Spirit, even the Comforter which was sent forth to teach the truth."* It is collected (page 40) among the book's scriptural quotes on the missionary calling and Spirit-led teaching.

**Context:** Doctrine and Covenants section 50 is an 1831 revelation given through [Joseph Smith](/notes/joseph-smith) on discerning true from false spirits. Verses 13–14 ask elders to remember the purpose of their ordination — to preach the gospel by the Spirit — a passage also leaned on in Kyle's Teaching Repentance notes.

## Where this appears

- [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book) — quoted verbatim (page 40) on being ordained to preach the gospel by the Spirit
