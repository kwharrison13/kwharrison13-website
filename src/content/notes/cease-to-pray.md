# Cease to Pray

"Cease to Pray" is a study theme Kyle drew from 3 Nephi 20:1 in his Come Follow Me notes for the week of October 12–18, 2020. The verse records that Christ "commanded the multitude that they should cease to pray, and also his disciples. And he commanded them that they should not cease to pray in their hearts." Kyle reads this as a standing commandment not to cease praying in the heart, paired with an honest self-examination: "What does it mean to pray in your hearts? Feels like I sometimes use it as an excuse to not say my prayers; 'well I'm praying in my heart all the time.'" He tags that worry [Questions of the Soul](/notes/questions-of-the-soul). The theme sits among the week's core threads alongside [Servant Leadership](/notes/servant-leadership), [Intercession of Christ](/notes/intercession-of-christ), and [Sign Seeking](/notes/sign-seeking).
