# Reyna I. Aburto

A General Officer of [The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints](/notes/the-church-of-jesus-christ-of-latter-day-saints) who served in the Relief Society General Presidency. In her April 2022 General Conference address, "We Are The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints", she recounts joining the Church at age 26 after accepting an invitation to "come and see" — recently separated from her first husband, with a three-year-old son, and "powerless with fear" — finding in the congregation "a refuge from the storm." Her thesis: "The Church is the members"; the buildings and ecclesiastical structure are the "scaffolding with which we build eternal families."
