# COP26

The 2021 UN climate conference, referenced in the Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 dossier through Watershed's COP26 debrief content. Watershed hosted a "Policy: COP26 and Beyond" breakout in which co-founder [Taylor Francis](/notes/taylor-francis) interviewed Nat Keohane (former White House climate advisor, then President of the Center for Climate and Energy Solutions). The takeaway captured in the notes: when discussing climate policy, don't lead with technical detail — set the stage with how the policy makes people's lives concretely better, then get into the how. The dossier also links a Watershed-hosted COP26 debrief video and a Mark Carney piece on investor demand for climate action.

**Context:** COP26 was the 26th UN Climate Change Conference of the Parties, held in Glasgow in November 2021, where nations negotiated commitments under the Paris Agreement and produced the Glasgow Climate Pact. (Note: COP26 is an event/conference rather than a company.)
