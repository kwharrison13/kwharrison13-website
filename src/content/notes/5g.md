# 5G

**One-line definition:** *Fifth-generation mobile network technology; a demand driver for smartphone upgrade cycles where the network is built out and computing is phone-centric.*

## How sources describe it

- In China the 5G network is well established and the overwhelming majority of phones sold are 5G, creating pent-up anticipation that drove a strong 5G [Apple](/notes/apple) iPhone cycle (source: [Apple Earnings, An Interview with Jay Goldberg About Chips and Intel](/notes/apple-earnings-an-interview-with-jay-goldberg-about-chips-and-intel)).
- 5G matters more (1) where it is widely available and (2) where most computing — including heavy streaming video — happens on the phone.

## Where it shows up

- [Apple Earnings, An Interview with Jay Goldberg About Chips and Intel](/notes/apple-earnings-an-interview-with-jay-goldberg-about-chips-and-intel) — 5G as the driver of China iPhone strength and mobile-video growth.
