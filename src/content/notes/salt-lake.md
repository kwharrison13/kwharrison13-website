# Salt Lake

**One-line definition:** *Salt Lake City — referenced through its economic development department's effort to recruit and retain tech and green-tech companies and grow local entrepreneurship.*

## How sources describe it

- Economic development department recruits and retains companies (and employees) to expand Utah's tech scene; tech and green technology are the two big target sectors, with unemployment so low the focus is matching skilled people to jobs. (June 28th, 2018 — William Wright (City of Salt Lake))
- Marketing a foreign trade zone (tariff-advantaged, keeps jobs in the US) and building an in-land port where goods rail-shipped from San Diego are considered imported in Salt Lake; I-15/I-80 make it strong for transportation and logistics.
- Department structure: a mayor-appointed director, a redevelopment agency (the city council, a division of the department), and four managers — business recruitment/retention, local entrepreneurs, workforce development, and research/marketing.
