# Lightyear

Lightyear is a telecom procurement startup founded and led by [Dennis Thankachan](/notes/dennis-thankachan), and one of the portfolio companies Kyle tracked via investor updates while at Contrary. Its pitch, recorded from a December 2021 investor update: "Buy and manage your business telecom services (internet, voice, WAN, data center) through a single, trustworthy pane of glass. Carrier neutral. Transparent. Fast." (lightyear.ai) (January 11th, 2022).

Across 2022 Kyle logged the company's progress from Thankachan's monthly updates. The March 2022 update reported Lightyear's first-ever paying SaaS customer (Bowery Farming, on a one-year bill-consolidation trial), new leadership hires in [Customer Success](/notes/customer-success) (VP Imran Ulla) and [Solutions Engineering](/notes/solutions-engineering) (Head Matt Pinto), and outbound meetings booked with Apple, Neiman Marcus, and Five Guys (March 30th, 2022 — Lightyear March Update (Dennis Thankachan)). Later updates (read while clearing email in November 2022) noted the SVPs of Service Delivery at Zayo and GTT — two of the largest global carriers — joining as advisors to help escalate customer install issues, plus a search for a business-operations leader on a potential COO track (November 7th, 2022 — Cleared Email).
