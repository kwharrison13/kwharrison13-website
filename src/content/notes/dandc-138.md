# D&C 138

Section 138 of the Doctrine and Covenants — [Joseph F. Smith](/notes/joseph-f-smith)'s vision of the redemption of the dead — appears in Kyle's study as a key text in the Plan of Salvation — Topical Study, where verse 32 describes the **spirit prison** (those who died without a knowledge of the truth, and those who rejected it) and verse 59 features in his "Hotel Hell" thread on the spirit world. His Miscellaneous Scriptures notes flag the section's origin — "Joseph F. Smith ponders the scriptures" — and Spiritual Gifts cites verse 56 in the context of premortal preparation and learning.

**Context:** Doctrine and Covenants Section 138 records a vision received by President [Joseph F. Smith](/notes/joseph-f-smith) on October 3, 1918, as he pondered the scriptures (notably 1 Peter on Christ preaching to the spirits in prison). It describes Christ's visit to the spirit world between His death and resurrection, the organizing of missionary work among the dead, and the redemption available to those who died without the gospel.
