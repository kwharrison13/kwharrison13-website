# Christina Huang

A [Stripe](/notes/stripe) alum quoted in Kyle's research for *The Talent Vortex* essay, describing Stripe's distinctive culture. In her telling, Stripe has "a very almost academic culture where people can spend time 'falafel'ing on concepts instead of coming to the fastest solution," which ends up attracting people with unusually wide hobbies and interests outside of work. She credits much of this culture of learning to [Patrick](/notes/patrick-collison) getting involved in issues beyond payments, and points to concrete artifacts: a book-publishing arm (Stripe Press) and "night class" sessions where they once brought in professors on subjects like astronomy.
