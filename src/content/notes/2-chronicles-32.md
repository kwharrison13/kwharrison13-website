# 2 Chronicles 32

Both of Kyle's references to **2 Chronicles 32** rest on a single verse, 32:31, read as a statement about being proved and tested. In Grace For Grace, the verse is glossed "To try him, to know what is there." In Patience — Topical Study, the same verse carries the lesson "There is something you are to learn from this experience" — God withdrawing to prove the heart.

**Context:** 2 Chronicles 32 recounts King Hezekiah of Judah and the Assyrian invasion under Sennacherib; verse 31 notes that when ambassadors came from Babylon, God "left him, to try him, that he might know all that was in his heart."

## Where this appears

- Grace For Grace — cites 2 Chronicles 32:31, God trying a man "to know what is there"
- Patience — Topical Study — reads 2 Chronicles 32:31 as God's testing teaching us something through the experience
