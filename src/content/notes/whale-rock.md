# Whale Rock

Investment firm with a private (crossover) side where [Wei Guo](/notes/wei-guo) invests, per a March 2020 career conversation.

## Notes

- ~$5B AUM; 8 people on the private side (as of early 2020).
- [Wei Guo](/notes/wei-guo) focuses on China, FinTech, and some gaming.
- Culture: high expectations but "not a douchey culture." No formal partner track (common across hedge funds); analysts work directly with the founder and bring ideas straight to him.
- Grouped by Wei alongside [Tiger Global Management](/notes/tiger-global-management) and Viking as "all very similar."
