# Thomas Tull

**One-line:** *Founder of [Legendary Entertainment](/notes/legendary-entertainment), cited in [The Hits Business](/essays/the-hits-business) as the foil to [A24](/notes/a24) — building blockbuster scale by weaponizing globalization.*

## What we know

- Built [Legendary Entertainment](/notes/legendary-entertainment) using a [formula](https://www.forbes.com/sites/natalierobehmed/2016/02/10/the-global-mogul/) that leaned on globalization and the [Chinese market](https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/wonk/wp/2016/09/24/chinas-influence-over-hollywood-grows/).
- Profiled as a ["secretive billionaire"](https://www.theinformation.com/articles/secretive-billionaire-thomas-tulls-never-ending-transformation?rc=ecjlpf) in The Information. Kyle flags his empire as "a whole other piece I could write."
