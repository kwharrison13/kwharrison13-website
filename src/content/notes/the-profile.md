# The Profile

The Profile is [Polina Marinova](/notes/polina-marinova)'s newsletter of curated long-form profiles, including a recurring "Profile Dossier" format of single-subject deep-dives. In Kyle's notes it surfaces as a reading source: on 2020-07-02 he read two editions back to back — a Musk-themed roundup ([The Profile: The Musk of steamy romance & the civil rights leader full of hope](/notes/the-profile-the-musk-of-steamy-romance-and-the-civil-rights-leader-full-of-hope)) and the [Elon Musk](/notes/elon-musk) "Architect of the Future" Profile Dossier ([The Profile Dossier: Elon Musk, the Architect of the Future](/notes/the-profile-dossier-elon-musk-the-architect-of-the-future)) — both captured in his July 2nd, 2020 daily note. The [Polina Marinova](/notes/polina-marinova) page names her as the writer behind it.

**Context:** The Profile is a newsletter and media brand founded by Polina Marinova Pompliano that curates and writes in-depth profiles of successful people and companies; its "Profile Dossier" series compiles a single notable figure's story, habits, and lessons.
