# Hallow

Faith / prayer-app company that [Katherine Boyle](/notes/katherine-boyle) of [a16z](/notes/a16z) cited as a marquee example of the firm's differentiated, "true believers" thesis. In the June 6th, 2022 — Katherine Boyle (a16z) conversation, Boyle argued that highly differentiated messaging — being "okay with the fact that 50% of people won't be a fit" — beats down-the-fairway deals, and capped the point with her quip that "God ([Hallow](/notes/hallow)) and Country ([Anduril](/notes/anduril)) are great." Hallow stands in for the "God" half of that pairing: the kind of mission-driven, values-aligned company a16z's American Dynamism / differentiated-conviction posture is built to attract, set against [Anduril](/notes/anduril) as the "Country" exemplar.

**Context:** Hallow is a Catholic prayer and meditation app founded in 2018; it raised venture funding from a16z and became one of the most downloaded faith apps in the U.S., often discussed as part of a16z's "American Dynamism" portfolio thesis.
