# Fintech

Fintech (financial technology) is a recurring coverage sector across Kyle's sourcing and diligence notes. The clearest grounding is a November 2021 deal-flow exchange in which Frank Rotman of [QED Investors](/notes/qed-investors) — a fintech-specialist fund — forwarded [Mark Goldberg](/notes/mark-goldberg) (then at Index Ventures) QED's internal notes on fintech companies "gearing up for fundraises," including one tracking ~$11M ARR and running ahead of Modern Treasury on payment volume. The same call surfaced QED's read on the broader market, including Tiger Global's plan to deploy $12B a year and its "35-box view of the market." Fintech also appears as [Inder Takhar](/notes/inder-takhar)'s primary coverage sector at [Village Capital](/notes/village-capital), where he sources entrepreneurs and executes investments across fintech and energy accelerator programs.

**Context:** Fintech refers to companies using software and technology to deliver or improve financial services — payments, lending, banking infrastructure, investing, insurance, and embedded finance — often competing with or supplying incumbent banks.
