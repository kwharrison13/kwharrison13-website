# Alma 16

A Book of Mormon chapter studied in Kyle's Alma 13–16 Come Follow Me week, which covers the aftermath of the events at Ammonihah. In Kyle's notes the chapter anchors a section titled **"Your Day of the Lord is at Hand."** He contrasts Alma's prophetic warning that "Now is the time to repent, for the day of salvation draweth nigh" (Alma 13:21) — issued 80+ years before Christ's coming — with the near-term destruction in Alma 16, where "the armies of the Lamanites had come in upon the wilderness side… and began to slay the people and destroy the city." Kyle's reading: prophetic warnings about repenting "now" aren't only big-picture, end-of-the-world warnings; sometimes they point to an immediate, personal need to repent. The chapter also feeds Kyle's tracing of what [Amulek](/notes/amulek) gave up for the gospel ("more universal love," Alma 16:14; "building a church," Alma 16:15).

**Context:** Alma 16 recounts a Lamanite invasion that destroys the city of Ammonihah (fulfilling earlier prophecy), the Nephite military response under the chief captain Zoram guided by Alma's revelation, and the continued ministry of Alma and Amulek across the land.
