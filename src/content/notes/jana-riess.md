# Jana Riess

Author of [The Next Mormons](/books/the-next-mormons) and a sociologist of religion who built the Next Mormons Survey (NMS), the dataset behind the book's portrait of generational change among Latter-day Saints. Kyle's engagement with the book is selective: his main note reframes a member's "openness-led-to-doubts" arc by asking "who taught her she needed to find fault with other people's belief system?", and contrasts it with [John Taylor](/notes/john-taylor)'s "bring all the good you have and let us see if we can add to it" posture as the lost alternative.

**Context:** Jana Riess is a journalist and scholar of religion (Ph.D., Columbia) known for her *Flunking Sainthood* column and her data-driven reporting on Mormonism. *The Next Mormons* (2019) drew on the original Next Mormons Survey to document how millennial and ex-Mormon Latter-day Saints differ from older generations.
