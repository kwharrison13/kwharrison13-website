# RBC

RBC appears in Kyle's notes only as the employer of [Nick Anger](/notes/nick-anger) and [Brian Blair](/notes/brian-blair), two contacts [Zach Edwards](/notes/zach-edwards) introduced to Kyle in April 2022. Per the April 5th, 2022 — Intro to Nick Anger & Brian Blair (RBC) intro email, the two "were looking to do something in venture/growth," and Zach connected them to Kyle as a way to get access to deals and people while Kyle was fundraising for Contrary Capital's growth fund. There is no deeper analysis of RBC as an institution in the corpus — the link is relationship/fundraising context, not company diligence.

**Context:** RBC (Royal Bank of Canada) is Canada's largest bank by market capitalization, a diversified global financial-services firm spanning personal/commercial banking, wealth management, and capital markets.
