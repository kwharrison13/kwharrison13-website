# The Spirit of Prophecy

In Kyle's Alma 36–38 study, "the spirit of prophecy" is the lens Alma uses to counsel his sons. It is anchored to two scriptures: Alma 37:15, where Alma says "by the spirit of prophecy" the sacred records will be taken away if his son transgresses; and Revelation 19:10, "for the testimony of Jesus is the spirit of prophecy." Kyle reads the concept as conditional promise — he ties it directly to the [Central Theme of the Book of Mormon](/notes/central-theme-of-the-book-of-mormon): "IF you keep the commandments THEN you shall prosper," and again at Alma 38:5, "as much as ye shall put your trust in God even so much ye shall be delivered out of your trials." The concept is tagged [Gospel Topics to Explore](/notes/gospel-topics-to-explore), marking it as a thread Kyle wants to develop further.

**Context:** In Latter-day Saint scripture, "the spirit of prophecy" is glossed by Revelation 19:10 as "the testimony of Jesus" — understood as the witness, borne by the Holy Ghost, that Jesus is the Christ, available to believers generally and not only to ordained prophets.
