# SPAC

A SPAC (Special Purpose Acquisition Company) is a "blank-check" shell company that raises capital through an IPO in order to merge with and take a private company public. It appears in Kyle's wiki via the [Chamath Palihapitiya](/notes/chamath-palihapitiya) profile, where he describes preferring the money-raising process of a SPAC to that of a VC fund — "short bursts of effort from a fundraising perspective, while VC fundraising requires more hand-holding and thus time and focus."

**Context:** SPACs became a dominant alternative route to the public markets around 2020–2021, with [Chamath Palihapitiya](/notes/chamath-palihapitiya)'s Social Capital Hedosophia vehicles (taking Virgin Galactic, Opendoor, and others public) among the highest-profile examples.
