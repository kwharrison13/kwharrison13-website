# Contrary Flywheel

The Contrary Flywheel is an internal [Contrary Capital](/notes/contrary) framing Kyle captured during a June 6th, 2022 call with [Joel Kwartler](/notes/joel-kwartler). The thesis, as recorded in the notes: peer talent programs like [Y-Combinator](/notes/y-combinator), On Deck, and Launch House have "all scaled past their capacities," whereas Contrary's edge is "trying to build a specific flywheel that most effectively curates the products they offer to a specific user." It pairs with the [Contrary Product Road Map](/notes/contrary-product-road-map) (Talent / Customers / Investors) — the road map names the constituencies, the flywheel is the curation-and-compounding mechanism that's meant to keep those audiences reinforcing each other rather than diluting as the program scales.
