# Cindy Wiser

Cindy Wiser is named in [Faith is Not Blind](/books/faith-is-not-blind) as the source of a pointed line Kyle saved among the book's highlights: "Whatever you were reading, I'm pretty sure it wasn't the [Book of Mormon](/notes/book-of-mormon)." The remark sits inside the Hafens' larger argument that running into criticism or a complicated point of historical opinion shouldn't shift the burden of proof onto the traditional source — a caution against letting incomplete or biased reading stand in for the primary text.
