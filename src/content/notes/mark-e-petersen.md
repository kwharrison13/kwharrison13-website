# Mark E. Petersen

An apostle of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, quoted in Kyle's notes on [Charlie Munger — A Lesson on Elementary Worldly Wisdom, Revisited](/notes/charlie-munger-a-lesson-on-elementary-worldly-wisdom-revisited) for the line that [Honesty](/notes/honesty) "is a principle of salvation in the kingdom of God" — used to amplify Munger's point about the importance of honesty.

**Context:** Mark E. Petersen (1900–1984) was a member of the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints from 1944 until his death, and a longtime newspaperman and editor in addition to his church service.
