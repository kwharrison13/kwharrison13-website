# Matthew 28

A New Testament chapter Kyle cites in his Abrahamic Covenant study for the Great Commission (Matthew 28:19) and the question of [Baptism](/notes/baptism) authority. In his scripture chain he sets it alongside [Revelation 1](/notes/revelation-1):5–6 and [Mark 16](/notes/mark-16):14–16: while the call to baptize is a common Christian belief, Kyle notes that Mark clarifies Jesus was speaking specifically to the eleven Apostles — supporting the Priesthood Authority argument that the commission to baptize was given to ordained men, not to everyone (paired with [Matthew 7](/notes/matthew-7):21–24, that not everyone is authorized to act in Christ's name).
