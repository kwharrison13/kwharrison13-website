# Laurene Powell Jobs

In the wiki, Laurene Powell Jobs appears as an early backer of [Watershed Climate](/notes/watershed-climate). The Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 dossier records her among the investors in Watershed's $14.7M Series A (at a $56.7M post-money valuation, closed July 16, 2020), alongside [Sequoia](/notes/sequoia) and [Kleiner Perkins](/notes/kleiner-perkins). Her participation is noted as part of the strong investor signal Index Ventures weighed when it pursued a proprietary look at Watershed's later Series B — Watershed was described as "the best-referenced product and team we've seen in climate tech."

**Context:** Laurene Powell Jobs is an American businesswoman and philanthropist, founder and president of Emerson Collective, which makes both impact investments and grants across climate, education, immigration, and media. She is the widow of Apple co-founder Steve Jobs. (General background, outside the wiki sources; her Watershed stake is attributed to Emerson Collective in earlier wiki notes.)
