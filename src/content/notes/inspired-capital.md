# Inspired Capital

Inspired Capital appears in Kyle's notes as the venture firm of [Mark Batsiyan](/notes/mark-batsiyan), surfaced during his diligence on [Teamshares](/notes/teamshares). In the March 2020 reference call, Batsiyan — joining the Teamshares board with a strong fintech background — describes Inspired as a **$200M fund** focused on seed and Series A, fintech-heavy (having just invested in Finix), with a 6-person team. He framed the biggest risk to Teamshares as **LP appetite** for what he sees as a genuinely new asset class, and was confident enough in Michael and Alex's early traction on the capital-markets side to back a "hungry and scrappy" junior hire over a more senior one. He was willing to share Inspired's investment memo. Inspired is also recorded on the [Teamshares](/notes/teamshares) page as the investor whose partner ([Mark Batsiyan](/notes/mark-batsiyan)) joined that board.

**Context:** Inspired Capital is a New York-based early-stage venture firm co-founded in 2019 by Alexa von Tobel and Penny Pritzker, investing across fintech and broader software at the seed and Series A stages.
