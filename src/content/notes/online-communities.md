# Online Communities

A recurring theme across Kyle's early-Perell long-reads: as the cultural mainstream collapses into niche, identity-based fandoms, online communities — convened through mobile apps — become the modern town square and the basic unit of culture after markets of scarcity give way to markets of abundance.

## Where this appears

- [Saving Retail](/notes/saving-retail) — mobile apps as the modern town-square convening mechanism
- [The Attention Game](/notes/the-attention-game) — niche, identity-based communities as the unit of culture once the mainstream collapses
