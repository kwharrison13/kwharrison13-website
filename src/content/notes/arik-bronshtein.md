# Arik Bronshtein

**One-line:** *Founder/operator at [UrbanLeap](/notes/urbanleap), a platform that helps cities structure and manage pilot tests of new solutions.*

## What we know

- Worked in government for 5 years and then served as a public-private partnership leader submitting requests to government, before [UrbanLeap](/notes/urbanleap).
- Believes the only way for governments to grow and innovate is to do more testing of new products, and that cities lack the capacity/knowledge to do as much testing as they should.
- Had been talking with [Mariel Reed](/notes/mariel-reed) (who may join the company) about the transition from a successful pilot (UrbanLeap) to RFP ([CoProcure](/notes/coprocure)).
