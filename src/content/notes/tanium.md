# Tanium

Tanium is an endpoint security and management company that appears in Kyle's notes as a competitive force shaping the [Uptycs](/notes/uptycs) cap table. In the September 22nd, 2021 — Ganesh Pai (Uptycs) diligence call, [Ganesh Pai](/notes/ganesh-pai) recounted that [Salesforce](/notes/salesforce) (along with [ServiceNow ($NOW)](/notes/servicenow)) was "eager to invest" in Uptycs but was blocked by Tanium — "thou shalt not invest" — apparently because Tanium held a position or relationship that gave it veto-like influence over a Salesforce check. Separately, a [CapitalG](/notes/capitalg) term sheet for Uptycs was shut down by the [CrowdStrike ($CRWD)](/notes/crowdstrike) team. Tanium is also named on the [Uptycs](/notes/uptycs) page as an adjacent comparable in the endpoint-security space.

**Context:** Tanium is a privately held cybersecurity company known for its endpoint management and security platform, which gives large enterprises real-time visibility and control across very large fleets of machines.

## Where this appears
