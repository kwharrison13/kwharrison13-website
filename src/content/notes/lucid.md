# Lucid

Lucid (Lucid Software) shows up in Kyle's notes both as a **Utah-tech talent source** and as a **product-design / whiteboarding tool** in the workflow stack. In the November 2021 sourcing call with [Serene Papenfuss](/notes/serene-papenfuss) ([Kickstart Seed Fund](/notes/kickstart-seed-fund)), Lucid is named within the [Utah Tech Ecosystem](/notes/utah-tech-ecosystem) as a company whose alumni are strong: per Serene, **product and engineering people coming out of Lucid and [Pluralsight](/notes/pluralsight) are "typically better than average."** In the Sprig (fka UserLeap) diligence notes, Lucid appears in the product-tooling map as a tool for **whiteboarding and brainstorming / process design** — grouped alongside Miro, FigJam, and [Figma](/notes/figma) for ideation, and called out as "important for brainstorming/process design" relative to Mural (whiteboarding only).

**Context:** Lucid Software is a South Jordan, Utah-based SaaS company known for Lucidchart (diagramming) and Lucidspark (whiteboarding), used for diagramming, process design, and visual collaboration.
