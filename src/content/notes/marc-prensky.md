# Marc Prensky

**One-line:** *Education writer who coined the "digital natives vs. digital immigrants" distinction in his 2001 essay.*

## What we know

- In his 2001 essay *Digital Natives, Digital Immigrants*, Prensky argues American "students have changed radically" and "are no longer the people our educational system was designed to teach" — cited by [David Perell](/notes/david-perell) in [It's Time To Disrupt Our Archaic Education System](/notes/it-s-time-to-disrupt-our-archaic-education-system).

## Mentioned in

- [It's Time To Disrupt Our Archaic Education System](/notes/it-s-time-to-disrupt-our-archaic-education-system) — the digital-natives framing Perell builds on.
