# Duncan Clark

Author of [Alibaba: The House That Jack Ma Built](/books/alibaba-the-house-that-jack-ma-built), the [Jack Ma](/notes/jack-ma) biography Kyle read in 2017 (a light-engagement company-building read). The book is Kyle's primary source on Alibaba's origin story, and Clark is listed alongside Jack Ma as one of its two central figures (author and subject).

**Context:** Duncan Clark is a British technology consultant and investor based in China — founder of the advisory firm BDA China and an early advisor to Alibaba — which gave him firsthand vantage on the company's rise, the basis for his 2016 book.
