# AdmitHub

AdmitHub surfaces in Kyle's notes as a portfolio company of [Strada Education Network](/notes/strada-education-network). In the May 31st, 2018 intro call with [Andre Bennin](/notes/andre-bennin), Bennin listed AdmitHub among the companies Strada had backed along the "student lifecycle" — named in the same breath as Project Retention and Inside Track. At the time, Strada's investment portfolio (~15 companies out of a ~$20M fund) had begun shifting from a "student success" focus (coaching, retention) toward "workforce transition" and the future of work. AdmitHub sits on the student-success / engagement side of that thesis.

**Context:** AdmitHub (later rebranded Mainstay) was an edtech company building AI-driven chatbots to support college students — answering admissions, enrollment, and retention questions at scale to reduce "summer melt" and improve completion. This is general background, not stated in the source.
