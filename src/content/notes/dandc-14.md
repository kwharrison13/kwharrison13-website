# D&C 14

A section of the [Doctrine and Covenants](/notes/doctrine-and-covenants) that Kyle cites in his Publication of the Book of Mormon study. **D&C 14:8** is invoked for the teaching that as we receive the Holy Ghost we are enabled to "stand as a witness of the things of which you shall both hear and see" — a verse Kyle ties to the principle of bearing witness.

**Context:** Doctrine and Covenants section 14 is an 1829 revelation given through [Joseph Smith](/notes/joseph-smith) to David Whitmer, one of the Three Witnesses of the Book of Mormon. Verse 8 promises that if one asks in faith with the intent to serve, the Holy Ghost will rest upon them so they can stand as a witness.
