# Maia Szalavitz

Science journalist and co-author, with child psychiatrist [Bruce D. Perry](/notes/bruce-d-perry), of [The Boy Who Was Raised as a Dog](/books/the-boy-who-was-raised-as-a-dog). In that book Perry's clinical cases anchor the neurodevelopmental argument and Szalavitz serves as the journalist collaborator who shapes the material into narrative — the standard division of labor in their partnership where the physician supplies the cases and the writer supplies the prose.

**Context:** Maia Szalavitz is an American journalist who writes about neuroscience, addiction, and child development. Beyond co-authoring *The Boy Who Was Raised as a Dog* with Bruce Perry, she is known for her own work on addiction, including *Unbroken Brain*.
