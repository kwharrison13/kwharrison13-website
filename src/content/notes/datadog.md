# Datadog

**One-line:** *Observability / monitoring platform (NASDAQ: DDOG), used by [Shardul Shah](/notes/shardul-shah) as a company-building case study in premium pricing, practitioner-led marketing, and TAM expansion.*

## What we know

[Shardul Shah](/notes/shardul-shah)'s five lessons from Datadog (September 22nd, 2021 — Shardul Shah on Datadog):

- **Pricing** — Be intentional (low-cost or premium). Datadog deliberately chose premium as "positive friction"; pushback on price signals you're not delivering enough value. (September 22nd, 2021 — Shardul Shah on Datadog)
- **Marketing** — Speak to the practitioner. Datadog leveraged educational content marketing (blogs, whitepapers that teach) plus events, which was inviting to the community. (September 22nd, 2021 — Shardul Shah on Datadog)
- **Market** — The TAM expanded every six months. (September 22nd, 2021 — Shardul Shah on Datadog)
- **Competition** — It's a competitive market; don't be afraid to invest in those, because competition usually means strong customer demand. (September 22nd, 2021 — Shardul Shah on Datadog)
- **Ownership** — A good decision was scooping up as much secondary as possible; be creative about building ownership in a winner. (September 22nd, 2021 — Shardul Shah on Datadog)
