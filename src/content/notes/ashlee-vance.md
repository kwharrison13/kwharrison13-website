# Ashlee Vance

**One-line:** *Technology journalist; author of the 2015 Musk biography [Elon Musk (Vance)](/books/elon-musk-tesla-spacex-and-the-quest-for-a-fantastic-future) — written with extensive cooperation from Musk and his colleagues — and host of the *Core Memory* podcast.*

## What we know

- Author of [Elon Musk (Vance)](/books/elon-musk-tesla-spacex-and-the-quest-for-a-fantastic-future) (Ashlee Vance, 2015).
- The biography is built primarily from direct interviews — many of the cited passages are from colleagues describing [Elon Musk](/notes/elon-musk) in their own words.
- Hosts the *Core Memory* podcast; interviewed [Chris Power](/notes/chris-power) of [Hadrian](/notes/hadrian) for episode 80, drawing on his own aerospace-reporting background (e.g. recalling how early SpaceX contracted turbo pumps to a family-run Colorado supplier, Barber-Nichols).
