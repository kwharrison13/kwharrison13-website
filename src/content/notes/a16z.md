# a16z

**One-line:** *Venture firm (Andreessen Horowitz) that recurs across Kyle's diligence and reading — a lead/early investor in companies he evaluated and the home of the [American Dynamism](/notes/american-dynamism) practice.*

## What we know

- Led the **$38M Series B** in [Sprig](/notes/sprig) (formerly UserLeap) in June 2021; the a16z brand mattered to customers — Codecademy noted Sprig "got onto everyone's radar" partly because it raised from a16z. (Sprig fka UserLeap - Diligence)
- An early backer of [Pave](/notes/pave): led the **$16M Series A in Aug 2020** at $75M post, and as of late 2021 had 50%+ Pave penetration across its portfolio (with a dedicated benchmarking tab to view comp across portfolio companies). (Pave Diligence — 2021)
- Publishes on future.com, where [Katherine Boyle](/notes/katherine-boyle) articulated the [American Dynamism](/notes/american-dynamism) thesis in [Building American Dynamism](/notes/building-american-dynamism) (2022); the firm runs a dedicated American Dynamism investing practice. ([Building American Dynamism](/notes/building-american-dynamism))
- [Katherine Boyle](/notes/katherine-boyle) (a16z) discussed how the firm "did a really good job of articulating our values" around differentiated missions, attracting "true believers" over down-the-fairway deals — "Turns out God ([Hallow](/notes/hallow)) and Country ([Anduril](/notes/anduril)) are great." (June 6th, 2022 — Katherine Boyle (a16z))
