# Umberto Eco

_Stub — Italian novelist, semiotician, and philosopher. Author of 'The Name of the Rose', 'How to Write a Thesis', and 'Foucault's Pendulum'. Quoted in Tietze's 'The Collector's Fallacy' on the illusion of knowledge created by photocopying texts._
