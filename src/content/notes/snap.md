# Snap

Snap Inc., the company behind Snapchat, appears in [Snapchat's Big Advantage](/notes/snapchat-s-big-advantage) — an early-Perell argument that Snap's real edge is cultural fluency rather than any single feature, sitting at the center of the shift in photography from memory-keeping to talking. Kyle annotates it with hindsight skepticism: "Have we closed the chapter on Snap vs. [Instagram](/notes/instagram)? Feels like none of those things ended up being sustainable moats."

**Context:** Snap Inc. (ticker $SNAP) is the American social-media and camera company founded by Evan Spiegel, Bobby Murphy, and Reggie Brown, best known for Snapchat and the Spectacles hardware line.

## Where this appears

- [Snapchat's Big Advantage](/notes/snapchat-s-big-advantage) — framed as the company whose advantage is cultural fluency; Kyle questions in hindsight whether its moats (effortlessness, ephemerality) were ever sustainable.
