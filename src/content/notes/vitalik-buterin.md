# Vitalik Buterin

In Kyle's research notes for It's Time To Build... But Build What - Research, Buterin appears as one voice in the "fertile conversation" of responses to [Marc Andreessen](/notes/marc-andreessen)'s Techno-Optimist Manifesto. His [X thread](https://x.com/VitalikButerin/status/1729251808936362327?s=20) is filed under the [Techno-Optimism](/notes/techno-optimism) debate as a contribution on techno-optimism and the fear of decentralization — sitting alongside responses from [Noah Smith](/notes/noah-smith), [Robin Hanson](/notes/robin-hanson), [Ezra Klein](/notes/ezra-klein), [Josh Wolfe](/notes/josh-wolfe), and others that the essay folds in.

**Context:** Vitalik Buterin is the co-founder of Ethereum, the smart-contract blockchain platform he proposed in 2013 at age nineteen. He writes prolifically on cryptography, decentralization, mechanism design, and the social philosophy of crypto, and is a prominent figure in debates over technology's role in society.
