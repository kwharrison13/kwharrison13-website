# 2 Corinthians 1

Paul's first chapter of his second epistle to the Corinthians. In Kyle's Diligence study, it is cited for verse 22 as evidence that perseverance through the rigors of life is not unaided: God "hath also sealed us, and given the earnest of the spirit in our hearts" (2 Corinthians 1:22). The verse supplies the promise of spiritual support that makes diligent, eyes-on-Celestial-Glory endurance possible.

**Context:** 2 Corinthians is a New Testament epistle written by the Apostle [Paul the Apostle](/notes/paul-the-apostle) to the church at Corinth. Chapter 1 opens the letter with Paul's reflections on comfort in affliction and God's faithfulness, including the metaphor of the Spirit given as a "down payment" (earnest) of the believer's inheritance.

## Where this appears

- Diligence — cited (1:22) for the promise that God seals believers and gives "the earnest of the spirit," strengthening perseverance through life's difficulties
