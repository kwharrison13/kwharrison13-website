# Value Added Ventures

Value Added Ventures is a Kyle-note tag in [Working Toward Zion](/books/working-toward-zion), applied beside the book's passages on cooperative and micro-lending economic models — specifically the [Grameen](/notes/grameen) / Lakota Fund micro-lending material and the [Mondragon](/notes/mondragon) cooperative complex's focus on "high value-added jobs in sophisticated, capital intensive industries." In the synthesis it sits alongside Mondragon and Grameen as a contemporary instance of the book's central argument: that the [United Order](/notes/united-order) is a genuinely distinct organizing principle for economic life, not "capitalism with charity added on." Kyle marks it twice in the highlights, tying the phrase to the value-added / job-creation thread that runs through the book's treatment of worker-owned and micro-enterprise development.

The reference is idiosyncratic to Kyle's reading — the wiki records it as his recurring framing tag for value-added, ownership-oriented enterprise models, not as a documented external organization with details in the source.
