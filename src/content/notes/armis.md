# Armis

Cybersecurity company that surfaces in Kyle's Noname Security - Diligence dossier (2021) on two threads. First, [Gili Raanan](/notes/gili-raanan) of [Sequoia](/notes/sequoia) / Cyberstarts listed Armis among his investments (alongside Transmit Security, Wiz, and Noname) when pitching Noname Security to Index's Shardul Shah — placing it inside the Cyberstarts-backed Israeli security portfolio. Second, in [Stuart McClure](/notes/stuart-mcclure)'s expert call, Armis is named as the company "Mike" (a former McAfee/[Cylance](/notes/cylance) colleague) joined after leaving Cylance; McClure had only secondhand impressions — "heard that he did good at Armis; didn't hear horrible things but also didn't hear great things either."

The dossier does not contain a product deep-dive on Armis; it appears as a portfolio comparable and a career waypoint rather than a diligence subject in its own right.

**Context:** Armis is an asset-intelligence / cyber-exposure-management company specializing in agentless visibility and security for connected devices (IoT, OT, medical, and unmanaged assets) across enterprise networks.

## Where this appears

- Noname Security - Diligence — cited as a [Gili Raanan](/notes/gili-raanan)/Cyberstarts portfolio company and as the firm a former [Cylance](/notes/cylance) executive joined.
