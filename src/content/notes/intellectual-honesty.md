# Intellectual Honesty

**One-line definition:** *The discipline that keeps [Storytelling](/notes/storytelling) from sliding into "hype" — testing whether your narrative actually represents reality or just one you want to buy into.*

## How it shows up

- The essay's landing: storytelling "is a weapon that can be wielded for good or evil," and becomes a slippery slope "if it's not coupled with significant amounts of intellectual honesty and a spirit of humility." [Charlie Munger](/notes/charlie-munger): "you're the easiest one to fool."
