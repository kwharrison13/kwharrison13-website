# Church News

Church News appears in Kyle's wiki as a cited source inside [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book) — a June 25, 2007 item attributed to it. The quoted passage records [President Monson](/notes/thomas-s-monson)'s counsel on good proselyting methods: "Work from areas of strength outward. Then you build for strength and you have the membership to help you. They are your greatest asset," along with his advice that the proper way to gain referrals is to ask members who they know who might be "mourning the loss of a loved one or who may be struggling with rearing a family." It is one entry among the many talks, scriptures, and conference quotes Kyle has gathered in that collection.

**Context:** The *Church News* is a weekly publication of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, covering church events, leaders, and member activities. It has been published since 1931.
