# ShopMonkey

ShopMonkey appears in Kyle's notes on the [Teamshares](/notes/teamshares) Series C deck (August 18th, 2021 — Teamshares Series C Deck). Kyle invokes it to push back on Teamshares' "[Stripe](/notes/stripe) for the old economy" framing: he points out that every vertical-SaaS company already wants to integrate payments, naming ShopMonkey "for car repair" and [ServiceTitan](/notes/servicetitan) "for technicians" as examples. The point is that the Stripe-for-X analogy is crowded — many vertical operating systems are racing to own the payment layer for their niche — so it's unclear what distinct claim Teamshares is making about going from company ownership and cash flows to payment volume.

**Context:** Shopmonkey is a cloud-based shop-management and operations platform for auto-repair shops, handling estimates, invoicing, scheduling, and integrated payments.
