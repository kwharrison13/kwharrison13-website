# Pragmatists

In the philosophy-of-knowledge framing from [The Notetaking Cold War](/notes/the-notetaking-cold-war), **pragmatists** treat categories not as discoveries of a fixed essence but as tools judged by their usefulness — so the "right" note system is whichever one works, not whichever mirrors the true structure of the world. The piece groups William James, [Wittgenstein](/notes/wittgenstein), and [Rorty](/notes/rorty) under this label and contrasts them with the [Essentialists](/notes/essentialists).

## Where this appears

- [The Notetaking Cold War](/notes/the-notetaking-cold-war) — the pragmatist/essentialist split is offered as the deep frame behind the Tiago Forte vs. Conor White-Sullivan debate over how to organize notes, mapping note systems onto rival theories of truth.
