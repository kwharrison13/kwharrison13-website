# Howard Schultz

In Kyle's wiki, Schultz appears as one of the named backers of [Steve Case](/notes/steve-case)'s [Rise of the Rest](/notes/rise-of-the-rest) seed fund — the $150M vehicle (co-led with J.D. Vance) that grew out of four years and six bus tours across 33 cities. He is grouped with [Jeff Bezos](/notes/jeff-bezos) and [Henry Kravis](/notes/henry-kravis) as a prominent investor lending capital and credibility to the thesis of backing startups outside the established coastal venture hubs.

**Context:** Howard Schultz (b. 1953) is the American businessman who built Starbucks into a global coffee chain, serving multiple stints as its CEO; he briefly explored a 2020 independent presidential run.

## Where this appears

- [Steve Case](/notes/steve-case) — listed among the Rise of the Rest fund's backers (with Jeff Bezos and Henry Kravis).
- [Rise of the Rest](/notes/rise-of-the-rest) — named as a backer of the $150M seed fund co-led with J.D. Vance.
