# Hotjar

Web-analytics / session-recording tool that appears in Kyle's Sprig fka UserLeap - Diligence notes as a discarded point of comparison. In that diligence, a [Codecademy](/notes/codecademy) product leader contrasted the session-replay landscape: [FullStory](/notes/fullstory) was rolled out company-wide and stuck (despite "outrageous" pricing), while Hotjar was dismissed as "just a worse version of FullStory" and ripped out of production. The mention is grounded entirely in that comparison — Hotjar functions as the "weaker incumbent" foil against FullStory's stickiness in the broader read on [Sprig](/notes/sprig)'s qualitative-feedback positioning.

**Context:** Hotjar is a Malta-based product-experience analytics tool (founded 2014) offering heatmaps, session recordings, and on-site surveys, typically aimed at smaller teams and marketers; it was acquired by Contentsquare in 2021.
