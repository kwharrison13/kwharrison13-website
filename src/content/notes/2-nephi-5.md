# 2 Nephi 5

A chapter of the Book of Mormon cited in Kyle's July 27–August 2, 2020. Alma 39–42: "The Great Plan of Happiness" study. The quoted verse, 2 Nephi 5:3, captures the elder brothers' murmuring — "Our younger brother thinks to rule over us… let us slay him" — as they reject Nephi's right to lead and seek his life, the breaking point that drives the separation of the Nephites and Lamanites.

**Context:** 2 Nephi 5 narrates Nephi's departure from his rebellious brothers after Lehi's death — the people who follow him become the Nephites, establishing a separate settlement, building a temple, and dividing from the Lamanites who remained behind.
