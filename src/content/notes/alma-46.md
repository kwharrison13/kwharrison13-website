# Alma 46

Book of Mormon chapter Kyle reads as Captain Moroni and the Title of Liberty. The verse he cites most is 46:12, where Moroni "rent his coat… and wrote upon it: In memory of our God, our religion, and freedom, and our peace, our wives, and our children" and raised it on a pole. It sits in his August 3–9, 2020. Alma 43–52: "Stand Fast in the Faith of Christ" Come Follow Me block and is quoted in full in his October 26–November 1, 2020. Mormon 1–6: "I Would That I Could Persuade All … to Repent" notes.

The verse carries its sharpest weight in his Godly Sorrow — Topical Study, where 46:12 (paired with [Mormon 2](/notes/mormon-2):23) becomes a warning: losing sight of God costs us "our religion, freedom, peace, families, lives, and even our eternal souls." A different verse, 46:8, appears in his Scripture Study — Topical Study on the weakness of men's hearts — how quickly people are "led away by one man's wickedness."

**Context:** Alma chapter 46 is in the Book of Mormon (Latter-day Saint scripture). It records Captain Moroni raising the Title of Liberty to rally the Nephites against Amalickiah's bid for power.
