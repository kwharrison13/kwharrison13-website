# Lee Scott

[Walmart](/notes/walmart) executive cited in the [Pricing](/notes/pricing) concept note for the line "our marketing strategy is our pricing strategy" — the idea that most of Walmart's marketing dollars go into *reducing prices* rather than advertising. The quote (traced to [The Everything Store](/books/the-everything-store)) appears in [Pricing](/notes/pricing) as a canonical example of pricing as the visible form of customer obsession, a thread the note runs through Walmart, [Amazon](/notes/amazon) (Bezos), and Costco: spend on price cuts, not ad spend, and let low prices do the marketing.

**Context:** H. Lee Scott Jr. (b. 1949) served as president and CEO of Walmart from 2000 to 2009, succeeding David Glass and preceding Mike Duke.
