# Nextdoor

Nextdoor enters Kyle's wiki through his notes on [The New Urban Crisis](/books/the-new-urban-crisis), where he raises it as a possible tool for one of the book's core problems: how to change the perception of a community. In a margin note responding to the book's argument about urban segregation and neighborhood reputation, Kyle asks, "How do you change the perception of a community? Can solutions like Nextdoor address that?" Nextdoor is also tagged in the book page's "Other" connections row as an example of "changing the perception of a community" — making it one of Kyle's city-building reference points rather than a company he has studied directly.

**Context:** Nextdoor is a hyperlocal social-networking platform (publicly traded, $KIND) organized around verified neighborhoods, used for local discussion, recommendations, and community notices.
