# Stewart Gollmer

Stewart Gollmer is a partner at [Tenaya Capital](/notes/tenaya-capital), the venture firm that led [Salt Security](/notes/salt-security)'s Series A. In Kyle's Salt Security Diligence — 2021 dossier (late 2021, at [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures)), Gollmer was on the diligence-outreach list as a back-channel reference on Salt Security — flagged with email as the planned contact method, since Tenaya was the company's earliest institutional backer. The cap table captured in that research runs from Tenaya's Series A through a $30M Series B from [Sequoia](/notes/sequoia) and a $70M Series C from [Alkeon](/notes/alkeon) + [Advent International](/notes/advent-international).

## Where this appears

- Salt Security Diligence — 2021 — listed as a diligence contact (Tenaya Capital, Series A investor); outreach planned via email.
- [Tenaya Capital](/notes/tenaya-capital) — named as the firm's partner / point of contact for the Salt Security back-channel.
