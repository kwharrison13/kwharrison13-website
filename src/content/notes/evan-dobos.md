# Evan Dobos

**One-line:** *Founder of [CivicLift](/notes/civiclift), a civic-engagement platform for cities and towns; previously ran a website-development shop building municipal sites.*

## What we know

- Built [CivicLift](/notes/civiclift) out of features his web-dev shop had built for cities and towns; relaunched the platform in late 2017, gaining traction in Connecticut (Farmington, Danbury, Waterbury).
- The platform aggregates events/content from any source for a town and surfaces data insights for economic-development decisions; priced at $9,500/yr to stay below the $10K bid threshold.
- Frustrated by long municipal sales cycles (e.g. 8 presentations for Danbury; re-selling after officials lose elections) and planning a pivot away from towns as the direct customer.
