# Sam Arons

Sustainability lead at [Lyft](/notes/lyft), listed in the Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 dossier as a reference-call / outreach target during Index Ventures' Series B diligence on [Watershed Climate](/notes/watershed-climate). He appears in the dossier among a set of corporate sustainability leaders Kyle's team wanted to reach for customer/expert reference checks — alongside [Kara Mangone](/notes/kara-mangone) at [Goldman Sachs](/notes/goldman-sachs), [Kate Brandt](/notes/kate-brandt) at [Google](/notes/google), and others — as a potential signal on Watershed's carbon-accounting product fit with large enterprises.

**Context:** Sam Arons has led sustainability at Lyft (director of sustainability), where his work focused on the company's carbon-neutrality commitments and electrification of its rideshare fleet; he previously worked on Google's data-center energy programs.
