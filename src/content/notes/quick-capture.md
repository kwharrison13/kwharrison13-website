# Quick Capture

**Quick Capture** is the tag Kyle used in his [Roam Research](/notes/roam-research) daily notes to mark a fast, low-friction save — a link, a tool, a watchlist item, or a pointer to revisit later. In practice it functions as an inbox marker: anything flagged #[Quick Capture](/notes/quick-capture) was jotted into that day's daily note for later triage rather than written up in the moment. The downstream wiki pages preserve the provenance — items note when they were "captured … via #[Quick Capture](/notes/quick-capture)."

Across the corpus it feeds several running lists. [Movies to Watch](/notes/movies-to-watch) is described as a "running watchlist — films and specials captured via #[Quick Capture](/notes/quick-capture) to watch later" (e.g. Cold Lasagne, captured Nov 8th, 2021). [Software TAM](/notes/software-tam) was "seeded from a #[Quick Capture](/notes/quick-capture) note" tracking references for market sizing. And individual captures sometimes graduate into their own dated entries, like the April 5th, 2022 quick-capture pointer to a personality-assessment tool.

## Where this appears

- [Movies to Watch](/notes/movies-to-watch) — the watchlist is explicitly populated by #[Quick Capture](/notes/quick-capture) saves, each annotated with its capture date.
- [Software TAM](/notes/software-tam) — a concept page seeded from a quick-capture note collecting market-sizing references.
- April 5th, 2022 — Quick Capture: Personality Assessments — a standalone meeting page split out from a single quick-capture item (a pointer to 16personalities).
- June 24th, 2021 — Quick Capture: The Rise of Superpower Tech — another dated quick-capture item promoted to its own page.
