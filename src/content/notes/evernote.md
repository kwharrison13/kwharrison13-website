# Evernote

Evernote is a note-taking and personal knowledge-management app, used in [The Notetaking Cold War](/notes/the-notetaking-cold-war) as the canonical example of the "blank page" approach: a "note" in Evernote is an undefined, infinitely flexible container — "a box you can stick anything into" — which the piece reads as the pragmatist end of the note-organization spectrum.

**Context:** Evernote is a cross-platform note-taking application launched in 2008, one of the earliest mass-market digital note apps, known for free-form notes, web clipping, and tagging.

## Where this appears

- [The Notetaking Cold War](/notes/the-notetaking-cold-war) — invoked to illustrate that a "note" is just a catch-all blank page that can hold an infinite number of different things.
