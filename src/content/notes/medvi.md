# MEDVI

**One-line:** *A [GLP-1](/notes/glp-1) [Telehealth](/notes/telehealth) company founded by [Matthew Gallagher](/notes/matthew-gallagher) (Sept 2024, from his LA house, ~$20K to launch with the AI stack) that the NYT profiled as the first "[One-Person Billion Dollar Company](/notes/one-person-billion-dollar-company)" — claiming $1.8B revenue in 14 months.*

## What we know

- Profiled by the NYT (April 2026) as an AI success story: ~$400M revenue in year one across 250K customers, claimed 16.2% net margin, built with ChatGPT/Claude/Grok/Midjourney/Runway for ~$20K. (source: The $1B Rorschach Test - Research)
- **Not, in any meaningful sense, a healthcare company** — employs no doctors, runs no pharmacy, does no clinical oversight. Every regulated function is outsourced to [CareValidate](/notes/carevalidate) (patient↔doctor/pharmacy matching) and [OpenLoop Health](/notes/openloop-health) (clinical infrastructure). It is a [marketing layer](/notes/wrapper-companies) on rented [Telehealth](/notes/telehealth) infrastructure. (source: The $1B Rorschach Test - Research)
- Operating in a grey area, if not outright illegal: 800+ fake/fabricated doctor profiles (e.g. "Dr. Tuckr Carlzyn") with stock photos; **FDA Warning Letter #721455** (Feb 2026) for false/misleading misbranding claims; AI-generated deepfake before/after photos; a Delaware class-action (Nov 2025). See [Fraud](/notes/fraud). (source: The $1B Rorschach Test - Research)
- Its clinician-network partner [OpenLoop Health](/notes/openloop-health) suffered a **data breach in Jan 2026 exposing 1.6M patient records**. (source: The $1B Rorschach Test - Research)
- No meaningful clinical guardrails — testers got approved with absurd inputs (birthdate Feb 31; 7'11"/350lb told 94% chance of hitting goal; "wanted to be 60 lbs," approved). (source: The $1B Rorschach Test - Research)
- Durability is questionable: thin/competed-away margins, high [CAC](/notes/customer-acquisition-cost) in a commoditized market, heavy churn, looming lawsuit costs — "a billion dollars of *what*?" (source: The $1B Rorschach Test - Research)
