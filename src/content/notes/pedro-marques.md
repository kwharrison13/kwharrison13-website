# Pedro Marques

A design lead at [Fast](/notes/fast) whom Kyle was introduced to on March 30th, 2022. By his own introduction, he led Fast's design-systems team and (as of that intro) had recently taken on ownership of the company's website. Before Fast he was Kyte's first design hire, where he redesigned the website and apps and built/maintained the design system. In his spare time he co-built Tangerine, a self-care/goals iOS app that was featured by Apple multiple times — a project he singled out as one he's especially proud of.
