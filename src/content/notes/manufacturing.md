# Manufacturing

Manufacturing as one of the domains where Marc Andreessen's "It's Time To Build" locates American complacency — alongside healthcare, housing, and [Education](/notes/education) — arguing the West has stopped building the physical capacity it once did. Tagged to [City Building](/notes/city-building) in Kyle's notes.

## Where this appears

- [It's Time To Build](/notes/it-s-time-to-build) — listed among the sectors (healthcare, housing, education, manufacturing) where the essay diagnoses a failure to build.
- [He Came From Oz To Save American Manufacturing — EP 80 Chris Power](/notes/he-came-from-oz-to-save-american-manufacturing-ep-80-chris-power) — [Chris Power](/notes/chris-power)'s [Hadrian](/notes/hadrian) automating the aging, family-run aerospace/defense supplier base as a reindustrialization case study.
- [Inside Micron's Attempts to Reindustrialize the Dying American Heartland](/notes/inside-micron-s-attempts-to-reindustrialize-the-dying-american-heartland) — [Micron](/notes/micron)'s $100B Syracuse memory-chip campus as a semiconductor-reindustrialization case study.
