# Manufacturing

Manufacturing as one of the domains where Marc Andreessen's "It's Time To Build" locates American complacency — alongside healthcare, housing, and [Education](/notes/education) — arguing the West has stopped building the physical capacity it once did. Tagged to [City Building](/notes/city-building) in Kyle's notes.
