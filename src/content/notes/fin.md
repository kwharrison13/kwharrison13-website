# Fin

**One-line:** *SaaS company (fin.com) that collects and analyzes employee behavior across SaaS applications to optimize the execution of human-powered processes — focused on the CX space, with a vision of machine-assistance for all knowledge work.*

## What we know

- Raising a Series B as of June 2022; $4.6M ARR at end of Q1 (up from $1.7M end of 2020), targeting $10M ARR in Q1 2023 (source: June 15th, 2022 — Fin Series B Update (CJ Longyear)).
- Customer list includes Airbnb, Coinbase, Twilio, DoorDash, Brex, Square, Uber (source: June 15th, 2022 — Fin Series B Update (CJ Longyear)).
- Evan Cummack (ex-Twilio) joined as CEO at the end of 2020; senior leaders added from Twilio, Coupa, Informatica, Lyft (source: June 15th, 2022 — Fin Series B Update (CJ Longyear)).
