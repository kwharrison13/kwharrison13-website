# Glossier

**One-line:** *DTC beauty brand founded by Emily Weiss; pursued (and later abandoned) a tech/community thesis to build a social shopping network, before pivoting to a vanilla retail strategy (wholesale, physical stores, Shopify).*

## What we know

- Emily Weiss's tech thesis: "Beauty has lagged behind other categories in terms of online sales"; ambition to build a social-network-driven shopping experience and a 3rd-party marketplace (source: June 15th, 2022 — Lawrence Xiong Rogers (Glossier)).
- Repeatedly built and then shut down mobile web/app experiences before launch; disbanded teams (including an Instagram-hired mobile team and an ex-Amazon social-network team) (source: June 15th, 2022 — Lawrence Xiong Rogers (Glossier)).
- By mid-2022 had given up tech bets, switched publicly to Shopify, and hired an ex-Nike Chief Commercial Officer to drive retail expansion (source: June 15th, 2022 — Lawrence Xiong Rogers (Glossier)).
