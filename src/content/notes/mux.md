# Mux

Mux is a video-infrastructure company Kyle tracked during his Index Ventures sourcing/diligence work. The August 18th, 2021 — Mux Notes page captures a metrics-and-market review of the company (preserved as image attachments in the original Roam log). In his January 11th, 2022 daily log it resurfaces in a video-tooling cluster as "another video one," noted alongside [Twelve Labs](/notes/twelve-labs) — placing it in Kyle's map of the developer-facing video space.

**Context:** Mux is a San Francisco-based developer platform for video, founded in 2015 by Jon Dahl and Steve Heffernan (creators of the open-source Video.js player). It provides API-driven video streaming/encoding (Mux Video) and video performance analytics (Mux Data), positioning itself as the "Stripe for video" for developers building video into their products.
