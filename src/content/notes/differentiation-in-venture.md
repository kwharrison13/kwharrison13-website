# Differentiation in Venture

**One-line definition:** *Kyle's thesis that differentiation will kill the long tail of "so-so" venture firms.*

## How sources describe it

- Founders will ask "what job am I hiring this firm for?"; firms that can't answer get stuck in the "Dead Zone." Tied to [Josh Wolfe](/notes/josh-wolfe)'s prediction that 50–75% of private-market investors disappear, and to venture becoming productized (per September 13th, 2022 — Harry Stebbings Podcast).
