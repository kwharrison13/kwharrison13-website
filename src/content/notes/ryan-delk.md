# Ryan Delk

Founder of [Primer](/notes/primer), noted in Kyle's March 30th, 2022 — Candor February Update (Kelsey Bishop) as one of the existing investors in [Candor](/notes/candor) (the company [Kelsey Bishop](/notes/kelsey-bishop) founded, which Contrary led the seed round for). In the update, Delk appears alongside other angels/funds on Candor's cap table — [Turner Novak](/notes/turner-novak) and [Alek Koenig](/notes/alek-koenig) (CEO of [Settle.co](/notes/settle-co)) — marking him as an active early-stage operator-investor in Contrary's orbit.

**Context:** Ryan Delk is the founder and CEO of Primer (an education company building microschools and a homeschool platform); he was previously an early operator at Gumroad and Omni, and is known as an angel investor in early-stage startups.
