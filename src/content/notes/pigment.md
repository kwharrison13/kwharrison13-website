# Pigment

**Pigment** is a business-planning / FP&A platform, a well-funded newer entrant in the modern planning wave alongside [Vareto](/notes/vareto), [Cube Software](/notes/cube-software), and [Mosaic.Tech](/notes/mosaic-tech). Referenced in Kyle's [Vareto](/notes/vareto) diligence as a competitor.

- **Customer read** (per the Ada Support reference call): "pretty powerful tool" — a team member who used Pigment previously said "you could do a lot more with Pigment than [Vareto](/notes/vareto)," but found its interface **less streamlined** than Vareto and wouldn't feel comfortable bringing business partners into it.
- **Financing** (per Kat Orekhova, Dec 2022): raised a ~$75M Series B in the 2021 peak on less ARR, then a down round at a ~$400M valuation.
