# Salvation

The redemption of "subjected intelligence" and its progress toward [Exaltation](/notes/exaltation) — the central object of the [Plan of Salvation](/notes/plan-of-salvation). In [Brigham Young](/notes/brigham-young)'s 1853 discourse Salvation — Brigham Young, every person alive is already laboring for "salvation" as he conceives it, but most chase a counterfeit; true salvation, "correctly and minutely understood, changes the course of mankind," and "the man or woman who lives worthily is now in a state of salvation" — a present condition, not only a future reward.
