# Gospel Principles Manual

The *Gospel Principles* manual — the LDS Church's foundational doctrinal handbook — appears in Kyle's wiki as the cited source for a quotation he collected in [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book): "To some people work is the dullest of drudgery, but to others it is an exciting part of life. One way to enjoy life's fullest benefits is to learn to love work." In that quote book it sits among scriptural and aphoristic lines on work and diligence.

This page overlaps with [Gospel Principles](/notes/gospel-principles), which records the manual's definition of covenant as a "sacred agreement or mutual promise between God and a person or a group of people." The two are the same source surfaced from different reading notes.

**Context:** *Gospel Principles* is a widely-distributed instructional manual published by The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, used as a basic doctrinal reference for converts and members; chapters cover core teachings on faith, covenants, the plan of salvation, and Christian living.
