# The 7 Second Rule

The 7 Second Rule is a focus heuristic from the [David Perell](/notes/david-perell) / [Matthew Kobach](/notes/matthew-kobach) Twitter workshop: keep your phone or Twitter more than seven seconds away while writing or reading, so the friction of reaching for it discourages distraction.
