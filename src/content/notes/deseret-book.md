# Deseret Book

Deseret Book is the LDS publishing house that runs through Kyle's gospel-study and religious-biography reading. [Sheri L. Dew](/notes/sheri-l-dew) — author of the authorized [Gordon B. Hinckley](/notes/gordon-b-hinckley) biography [Gordon B. Hinckley — Go Forward With Faith](/books/gordon-b-hinckley-go-forward-with-faith) — is its longtime publishing executive (CEO). Several titles in Kyle's library carry the Deseret Book imprint: Dew's Hinckley biography and the companion [Howard W. Hunter by Eleanor Knowles](/books/howard-w-hunter-by-eleanor-knowles) are described as sharing the same "Deseret Book hagiographic form," and [Barbara Morgan Gardner](/notes/barbara-morgan-gardner)'s *The Priesthood Power of Women* (2019) was published by Deseret Book. The house also surfaces historically in the [Leonard Arrington and the Writing of Mormon History](/books/leonard-arrington-and-the-writing-of-mormon-history) notes, where Deseret Book's board figures into the institutional reception of Arrington's Mormon-history work.

**Context:** Deseret Book Company is a Salt Lake City publisher and retailer owned by Deseret Management Corporation (a holding company of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints), publishing devotional, doctrinal, and Church-history titles.
