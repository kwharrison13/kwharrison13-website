# Michael Dempsey

Investor and writer behind the [Curiosity Phase](/notes/curiosity-phase) framing of AI company-building, affiliated with [Eads Bridge Holdings](/notes/eads-bridge-holdings). He authored [Company Building In The Curiosity Phase of AI](/notes/company-building-in-the-curiosity-phase-of-ai), the 2022 essay that names the current moment in AI as a "Curiosity Phase" — the "everyone wants to try it" phase — where effective demos lead to a barrage of AI-first products that see high short-term usage followed by material churn driven by collective industry curiosity (source: [Company Building In The Curiosity Phase of AI](/notes/company-building-in-the-curiosity-phase-of-ai)).

His central, contrarian claim is that in this phase the seemingly endless user demand is a trap: founders "must at times ignore the seemingly endless user demand and instead litigate each decision they make with a lens for building long-term defensibility and value" — product expansion, prioritization, go-to-market, and customer success all become minefields amid rising industry sentiment (source: [Company Building In The Curiosity Phase of AI](/notes/company-building-in-the-curiosity-phase-of-ai)). Kyle picks up the "Curiosity Phase" label directly in [The Enchanted Notebook](/notes/the-enchanted-notebook), placing the present moment past the Peak of Inflated Expectations and Trough of Disillusionment — exciting enough to lure people in, while still a difficult spot in which to build an AI-powered company (source: [The Enchanted Notebook](/notes/the-enchanted-notebook)). He also appears in the source roster for Kyle's [The Productization of Venture Capital](/essays/productization) research.
