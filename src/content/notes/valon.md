# Valon

A fintech company appearing in Kyle's notes through Hannah Stulberg, who ran two business lines there: payments and distributions — handling who is owed what so the company can correctly allocate the payments it receives — and who told Kyle she helped build infrastructure that processed $1B+ in payments (November 7th, 2022 — Hannah Stulberg (Valon), intro'd by [Nisha Swarup](/notes/nisha-swarup)). **Context:** Valon is a mortgage-servicing fintech that operates a software platform aiming to modernize home-loan servicing for borrowers and investors.
