# A.A. Hodge

A.A. Hodge appears in Kyle's Topical Study as the source of an epigraph on the value of knowing where knowledge lives: "He is wise who knows the source of knowledge—who knows who has written and where it is to be found." The quote opens the front-cover section of Kyle's topical journals alongside lines from [Samuel Johnson](/notes/samuel-johnson) and [Richard G. Scott](/notes/richard-g-scott), framing the journals' purpose as a curated record of where good ideas are found.

**Context:** Archibald Alexander Hodge (1823–1886) was an American Presbyterian theologian who taught at Princeton Theological Seminary, son of theologian Charles Hodge and a notable figure in the Old Princeton tradition of Reformed theology.

## Where this appears

- Topical Study — quoted on the front cover ("He is wise who knows the source of knowledge…") as one of the epigraphs framing Kyle's topical journals.
