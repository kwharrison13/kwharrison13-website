# Packy McCormick

**One-line:** *Writer behind [Not Boring](/notes/not-boring); one of the "bards of company building and investing" Kyle cites in [Open-Source Knowledge](/essays/open-source-knowledge).*

## What we know

- Author of [Not Boring](/notes/not-boring) and an investor; writes long, entertaining narratives about companies rather than traditional analysis.
- In ["Idea Legos"](https://www.notboring.co/p/idea-legos) quotes [Tim Urban](/notes/tim-urban): "Language made ideas composable... Ideas got bigger and better faster and faster" — the compounding-ideas passage Kyle uses for the "Interconnectivity" section.
- Author of [Business is the New Sports](https://www.notboring.co/p/business-is-the-new-sports), which Kyle leans on for the "people are desperate for the play-by-play" framing.
