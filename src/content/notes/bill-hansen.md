# Bill Hansen

The leader credited with steering [USA Funds](/notes/usa-funds) through its reinvention into [Strada Education Network](/notes/strada-education-network). Per Kyle's May 2018 intro call with [Andre Bennin](/notes/andre-bennin), USA Funds was a student-loan guarantor; under Hansen it wound down the guaranteeing business and rebranded as Strada, coalescing the organization around a "completion with a purpose" theme — finishing education and transitioning into the workforce. That pivot is Strada's origin story and the source of the roughly $1.4B balance sheet that funds its student-lifecycle and future-of-work investing. (The name appears as a hedged reference in the raw call notes — "Bill Hansen?" — but is treated as the figure who led the wind-down.)

**Context:** William D. "Bill" Hansen is a U.S. education-policy figure who served as Deputy Secretary of Education and later as president and CEO of USA Funds / Strada Education Network.
