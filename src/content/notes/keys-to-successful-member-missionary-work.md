# Keys to Successful Member-Missionary Work

A talk/source by [Ezra Taft Benson](/notes/ezra-taft-benson) cited in Kyle's gospel study as a touchstone on missionary work. In the June 22–28, 2020. Alma 17–22: "I Will Make an Instrument of Thee" notes it is the attribution behind Benson's line on the primacy of effort: "one of the greatest secrets of missionary work is work! If a missionary works, he will get the Spirit; if he gets the Spirit, he will teach by the Spirit; and if he teaches by the Spirit, he will touch the hearts of the people and he will be happy." It sits in that page's list of missionary-work sources alongside [Preach My Gospel](/notes/preach-my-gospel), [The Power of Everyday Missionaries](/books/the-power-of-everyday-missionaries), and related talks.
