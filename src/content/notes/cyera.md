# Cyera

Cyera is an Israeli cloud data-security company that Kyle logged in a March 30th, 2022 deal-announcements roundup (sourced from Axios Pro Rata). Per the note, Cyera announced a $60M financing round led by Sequoia Capital, with participation from Accel, Cyberstarts, and former [Palo Alto Networks ($PANW)](/notes/palo-alto-networks) CMO [René Bonvanie](/notes/ren-bonvanie), who joined the board. Kyle flagged that the company was founded only ten months earlier and came out of stealth that same day — a notable signal of how fast the cloud-security category was attracting capital in early 2022.

**Context:** Cyera is a data security posture management (DSPM) company that discovers and classifies sensitive data across cloud environments. It has since raised much larger rounds at multi-billion-dollar valuations. This general background goes beyond what Kyle's note captures.
