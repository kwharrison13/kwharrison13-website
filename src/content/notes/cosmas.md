# Cosmas

A personal tag Kyle attached to one of his marginal notes in the [Berkshire Hathaway Annual Letters](/books/berkshire-hathaway-annual-letters). It appears against a passage where Buffett notes that Shaw's carpet costs rose with oil prices ("when you walk on carpet you are, in effect, stepping on processed oil"); Kyle's note asks, "Do oil alternatives also address these types of process use cases? #Cosmas." The tag reads as a pointer to a specific company, deal, or thesis around oil-alternative / process-input substitution that Kyle is tracking elsewhere — its referent is not defined within the sourced material here.

## Where this appears

- [Berkshire Hathaway Annual Letters](/books/berkshire-hathaway-annual-letters) — tagged on Kyle's note about whether oil-alternative materials address industrial process use cases (Shaw carpet as "processed oil").
