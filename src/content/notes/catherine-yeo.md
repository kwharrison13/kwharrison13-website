# Catherine Yeo

**One-line:** *Creator-economy writer and longtime Wikipedia editor; author of "Wikipedia's Struggle with Creators," the source of Kyle's "notability is inherently subjective" quote.*

## What we know

- Wrote [Wikipedia's Struggle with Creators](https://cyeo.substack.com/p/wikipedia-struggle-creators).
- Argues notability is "inherently subjective," that [Wikipedia](/notes/wikipedia) editing is gatekept by a small (90% male) group of top editors, and that Wikipedia and the media reinforce each other.
- Has been an active creator since age 12 and researches content creators.
