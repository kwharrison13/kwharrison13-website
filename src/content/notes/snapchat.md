# Snapchat

The parent product behind Spectacles in [A Short Guide to Spectacles](/notes/a-short-guide-to-spectacles) — the hands-free camera hardware is framed as Snapchat's extension into a new form factor, a chance to invent video conventions that only such a device makes possible.

**Context:** Snapchat is the ephemeral-messaging and camera app built by Snap Inc. (founded by Evan Spiegel, Bobby Murphy, and Reggie Brown). Spectacles were Snap's camera-equipped sunglasses, an attempt to extend the app into wearable hardware.
