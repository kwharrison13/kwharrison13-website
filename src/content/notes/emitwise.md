# Emitwise

Emitwise appears in [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures)' 2021 Watershed Climate diligence as a [carbon-accounting](/notes/carbon-accounting) competitor positioned against [Watershed Climate](/notes/watershed-climate). The thesis groups it with [Persefoni](/notes/persefoni) as a tool that "help[s] with carbon accounting but [is] more focused on heavy emitters like manufacturing and ha[s] significant burn." In the risk section, Index contrasts Watershed's tech-customer base with companies "like Emitwise that have focused on manufacturing / packaging businesses with large complex footprints and deployed massive service businesses to clean data in-house but have been pretty inefficient at it" — i.e. Emitwise is cast as the heavy-emitter, services-heavy alternative to Watershed's more automated approach.

**Context:** Emitwise is a UK-based carbon-accounting and supply-chain emissions software company, founded in 2019, aimed at measuring Scope 1–3 emissions for manufacturers and other heavy-industry firms.
