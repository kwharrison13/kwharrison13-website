# Keith Teare

Teare is the founder behind [SignalRank](/notes/signalrank), the company that "productizes pro rata" by ranking investors via [Crunchbase](/notes/crunchbase) data and buying into the best seed managers' breakout portfolios at the Series B. In Kyle's notes from the June 6th, 2022 — Rob Hodgkinson (SignalRank) call, his track record is the through-line that gives SignalRank credibility: he built **Europe's first ISP** and one of its earliest unicorns, then started **RealNames** in the US, and then **created [Crunchbase](/notes/crunchbase) before selling it** — the same Crunchbase dataset SignalRank now uses to rank every investor into every unicorn by how many they backed and how early. The recurring serial-founder pattern (infrastructure → consumer naming layer → the canonical startup database) is what positions him to build a data-driven venture product on top of his own prior creation.

**Context:** Keith Teare is a British-American serial entrepreneur and investor associated with EasyNet (an early UK ISP), RealNames, TechCrunch/Archimedes Labs, and the founding of Crunchbase. He cofounded SignalRank with venture investor Rob Hodgkinson.
