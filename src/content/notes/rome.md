# Rome

Referenced once, as the seat of power held by [Marcus Aurelius](/notes/marcus-aurelius). Naval Ravikant calls *Meditations* "absolutely life-changing," describing Marcus Aurelius as "the emperor of Rome" and "probably the most powerful human being on Earth at the time he lived" — the point being that even Rome's most powerful man still struggled internally, showing that success and power don't by themselves improve one's internal state.
