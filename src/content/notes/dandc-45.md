# D&C 45

Section 45 of the Doctrine and Covenants appears in Kyle's notes chiefly as the **"advocate with the Father"** passage. In his study of Russell M. Nelson's "Christ Is Risen", he quotes D&C 45:3–5 — where Christ pleads the believer's cause, "behold the blood of thy Son which was shed... spare these my brethren" — as the fifth of five ways to develop faith: ask Heavenly Father for help, knowing Christ is your *literal* advocate.

In Miscellaneous Scriptures he cross-references D&C 45 against [D&C 46](/notes/dandc-46):12–26 and [1 Corinthians 12](/notes/1-corinthians-12) as a passage on the **gifts of God and the reasons we receive revelation**, comparing it to the Sermon on the Mount.

- Miscellaneous Scriptures — D&C 45 cross-referenced with [D&C 46](/notes/dandc-46) and [1 Corinthians 12](/notes/1-corinthians-12) on the gifts of God and reasons for revelation
