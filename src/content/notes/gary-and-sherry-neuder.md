# Gary & Sherry Neuder

An older convert couple Kyle remembers from his own church experience, cited in his Alma 23–29 study as a counterexample about generational faithfulness. Reflecting on Alma's converts who "never did fall away," Kyle recalls families of active older converts — the Zook's, Prosperi's, Cook's — whose children largely fell inactive, and then the Neuders: also active, also converts, but whose children all stayed active. When Kyle once asked them how (while helping a sister move), they answered simply, "they just have to learn to be obedient." Kyle tagged the exchange #[Righteous in the Dark](/notes/righteous-in-the-dark), treating their plainspoken answer as a small data point on what makes faith stick across a generation.
