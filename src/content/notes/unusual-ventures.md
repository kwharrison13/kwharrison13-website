# Unusual Ventures

The seed-stage venture firm founded by [John Vrionis](/notes/john-vrionis). In Kyle's wiki it surfaces through Vrionis himself — the source of a widely-shared LinkedIn cartoon mapping the different "types" of VC, which Kyle cites in [What Is An Investor](/essays/what-is-an-investor) when bucketing investor archetypes. The firm is central to Vrionis's identity as a practitioner whose tongue-in-cheek taxonomy anchors that essay's discussion of what an investor actually is.

**Context:** Unusual Ventures is an early-stage venture firm co-founded by John Vrionis (previously a partner at Lightspeed Venture Partners), positioned around hands-on seed-stage support for founders.
