# Offline Communities

In-person community and friendship of the kind the church and the neighborhood once provided — which [Equinox Is More Than A Gym. It's A Church](/notes/equinox-is-more-than-a-gym-it-s-a-church) argues wellness brands now supply. As the world secularizes, younger generations seek the tribal, luxurious-but-not-intimidating, fun community that a gym-as-church can deliver.
