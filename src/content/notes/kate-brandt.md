# Kate Brandt

Sustainability contact at [Google](/notes/google) whom Kyle emailed as a reference/customer-diligence outreach target during the Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 work — the evaluation of Watershed's Series B (Index Ventures, 2021). In the diligence notes she appears on Kyle's "Emailed" list of corporate sustainability leaders contacted to validate demand for Watershed's carbon-measurement platform (alongside [Teesha Chowdhury](/notes/teesha-chowdhury), also at Google).

**Context:** Kate Brandt is widely known as Google's Chief Sustainability Officer, a role she held after serving as the U.S. government's first Federal Chief Sustainability Officer.
