# Meb Faber

Investor cited in [Naked Brands- The Future of Finance](/notes/naked-brands-the-future-of-finance) as an example of building a financial firm through content and conviction rather than asset-gathering — "Content is sales. We put out a bunch of the content and people find us," and the distinction between firms that "put their own money in" products they believe in versus firms that capitalize on the theme of the day.

**Context:** Meb Faber is a quantitative investor, author, and podcaster; co-founder and CIO of Cambria Investment Management. He is known for *The Ivy Portfolio*, his "Quantitative Approach to Tactical Asset Allocation" research, and *The Meb Faber Show* podcast — a prominent example of content-led finance.
