# Ro

A direct-to-consumer healthcare company that appears in Kyle's notes as a marquee customer reference for [Pave](/notes/pave). In the Pave Diligence — 2021 dossier, Ro is one of the customers Pave cites as loving the product (alongside Flipkart and Addepar), and it anchors a published Pave case study: a Ro compensation leader praises Pave's ability to "customize their product to those complexities" of how Ro runs compensation. Ro used Pave's tools to build a Personalized Total Rewards Visualization for candidates joining through acquisition, helping create excitement about equity options. The diligence notes Ro's hypergrowth context — hiring 20+ people a month plus adding employees through acquisitions, which made explaining equity and total-rewards packages increasingly important — and that Ro runs [Carta](/notes/carta) for equity and [Zenefits](/notes/zenefits) as its HRIS, both of which integrate with Pave. In the November 2nd, 2021 — Armand Farrokh (Pave) call, Ro is named (next to [Allbirds](/notes/allbirds)) as a great example of a customer.

**Context:** Ro (Roman Health Ventures) is a U.S. direct-to-consumer digital health company offering telehealth and pharmacy services across men's health, weight management, and other conditions.
