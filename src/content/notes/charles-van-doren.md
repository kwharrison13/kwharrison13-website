# Charles Van Doren

Charles Van Doren is the co-author, with [Mortimer Adler](/notes/mortimer-adler), of *[How To Read a Book](/books/how-to-read-a-book)* — the classic Kyle read with a constant religious-text overlay and explicit cross-reference to his [Learning, No Greater Responsibility](/notes/learning-no-greater-responsibility) essay-in-progress. In Kyle's notes the two authors are treated as a unit ("Adler and Van Doren's classic"), the source of the book's framework of reading levels (elementary, inspectional, analytical, syntopical) and its argument for active reading.

**Context:** Charles Van Doren (1926–2019) was an American writer and editor who joined Mortimer Adler at the Institute for Philosophical Research and co-authored the 1972 revised edition of *How to Read a Book*. He is also widely remembered for the 1950s *Twenty-One* television quiz-show scandal.
