# Airpods

Apple's wireless earbuds, framed in Kyle's wiki not as a mere accessory but as the groundwork for a voice-first, [augmented reality](/notes/augmented-reality) future. In [What You Don't Know About The Headphone Jack](/notes/what-you-don-t-know-about-the-headphone-jack), the argument is that by killing the headphone jack Apple was laying the foundation for an always-on [virtual assistant](/notes/virtual-assistant) and a world where "the line between the digital and analog" blurs.

**Context:** AirPods are Apple's wireless Bluetooth earbuds, first released in 2016 alongside the iPhone 7, the model that removed the 3.5mm headphone jack.

## Where this appears

- [What You Don't Know About The Headphone Jack](/notes/what-you-don-t-know-about-the-headphone-jack) — AirPods cast as the on-ramp to a voice-first, AR future and the design philosophy (via [Jony Ive](/notes/jony-ive)) behind killing the jack.
