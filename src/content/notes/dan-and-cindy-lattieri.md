# Dan & Cindy Lattieri

Dan and Cindy Lattieri are people Kyle encountered, most likely on his mission — recurring touchstones in his gospel study for an experience of **powerful, witnessed faith**. In his Alma 13–16 study he pairs them with the account of Alma healing Zeezrom of his fever (Alma 15:3–12), framing the couple alongside a scriptural healing-by-faith narrative.

In his Alma 17–22 study, Kyle returns to them while reflecting on "innocent faith" — people who "will give anything for answers to what they feel," believing because they sense the godliness coming from the source. He writes that the conversions of Lamoni and his household remind him of the Lattieris, "especially Cindy, after they saw us bless Dan" — indicating he was part of giving Dan a priesthood blessing, and that the experience marked Cindy's faith.

## Where this appears

- June 15–21, 2020. Alma 13–16: "Enter into the Rest of the Lord" — the Lattieris noted alongside Alma's faith-healing of Zeezrom (Alma 15)
- June 22–28, 2020. Alma 17–22: "I Will Make an Instrument of Thee" — Cindy's faith after witnessing a blessing given to Dan, compared to the conversions in Alma 17–22
