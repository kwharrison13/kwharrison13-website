# Alma 1

The opening chapter of the Book of Alma, where Kyle's notes locate **Nehor** — one of the three Book of Mormon anti-Christs. In Anti-Christ — Topical Study, Alma 1 is the case study for Nehor, who taught priestcraft and universal salvation and was executed for murdering Gideon. Like the other anti-Christs (Sherem and Korihor), Nehor attacks the [Atonement](/notes/atonement); the study pairs his story with the principle that the devil will not support his children (Alma 1:15). Alma 1 also surfaces as a cross-reference in Kyle's broader study of Alma's ministry (see Alma 13–16 — Enter into the Rest of the Lord).

**Context:** Alma 1 (in the Book of Mormon) opens the reign of the judges among the Nephites and recounts Nehor introducing priestcraft, slaying the church teacher Gideon, and being put to death — establishing the chapter as the textbook example of an anti-Christ figure for Latter-day Saint readers.
