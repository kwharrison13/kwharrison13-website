# VC Twitter

The observation, in [How to Crush It on Twitter — David Perell and Matthew Kobach Workshop](/notes/how-to-crush-it-on-twitter-david-perell-and-matthew-kobach-workshop), that VCs "crush it on Twitter because the job rewards novelty" — their work is to have ideas others don't, so they spend a lot of time thinking about novelty, which is what makes people react positively. The workshop's advice: "be a contrarian without going too far," echoing [Tyler Cowen](/notes/tyler-cowen)'s essay on why VC Twitter is so peculiar.
