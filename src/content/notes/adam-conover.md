# Adam Conover

Adam Conover surfaces in Kyle's research for The Renaissance of Rise and Grind - Research as the foil for the essay's "A Defense Nobody Asked For" section. A viral TikTok of Conover's becomes the prompt: it dismisses [Elon Musk](/notes/elon-musk)'s record by reducing each company to a one-line insult — PayPal as "a payment system that sucks," Tesla as a company where Musk "kicked out the founder," and SpaceX as "milking the government." Kyle files Conover's clip under a "[Techlash](/notes/techlash) Rising" heading and pushes back, noting he's "not an Elon Musk fanboy" but is "gobsmacked" by the flat "Elon Musk is a moron" framing, countering it with the Gwynne Shotwell SpaceX TED talk as evidence of substance the dismissal ignores.

**Context:** Adam Conover is an American comedian, writer, and TV host best known for the truTV series *Adam Ruins Everything* and as a prominent figure in the Writers Guild of America. He is active on TikTok and YouTube with explainer-style commentary, often skeptical of tech-industry figures and "great founder" narratives.
