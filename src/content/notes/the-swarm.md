# The Swarm

The Swarm is a [Contrary Capital](/notes/contrary) portfolio company run by founder [David Connors](/notes/david-connors), tracked across Kyle's 2022 portfolio-update reading. It started as a **network recruiting** product: by the March 2022 update its waitlist had grown to 275+ startups, with 65+ companies having raised $1M+ in funding (the stated target market), and it had hired [Michal Bil](/notes/michal-bil) as CTO. By the September 2022 update an MVP had launched to a "friends of the Swarm" cohort for bug-fixing and UI iteration.

The throughline in the updates is Connors teasing a broader ambition: a "Network Relationship Management" (NRM) category that extends beyond network recruiting into go-to-market, investing, and more — positioning The Swarm to lead a new product category rather than stay a recruiting tool. By the October 2022 update (read in the November 7th, 2022 — Cleared Email digest), the messaging had turned macro and cautious: citing the Fed, Goldman Sachs, Bloomberg, and JP Morgan, Connors warned a US recession would land by mid-2023 and likely persist 2+ years.
