# Lumina Foundation

The Lumina Foundation surfaces in Kyle's notes through [The Expertise Economy](/books/the-expertise-economy), where its Connecting Credentials rubric is cited as a way to set standards and measure mastery: "a popular eight-level rubric that is applicable to any skill and serves as a useful way to set standards and measure mastery." It appears in the book's discussion of how organizations can benchmark skill development — a reference for measuring expertise, not a company Kyle worked with directly.

**Context:** Lumina Foundation is an Indianapolis-based independent foundation focused on increasing the share of Americans with postsecondary credentials; its "Connecting Credentials" framework was an effort to standardize how degrees, certificates, and skills map onto a common levels-of-mastery scale.
