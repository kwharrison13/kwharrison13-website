# Mark Cuban

**One-line:** *Entrepreneur and investor who argues AI/ML will drive massive near-term labor disruption, with a two-part fix that is neither UBI nor laissez-faire.*

## What we know

- Believes in the "earthquake power" of AI and machine learning and that massive labor disruptions are coming sooner than later. (source: Unions, Unions Everywhere - Research)
- His proposed solution is twofold, and notably **not** universal basic income: (1) many low-skilled jobs can be replaced by rising demand for **data tagging and labeling** — "the 21st century version of an assembly line"; (2) a broad expansion of programs like AmeriCorps, reallocating the macroeconomic gains from automation into classrooms and domestic infrastructure.
