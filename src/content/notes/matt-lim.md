# Matt Lim

Co-founder of [FormFn](/notes/formfn), the company brought to a December 2021 seed investment committee. Per the pre-brief, Lim is a full-stack engineer (Caltech, Facebook, Oculus) and a Solana micro-influencer (Twitter @pencilflip). He co-founded FormFn with designer [Katherine Liu](/notes/katherine-liu); the two had known each other for seven years (they met at a summer internship) and had collaborated for three years across several projects. They were referenced as competent, mission-driven, focused, and very motivated, with complementary skillsets — Lim engineering, Liu design.

Before FormFn, Lim and Liu built a string of Solana/NFT projects: **Mycoverse** (3,333 mushroom-themed Solana NFTs), **Nifty Pixels** (a pixel-art NFT marketplace), **Nifty Generator** (a no-code NFT collection generator for Solana/Ethereum), and **Minymon** (website "pets"). The open question flagged in the IC was whether the team of two had the "killer instinct" and team-building ability the firm looks for.
