# Coinbase

Coinbase enters Kyle's notes not as a company he diligenced but through its co-founder, Fred Ehrsam, who recurs as a marquee name on operator-founder cap tables. In Kyle's June 2022 review of [Maximus](/notes/maximus)'s AngelList docs, Ehrsam ("a co-founder of Coinbase") appears among the [Super Angels](/notes/super-angels) backing the men's-health startup, alongside [Dave Asprey](/notes/dave-asprey) (Bulletproof), Brian Lee (Honest Company), Brian Norgard (Tinder), Jeff Weiser (ex-[Shopify](/notes/shopify) CMO), and [Ryan Holiday](/notes/ryan-holiday) (Daily Stoic). In that context Coinbase functions as shorthand for crypto-founder credibility on a [Super Angels](/notes/super-angels)-stacked cap table rather than as a diligence subject in its own right.

**Context:** Coinbase ($COIN) is a US-based cryptocurrency exchange founded in 2012 by Brian Armstrong and Fred Ehrsam; it went public via direct listing in April 2021 and is one of the largest regulated crypto platforms.
