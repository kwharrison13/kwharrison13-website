# D&C 104

Section 104 of the [Doctrine and Covenants](/notes/doctrine-and-covenants) recurs in Kyle's gospel study around [Consecration](/notes/consecration) and [Zion](/notes/zion). In 8 Points of the Gospel he cites D&C 104:68–70 under the heading on consecration as "the yielding up of oneself to God." In The Process of Zion he places D&C 104:59 alongside [D&C 101](/notes/dandc-101):46–51 and [D&C 105](/notes/dandc-105):5 as scriptures on how temples teach us to live in Zion — "We are preparing to live in Zion." It also appears (D&C 104:14–18) in Fasting - September 5, 2021.

**Context:** Doctrine and Covenants Section 104 (April 1834) is a revelation concerning the United Firm and the law of consecration and stewardship — the management of the Saints' temporal storehouse and properties — including the principle that "the earth is full, and there is enough and to spare" (D&C 104:17).

## Where this appears

- 8 Points of the Gospel — D&C 104:68–70 cited on full consecration / the yielding up of oneself to God
- The Process of Zion — D&C 104:59 grouped with temple scriptures on preparing to live in Zion
- Fasting - September 5, 2021 — D&C 104:14–18 referenced in fasting study notes
