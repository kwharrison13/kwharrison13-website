# Alma 6

A chapter in the Book of Mormon that Kyle cites across two distinct gospel-study threads. In his study of Fasting (Fasting - September 5, 2021), Alma 6:6 supports the practice of gathering to fast as a community — "the children of God were commanded that they should gather themselves together oft, and join in fasting and mighty prayer in behalf of the welfare of the souls of those who knew not God" — paired with [Moroni 6](/notes/moroni-6):5 as a model for attending fast-and-testimony meeting as a family.

In his Abrahamic Covenant notes, Alma 6:12 appears in a chain of references (alongside [1 Nephi 5](/notes/1-nephi-5):14 and [1 Nephi 6](/notes/1-nephi-6):2) supporting the point that [Joseph Smith](/notes/joseph-smith) was of the lineage of Ephraim and held the "stick of Joseph" when he held the plates.

## Where this appears

- Fasting - September 5, 2021 — Alma 6:6 grounds communal fasting and gathering "oft" to fast and pray
- Abrahamic Covenant — Alma 6:12 cited in the lineage-of-Ephraim / stick-of-Joseph argument
