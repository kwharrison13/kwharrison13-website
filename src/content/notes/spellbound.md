# Spellbound

**One-line:** *No-code platform for embedding interactive user experiences (forms, surveys, bookings, signups, transactions) directly inside emails to drive conversions; founded by [Akshaya Dinesh](/notes/akshaya-dinesh).*

## What we know

- Lets companies create interactive in-email experiences without writing code, driving actions where users already are. (source: December 14th, 2021 daily-note)
- Raised a pre-seed co-led by Shahed at Hyper; signed large brands to a private beta via a design-partner program and was considering a seed. (source: December 14th, 2021 daily-note)
- Backed by founders/CEOs of Github, Notion, Loom, Kayak, Sendgrid; design partners included Product Hunt, Mailgun, DoNotPay, and Pave. (source: December 14th, 2021 daily-note)
