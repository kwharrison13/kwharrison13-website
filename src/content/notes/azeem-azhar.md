# Azeem Azhar

Writer and podcast host behind **[Exponential View](/notes/azeem-azhar)**, recurring across Kyle's media-and-technology notes. In The State of Startup Media - Research, Exponential View appears in Kyle's 2017 "startup-media inventory" — the snapshot of newsletters/podcasts he was following before the post-COVID explosion — alongside [Stratechery](/notes/stratechery) ([Ben Thompson](/notes/ben-thompson)), [Gates Notes](/notes/bill-gates) ([Bill Gates](/notes/bill-gates)), and [Feld Notes](/notes/brad-feld) ([Brad Feld](/notes/brad-feld)).

His work threads through Kyle's [Future of Work](/notes/future-of-work) notes via an Exponential View conversation with **Jeff Sachs on AI, automation, and the economy**, and through climate-tech diligence: in Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021, Azhar hosts the HBR / Exponential View podcast episode "How Data Helps Companies Honor Their Climate Goals" with [Christian Anderson](/notes/christian-anderson).

**Context:** Azeem Azhar is a London-based technologist, investor, and writer; *Exponential View* is his widely-read newsletter and podcast on exponential technologies, and he authored *The Exponential Age* (2021). The podcast has run in partnership with Harvard Business Review.
