# Eric Tarczynski

Eric Tarczynski is the founder of [Contrary](/notes/contrary), the venture firm where Kyle became a Partner, and a recurring counterpart across Kyle's notes from 2020 through 2022. The Contrary model he built — described in Kyle's notes as a "people-centric community" first and a fund second — assembled ~100 future founders before raising a fund, growing to >$100M AUM and >400 active members, with a product relevant to community members across their whole careers (first checks for founders, plus involvement in companies like [Ramp](/notes/ramp) where members congregate).

Kyle's earliest reference is the March 5th, 2020 — Eric Tarczynski (Contrary Capital) call, an early conversation during Kyle's [Teamshares](/notes/teamshares) diligence that touched on SaaS roll-ups ([Turn River Capital](/notes/turn-river-capital)) and the stay-the-course-vs-jump tradeoff. He resurfaces across the firm's evolution: the June 2021 brainstorm with [Gokul Rajaram](/notes/gokul-rajaram) and [Saanya Ojha](/notes/saanya-ojha) on mapping a sourcing "universe" off angel portfolios and pre-empting rounds, and the April 2022 call with [Frederik Gieschen](/notes/frederik-gieschen) working through the strategy for [Contrary Research](/essays/contrary-research) — the Research Fellow role, T-shaped coverage, differentiation, and how to measure success. Related notes also tie him to [Contrary 2.0](/notes/contrary-2-0) and Contrary Growth.

**Context:** Eric Tarczynski is the founder and managing partner of Contrary, a venture capital firm that started as a community of student and early-career technologists (originally "Contrary Capital") before scaling into a multi-stage fund and publishing arm (Contrary Research).
