# Deviance

**One-line definition:** *The willingness to push back against the norms society has put in place — "a willingness to disregard likability."*

## How sources describe it

- [Visakan Veerasamy](/notes/visakan-veerasamy) (the [2023 talk](https://youtu.be/f_YUAQu-t0Q?t=795)): so much of human creativity has been spent suppressing human creativity, which is why we lack 1,000X the greatness we could have. Deviance is the reclaiming of that suppressed creativity.
- "Once you have succeeded at some kind of deviant shit your ontology is permanently corrupted." Archetype: [Elon Musk](/notes/elon-musk)'s 2007 trip to Russia to buy missiles despite everyone telling him it was a stupid idea.
- Kyle's refinement: deviance for its own sake is hollow; the purest, world-changing form is deviance *in pursuit of conviction* — i.e., [Conviction-Led Contrarianism](/essays/conviction-led-contrarianism).
