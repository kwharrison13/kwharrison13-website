# Tad R. Callister

Tad R. Callister appears in Kyle's Humility — Topical Study as the source of a [C.S. Lewis](/notes/c-s-lewis) passage on perfection and surrender to God — the lines in which the Savior says "I will never rest nor let you rest until you are literally perfect," and that the moment we put ourselves in His hand, "absolute perfection" is what we are in for. Callister is cited as the one who quotes Lewis, anchoring the study's argument that humility means yielding our hearts to God rather than qualifying ourselves through our own strength.

**Context:** Tad R. Callister is an American author and religious leader in The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, who served in the Presidency of the Seventy and as Sunday School General President; he is known for doctrinal books such as *The Infinite Atonement*.
