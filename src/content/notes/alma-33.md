# Alma 33

A chapter in the Book of Mormon (book of Alma), falling within the [Come, Follow Me](/notes/come-follow-me) reading week July 13–19, 2020. Alma 32–35: "Plant This Word in Your Hearts". It appears in that page only as part of the chapter list ([Alma 32](/notes/alma-32) · Alma 33 · [Alma 34](/notes/alma-34) · [Alma 35](/notes/alma-35)); Kyle's study notes for the week center entirely on [Alma 32](/notes/alma-32), and the source explicitly notes that Alma 33 has no notes attached.

**Context:** Alma chapter 33 continues Alma's sermon to the poor among the Zoramites, drawing on the prophets Zenos and Zenock to teach about prayer, worship in all circumstances, and faith in the coming of the Son of God.
