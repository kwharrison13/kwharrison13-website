# Boostup.ai

**One-line:** *Revenue-operations / sales-forecasting platform (founded 2019) positioning against [Clari](/notes/clari); led by [Sharad Verma](/notes/sharad-verma).*

- Self-described "fastest growing revenue operations company"; claims to beat [Clari](/notes/clari) in 3 of 4 deals and 6 of 10 G2 fields, shipping 3-4x faster, as a product-led deep-tech team vs. Clari's sales-led org. (source: December 14th, 2021 daily-note)
- Used ~20 min/user/day across reps, managers, SEs, and even some CFOs (e.g. Iterable); won a $500K deal at Teradata and $250K at Cloudflare. (source: December 14th, 2021 daily-note)
- GTM is top-down (CRO + Sales Ops), ~50% self-serve; sees a future [Product Led Growth (PLG)](/notes/product-led-growth-plg) motion for Ops. (source: December 14th, 2021 daily-note)
- Traction: ~130% net retention, 300% ARR growth, $1M → $4M in 2021, plan to $12M then $36M; insiders wrote a $6M check, targeting a ~$30M Series B. (source: December 14th, 2021 daily-note)
