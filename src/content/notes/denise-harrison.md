# Denise Harrison

Appears once in [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book), the verbatim LDS quotation collection Kyle's brother Chad gave him. She is cited as the interpreter of a [Dieter F. Uchtdorf](/notes/dieter-f-uchtdorf) "Forget Me Not" message — the entry recording his counsel to be patient with oneself, to distinguish a good sacrifice from a foolish one, to be happy now and find joy in the journey, to remember the "why" of the gospel, and that the Lord loves you. The attribution reads "[Dieter F. Uchtdorf](/notes/dieter-f-uchtdorf) (as interpreted by Denise Harrison)," indicating she relayed or summarized the talk rather than authored the quote.

Beyond this single relayed quotation, the sources give no further detail on her identity; given the shared surname she is plausibly a Harrison family member, but the notes do not confirm this.
