# Jonathon Davidson

Jonathon Davidson appears in Kyle's [gospel-study](/notes/gospel-study) of Alma 30–31 as a companion from Kyle's mission. Studying Alma 30:8 ("Choose ye this day, whom ye will serve"), Kyle recalls that Davidson had committed him to reading that verse in companionship study, which prompted the questions "Who am I following? Who am I serving?" — a line of self-examination that led Kyle to identify [Anger](/notes/anger), [Pride](/notes/pride), [Envy](/notes/envy), and lust as the things holding him back, and to study his vices to understand the root of sin.
