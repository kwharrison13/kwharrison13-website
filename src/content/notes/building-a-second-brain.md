# Building a Second Brain

**Why it's on the list:** *The popularized "second brain" methodology — a system for capturing and organizing notes so that content can be produced modularly by recombining existing ideas, the way [David Perell](/notes/david-perell) runs a "digital model of his brain."*

**Where I saw it:** Referenced in [Hyper Publishing](/notes/hyper-publishing) as the note-storage system that makes modular publishing possible ("Content is like software: expensive to produce, cheap to reproduce"), and in [Not So Lazy & Entitled Millennials- David Perell](/notes/not-so-lazy-and-entitled-millennials-david-perell), where Perell describes saving all knowledge so he "never has to remember anything."
