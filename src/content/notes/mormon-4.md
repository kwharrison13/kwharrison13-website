# Mormon 4

A Book of Mormon chapter in the closing war narrative, studied in Kyle's Come Follow Me notes on Mormon 1–6. Its central takeaway: *"by the wicked the wicked are punished"* — the fallen world hands power to those who move away from God, so that the wicked become the instruments of judgment on the wicked (4:5). The chapter continues the pride theme of [Mormon 3](/notes/mormon-3): when the Nephites repulse the Lamanites, "they did again boast of their own strength" and "went forth in their own might" (4:8), repeating the cycle that dooms them.

## Where this appears

- Come Follow Me — Mormon 1–6 — "by the wicked the wicked are punished" (4:5); the Nephites again boasting in their own strength after victory (4:8).
