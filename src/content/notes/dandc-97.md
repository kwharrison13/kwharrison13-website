# D&C 97

A section of the Doctrine and Covenants that Kyle cites in his Scripture Study — Topical Study, grouped with D&C 18:35–36 and D&C 88:66 as references for recognizing the voice of the Spirit. In that study it sits within a sequence on discernment — exercising righteousness, practicing virtue and holiness, and learning that "the voice of the Lord is a 'still small voice'" (D&C 85:6), illustrated by [Boyd K. Packer](/notes/boyd-k-packer)'s teaching that the Spirit whispers and caresses gently rather than shouting.

**Context:** Doctrine and Covenants Section 97 is an 1833 revelation given through Joseph Smith at Kirtland, Ohio, concerning the Saints in Missouri and the building of a temple in Zion. Verse 1 expresses the Lord's regard for those who are seeking to learn and keep his commandments.
