# Jonathan F. Dippold

Attributed source of a quote in [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book), Kyle's verbatim collection of gospel and motivational quotations. On page 45 of the collection — amid passages from [Gordon B. Hinckley](/notes/gordon-b-hinckley) and [Thomas C. Frederick](/notes/thomas-c-frederick) on commitment and devotion to missionary work — Dippold is quoted with a starkly martial framing of the missionary cause: "We're not messing around here. This isn't a game, it's a war, as real as any war that we see going on right now. You have met the people who have died, and it is up to you to bring about their salvation." The line sits among the collection's exhortations on the urgency and stakes of preaching the gospel.
