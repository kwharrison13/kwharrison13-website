# John 5

A chapter of the Gospel of John in the New Testament. In Kyle's Come Follow Me study for December 31–January 6, 2019. "We Are Responsible for Our Own Learning", John 5:39 is a keystone verse — "Search the scriptures; for in them ye think ye have eternal life: and they are they which testify of me." It appears in the "nurturing your own testimony" thread (ask, seek, knock; search the scriptures; act in faith), grouped with [Luke 11](/notes/luke-11):9-13, [Acts 17](/notes/acts-17):10-12, [Alma 5](/notes/alma-5):45-46, and [Alma 32](/notes/alma-32):27.

**Context:** John 5 recounts Christ healing the man at the pool of Bethesda and His discourse on bearing witness; verse 39's charge to "search the scriptures" is a frequently cited proof-text for personal scripture study.
