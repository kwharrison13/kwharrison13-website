# Cabin

Cabin appears in Kyle's notes as a client of [Matt Curwood](/notes/matt-curwood)'s mobility consultancy. Curwood describes it as an overnight LA–SF bus service, and his engagement focused on fixing its maintenance and operations. It sits alongside [Bird](/notes/bird) and Motor Coach Industries in his roster of ground-transportation clients, reflecting his operating background running shuttle and coach companies.

**Context:** Cabin was a San Francisco–based startup that ran an overnight "moving hotel" bus between LA and the Bay Area — passengers boarded sleeping pods at night and woke up in the destination city. It shut down its overnight service in 2019.
