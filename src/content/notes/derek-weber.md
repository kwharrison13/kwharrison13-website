# Derek Weber

**One-line:** *Operator behind [SaxonWeber](/notes/saxonweber) / [ShareWell](/notes/sharewell), a private-markets secondaries trading and brokerage platform.*

## What we know

- Runs [SaxonWeber](/notes/saxonweber), whose core business [ShareWell](/notes/sharewell) focuses on secondary markets around trading data plus trading/brokerage; serves a retail seller group and institutional investors, mostly skipping individual/angel investors (source: June 20th, 2023 — Derek Weber (SaxonWeber)).
- Launched their own platform ~90 days before the call, with ~1K platform users and a broader base of 24K individual and 15K institutional users; positions on trading rather than data leadership (vs. Caplight) (source: June 20th, 2023 — Derek Weber (SaxonWeber)).
- Discussed a potential content/data partnership embedding [Contrary Research](/notes/contrary) under his brand, and offered Kyle "carte blanche" on the data since they don't monetize it (source: June 20th, 2023 — Derek Weber (SaxonWeber)).
