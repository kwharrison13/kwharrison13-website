# 1 Nephi 13

A Book of Mormon chapter cited in Kyle's Come Follow Me study of November 2–8, 2020. Mormon 7–9: "I Speak unto You As If Ye Were Present". Verses 38–41 are used as evidence of God's long preparation to bring forth the Book of Mormon — Nephi foreseeing the book "which had proceeded forth from the mouth of the Jew" coming to the Gentiles. The note groups it with [2 Nephi 3](/notes/2-nephi-3):11–12 (the seer raised up to convince men of the word) and [D&C 33](/notes/dandc-33):16 / [D&C 42](/notes/dandc-42):12–13 (scriptures "given of me for your instruction" containing "the fulness of the gospel"), under the theme that the Book of Mormon is a "Pearl of Great Price."

**Context:** 1 Nephi 13 contains Nephi's vision of the formation of the great and abominable church, the discovery of the Americas, and the coming forth of the Bible and Book of Mormon to the Gentiles in the latter days.
