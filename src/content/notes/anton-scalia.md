# Anton Scalia

Cited in Kyle's General Conference- October 2021 notes — in a thread about disagreement within the church and the value of being able to "successfully disagree and still respect each other" — as one of a pair of "great contenders" alongside [Ruth Bader Ginsburg](/notes/ruth-bader-ginsburg). The Scalia–Ginsburg friendship is invoked as a model for working through serious ideological disagreement while preserving mutual respect.

**Context:** Antonin Scalia (1936–2016) was a U.S. Supreme Court Justice (1986–2016) and a leading conservative originalist. His close personal friendship with the liberal Justice Ruth Bader Ginsburg — despite sharp jurisprudential disagreement — is a frequently cited example of civil discourse across ideological lines. (The wiki page title spells the first name "Anton"; the standard form is "Antonin.")
