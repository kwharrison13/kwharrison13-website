# L.M. Sacasas

_Stub page — not yet written. Writer behind the newsletter **The Convivial Society**, focused on technology, culture, and the human condition. His essay ["Resistance Is Futile: The Myth of Tech Inevitability"](https://theconvivialsociety.substack.com/p/resistance-is-futile-the-myth-of) — on the "rhetoric of technological determinism" and how narratives of inevitability "habituate us to the present" — supplies the closing argument in [The Value Chain of Capital](/essays/the-value-chain-of-capital) that progress is not inevitable._
