# Personal Websites

A personal website as the mechanism that makes you findable and lets evergreen content compound. In [How to Maximize Serendipity](/notes/how-to-maximize-serendipity), maintaining a website lets people find your bio, share your work, and describe you favorably. In [Hyper Publishing- The Wiki Strategy](/notes/hyper-publishing-the-wiki-strategy), past critical mass a website becomes an "expansive ecosystem of articles and ideas" where repeat visitors have higher lifetime value and evangelize the brand.
