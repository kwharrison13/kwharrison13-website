# Microeconomics

Microeconomics appears in [Naval Ravikant — The Angel Philosopher](/notes/naval-ravikant-the-angel-philosopher) as one of the disciplines [Naval Ravikant](/notes/naval-ravikant) holds to be fundamental: "you can't succeed in business or modern capitalist society without a strong grasp of supply and demand, labor vs. capital, and game theory." He sets it against [Macroeconomics](/notes/macroeconomics), which he dismisses as junk science.

**Context:** Microeconomics is the branch of economics that studies the behavior of individual agents — households, firms, markets — and how supply, demand, and incentives set prices and allocate resources.

## Where this appears

- [Naval Ravikant — The Angel Philosopher](/notes/naval-ravikant-the-angel-philosopher) — held up (with [Game Theory](/notes/game-theory)) as fundamental to business and capitalism, the counterweight to "junk" [Macroeconomics](/notes/macroeconomics).
