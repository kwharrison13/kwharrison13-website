# Buildots

Israel-based vertical-SaaS company for construction, surfaced for Kyle by [Molly Alter](/notes/molly-alter) in the November 8th, 2021 — Buildots (from Molly Alter) sourcing note. Buildots takes data from construction sites — including cameras mounted on workers' helmets — to build a constantly-updating digital twin of a site and improve on-site productivity. It is positioned as competitive with [Openspace.ai](/notes/openspace-ai) and Disperse.io, with the key differentiator that its data is more granular, making it more operational and driving higher engagement (a framing echoed on the [Openspace.ai](/notes/openspace-ai) page). The note records explosive growth — from $50K ARR last year to $6M — and a Series A from Lightspeed and TLV Partners (not actively raising at the time). The open diligence questions: project-based revenue (non-recurring, exposed to the construction cycle), whether it can become a system of record rather than just an analytical tool, and whether end-workers actually use it versus only top-down visibility.

**Context:** Buildots is an Israeli construction-technology company using AI and 360° helmet-camera capture to track construction progress against project plans; it has raised venture funding from investors including Lightspeed and TLV Partners. (General background.)
