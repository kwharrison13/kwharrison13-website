# Mosaic (Coatue)

Mosaic is [Coatue](/notes/coatue)'s internal data platform — the in-house quantitative/data infrastructure the firm built to support its investing. In Kyle's March 30, 2022 call with [Rahul Chitturi](/notes/rahul-chitturi), Chitturi described it as something "very few firms have built": a roughly **$20M annual budget**, **12 people on headcount**, and **50% of the budget spent on data sets**. The scale and dedicated headcount frame Mosaic as a serious, standalone capability rather than a side tool — Kyle filed the call under the `data-driven-investing` theme, treating Mosaic as a concrete example of a fund operationalizing data as a competitive edge.

**Context:** Coatue Management is a New York technology-focused hedge fund founded by Philippe Laffont. Mosaic is its proprietary data and analytics platform, used to ingest and analyze data sets across the firm's investments.
