# Mark Zuckerberg

**One-line:** *Facebook/Meta founder; source of the "young people are just smarter" line Kyle interrogates in [The Rising Generation](/essays/the-rising-generation).*

## What we know

- In 2007, when Facebook was ~3 years old, told a group of [Y Combinator](/notes/y-combinator) founders: *"Young people are just smarter. Why are most chess masters under 30?… Young people just have simpler lives."* Kyle uses this as a foil — the dominant players in tech are no longer 20-year-olds. (source: The Rising Generation - Research)
