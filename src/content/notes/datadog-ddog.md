# Datadog ($DDOG)

**One-line definition:** *Observability/monitoring platform; [Shardul Shah](/notes/shardul-shah) used it as a case study in premium pricing, practitioner marketing, and TAM expansion.*

## How sources describe it

- [Shardul Shah](/notes/shardul-shah)'s lessons from Datadog — (1) be intentional about pricing; Datadog chose premium as "positive friction," and pushback on price means you're not delivering enough value; (2) speak to the practitioner via educational content marketing plus events; (3) the TAM expanded every six months; (4) don't fear competitive markets — competition signals demand; (5) scoop up as much secondary as possible to build ownership in a winner. (September 22nd, 2021 — Shardul Shah on Datadog)
