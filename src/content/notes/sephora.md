# Sephora

Mentioned in passing in the Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 dossier as the prior employer of [Alison Colwell](/notes/alison-colwell), who by the time of [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures)' Watershed diligence (Nov 2021) had become the first Director of Sustainability at [Okta ($OKTA)](/notes/okta). Sephora appears only as a line in Colwell's background during a sustainability expert/customer reference call — it is not itself a subject of the diligence, just context for one of the climate practitioners Kyle interviewed.

**Context:** Sephora is a multinational beauty and cosmetics retailer, owned by LVMH, operating a large chain of stores and a major e-commerce platform.

## Where this appears

- Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 — named as [Alison Colwell](/notes/alison-colwell)'s former employer before she became Okta's first Director of Sustainability.
