# Naval's Recommended Reading

The "Bonus" section of *The Almanack of Naval Ravikant*, where [Naval Ravikant](/notes/naval-ravikant)'s book recommendations are compiled — split into Nonfiction and Philosophy and Spirituality lists, plus his answers on what he's currently rereading. Framed with his line: "The truth is, I don't read for self-improvement. I read out of curiosity and interest. The best book is the one you'll devour."
