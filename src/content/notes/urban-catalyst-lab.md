# Urban Catalyst Lab

**One-line:** *Organization using art as a change catalyst at the community, city, and federal level — helping local governments engage larger bodies like the UN around "The New Urban Agenda."*

## What we know

- Helps local governments come together with larger organizations like the UN, anchored in "The New Urban Agenda" (the UN's ~20-year urban-development vision), using art at the community, city, and federal level to drive change (per [Ruxanda Renita](/notes/ruxanda-renita), June 28th, 2018 — Ruxanda Renita (Urban Catalyst Lab)).
- Treats "cities as museums" — public spaces transform people and communities, and artists work with communities to address local situations (e.g. a Jone's Creek project addressing social cohesion across siloed cultures) (June 28th, 2018 — Ruxanda Renita (Urban Catalyst Lab)).
- Next iteration leverages IoT — "intelligent art" — to measure behavioral change, social cohesion, education, and safety; their biggest project is a summit to catapult intelligent art as a change catalyst (June 28th, 2018 — Ruxanda Renita (Urban Catalyst Lab)).
