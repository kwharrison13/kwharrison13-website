# Jessica Riolfi

Jessica Riolfi is a founder at [Uprise](/notes/uprise), a fintech startup whose monthly investor/portfolio updates Kyle read while clearing emails as a [Contrary](/notes/contrary) backer. Uprise targets single young professionals in their first five years post-graduation — people at medium-to-large companies who are still building net worth (<$100K) and rate low confidence managing finances. Across 2022 her updates show steady product traction: Uprise was the #2 finance app on ProductHunt, 3x'ed monthly plans completed, and lifted onboarding conversion to ~11% (June 6th, 2022 — Uprise May Update (Jessica Riolfi)).

Riolfi's later updates (read in the November 7th, 2022 — Cleared Email batch) describe an emerging go-to-market wedge: distribution as an employer benefit, with early pilots opened up with a few employers of a few hundred employees each — and an explicit ask for B2B sales experience and intros to decision-makers who might offer Uprise as an employee benefit.
