# Synthesis School

Education startup that Kyle associates with [Ana Lorena Fabrega](/notes/ana-lorena-fabrega) ("Ms. Fab"), its Chief Evangelist, an educator focused on independent thinking and mental models. In Cultivating Cults - Research she is flagged as an interview target *at Synthesis School*. (Cultivating Cults - Research, [Ana Lorena Fabrega](/notes/ana-lorena-fabrega))

The school recurs as a worked example of the "build new education systems" call: Kyle tags it against [It's Time To Build](/notes/it-s-time-to-build) in the passage arguing that despite knowing one-to-one [Tutoring](/notes/tutoring) yields a two-standard-deviation gain (the [Bloom two-sigma effect](/notes/bloom-two-sigma-effect)) and having the internet, we haven't built systems to match every learner with a tutor. ([It's Time To Build](/notes/it-s-time-to-build)) It also shows up as a name-to-watch in two March 2022 sourcing calls — flagged in the [Daniel Appel](/notes/daniel-appel) (Instructure) conversation, where the question was whether Synthesis sells to school districts or direct to parents. (March 30th, 2022 — Daniel Appel (Instructure), March 30th, 2022 — Josh Curry (Uber Eats))

**Context:** Synthesis is an online enrichment program (originally spun out of the simulation-and-games curriculum at SpaceX's Ad Astra school) that teaches kids complex problem-solving and decision-making through collaborative games rather than lectures.
