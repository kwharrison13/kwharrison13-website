# Linguistic Relativity

**One-line:** *The Whorfian hypothesis that the structure of a language influences — or even determines — how its speakers perceive and think about the world. Kyle's load-bearing premise in [We Need a Renaissance of Rhetoric](/essays/we-need-a-renaissance-of-rhetoric): "the limits of your language are the limits of your world" (Wittgenstein), so ceding your language to AI or a political party means ceding your worldview.*

- [The Openness of AI](/essays/the-openness-of-ai) — Kyle's deep dive raises it in the context of AI censorship: as AI systems author the majority of our data, their output begins to frame reality.
