# Crayon

Crayon appears in Kyle's notes as a comparison company in the September 13th, 2022 — Nisha Swarup call, where [Nisha Swarup](/notes/nisha-swarup) was building a vendor [Discovery + Diligence](/notes/discovery-diligence) product for [Procurement](/notes/procurement). When laying out case studies, Crayon was grouped under the [Sales Intelligence](/notes/sales-intelligence) category alongside [Klue](/notes/klue) and [Slintel](/notes/slintel) — the competitive-intelligence end of the comp set, distinct from the intent-data / contact-data vendors like [Bombora](/notes/bombora) and [ZoomInfo](/notes/zoominfo). It serves as a reference for how teams track and aggregate signals about other companies, not as a diligence target itself.

**Context:** Crayon is a competitive-intelligence platform that automatically tracks competitors' digital footprints — pricing, messaging, product, and marketing changes — and packages those signals into battlecards and alerts for sales and product teams.
