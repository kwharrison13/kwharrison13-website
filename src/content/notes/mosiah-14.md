# Mosiah 14

A Book of Mormon scripture reference Kyle catalogs in Miscellaneous Scriptures. Mosiah 14:5 — "Bruised for our iniquities" — is noted there as Abinadi's quotation of [Isaiah 53](/notes/isaiah-53):5, one of the "Key Writers All See Christ" cross-references on the Atonement and the prophetic foreshadowing of Christ's suffering.

## Where this appears
