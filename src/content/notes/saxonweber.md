# SaxonWeber

**One-line:** *[Derek Weber](/notes/derek-weber)'s private-markets secondaries firm whose core business [ShareWell](/notes/sharewell) focuses on trading/brokerage rather than data leadership.*

## What we know

- Core business is [ShareWell](/notes/sharewell) — secondaries around trading data plus trading/brokerage; two core client types: a retail seller group (current/former tech operators) and institutional investors (family offices, asset managers, large VCs), mostly skipping individual/angel investors (source: June 20th, 2023 — Derek Weber (SaxonWeber)).
- Launched its own platform ~90 days before the call with ~1K platform users; reports 24K individual and 15K institutional users overall (source: June 20th, 2023 — Derek Weber (SaxonWeber)).
- Positions on trading rather than being the data leader (contrast Caplight); competitors include Notice, Forge, Caplight, and NASDAQ Private Markets (source: June 20th, 2023 — Derek Weber (SaxonWeber)).
- Explored embedding [Contrary Research](/notes/contrary) content under its brand and offered Kyle open access to its (unmonetized) data (source: June 20th, 2023 — Derek Weber (SaxonWeber)).
