# Knoetic

Knoetic is an HR-tech company founded and led by [Joseph Quan](/notes/joseph-quan) — "the all-in-one platform built with CPOs, for CPOs" — that pairs a people-analytics product with **CPOHQ**, a private community of Chief People Officers. Kyle tracked it as a [Contrary Capital](/notes/contrary) portfolio company across a run of Quan's quarterly updates. The Q1 2022 update is the richest snapshot: over 2021 Knoetic **5x'd ARR (from $430K to $2MM+)**, 5x'd analytics weekly active users, 3x'd CPOHQ's reach (ending the year with **1,350 CPOs**), and scaled the team from 6 to 30+ — while flagging its first-ever competition-driven downsells/churn, notably underlying systems like [ADP](/notes/adp) beginning to build their own analytics products. Earlier (January 31st, 2022) Quan laid out the CPO-platform thesis and a Synack onboarding.

By September 2022 (November 7th, 2022 — Cleared Email) Quan announced Knoetic's **Series B — $36M to unify diverse sources of HR data** — reshared by 150+ Chief People Officers and covered by TechCrunch. The company also surfaces in Kyle's [Investors to Know](/notes/investors-to-know) tracking: reading Knoetic's update produced a roster of the ~100 super-angels (Bradley Horowitz, Gokul Rajaram, Mar Hershenson, Pejman Nozad, and others) who backed its Series A.

**Context:** Knoetic is a New York-based HR software company that combines workforce analytics with the CPOHQ executive community for Chief People Officers, founded by Joseph Quan.
