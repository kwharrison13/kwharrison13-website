# Mere Christianity

**Why it's on the list:** *[C.S. Lewis](/notes/c-s-lewis)'s classic case for Christian belief — the source of the "liar, lunatic, or Lord" trilemma about Jesus's claim to be God, which Kyle annotates against Hitchens in [Why You're Christian](/notes/why-you-re-christian).*

**Where I saw it:** Cited in [Why You're Christian](/notes/why-you-re-christian), where Kyle quotes Lewis's argument that one cannot accept Jesus as merely "a great moral teacher" while rejecting his claim to be God.
