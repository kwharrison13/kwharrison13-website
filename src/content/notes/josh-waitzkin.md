# Josh Waitzkin

Chess prodigy turned author of The Art of Learning, cited in [Just Flow With It](/notes/just-flow-with-it) for the idea of "creating your own earthquakes" — first flowing with whatever comes, then using it to your advantage, then becoming self-sufficient enough to generate explosive inspiration without outside stimulus.

**Context:** Josh Waitzkin is an American author, chess player, and martial artist whose childhood chess career was the subject of the film *Searching for Bobby Fischer*. He later became a Tai Chi Push Hands world champion and wrote *The Art of Learning* (2007), about the meta-skill of learning across domains.
