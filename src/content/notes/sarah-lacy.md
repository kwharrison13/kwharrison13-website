# Sarah Lacy

Named only in passing as one of the interviewers whose conversations with Naval Ravikant were excerpted to build *The Almanack of Naval Ravikant*, alongside [Shane Parrish](/notes/shane-parrish), [Joe Rogan](/notes/joe-rogan), and Tim Ferris. The book does not distinguish between interviewers or say more about her specifically.
