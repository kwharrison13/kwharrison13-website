# Bloom

Bloom is a [Contrary](/notes/contrary) portfolio consumer-finance app whose investor updates [Sonny Mo](/notes/sonny-mo) writes. Its growth signal in early 2022 was App Store ranking: in the May update it had cracked #110 in Finance — above E*Trade — pulling 2K–3K daily signups on ~$4K/mo of ad spend, "less than 1/10th of what other players are spending" (June 6th, 2022 — Bloom May Update (Sonny Mo)). Later 2022 updates Kyle logged show the trajectory: by September Bloom set an internal goal of reaching $1M ARR by March 2023 and was asking for help optimizing SKAdnetwork / Facebook ads, and by October it was exploring B2B opportunities with traditional banks and credit unions and seeking warm intros (November 7th, 2022 — Cleared Email).
