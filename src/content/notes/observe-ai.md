# Observe.ai

Observe.ai is the contact-center conversation-intelligence vendor that recurs throughout Kyle's Cresta.ai - Diligence as [Cresta.ai](/notes/cresta-ai)'s single most-cited head-to-head competitor. Across the expert calls, Observe is characterized by its **post-call QA heritage** — it "listens to 100% of calls" and automatically scores them against a scorecard (probing for empathy, branding the opening, etc.), automating what traditional QA did manually at ~2 calls/week per agent. That post-call origin is the recurring dividing line: where Cresta and [Balto.ai](/notes/balto-ai) deliver real-time *live* coaching mid-conversation, Observe's roots in after-the-fact scoring repeatedly cost it deals where buyers wanted live guidance (one expert "eliminated them" for exactly this reason).

The diligence guests drew a consistent product comparison: **Observe's UX, dashboards/analytics, integration, and support are better; Cresta's underlying AI and answer quality are better.** An Apple expert who ran both put it as *"Observe might give quicker responses but Cresta is better responses... all the metrics were better with Cresta across the board,"* and judged Cresta could more easily build Observe's analytics than Observe could build better AI — the harder piece. Observe also competed on **pricing model**: it bills per agent/seat (~$20/agent/month, the low end of the ~$20–60 band) rather than Cresta's per-interaction model, which buyers found more predictable — Samsung chose Observe partly for this. Other recurring notes: Observe "struggled to scale" during COVID volume spikes (a Samsung expert started re-evaluating Cresta as a result), and it was praised as a strong fit for **smaller contact centers** and pure quality-monitoring use cases. Multiple experts expected a CCaaS/PBX player ([Five9](/notes/five9), Genesys, NICE inContact) or [Medallia](/notes/medallia) to eventually acquire one of Cresta/Balto/Observe.

**Context:** Observe.AI is a San Francisco-based conversation-intelligence and AI platform for contact centers, founded in 2017, which expanded from automated post-call QA into real-time agent assist and analytics.
