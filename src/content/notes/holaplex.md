# Holaplex

Holaplex appears in Kyle's December 2021 [FormFn](/notes/formfn) seed investment-committee memo as the startup's closest competitor. Both help independent creators set up individual storefronts to sell 1/1 NFTs on Solana, and on Kyle's competitive positioning map FormFn and Holaplex occupy the same "marketplace × 1/1-NFTs" quadrant (distinct from collections-focused players like Solanart, Magic Eden, and DigitalEyes, or Ethereum/Tezos venues like Foundation, SuperRare, and Objkt). The memo argues FormFn would differentiate on built-in discovery features and a more seamless user experience: Kyle set up a Holaplex store himself and found the site often failed to load and was confusing to navigate, contrasting it with the product craft of FormFn's founders Matt and Katherine. He also notes Holaplex had just raised a $6M seed from CoinFund.

**Context:** Holaplex is a Solana-based NFT tooling and marketplace company that lets creators launch their own storefronts and mint/sell NFTs.
