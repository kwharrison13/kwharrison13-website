# James 4

A chapter from the New Testament epistle of James. In Kyle's Virtue topical study, James 4:7 — "Submit yourselves therefore to God. Resist the devil, and he will flee from you." — is cited under the heading on *submission* ("Yield, Give, or Offer"). The study draws the distinction that a command allows for submission (something freely offered) rather than mere subjection, and uses this verse to frame submitting to God as the path to becoming a saint through the [Atonement](/notes/atonement).

**Context:** The Epistle of James is a New Testament book traditionally attributed to James, brother of Jesus. Chapter 4 addresses worldliness, pride, and submission to God, and includes the well-known exhortation to "Resist the devil, and he will flee from you" (James 4:7).
