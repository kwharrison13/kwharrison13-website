# Sharad Verma

Founder/executive at [Boostup.ai](/notes/boostup-ai), a revenue-operations / sales-forecasting platform (founded 2019) positioning head-to-head against [Clari](/notes/clari). Verma pitched Boostup to Kyle on a December 2021 [Index](/notes/index-sourcing-calls) sourcing call; Kyle's notes flag that he came across as cagey, asking a bunch of unusual questions. Verma frames Boostup as the "fastest growing revenue operations company" — a deep-tech engineering team led by product people, in contrast to [Clari](/notes/clari) (founded 2015) as a sales-led organization — claiming Boostup beats Clari in three of four head-to-head deals, wins six of ten G2 fields, and ships 3–4x faster.

On GTM and vision he described a top-down motion driven by the CRO and sales operations, ~50% self-serve, and a belief that revops tooling could eventually become a [Product Led Growth (PLG)](/notes/product-led-growth-plg) motion "for Ops, not for reps" — letting a RevOps person pull something off the shelf and replace a spreadsheet without talking to anyone. On the call he reported 130% net retention, 300% ARR growth (from $1M to $4M that year, targeting $12M then $36M), a $6M insider check, and a desire to raise a ~$30M Series B.
