# Proverbs 28

A chapter from the Old Testament book of Proverbs, used in Kyle's scripture study as a cross-reference on repentance. In the Miscellaneous Scriptures study notes, Proverbs 28:13 is cited twice — "If you conceal sin you will not prosper" — and is anchored to the step "Forsake sin" within a six-step framework of repentance (godly sorrow, confess to God, ask forgiveness, rectify problems, forsake sin, receive forgiveness).

**Context:** Proverbs is a book of Hebrew wisdom literature in the Old Testament, traditionally associated with King Solomon. Proverbs 28:13 reads in full: "He that covereth his sins shall not prosper: but whoso confesseth and forsaketh them shall have mercy."

## Where this appears

- Miscellaneous Scriptures — verse 13 cited as scriptural support for confessing and forsaking sin within a six-step model of repentance
