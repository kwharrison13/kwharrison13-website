# Regal Voice

**One-line:** *A B2C CRM / outbound-communications SaaS company (associated with [Alex Levin](/notes/alex-levin)) building toward a "system of record" for B2C, which Kyle diligenced and passed on in 2023.*

## What we know

- Positioning toward a system-of-record B2C CRM with a GTM engine driven by SDRs; retention across customers was solid despite a few months of softness (source: June 20th, 2023 — Alex Levin (Regal Voice)).
- Kyle benchmarked it against [Gong](/notes/gong) (>100% YoY growth until >$100M revenue), Amplitude (~$70M run rate at IPO, burning ~$16M, trading 3-4x forward), and [Hubspot](/notes/hubspot) (steady compounding, ~11x forward) — the platform/big-vision durability being the key question (source: June 20th, 2023 — Alex Levin (Regal Voice)).
- An investor named Jake was already on the cap table at a lower valuation and willing to take the GTM-proof-out risk; Kyle passed given the return math at later stage (source: June 20th, 2023 — Alex Levin (Regal Voice)).
