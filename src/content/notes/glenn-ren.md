# Glenn Ren

Glenn Ren is an engineering hire at [Standard.re](/notes/standard-re), the on-chain life-insurance startup founded by [Max Gasner](/notes/max-gasner) and [Zac Townsend](/notes/zac-townsend). He surfaces in Kyle's notes from the June 6th, 2022 — Max Gasner & Zac Townsend (Standard.re) fundraise call, where the founders cited him as a recent addition to a four-person engineering team drawn from Slack, [Ramp](/notes/ramp), Airbnb, Corelight, and Elementl. Ren came from [Ramp](/notes/ramp); on the same call the founders noted the team still needed someone who knows Solidity for the on-chain work.
