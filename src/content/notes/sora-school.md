# Sora School

Sora School shows up as a Kyle annotation in [The Self-Driven Child](/books/the-self-driven-child), tagged against the book's "Inspire — but don't require" idea: that teachers should inspire kids to want to learn outside the classroom and that most homework should be voluntary and ungraded. Kyle flags Sora as a real-world model of the alternative-education philosophy the book advocates — autonomy, intrinsic motivation, and lower-stress learning environments — rather than the test-and-homework-driven default. The book page also lists it as a referenced "alt-education model."

**Context:** Sora is an online, project-based independent middle and high school built around student autonomy and interest-driven learning rather than a traditional grade-and-homework structure.
