# Steve Malouf

A sales leader at [Datadog ($DDOG)](/notes/datadog) who joined the Index Mentors team to help on sales topics. In a September 2021 call with Kyle, Malouf described advising [Prelay](/notes/prelay) — the deal-management startup led by [Gabriella DeFlorio](/notes/gabriella-deflorio) — whom he signed on with primarily because he found DeFlorio "very talented, driven, ambitious." He introduced her to Datadog's [VP of Sales Engineering](/notes/vp-of-sales-engineering), who began evaluating Prelay's technology, and was opening doors at [Atlassian](/notes/atlassian) and [Databricks](/notes/databricks). His read on Prelay: a strong fit for long, complex (60+ day) sales cycles where many stakeholders touch a process and context gets lost, with POC management as a wedge use case and an "expansion flywheel" once deployed. On hiring, he praised DeFlorio's "contagious" energy and competitiveness while noting her most pressing need was building out the sales team rather than carrying it herself.
