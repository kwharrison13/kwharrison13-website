# Gong

Gong appears in Kyle's notes as the leading **sales conversation-intelligence platform** — repeatedly paired with [Chorus](/notes/chorus) as the "sales-call oriented" reference point against which contact-center tools are contrasted. In the Cresta.ai - Diligence, experts position [Cresta.ai](/notes/cresta-ai) as more service/support-capable than the sales-first tools (Gong, Chorus); the recurring "why not Gong/Chorus" reasoning is that they're geared to sales-type calls and lack the depth needed for purpose-built contact-center coaching. Gong, Chorus, and Cresta all do live-call analysis, but the sales-vs-support orientation is the dividing line.

Gong also recurs in the CaptivateIQ Diligence — 2021 notes as a real customer and cultural touchpoint: CaptivateIQ counted Gong as a customer, several interviewees came from Gong, and one cited Gong CEO Amit Bendov's line — "if you provide the best product then even if you have 40% market share you're going to have 80% of the profit in the market." Gong's ARPU is noted there (alongside Zoominfo and Outreach) as typically $1K+, well above CaptivateIQ's then-$400.

**Context:** Gong is a "revenue intelligence" company that records, transcribes, and analyzes sales calls/meetings to surface deal and coaching insights; founded in 2015 and co-led by CEO Amit Bendov.
