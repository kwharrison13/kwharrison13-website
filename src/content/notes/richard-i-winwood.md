# Richard I. Winwood

An attributed source in [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book), the LDS quotation collection Kyle's brother Chad assembled. Kyle recorded a Winwood passage that dramatizes a missionary's "purpose" — imagining Christ appearing to say "your purpose is to invite others to come unto Me through faith in Me and My atonement, repentance, baptism, receiving the gift of the Holy Ghost, and enduring to the end," then noting the disciple already knew this and only needed "the faith to do it." The quote sits among the collection's missionary-purpose and faith-to-act material.

## Where this appears

- [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book) — quoted on a missionary's purpose and the gap between knowing the gospel and having faith to act on it.
