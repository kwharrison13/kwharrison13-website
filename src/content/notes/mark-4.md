# Mark 4

A chapter in the New Testament Gospel of Mark, cited in Kyle's gospel study. In Faith — Topical Study, Mark 4:40 is used as "an experience of lack of faith, not understanding the attributes of God" — Christ's rebuke to the disciples in the storm ("Why are ye so fearful? how is it that ye have no faith?"), drawn in as a study point on what insufficient faith looks like.

**Context:** Mark 4 contains the parable of the sower and other parables of the kingdom, closing with Jesus calming the storm on the Sea of Galilee and questioning the disciples' faith (Mark 4:35–41).
