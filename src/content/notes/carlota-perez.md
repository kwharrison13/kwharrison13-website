# Carlota Perez

_Stub — Venezuelan-British scholar; known for her framework on technological revolutions and the 'Surge Cycle' — the pattern by which new techno-economic paradigms unfold through an installation phase (bubble and crash) followed by a deployment phase (golden age). Author of 'Technological Revolutions and Financial Capital'._
