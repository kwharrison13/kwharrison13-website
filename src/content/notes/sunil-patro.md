# Sunil Patro

Sunil Patro is the founder and CEO of [SignEasy](/notes/signeasy), the mobile-first e-signature tool for SMBs that he started ~10 years ago and bootstrapped. His path was bottom-up and organic: SignEasy saw millions of mobile downloads and monetized via in-app purchases, and the product became the only e-signature solution besides [DocuSign ($DOCU)](/notes/docusign) that Apple promotes (September 22nd, 2021 — Sunil Patro (SignEasy)). In a 2021 sourcing call with Kyle (at [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures)) he laid out SignEasy's thesis: SMBs reject both narrow point solutions and clunky enterprise tools, so a cross-device, virtually-no-setup product wins, with a [Calendly](/notes/calendly)/[Loom](/notes/loom)-style PLG motion where ~80% of users arrive free through app stores and marketplaces. He knows [Akshay Kothari](/notes/akshay-kothari) at [Notion](/notes/notion).
