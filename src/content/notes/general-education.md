# General Education

General Education is the theme of broad, multidisciplinary curriculum that [Charlie Munger](/notes/charlie-munger) argues for — making more courses mandatory and requiring those who set the curriculum to hold large, fluent, cross-disciplinary knowledge.

## Where this appears

- [Charlie Munger — Harvard Law School Fiftieth Reunion Address](/notes/charlie-munger-harvard-law-school-fiftieth-reunion-address) — Munger's argument that more courses should be mandatory and curriculum-setters must possess multidisciplinary knowledge maintained in fluency.
