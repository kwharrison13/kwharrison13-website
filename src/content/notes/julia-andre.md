# Julia Andre

Julia Andre is a partner at [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) who recurs across Kyle's 2021 sourcing and diligence notes as the author of several deal briefs he read. She authored (with Carlos) the [Sylvera](/notes/sylvera) Series A pre-brief that proposed Index co-lead a $25M round at a $125M post — building on Index's January 2021 seed ($2.5M for 13%) and racing pre-emptive offers from [Coatue](/notes/coatue) and [Balderton Capital](/notes/balderton-capital) (November 5th, 2021 — Sylvera Pre-Brief (Julia Andre)). She is the connective thread on Index's [Carbon Offsets](/notes/carbon-offsets) work, surfacing alongside [Carlos Gonzalez-Cardenas](/notes/carlos-gonzalez-cardenas) on Sylvera and the partnership review of [NCX](/notes/ncx).

Beyond climate, her briefs span fintech and emerging markets. She passed along the PAYE brief — a B2B [Buy Now, Pay Later (BNPL)](/notes/buy-now-pay-later-bnpl) play building the payment enabler for digital B2B trade (September 7th, 2021 — PAYE Brief (Julia Andre)) — and co-authored, with [Martin Mignot](/notes/martin-mignot), the pre-brief for Shara, a community-driven SME-finance product for [Africa](/notes/africa) (June 29th, 2021 — Shara (Pre-Brief)). At the December 13th, 2021 — NCX Partner Presentation she sat among the Index partnership reviewing the forest-carbon marketplace's Series B.
