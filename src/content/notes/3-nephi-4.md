# 3 Nephi 4

A chapter in the Book of Mormon, covered as part of Kyle's Come Follow Me study of 3 Nephi 1–7. In that week's notes it appears in the chapter roster for the block but draws no separate verse-level commentary — Kyle's substantive notes for the week concentrate on [3 Nephi 1](/notes/3-nephi-1) (the fulfillment of Samuel's sign, [Christlike Sorrow](/notes/christlike-sorrow), Satan's lies, and the [rising generation](/essays/the-rising-generation) who "became for themselves").

**Context:** 3 Nephi 4 narrates the climax of the Nephites' war against the Gadianton robbers under Giddianhi and Zemnarihah — the people gather into a defensible center, prevail through reliance on God, and afterward cry praises for their deliverance.
