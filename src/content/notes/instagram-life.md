# Instagram Life

"Instagram Life" is a phrase Kyle uses in his notes on [How To Take Smart Notes](/books/how-to-take-smart-notes) to capture how people live their lives differently because they know so much of it will end up posted on social media — for example, spending money on experiences rather than 'things.' He raises it as a parallel to [Information Ingestion](/notes/information-ingestion): just as deliberate, public-facing posting changes how you live, consuming information *deliberately* with the purpose of feeding public thinking changes how you read, listen, and take notes. The connection is the behavioral shift that comes from knowing your input is destined for a public output. Kyle tags the observation `#Influencers`.
