# Divvy

Divvy is a Utah-based spend-management / corporate-card company that appears twice in Kyle's notes as an ecosystem and reference touchpoint. In the November 8th, 2021 [Serene Papenfuss](/notes/serene-papenfuss) ([Kickstart Seed Fund](/notes/kickstart-seed-fund)) sourcing call, Divvy is cited as a major source of departing talent in the [Utah Tech Ecosystem](/notes/utah-tech-ecosystem) — though Serene noted that, having met multiple people coming out of Divvy, the Divvy pedigree was "not as good a signal" as product/engineering talent coming out of [Lucid](/notes/lucid) or [Pluralsight](/notes/pluralsight).

In the CaptivateIQ diligence (2021), Sterling Snow at Divvy was a reference contact on sales-commission tooling. His response: "I've looked at all of them — Spiff, Captivate, Xactly. None of them work for us because all our commissions are usage based. So we use Tableau." — a useful negative data point on the limits of commission-automation tools for usage-based comp.

**Context:** Divvy was a Lehi, Utah corporate-card and expense-management startup acquired by Bill.com (BILL) in 2021. This general background goes beyond what Kyle's notes record.
