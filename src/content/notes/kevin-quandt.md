# Kevin Quandt

Kevin Quandt is a sustainability contact at [Sweetgreen](/notes/sweetgreen) who appears in the Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 dossier as one of the corporate-sustainability leads Kyle emailed during outreach for the Watershed Series B diligence (the standard "I've been spending a lot of time in corporate sustainability… focused on better understanding how large businesses are thinking about their ESG programs" note). Sweetgreen is one of Watershed's marquee customers in the dossier — used as the running example of carbon accounting in practice (it "started by adding a list of its food purchases" and uses Watershed across procurement, marketing, and finance) — so Quandt sits on the customer-reference side of the diligence rather than as an expert call.

## Where this appears
