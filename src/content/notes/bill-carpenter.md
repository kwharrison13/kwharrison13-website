# Bill Carpenter

Bill Carpenter appears in Kyle's mission-era Teaching Repentance study (drawn from Topical Journal 1) as a voice on the "inviting spirit" of missionary work. He is quoted in the *Boldness Born of Love* section: "Do you feel that spirit? I do too, very strongly. That is the spirit that God uses to invite His children to obey His commandments." The note immediately reframes the missionary practice away from the "baptismal CHALLENGE" (which "plays off of pride") toward extending sweet, direct baptismal *invitations* — placing Carpenter alongside [Jason Bluemel](/notes/jason-bluemel) as a figure who modeled love-driven boldness.

The available evidence is a single attributed quote, so his exact role (likely a mission leader or fellow missionary) is not pinned down by the source.
