# ToTango

In Kyle's Planhat diligence, ToTango is repeatedly named as **one of the two omnipresent US incumbents in [Customer Success](/notes/customer-success) tooling alongside [Gainsight](/notes/gainsight)** — the pair that, with [ChurnZero](/notes/churnzero), dominate US buyer mindshare while [Planhat](/notes/planhat) is "largely absent from US mindshare." Expert callers place ToTango at the larger end of the segment: it lands big enterprises (Zoom, Adobe, Cisco, Glassdoor) and is described as the ~200–1K FTE sweet spot, while Planhat skews to startups/scaleups (~50–200 FTE). Several callers rank the omnipresent three as Gainsight, ChurnZero, ToTango. Like its peers, ToTango ships [playbooks](/notes/playbook) and CTAs as table-stakes features; one caller noted that vendors like ToTango "all kind of look the same" in demos, with the real differentiation coming from the pre-sale experience.

**Context:** Totango is a customer-success software platform that helps SaaS companies track customer health, drive onboarding/renewal, and reduce churn; it later merged with Catalyst.
