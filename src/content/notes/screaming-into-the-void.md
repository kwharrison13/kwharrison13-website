# Screaming into the Void

Kyle's phrase, in his notes on [College Students- Focus On This](/notes/college-students-focus-on-this), for the feeling that [Writing](/notes/writing) sometimes goes unread and unanswered — publishing into apparent silence. It pairs with the essay's imperative (via [Martha Graham](/notes/martha-graham)) to "keep the channel open" and publish regardless of whether anyone seems to be listening.

## Where this appears

- [College Students- Focus On This](/notes/college-students-focus-on-this) — Kyle annotates: "I sometimes feel like [Writing](/notes/writing) is [Screaming into the Void](/notes/screaming-into-the-void)."
