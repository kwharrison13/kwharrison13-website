# Snowbird Ski Resort

Snowbird appears in Kyle's personal [Snowboarding](/notes/snowboarding) logistics note as a Utah resort option. The note flags Snowbird's "Chickadee" run — a smaller hill set aside for young children or true beginners — and records the Ticket Office contact (801.933.2220 / ticketsales@snowbird.com) for lift-ticket date changes. It is weighed alongside [Sundance Ski Resort](/notes/sundance-ski-resort) as candidate destinations.

**Context:** Snowbird is a ski and snowboard resort in Little Cottonwood Canyon in Utah's Wasatch Range, near Salt Lake City, known for steep terrain and deep snowfall.
