# Standard.re

**One-line:** *On-chain life-insurance startup ([Max Gasner](/notes/max-gasner), [Zac Townsend](/notes/zac-townsend)) pursuing a Bermuda-first regulatory path to issue crypto-denominated insurance products.*

## What we know

- ~10-person team: a four-person engineering team (ex-Slack, Ramp, Airbnb, Corelight, Elementl), a three-person insurance team with ~50 years combined experience (incl. a former life-insurance leader at a top-3 US insurer and a Bermuda CEO/actuary), and legal/ops from a large fintech and McKinsey. (June 6th, 2022 — Max Gasner & Zac Townsend (Standard.re))
- Fundraise: aiming for a ~$5M round on $100M post (could flex to $6-7M); says it has 2.5 years of runway regardless. Talking to strategics (Coinbase, Alchemy, DCG) and reinsurer venture arms after Dragonfly pulled a signed term sheet on "Luna Wednesday" (the day the Luna collapse blew up). (June 6th, 2022 — Max Gasner & Zac Townsend (Standard.re))
- Product roadmap over ~18 months: offshore whole life (estate-tax coverage for "whales") → offshore variable universal life → onshore indexed universal life and indexed annuity; launching first for ETH. (June 6th, 2022 — Max Gasner & Zac Townsend (Standard.re))
- Regulatory roadmap: license in Bermuda (Q3 2022, expects to be licensed by end of summer), Singapore (Q4 2022), an Ethereum co. in Bermuda (Q1 2023), UAE (Q4 2023), and a single-state US license (H2 2023); retained the former governor of Wyoming to help navigate state legislators. (June 6th, 2022 — Max Gasner & Zac Townsend (Standard.re))
- Hired [Glenn Ren](/notes/glenn-ren) (ex-[Ramp](/notes/ramp)) in engineering; said it still needs someone who knows Solidity. (June 6th, 2022 — Max Gasner & Zac Townsend (Standard.re))
