# APIMetrics

API performance-monitoring company that Kyle evaluated as an [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) sourcing call with founder [David O'Neill](/notes/david-o-neill) on November 8th, 2021. Per O'Neill, APIMetrics started around the same time as [Postman](/notes/postman), Runscope, and API Science, building a product to do API testing "the way they wanted to" — with a focus on **security and performance in production**, where competitors like [Appdynamics](/notes/appdynamics) and Dynatrace had failed to deliver real visibility. The positioning is to be the **trusted, neutral source of API performance data** so that banks and their third-party providers (e.g. Google Workspace vs. Microsoft Graph disputes) can resolve who actually has a problem. The company stores every synthetic test it runs regardless of cost, was building AI to detect performance changes, and was moving toward verifying compliance and governance in production — exploring a partnership with [42 Crunch](/notes/42-crunch) for security monitoring. Traction: customers include US Bank and ~70% of UK high-street banks (the UK led because of regulatory maturity); contracts ranged $14K–$170K (HSBC their largest, potentially $300K+ across all brands/regions). ARR had stalled at $400K for two years, then doubled in the prior six months, with $5M of pipeline they couldn't service fast enough.
