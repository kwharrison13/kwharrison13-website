# Felipe Abed

Felipe Abed is a Senior Customer Success Manager (UK&I) at Smartly (social advertising) — formerly an Account Manager at Facebook and an external auditor at PwC Argentina — who appears in Kyle's wiki as an expert-call source in the Planhat - Diligence customer-success diligence. As a **current Planhat user**, Abed is one of the strongest product-led voices in the call: he describes Planhat as "really good," praises its customization and visualization options, and calls its integrations "really really good" (calendar, email, Intercom chat), noting their CRM/admin tool can deep-link directly into Planhat — "a huge time saver & fully automated."

His two most critical buying criteria are **customization** ("no two companies are the same" — can he track the KPIs that matter?) and **integrations** (how easily it connects to Salesforce, HubSpot, etc., so customer info lives in one place). His main criticism, which the diligence flags as a recurring limitation, is that Planhat makes it hard to map a clear customer-success path on a macro level — to lay out all historic exchanges and tie CS into milestone steps. Despite that, he says he would recommend it.
