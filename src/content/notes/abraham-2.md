# Abraham 2

Abraham 2 is the chapter of the Book of Abraham where the Abrahamic Covenant is formally stated, and Kyle treats Abraham 2:9–11 as the canonical text for it across two studies. In his Abrahamic Covenant topical study, he cites Abraham 2:9–11 under "Authority — the seed of Abraham" as the promise that defines the seed of Abraham's charge to bless the nations and administer the ordinances of salvation. In Miscellaneous Scriptures, he chains the same verses into a study of the priesthood: Abraham 2:9–10 (God will make of Abraham a great nation and bless them) leading to 2:11 ("through thy name") — read as the promise that all families will be blessed through the priesthood. From verse 11 he draws his "Blessings of the Priesthood" list: the gospel, resurrection, and exaltation.

**Context:** Abraham 2 is part of the Book of Abraham in the Latter-day Saint Pearl of Great Price. It recounts the Lord's covenant with Abraham — promising a numerous seed, the priesthood, and that through his lineage all the families of the earth would be blessed.
