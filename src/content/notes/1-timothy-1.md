# 1 Timothy 1

A New Testament chapter cited in Kyle's Charity & Love gospel-study notes. There, 1 Timothy 1:5 ("Now the end of the commandment is charity out of a pure heart, and of a good conscience, and of faith unfeigned") is used to argue that every commandment points toward love — paired with [Matthew 22](/notes/matthew-22):37–40 to make the case that [Charity](/notes/charity), the pure love of Christ, is the aim behind all the commandments.

**Context:** 1 Timothy 1 opens Paul's first pastoral letter to Timothy, warning against false teachers and vain disputation and reaffirming that the goal ("end") of his charge is love from a pure heart.
