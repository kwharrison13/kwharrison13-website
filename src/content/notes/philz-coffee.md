# Philz Coffee

A coffee chain that appears in the Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 notes through [Andi Mersch](/notes/andi-mersch), its VP of Coffee and Sustainability (intro logged October 19th, 2021). The notes describe how Mersch expanded Philz's sustainability work beyond the supply chain to become company-wide — partly driven by investor TPG, which toured the coffee-roasting plant after investing and gave her leverage (via Philz's sustainable leadership council) to push the company further. Philz surfaces here as a sustainability-practitioner contact useful to [Watershed Climate](/notes/watershed-climate)'s diligence rather than as a Watershed customer per se.

**Context:** Philz Coffee is a San Francisco-founded specialty coffee chain known for its custom, individually brewed cups; TPG is among its investors.

## Where this appears

- Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 — context for [Andi Mersch](/notes/andi-mersch), Philz's VP of Coffee and Sustainability, and her TPG-backed expansion of the company's sustainability program.
