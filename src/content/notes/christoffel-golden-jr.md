# Christoffel Golden Jr.

Christoffel Golden Jr. appears in Kyle's notes on General Conference- October 2021 as a speaker Kyle pulled several lines from. Under the "Do you love me more than these things of the world?" theme, Kyle records Golden's warning that "We live in a modern world full of great knowledge and great skill. However, these things too often camouflage the unstable founding on which they are built," tagged [Spirit of Humility](/notes/spirit-of-humility). Golden is also the source of two further highlights Kyle saved: the D&C 128:22 "shall we not go on in so great a cause" passage, and a doctrine-changes-behavior point quoting [Boyd K. Packer](/notes/boyd-k-packer) — "True doctrine, understood, changes attitudes and behavior. The study of the doctrines of the gospel will improve behavior quicker than a study of behavior will improve behavior."

**Context:** Christoffel Golden Jr. is a South African–born general authority of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, a member of the Quorums of the Seventy.
