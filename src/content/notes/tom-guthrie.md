# Tom Guthrie

Founder/operator at [Sylva](/notes/sylva), a Contrary Capital portfolio company that acquires and operates online communities. Tom Guthrie surfaces across Kyle's Contrary-era daily logs as the author of Sylva's monthly investor updates, which Kyle read while clearing email. His updates trace Sylva's thesis evolution through 2022: that **larger communities are more attractive acquisition targets** — more predictable (consistent customer base, multiple revenue streams, resilient to economic shocks), more profitable (payroll-dominated cost structure means EBITDA-positive past ~$500k revenue), and easier to operate (more mature, commercial management teams needing less intervention). By the October 2022 update, Sylva's stated priorities were establishing a profitable individual-member acquisition channel per community, diversifying revenue, and implementing core data infrastructure; the team was hiring an Engineering Lead and Sr. Corporate Counsel, with an investor intro credited to Ryan Moore at Accomplice.
