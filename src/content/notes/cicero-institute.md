# Cicero Institute

The Cicero Institute is the non-partisan legislative organization [Joe Lonsdale](/notes/joe-lonsdale) founded, cited in his essay [To Save America, Restore Our Frontier](/notes/to-save-america-restore-our-frontier) as the concrete instrument of his prescription. Where the essay diagnoses America's decline as a slide "from being bold and adventurous to being dysfunctional and bureaucratic" — and argues that fighting wokeness is a sideshow versus building real alternatives to ossified institutions — the Cicero Institute is Lonsdale's vehicle for pursuing those alternatives through actual law. Per the essay, it pursues "non-partisan legislative solutions, with dozens of laws now on the books."

In the article's framing, the Institute is the legislative counterpart to the essay's broader "restore the Frontier" agenda — chartering bold governance experiments, funding new science and universities, and breaking up bureaucratic monopolies (the FDA, healthcare cartels) by creating competing alternatives rather than reforming the incumbents from within.

**Context:** The Cicero Institute is a public-policy think tank founded by Palantir and Addepar co-founder Joe Lonsdale, focused on drafting and advancing state-level legislation in areas such as healthcare pricing, homelessness, criminal justice, and government transparency.
