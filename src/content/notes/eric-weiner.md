# Eric Weiner

Eric Weiner is the author of [The Geography of Genius](/notes/the-geography-of-genius), a book on Kyle's anti-library (wiki/books-to-read/The Geography of Genius). Per Kyle's notes, the book "travels through history's great creative hotspots — Athens, Hangzhou, Florence, Edinburgh, Calcutta, Vienna, Silicon Valley — to ask why genius clusters in particular places and eras rather than in particular people." It connects to Kyle's interests in [Genius](/notes/genius) and [City Building](/notes/city-building) / creative-cluster dynamics. Kyle saved it from Amazon, tagged `#genius` `#books-to-read`.

**Context:** Eric Weiner is an American author and former NPR foreign correspondent known for travel-and-ideas books including *The Geography of Bliss*, *The Geography of Genius*, and *The Socrates Express*.
