# Jeff Brownlow

A contact of Kyle's, credited in his Great Softening research notes with a "triangle" framing of American upside/downside. The single grounded reference reads: "America has seemingly uncapped upside but also uncapped downside ([Jeff Brownlow](/notes/jeff-brownlow)'s triangle)" — the same structure Kyle returns to throughout that research, that opening the ceiling on possible outcomes also opens the floor (the real possibility of destitution and failure). The model gets its own concept page at [Brownlow's Triangle (uncapped upside/downside)](/notes/brownlow-s-triangle-uncapped-upside-downside), and the surrounding notes contrast it with countries that "may have low ceilings but a much higher floor."

Who Brownlow is in the world and the full shape of the "triangle" model are not spelled out in the source.
