# People to Follow

"People to Follow" is Kyle's running tag for two related lists: (1) historical and cultural figures worth featuring or following, and (2) contemporary writers and operators worth tracking. The historical strand comes out of a Nov 8, 2021 brainstorm that gathered figures such as Robert Noyce, Steve Jobs, Rosa Parks, Sally Ride, Jean-Michel Basquiat, Grace Hopper, Ada Lovelace, Claude Shannon, the *Hidden Figures* NASA mathematicians (Katherine Johnson, Dorothy Vaughan, Mary Jackson), Margaret Hamilton, and Virgil Abloh — a mix of scientists, engineers, artists, and pioneers framed as a "list of historical and cultural figures to feature / follow."

The contemporary strand is fed by saved links tagged `#people-to-follow`: Gabriel's Daily Blog on reading.supply (a daily project-building/entrepreneurship log Kyle flagged as a writer worth tracking) and The SaaS Playbook Substack, which Kyle cross-tags with [Playbooks](/notes/playbooks). The concept overlaps with [Playbooks](/notes/playbooks) as an "operators and operating knowledge worth following" cluster.
