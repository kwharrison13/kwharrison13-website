# Booking.com

Surfaces in Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 as a prospective customer for [Watershed Climate](/notes/watershed-climate)'s carbon-accounting platform. It appears in the travel vertical of Watershed's sales-lead lists — a warm-able connection through Booking's CTO Rob Francis, flagged as one that "would [be] good now." Booking.com is not a subject of the diligence; it shows up only as a named pipeline lead.

**Context:** Booking.com is the flagship online-travel-agency brand of Booking Holdings, one of the largest accommodation- and travel-booking marketplaces in the world.
