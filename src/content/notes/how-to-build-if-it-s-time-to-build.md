# How To Build If… It's Time To Build?

**Author:** [Bilal Zuberi](/notes/bilal-zuberi) ([Lux Capital](/notes/lux-capital))
**URL:** https://medium.com/lux-capital/how-to-build-if-its-time-to-build-f5294d5eb9d
**One-line:** *A practitioner's response to Andreessen's "It's Time to Build" — agreeing on the imperative but laying out six concrete hurdles that keep deep tech from attracting mainstream investment.*

## Key Highlights

- Written as a direct response to [Marc Andreessen](/notes/marc-andreessen)'s [It's Time To Build](/notes/it-s-time-to-build) essay (April 18, 2020), which Zuberi calls "likely to be remembered, discussed, and cited as much as Marc's last seminal essay 'Software is Eating the World.'"
- Lux Capital is one of the few large VC firms that has routinely invested in deep tech / hardware: DesktopMetal, Saildrone, Zoox, AirMap, Anduril, Recursion, and others.
- **Six hurdles keeping deep tech from mainstream investment:**
  1. **Lower margin** — hardware companies realistically target 40–50% margins, not the 70–80%+ of software. VCs must recalibrate what "successful" looks like.
  2. **Long sales cycles** — large buyers in infrastructure/defense buy through intermediaries and established primes; startups need purchase orders, not just grants.
  3. **Complex supply chains** — COVID exposed the fragility of globally distributed hardware supply chains; need resiliency premiums, distributed manufacturing, local sourcing.
  4. **Regulatory hurdles** — hardware, autonomous systems, healthcare, and security are regulated; need fast-track mechanisms akin to FDA Emergency Use Authorization.
  5. **Capital intensity** — R&D, manufacturing, and distribution of physical products require staged financing and larger syndicates to share the risk.
  6. **Fewer acquirors** — without big tech as acquiror, exit multiples are lower; more small-company IPOs would open liquidity.
- **The closing call:** Inaction on building the things we need to survive natural or human-caused disasters is not an option. "I am ready. Lux is ready. Are you?"
