# Nancy Falk Winland

Nancy Falk Winland is Head of [Customer Success](/notes/customer-success) EMEA at Epidemic Sound (a Stockholm music-tech company), where she rose through Licensing → Senior CSM → Team Lead → Head of CS EMEA; she was previously Head of Global Property Investment at Churchill Capital (PE, London). She appears in Kyle's Planhat - Diligence research as a current, daily [Planhat](/notes/planhat) user — one of the strongest reference points in the diligence.

She considered [Gainsight](/notes/gainsight) but concluded "Planhat is definitely the winner for a lot of different reasons." Her standout praise was for onboarding: she called the sales→CS handoff a key concern and said onboarding is "extremely clear in Planhat," with staged tasks, clear descriptions and timelines, and defined phases (onboarding → success → renewal). She also highlighted a customer-facing dashboard her team can share directly with clients as a key feature. Her team uses it for CS only (not sales/marketing) but relies on it daily, flagged no material weaknesses, and gave an unqualified recommendation — "a game changer for us," "a breath of fresh air," and "I can't imagine we'd be shopping around for another tool." In the diligence synthesis she is cited (alongside Brosch, Wadhwa, and Abed) as evidence of strong product-led user love in the EMEA scaleup segment.
