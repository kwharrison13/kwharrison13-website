# Byrne Hobart

**One-line:** *Writer (The Diff) and co-author of [Boom — Bubbles & The End of Stagnation](/books/boom-bubbles-and-the-end-of-stagnation). Cited in [We Need a Renaissance of Rhetoric](/essays/we-need-a-renaissance-of-rhetoric) for the argument that technological innovation is driven more by [thymos](/notes/thymos) — "spiritedness," a relentless drive to transcend the listless present — than by rational cost-benefit analysis.*
