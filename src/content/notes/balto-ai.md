# Balto.ai

Balto is a real-time agent-guidance vendor for contact centers, surfaced throughout Kyle's Cresta.ai - Diligence as one of [Cresta.ai](/notes/cresta-ai)'s closest competitors in the "agent assist / conversation intelligence" landscape (alongside [Observe.ai](/notes/observe-ai), [CallMiner](/notes/callminer), [Gong](/notes/gong), and [Chorus](/notes/chorus)). Customer references consistently praised it as the **easiest to implement** (a playbook in ~1 hour of training), the most affordable, and sales-first in origin (it began in sales before moving to contact center). Its standout feature is **in-line phrasing**: Balto checks off a scripted phrase in the pop-up once the agent actually says it — so supervisors *know* it was executed — whereas Cresta and CallMiner show a next-best-action pop-up with 2–3 options the agent "may or may not use, and you never know whether they did."

Diligence guests reported concrete results: an eHealth customer cut supervisors ~50% (≈40 → ~20) after Balto and improved call-volume hit-rates; [Randy Clapp](/notes/randy-clapp) (CRO at Advantage Communications) was a current Balto customer who had evaluated Cresta, [Observe.ai](/notes/observe-ai), and [Gong](/notes/gong). Pricing sat in the ~$20–60/agent/month band (slightly above Observe). Weaknesses noted: it "can't figure out *what* problem you're solving" (you must already know the behavior to improve), less industry depth (strong in home improvement/insurance, weak in telecom/B2C support — a Samsung guest passed for that reason), and a very B2B-sales tilt. [RingCentral](/notes/ringcentral) is described as a lead investor in Balto's latest round, and guests expected a PBX/CCaaS player ([Five9](/notes/five9), Genesys, NICE inContact) or [Medallia](/notes/medallia) to eventually acquire one of Cresta/Balto/Observe.

**Context:** Balto (Balto Software, St. Louis) is a real-time guidance / "RTG" platform for contact-center agents that prompts agents live during calls; it has raised venture funding with RingCentral Ventures among its investors.
