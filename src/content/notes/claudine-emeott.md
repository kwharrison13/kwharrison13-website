# Claudine Emeott

Climate/impact investing lead at [Salesforce Ventures](/notes/salesforce-ventures), where 50–75% of her time is focused on climate investments. Kyle spoke with her on November 1st, 2021 (see November 1st, 2021 — Claudine Emeott (Salesforce Ventures)) about Salesforce Ventures' climate focus, the [Carbon Accounting](/notes/carbon-accounting) landscape, and co-investment appetite. In that call she mapped the competitive field around carbon accounting — naming [Watershed Climate](/notes/watershed-climate) as the most directly competitive solution, Persephone (fresh off a $101M round) as another contender, plus Sinai and Measurable (a 2018 real-estate-focused investment) — and noted Salesforce is itself a customer of [Sylvera](/notes/sylvera). She expressed interest in following up on [Patch](/notes/patch) as a potential investment and partnership, and described the ESG analytics space as "all so frothy." She also appears in Kyle's Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 notes.
