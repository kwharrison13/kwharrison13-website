# Adam

**One-line:** *The first man, formed of the dust of the ground in [Genesis 2](/notes/genesis-2); with [Eve](/notes/eve) he dwells in the [Garden of Eden](/notes/garden-of-eden), partakes of the forbidden fruit, and is sent forth in the Fall.*

## What we know

- Formed of the dust of the ground; God breathed into his nostrils the breath of life and "man became a living soul" ([Genesis 2](/notes/genesis-2):7).
- Placed in the [Garden of Eden](/notes/garden-of-eden) to dress and keep it; commanded not to eat of the tree of knowledge of good and evil ([Genesis 2](/notes/genesis-2):15–17).
- Names every living creature ([Genesis 2](/notes/genesis-2):19–20); [Eve](/notes/eve) is made from his rib ([Genesis 2](/notes/genesis-2):21–23).
- Eats of the fruit with Eve and is sent forth from the garden to till the ground (Genesis 3).
- Father of [Cain](/notes/cain), [Abel](/notes/abel), and [Seth](/notes/seth) (Genesis 4).

## Mentioned in

- [Genesis 2](/notes/genesis-2) — the forming of man and the making of woman
- Genesis 3 — the Fall and expulsion from Eden
- Genesis 4 — father of Cain, Abel, and Seth
- [The Fall of Adam of Eve](/notes/the-fall-of-adam-of-eve) — "Adam fell that men might be"
