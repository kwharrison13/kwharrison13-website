# Stephen Wilson

Senior Director of Customer Success at ON24 (webinar / marketing analytics) and a 20-year Microsoft veteran (Senior Technical Account Manager), with 30+ years in tech and CS, interviewed as expert call #8 in Kyle's Planhat - Diligence dossier. His view anchors the diligence's "incumbents still dominate" thread: before modern tools everything was done on spreadsheets, and today **[Gainsight](/notes/gainsight) and [ToTango](/notes/totango) are still the top two by a fairly large margin**. On [Planhat](/notes/planhat) he said only "heard of it but not used. I know it's a competitor in the market" — a data point Kyle cites as evidence of Planhat's low US brand awareness.

He's willing to pay a premium for a better product, wants a strong **trigger algorithm** (e.g. a trigger when utilization drops) plus strong reporting, and noted both ToTango and Gainsight use "what makes a healthy client" reporting. Notably, he said his team was "looking at it very heavily right now" — actively reconsidering tooling, having been happier initially with Gainsight before moving to ToTango — and would switch for a compelling workflow change or if they couldn't trust the current data.
