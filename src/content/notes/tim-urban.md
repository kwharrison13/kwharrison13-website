# Tim Urban

**One-line:** *Writer behind Wait But Why; source (via [Packy McCormick](/notes/packy-mccormick)) of the "language made ideas composable" passage on compounding ideas.*

## What we know

- [Packy McCormick](/notes/packy-mccormick) quotes Urban's [Neuralink piece](https://waitbutwhy.com/2017/04/neuralink.html): "Language made ideas composable. 'Knowledge, when shared, becomes like a grand, collective, inter-generational collaboration.'" — the backbone of the "Interconnectivity" section of [Open-Source Knowledge](/essays/open-source-knowledge).
