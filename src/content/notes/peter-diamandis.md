# Peter Diamandis

In Kyle's notes, Peter Diamandis surfaces as the author of a LinkedIn "Future of Smart Cities" piece captured in the reading-list material behind [The New City](/notes/the-new-city) — one of several smart-city / city-building sources Kyle was collecting on that theme. The wiki holds only the saved link, not a synthesis of his argument, so the connection is provenance-level: a city-futures author whose work fed Kyle's [The New City](/notes/the-new-city) notes.

**Context:** Peter Diamandis is an engineer and entrepreneur best known as the founder and chairman of the XPRIZE Foundation and co-founder of Singularity University. He writes and speaks widely on exponential technologies and abundance (co-author of *Abundance* and *The Future Is Faster Than You Think*), which is the lens his "Future of Smart Cities" writing comes from.
