# Milton Chen

Milton Chen is the author of [Education Nation](/books/education-nation) and the former executive director of the George Lucas Educational Foundation, which runs [Edutopia](/notes/edutopia). His central argument is that American education's most innovative practices already exist at the "edges" of the system, and that an "Education Nation" is born when those pockets of innovation — [Project-Based Learning](/notes/project-based-learning), one-to-one computing, integrated assessment — converge and move to the center.

His working definition of innovation recurs through the book: "take the best elements of what has been, integrate diverse sources of knowledge and talent, and create a breakthrough that hasn't been imagined before."
