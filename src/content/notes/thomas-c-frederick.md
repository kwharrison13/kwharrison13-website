# Thomas C. Frederick

A mission leader quoted in [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book), Kyle's verbatim collection of quotes gathered during his full-time mission. He is referenced once as "President Thomas C. Frederick" — placing him as a mission president — and appears with two missionary-work exhortations: "I promise, if we'll just follow all the rules and plans, the Harvest will increase. And wouldn't that make missionary work more fun?" and "You are invited to participate in the fulfillment of promises made by ancient and modern prophets, seers, and revelators."

## Where this appears

- [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book) — quoted twice (as "President Thomas C. Frederick"), on obedience to mission rules and on participating in prophetic promises.
