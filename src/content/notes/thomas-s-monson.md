# Thomas S. Monson

LDS Church leader who appears across Kyle's gospel-study notes as a source of often-quoted counsel. In Testimony — Topical Study, Monson supplies the line Kyle uses to frame the reciprocal nature of bearing witness: "That which we willingly share, we keep." In Spiritual Quotes — Greatest Hits — Kyle's running collection centered on LDS leadership — Monson is quoted on education and self-development: "I urge you to pursue your education… Your talents will expand as you study and learn." In [Gordon B. Hinckley: Go Forward With Faith](/books/gordon-b-hinckley-go-forward-with-faith), Monson appears as a fellow leader attesting to [Gordon B. Hinckley](/notes/gordon-b-hinckley)'s breadth: "President Hinckley can converse intelligently about anything with anyone… he is perfectly at home with high and low, and can converse with ease about everything from satellite technology to pruning trees."

He recurs as a cross-reference in Kyle's weekly Come Follow Me pages and topical studies (e.g. Testimony — Topical Study, the Moroni and Ether weeks), and is grouped in the Spiritual Quotes — Greatest Hits connections with the cluster of LDS voices Kyle returns to — [Jeffrey R. Holland](/notes/jeffrey-r-holland), [Boyd K. Packer](/notes/boyd-k-packer), [Dieter F. Uchtdorf](/notes/dieter-f-uchtdorf), [Brigham Young](/notes/brigham-young), and others.

**Context:** Thomas S. Monson (1927–2018) was the 16th President of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, serving from 2008 until his death in 2018. He was widely known for his ministry of personal service and for sermons built around stories of helping individuals.
