# Chao Xu

Chao Xu is VP of Technology at Eta Compute (an IoT/edge-ML company with offices in Texas and China), and one of the expert calls in Kyle's Cresta.ai - Diligence dossier (November 3rd, 2021). Eta Compute's call centers — roughly 250–300 agents across four centers in China and India, with meaningful turnover — handle both US technical/customer support and inbound marketing/sales, and they resell the resulting conversation analytics back to ecommerce customers. Xu's distinctive contribution to the diligence was that Eta Compute ran a **deliberate head-to-head bake-off**: they put [Cresta.ai](/notes/cresta-ai) in two centers and [Observe.ai](/notes/observe-ai) in the other two to compare performance, ease of setup, and required investment.

His numbers were among the most concrete in the dossier: Eta Compute spends **~$300K** on Cresta (which runs on [Five9](/notes/five9) as the underlying CCaaS) and **~$500K** on Observe.ai (more agents on it). He credited Cresta with cutting agent ramp time on Five9 from 3–6 months of shadowing to roughly one month. His stated intent was to **keep two vendors permanently** — a backup is always good, and running more than one preserves pricing/terms negotiating leverage. He picked Cresta mid-2020 and Observe a bit earlier (end of 2019), and remained familiar with [Balto.ai](/notes/balto-ai) as well.
