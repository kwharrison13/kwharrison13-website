# Kapil Gupta

Naval Ravikant recommends "everything by Kapil Gupta, MD" in his book list, adding: "Kapil recently became a personal advisor and coach to me, and this comes from a person who doesn't believe in coaches." Naval also names him (alongside Jed McKenna, the Vashistha Yoga, and Schopenhauer) among the writers he now includes in his regular rereading rotation.
