# SoFi

SoFi is a personal-finance / lending company that recurs across Kyle's notes mostly as a reference playbook and a talent source rather than a deal in its own right. In the Cresta.ai - Diligence, Kyle flagged a TODO to introduce SoFi to [Pathlight](/notes/pathlight) and other performance-management vendors on his list, treating it as an intro target. In the [CleanCapital](/notes/cleancapital) notes, SoFi is the inspiration for the business model: CleanCapital was framed as applying "how SoFi streamlined loan refinancing" to clean-energy finance — the loan-refinancing playbook translated into a new asset class.

SoFi also surfaces as a talent-pedigree marker. In the June 24th, 2021 — Eric Menees (TradeUp) call, TradeUp's Business Development leader ("Bob") is noted as one of SoFi's first ~30 employees, who later built out the business-development process at Meritize before being recruited to TradeUp.

**Context:** SoFi (Social Finance, Inc.) is a U.S. fintech that began with student-loan refinancing and expanded into personal loans, mortgages, brokerage, and banking; it went public in 2021.
