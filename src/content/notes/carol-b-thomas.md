# Carol B. Thomas

Carol B. Thomas appears in Kyle's Humility — Topical Study, quoted twice. On baptism and the change wrought by the [Holy Ghost](/notes/holy-ghost): "Because you have received the Holy Ghost, you are changed. You are a different person. It means you cannot be part of the world anymore; you can NEVER go back." And on the path beyond it: "So how do we do it? How can we become more holy so we can claim our royal heritage?" — the question Kyle uses to pivot the study from baptism toward seeking [Sanctification](/notes/sanctification) and the royal-heritage theme of the son of King Louis XVI.

**Context:** Carol B. Thomas served as a counselor in the Young Women general presidency of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints from 1997 to 2002.

## Where this appears

- Humility — Topical Study — quoted on the irreversible change of receiving the Holy Ghost at baptism, and on how to grow holier to claim one's "royal heritage."
