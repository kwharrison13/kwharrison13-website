# Toby Halford

A fellow missionary or mission leader quoted in [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book), Kyle's verbatim collection of quotes gathered during his full-time mission. Recorded as "Elder Toby Halford" on page 43, with a one-word answer to a recurring missionary question — "What is the purpose of life? BAPTISMILIZING!" — an exuberant, invented coinage that fits the missionary-zeal tone of that stretch of the quote book.
