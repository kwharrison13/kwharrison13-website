# Oura

A consumer health-sensor company, grouped in [HealthOS](/notes/healthos) alongside [WHOOP](/notes/whoop) and [Eight Sleep](/notes/eight-sleep) as one of the sensor specialists Apple is content to be "good enough" against while deepening its own health-data moat via the Health app and HealthKit API.

**Context:** Oura makes the Oura Ring, a finger-worn wearable that tracks sleep, heart rate, temperature, and recovery.

## Where this appears

- [HealthOS](/notes/healthos) — cited with WHOOP and Eight Sleep as a third-party sensor maker building on Apple's platform while Apple keeps the data layer.
