# Contrary Growth

A proposed growth/opportunity fund that Kyle and [Saanya Ojha](/notes/saanya-ojha) brainstormed in November 2021 as an expansion of Contrary's strategy beyond its early-stage roots. The core challenge they articulated: "We can't win if we play the same game as we're playing right now at 1/100th the scale — clearly articulated differentiation is a must." The brainstorm was prep for a conversation with [Eric Tarczynski](/notes/eric-tarczynski), structured around what they knew to be true, open questions, deal types, "why we win," and a proposed fund structure.

The open strategic questions centered on the anatomy of a winning growth deal (opportunity-first vs. person-first), how to handle portfolio conflicts (e.g. Jeeves vs. Ramp as comps), whether deals from Contrary's early-stage networks could be won on price and speed, and exposure to crypto tokens. The contemplated structure ran around a $150M "Fund IV" and a $150–300M "Opportunity Fund I," alongside frank personal and career questions for both Kyle and Saanya — exit strategy, compensation (starting at $250K with a possible step-up at close), and whether the move was worth doing primarily for the chance to work together. The notes are wholly private and idiosyncratic to Kyle's career deliberations at Contrary.
