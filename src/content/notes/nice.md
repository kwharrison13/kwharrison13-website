# NICE

NICE is a contact-center software incumbent that surfaces in Kyle's Cresta.ai - Diligence in two roles. First, NICE inContact appears in the [CCaaS](/notes/ccaas) list — the cloud contact-center platforms ([Five9](/notes/five9), [Genesys](/notes/genesys), [Amazon Connect](/notes/amazon-connect), NICE inContact) that several experts expect to consolidate the real-time agent-assist category by buying or building into it; in Randy Clapp's call, "PBX/CCaaS players (NICE inContact, Five9, Genesys PureCloud)" are explicitly named as the likely acquirers of one of Cresta / [Balto.ai](/notes/balto-ai) / [Observe.ai](/notes/observe-ai). Second, NICE IEX shows up concretely as a deployed workforce-management tool: in the Chris Purpura (Neiman Marcus) call, NICE IEX was the scheduling/WFM (AQM) system Neiman Marcus was switching away from to Calabrio "because NICE doesn't work well with [Twilio](/notes/twilio-flex)."

So in the notes NICE plays the dual part of (a) a CCaaS/WFM incumbent already entrenched in contact-center stacks, and (b) part of the looming platform-consolidation bear case hanging over standalone agent-assist vendors like Cresta.

**Context:** NICE Ltd. (NASDAQ: NICE) is an Israel-founded enterprise software company whose contact-center portfolio includes the NICE inContact (CXone) cloud platform and NICE workforce-management/quality tools (IEX). It is one of the largest contact-center and CCaaS vendors alongside Genesys and Five9.
