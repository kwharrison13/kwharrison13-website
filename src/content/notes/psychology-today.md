# Psychology Today

In Kyle's behavioral-health diligence notes, Psychology Today functions as the default therapist-directory and lead-generation channel that companies like [Grow Therapy](/notes/grow-therapy) build around. As part of its "one-stop-shop" for therapists, Grow markets providers by paying for their Psychology Today profile — making the directory a customer-acquisition surface Grow operates on its providers' behalf (with a noted problem over which name/brand got listed on the profile). The October 2021 behavioral-health expert notes use it as the canonical example of how supply brings its own demand: therapists list "accepts Aetna" on Psychology Today, and the insurance-enablement startups haven't yet built the patient-demand side that the directory already aggregates.

**Context:** Psychology Today operates one of the largest online therapist-finder directories in the US, where clinicians pay a monthly listing fee to be discoverable by prospective clients filtering on insurance, specialty, and location. (General background; Kyle's notes attest its role as a lead-gen/listing channel in behavioral-health diligence.)
