# Andy Weir

Science-fiction author who appears in Kyle's [Historical Futurism](/essays/historical-futurism) research as one of the writers in his roster of futurist/sci-fi voices, listed alongside figures like [Arthur C. Clarke](/notes/arthur-c-clarke), [Isaac Asimov](/notes/isaac-asimov), [William Gibson](/notes/william-gibson), [Ursula K. Le Guin](/notes/ursula-k-le-guin), and [Ted Chiang](/notes/ted-chiang). He is catalogued there as part of the "people" feeding the historical-futurism theme — the body of imaginative, forward-looking fiction Kyle draws on — rather than discussed at length.

**Context:** Andy Weir is the American novelist best known for *The Martian* (2011), *Artemis*, and *Project Hail Mary*, associated with hard, scientifically-grounded science fiction.
