# Strikedeck

In the Planhat - Diligence expert calls, Strikedeck is positioned as an older [Customer Success](/notes/customer-success) tool that lacked the deep technical and data-mapping configurability the newer platforms offer. Tim McWhirk (VP Customer Success at Grin, ex-Director of CS Ops at Pendo.io) — who had been a Strikedeck customer — grouped it with [ChurnZero](/notes/churnzero) and [ToTango](/notes/totango) as tools that "couldn't do the deep technical tweaks (data mapping, lead-scoring formula changes) inside the application." It functions in the diligence as a contrast point illustrating where legacy CS tools fall short relative to [Gainsight](/notes/gainsight) and the modern challengers like Planhat and [Catalyst](/notes/catalyst).

**Context:** Strikedeck was a customer-success automation platform founded in 2015; it was acquired by Medallia in 2020.
