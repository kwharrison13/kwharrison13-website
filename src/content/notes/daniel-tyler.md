# Daniel Tyler

Appears in [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book) as the source attributed for a teaching of [Joseph Smith](/notes/joseph-smith) on being "born again" (John 3:3) — the passage distinguishing seeing the kingdom of God from entering into it, framed as a change of heart but not yet of state. The quote is cited "[Joseph Smith](/notes/joseph-smith) via [Daniel Tyler](/notes/daniel-tyler)," indicating Tyler as the one who recorded or relayed the teaching.

**Context:** Daniel Tyler (1816–1906) was an early Latter-day Saint who recorded recollections of Joseph Smith's teachings, frequently cited in later LDS gospel-study material.

## Where this appears

- [Chad's Big Quote Book](/notes/chad-s-big-quote-book) — cited as the recorder of a [Joseph Smith](/notes/joseph-smith) teaching on baptism and being born again
