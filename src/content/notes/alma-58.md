# Alma 58

A chapter of the Book of Mormon falling within the Come Follow Me reading block for August 10–16, 2020 (Alma 53–63). It is listed in that study's chapter range, but Kyle's notes for that week cover [Alma 53](/notes/alma-53) through [Alma 57](/notes/alma-57) only — there is no annotation specific to Alma 58 in the source.

**Context:** In the Book of Mormon narrative, Alma 58 continues [Helaman](/notes/helaman)'s epistle, recounting how the Nephite armies — short on provisions and reinforcements — pray for deliverance and are filled with assurance that God will deliver them.
