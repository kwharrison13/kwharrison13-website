# Brigit

Brigit appears in Kyle's notes as the prior employer of [Rishi Ahuja](/notes/rishi-ahuja), an expert source in the Sprig (fka UserLeap) diligence. Ahuja was Brigit's first product manager (03/2020–03/2021), where he helped build V1 of Credit Builder, before moving on to a PM role at [Square](/notes/square). That is the full extent of what the source attests — Brigit is a line in Ahuja's bio rather than a diligence subject in its own right.

**Context:** Brigit is a New York–based consumer fintech app offering cash advances, overdraft protection, and credit-building tools aimed at users living paycheck to paycheck; its Credit Builder product reports an installment-loan/savings account to the bureaus to help users raise their credit scores.
