# SuperRare

A curated NFT marketplace on Ethereum, cited as a competitor and comparable in the FormFn seed investment-committee memo. The memo uses an analogy to position the market: "FormFn is Etsy; OpenSea is Amazon; SuperRare and Foundation are Sotheby's and Christie's" — i.e. SuperRare is the high-end, curated, gatekept tier where independent artists pay hundreds of dollars to list. It's used as proof of demand for curated 1/1 art: SuperRare reached $208M in sales in 2021 ($22.2M in the trailing 30 days at the time), evidence that creators will pay premium fees for curation. FormFn's thesis was that those numbers "could be so much bigger" by moving to Solana to reach the average, less-capitalized graphic artist. On the competitive positioning map, FormFn placed itself against SuperRare/Foundation (Ethereum) and Objkt.com (Tezos).

**Context:** SuperRare is an Ethereum-based marketplace founded in 2018 for single-edition ("1/1") digital artworks, known for an invite-only, heavily curated artist roster — positioning itself as the premium end of the NFT art market.
