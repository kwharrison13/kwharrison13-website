# The Priesthood and the Savior's Atoning Power

A General Conference talk by [Dale G. Renlund](/notes/dale-g-renlund) ([General Conference: October 2017](/notes/general-conference-october-2017)) that Kyle studied alongside Alma 13–16. From his notes: the [Atonement](/notes/atonement) gave [Jesus Christ](/notes/jesus) the "power and authority to redeem us; otherwise we would be infinitely lost," and the [Priesthood](/notes/priesthood) "permits priesthood holders to help Heavenly Father and Jesus Christ in Their work — to bring about the salvation and exaltation of God's children." Kyle's takeaway is that God does not strictly *need* us to use the priesthood to share the gospel or perform ordinances for the dead — "he could find a way without us" — but blesses us with the opportunity to "come along for the ride."

**Context:** Dale G. Renlund is a member of the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints; this talk was delivered at the October 2017 General Conference.
