# Charles Limb

Charles Limb is the researcher whose brain scans of improvising musicians appear in [Just Flow With It](/notes/just-flow-with-it): during improvisation the self-expression network lit up while the self-censoring network quieted — "as if the brain turned off its own ability to criticize itself."

**Context:** Charles Limb is a physician and neuroscientist known for fMRI studies of jazz musicians and rappers during improvisation, showing deactivation of the brain's self-monitoring regions and increased activity in areas linked to self-expression and flow.
