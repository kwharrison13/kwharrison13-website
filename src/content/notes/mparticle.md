# mParticle

**One-line definition:** *Real-time [Customer Data Platform](/notes/customer-data-platform) — the data-infrastructure "silo-buster" layer brands were otherwise building themselves. Mobile-leaning; seen as one half of a "2-horse CDI race" with [Segment](/notes/segment).*

## How sources describe it

- Founded by the team behind adtech business **InterClick** (IPO'd 2009, acquired by Yahoo for $300M in 2011); CEO [Michael Katz](/notes/michael-katz). Originally called itself "data automation" before the CDP category existed.
- ~120 customers, ~$20M run-rate, ~100% YoY growth, ~150% net retention (Oct 2018); MAU-based pricing; focused on "biggest and best" brands (Walmart, Spotify, Airbnb, EA). See mParticle — Diligence (2018).
