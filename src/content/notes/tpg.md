# TPG

TPG surfaces across Kyle's notes as a private-equity firm with a notably active ESG/sustainability practice. In the Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021, a Philz Coffee operator recounts that TPG — an investor in Philz — toured the company's coffee-roasting plant after investing, that she spent significant time with "the [TPG](/notes/tpg)'s ESG team," and that the firm has "quite a focus in helping their portfolio companies"; she ultimately joined TPG's sustainable leadership council to make Philz more sustainable. The diligence also flags TPG as a co-lead (with Prelude) on Persefoni's $101M Series B, a competing climate-accounting platform. Separately, in [On the Nature of Long-Term Holds](/notes/on-the-nature-of-long-term-holds), TPG appears as part of [Dave Whorton](/notes/dave-whorton)'s background: before founding the [Tugboat Institute](/notes/tugboat-institute), Whorton invested with Kleiner Perkins and TPG Ventures.

**Context:** TPG Inc. (NASDAQ: TPG) is a large global alternative-asset manager headquartered in Fort Worth and San Francisco, with private equity, growth, impact (TPG Rise), and credit strategies; its impact/ESG platform is among the largest in private markets.
