# InsideTrack

InsideTrack (also written "Inside Track") appears in Kyle's notes as a portfolio company / theme of [Strada Education Network](/notes/strada-education-network), the education-focused nonprofit and investor (formerly USA Funds). In the May 2018 sourcing call with Andre Bennin of Strada, InsideTrack is listed alongside Emsi, AdmitHub, RoadTrip Nation, and Project Retention as part of Strada's student-lifecycle portfolio — specifically tied to the **student success / coaching** theme, the part of Strada's thesis that was shifting toward workforce transition and skills for "the work of the future." It has no standalone treatment in the wiki beyond that portfolio-company provenance.

**Context:** InsideTrack is a nonprofit organization known for student-success coaching, providing one-on-one coaching and advising programs to help students persist and complete higher education.
