# ChurnZero

ChurnZero recurs throughout the Planhat - Diligence expert calls as one of the established players in the [Customer Success](/notes/customer-success) software landscape, typically grouped with [ToTango](/notes/totango) as an upper/mid-market "pure CS" tool sitting just below [Gainsight](/notes/gainsight) and ToTango in tenure and mindshare. Experts placed its sweet spot at roughly the 200–1,000 FTE range: at ~100 FTE a buyer might choose [Planhat](/notes/planhat) or [Vitally](/notes/vitally), but bigger companies were steered toward ToTango or ChurnZero. Multiple interviewees named ChurnZero among the US incumbents "hammering" them with outreach (alongside Gainsight and ToTango), underscoring that — unlike Planhat — it has strong US brand awareness.

On pricing, one EMEA expert reported an Austrian client at €200K MRR spending €25K/year on ChurnZero, within a broader CS-tool range of ~€10K to €40–50K. Several CS leaders had evaluated or previously bought ChurnZero before moving to or alongside other tools (Gainsight, [Catalyst](/notes/catalyst), [Custify](/notes/custify)), positioning it as a credible but not category-defining option.

**Context:** *ChurnZero is a Washington, DC–based customer-success software platform aimed at subscription and SaaS businesses, providing health scores, automated "plays," and churn-prediction tooling for customer-success teams; it serves primarily the mid-market.*
