# Michael Miao

An investor at [IVP](/notes/ivp) and a [Dropbox](/notes/dropbox) alumnus who appears across Kyle's essay research as a survey respondent. He is listed among the [Dropbox](/notes/dropbox) contacts Kyle surveyed for [The Talent Vortex — Mafias and Magnets](/essays/talent-vortex) — operators tapped to explain why companies like Dropbox, [Stripe](/notes/stripe), and [Plaid](/notes/plaid) concentrate talent. In the survey for The Death of a Venture Fund, asked what would most likely kill his firm, Miao answered (as an IVP investor): "We get too skittish or valuation-sensitive during this downturn and cede market share to other growth investors. Basically lose optimism." He is also among the IVP respondents canvassed for The Productization of Venture Capital.
