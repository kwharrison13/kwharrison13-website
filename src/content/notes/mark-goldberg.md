# Mark Goldberg

A venture investor at [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) and the partner Kyle worked under during his Index era. Their relationship traces back to a cold email Kyle sent — one Goldberg singled out (per the September 13th, 2022 — Harry Stebbings Podcast prep) as "the best cold email ever," good enough that it led Goldberg to hire Kyle roughly three years later; the email worked because it pointed to interests and companies Goldberg already cared about, drawn from reading his writing. Goldberg was Kyle's direct source of deal flow and feedback: he brought Kyle the [Anrok](/notes/anrok) brief ("founder we know, big market, exposed incumbent"; November 8th, 2021 — Mark Goldberg (Anrok)) and forwarded the TradeUp seed-extension email about bridging a $100M ISA credit facility (June 22nd, 2021 — Mark Goldberg on TradeUp Seed Extension).

He also delivered Kyle's sharpest career feedback. In the September 22nd, 2021 — Mark Goldberg (Career Feedback) 1:1, Goldberg told Kyle the work was "too junior" and wasn't moving the sponsor's conviction on deals — the push being to take ownership, lead with synthesis and a crisp "elevator pitch," and especially to **source net-new growth deals** ("you're more classically trained in growth investing than anyone on the team"), citing the missed [Ramp](/notes/ramp) markup as the kind of conviction-driven sourcing he wanted. On the diligence side he appears in the Pave Diligence — 2021 dossier (the Index Series C pre-empt of Pave/Trove) and convened the partnership for the December 13th, 2021 — NCX Partner Presentation.

**Context:** Mark Goldberg is a fintech-focused venture investor; he was a partner at Index Ventures before co-founding the early-stage fund Chemistry. (Kyle's notes attest his Index tenure and fintech focus directly; the post-Index move is general background.)
