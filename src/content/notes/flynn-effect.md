# Flynn Effect

**One-line:** *The consistent generation-over-generation rise in IQ scores since at least the 1930s — which started to slow, and in some cases reverse, around 2006. Used in [We Need a Renaissance of Rhetoric](/essays/we-need-a-renaissance-of-rhetoric) as evidence that cognitive off-loading may already be eroding human intelligence ([Robert Sternberg](/notes/robert-sternberg)).*
