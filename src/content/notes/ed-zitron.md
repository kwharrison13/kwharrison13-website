# Ed Zitron

**One-line:** *Tech commentator and author of the "Where's Your Ed At" newsletter, known for combative, deeply-researched criticism of the AI industry's finances and hype.*

## What we know

- Writes a premium newsletter (per his own description, "usually anywhere from 5,000 to 18,000 words") with detailed financial analyses of NVIDIA, Anthropic, and OpenAI, and a running series on "the AI bubble." [Let AI Burn](/notes/let-ai-burn)
- In [Let AI Burn](/notes/let-ai-burn), argues the AI industry should receive "no bailouts, no handouts, no special treatment, no tax breaks, no CHIPS act, and no sovereign wealth fund" when it eventually fails, calling generative AI economically inessential and its demand largely a product of subsidized pricing and manufactured hype rather than organic demand.
- Estimates that OpenAI and Anthropic together represent roughly 89% of the revenue of the largest AI companies, and that removing them leaves the rest of the AI industry pulling in roughly $20 billion in annual revenue.

## Mentioned in

- [Let AI Burn](/notes/let-ai-burn) — his central thesis piece arguing the AI industry is a bubble that should be allowed to collapse without government intervention.
