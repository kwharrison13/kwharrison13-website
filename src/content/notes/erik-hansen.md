# Erik Hansen

A conversation partner of Kyle's whose wide-ranging discussion on work, ambition, and American decline became the seed braindump for the essay [The Hardening Of The Great Softening](/essays/the-hardening-of-the-great-softening) (captured in The Hardening Of The Great Softening - Research). The "Conversation with Erik Hansen" notes range across the [Keynes](/notes/john-maynard-keynes) 15-hour week prediction, [Primitivism](/notes/primitivism), [American exceptionalism](/notes/american-exceptionalism-on-trial), [temporarily embarrassed millionaires](/notes/temporarily-embarrassed-millionaires), the [Veil of Ignorance](/notes/veil-of-ignorance), and whether the American dream is uncapped upside or a stable floor.

Erik also surfaces in Kyle's religious reading. He recommended [Law of the Harvest](/books/law-of-the-harvest) (David G. Stewart's data-driven critique of LDS missionary retention), and in Kyle's notes on [Reinventing Knowledge — From Alexandria To The Internet](/books/reinventing-knowledge-from-alexandria-to-the-internet) Kyle ties the principle "we accept all truth no matter the source" to "Erik Hansen's stuff" — suggesting Erik is a recurring source for Kyle's thinking on truth-seeking across secular and spiritual domains.
