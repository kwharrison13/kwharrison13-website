# Simon Hauger

Simon Hauger is the educator whose experience with career academies is cited in [Education Nation](/books/education-nation) to illustrate how hard it is to integrate rigorous academic and technical curricula with workplace internships under traditional schedules.

## Where this appears

- [Education Nation](/books/education-nation) — career-academy experience illustrating the difficulty of integrating school and workplace learning.
