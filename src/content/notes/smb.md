# SMB

**One-line definition:** *Small and medium-sized businesses — the asset class Teamshares buys (often $2–5M, e.g. doctors, dentists, small franchisees), recurringly discussed as the best asset class to be in.*

## How sources describe it

- The target asset class for [Teamshares](/notes/teamshares) and related roll-up strategies; small owner-operated businesses with no real management bench, where transition is the major risk (per the March 5th, 2020 diligence calls).
