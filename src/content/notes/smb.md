# SMB

**One-line definition:** *Small and medium-sized businesses — the asset class Teamshares buys (often $2–5M, e.g. doctors, dentists, small franchisees), recurringly discussed as the best asset class to be in.*

## How sources describe it

- The target asset class for [Teamshares](/notes/teamshares) and related roll-up strategies; small owner-operated businesses with no real management bench, where transition is the major risk (per the March 5th, 2020 diligence calls).

## Related concepts

- [Teamshares](/notes/teamshares) — the firm whose strategy is buying SMBs (often $2–5M) as its core asset class.
- [Private Equity](/notes/private-equity) — the broader category for the roll-up strategies referenced as buying up SMBs.
- [Capital Allocation](/notes/capital-allocation) — the lens under which SMBs are framed as "the best asset class to be in."
- [Moses Kagan](/notes/moses-kagan) — participant in the March 5th, 2020 Teamshares diligence calls discussing the SMB asset class.
- [JD Rasmussen](/notes/jd-rasmussen) — participant in the March 5th, 2020 Teamshares diligence calls discussing the SMB asset class.
