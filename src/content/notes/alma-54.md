# Alma 54

A chapter of the Book of Mormon (Alma 53–63 — Preserved by His Marvelous Power reading block) covering the exchange of epistles between [Moroni](/notes/moroni) and the Lamanite leader Ammoron over a prisoner exchange. In Kyle's Come Follow Me study, the verse he flagged is Alma 54:22, where Moroni taunts Ammoron — "if it so be that there is a devil and a hell, behold will he not send you there to dwell with my brother whom ye have murdered… But behold these things matter not." Kyle's note paraphrases the logical knot Moroni ties: "There's no such thing as hell, but if there is you'll probably go there with my brother whose in hell. But he's not in hell. Cause there is no hell. So…yeah." It's logged under the heading "Nice One" — a moment of dry rhetorical wit in the war chapters rather than a doctrinal theme.
