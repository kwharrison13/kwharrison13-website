# Gene R. Cook

A General Authority Seventy of [The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints](/notes/the-church-of-jesus-christ-of-latter-day-saints). In Kyle's corpus he appears as the author of Moroni's Promise — Gene R. Cook (Ensign, April 1994), a close reading of Moroni 10:3–5 arguing that the Book of Mormon's promise teaches a repeatable process for confirming *all* truth — remember God's mercy, *receive* and ponder, then ask in faith for confirmation.

## Where this appears

- Moroni's Promise — Gene R. Cook — author; the three-verse exegesis of Moroni 10:3–5 and the account of "Brother Smith," a Protestant minister converted through that process.
