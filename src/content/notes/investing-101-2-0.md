# Investing 101 2.0

**One-line definition:** *Kyle's investing publication and framework — a first-principles project to learn the fundamentals of investing (101) while tracking the ways the practice is being reinvented (2.0), thought through in public.*

## How sources describe it

- Its own stated mission: "Investing 101 2.0 is my occasional home for reflecting on all things investing. The overall theme here is one of first-principles: Learning the fundamentals of investing (101) while looking for the ways it's being reinvented (2.0)." [Networked Conviction — Roam + Investing](/essays/networked-conviction-roam-investing)
- A method built on a tool: Kyle uses Roam Research to "collect ideas, question them, find companies around those ideas, and ask the same sets of questions. And then I share what I learn" — with a public **Investing 101 2.0 Sandbox** of templates. [Networked Conviction — Roam + Investing](/essays/networked-conviction-roam-investing)
- A beginner's-mind premise: "reinventing the practice in the process of learning it," with investing defined as "the art and science of allocating finite resources to create an optimal outcome." [The Art and Science of Investing](/essays/the-art-and-science-of-investing)
- The "2.0" agenda is concrete — exploring reinvention "through data in investing, bifurcation of investment firms, solo capitalists, equity crowdfunding, diversity & inclusion, democratized secondary transactions, or triple-bottom-line investing." [Networked Conviction — Roam + Investing](/essays/networked-conviction-roam-investing)
- Sits on a learning-in-public thesis — "what other types of work can you open source?" reframes investment research as something to share rather than hoard. [Remote — Office Not Required](/books/remote-office-not-required)
- Book pages from across the library are tagged into it as case studies — Amazon's operating philosophy, the VC power law, public maintenance of ideas. [The Everything Store](/books/the-everything-store), [The Power Law — Venture Capital & the Making of the New Future](/books/the-power-law-venture-capital-and-the-making-of-the-new-future), [Working in Public](/books/working-in-public)
