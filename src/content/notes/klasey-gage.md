# Klasey Gage

Klasey Gage is Head of Global Customer Support at [Adobe](/notes/adobe), where he appears in Kyle's notes as an expert-call source (November 16th, 2021) for the [Cresta.ai](/notes/cresta-ai) diligence (Cresta.ai - Diligence). He runs an Adobe support org of roughly 3,000 agents (including BPOs across India, Europe, and JPAC) handling ~8M conversations/year, the majority digital (70% chat/messaging, 30% voice). Adobe uses Cresta on the chat side only. Before Adobe he spent 13 years at a cloud contact-center solution built on and acquired by [Genesys](/notes/genesys), and was previously Head of Apple Professional Services; the diligence notes record his earlier path as ex-Genesys and ex-Apple.

His assessment of Cresta was the careful-buyer view: he liked that Cresta applies ML/AI to unstructured conversation data and pursues an ROI-led sales motion, but flagged it as "a bit of a black box" Adobe can't self-configure, with agent trust being the hardest piece ("like Tesla's driverless car"). Pricing was transactional/per-interaction; Adobe was ~40% rolled out on a six-figure contract, with full rollout pegged at $1M+ once ROI is proven. His framing — clear ROI on save-rate use cases (e.g. Creative Cloud cancellation retention) but harder-to-quantify ROI elsewhere — fed the diligence's "replaceability today" and self-serve-control concerns, alongside his blunt note that the Cresta product team rated 7/10 but the sales team was "borderline toxic."
