# Washington Post

The Washington Post enters the wiki only through [Jeff Bezos](/notes/jeff-bezos), who is described as its current owner alongside his roles founding [Amazon](/notes/amazon) and [Blue Origin](/notes/blue-origin). In the notes from [Amazon Unbound](/books/amazon-unbound), the paper is flagged as an open question rather than a developed thread: how Bezos's ownership of the Post influenced his operating playbook in the news industry. The book introduces the ownership "only superficially in the sections read," so the wiki currently has the connection but not the substance behind it.

**Context:** The Washington Post is a major American daily newspaper founded in 1877. Jeff Bezos purchased it personally (not through Amazon) in 2013 for $250 million.
