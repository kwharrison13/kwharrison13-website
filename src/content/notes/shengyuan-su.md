# Shengyuan Su

Shengyuan Su appears once in Kyle's notes, in the Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 dossier, as a sustainability contact at [Salesforce ($CRM)](/notes/salesforce). During Index Ventures' Series B diligence on [Watershed Climate](/notes/watershed-climate) (late 2021), Su was one of roughly a dozen corporate-sustainability leads Kyle emailed for reference/expert calls — part of the outbound outreach to heads of sustainability at large companies to understand how enterprises were thinking about their ESG and carbon-accounting programs. The notes record only the outreach itself, not the outcome of any conversation.
