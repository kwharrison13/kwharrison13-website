# Ken Liu

Science-fiction author noted in Kyle's Historical Futurism - Research as the host of [Story Summit](/notes/story-summit) (run via Alexandria Labs). He appears in the research's catalog of writers and futurists — alongside figures like [Ted Chiang](/notes/ted-chiang), [Ursula K. Le Guin](/notes/ursula-k-le-guin), [William Gibson](/notes/william-gibson), and [Andy Weir](/notes/andy-weir) — whose work feeds the essay's throughline about science fiction translating dreams of the future back to the everyday person.

**Context:** Ken Liu is an American author and translator best known for the Dandelion Dynasty "silkpunk" epic-fantasy series and short fiction including "The Paper Menagerie." He translated Liu Cixin's *The Three-Body Problem* into English.
