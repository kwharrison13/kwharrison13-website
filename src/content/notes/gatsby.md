# Gatsby

A company that appears in Kyle's Pave Diligence — 2021 notes only as a named customer of [Welcome (Comp Planning)](/notes/welcome-comp-planning), one of Pave's direct compensation-planning competitors. It surfaces in the competitive-landscape mapping (alongside [Mercury](/notes/mercury) and [Unqork](/notes/unqork) as other Welcome customers) and carries no further annotation in the source — a data point on which logos competing comp tools had landed, rather than a subject Kyle studied in its own right.

**Context:** Gatsby is best known as the company behind the open-source Gatsby.js React framework for building websites; there are also unrelated startups using the name, so the exact entity here is uncertain from the notes alone.
