# D&C 78

Section 78 of the Doctrine and Covenants appears in Kyle's notes as a **"be of good cheer, I will lead you along"** text. He cites D&C 78:17–18 in his study of Russell M. Nelson's "Christ Is Risen" and again in his Alma 30–31 study: "ye are little children, and ye have not as yet understood how great blessings the Father hath... ye cannot bear all things now; nevertheless, be of good cheer, for I will lead you along. The kingdom is yours and the blessings thereof are yours, and the riches of eternity are yours." Kyle reads it as a reassurance about gradual spiritual growth and the eternal inheritance promised to the faithful.
