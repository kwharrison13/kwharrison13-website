# 2 Corinthians 9

Paul's chapter on cheerful, willing giving. In Kyle's Fasting - September 5, 2021 study, it is cited for verses 6–7 under the heading of teaching one's family to give offerings willingly and cheerfully: "He which soweth sparingly shall reap also sparingly... Every man according as he purposeth in his heart, so let him give; not grudgingly, or of necessity: for God loveth a cheerful giver."

**Context:** 2 Corinthians 9, written by the Apostle [Paul the Apostle](/notes/paul-the-apostle), continues chapter 8's appeal for the collection for the saints, urging giving that is generous and freely chosen — the source of the often-quoted "God loveth a cheerful giver" (v. 7).
