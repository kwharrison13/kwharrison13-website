# Eamon Jubbawy

Co-founder and Chief Operating Officer of [Onfido](/notes/onfido), and a customer reference in Kyle's Watershed Climate Diligence — 2021 (an October 28th, 2021 reference call). Per the diligence notes, he founded Onfido in 2012, was an early pioneer in face-recognition verification, and runs the company's day-to-day leadership and general management as it scaled internationally; he also served as Deputy Chair on the UK Fintech Delivery Panel and sells into fintech businesses across 200+ countries. On Watershed specifically, he described the carbon-accounting adoption starting in the management team — his CEO cared, and the Chief Product Officer cared in particular about climate and ESG. He also noted Onfido's reliance on a human component, since new versions of identity documents keep emerging and human analysts are needed to understand them before feeding the machines.
