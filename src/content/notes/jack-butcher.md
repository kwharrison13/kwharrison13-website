# Jack Butcher

The illustrator of *The Almanack of Naval Ravikant* — his artwork accompanies Naval's ideas throughout the book and is also collected on Navalmanack.com and his own site, Visualize Value. Editor Eric Jorgenson's acknowledgments describe Butcher reaching out to offer his illustrations for the project and call his Visualize Value work "simple genius."
