# NEPC

NEPC is an investment-consulting / allocator firm that surfaces in Kyle's notes purely as a fundraising relationship for Contrary Capital. On April 5th, 2022, [Scott Hutchins](/notes/scott-hutchins) at [Solamere Capital](/notes/solamere-capital) emailed an introduction connecting Kyle to [Josh Beers](/notes/josh-beers) at NEPC (jbeers@nepc.com) regarding Contrary's first growth fund — Hutchins framed it as "they are raising their first growth fund and I think extremely highly of Kyle and the strategy at Contrary." NEPC is treated here as a potential LP / allocator channel, with [Josh Beers](/notes/josh-beers) as the contact on its side.

**Context:** NEPC, LLC is a large independent investment-consulting firm (headquartered in Boston) that advises institutional investors — pensions, endowments, foundations, and family offices — on asset allocation and manager selection. Consultants like NEPC act as gatekeepers and allocators that emerging managers court when raising new funds.
