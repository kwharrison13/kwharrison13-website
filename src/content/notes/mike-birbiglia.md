# Mike Birbiglia

**One-line:** *One of Kyle's favorite comedians; his Lincoln bit surfaced the "Whatever you are, be a good one" line that titles [The Rising Generation](/essays/the-rising-generation).*

## What we know

- Kyle credits a Birbiglia joke — riffing on Abraham Lincoln quotes in a D.C. hotel ("'Whatever you are, be a good one.' I just don't feel like he should get credit for generalities like that. 'How Are Ya?' — Abraham Lincoln.") — for bringing the saying to his attention. The line became the essay's subtitle. (source: The Rising Generation - Research)
