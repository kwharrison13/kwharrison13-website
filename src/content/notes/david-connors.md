# David Connors

David Connors is the founder of [The Swarm](/notes/the-swarm), a [Contrary Capital](/notes/contrary) portfolio company that Kyle tracked through its 2022 investor updates. Across those updates, Connors moves The Swarm from a **network recruiting** product (waitlist of 275+ startups and 65+ funded companies, CTO [Michal Bil](/notes/michal-bil) hired, MVP launched to a "friends of the Swarm" cohort) toward a broader vision: leading a new "Network Relationship Management" (NRM) category spanning go-to-market, investing, and recruiting.

His updates also read as macro commentary: in the October 2022 update (captured in the November 7th, 2022 — Cleared Email digest) Connors cited the Fed, Goldman Sachs, Bloomberg, and JP Morgan to warn that a multi-year US recession would impact the country by mid-2023 and likely persist for 2+ years.
