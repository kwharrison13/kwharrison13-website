# Sylvera

Sylvera is a carbon-offset ratings company building what its own pitch calls "the first trust infrastructure for the carbon offsets market." It surfaces throughout Kyle's 2021 [Index Ventures](/notes/index-ventures) climate diligence: Index invested at seed in January 2021 ($2.5M for 13%), and the November 2021 Series A pre-brief from [Julia Andre](/notes/julia-andre) and Carlos proposed co-leading a $25M round at a $125M post (~$13M from Index → ~20% blended ownership), moving fast ahead of pre-emptive offers from [Coatue](/notes/coatue) and [Balderton Capital](/notes/balderton-capital) (November 5th, 2021 — Sylvera Pre-Brief (Julia Andre)). The thesis: nature-based offsets (REDD+ avoided-deforestation and ARR reforestation credits) will supply the bulk of a market that could be worth $50B+ by 2030, but the market lacks measurement, verification, and rating infrastructure — "today there is literally nothing to effectively grade these offsets," which makes credits non-fungible and impossible to price apples-to-apples (November 5th, 2021 — Sylvera Call).

Sylvera's product ingests satellite imagery plus proprietary Lidar ground-truth data (collected with NASA/JPL, UCLA, UCL) and deep-learning models to produce ratings delivered via API and SaaS, serving corporate buyers, project developers, and carbon exchanges. A core strategic stance Kyle noted: Sylvera deliberately refuses to sell offsets itself, to stay unbiased — the contrast it draws against [Pachama](/notes/pachama), whose dual role as a credit seller means its ratings "cannot be considered an unbiased market standard." Long-term, Sylvera wants to inject its ratings into standardized commodity carbon contracts and net-zero asset validation.

**Context:** Sylvera is a London-based carbon-credit ratings startup founded in 2020 by Allister Furey and Sam Gill.
