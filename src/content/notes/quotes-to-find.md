# Quotes to Find

A personal to-do tag Kyle uses to mark specific quotations he wants to track down and source properly. In his [Poor Charlie's Almanack](/books/poor-charlie-s-almanack) notes, the tag flags two he's chasing: a Warren Buffett line about forecasts and a Mitt Romney line about synergies. It functions as a lightweight research-queue marker — a reminder that the exact wording or attribution still needs to be located — rather than a concept in its own right.
