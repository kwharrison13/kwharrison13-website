# Pure Truth, Pure Doctrine, and Pure Revelation

A general conference talk by [Russell M. Nelson](/notes/russell-m-nelson), delivered in the October 2021 session and noted in Kyle's conference study. The talk sits inside a session Kyle records as centering on revelation: Nelson's opening welcome framed the weekend as "a time of revelation and reflection" and warned that "in coming days, it will not be possible to survive spiritually without the guiding, directing, comforting, and constant influence of the Holy Ghost," promising "pure revelation to the questions in your heart."

In Kyle's notes the talk is filed under the conference's "Talks" list alongside the session's larger theme of being "all in" (drawn from [Jeffrey R. Holland](/notes/jeffrey-r-holland)'s remarks and the C.S. Lewis "whole tree down" passage) — the idea that pure truth, doctrine, and revelation come only to those who hand over the "whole natural self" and "make time for Him in your personal life each and every day."
