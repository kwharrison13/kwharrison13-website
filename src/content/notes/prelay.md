# Prelay

Prelay is a deal-management software startup led by [Gabriella DeFlorio](/notes/gabriella-deflorio) that Kyle sourced during his Index Ventures work, learning about it via a September 17th, 2021 call with [Steve Malouf](/notes/steve-malouf) of [Datadog ($DDOG)](/notes/datadog) (an Index Mentor advising the company — see September 17th, 2021 — Steve Malouf (Datadog) on Prelay). Per Malouf, the product fits long, complex enterprise sales cycles (60+ days) where many people touch the process and context gets lost; one core use case is POC management — triggering the platform to pull the right people into a "deal room" and track all requirements. Malouf framed the GTM as a double-edged sword: it requires multiple stakeholders across sales, implementation, and other orgs to buy in (which can lengthen the cycle), but if sold it yields many potential users and an expansion flywheel — Prelay augments rather than replaces existing systems, so the sell is to focus on a narrow use case and expand later. Malouf was advising primarily because of DeFlorio's talent and drive, and had introduced her to Datadog's [VP of Sales Engineering](/notes/vp-of-sales-engineering) plus prospects at [Atlassian](/notes/atlassian) and [Databricks](/notes/databricks). He flagged time/capacity management (helping reps forecast their own bandwidth) as a natural product extension.

**Context:** Prelay is a "deal collaboration" / revenue-team software company founded by Gabriella DeFlorio and Nicki Vartanian, built to coordinate the many internal stakeholders (sales engineering, legal, finance, leadership) involved in complex B2B deals.
